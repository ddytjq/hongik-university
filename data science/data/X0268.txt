<H1>Graphic Standards and Logos</H1>
<P>Check out the new <A HREF="long_live_ads.html"><B>Long Live Long Rides Web Banners, Posters, and Other Logos</B>.</A>


<P> IMBA encourages all members to show your support by using use these standard
IMBA logos and graphics in your marketing materials. <I>Please do not alter any IMBA
graphics. Do not change the colors, proportions, words, style, etc. </I></P>

<P> To download a high resolution .eps or .tif logo, click on the text link below each
image you want to download. Do not use the thumbnail image - It is too small for
most situations and won't look good. </P>

<P> Also, take a look at our "<A HREF="long_live_ads.html">Long Live Long Rides</a>" Ad.  We can
provide this ad at short notice in several different sizes and colors. Contact
<A HREF="mailto:pete@imba.com">pete@imba.com</A> for details. </P>

<P>IMBA's Webmaster can provide any of these logos web-ready with custom dimensions. Please email
<A HREF="mailto:webmaster@imba.com">webmaster@imba.com</A>

<P> If you have technical questions contact <A HREF="mailto:info@imba.com">info@imba.com</A> or 303-545-9011. </P>
