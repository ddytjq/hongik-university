<B>Bluefish</B> <BR ALIGN=Center>
<I>[Pomatomus saltatrix]</I>
<P ALIGN=Left>
&nbsp; &nbsp; The Bluefish is a highly migratory fish which can grow to over
40 lbs. Blues as some anglers call them usually travel in schools. This is
good to know because when one is sighted you know there are more around.
These fish go into feeding frenzies when schools of bunker or herring are
around. Blues can usually be found by "looking for the birds." This is another
fish that can be caught pretty much anywhere on Long Island. They are very
strong with sharp teeth so be carefull removing the hook.
