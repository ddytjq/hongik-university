<B>Weakfish</B> <BR ALIGN=Center>
<I>[</I><I>Cynoscion regalis]</I>
<P ALIGN=Left>
&nbsp; &nbsp; The Weakfish has been named so because its mouth is soft and
tears easily when it is hooked. You can find them mostly on the south shore
of Long Island in shallow water over a sandy bottom feeding on worms or
crustaceans. Weakfish can also be found in mid-water or the surface feeding
on small fish. The normal size is about 4 lbs. This is a very tasty fish
at the table.
