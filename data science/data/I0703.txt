 Engine Ver 0.31</H4><H1 class="entry">Swindon Town</H1><H2 class="entry">County Ground</H2><table class="entry" cellspacing=0 width="100%"><tr><td><H3 class="entry">The Ground</H3><table class="entry" cellspacing=0><TR><TD class="entry"><P><TABLE><TR><TD ALIGN="CENTER"><A HREF="/cgi-bin/ag.pl?club=disppic&pfname=/images/Swindon-Magic-Full.jpg" TARGET="_new"><IMG SRC="/images/Swindon-Magic-Thumb.jpg" ALT="Magic Roundabout &amp; Ground" WIDTH="100" HEIGHT="75" BORDER="0" ALIGN="TEXTTOP"></A></TD></TR><TR><TD ALIGN="CENTER" class="entry"><B>Magic Roundabout &amp; Ground</B></TD></TR><TR><TD ALIGN="CENTER" class="entry">Click for bigger picture</TD></TR></TABLE></P></TD><TD class="entry"><P><TABLE><TR><TD ALIGN="CENTER"><A HREF="/cgi-bin/ag.pl?club=disppic&pfname=/images/Swindon-Ground2-Full.jpg" TARGET="_new"><IMG SRC="/images/Swindon-Ground2-Thumb.jpg" ALT="Away Turnstiles" WIDTH="100" HEIGHT="75" BORDER="0" ALIGN="TEXTTOP"></A></TD></TR><TR><TD ALIGN="CENTER" class="entry"><B>Away Turnstiles</B></TD></TR><TR><TD ALIGN="CENTER" class="entry">Click for bigger picture</TD></TR></TABLE></P></TD><TD class="entry"><P><TABLE><TR><TD ALIGN="CENTER"><A HREF="/cgi-bin/ag.pl?club=disppic&pfname=/images/Swindon-Ground4-Full.jpg" TARGET="_new"><IMG SRC="/images/Swindon-Ground4-Thumb.jpg" ALT="Arkells Stand" WIDTH="100" HEIGHT="75" BORDER="0" ALIGN="TEXTTOP"></A></TD></TR><TR><TD ALIGN="CENTER" class="entry"><B>Arkells Stand</B></TD></TR><TR><TD ALIGN="CENTER" class="entry">Click for bigger picture</TD></TR></TABLE></P></TD><TD class="entry"><P><TABLE><TR><TD ALIGN="CENTER"><A HREF="/cgi-bin/ag.pl?club=disppic&pfname=/images/Swindon-Ground5-Full.jpg" TARGET="_new"><IMG SRC="/images/Swindon-Ground5-Thumb.jpg" ALT="Stratton Bank 
" WIDTH="100" HEIGHT="75" BORDER="0" ALIGN="TEXTTOP"></A></TD></TR><TR><TD ALIGN="CENTER" class="entry"><B>Stratton Bank 
</B></TD></TR><TR><TD ALIGN="CENTER" class="entry">Click for bigger picture</TD></TR></TABLE></P></TD><TD></TD></TR></TABLE><p><P>The County Ground is all seater and visiting fans are usually allocated
part of the covered Arkells Stand  (the end nearest the Stratton Bank).
If there are a lot of visiting fans they are usually given the uncovered
Stratton Bank stand behind the goal as well. There is food available at
the ground. Some seats in the Arkles Stand have obscured views.
</P><P>Swindon has hundreds of roundabouts (all individually named) and the
football ground is located on one of the most famous, The Magic
Roundabout, which is actually composed of five mini-roundabouts and is
possibly the most bizarre piece of road design in the world.
</P></P><p>Click <a class="entry" href="http://uk.multimap.com/p/browse.cgi?pc=SN12ED&Title=County+Ground+(Swindon+Town)&scale=25000&cat=loc" target="_new">here</a> to see a map of the surrounding area</P></td></tr></table><BR><table class="entry" cellspacing=0 width=100%><tr><td><H3 class="entry">Pubs</H3><p><P>Most pubs around Swindon are normally happy to admit away fans, the
exceptions being The County Ground Hotel and (sometimes) The Grove.
</P><P>Close to the ground and popular with away fans are Malt & Hops (formerly The Oxford) on Drove Rd and The Tap
&amp; Barrel (an Irish theme pub) on Manchester Rd, both are about 5-10
minutes walk.
</P><P>Additionally most pubs between the station and the ground are a fairly
safe bet including The Savoy and The Rifleman. 
</P></p><TABLE><TR><TD><P><TABLE><TR><TD ALIGN="CENTER"><A HREF="/cgi-bin/ag.pl?club=disppic&pfname=/images/Swindon-County-Full.jpg" TARGET="_new"><IMG SRC="/images/Swindon-County-Thumb.jpg" ALT="The County (Home Only)" WIDTH="100" HEIGHT="75" BORDER="0" ALIGN="TEXTTOP"></A></TD></TR><TR><TD ALIGN="CENTER"><B>The County (Home Only)</B></TD></TR><TR><TD ALIGN="CENTER">Click for bigger picture</TD></TR></TABLE></P></TD><TD><P><TABLE><TR><TD ALIGN="CENTER"><A HREF="/cgi-bin/ag.pl?club=disppic&pfname=/images/Swindon-Town-Full.jpg" TARGET="_new"><IMG SRC="/images/Swindon-Town-Thumb.jpg" ALT="Rifleman &amp; Savoy
" WIDTH="100" HEIGHT="75" BORDER="0" ALIGN="TEXTTOP"></A></TD></TR><TR><TD ALIGN="CENTER"><B>Rifleman &amp; Savoy
</B></TD></TR><TR><TD ALIGN="CENTER">Click for bigger picture</TD></TR></TABLE></P></TD></TR></TABLE><H5 class="entry">Malt & Hops (formerly The Oxford)
</H5><P ALIGN=LEFT></P><TABLE><TR><TD><P><TABLE><TR><TD ALIGN="CENTER"><A HREF="/cgi-bin/ag.pl?club=disppic&pfname=/images/Swindon-Oxford-Full.jpg" TARGET="_new"><IMG SRC="/images/Swindon-Oxford-Thumb.jpg" ALT="Malt & Hops 
" WIDTH="100" HEIGHT="75" BORDER="0" ALIGN="TEXTTOP"></A></TD></TR><TR><TD ALIGN="CENTER"><B>Malt & Hops 
<B></TD></TR><TR><TD ALIGN="CENTER">Click for bigger picture</TD></TR></TABLE></P></TD></TR></TABLE><p>Has the feel of a locals pub, but welcomes both home and away fans. It has
a selection real ales and Sky TV.
</P></p><H5 class="entry">The Grove
</H5><P ALIGN=LEFT></P><TABLE><TR><TD><P><TABLE><TR><TD ALIGN="CENTER"><A HREF="/cgi-bin/ag.pl?club=disppic&pfname=/images/Swindon-Grove-Full.jpg" TARGET="_new"><IMG SRC="/images/Swindon-Grove-Thumb.jpg" ALT="The Grove
" WIDTH="100" HEIGHT="75" BORDER="0" ALIGN="TEXTTOP"></A></TD></TR><TR><TD ALIGN="CENTER"><B>The Grove
<B></TD></TR><TR><TD ALIGN="CENTER">Click for bigger picture</TD></TR></TABLE></P></TD></TR></TABLE><p>The Grove is a Beefeater pub about 10-15 minutes walk up Drove Rd, whilst
displaying a Home Supporters only sign outside they seem to welcome
visiting families and those being discrete with colours. 
</P></p></td></tr><table><BR><table class="entry" cellspacing=0 width=100%><tr><td><H3 class="entry">Food</H3><p><P>The Frying Fish chippie on the Magic Roundabout comes very highly
recommended. A Burger King can also be found within walking distance (on
Queens Drive) and several burger and kebab vans can be found around the
ground (most prominent on the Nationwide Stand side).
</P><P>The post office on the Magic Roundabout also doubles as a newsagents and
off licence. Further choice can be found 10-15 minutes walk away in the
town centre, including a McDonalds. 
</P></p></td></tr><table><BR><table class="entry" cellspacing=0 width=100%><tr><td><H3 class="entry">Cash Machines</H3><p><P>The nearest cash machines are at Tesco's on Ocotal Way, just behind the
Cricket club (which borders the Arkles stand)
</P></p><p>Click <a class="entry" href="http://link.infonow.net/bin/findNow?CLIENT_ID=LINK_ATM" target="_new">here</A> to go to <a class="entry" HREF="http://www.link.co.uk"  TARGET="_NEW">LINKs</a> locate a cash machine page. The local postcode is SN1 2ED</P></td></tr><table><BR><table class="entry" cellspacing=0 width=100%><tr><td><H3 class="entry">Bookmakers</H3><p>A Stanley Racing can be found in the back Streets between Flemming Way and
County Road near the ground.
</P></p><p>Online betting is always available through <a class="entry" 
href="https://www.ladbrokes.co.uk/bet?AFF_ID=121"  TARGET="_NEW">Ladbrokes</a> by
clicking <a class="entry" href="https://www.ladbrokes.co.uk/bet?AFF_ID=121"  TARGET="_NEW">here</a>.</P></td></tr><table><BR><table class="entry" cellspacing=0 width=100%><tr><td><H3 class="entry">Directions</H3><p>If approaching from the South, East or West approach on the M4. Exit at
junction 15 onto the A419 towards Swindon and then take the left turn at 
the roundabout onto the A4259. Continue down here and at the roundabout
take the second turning into Queens Drive. At the 'Magic Roundabout'
take the third exit onto County Road (still A4259) and the ground is
on the right. The 'Magic Roundabout' is a combination of six
mini-roundabouts all together which used to be one very large roundabout.
The ground is visible from the 'Magic Roundabout'.
</P><P>If approaching from the North exit the M40 at junction 9 onto the A34
towards Kidlington and Oxford. On the outskirts of Oxford join the A420
towards Swindon. After 25 miles join the A4312 towards Swindon. Follow
this road for 2 miles and at the roundabout at the end turn right onto
Queens Drive. At the 'Magic Roundabout' (see above) take the third exit
onto County Road and the ground is on the right.
</P><P>The ground is signposted, but not very well.
</P></p></td></tr><table><BR><table class="entry" cellspacing=0 width=100%><tr><td><H3 class="entry">Parking</H3><p>There is parking available at the ground but you need to arrive early to
get on. The Cricket Club next to the County Ground also provides parking, 
as does the Fire station for a small donation.
</P><P>The official away parking is signposted off Flemming Way (which is off
the Magic Roundabout). Beware of residents parking schemes in the area.
</P></p></td></tr><table><BR><table class="entry" cellspacing=0 width=100%><tr><td><H3 class="entry">Train</H3><p>Swindon train station is about 20 minutes walk from the County Ground.
From the station exit and turn left onto Station Road. Follow this until
the first roundabout and then turn right onto to County Road. The ground
is on the left. 
</P></p><p>The nearest station is Swindon
. Click <a class="entry" href="http://212.87.65.227/bin/newquery.exe/en" target="_new">here</a> to check train times</p></td></tr><table><BR><table class="entry" cellspacing=0 width=100%><tr><td><H3 class="entry">Accommodation</H3><p>There is at least 1 B&amp;B on Drove Rd (off the Magic Roundabout)
and a large Thistle Hotel in the town centre.  
</P></p><p>Click 
<a class="entry" href="http://www.smoothhound.co.uk/swindon.html"  TARGET="_NEW">
here</a> to see the local accomodation available from 
<a class="entry" href="http://www.smoothhound.co.uk"  TARGET="_NEW">  Smoothhound</a>.</p></td></tr><table><BR><table class="entry" cellspacing=0 width=100%><tr><td><H3 class="entry">Local Fans</H3><p>Trouble is very rare and only likely at derby games. The main local rivals
are Oxford.
</P></p></td></tr><table><BR><table class="entry" cellspacing=0 width=100%><tr><td><H3 class="entry">Chants</H3><p>Swindons most famous chant in recent years is "Can't help falling in love
with you" by UB40, although it's been heard a lot less during the last
couple of seasons.
</P></p></td></tr><table><BR><table class="entry" cellspacing=0 width=100%><tr><td><H3 class="entry">After The Game</H3><p>Swindon centre has a large number of restaurants to choose from, most of
which are shown on the maps dotted around the town centre.
</P></p></td></tr><table><BR><table class="entry" cellspacing=0 width=100%><tr><td><H3 class="entry">Local Attractions</H3><p>Swindon is a short drive away to Stonehenge and Longleat Safari Park.
Swindon itself has a large pedestrianised shopping area with plenty of
shops, a designer outlet village and museums.
For further details of what there is to in and around Swindon click <a class="entry" href="http://www.swindon.gov.uk">here</a>.
</P><P>Alternatively you could of course go and count all the roundabouts !
