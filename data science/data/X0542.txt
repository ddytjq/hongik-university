<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>ATLANTA&nbsp;-- Tennessee's inspiration coming into the SEC Tournament?</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<?xml:namespace prefix = o ns = "urn:schemas-microsoft-com:office:office" /><o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>Siena.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>That's right&nbsp;-- the tiny school from Loudonville, N.Y., that ignored its No. 7 seed and won the Metro Atlantic Athletic Conference Monday night in Albany.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>"We saw what they did," first-year UT coach Buzz Peterson said during Wednesday's press conferences here at the Georgia Dome. "We've had an interesting season, and this is a wide-open tournament."</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>"Interesting" doesn't even begin to describe the Vols' 2001-02 go-round.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>Peterson's inaugural bunch played 13 games that were decided by five or fewer points. UT lost the first seven of those and then won four of the final six.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>The difference between the Vols' 14-15 record and a potential 21-8 ledger, in fact, turned out to be exactly 16 points and less than 10 seconds overall.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>Tough-luck Tennessee opens tourney play with a 3:15 tip against Arkansas Thursday afternoon at the Dome.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>But win or lose against the Razorbacks&nbsp;-- or against anybody else in the next couple days&nbsp;-- Tennessee seems like a poster child of sorts for this winter's wacky SEC.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>The grueling 12-team circuit&nbsp;-- No. 1 in the conference rankings on CollegeRPI.com&nbsp;-- features four ranked teams and six 20-game winners heading into this weekend's postseason smorgasbord.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>Six teams have gone from the SEC to the NCAAs in each of the last three seasons. That figure is a certainty down the stretch here in 2002. And it could swell to seven depending on what happens down here in Dixie.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>Nine different squads&nbsp;-- that is, all of them except LSU, Vanderbilt and Auburn&nbsp;-- are among the RPI's top 54. Florida is sixth, Alabama is No. 8 and Kentucky is No. 10. Tennessee&nbsp;-- with a first-team all-conference player in Vincent Yarbrough, a second-teamer in Marcus Haislip and a sub-.500 mark&nbsp;-- is at No. 54.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>And that's downright wacky.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>"The league is quite nuts," quote-worthy LSU coach John Brady said. "It's pretty unpredictable, but that's what makes this the best league in the country. The difference between the top teams and the bottom teams is not what is it in some other leagues."</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>Ask Florida.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>Billy Donovan's team is 12th in this week's USA Today/ESPN coaches poll and 11th in the AP's version. Yet the Gators finished 10-6 in league action&nbsp;-- tied for the East Division title with Georgia and Kentucky&nbsp;-- and have to play Auburn Thursday night.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>Four games in four days to be conference tourney champ? For a divisional winner?</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>"It's anybody's tournament," Donovan said. "Whatever team can put together three or four days of good basketball will be the one who wins it."</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>And there are a wide variety of teams that could do that.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>"Alabama won the league by two games," Anderson said. "Florida may have the best talent in the league. Georgia's playing well. It's all about how you're playing at this point in time."</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>Even for the supposed "favorites."</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>"Our RPI is No. 11," said Rick Stansbury, whose Mississippi State team finished the regular season at 23-7 overall and 10-6 in SEC games. "Only Kentucky and Alabama are ahead of us, and we beat them both. And only Alabama&nbsp;-- the conference champion&nbsp;-- has won more games than us."</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>So why not Peterson and his Tennessee team?</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>The Vols got 18 points and 11 rebounds from Haislip and 17 points from Yarbrough to notch a dramatic 71-63 Senior Night overtime win over Georgia to close the season on a high note.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>And if these games are going to be close here at the SEC's postseason party&nbsp;-- and all indications point to just that&nbsp;-- then UT's coaches and players say bring 'em on.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>"We've been through so many tight games," Peterson said. "Our guys are so used to it. There's no pressure at all."</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>For Tennessee or any of the other teams that have to play four games. Could it happen? Charles Barkley and Auburn did it in 1985. Brandon Dean and Arkansas did it two years ago right here in the Dome.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>"When you start hitting some shots&nbsp;-- from the perimeter, threes, whatever&nbsp;-- it's amazing how it gets your players ore energy and momentum," Peterson said. "You never know what can happen. This league has been very interesting. Some of the bottom teams can knock off some of the top teams."</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>True. The only thing for sure at the SEC, in fact, is that nothing is for sure.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>All Jenis Grindstaff knows is this: "Our mindset is to win four straight," the UT senior point guard said Wednesday afternoon. "Hopefully we'll be able to do that."</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>It'd be no crazier than what little ol' Siena did up in the MAAC.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<H1 style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>PEPPERDINE DESERVES THE CHANCE TO DANCE</FONT></H1>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>Folks in the Pepperdine Department of Athletics announced on Wednesday afternoon plans to host a Selection Sunday gathering on the school's Malibu, Calif., campus.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>"All interested fans are invited to attend," Pepperdine basketball publicist Mike Zapolski said, "as the Waves will learn where they will be playing in this year's NCAA Tournament."</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>Such confidence.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>Coach Paul Westphal's Waves are 22-8 and boast an RPI of 52. They have some bad losses (Nov. 20 in double overtime at Cal-Irvine, Dec. 8 at Santa Barbara) and more good wins (Nov. 28 at UCLA, Dec. 6 at USC, Jan. 18 over Gonzaga).</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>That makes them the best team in Southern California. But does it make them a team worthy of a bid to the Field?</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>Absolutely Pepperdine is worthy. But this is March Madness. This is bubble talk. This sometimes doesn't make sense.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>Still ...</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>"The thing I wonder is, Why are we even being asked?" Westphal said after Gonzaga's thrilling win over his Waves in the West Coast Conference championship game late Monday night.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>"We beat UCLA on their home court, for example, and they've split their last 16 games. Yet no one is asking them if they are in danger. And we beat USC and no one is asking them that question."</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>Shame, isn't it?</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>Pepperdine has lost twice since Dec. 30. Both of those losses came to the Zags&nbsp;-- the sixth-ranked team in America, remember&nbsp;-- and the Waves played a schedule that ranks among the nation's five most difficult.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>They should be in. Period.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>Ditto for another "P" school: Penn.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>Fran Dunphy's Quakers are 24-6 and waiting for Princeton and Yale to play each other Thursday night in an effort to earn a Saturday date against Penn&nbsp;-- all part of the crazy Ivy League playoff format that'll produce the conference's automatic bid to the NCAAs.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>If Princeton wins two games to earn that bid, though&nbsp;-- or if Yale does it, for that matter&nbsp;-- Penn should still make the Field.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>The Quakers are good enough. (That's not new.) And so is the Ivy. (That is.) Seriously, though, the Ancient Eight is No. 13 in CollegeRPI.com's conference rankings.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>Please make Pepperdine and Penn NCAA tourney teams before, say, Syracuse or Georgetown or Memphis or Virginia or Vegas.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>For the love of any and all college basketball gods.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<H1 style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>AROUND AND ABOUT</FONT></H1>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>*** Dukies everywhere! <B>Jason Williams</B> and <B>Mike Dunleavy</B> were both named NABC first-team All-Americans this week. Williams, well, everyone knows what Williams did this year&nbsp;-- and what he's done for the past several years&nbsp;-- and Dunleavy was one of just two players in the ACC ranked in the top 10 in seven different statistical categories. Fellow Blue Devil <B>Carlos Boozer</B> earned third-team honors.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>*** <B>David West</B> became the third player in Atlantic 10 history to garner back-to-back outright league player of the year laurels. He also got a place on the league's all-defensive team. And why not? Throw any and all awards his way. The Xavier big man is averaging a team-best 18.5 points and a conference-leading 10 boards a game.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>*** Smart cookies, those Spiders. Three UR hoopsters worked their way onto the A-10's All-Academic team&nbsp;-- <B>Tony Dobbins</B>, a defensive ace; <B>Mike Skrocki</B>, a sleepy-eyed shooter; and <B>Scott Ungerer</B>, the husky "point guard" who's one of the more versatile players on the planet.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>*** So <B>Jeff Capel III</B> is ready to be a Division I head coach, huh? Wasn't it just yesterday that Capel threw in a half-court shot to take North Carolina to overtime in that classic at Cameron Indoor Stadium? Now he's a coach? A head coach, no less? Apparently the sports brass at Virginia Commonwealth think so. <B>Mack McCarthy</B> stepped down as Rams head man. The 27-year-old will take over next season. Maybe he can hire kid brother <B>Jason Capel</B> as an assistant&nbsp;... because he's qualified for that, too.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>*** Duh: <B>Drew Gooden</B> was named Big 12 Player of the Year this week. Expect the honors to keep rolling on in for the dominant Kansas post player. (He should be the National Player of the Year over Williams or Dunleavy or Cincinnati's <B>Steve Logan</B> or anybody else ? but that's a different column for another time.) Gooden is averaging 20.6 points and 11.1 rebounds an outing for the No. 1 Jayhawks. His 20 double-doubles are one shy of <B>Raef LaFrentz</B>'s four-year-old school record. "Drew Gooden is deserving," KU coach <B>Roy Williams</B> said. "He had a phenomenal year. During a couple of stretches he played as well as&nbsp;-- if not better than&nbsp;-- any player I've ever had." Let's see&nbsp;... <B>Jacque Vaughan</B>&nbsp;... LaFrentz&nbsp;... <B>Paul Pierce</B>&nbsp;... yeah&nbsp;... that's pretty good.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>*** Too bad Vermont went down to Maine in overtime in the America East semifinals last weekend in Boston. The Catamounts put together a dream season&nbsp;-- in spite of the unexpectedly premature ending. Coach <B>Tom Brennan</B>'s squad posted the school's first-ever 20-win season and first-ever regular-season conference title. The 21-8 record&nbsp;-- and 13-3 mark in the AEC&nbsp;-- constitute the program's finest season ever. Forward <B>Trevor Gaines</B> was the best player in the league, <B>T.J. Sorrentine</B> was the nation's best point guard folks really didn't get a chance to know, and Brennan was NABC District I (New England) Coach of the Year.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>*** Sign of the times? A sophomore was named Big 10 Player of the Year for the second straight season. Illinois guard <B>Frank Williams</B> won the award in 2000-2001. IU swingman <B>Jared Jeffries</B> was top dog this time around. He's averaging a well-balanced 15.1 points, 7.9 rebounds, 2.1 blocks and 1.5 steals an outing this winter.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>*** How about Georgia swingman <B>Jarvis Hayes</B> gaining a spot on the SEC's tippety-top all-league team? The redshirt sophomore joined Alabama's <B>Erwin Dudley</B>, Kentucky's <B>Tayshaun Prince</B>, Tennessee's <B>Vincent Yarbrough</B> and Florida's <B>Udonis Haslem</B> on the league's best-of-the-best roster. Think about this: Two years ago Hayes was tearing up the Southern Conference as a Western Carolina Catamount.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>*** Who wants Co-Players of the Year? The Big East seems particularly fond of spreading the wealth. Boston College's <B>Troy Bell</B> shared the award with Notre Dame's <B>Troy Murphy </B>last season. And Pittsburgh's <B>Brandin Knight</B> and Connecticut's <B>Caron Butler</B> split the honor this year's honor earlier this week. The league also put&nbsp;-- because of ties in balloting&nbsp;-- <I>seven</I> players on its all-league first team. And what's up with the conference administrators' insistence that BIG EAST be capitalized?</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>*** Usually it's the coaches who know what they're talking about and they media that are clueless. But it was the other way around in the Southern Conference's end-of-season voting. The coaches tabbed East Tennessee State's <B>Dimeco Childress</B> as Player of the Year even though the media went with VMI freshman <B>Jason Conley</B>. The high-scoring Keydet led the nation in scoring at 29.3 points a game and proved over and over again that he was the best talent in the league.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>*** Kudos to the SEC basketball brass for providing a most useful and timely free gift&nbsp;-- a neato black backpack&nbsp;-- that's been key for toting media guides and notebooks to and fro down here in the A-T-L.</FONT></P></DIV>
