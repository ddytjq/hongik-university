This page will provide you information on links with VB Center.

<P>
<TABLE BORDER=0 WIDTH=100% BGCOLOR="#AAAAAA"><TR><TD>
<STRONG><A NAME="1">1. Front page links</STRONG></TD>
</TR></TABLE>

<P>
You may have noticed a new section on VB Center's front page. That's
the featured link, changed bi-weekly, where you can put an ad for
some important VB-related event on the web. For example, we're for
this first time displaying a link to WorldTrain, that will provide
online classes on Internet Programming. If you would like to have
your link there in the next 2 weeks,
<A HREF="mailto:scarneiro@tba.com.br?Subject='Featured link'">
e-mail us</A> and your suggestion will be put in the list and displayed
when it's your turn.
<P>In the e-mail, don't forget to provide:
<P>- The name of the site
<BR>- Its web address
<BR>- A description of what's going to happen
<P>And if it's ok I would like to have a link to VB Center on your page :)


<P>

<TABLE BORDER=0 WIDTH=100% BGCOLOR="#AAAAAA"><TR><TD>
<STRONG>2. Normal links</STRONG></TD>
</TR></TABLE>

<P>
If what you want is just a simple link, it's just
<A HREF="mailto:scarneiro@tba.com.br?Subject='Normal Link'">
e-mailing us</A> telling your page's name and address. Only if it was
very bad it wouldn't be added. Of course, it must be about VB, too.
And, again, if you could add a link to VB Center on your page I would
really appreciate.
