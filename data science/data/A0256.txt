      <h5><b><a name="personalaccount"></a><br>
        <u>Personal Accounts</u></b></h5>
      <p><b><a name="ufirstaccount"></a>U First Account</b><br>
        No transaction charges will be applied in respect of cheques, automated
				debits and lodgments or non-automated debits and lodgments, 
				standing orders, Direct Debits and Switch payments.</p>

      <p>An automatic debit interest free buffer limit of £250 applies to every
        U First Account. If however your debit balance exceeds the buffer
        limit, standard rate of interest will apply to the whole of the debit 
        balance and in addition surcharge interest will apply to the balance
        in excess of the buffer limit.</P>

      <p>A statement of your debit interest, if applicable, will be forwarded to
        you at least 2 weeks before it is applied to your account. </P>
      <p align="right"><a class="content" style="text-decoration:none;" href="#top">top 
        ^</a><b></b></p>
      <p><b><a name="currentaccount"></a> Current Account</b><br>
        Account fees are calculated on the third Friday in February, May, August 
        and November and applied to your account three weeks after the calculation 
        date. These fees are based on the activity of your account during the 
        charging period, eg a fee applied to your account in September relates 
        to the activity of your account from the third Friday in May to the third 
        Friday in August. When you maintain a credit balance during the three 
        month charging period, no account fees are incurred.</p>
      <p align="right"><a class="content" style="text-decoration:none;" href="#top">top 
        ^</a><b></b> </p>
      <p><b><a name="currentplus"></a><br>
        Current Plus</b><br>
        No transaction fees will be applied in respect of Cheques, ATM withdrawals, 
        Standing Orders, Direct Debits and Switch payments. However, 
        a Monthly Account Charge will be charged for the month or part of a month 
        during which the account is overdrawn. This fee, if applicable, will be 
        calculated on the third Friday of each month and applied to accounts on 
        the following Monday.</p>
      <p>A statement of your debit interest, if applicable, will be forwarded 
        to you at least 2 weeks before it is applied to your account.</p>
      <p align="right"><a class="content" style="text-decoration:none;" href="#top">top 
        ^</a><b></b></p>
      <p><b><a name="dualaccount"></a><br>
        Dual Account<br>
        </b>No charge will be made in respect of debit interest or transaction 
        fees. However, a Monthly Account Charge will be charged for the month 
        or part of a month during which you account is overdrawn. This fee, if 
        applicable, will be calculated on the third Friday of each month and applied 
        to accounts on the following Monday.</p>
      <p align="right"><a class="content" style="text-decoration:none;" href="#top">top 
        ^</a><b></b></p>
      <p><b><a name="premiumaccount"></a><br>
        Premium Account (no longer available to new customers)</b> <br>
        Account fees are calculated on the third Friday in February and August 
        and applied to your account three weeks after the calculation date. These 
        fees are based on the activity of your account and apply to any items 
        in excess of the 'free' transactions during the charging period eg a fee 
        applied to your account in September relates to the activity of your account 
        from May to August.</p>
      <p align="right"><a class="content" style="text-decoration:none;" href="#top">top 
        ^</a><b></b></p>
      <p align="center"><b><a name="notification"></a><br>
        Pre-Notification of Charges*</b></p>
      <p align="center"><b><br>
				<map name="Map">
					<area shape="rect" coords="6,210,133,224" href="#acmaintenance" alt="Account Maintenance" title="Account Maintenance">
					<area shape="rect" coords="5,224,151,236" href="#nonautolodg" alt="Non-Automated Lodgments" title="Non-Automated Lodgments">
					<area shape="rect" coords="5,251,141,269" href="#autolodg" alt="Automated Lodgments" title="Automated Lodgments">
					<area shape="rect" coords="5,300,109,320" href="#otherdebits" alt="Other Debits" title="Other Debits">
				</map> 
        <img src="/static/AccfeesNI_1.gif" width="368" height="412" align="middle" usemap="#Map" border="0" alt="">
        </b></p>
      <P>*Pre-notification of Charges on U First and Current Plus Accounts
         is applicable to debit interest only.<br>
        *Pre-notification of Charges is not applicable to Dual Accounts.</p>
      <p>A statement giving a breakdown of your account fees and debit interest 
        will be forwarded to you at least two weeks before those charges are applied 
        to your account. An example of the statement and the information provided 
        is shown above. When you receive the statement there is no need for you 
        to take any further action - it is simply advance notice of the charges 
        which will be automatically applied to your account. However, if you have 
        any queries please contact your branch where a member of staff will be 
        pleased to help.</p>
      <p align="right"><a class="content" style="text-decoration:none;" href="#top">top 
        ^</a><b></b></p>
      <p><b><a name="acmaintenance"></a><br>
        A. Account Maintenance<br>
        </b>This fee covers the provision of current account services including 
        bank statements and dealing with general account queries. </p>
      <p align="right"><a class="content" style="text-decoration:none;" href="#top">top 
        ^</a><b></b></p>
      <p><b><a name="nonautolodg"></a><br>
        B. Non-Automated Lodgments<br>
        </b>These are lodgments which take place at the counter of an Ulster Bank 
        branch or another bank.</p>
      <p align="right"><a class="content" style="text-decoration:none;" href="#top">top 
        ^</a><b></b></p>
      <p><b><a name="autolodg"></a><br>
        C. Automated Lodgments<br>
        </b>These are lodgments which take place electronically or without the 
        use of paper. They are sent to your bank through the automated clearing 
        system.</p>
      <p align="right"><a class="content" style="text-decoration:none;" href="#top">top 
        ^</a><b></b></p>
      <p><b><a name="otherdebits"></a><br>
        D. Other Debits<br>
        </b>These include the following:</p>
      <ul>
        <li>non-cheque withdrawals at the counter</li>
        <li>non-automated direct debits</li>
        <li>withdrawals at cash machines using a eurocheque card.</li>
      </ul>
      <p>Ony fee details relevant to your account will appear on each statement 
        eg if you do not have any Direct Debits, the line 'Direct Debits' will 
        not appear.</P>
      <p align="right"><a class="content" style="text-decoration:none;" href="#top">top 
        ^</a><b></b><br>
      </p>
      <h5><b><a name="nonpersonalacs"></a><br>
        <u>Non-Personal Accounts</u></b></h5>
      <p>Since non-personal customers have individual needs, each account is assessed 
        on an individual basis to calculate a fee based on the level of activity. 
        Account fees are charged to these accounts each quarter in March, June, 
        September and December. <a href="fees.asp">See Account Fees and Ancillary 
        Charges for details.</a></p>
      <p align="right"><a class="content" style="text-decoration:none;" href="#top">top 
        ^</a><b></b></p>
      <h5><b><br>
        <a name="cleared"></a><br>
        <u>Personal and Non-Personal Accounts</u></b></h5>
      <p><b> </b><b> Cleared/Uncleared Items</b><br>
        Whenever you pay a cheque into your account the amount of the cheque will 
        be credited to your account that day.<br>
        However, it will usually take two working days for the cheque to be processed 
        (ie passed through to the bank and branch on which it is drawn and payment 
        obtained). During this period the cheque is said to be 'uncleared'.<br>
        <br>
        It is therefore important to remember that the balance of your account 
        may contain some funds which are still 'uncleared'. If you draw against 
        any uncleared funds, and you do not have enough in your account to cover 
        the amount of the drawing, interest will be charged. Your branch will 
        be pleased to tell you your cleared balance on request.</p>
      <p>While the above explanation covers the clearance of cheques for value 
        in your account, it does not deal with the fate of these cheques ie the 
        payment of them. Sometimes a cheque which you have paid in may be refused 
        by the bank on which it is drawn (eg because the person who gave it to 
        you has insufficient money in his or her account).</p>
      <p>Your branch will advise you if this happens and the additional cost of 
        dealing with any unpaid cheques will be charged to your account immediately.</p>
      <p align="right"><a class="content" style="text-decoration:none;" href="#top">top 
        ^</a><b></b></p>
      <p><b><a name="agency"></a><br>
        Agency Arrangements</b><br>
        Full banking facilities are available by arrangement to customers of other 
        associated banks and other licensed banks. Details of these services and 
        charges are available on request from any of our branches.</p>
      <p align="right"><a class="content" style="text-decoration:none;" href="#top">top 
        ^</a><b></b></p>
      <p><b><a name="ancillarycharges"></a><br>
        Ancillary Charges<br>
        </b>These apply to all personal (including U First, Current 
        and Current Plus) and non-personal 
        accounts irrespective of whether they are conducted in credit or debit 
        and are in addition to account fees. They are usually applied to your 
        account at the time the service is provided. <a href="fees.asp">See Account 
        Fees and Ancillary Charges for details.</a></p>
