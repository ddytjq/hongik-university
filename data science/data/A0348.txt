            <p class="contentsubhead">Important Information</p>
            <p>This Facility is not available for business purposes.</p>
            <p>Written quotations are available on request. All loans are subject 
              to status and applicants must be 18 or over. A first mortgage or 
              charge over your property will be required as security for this 
              facility.</p>
            <p>The minimum loan amount is &pound;50,000. Your salary (or in the 
              case of joint borrowers the primary salary) must be paid into the 
              current account.</p>
            <p>The Safety Limit reduces until the loan is fully repaid and can 
              be set to reduce over any term up to 25 years. The Safety Limit 
              is based on what would otherwise have been the balance of our 25 
              year standard repayment home loan.</p>
            <p>The savings which you are able to make, are based on comparing 
              our Rapid Repay Facility to our standard 25 year repayment Home 
              Loan (on which daily interest is calculated) of the same amount. 
              For example, if you had a Rapid Repay Facility of &pound;65,000 
              and you paid &pound;2,000 into your account each month and spent 
              &pound;1,500 at even intervals throughout the month, you would have 
              a surplus of &pound;500 in the account to reduce your outstanding 
              loan amount and cover any debit interest due. By leaving this amount 
              each month you could pay off your Home Loan 7 years and 8 months 
              early and save &pound;24,645.39 in interest.</p>
            <p>Interest rates and charges are correct as at 1 June 2002 but these 
              may vary which could affect repayments.</p>
            <p>Typical example: for a Rapid Repay Facility of &pound;65,000 over 
              17 years and 4 months if you paid &pound;2,000 into your account 
              each month and spent &pound;1,500 at even intervals throughout the 
              month, you will pay interest at the rate of 5.49% per annum (variable) 
              <b>(APR 6.2% variable).</b> You must pay whatever amount is required at 
              any time to ensure that your Safety Limit is not exceeded. The APR 
              includes an arrangement fee of &pound;200, solicitor's mortgage 
              charges estimated to be &pound;235.00, a professional valuation 
              estimated at &pound;146.88, a monthly maintenance fee of &pound;10 
              and a deeds release fee currently &pound;50. The maximum loan is 
              90% of the value of the property for Rapid Repay Facility, based 
              on the lower of the purchase price or valuation. Loans of more than 
              75% of the lower of purchase price or valuation may require a professional 
              valuation and an additional fee.</p>
