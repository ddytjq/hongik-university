		<TD COLSPAN=3 CLASS=body><SPAN CLASS=header>About Internet Banking</SPAN>

<p>
Bank of Scotland's Internet Banking service allows you to enjoy complete control of your finances, free of charge, 24 hours a day.
</p>

<p>
In a few clicks you can communicate with us securely to:
</p>

<ul>
	<li>View account balances and transaction details </li>
	<li>Transfer funds between your accounts </li>
	<li>Pay bills </li>
	<li>View and amend Standing Orders </li>
	<li>View details of Direct Debits </li>
</ul>

<p>
Not only can you transfer funds between your accounts, but you can also action payments to any third party in the UK, whether an individual, business or utility.
</p>

<p>
If your business requires it, you can also set up multiple employee access to Internet Banking, and control their level of access and authority to transact.
</p>

<p>
With Internet Banking's secure messaging facility you can request the following:
</p>

<ul>
	<li>Assistance on how to use Internet Banking</li>
	<li>Transfer Direct Debits to a linked account</li>
	<li>Reinstate previous Direct Debits</li>
	<li>Cancel Direct Debits</li>
	<li>Order replacement plastic key cards (please note that this does not include replacement credit cards - telephone 01383 628444 to enquire about a replacement credit card)</li>
	<li>Order replacement pin numbers (for replacement credit card pin numbers please telephone 01383 628444)</li>
	<li>Adjust standing order amounts</li>
	<li>Adjust standing order dates and frequency</li>
	<li>Suspend a standing order payment</li>
	<li>Order a copy statement</li>
	<li>Order a Bank Giro Credit Book</li>
	<li>Request a Certificate - we can provide a balance of account, interest received/paid, CDIT (deduction of Income Tax) and MIRAS 5 certificates</li>
</ul>

<p>
If you'd like to see how your business can benefit from Internet Banking, please take the <a class="topnav" HREF="tour/bus_tour/index.html" TARGET="tour" onClick="windowOpen('tour',760,400)">tour</a>.
</p>

<p>
<b>Getting Started</b> <br>
To take advantage of Internet Banking, you need to have a Bank of Scotland Business Banking account.
</p>

<p>
If you wish to apply for Internet Banking, please <a class="topnav" href="applicationform.html">click here</a>.
</p>

<p>
<b>Case studies</b> <br>
<a class="topnav" href="ibcasestudies.html">Click here</a> to see how someone else has benefited from banking online with us.
</p>
