<font color="red">
<B>Graphics</b>
</font>
</p>

<p align=left>
<font face="tahoma">
<font size="2">
A few of VB's graphic controls include, the <B>Picture Box</b>, the <B>Image Box</b>, the <B>Line control</b>, and of coarse the <B>Shapes</b> control.<Br>
<Br>
<Br>
<font color="blue">The Picture Box<Br>
</font>
<Br>
The Picture Box control is what it says, all it does is show a picture on the form.<Br>
<Br>
<font color="blue">
Picture Box Properties:<Br>
</font>
<Br>
<font color="red">
AutoSize</font> - When true it resizes the Picture box to fit the picture<Br>
<Br>
<font color="red">
Picture</font> - Add a picture to your project<Br>
<Br>


<p align=center>
<font face="tahoma">
<font size="2">
<font color="red">
<img src="http://www.angelfire.com/ny3/visualbasic/images/note.jpg"> <B>Note:</b> You can load pictures at run time like so "Picture1.Picture = LoadPicture("C:\main.bmp")" cool eh?
</font>
</p>


<p align=left>
<font face="tahoma">
<font size="2">
<font color="blue">The Image Control<Br>
</font>
<Br>
The Image control, unlike the picture box, can stretch. Meaning you can literally stretch it out and it gets bigger, or make it smaller.<Br>
<Br>
<font color="blue">
Image Control Properties:<Br>
</font>
<Br>
<font color="red">
Picture</font> - Add a picture to your project<Br>
<Br>
<font color="red">
Stretch</font> - If set to true you can stretch your image out from its original size<Br>


<p align=left>
<font face="tahoma">
<font size="2">
<font color="blue">The Line & Shapes controls<Br>
</font>
<Br>
<font color="red">
Line</font> - Simple line you can draw on your form<Br>
<Br>
<font color="red">
Rectangle</font> - Rectangle you can put on your form<Br>
<Br>
<font color="red">
Square</font> - Square you can put on your form<Br>
<Br>
<font color="red">
Oval</font> - Oval you can put on your form<Br>
<Br>
<font color="red">
Circle</font> - Circle you can put on your form<Br>
<Br>
<font color="red">
Rounded Rectangle</font> - Rounded rectangle you can put on your form<Br>
<Br>
<font color="red">
Rounded Square</font> - Rounded Square you can put on your form<Br>
<Br>
<Br>
<font color="blue">
Line Properties:<Br>
</font>
<Br>
<font color="red">
BorderStyle</font> - Changes its look<Br>
<Br>
<font color="red">
BorderWidth</font> - Changes its width<Br>
<Br>
<font color="blue">
Shape Properties:<Br>
</font>
<Br>
<font color="red">
BackStyle</font> - Determines if it's transparent or not<Br>
<Br>
<font color="red">
BorderWidth</font> - Changes its width<Br>
<Br>
<font color="red">
FillStyle</font> - Determines how it's filled<Br>
</p>
