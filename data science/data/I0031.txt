<p><font face="Arial,Helvetica" size="2"><strong>Format Explained<br>
</strong>Four 'Groups' are involved in finally establishing the 16-team knockout UEFA Cup,
with no automatic qualifiers. The composition of the four groups:</font> 

<ul>
  <li><font face="Arial,Helvetica" size="2"><strong>Group 1:</strong> The eight UEFA Champions
    League teams who are ranked in third place in their respective groups after the first UEFA
    Champions League group stage has concluded. These teams enter the UEFA Cup at the Third
    Round - <strong>R3</strong> - stage.</font></li>
  <li><font face="Arial,Helvetica" size="2"><strong>Group 2:</strong> The 16 teams who are
    eliminated from the final UEFA Champions League qualifying phase. These clubs join the
    UEFA Cup at the First Round - <strong>R1</strong> - stage.</font></li>
  <li><font face="Arial,Helvetica" size="2"><strong>Group 3: </strong>In total, 39 clubs from
    four distinct sub-groups. Firstly, there are the 17 national cup winners from the
    associations ranked 1-17. The second sub-group consists of 18 clubs, namely the three
    sixth-placed teams from the domestic leagues of the countries ranked from 1-3, eight
    fifth-placed teams from countries ranked from 1-8, five fourth-placed teams from countries
    ranked from 4-8 and two third-placed teams from the countries ranked 7-8. The final two
    sub-groups comprise three qualifiers from the UEFA Intertoto Cup and the 1998/99 UEFA Cup
    winner. All of these clubs join the UEFA Cup at the First Round - <strong>R1</strong> -
    stage.&nbsp; </font></li>
  <li><font face="Arial,Helvetica" size="2"><strong>Group 4: </strong>The final group consists
    of 82 teams who enter the the UEFA Cup at one Qualifying Phase - <strong>Q3</strong> -
    prior to the start of the competition proper. This group has three sub-groups comprising
    firstly of 17 clubs, namely 13 third-placed clubs from countries ranked 9-21, the champion
    from Andorra, and three Fair Play clubs. The second sub-group comprises 32 national cup
    winners from associations ranked 18-49, while the third group consists of 33 runners-up in
    domestic championships from countries ranked 16-48.</font></li>
</ul>

<p><font face="Arial,Helvetica" size="2">At the culmination of the UEFA Cup Third Round,
the 16 advance to the <strong>Fourth Round</strong>, when the competition adopts the
knockout format that currently exists until two teams compete in the <strong>Final</strong>.<br>
The following tables detail the dates of the matches and the format of the matches.</font></p>
