In       addition to this amount, you are able to set up an unsecured credit       facility for any amount between £1,000 and £30,000.</FONT>                                                                     </p>        
        <p><FONT face=Arial><i><b>together </b></i> <b>INTEREST RATES</b></FONT> 
        </p>
        <p><FONT face=Arial>The interest rate charged on <I>together</I> is currently 5.69% P.A. (<B>6.1% APR</B>) where the mortgage is up to 75% of the lower of the purchase price or value of your property, 5.79% P.A. (<B>6.1% APR</B>) where the mortgage is  up to  90% of the lower of the purchase price or value of your property and 5.84% P.A. (<B>6.1% APR</B>) where the mortgage is up to       95% of the lower of the purchase price or value of your property. These rates are variable.</FONT>                 </p><p><FONT face=Arial>The 95% loan to value product is       guaranteed to be no more than 1.85% above the *Bank of England Base Rate       until 1 January 2005. The 90% loan to value product will remain below the       95% loan to value product and the 75% loan to value product will remain       below the 90% loan to value product for the life of the loan.</FONT>                                                              </p><p><FONT face=Arial>After the guaranteed rate period has ended, the variable interest rate that will then apply for the whole of the remaining loan term is guaranteed to be below our Standard Variable Mortgage Base Rate for existing customers.
