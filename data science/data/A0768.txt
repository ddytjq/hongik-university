<P>
		<a name="partnerships"><span class="topnav">Partnerships</span></a>
<P>
		<span class="body">The Infrastructure Finance Partnerships team specialises in delivering integrated funding solutions for PFI/PPP projects.  Working in partnership with key industry sponsors, we aim to provide the optimum funding mix of senior debt, subordinated debt, mezzanine finance and equity.  Our dedicated team has vast  experience of structuring and arranging finance for large-scale infrastructure projects across the full spectrum of industry sectors, including health, education and defence.  Contact the <a href="javascript:openWindow('contact-infrastructure.html');"><span class="bold">team</span></a> for more information.</span>
<P>
		<a name="riskcapital"><span class="topnav">Risk Capital</span></a>
<P>
		<span class="body">Bank of Scotland Infrastructure Finance is one of the UK's leading providers of equity, subordinated debt and mezzanine finance to the PFI/PPP market.  We have a specialised team with extensive experience of project and bid development through to operations.  With investments in more than 25 projects (including five major acute hospitals and seven grouped schools projects) and two specialist funds, the team has well-established relationships with many leading PFI/PPP sponsors.  Contact the <a href="javascript:openWindow('contact-infrastructure.html');"><span class="bold">team</span></a> for more information.</span>
<P>
		<a name="corporate"><span class="topnav">Corporate PFI &#38; Real Estate Partnerships</span></a>
<P>
		<span class="body">Building on our success in the PFI/PPP sector and our reputation for continual innovation, Bank of Scotland Infrastructure Finance has a growing expertise in the Corporate PFI and Real Estate Partnerships market. Our activity in this developing sector involves the funding of large-scale property outsourcing and asset procurement projects for companies that recognise the key benefits of value for money, transfer of risk and capital efficient procurement, which PFI provides.  Contact the <a href="javascript:openWindow('contact-infrastructure.html');"><span class="bold">team</span></a> for more information.</span>
<P>
		<a name="europe"><span class="topnav">Europe & Overseas</span></a>
<P>
		<span class="body">Bank of Scotland is active in the developing PPP market in Europe, where we currently provide funds for a number of large-scale infrastructure projects.  With our reputation as the leading provider of finance to the UK PFI/PPP market, we are developing a series of strategic partnerships with major European corporates.  Our wide network of local European offices, combined with the extensive knowledge of our UK-based teams, ensures that Bank of Scotland is well placed to deliver the best possible PPP and infrastructure funding solution.</span>
<P>
		<span class="body">With Bank of Scotland's fully staffed office based in Sydney, we can now offer our expertise to the emerging Australian PPP infrastructure market.  Contact the <a href="javascript:openWindow('contact-infrastructure.html');"><span class="bold">team</span></a> for more information.</span>
<P>
		<a name="asset"><span class="topnav">Asset Management</span></a>
<P>
		<span class="body">Managing a portfolio of more than 60 projects spread across all major sectors of PFI, with a combined value in excess of &#163;2billion, the Infrastructure Finance Asset Management team works closely with transacting areas, bringing a wealth of experience to the on-going management of project funds.  Specialising in syndications and building close relationships with existing borrowers, the team provides a high-level of client service while exploring new opportunities. Contact the <a href="javascript:openWindow('contact-infrastructure.html');"><span class="bold">team</span></a> for more information.</span>
