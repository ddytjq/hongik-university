        </font><font face="Arial" size="3">Fixed Rate Mortgages</font></strong></span><p><font face="Arial,Helvetica" size="2" color="#000000"><!--Start Content--></font></p>
        <p><strong><font face="Arial" size="2">If you want to borrow £25,000 or more and want the
        certainty of a fixed monthly payment, choose a Fixed Rate Mortgage. </font></strong></p>
        <b><p><font face="Arial" size="2">Features:</font></b><ul>
          <li><font face="Arial" size="2">No deposit required </font></li>
          <li><font face="Arial" size="2">Monthly payments don't change during the term of your fixed
            rate</font></li>
        </ul>
        <b><p><font face="Arial" size="2">Plus:</font></b><ul>
          <li><font face="Arial" size="2">3 months free Mortgage Loanguard premiums</font></li>
          <li><font face="Arial" size="2">No Maximum Advance Premium if you have a 10% deposit</font></li>
          <li><font face="Arial" size="2">Choose how to repay - Capital Repayment, Endowment, Pension
            Plan or Individual Savings Account</font></li>
          <li><font face="Arial" size="2">Mortgage term can range from 3 years to retirement or age
            70, whichever is earlier</font></li>
          <li><font face="Arial" size="2">Right to Buy customers can benefit from this mortgage if
            they are borrowing £10,000 or more</font></li>
        </ul>
        <p><font face="Arial" size="1">There is an arrangement fee of £295 payable when booking a
        fixed rate. This secures the rate you have chosen for a period of 3 months. This means
        that even if we change the fixed rate or it is withdrawn from sale, we will still honour
        the rate we agreed with you. This fee is non-refundable.</font></p>
      <p><font face="Arial" size="1">Fixed rate mortgages are strictly subject
      to availability. The maximum amount for a fixed rate mortgage is normally
      £500,000, however applications in excess of this amount will be
      considered on an individual basis. We would advise that, where a fixed
      rate is chosen, funds are booked as soon as possible.</font></p>
        <p><font face="Arial" size="1">Typical example: based on a £75,000 capital repayment
        mortgage taken out over 25 years. Rates are fixed until the expiry date shown. At the end
        of the fixed rate period, our standard variable Mortgage Rate will apply which is
        currently 5.95%. If Mortgage Rate changes, your payments will also change. Early
        repayments/switching charges are expressed as a percentage of the balance redeemed. Please
        see Important Information.</font></p>
