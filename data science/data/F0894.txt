Moving a Form Without a Title Bar

<BR><HR SIZE=1 WIDTH=200>
</FONT></B></CENTER>
<P>
<!-- End Title -->

Sometimes, you may want remove the title bar of a form. But, when you do this,
you're unable to move it. So, to fix this problem, use this code. It will
move the form when you click and drag it.
<P>

'This code in the declarations section of your form:<P>
<B>Private Declare Function</B> SendMessage <B>Lib</B> "User32" _<BR>
<B>Alias</B> "SendMessageA" (ByVal hWnd As Long, _<BR>
ByVal wMsg As Long, _<BR>
ByVal wParam As Long, _<BR>
lParam As Any) <B>As Long</B><P>

<B>Private Declare Sub</B> ReleaseCapture <B>Lib</B> "User32" ()<BR>
Const WM_NCLBUTTONDOWN = &HA1<BR>
Const HTCAPTION = 2<P>

'And this at the form's MouseMove event:<P>

<B>Private Sub</B> Form_MouseMove(Button As Integer, Shift As Integer, X As Single, Y As Single)<BR>
<B>Dim</B> lngReturnValue As Long<BR>
<B>If</B> Button = 1 <B>Then</B><BR>
<B>Call</B> ReleaseCapture<BR>
lngReturnValue = SendMessage(Form1.hWnd, WM_NCLBUTTONDOWN, _<BR>
HTCAPTION, 0&)<BR>
<B>End If</B><BR>
<B>End Sub</B><P>

<BR>Done! Now run the project, drag the form and see what happens!
