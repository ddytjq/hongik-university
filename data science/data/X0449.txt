<B>Summer Flounder (Fluke)</B> <BR ALIGN=Center>
<I>[Paralichthys dentatus]</I>
<P ALIGN=Left>
&nbsp; &nbsp; The Fluke is very common in the Long Island area and very relaxing
to go after. This is a left-eye flatfish that can reach well over 10 lbs.
Fluke are a very agressive species. They feed like mad on small fish, squid
and worms. Fluke are around all summer long with the larger ones usually
being caught toward the end of summer. They bite on a variety of rigs. Some
working better than others.
