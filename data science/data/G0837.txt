<h1>The Extreme Basics of Spacetime Diagrams</h1>
I admit that spacetime diagrams are tricky things.  It took me a while to
get used to them and to be able to read them accurately at first try.  I
realize that this pseudo-tutorial I'm giving you is less than adequate for
someone who has never before seen a spacetime diagram.  I apologize for
this.  As it is, if you are utterly baffled, you can always <a
href="gothos9@juno.com">email me (gothos9@juno.com)</a>,
and I'll try to clear things up for you. That's a standing offer for
anything on these pages for as long as these pages are up and running! Oh
yes, I try to explain some things on these pages with the help of my
valiant cockatiel <a
href="http://www.dragonweave.com/gothos/html/lupin.html">Lupin</a>.
He's a bird. That's him on the right.
