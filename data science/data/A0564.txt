		  <p class="contentsubhead">Tailoring Your Home Loan</p>
		  <p>The key to Tailoring your Home Loan and it’s Repayment Schedule is <strong>choice</strong>, we explain the options and help you make an informed choice.</p>
      <p> We offer you <strong>flexibility</strong> and we give you <strong>contol</strong>. If you 
        take control and Tailor your repayment schedule you will <strong>save money</strong> 
        on interest costs and could <strong>save time</strong> by repaying your Home Loan 
        early.</p> 
      <p> You also have the <strong>peace of mind</strong> that comes from knowing that 
        <strong>you</strong> are in <strong>control of your home loan</strong> and that we have the 
        <strong>flexibility</strong> to cope with your ever changing needs. </p>
      <p> <strong>Ten Questions you Should Ask your Home Loan Provider</strong></p>
      <p> 1. Can I save thousands of pounds in interest charges on my Home Loan?</p>
      <p> 2. Can I repay my Home Loan years early, if I choose to do so?</p>
      <p> 3. Can I make repayments weekly or fortnightly, as well as monthly?</p>
      <p> 4. Can I make lump sum repayments when I can afford to do so, without 
        penalty?</p>
      <p> 5. Can I vary my repayments if my financial circumstances change?</p>
      <p> 6. Is the interest on my Home Loan calculated daily to minimise the 
        overall cost?</p>
      <p> 7. Will I be charged interest in arrears, rather than in advance, so 
        that my Home Loan reduces more quickly?</p>
      <p> 8. Will you contact me on a regular basis to review my Home Loan?</p>
      <p> 9. Do you have the flexibility to cope with my changing circumstances 
        during the term of my Home Loan?</p>
      <p> 10. Who is in control of my Home Loan?</p>
      <p> <em>With Northern the answer is always <strong>yes</strong> and you the Customer 
        are in control.</em></p>
