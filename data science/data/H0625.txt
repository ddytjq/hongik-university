<h3 align="left">Ordering Information</h3><p>

SFI does not have books available for sale. All books in the SFI series, except those no longer in print, are now 
available through Perseus Books Group. Click on a book's ISBN number in the following list for ordering information 
through the Perseus Books Group web site or visit their main <a href="http://www.perseusbooksgroup.com/front.html">
search page</a> to enter your own query.<p>


If you do not want to purchase books over the web, please contact your local bookstore or, call Perseus Books 
1-800-386-5656 in the U.S. or fax your order to 1-303-449-3356; or write to: Perseus Books Group Customer Service 
Department, 5500 Central Avenue, Boulder, CO 80301. Customer service representatives are available Monday through
Friday, 8:00 a.m.-5:00 p.m. MST to take your calls. Please have the authors, titles, and if possible the
ISBNs of the titles you are ordering when you call. Books are sold for cover price plus shipping. <p>
