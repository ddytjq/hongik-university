Itera X Commonwealth Champions Cup [Jan 19-27, 2002, Moscow] 

Because of the organizators' fear that a participant from Ukraine (Dynamo Kyiv) 
that was relegated to the First League last season may refuse to play at the 
second level of the Cup, the tournament reverted to the old format with four equal 
qualification groups and no leagues. 
The winners will get $35,000 and the finalists $15,000. 
Matches will be played in Olympic Sport Complex and Spartak Sport Complex.
