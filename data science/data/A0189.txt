		<p>
		<a name="flexibility"><span class="topnav">Flexibility</span></a> <span class="body">- the number of individual accounts that can be accommodated by the system is almost unlimited. Up to 5 different deposit and 4 debit interest rate bands can be allocated to those individual accounts, with direct access to funds on deposit allowed.</span>
<p>
<span class="body">Individual accounts can be opened by the corporate customer either on a designated account or in the client's own name, with a third party mandate giving the corporate customer full authority to operate the account.</span>
<p>
<span class="body">The accounts are operated within the virtual branch as interest-bearing current accounts with the deposit interest rate being set by the corporate customer in agreement with the Bank. The corporate customer can earn an interest turn based on the aggregate deposit balance within their virtual branch.</span>
<p>
<span class="body">The Bank handles all accounting and reporting to the Inland Revenue with appropriate certificates for the tax year provided for each account.</span>
<p>
		<a name="security"><span class="topnav">Security</span></a> <span class="body">- as expected from a major UK Bank, the Cash Management Service gives both corporate customers and individual clients peace of mind. Varying levels of access are available with a secure combination of identification numbers and security passwords to ensure system access only at the authorised level.</span>
<p>
		<a name="partnership"><span class="topnav">Partnership</span></a> <span class="body">- Bank of Scotland makes an ideal partner, with a reputation for technological innovation. This is reflected in advanced systems with a high level of support and service which are continually reviewed and upgraded. Ownership of the individual client remains with the corporate customer at all times and the Bank undertakes not to cross-sell products without prior consent from the corporate customer.</span>
<p>
		<a name="support"><span class="topnav">Support</span></a> <span class="body">- each corporate customer is allocated a dedicated Project Manager, providing support and access to the services necessary for the successful implementation of the Cash Management Service. Specialist administration and systems teams provide on-going support and training.</span>
<p>
<span class="body">Following the implementation, a Relationship Manager will be assigned to each corporate customer and will act as a personal contact going forward. In addition to dealing with issues relating to the Cash Management Service, the Relationship Manager will be available to assist with specific marketing initiatives and with the delivery of additional deposit and lending-based products if required.</span>
