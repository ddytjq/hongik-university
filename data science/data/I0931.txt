 Engine Ver 0.31</H4><H1 class="entry">York City</H1><H2 class="entry">Bootham Crescent</H2><table class="entry" cellspacing=0 width="100%"><tr><td><H3 class="entry">The Ground</H3><table class="entry" cellspacing=0><TR><TD class="entry"><P><TABLE><TR><TD ALIGN="CENTER"><A HREF="/cgi-bin/ag.pl?club=disppic&pfname=/images/York-Ground1-Full.jpg" TARGET="_new"><IMG SRC="/images/York-Ground1-Thumb.jpg" ALT="Away Turnstiles" WIDTH="100" HEIGHT="75" BORDER="0" ALIGN="TEXTTOP"></A></TD></TR><TR><TD ALIGN="CENTER" class="entry"><B>Away Turnstiles</B></TD></TR><TR><TD ALIGN="CENTER" class="entry">Click for bigger picture</TD></TR></TABLE></P></TD><TD class="entry"><P><TABLE><TR><TD ALIGN="CENTER"><A HREF="/cgi-bin/ag.pl?club=disppic&pfname=/images/York-Ground2-Full.jpg" TARGET="_new"><IMG SRC="/images/York-Ground2-Thumb.jpg" ALT="Main Entrance" WIDTH="100" HEIGHT="75" BORDER="0" ALIGN="TEXTTOP"></A></TD></TR><TR><TD ALIGN="CENTER" class="entry"><B>Main Entrance</B></TD></TR><TR><TD ALIGN="CENTER" class="entry">Click for bigger picture</TD></TR></TABLE></P></TD><TD class="entry"><P><TABLE><TR><TD ALIGN="CENTER"><A HREF="/cgi-bin/ag.pl?club=disppic&pfname=/images/York-Ground3-Full.jpg" TARGET="_new"><IMG SRC="/images/York-Ground3-Thumb.jpg" ALT="Side of Ground 
" WIDTH="100" HEIGHT="75" BORDER="0" ALIGN="TEXTTOP"></A></TD></TR><TR><TD ALIGN="CENTER" class="entry"><B>Side of Ground 
</B></TD></TR><TR><TD ALIGN="CENTER" class="entry">Click for bigger picture</TD></TR></TABLE></P></TD><TD></TD></TR></TABLE><p><P>The ground is hidden away among terraced housing and not the easiest to
see. Away fans are allocated the uncovered Grosvenor Road Terrace which
has food available. The entrance is on Grosvenor Road (not surprisingly)
which is just off Burton Stone Street. Visiting fans are usually also
allocated some seats in The Popular Stand. Flags, banners and drums are
usually allowed into the ground.
</P><P>Visiting families are welcome to use the family stand if they prefer.
</P></P><p>Click <a class="entry" href="http://uk.multimap.com/p/browse.cgi?pc=YO307AQ&Title=Bootham+Crescent+(York+City)&scale=25000&cat=loc" target="_new">here</a> to see a map of the surrounding area</P></td></tr></table><BR><table class="entry" cellspacing=0 width="100%"><tr><td><H3 class="entry">Disabled</H3><p>The club has some spaces for wheelchair users. For more information or to
book contact the club on 01904 624447.
</P></p></td></tr></table><BR><table class="entry" cellspacing=0 width=100%><tr><td><H3 class="entry">Pubs</H3><p><P>There are two pubs within 5 minutes walk of the ground, the Burton Stone
and the Corner house, both on Burton Stone Street. A better option for
families is The Old Grey Mare on the A19. The best pub for food is
The Exhibition Hotel in Bootham which serves top class food between
12-2.30 and from 5pm.
</P></p><TABLE><TR></TR></TABLE><H5 class="entry">Burton Stone 
</H5><P ALIGN=LEFT></P><TABLE><TR><TD><P><TABLE><TR><TD ALIGN="CENTER"><A HREF="/cgi-bin/ag.pl?club=disppic&pfname=/images/York-Burton-Full.jpg" TARGET="_new"><IMG SRC="/images/York-Burton-Thumb.jpg" ALT="The Burton Stone
" WIDTH="100" HEIGHT="75" BORDER="0" ALIGN="TEXTTOP"></A></TD></TR><TR><TD ALIGN="CENTER"><B>The Burton Stone
<B></TD></TR><TR><TD ALIGN="CENTER">Click for bigger picture</TD></TR></TABLE></P></TD></TR></TABLE><p>The Burton Stone pub is located on the junction of the A19 and Burton
Stone Street. Both home and away fans mingle in this pub which is about
5 minutes walk from the ground. There is a beer garden and pool table but
no food available. There is also a very small car park. 
</P></p><H5 class="entry">Corner House
</H5><P ALIGN=LEFT></P><TABLE><TR><TD><P><TABLE><TR><TD ALIGN="CENTER"><A HREF="/cgi-bin/ag.pl?club=disppic&pfname=/images/York-Corner-Full.jpg" TARGET="_new"><IMG SRC="/images/York-Corner-Thumb.jpg" ALT="The Corner House
" WIDTH="100" HEIGHT="75" BORDER="0" ALIGN="TEXTTOP"></A></TD></TR><TR><TD ALIGN="CENTER"><B>The Corner House
<B></TD></TR><TR><TD ALIGN="CENTER">Click for bigger picture</TD></TR></TABLE></P></TD></TR></TABLE><p>The Corner House is situated at the opposite end of Burton Stone Street
to the Burton Stone pub, again about 5 minutes away from the ground. Both
home and away fans are welcome. It is a Mansfield Pub and there is a pool
table. 
</P></p><H5 class="entry">The Old Grey Mare
</H5><P ALIGN=LEFT></P><TABLE><TR></TR></TABLE><p>The Old Grey Mare is on the A19 towards the ground, about 10 minutes walk
away. This is possibly a better option for families than the two pubs
near to the ground. The pub has it's own car park and also a beer garden.
Food is served all day and families are welcome. The Old Grey Mare also
offers accommodation. 
</P></p></td></tr><table><BR><table class="entry" cellspacing=0 width=100%><tr><td><H3 class="entry">Food</H3><p><P>There are two food options next to the Cornerhouse pub, the Hong Kong
House Chinese and the Star of Bengal Balti restaurant/cafe. There is also
an Off licence/general store which does sandwiches/snacks.
</P><P>If you want more choice head towards the city centre which is 15
minutes away where you will find Pizza Hut, McDonalds and Burger King.
</P></p></td></tr><table><BR><table class="entry" cellspacing=0 width=100%><tr><td><H3 class="entry">Cash Machines</H3><p><P>The nearest cash machine (Lloyds TSB) is in the city centre in St Helens
Square off Coney street.
</P></p><p>Click <a class="entry" href="http://link.infonow.net/bin/findNow?CLIENT_ID=LINK_ATM" target="_new">here</A> to go to <a class="entry" HREF="http://www.link.co.uk"  TARGET="_NEW">LINKs</a> locate a cash machine page. The local postcode is YO30 7AQ</P></td></tr><table><BR><table class="entry" cellspacing=0 width=100%><tr><td><H3 class="entry">Bookmakers</H3><p>There is a William Hill bookmakers on Burton Stone Street in the row of
shops next to the Cornerhouse Pub.
</P></p><p>Online betting is always available through <a class="entry" 
href="https://www.ladbrokes.co.uk/bet?AFF_ID=121"  TARGET="_NEW">Ladbrokes</a> by
clicking <a class="entry" href="https://www.ladbrokes.co.uk/bet?AFF_ID=121"  TARGET="_NEW">here</a>.</P></td></tr><table><BR><table class="entry" cellspacing=0 width=100%><tr><td><H3 class="entry">Directions</H3><p>If coming from the North approach via the A19. At the roundabout junction
with the A1237 carry straight on on the A19 (Shipton Road). After nearly 2
miles turn left by the Burton Stone pub onto Burton Stone Lane and the
ground will be on your right.
</P><P>If approaching from the south head for the A64. Exit the A64 onto the
A1237 which runs past the West of York towards Harrogate. Follow this
road, going straight on over 3 roundabouts, for just over 5 miles. At the
fourth roundabout turn right onto the A19 (Shipton Road).After nearly 2
miles turn left by the Burton Stone pub onto Burton Stone Lane and the
ground will be on your right.
</P></p></td></tr><table><BR><table class="entry" cellspacing=0 width=100%><tr><td><H3 class="entry">Parking</H3><p>Parking around the ground is severely limited as there is no car park at 
the ground and the majority of streets around the ground have residents
parking schemes in operation. There is limited street parking near to the
ground and the further from the ground you travel the more parking becomes
available, so expect a walk unless getting there very early.
</P><P>There are several Park and Ride Schemes which have been set up on the
outskirts of York which you may prefer to use.
</P></p></td></tr><table><BR><table class="entry" cellspacing=0 width=100%><tr><td><H3 class="entry">Train</H3><p>The train station is a 15 minutes walk from the ground. Upon exiting the
station turn left onto Station Road and take the first left (sign-posted
Leeman Road Industrial Estate, National Railway Museum). At the end of the
road follow it round to the left, passing the Royal Mail Depot. Just
before the Railway Bridge turn right along the path and go over the
river. Follow this path between the river and the car park and at
the end of the car park turn left through the underpass and then right
into Bootham Terrace. At the main road turn left and then take the second
right into Bootham Cresent, which leads to the ground.
</P></p><p>The nearest station is York
. Click <a class="entry" href="http://212.87.65.227/bin/newquery.exe/en" target="_new">here</a> to check train times</p></td></tr><table><BR><table class="entry" cellspacing=0 width=100%><tr><td><H3 class="entry">Accommodation</H3><p>There are plenty of guest houses and small hotels in the area around the
ground and lots more choice in the town centre. 
</P></p><p>Click 
<a class="entry" href="http://www.smoothhound.co.uk/york.html"  TARGET="_NEW">
here</a> to see the local accomodation available from 
<a class="entry" href="http://www.smoothhound.co.uk"  TARGET="_NEW">  Smoothhound</a>.</p></td></tr><table><BR><table class="entry" cellspacing=0 width=100%><tr><td><H3 class="entry">Local Fans</H3><p>The fans are a friendly bunch and any trouble is very rare.
</P></p></td></tr><table><BR><table class="entry" cellspacing=0 width=100%><tr><td><H3 class="entry">Local Police And Stewards</H3><p><P>The stewards are generally easy going.
</P></p></td></tr><table><BR><table class="entry" cellspacing=0 width=100%><tr><td><H3 class="entry">After The Game</H3><p>There is plenty of choice in the city centre for a good night out.
</P></p></td></tr><table><BR><table class="entry" cellspacing=0 width=100%><tr><td><H3 class="entry">Local Attractions</H3><p>There are plenty of attractions in York. These include the National Train
Museum, the York Minster, York Dungeon and the Yorvik Centre. York is also
a good shopping centre and the famous 'Shambles'. For further information
check out <a class="entry" href="http://www.york-tourism.co.uk">York's tourism site</a>  
</P></p><P>You can also use the following to search for events in the area</p><BR><CENTER>
