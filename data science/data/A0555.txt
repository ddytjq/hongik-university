		<P>
		Bank of Scotland offers a range of payment services allowing customers to transfer funds to third parties electronically, both in the UK and worldwide, to settle suppliers invoices, make commission payments, pay wages, salaries and expenses, and undertake other transactions.
<P>
<span class= "topnav">Outward Payments</span>


		<TABLE BORDER=0>
		<TR>

		<TR>
			
			<TD CLASS=body colspan=2>BACS<BR> Bankers' Automated Clearing Service - payments are primarily used to pay suppliers and employees. There are a number of ways of accessing BACS payments:    </td></TD>
		</TR>

		<TR>
			<TD colspan=2 class=body><img src="img/clear.gif" WIDTH=1 HEIGHT=2 BORDER=0></td>

		<TR>
			<TD valign=top><IMG SRC="img/weered.gif" WIDTH=4 HEIGHT=4 BORDER=0 VSPACE=4></TD>
			<TD CLASS=body>via our Business HOBS&#174; and Corporate HOBS&#174; PC banking services </TD>
		</TR>
		<TR>
			<TD colspan=2 class=body><img src="img/clear.gif" WIDTH=1 HEIGHT=10 BORDER=0></td>
		</tr>
		<TR>
			<TD  valign=top><IMG SRC="img/weered.gif" WIDTH=4 HEIGHT=4 BORDER=0 VSPACE=4></TD>
			<TD CLASS=body>by direct access via a system called BACSTEL using a modem and appropriate BACS software </td></TD>
		</TR>
		<TR>
			<TD colspan=2 class=body><img src="img/clear.gif" WIDTH=1 HEIGHT=10 BORDER=0></td>
		</tr>
		<TR>
			
			<TD CLASS=body colspan=2>CHAPS<BR>Clearing House Automated Payment Services - provide same-day (2 hour turn-round) payments to bank accounts within the UK. This facility is generally used when it is essential that funds are lodged with a third party within a very short timescale. CHAPS payments can be instructed manually on a paper request to customers' account holding branch or office, or electronically via our Corporate HOBS service.  </td></TD>
		</TR>

		<TR>
			<TD colspan=2 class=body><img src="img/clear.gif" WIDTH=1 HEIGHT=10 BORDER=0></td>
		</tr>
		<TR>
			
			<TD CLASS=body colspan=2>International<BR>Bank of Scotland offers a variety of means for effecting payments around the world in most currencies. These can be instructed manually on a paper International Payment (or Draft) application form to customers' account holding branch or office, or electronically through our Corporate HOBS service. </td></TD>
		</TR>

		<TR>
			<TD colspan=2 class=body><img src="img/clear.gif" WIDTH=1 HEIGHT=10 BORDER=0></td>
		</tr>
		<TR>
			
			<TD CLASS=body colspan=2>PC Banking with Business HOBS and Corporate HOBS<BR>Bank of Scotland offers two electronic banking services for your office PC. Both services include a range of facilities, such as account information and statements on all Bank of Scotland sterling, foreign currency and credit card accounts, transfer of funds between accounts, and a range of third party payment types. Business HOBS and Corporate HOBS can also be integrated with other accounting software through their data export and instruction import facilities.  </td></TD>
		</TR>

			<TD CLASS=body colspan=2>Direct Debit<BR>Customers who wish to utilise an automated method of collecting funds from third party accounts may be able to use Bank of Scotland's Direct Debit facility. There are around 9,000 organisations using BACS (the Bankers' Automated Clearing Service) to collect a variety of regular bills including utility payments, insurance, mortgage and loan repayments, subscriptions etc. </td></TD>
		</TR>

These services allow customers to closely monitor receipt of BACS or CHAPS payments being lodged directly to their accounts, simply by connecting electronically to the Bank.</td></TD>
