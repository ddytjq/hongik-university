<td width=670>

Images of 31 galaxies were taken with the 1.5 meter telescope of the
<a href="http://astro.caltech.edu/observatories/palomar/">Palomar
Observatory</a> in 1991; images of the other 82 galaxies were taken
with the 1.1 meter telescope of the <a
href="http://www.lowell.edu/">Lowell Observatory</a> in 1989.  At
Palomar we used a camera with an 800x800 TI CCD, at Lowell the
camera had an RCA 512x320 CCD. Palomar images are available in three
passbands of the Thuan-Gunn system: <i>g</i>, <i>r</i>, and
<i>i</i>. Lowell images are in two passbands (<i>J</i> and <i>R</i>) of
the filter system developed by Gullixon et al.

<p>

The first link below is to a detailed <a
href="Gcat_htm/cat_ug.htm">guide</a> to the catalog.  Please read this
guide!  You will learn about the graphics <a
href="Gcat_htm/cat_ug_2.htm#formats">file formats</a> we used, about
freely available <a href="Gcat_htm/cat_ug_2.htm#viewers">viewing
software</a> for many platforms, and about <a
href="Gcat_htm/cat_ug_2.htm#monitor">adjusting your monitor</a> for
optimal performance. A detailed description of the <a
href="Gcat_htm/cat_ug_3.htm#observations">observations</a> and
subsequent <a href="Gcat_htm/cat_ug_3.htm#processing">image
processing</a> steps is also included. Another section describes the
algorithm we used to create the <a
href="Gcat_htm/cat_ug_4.htm">color-composite images</a>.

<p>

The second link is to a <a href="Gcat_htm/cat_ajp.htm">scientific
paper</a> that reports about this catalog.  This paper appeared in the
Astronomical Journal in January, 1996.  HTML, PDF, and PostScript
versions are available here. The third link is to a collection of
small, thumbnail-size <a href="Gcat_htm/cat_ims.htm">preview
images</a> of all 113 galaxies in the catalog. The last link is to a
<a href="Gcat_htm/cat_ngc.htm">text-only list</a> of the New Galaxy
Catalog (NGC) numbers of all the galaxies. You can use the
thumbnail-size images or the NGC numbers to select any of the galaxies
you want to view.
