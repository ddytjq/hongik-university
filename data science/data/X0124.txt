function FWFindImage(doc, name, j) {
    var theImage = false;
    if (doc.images) {
        theImage = doc.images[name];
    }
    if (theImage) {
        return theImage;
    }
    if (doc.layers) {
        for (j = 0; j < doc.layers.length; j++) {
            theImage = FWFindImage(doc.layers[j].document, name, 0);
            if (theImage) {
                return (theImage);
            }
        }
    }
    return (false);
}

          <p><span class="headsub">The Organization</span><br>
            World Runners is an international organization of runners with headquarters 
            in Santa Rosa, California. World Runners members organize and participate 
            in races in the U.S. and internationally to be messengers for a better 
            world and to raise funding for projects related to the advancement 
            of health, prosperity, and peace in local communities and in the communities 
            of the world. </p>
          <p><img border="0" src="../images/img20_copy.jpg" hspace="5" vspace="5" width="286" height="165"></p>
          <p><span class="headsub">Major Milestones</span><br>
            During the past 20 years, World Runners has helped people, foremost 
            children, to exist and live.</p>
          <p><font color="#353500">Major World Runners milestones include the 
            Moscow Peace Marathons through the 1980's. 1,000 runners raised $2million 
            for organizations combating world hunger, and the message of a joint 
            U.S.-Soviet partnership for a better world reached hundreds of millions 
            of people worldwide.</font></p>
          <p><font color="#353500">The Save the Children Relays involved 25,000 
            participants who raised over $1 million for child health programs 
            in the U.S. and internationally. Over 100,000 children were immunized 
            against fatal childhood diseases. Two transcontinental relays carried 
            the child survival messages throughout the U.S. and 10 other nations, 
            gaining proclamations from scores of national, state and local governments.</font></p>
          <p><font color="#353500">Other major fundraising campaigns raised over 
            $4 million through teams running for pledges at the New York, Boston, 
            and other major marathons, as well as hundreds of other large and 
            small events. World Runners peaked at 13,000 members in 50 countries.</font></p>
          <p><font color="#353500">At the end of this millennium, World Runners 
            is determined to build a glorious past and recapture this spirit for 
            advancing the quality of life in our communities and in the world.</font></p>
