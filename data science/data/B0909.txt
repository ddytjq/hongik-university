class="bodytext">						<p class="bodytext"><span class="bodytext">The Banking Code is a voluntary code to which Banks and Building Societies, including Tipton &amp; Coseley, subscribe. It sets the standards of good banking practice. As a voluntary Code, it allows competition and market forces to operate to encourage higher standards for the benefit of the customers.</span></p>						<p class="bodytext"><span class="bodytext">By subscribing to the Code, Tipton &amp; Coseley, promises to uphold the 11 key commitments outlined below. These commitments apply to the conduct of business for all products and services provided to customers.</span></p>						<ul class="bodytext">							<li class="bodytext"><span class="bodytext">act fairly and reasonably in all our dealings with you</span>							<li class="bodytext"><span class="bodytext">ensure that all services and products comply with this Code, even if they have their own terms and conditions</span>							<li class="bodytext"><span class="bodytext">give you information on our services and products in plain language, and offer help if there is any aspect which you do not understand.</span>							<li class="bodytext"><span class="bodytext">help you choose a service or product to fit your needs</span>							<li class="bodytext"><span class="bodytext">help you to understand the financial implications of:<br>									- a mortgage<br>									- other borrowing<br>									- savings and investment products<br>									- card products</span>							<li class="bodytext"><span class="bodytext">help you to understand how your accounts work</span>							<li class="bodytext"><span class="bodytext">have safe, secure and reliable banking and payment systems</span>							<li class="bodytext"><span class="bodytext">ensure that the procedures our staff follow reflect the commitments set out in this Code</span>							<li class="bodytext"><span class="bodytext">correct errors and handle complaints speedily</span>							<li class="bodytext"><span class="bodytext">consider cases of financial difficulty and mortgage arrears sympathetically and positively</span>							<li class="bodytext"><span class="bodytext">ensure that all services and products comply with relevant laws and regulations</span>						</ul>					</div>					<p class="bodytext"></p>					<p class="bodytext">Copies of the Banking Code are available from any of our branches or visit the <a href="http://www.bba.org.uk/" target="_blank">British Bankers&#146;
