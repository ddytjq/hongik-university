<font face="verdana,arial,helvetica" size="2" class="standardfont">
Motorsport Forums are part of a large network of forums called Motorsport Forums. Many motorsport related sites use the forums but users from each site are presented with a unique interface meaning the forums look just like they are part of the site in question.
<p>
You can use these forums on your site by joining our partner program.
<p>
There are few things more addictive than an easy to use and popular discussion forum, by using the forums on your site you will find that many users come back every day, or even several times a day to check their topics on the forums, as they will come through your main site this means more visitors for your site and once people have visited your site for the first time it ensures that they keep coming back again and again. 
<p>
The problem is that running a discussion forum is not a simple job, and building discussion up to 'critical mass' is not easy or quick. As we have already aquired the lastest software and take care of the web hosting and moderating the forums all the work you need do it provide a link on your website and best of all there is no charge.
<p>
You need not have any technical knowledge about the workings of forums and once the forums are on your site you need not have to spend time looking after them, thus leaving you to focus your efforts on creating content for your site. However if you feel you could spare enough time to be a moderator then we would also like to hear from you. 
<p>
We are the only discussion fourm system to implement fully transparent co-branding. A user visiting through your site will only see your site interface, and all email notifications etc will only carry your site name. Even the link back to this information which normally apppears at the bottom of every page can easily be removed.
<p>
You need not worry that your site may not generate enough traffic to justify a forums system, as our network of partner sites bring visitors to the forums, whenever someone posts on the forums of one of our partner sites the same post will instantly appear on your site. In addition to this your visitors need not know that you are using anything else but your own forums system as we will set up your account so that the forums you use will look just like your site, there are no compulsory links back to us. Plus you are free to link directly to a specific forum or category, or even exclude a range of forums if you do not wish your users to view the full list. 
<p>
Even if you already have a discussion forum we can help you. By moving your currrent threads across to this forum (this work will be done by us) then you can let your users carry on their same coversations but with a far larger audience.
<p>
Fill in the form below to apply to join or to ask any questions you wish. Answers to some questions can be found on our <a href="/forums/partnerfaq.php">Frequently Asked Questions</a> page.
