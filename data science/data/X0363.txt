<P>Sudbury area resident <B>Roger Soucy</B> will be having a lot of fun both on the road and on the water this summer after winning a prize valued at <B>over $92,000</B> in the 26th Annual O.F.A.H./Conservation Lottery. </P>

<P></P>
<P><B>Robert Wagg</B>, of Plainfield, Ontario, won second prize: a 2000 Jeep Cherokee Sport, towing a Vanguard II Argo 6X6 all-terrain vehicle on a Northtrail trailer -- a package worth <B>over $51,000</B>. </P>
<P></P>
<P>Third prize winner <B>Maurice Decaire</B>, of Comber, Ontario, will take home a 2000 Dodge Dakota Truck- Quad Cab worth <B>over $34,000</B>. </P>
<P>In all, <B>over $249,000</B> in prize packages were awarded to <B>100</B> lucky ticket holders across Ontario. </P>

<P>Lottery funds have contributed to many O.F.A.H. programs over the past 26 years, including the battle against invading species, working for better wildlife and forestry management, educational programs, reintroducing lost wildlife species, and habitat enhancement projects. </P>
