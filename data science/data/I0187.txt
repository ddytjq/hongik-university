          <p><font size="+2" face="Times New Roman, Times, serif">I</font><font face="Times New Roman, Times, serif">n 
            this archive you can find all matches ever played in five main European 
            Cups. Because there are 11922 such matches (with 34777 goals scored, 
            special thanks to Miguel Revilla for corrections), you are able to 
            make queries about some period, cup, round, country, particular club 
            or all that together.<br>
            <BR>
            You can use transcribed (transliterated) or original (accented) 
            names. Everything will be accepted!<BR><BR>
            For particular <b><i>period</i></b> enter two last digits of beginning 
            year (between 55 (stands for season 1955/56) and 01 (2001/2002)).<br>
            For particular <i><b>cup</b></i>, <b><i>round</i></b> and <b><i>country</i></B> 
            you have menu.<br>
            <i><b>Club</b></i> is represented with its name, but you can enter 
            only <b>few significant letters</b> if they aren't often.<br>
            Result is sorted by season, then by cup and then by the round. If 
            you make difference between "first" and "second" side, at the end 
            of result you'll be able to see statistics for the "first" side (matches 
            played, won, draws, lost and goal difference).<br>
            <i><b>NOTE</b></i>: You don't have to fill all fields!</font></p>
