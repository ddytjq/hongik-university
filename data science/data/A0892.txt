        <p><font face="Arial" size="2"><strong>If you want to borrow £25,000* or more and would
        like reduced monthly repayments at the start of your mortgage, choose a Discounted
        Variable Rate Mortgage. </strong></font></p>
        <b><p><font face="Arial" size="2">Features:</font></b><ul>
          <li><font face="Arial" size="2">Discounted rates mean lower payments in the early years</font></li>
          <li><font face="Arial" size="2">Choice of discounted term </font></li>
          <li><font face="Arial" size="2">No deposit required</font></li>
        </ul>
        <b><p><font face="Arial" size="2">Plus:</font></b><ul>
          <li><font face="Arial" size="2">3 months free Mortgage Loanguard premiums</font></li>
          <li><font face="Arial" size="2">No Maximum Advance Premium if you have a 10% deposit</font></li>
          <li><font face="Arial" size="2">Choose how to repay - Capital Repayment, Endowment, Pension
            Plan or Individual Savings Account</font></li>
          <li><font face="Arial" size="2">Mortgage term can range from 3 years to retirement or age
            70, whichever is earlier</font></li>
          <li><font face="Arial" size="2">Right to Buy customers can benefit from this mortgage if
            they are borrowing £10,000 or more</font></li>
        </ul>
        <p><font face="Arial" size="1">*Minimum loan £25,000 for over 95% 1, 2 &amp; 3 Year
        Discount&nbsp; and £35,000 for Standard 1, 2 &amp; 3 Year Discounts.</font></p>
        <p><font face="Arial" size="1">Typical Examples based on a £75,000 mortgage taken over 25
        years; purchase price £100,000 in the case of up to 95% mortgages, £75,000 for 95% or 
        above.
        At the end of the discounted period the Bank's variable mortgage rate will apply. Mortgage
        rate is currently 5.95%. If Mortgage Rate changes, your repayments will also change. </font></p>
