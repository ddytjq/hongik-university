<p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2"><B>Mortgagecare</B></font></p>
                                <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">Cover
                                  is available to new and existing mortgage-holders,
                                  aged over 18 and under 60 who are in work and
                                  have been so for the previous six months. It
                                  cannot be provided to customers whose mortgage
                                  payments are in arrears. As with all insurance
                                  schemes there are some limitations but these
                                  have been kept to a minimum. All insured customers
                                  will receive a copy of the policy which contains
                                  full policy details and details of the complaints
                                  procedures. </font></p>
                                <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">Mortgagecare
                                  is arranged by TMB acting as an agent for the
                                  Insurer, St Andrew's Insurance plc (Registered
                                  No: 3104671) (England) (&quot;the Company&quot;),
                                  St Andrew's House, Portsmouth Road, Esher, Surrey,
                                  KT10 9SA. The Company will choose English law
                                  as the law which applies to the insurance (unless
                                  you ask for another law and it agrees to this
                                  before the commencement date). You can at any
                                  time ask for detailed information about its
                                  compensation arrangements if it goes into liquidation
                                  and cannot pay its debts.</font></p>
                                <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">TMB
                                  and St Andrew's Insurance plc are connected
                                  companies as both are Bank of Scotland Group
                                  Companies. Telephone calls to St Andrew's may
                                  be monitored or recorded to assist with staff
                                  training and for quality control purposes.</font></p>
                                  <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2"><B>Mortgages</B></font></p><p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">
                                  TMB subscribes to the Mortgage Code; copies
                                  are available on request. </font></p>
                                <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2"><br>
                                  Telephone calls may be recorded for security
                                  purposes and monitored under our quality control
                                  procedures.</font></p>
                                <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">You
                                  may contact us using Type Talk. </font></p>
                                <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">Information
                                  is available in large print, audio and Braille
                                  on request; please call for details</font></p>
                                <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2"><b><font size="3">YOUR
                                  HOME IS AT RISK IF YOU DO NOT KEEP UP REPAYMENTS
                                  ON A MORTGAGE OR OTHER LOAN SECURED ON IT. </font></b></font></p>
                                <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">Mortgage
                                  facilities are provided subject to status by
                                  The Mortgage Business plc, Thistle House, City
                                  Road, Chester, CH88 3AN. Written quotations
                                  are available on request. To apply, you must
                                  be at least 20 years of age at the application
                                  date. Applicants must have been resident in
                                  the UK (excluding the Channel Islands and the
                                  Isle of Man) for at least 2 years unless a member
                                  of HM forces serving abroad. <b>It is strongly
                                  recommended that adequate life assurance cover
                                  is obtained.</b> Loans must be secured on a
                                  first mortgage on a residential property in
                                  England, Wales, Northern Ireland or mainland
                                  Scotland. A Higher Percentage Advance fee may
                                  be applicable. </font></p>
                                <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">For
                                  example, a &pound;50,000 mortgage over 25 years
                                  taken out in October 2001 would initially
                                  require 16 interest only monthly payments of
                                  &pound;228.75, calculated at the discounted
                                  rate of 5.49%. From the end of the discounted
                                  rate period, the standard rate would prevail
                                  for the remaining term of the mortgage. At the
                                  current standard rate of 6.49%, the remaining
                                  interest only monthly payments would be 284
                                  of &pound;270.42, followed by one capital repayment
                                  of &pound;50,000. The total amount payable would
                                  be &pound;131,173 and the overall <b>APR</b>
                                  for the agreement would be <b>6.6</b>.</font></p>
                                <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">The
                                  total amount payable includes &pound;190 valuation
                                  fee and &pound;225 legal fee payable in advance,
                                  and &pound;299 completion fee, payable with
                                  the final mortgage repayment. Redemption of
                                  the mortgage in the first year will be 3 months
                                  gross interest, in the second year 2 months
                                  gross interest and one months interest in the
                                  third year, plus one months notice or interest
                                  in lieu of notice thereafter.</font></p>
                                <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">&middot;
                                  Rates are correct as at 10/10/01</font></p>

                                  TMB will disclose details about you and your

                                  to the provisions of the Data Protection Act.
                                  If you do not want these details to be used
                                  for marketing, please inform TMB in writing.</font></p>
