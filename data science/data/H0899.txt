					<h3>1 - 10 of 11</h3><p><strong><a href="http://biosafety.ihe.be/">Belgian Biosafety Server</a></strong><br />The Belgian Biosafety Server informs about the different European regulations of the release and marketing of GMO's.</p>
<p><strong><a href="http://biosafety.ihe.be/Menu/BiosEur.html">Biosafety in Europe</a></strong><br />The Belgian Biosafety Server offers a lot of information about the main specific EU legislation and European framework of Biotech Institutions.</p>
<p><strong><a href="http://www2.ari.net/foley/">Biotech Law Web Server</a></strong><br />The Biotech Law Web Server offers legal and scientific information of interest to researchers in biotechnology/pharmaceutical science.</p>
<p><strong><a href="http://www.oecd.org/ehs/country.htm">BioTrade Online</a></strong><br />BioTrade informs about the regulatory developments of biotechnology in OECD member countries and indicates the responsible ministry/agency for each member.</p>
<p><strong><a href="http://www.buwal.ch/nachh/chm/">CH CHM Biodiversity Website</a></strong><br />The Swiss Clearing-House Mechanism Biodiversity, initiated by the Swiss Agency for the Environment, Forest and Landscape, is the information plattform of Switzerland for the application of the Rio convention on Biological Diversity. National action plan, news and general information on research in Switzerland.</p>
<p><strong><a href="http://www.cordis.lu/rtd2002/science-society/ethics.htm">European Research Area - Ethics</a></strong><br />The European Commission promotes responsible research in Europe in harmony with the ethical values of all Europeans. This site guides researchers towards reference texts in the area of ethics in research, the European legislation and international conventions, as well as international declarations.</p>
<p><strong><a href="http://www.bustpatents.com/">Internet Patent News</a></strong><br />This site offers you a collection of studies, statements and press releases on biotechnology patent issues.</p>
<p><strong><a href="http://www.maff.gov.uk/">Ministry of Agriculture, Fisheries and Food (MAFF), UK</a></strong><br />Structure of the British Ministry of Agriculture, Fisheries and Food (MAFF) and its opinion about food safety and food standarts with special emphasis on novel food.</p>
<p><strong><a href="http://www.oecd.org/ehs/experts.htm">OECD's Expert Group</a></strong><br />The expert working group was formed to manage the implementation of OECD's Programme on the Harmonization of Regulatory Oversight in Biotechnology. This page gives the terms of reference of the Expert Group.</p>
<p><strong><a href="http://www4.od.nih.gov/oba/">Office of Biotechnology Activities (OBA)</a></strong><br />The Office of Biotechnology Activities (OBA) of the National Institutes of Health monitors scientific progress in human genetics research in order to anticipate future developments, including ethical, legal, and social concerns.</p>
