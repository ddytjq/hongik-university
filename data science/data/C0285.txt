				<td class=body colspan="2"><B>A simple, safe way to help your savings grow faster</B>
				<P>
Start a Scottish Friendly Growth &#38; Security Bond and your savings could work much harder for you.  This special investment gives your savings higher growth potential than a building society because it offers you the potential of stockmarket returns but with less risk than direct stockmarket investment.  So it's a safer way to ensure your savings can grow steadily and do more than keep pace with inflation.
<p>
When you start a Bond, your money is invested in our highly successful With Profits Fund.  The profits it makes in times of strong stockmarket growth are pooled to create a 'cushion' of reserves.  These are distributed over time in the form of bonuses - even when stockmarkets are low.  And once a bonus is added to your Bond, you have the reassurance of knowing it cannot be taken away, unless a Market Value Adjustment (MVA) is made.  Further details of the MVA can be found in the Key Features document online.
