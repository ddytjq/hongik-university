					<h3>1 - 10 of 55</h3><p><strong><a href="http://www.agbios.com/default.asp">Agriculture and Biotechnology Strategies Inc. (AgBioS)</a></strong><br />Agriculture & Biotechnology Strategies Inc. (AgBioS), a Canadian company, consultes the agricultural biotechnology industry, government departments and producer groups. Their website offers general agricultural biotechnology related information and a huge database of articles related to biosafety, environmental impact and ethics.

</p>
<p><strong><a href="http://www.pi.csiro.au/">Australian research centre in the plant sciences (CSIRO)</a></strong><br />CSIRO is an Australian research centre in the plant sciences to promote profitable and sustainable agri-food and to improve natural resource management. Its main goal is genetic engineering for plant improvement.</p>
<p><strong><a href="http://www.iisd.ca/biodiv/excop/">Biosafety Agreement</a></strong><br />The "Biosafety Protocol" to the United Nations Convention on biological diversity was adopted by 130 countries in Montreal on 29 January. It is the first global treaty regulating trade in genetically modified products world-wide and provides rules intended to protect the environment from genetically altered plants, animals and bacteria. 
Pictures and summary of the meeting.
   </p>
<p><strong><a href="http://www.biotech-info.net/">BioTech InfoNet</a></strong><br />General information about genetic engineering applications, insect resistance, environmental impacts and health riscs.</p>
<p><strong><a href="http://www.bbsrc.ac.uk/">Biotechnology and Biological Sciences Research Council (BBSRC)</a></strong><br />The Biotechnology and Biological Sciences Research Council (BBSRC) is the UK's leading funding agency for academic research in the non-medical life sciences. Overview on the areas of science research and the institutes sponsored by the BBSRC.
</p>
<p><strong><a href="http://www.nal.usda.gov/bic/bio21/tablco.html">Biotechnology for the 21th Century</a></strong><br />Large site by the National Science and Technology Council (NSTC), USA, with many links in agricultural biotechnology, environmental bioremediation, marine biotechnology and research interests of governmental societies.</p>
<p><strong><a href="http://www.buwal.ch/stobobio/biotechnologie/">Biotechnology Services by the SAEFL</a></strong><br />The Biotechnology Services of the SAEFL evaluate the impact on the environment of the use of genetically modified and pathogenic organisms (GMO, PO) and informas about its consequences in terms of biosafety, biodiversity and ethical considerations. Very useful. 
</p>
<p><strong><a href="http://www.oecd.org/ehs/bioabout.htm">BioTrack Online</a></strong><br />BioTrack Online, a website by the OECD, focuses on information related to the regulatory oversight of products of biotechnology which is used by governments, industry and other stakeholders.</p>
<p><strong><a href="http://www.cabi.org/BIOSCIENCE/switz.htm">CAB Bioscience Switzerland</a></strong><br />CAB International is a global non-profit organisation delivering practical knowledge solutions in applied life sciences by utilizing its expertise in publishing and biological sciences. The CABI Bioscience Centre Switzerland in Delémont has developed a substantial programme of weed and arthropod biological control.</p>
<p><strong><a href="http://www.cabi.org/BIOSCIENCE/index.htm">CABI Biosciences</a></strong><br />CABI Bioscience is a multidisciplinary scientific team dedicated to raising agricultural productivity in sustainable systems, still protecting the environment from the damaging effects of human activity. 
</p>
