<CENTER>
1978 CADILLAC COUPE DEVILLE
<BR>
425 c.i. engine - paid $150.00
<BR>
Car ran good, but had something bouncing around in the torque converter.
<BR>
The car ran well in the derby, but was up against several wagons and a chrysler imperial.
<BR>
They tore the car up pretty bad.  The frame got bent down and dragged the ground.  
<BR>
Eventually I could hardly move and was disqualified. I changed a tire in the pits and ran the 
<BR>
consultation heat.  The frame started dragging again and I busted a u-joint.
<BR>
This car sustained more damage than any other car I've raced.
