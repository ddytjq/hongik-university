            <p class="contentsubhead">Rapid Repay Personal Illustration</p>
            <p>Rapid Repay - one account to help you manage your finances.</p>
            <p>Rapid Repay is the innovative new account from Northern, that is designed to make your money work much harder 
              for you and could allow you to save time and money when buying your home.</p>
            <p>We have developed a special PC based Rapid Repay Calculator, which allows you to see how much you could save 
              in time and money by comparing the Rapid Repay Facility to a 25 year standard repayment Home Loan with regular 
              monthly repayments. The calculator can help you to work out how quickly you could pay off your Home Loan and 
              how much you could save in interest charges.</p>
            <p>We have also developed a Rapid Repay Planner, which allows you to look in more detail at how you can budget 
              your finances to maximise the potential benefits</p>
            <p><br>
              You can <a href="nbon_web_per_php_093.php" class="link">download</a> the Rapid Repay Calculator
              and the Rapid Repay Planner from this Website. The Planner 
              will only take a few minutes to download whereas the calculator will take about 10 minutes.</p>
            <p>The Rapid Repay Calculator which you can download is a sample programme. Once you have downloaded the Calculator 
              it will detail a default interest rate of 8%. To find out the current % p.a. you can view the <a href="#" onClick="POP('nbon_web_per_php_072.php?popup=yes');return false;" class="link">current 
              interest rates</a> here.</p>
            <p>For the Rapid Repay Planner, full details of all income and expenditure will require to be entered as this 
              will allow for a more accurate Illustration.</p>
            <p><br>
              You can also download the <a href="../download/Rapid_Repay_User_Guide.pdf" class="link">Rapid Repay User Guide</a> 
              to help you use the Calculator and Planner to maximum effect. This User Guide is in PDF file format and you 
              will need a copy of Adobe Acrobat to read it.</p>
            <p><br>
              Alternatively you can complete the attached <a href="nbon_web_per_php_092.php" class="link">Rapid Repay Additional 
              Information</a> form or contact any local branch to request a free Rapid Repay information pack which includes 
              the Calculator and Planner on a choice of either CD or disk.</p>
