        <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="4"><b><font face="Arial" size="2"><strong>homebuyers 
          | </strong></font><font size="2">Equity Release Mortgage</font></b></font></p>
        <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">The Equity Release 
          Mortgage is a special kind of loan designed for homeowners aged 70 and 
          over, which enables you to release cash from your home without having 
          to sell it.</font></p>
        <p><a href="#features" onMouseOut="MM_swapImgRestore()" onMouseOver="MM_swapImage('Image1','','../images/fact-features_H_MO.gif',1)"><img name="Image1" border="0" src="../images/fact-features_H.gif" width="195" height="50"><br>

        <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2"><br>
          <img src="../images/HB-features-title.gif" width="110" height="67"><a name="features"></a><br>
          &nbsp;<br>
          <b> Receive a cash lump sum</b></font></p>
        <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">Your home is probably 
          your greatest asset. Think what you could do if you released some of 
          its value. For example, you could buy an annuity to supplement your 
          income; improve your home or help care for your family's needs by helping 
          with education costs. The Equity Release Mortgage allows you to do this.</font></p>
        <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2"><b>No mortgage repayments 
          to be made</b></font></p>
        <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">With the Equity 
          Release Mortgage, unlike a conventional mortgage, there is no set repayment 
          date and no mortgage repayments to be made. Instead, the mortgage is 
          repayable through the sale of your property, if you move into long-term 
          care/sheltered accommodation or when you die. The amount repayable will 
          include the amount borrowed and interest accrued over the mortgage term.</font></p>
        <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2"><b>Lifetime Occupancy 
          Guarantee</b></font></p>
        <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">We guarantee that 
          you can live in your home as long as you wish. If your spouse or partner 
          dies or has to go into long term care, nothing changes - you are secure 
          to carry on living in your home as before.</font></p>
        <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2"><b>No Negative Equity 
          Guarantee</b></font></p>
        <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">If the outstanding 
          mortgage debt is greater than the sale price of the property and you 
          have complied with the terms and conditions of this loan, we guarantee 
          that your estate will not have to repay the shortfall.</font></p>
        <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2"><b>Continue to Enjoy 
          the Financial Benefits of Home Ownership</b></font></p>
        <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">The Equity Release 
          Mortgage enables you to keep ownership of your home, so any increases 
          in value over the years will benefit you. However, you need to bear 
          in mind that property prices can fall as well as rise.</font></p>
        <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2"><b>Variable Interest 
          Rate of 6.05% (6.10% APR#)</b></font></p>
        <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">The interest on 
          your mortgage will be set 1% above Newbury Building Society's standard 
          variable mortgage interest rate, currently 5.05%. Interest is charged 
          on an annual rest basis, which means that interest is charged for the 
          whole year on the mortgage balance at the beginning of the year (1 November). 
          This interest charge is adjusted each time Newbury Building Society's 
          interest rate changes. Please see our 'typical' example below.</font><br>
        </p>
        <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="1"><a href="#top">Back 
          to menu</a></font> 
        <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2"><img src="../images/HB-access-title.gif" width="110" height="67"></font> 
          <a name="access"></a> 
        <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">Available to applicants 
          aged 70 years and over. In the event of joint ownership, both applicants 
          must have reached the age of 70. Birth certificates are required. </font>
        <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">Available on properties 
          that are mortgage free (any existing mortgage must be repaid using the 
          proceeds of our mortgage advance). </font><br>
        </p>
        <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="1"><a href="#top">Back 
          to menu</a></font> 
        <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2"><img src="../images/HB-charges-title.gif" width="110" height="67"></font> 
          <a name="charges"></a> 
        <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">A &pound;200 arrangement 
          fee is payable, which will be deducted from the funds on completion 
          of your mortgage.</font>
        <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">We will need to 
          value your home to ensure it is suitable security for the mortgage. 
          There is a charge for this, which varies according to the valuation 
          figure. Please see our Current Mortgage <a href="fees.htm" target="mainFrame">Terms 
          and Valuation Fees</a>.</font></p>
        <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">No early repayment 
          charges. This means that if you decide you want to repay (in part or 
          full) your Equity Release Mortgage early, you can do so without charge. 
          </font></p>
        <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">All legal fees are 
          payable by you. This will include charges for independent legal advice 
          as well as for the mortgage transaction.</font></p>
        <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">The cash lump sum 
          is issued by telegraphic transfer to your solicitor on completion of 
          your mortgage. A telegraphic transfer fee will be deducted from the 
          monies. See <a href="../admin_fees.htm" target="_top">Administration 
          Fees</a> for details.</font></p>
        <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">We do charge administration 
          fees for specific mortgage services required during the life of the 
          mortgage. Please see our <a href="../admin_fees.htm" target="_top">Administration 
          Fees</a>.</font><br>
        </p>
        <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="1"><a href="#top">Back 
          to menu</a></font> 
        <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2"><img src="../images/HB-terms-title.gif" width="110" height="67"><a name="terms"></a></font>
        <p align="left"><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">The 
          Equity Release Mortgage is available from 15 April 2002. A written quotation 
          is available on request.</font>
        <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">To apply for the 
          Equity Release Mortgage you must be at least 70 years old. All loans 
          are subject to status and independent valuation.</font></p>
        <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">Minimum loan is 
          &pound;25,500. Maximum loan to value is 25%.</font></p>
        <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">We require all applicants 
          to take independent legal advice on the mortgage contract they are entering 
          into. We will require a certificate from the solicitor confirming you 
          have taken this advice.</font></p>
        <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">We strongly recommend 
          that you discuss your intention to apply for an Equity Release Mortgage 
          with your next of kin, immediate family or any other person having an 
          interest in the property, before applying.</font></p>
        <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">The cash lump sum 
          is tax free but if you choose to invest it, the return on your investments 
          may be taxable and may affect your tax status. In some instances a cash 
          windfall could also affect your entitlement to state benefits. This 
          will depend entirely on your financial circumstances. Please take advice 
          on this important aspect of your mortgage application.</font></p>
        <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">We require that 
          you have satisfactory buildings insurance to at least the value of the 
          sum assured given by the Valuer. You do not have to arrange your buildings 
          insurance through Newbury Building Society, although we will be pleased 
          to give you a quote.</font></p>
        <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">Your property is 
          required as security for the loan. We reserve the right to <br>
          re-inspect the property at regular intervals but no more than once every 
          three years.</font></p>
        <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">Registered intermediaries 
          receive a fee when introducing mortgages to us, which if applicable, 
          will be disclosed to you.</font></p>
        <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">The Society frequently 
          discloses black and white data on mortgage customers to a credit reference 
          agency. This procedure has strict rules in order to protect account 
          holders and has been approved by the Information Commissioner.<br>
          </font></p>
