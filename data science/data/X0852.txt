<!-- end nested Table --><p>Eastern Shore Waterfowlers are based in Kent and Queen Annes 
    Counties on Maryland’s Eastern Shore. The Eastern Shore of Maryland has 
    always offered some of the best goose hunting in the world. At one time the 
    Eastern Shore of Maryland was considered the Goose hunting Capital of the 
    World. </p>


    <p>The Shore is abundant with marshes, creeks, ponds etc. that attract a 
    variety of waterfowl. The fields adjacent to the waters contain a variety of 
    food sources that attract the Canadian geese and provide the hunter an 
    excellent place to harvest them during their winter migration.</p>

<!-- nested Table -->

<!-- end nested Table -->

<p>In the mid 90’s it was necessary to ban goose hunting along the 
Eastern Flyway due to poor management practices. The State of Maryland has proposed a short season during the 2001-2002 season that offers waterfowlers 
abroad to return to the field and hunt the Canadian Goose once again. </p>

<p>The Canadian Geese have made a tremendous comeback and flourish in great 
numbers. They have adapted well in the last few years and feel comfortable 
feeding in small yards, golf courses, along roadways etc. During the last few 
years our youth has awaited for the opportunity to harvest geese like their 
fathers and grandfathers did in previous years. The wait is over and their 
opportunity is here.</p>

<p>Eastern Shore Waterfowlers would like to invite you and your family to join us on a memorable Canadian Goose hunt. We have blinds located in prime habitat. Our guides possess years of experience in harvesting the unique Canadian Goose. Why not call now and make arrangements to hunt with one of the Eastern Shores most productive guide services. We plan to book quick. 
Call now and plan a memorable hunt with your friends and family. We have always strived hard to satisfy our clients. Your satisfaction is our number one priority.</p>
