<center><b><font color="#FF0000"><font size=+1>Introduction</font></font></b></center>

<p>This site contains a series of articles written by myself or linked
from other sites that are designed to demonstrate the evidence for evolution
and common ancestry of all life on earth. If you read the articles listed
in the menu down the left hand side of the page (click <a href="EvoEvidence.html" target="_top">here</a>
if you don't see a menu), you will get an idea of how the evidence for
evolution builds upon basic facts.
<p>Almost all of this material has been posted at various creationism-evolution
debate sites on the internet. I have found it to be quite useful for presenting
the case for evolution. I think that the most interesting material on this
page is found in the section on <a href="EvoContents.html#hum_ape_ca" target="contents">Evidence
for Human and Ape Common Ancestry</a>. This is, in my opinion, the kind
of evidence that makes creationism absolutely untenable. At least it is
the one area most creationists refuse to compromise on, no matter how strong
the evidence is. In particular, please see my essay <a href="hum_ape_chrom.html">Comparison
of the Human and Great Ape Chromosomes as Evidence for Common Ancestry</a>.
<p>An exciting development that I have been following closely is the research
that is beginning to work out the genetic differences between related species
that are responsible for the anatomic differences between those species.
See&nbsp; <a href="http://www.gate.net/~rwms/EvoGenetics.html">The Genetic
Basis of Evolution</a> to learn more about this fascinating topic.
<p>I have just added a whole new section on <a href="crebuttals.html" target="_top">The
Evidence Against Creationism</a>. You can expect to see that page grow
a lot.
