            <p class="introtitle">Deciding where to invest </p>
            <p class="body"><span class="bodytitle">The Lifestyle Profile</span> 
              <br>
              Investments linked to the stockmarket can offer greater potential 
              for growth over a long time rather than investing in a traditional 
              savings or deposit based account. However, stockmarket investments 
              can be bewildering. In addition, you can often be faced with difficult 
              decisions on when and how to switch your investments.</p>
            <p class="body"><span class="bodytitle">A simple solution</span> <br>
              We are committed to making Stakeholder Pensions simple and easy 
              to understand. We have a solution that gives you all the benefits 
              of stockmarket investment with none of the hassle normally involved. 
            </p>
            <p><span class="body">This solution is called the Lifestyle Profile 
              and we believe that it will meet the needs of most people who want 
              to invest in a Stakeholder Pension. </span></p>
            <p class="body">The Lifestyle Profile is also the 'default' fund. 
              This means that if you do not choose a fund we will invest contributions 
              in the Lifestyle Profile until you tell us otherwise.</p>
            <p><span class="body">The Lifestyle Profile aims for growth in the 
              early years. But in the five years before retirement the funds are 
              moved into more secure investments to minimise losses caused by 
              the up and down movements in share prices.</span></p>
            <p> <span class="body">The Lifestyle Profile is made up of three special 
              funds:</span></p>
            <ul>
              <li><span class="body"> Lifestyle FTSE All Share Tracker Fund - 
                aims to provide long term growth.</span> </li>
              <li><span class="body"> Lifestyle Fixed Interest Fund - aims to 
                provide high yield and maximise total return.</span> </li>
              <li><span class="body"> Lifestyle Money Market Fund - provides a 
                high level of income while securing capital.</span></li>
            </ul>
            <p class="body">Your contributions will be invested in the Lifestyle 
              FTSE All Share Tracker Fund until you are within five years of your 
              selected retirement age. When you are within five years of your 
              selected retirement the Lifestyle funds you have invested in gradually 
              change each year on your birthday. This change will happen automatically. 
              Existing investments and future contributions will be re-directed 
              into the lower risk Lifestyle Fixed Interest fund and Lifestyle 
              Money Market funds. Automatic switches will continue progressively 
              so that by the year before you retire you will have no investments 
              left in the Lifestyle FTSE All Share Tracker Fund. </p>
            <p class="body">If you change your chosen retirement age while you 
              are investing in the Lifestyle Profile, your investments will change 
              overnight to reflect the revised number of years until your new 
              retirement age.</p>
            <p class="body">You can combine the Lifestyle Profile with the other 
              investment funds available. However, you should bear in mind that 
              the Lifestyle Profile is designed to ensure that you do not have 
              to actively make changes to your investments as you get close to 
              retirement. </p>
            <p class="body"><span class="bodytitle">Choice if you need it</span><br>
              We recognise that some people enjoy the challenge of choosing and 
              managing their own investments. We therefore offer seven further 
              funds for you to choose from: </p>
            <ul>
              <li><span class="body"> European Index Tracker Fund</span> </li>
              <li><span class="body"> FTSE All Share Tracker Fund</span> </li>
              <li><span class="body"> Household Names Fund</span> </li>
              <li><span class="body"> Fixed Interest Fund</span> </li>
              <li><span class="body"> Money Market Fund</span> </li>
			  <li><span class="body"> Stakeholder Balanced Fund</span> </li>
			  <li><span class="body"> Stakeholder International Fund</span> </li>
            </ul>
            <p class="body">When choosing your funds you need to consider, 'what 
              will make your money grow?'. </p>
            <p class="body">Funds that give you better growth over a long period 
              of time (10-30 years) are usually volatile. This means that fund 
              value may move up and down but over the long term the value will 
              rise much more than funds which are classed as secure. </p>
            <p class="body">When you are within five years of retirement and your 
              pension has grown you should consider moving your funds, to funds 
              which are more secure. This may mean that you lose out on some growth 
              but more importantly you are protecting your pension from any big 
              losses. </p>
            <p class="body">Our Lifestyle Profile invests in the same way. First 
              in the FTSE All Share Tracker Fund, which is medium risk, for the 
              majority of your pension. When you are just five years from retirement 
              the funds are switched into a combination of Lifestyle Fixed Interest 
              Fund and the Lifestyle Money Market Fund which are more secure. 
            </p>
            <p class="body">To help you decide which of these funds may be suitable 
              for you, below we have rated each fund according to risk and provided 
              some information on each. </p>
