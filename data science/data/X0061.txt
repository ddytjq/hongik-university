        horse called Blue Jacket is backed in from 15/1 to 6s in the Footscray Steeple on the last
        day of the Grand National meeting at Flemington. A telegram was sent following the race to
        Melbourne betting shops saying the plunge horse had won, but a while later after a lot of
        money had been paid out an authentic telegram was received saying the aptly named Error
        had won the race. Blue Jacket had been scratched. A punter and telegram clerk were both
        arrested but the major beneficiaries were not found.</font></td>
