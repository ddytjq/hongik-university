becomes ever clearer. Listed below is the latest provisional list of participants for the 2002/03 UEFA Champions League.</SPAN></P>
<P><SPAN class=bBody><STRONG>Format<BR></STRONG>• Assoc: The table lists the national associations and the title holders in this column.<BR>• Rank: The 'Rank' column shows the place of each club in their domestic championship. For Albania, only the league champion can enter the UEFA Champions League, but for England, there is the possibility of having four teams in the competition, with two clubs qualifying automatically for the first group stage, while the other two must enter at the third qualifying round phase.<BR>• Entry: This column explains the entry level for each club in the competition. For example, for the Cypriot champion, they must enter the competition at the first qualifying round stage, while the Croatia champion enters at the next stage of the competition. The Norwegian champions enter at the third qualifying round, with the Italian champions qualifying directly for the first group stage.<BR>• Club: Clubs listed in <SPAN class=bTitle>bold </SPAN>text have qualified to participate in the 2002/03 competition as their domestic league has finished or is already decided. Clubs appearing in normal text denotes provisional participation.</SPAN></P>
