  <p align="left" style="word-spacing: 0; line-height: 100%; text-indent: 0; margin: 0"><font face="Times New Roman" size="4">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
  In 1984, England were drawn in group 3 with Denmark, yet again, and also
  Luxembourg, Greece and Hungary.&nbsp; The Danes had improved dramatically,
  from finishing bottom of the group four years ago, to finishing top of this
  group and securing the only qualification place.&nbsp; Players such as Jesper
  Olsen, Jan Molby and Brian Laudrup helped them to achieve this, starting with
  a 2-2 draw in Copenhagen against England.&nbsp; Both ourselves and the Danes
  were strong teams and the group was effectively decided between us at Wembley
  in September 1983, the Danes triumphed 1-0 thanks to a Simonsen penalty.&nbsp;
  They secured their passage to France with a 2-0 win in Greece, making out
  final match irrelevant.&nbsp; Bobby Robsons team only lost one game and even
  managed to beat Luxembourg 9-0 where Luthor Blissitt scored a hat-trick on his
  international debut.</font></p>
