<p>The ACCU Spring Conference will take place on the 3rd, 4th, 5th and 6th
of April 2002, at the
<a href="http://www.heritage.org.uk/">Heritage Motor Centre</a>,
Gaydon (25 miles from Oxford on the M40).  Note: please
be prepared to book promptly as soon as bookings open as we are
very likely to have to cap the number of places.</p>

<p>The <strong><font style="background-color:yellow;color:black">AGM</font></strong>
location and time will be announced.</p>

<p>This conference is being organised by ACCU in partnership with
<strong><a href="http://www.desktop-associates.co.uk/">Desktop Associates
Ltd</a></strong></p>

<p><em>Please note that these details may be subject to change,
any updates will be published as soon as they become available.</em></p>
