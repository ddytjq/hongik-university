</p>
<p>Subject to the terms of the investment that you have chosen, you are able to

<p>Once you have subscribed the maximum amount allowed by the regulations, no further subscriptions are permitted during that tax year, regardless of how much you withdraw.  Please note it is not a question of how much you have in your ISA at any one time, but how much you have invested during the relevant tax year.
</p>

conditions attached to withdrawals on the 2 Year Fixed Rate ISA.
