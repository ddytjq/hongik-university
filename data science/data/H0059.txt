<P><A NAME="suboccipital"><H2 ALIGN=CENTER>Suboccipital Musculature</H2>
<p><p align=center><A HREF="#page_top"><IMG SRC="images/colorbar_up.gif" WIDTH=504 HEIGHT=13 BORDER=0 ALT=
"------------------------====================------------------------"></A>

<BR>
<P><A NAME="obliquus capitis inferior"><FONT SIZE=+1>Obliquus capitis inferior</FONT>
<UL><LI>Origin: spinous process of axis (C2)
<LI>Insertion: transverse process of atlas (C1)
<LI>Action: rotates the head to the contracted side
<LI>Blood: muscular branches of vertebral artery
<LI>Nerve: suboccipital nerve, (dorsal rami C1)</UL>


<BR>
<P><A NAME="obliquus capitis superior"><FONT SIZE=+1>Obliquus capitis superior</FONT>
<UL><LI>Origin: transverse process of atlas (C1)
<LI>Insertion: between superior and inferior nuchal line of occiput
<LI>Action:
	<OL><LI>bilaterally extends the head
	<LI>laterally flexes to the contracted side</OL>
<LI>Blood: muscular branches of vertebral artery
<LI>Nerve: suboccipital nerve, (dorsal rami C1)</UL>


<BR>
<P><A NAME="rectus capitis posterior major"><FONT SIZE=+1>Rectus capitis posterior major</FONT>
<UL><LI>Origin: spinous process of axis (C2)
<LI>Insertion: inferior nuchal line (lateral to minor)
<LI>Action:
	<OL><LI>bilaterally extends the head
	<LI>rotates the head to the contracted side</OL>
<LI>Blood: muscular branches of vertebral artery
<LI>Nerve: suboccipital nerve, (dorsal rami C1)</UL>


<BR>
<P><A NAME="rectus capitis posterior minor"><FONT SIZE=+1>Rectus capitis posterior minor</FONT>
<UL><LI>Origin: posterior tubercle of atlas (C1)
<LI>Insertion: inferior nuchal line (adjacent to midline)
<LI>Action: bilaterally extends the head
<LI>Blood: muscular branches of vertebral artery
<LI>Nerve: suboccipital nerve, (dorsal rami C1)</UL>


<BR>


<P><A NAME="prevertebral"><H2 ALIGN=CENTER>Prevertebral Musculature</H2>
<P><P ALIGN="center"><A HREF="#page_top"><IMG SRC="images/colorbar_up.gif" WIDTH=504 HEIGHT=13 BORDER=0 ALT=
"------------------------====================------------------------"></A>

<BR>
<P><A NAME="longus colli"><FONT SIZE=+1>Longus colli</FONT>
<UL><LI>Origin: lower anterior vertebral bodies and transverse processes
<LI>Insertion: anterior vertebral bodies and transverse processes several segments above
<LI>Action: flexes the head and neck
<LI>Blood: muscular branches of the aorta
<LI>Nerve: ventral rami C2-C6</UL>


<BR>
<P><A NAME="longus capitis"><FONT SIZE=+1>Longus capitis</FONT>
<UL><LI>Origin: upper anterior vertebral bodies and transverse processes
<LI>Insertion: anterior vertebral bodies and transverse processes several segments above
<LI>Action: flexes the head and neck
<LI>Blood: muscular branches of the aorta
<LI>Nerve: ventral rami C1-C3</UL>

				
<BR>
<P><A NAME="rectus capitis anterior"><FONT SIZE=+1>Rectus capitis anterior</FONT>
<UL><LI>Origin: anterior base of the transverse process of the atlas
<LI>Insertion: occipital bone anterior to foramen magnum
<LI>Action: flexes the head
<LI>Blood: muscular branches of the aorta
<LI>Nerve: ventral rami C2,3</UL>

					
<BR>
<P><A NAME="rectus capitis lateralis"><FONT SIZE=+1>Rectus capitis lateralis</FONT>
<UL><LI>Origin: transverse process of the atlas
<LI>Insertion: jugular process of the occipital bone
<LI>Action: bends the head laterally
<LI>Blood: muscular branches of the aorta
<LI>Nerve: ventral rami C2,3</UL>
			
<BR>

<P><A NAME="anterolateral neck"><H2 ALIGN=CENTER>Anterolateral Neck Musculature</H2>
<P><P ALIGN="center"><A HREF="#page_top"><IMG SRC="images/colorbar_up.gif" WIDTH=504 HEIGHT=13 BORDER=0 ALT=
"------------------------====================------------------------"></A>

<BR>
<P><A NAME="anterior scalene"><FONT SIZE=+1>Anterior scalene</FONT>
<UL><LI>Attachment A: anterior tubercles of transverse processes of C3-C6
<LI>Attachment B: 1st rib
<LI>Action:
	<OL>if transverse process fixed:
		<LI>elevates the ribs for respiration
	<BR>if ribs fixed:
		<LI>rotates to side opposite of contraction
		<LI>laterally flexes to the contracted side
		<LI>bilaterally flexes the neck</OL>
<LI>Blood: inferior thyroid artery (branch of the thyrocervical trunk)
<LI>Nerve: ventral rami C3-C6</UL>


<BR>
<P><A NAME="scalenus minimus"><FONT SIZE=+1>Scalenus minimus</FONT> (may be absent)
<UL><LI>Attachment A: anterior tubercles of transverse processes of C6 & 7
<LI>Attachment B: 1st rib and/or supraplural membrane
<LI>Action:
	<OL>if transverse process fixed:
		<LI>elevates the ribs for respiration
	<BR>if ribs fixed:
		<LI>rotates to side opposite of contraction
		<LI>laterally flexes to the contracted side
		<LI>bilaterally flexes the neck</OL>
<LI>Blood: ascending cervical artery
<LI>Nerve: variable (cervical and brachial plexus)</UL>


<BR>
<P><A NAME="middle scalene"><FONT SIZE=+1>Middle scalene</FONT>
<UL><LI>Attachment A: transverse processes of all cervical vertebrae
<LI>Attachment B: 1st rib (behind anterior scalene)
<LI>Action:
	<OL>if transverse process fixed:
		<LI>elevates the ribs for respiration
	<BR>if ribs fixed:
		<LI>rotates to side opposite of contraction
		<LI>laterally flexes to the contracted side
		<LI>bilaterally flexes the neck</OL>
<LI>Blood: ascending cervical artery
<LI>Nerve: ventral rami C3-C8</UL>


<BR>
<P><A NAME="posterior scalene"><FONT SIZE=+1>Posterior scalene</FONT>
<UL><LI>Attachment A: posterior tubercles of transverse processes of C5 & C6
<LI>Attachment B: 2nd and/or 3rd rib
<LI>Action:
	<OL>if transverse process fixed:
		<LI>elevates the ribs for respiration
	<BR>if ribs fixed:
		<LI>rotates to side opposite of contraction
		<LI>laterally flexes to the contracted side
		<LI>bilaterally flexes the neck</OL>
<LI>Blood: ascending cervical artery
<LI>Nerve: ventral rami C5-C7</UL>


<BR>

<P><A NAME="superficial neck"><H2 ALIGN=CENTER>Superficial Neck Musculature</H2>
<P><P ALIGN="center"><A HREF="#page_top"><IMG SRC="images/colorbar_up.gif" WIDTH=504 HEIGHT=13 BORDER=0 ALT=
"------------------------====================------------------------"></A>

<BR>
<P><A NAME="sternocleidomastoid"><FONT SIZE=+1>Sternocleidomastoid</FONT>
<UL><LI>Origin: (two heads)
	<OL><LI>manubrium of sternum
	<LI>medial portion of clavicle</OL>
<LI>Insertion: mastoid process of temporal bone
<LI>Action:
	<OL><LI>rotates to side opposite of contraction
	<LI>laterally flexes to the contracted side
	<LI>bilaterally flexes the neck</OL>
<LI>Blood:
	<OL><LI>occipital artery
	<LI>superior thyroid artery</OL>
<LI>Nerve:
	<OL><LI>motor: spinal accessory (XI cranial)
	<LI>sensory: ventral rami of C2,(C3)</OL></UL>

<BR>
<P><A NAME="platysma"><FONT SIZE=+1>Platysma</FONT>
<UL><LI>Origin: subcutaneous skin over delto-pectoral region
<LI>Insertion: invests in the skin widely over the mandible
<LI>Action:
	<OL><LI> depress mandible and lower lip
	<LI>tenses the skin over the lower neck</OL>
<LI>Blood: superficial vessels of the neck
<LI>Nerve: cervical branch of facial nerve (VII cranial)</UL>


<BR>

<P><A NAME="anterior neck"><H2 ALIGN=CENTER>Anterior Neck</H2>
<P><P ALIGN="center"><A HREF="#page_top"><IMG SRC="images/colorbar_up.gif" WIDTH=504 HEIGHT=13 BORDER=0 ALT=
"------------------------====================------------------------"></A>

<BR>
<P><A NAME="sternohyoid"><FONT SIZE=+1>Sternohyoid</FONT>
<UL><LI>Origin:
	<OL><LI>posterior aspect of manubrium
	<LI>sternal end of clavicle</OL>
<LI>Insertion: body of hyoid
<LI>Action:
	<OL><LI>depresses hyoid & larynx
	<LI>acts eccentrically with the suprahyoid muscles to provide them a stable base</OL>
<LI>Blood:
	<OL><LI>inferior thyroid artery (primary)
	<LI>superior thyroid artery</OL>
<LI>Nerve:
	<OL><LI>upper portions: superior root of ansa  cervicalis, C2
	<LI>lower portions: inferior root of ansa  cervicalis, C2,3</OL></UL>


<BR>
<P><A NAME="omohyoid"><FONT SIZE=+1>Omohyoid</FONT>
<UL><LI>Attachments:
	<OL><LI>superior belly: hyoid bone (lateral to sternohyoid)
	<LI>inferior belly: superior scapular border (medial to suprascapular notch)</OL>
<LI>both bellies meet at the clavicle & are held to the clavicle by a pulley tendon
<LI>Action:
	<OL><LI>depresses hyoid & larynx
	<LI>acts eccentrically with the suprahyoid muscles to provide them a stable base</OL>
<LI>Blood:
	<OL><LI>inferior thyroid artery (primary)
	<LI>superior thyroid artery</OL>
<LI>Nerve:
	<OL><LI>upper portions: superior root of ansa  cervicalis, C2
	<LI>lower portions: inferior root of ansa  cervicalis, C2,3</OL></UL>


<BR>
<P><A NAME="sternothyroid"><FONT SIZE=+1>Sternothyroid</FONT>
<UL><LI>Origin: posterior aspect of manubrium
<LI>Insertion: oblique line of thyroid cartilage
<LI>Action:
	<OL><LI>depresses hyoid & larynx
	<LI>acts eccentrically with the suprahyoid muscles to provide them a stable base</OL>
<LI>Blood:
	<OL><LI>inferior thyroid artery (primary)
	<LI>superior thyroid artery</OL>
<LI>Nerve:
	<OL><LI>upper portions: superior root of ansa  cervicalis, C2
	<LI>lower portions: inferior root of ansa  cervicalis, C2,3</OL></UL>


<BR>
<P><A NAME="thyrohyoid"><FONT SIZE=+1>Thyrohyoid</FONT>
<UL><LI>Origin: oblique line of thyroid cartilage
<LI>Insertion: body of hyoid
<LI>Action:
	<OL><LI>depresses hyoid
	<LI>may assist in larynx elevation</OL>
<LI>Blood:
	<OL><LI>inferior thyroid artery (primary)
	<LI>superior thyroid artery</OL>
<LI>Nerve:
	<OL><LI>upper portions: superior root of ansa  cervicalis, C2
	<LI>lower portions: inferior root of ansa  cervicalis, C2,3</OL></UL>


<BR>
<P><A NAME="stylohyoid"><FONT SIZE=+1>Stylohyoid</FONT>
<UL><LI>Origin: styloid process of temporal bone
<LI>Insertion: lateral margin of hyoid (near greater horn)
<LI>Action:
	<OL><LI>pulls the hyoid superiorly & posteriorly during swallowing
	<LI>fixes the hyoid bone for infrahyoid action</OL>
<LI>Blood: facial & occipital artery
<LI>Nerve: facial nerve (VII cranial)</UL>


<BR>
<P><A NAME="digastric"><FONT SIZE=+1>Digastric</FONT>
<UL><LI>Attachments:
	<OL><LI>post belly: mastoid process of temporal bone
	<LI>anterior belly: digastric fossa of internal mandible</OL>
<LI>both bellies meet and attach at the lateral aspect of body of hyoid  by a pulley tendon
<LI>Action:
	<OL><LI>open mouth by depressing mandible
	<LI>fixes hyoid bone for infrahyoid action</OL>
<LI>Blood: branches of the external carotid
<LI>Nerve: 
	<OL><LI>posterior belly: facial nerve (VII cranial)
	<LI>anterior belly: mylohyoid nerve</OL></UL>


<BR>
<P><A NAME="mylohyoid"><FONT SIZE=+1>Mylohyoid</FONT>
<UL><LI>Origin: inner surface of mandible off the mylohyoid line
<LI>Insertion:
	<OL><LI>body of hyoid
	<LI>along midline at mylohyoid raphe</OL>
<LI>Action:
	<OL><LI>elevates the hyoid bone
	<LI>raises floor of mouth (for swallowing)
	<LI>depresses mandible when hyoid is fixed</OL>
<LI>Blood: lingual artery
<LI>Nerve: mylohyoid nerve (branch of mandibular division, V3 cranial)</UL>

<BR>
<P><A NAME="geniohyoid"><FONT SIZE=+1>Geniohyoid</FONT>
<UL><LI>Origin: inner surface of the mandible off the mental spines
<LI>Insertion: body of hyoid (paired muscles separated by a septum)
<LI>Action:
	<OL><LI>elevates the tongue
	<LI>depress the mandible
	<LI>works with mylohyoid</OL>
<LI>Blood: lingual artery
<LI>Nerve: branch from C1 (following hypoglossal nerve)</UL>


<BR>


<P><A NAME="epicranial"><H2 ALIGN=CENTER>Epicranial Musculature</H2>
<P><P ALIGN="center"><A HREF="#page_top"><IMG SRC="images/colorbar_up.gif" WIDTH=504 HEIGHT=13 BORDER=0 ALT=

<BR>
<P><A NAME="occipitalis"><FONT SIZE=+1>Occipitalis</FONT> (2 bellies)
<UL><LI>Origin:
	<OL><LI>lateral 2/3 of superior nuchal line
	<LI>external occipital protuberance</OL>
<LI>Insertion: galea aponeurosis, over the occipital bone
<LI>Action: draws back the scalp to raise the eyebrows and wrinkle the brow
<LI>Blood: occipital artery
<LI>Nerve: posterior auricular branch of facial nerve</UL>


<BR>
<P><A NAME="frontalis"><FONT SIZE=+1>Frontalis</FONT> (2 bellies)
<UL><LI>Origin: galea aponeurosis, anterior to the vertex
<LI>Insertion: skin above the nose and eyes
<LI>Action: draws back the scalp to raise the eyebrows and wrinkle the brow
<LI>Blood: ophthalmic artery
<LI>Nerve: temporal branch of facial nerve</UL>


<BR>

<P><A NAME="face"><H2 ALIGN=CENTER>Muscles of Facial Expression</H2>
<P><P ALIGN="center"><A HREF="#page_top"><IMG SRC="images/colorbar_up.gif" WIDTH=504 HEIGHT=13 BORDER=0 ALT=
"------------------------====================------------------------"></A>

<BR>
<P><A NAME="orbicularis oculi"><FONT SIZE=+1>Orbicularis oculi</FONT>
<UL><LI>Origin:
	<OL><LI>orbital portion: nasal process of frontal bone
	<LI>palpebral portion: palpebral ligament
	<LI>lacrimal portion: lacrimal crest of lacrimal bone</OL>
<LI>Insertion: circumferentially around orbit meeting in palpebral raphe
<LI>Action: powerfully closes the eye
<LI>Blood: ophthalmic artery
<LI>Nerve: zygomatic branch of facial nerve</UL>


<BR>
<P><A NAME="corrugator supercilii"><FONT SIZE=+1>Corrugator supercilii</FONT>
<UL><LI>Origin: frontal bone just above the nose
<LI>Insertion: skin of the medial portion of the eyebrows
<LI>Action: draws the eyebrows downward and medially
<LI>Blood: ophthalmic artery
<LI>Nerve: zygomatic branch of facial nerve</UL>


<BR>
<P><A NAME="orbicularis oris"><FONT SIZE=+1>Orbicularis oris</FONT>
<UL><LI>Origin:
	<OL><LI>alveolar border of maxilla
	<LI>lateral to midline of mandible</OL>
<LI>Insertion:
	<OL><LI>circumferentially around mouth
	<LI>blends with other muscles</OL>
<LI>Action:
	<OL><LI>closes the lips
	<LI>protrudes the lips</OL>
<LI>Blood: facial artery
<LI>Nerve: buccal branch of facial nerve</UL>


<BR>
<P><A NAME="levator labii superioris alaeque nasi"><FONT SIZE=+1>Levator labii superioris alaeque nasi</FONT>
<UL><LI>Origin: frontal process of maxilla
<LI>Insertion:
	<OL><LI>upper lip muscles
	<LI>nasal cartilage</OL>
<LI>Action:
	<OL><LI>elevates the upper lip
	<LI>flares the nostrils</OL>
<LI>Blood: facial artery
<LI>Nerve: buccal branch of facial nerve</UL>


<BR>
<P><A NAME="levator labii superioris"><FONT SIZE=+1>Levator labii superioris</FONT>
<UL><LI>Origin: medial 1/2 of infraorbital margin
<LI>Insertion: upper lip muscles
<LI>Action: elevates the upper lip
<LI>Blood: facial artery
<LI>Nerve: buccal branch of facial nerve</UL>


<BR>
<P><A NAME="zygomaticus minor"><FONT SIZE=+1>Zygomaticus minor</FONT>
<UL><LI>Origin: zygomatic bone, posterior to maxillary-zygomatic suture
<LI>Insertion: skin of the upper lip
<LI>Action: elevates the upper lip
<LI>Blood: facial artery
<LI>Nerve: buccal branch of facial nerve</UL>


<BR>
<P><A NAME="zygomaticus major"><FONT SIZE=+1>Zygomaticus major</FONT>
<UL><LI>Origin: anterior to zygomatic-temporal suture
<LI>Insertion: modiolus (angle of the mouth)
<LI>Action: lifts and draws back the angle(s) of the mouth (as in smiling)
<LI>Blood: facial artery
<LI>Nerve: buccal branch of facial nerve</UL>


<BR>
<P><A NAME="risorius"><FONT SIZE=+1>Risorius</FONT> (may be absent)
<UL><LI>Origin: parotid fascia
<LI>Insertion: modiolus (angle of the mouth)
<LI>Action: draws the mouth laterally (as in smiling)
<LI>Blood: facial artery
<LI>Nerve: buccal branch of facial nerve</UL>


<BR>
<P><A NAME="levator anguli oris"><FONT SIZE=+1>Levator anguli oris</FONT>
<UL><LI>Origin: maxilla, inferior to infraorbital foramen
<LI>Insertion: modiolus (angle of the mouth)
<LI>Action: lifts the angle(s) of the mouth (as in smiling)
<LI>Blood: facial artery
<LI>Nerve: buccal branch of facial nerve</UL>


<BR>
<P><A NAME="buccinator"><FONT SIZE=+1>Buccinator</FONT>
<UL><LI>Origin:
	<OL><LI>posterior alveolar process of maxilla
	<LI>posterior alveolar process of mandible
	<LI>along the pterygomandibular raphe</OL>
<LI>Insertion: modiolus
<LI>Action: compresses the cheek(s)
<LI>Blood: facial artery
<LI>Nerve: buccal branch of facial nerve</UL>


<BR>
<P><A NAME="depressor anguli oris"><FONT SIZE=+1>Depressor anguli oris</FONT>
<UL><LI>Origin:
	<OL><LI>along the oblique line of mandible
	<LI>lateral aspect of mental tubercle of the mandible</OL>
<LI>Insertion: modiolus
<LI>Action: lowers the angle(s) of the mouth (as in frowning)
<LI>Blood: facial artery
<LI>Nerve: mandibular branch of facial nerve</UL>


<BR>
<P><A NAME="depressor labii inferioris"><FONT SIZE=+1>Depressor labii inferioris</FONT>
<UL><LI>Origin:
	<OL><LI>mandible, between symphysis and mental foramen
	<LI>along oblique line of the mandible</OL>
<LI>Insertion: skin of the lower lip
<LI>Action: draws the lower lip downward and laterally
<LI>Blood: facial artery
<LI>Nerve: mandibular branch of facial nerve</UL>



<BR>

<P><A NAME="mastication"><H2 ALIGN=CENTER>Muscles of Mastication</H2>
<P><P ALIGN="center"><A HREF="#page_top"><IMG SRC="images/colorbar_up.gif" WIDTH=504 HEIGHT=13 BORDER=0 ALT=
"------------------------====================------------------------"></A>

<BR>
<P><A NAME="masseter"><FONT SIZE=+1>Masseter</FONT>
<UL><LI>Origin:
	<UL><LI>Superficial:
		<OL><LI>zygomatic process of the maxilla
		<LI>inferior border of zygomatic arch</OL>
	<LI>Intermediate: inner surface of zygomatic arch
	<LI>Deep: posterior aspect of inferior border of zygomatic arch</UL>
<LI>Insertion:
	<UL><LI>Superficial:
		<OL><LI>angle of mandible
		<LI>lateral surface of mandibular ramus</OL>
	<LI>Intermediate: ramus of mandible
	<LI>Deep:
		<OL><LI>superior ramus of mandible
		<LI>coronoid process of mandible</OL></UL>
<LI>Action:
	<OL><LI>closes the lower jaw (clenches the teeth)
	<LI>may deviate mandible to opposite side of contraction</OL>
<LI>Blood: masseteric artery
<LI>Nerve: masseteric nerve</UL>


<BR>
<P><A NAME="medial pterygoid"><FONT SIZE=+1>Medial pterygoid</FONT>
<UL><LI>Origin:
	<OL><LI>medial surface of lateral pterygoid plate of the sphenoid
	<LI>palatine bone
	<LI>pterygoid fossa</OL>
<LI>Insertion:
	<OL><LI>inner surface of mandibular ramus
	<LI>angle of the mandible</OL>
<LI>Action:
	<OL><LI>closes the lower jaw (clenches the teeth)
	<LI>can protrude the mandible in combination with the lateral pterygoid</OL>
<LI>Blood: medial pterygoid artery
<LI>Nerve: medial pterygoid nerve</UL>


<BR>
<P><A NAME="lateral pterygoid"><FONT SIZE=+1>Lateral pterygoid</FONT>
<UL><LI>Origin:
	<OL><LI>Superior head: lateral surface of the greater wing of the sphenoid
	<LI>Inferior head: lateral surface of the lateral pterygoid plate</OL>
<LI>Insert together:
	<OL><LI>neck of the mandibular condyle
	<LI>articular disk of the TMJ</OL>
<LI>Action:
	<OL><LI>deviates mandible to side opposite of contraction (during chewing)
	<LI>opens mouth by protruding mandible (inferior head)
	<LI>closes the mandible (superior head)</OL>
<LI>Blood: lateral pterygoid artery
<LI>Nerve: lateral pterygoid nerve</UL>

<UL><LI>Origin: inferior aspect of the lesser wing of sphenoid (adjacent to the common annular tendon)
<LI>Insertion:
	<OL><LI>medial and lateral walls of the orbit

<LI>Action: elevates the eyelid
<LI>Blood: branches of ophthalmic artery
<LI>Nerve: oculomotor nerve (III cranial)</UL>

<LI>Action: abducts eye
<LI>Blood: branches of ophthalmic artery
