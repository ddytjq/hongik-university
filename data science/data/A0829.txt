            <p class="contentsubhead">Investing for the future</p>
			<p>Investments can be made directly through an Open Ended Investment Company or through an Individual Savings Account or Personal Equity Plan, which offer certain tax advantages*.</p>
			<p class="contenttextbold">Open Ended Investment Company (OEIC)</p>
			<p>An OEIC is a modern version of a Unit Trust. They have all of the same benefits, but provide increased flexibility and simplicity. Like Unit Trusts, investors' money is pooled with those of other investors in each appropriate fund, with each fund having a particular investment objective matched to your needs.</p>
			<p class="contenttextbold">Individual Savings Account (ISA)</p>
			<p>An ISA allows you to invest tax efficiently* through a wide range of investments to suit different needs. There are three investment options for ISAs - cash, stocks and shares and life assurance. There are two types of ISAs you can invest in, a Maxi ISA and a Mini ISA. However, you can only subscribe to one Maxi ISA or one of each type of Mini ISA in each tax year.</p>
			<p><span class="contenttextbold">Maxi ISA</span>: A Maxi ISA allows you to save up to a total of £7,000 per tax year** into MLC investment portfolios with all the proceeds free from tax. Investors looking to invest the maximum into our funds free of tax are best suited to a Maxi ISA.</p>
			<p><span class="contenttextbold">Mini ISA</span>: Instead of a Maxi ISA, you can invest up to a total of £3,000 per tax year** in a Mini Stocks and Shares ISA and also invest up to a total of £3,000 per tax year** in a separate Mini Cash ISA if you wish.</p>
			<p class="contenttextbold">Personal Equity Plan (PEP)</p>
			<p>PEPs were replaced by ISAs in 1999. This means you can't open a new PEP account or invest new money into an existing PEP account. However, you can switch all or part of your existing PEP investment into different investment funds and/or transfer PEPs between different plan managers and continue to enjoy the same tax advantages.</p>
