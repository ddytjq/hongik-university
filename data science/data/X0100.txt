<IMG SRC="../images/clear.gif" ALT="" WIDTH="4" HEIGHT="5"><br>

Steve has been a great friend to the International Tennis Hall of Fame Museum 
for many years. He served on the Museum's 
Project Committee which was responsible for the $7.5 
million renovation and redesign of the <A HREF="../museum.html">Museum galleries</a> in Newport, Rhode 
Island. He also wrote the Museum's gallery text and served as "resident 
tennis historian," though he lives in New York, not Newport.<br>
<IMG SRC="../images/clear.gif" ALT="" WIDTH="4" HEIGHT="5"><br>

His latest project was developing the text for 
<a href="../splashscreen.html">Martina The Magnificent</a>, a retrospective of Martina Navratilova's career on view in the Museum's Atrium Gallery through the spring of 2001. <br>
<IMG SRC="../images/clear.gif" ALT="" WIDTH="4" HEIGHT="5"><br>
We think you'll enjoy Steve's book. He really knows the game. He was the editor of World Tennis Magazine, where he worked from 1974-91. As a senior correspondent for Tennis Week, he covers the French Open 
and Wimbledon for WCBS radio and appears as a TV commentator on cable 
networks. <br>
