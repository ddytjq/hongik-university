      <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2"><b>Our commercial 
        quotation system will enable you to quote for a variety of products and 
        the schemes currently available are;</b></font></p>
      <P><b><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">Liability</font><br>
        </b><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">This comprehensive 
        Master Tradesman policy is for sole traders, partnerships and small limited 
        companies in the building and allied trades. Cover incorporates Public 
        Liability Indemnity Limits of £500,000, £1,000,000, £2,000,000 and £5,000,000 
        with an option for Employer’s Liability Indemnity Limit of £10,000,000. 
        Limited Tools cover is available and the special features of this policy 
        also include a large variety of acceptable trades, cover for bona fide 
        sub contractors, products liability, contractual liability, indemnity 
        to principle and injury to working partners. Full notes and details of 
        this policy are accessible from the quotation system.</font></P>
      <P><b><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">Office</font><br>
        </b><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">This is a competitively 
        priced comprehensive and flexible policy, which offers many special features 
        and benefits so that the Insured can enjoy top quality cover at an attractive 
        price. Contents cover includes material damage, theft, money, glass and 
        liability covers with an option to add buildings, business interruption 
        and book debts. Full notes and details of this policy are accessible from 
        the quotation system.</font></P>
      <P><b><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">Office at Home</font><br>
        </b><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">This policy is 
        intended to meet the general insurance needs that arise from the business 
        of running an office from home premises. This easy to understand policy 
        provides all your cover requirements with a choice of sums insured of 
        £7,500 or £12,500 with a limit of £5,000 electronic office equipment included 
        in the total cover you select. Premiums are competitively priced and full 
        details of this cover are accessible from the quotation system.</font></P>
      <P><b><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">Revenue Protector</font><br>
        </b><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">The cover is designed 
        for people classed as having professional occupations such as vets, solicitors, 
        dentists, accountants and will help cover the cost of providing a locum 
        or the cost of continuing practice expenses if an illness or accident 
        stops them or an associate working. The amount of benefit received is 
        subject to documentary proof of expenses incurred and revenue lost and 
        benefits start immediately [subject to a minimum of 7 days continuous 
        incapacity]. Benefits continue until they or their associates are fit 
        enough to return to work or until they have been incapacitated for 52 
        weeks, whichever is the sooner. The age limits are 65 for sickness cover 
        and 70 for accident cover. Full details of this product are available 
        from the quotation system.</font></P>
      <P><b><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">Shops</font><br>
        </b><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">Cover under this 
        contract is comprehensive, competitive and includes material damage, theft, 
        business interruption, money, glass, liability covers with an automatic 
        seasonal increase on the stock in trade. Optional covers include buildings, 
        personal accident and sickness and employee dishonesty. Apart from providing 
        a quotation the underwriting notes and full details are accessible from 
        the system.</font></P>
      <P><b><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">Commercial Property 
        Owners</font><br>
        </b><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">This policy is 
        designed for owners, head lessees and mortgages who are responsible for 
        insuring tenanted commercial and industrial premises and is suitable for 
        single properties or up to 5 large portfolios. Cover can be tailored according 
        to the requirements at each location. Standard cover includes the buildings, 
        Public Liability at £2,000,000 Indemnity Limit and fixed glass. Optional 
        extensions available include accidental damage and loss of rent covers. 
        For a full summary of cover see the notes section on the quotation system.</font></P>
      <P><b><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">Quotes are valid 
        for 30 days</font><br>
        </b><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">Quotations are 
        only valid for a period of 30 days and if you are quoting for a case where 
        the risk date is more than 30 days ahead we may have to re-quote the business 
        prior to it being placed on risk. This is due to various changes, such 
        as a rate or IPT increase, which may invalidate the quotation.</font></P>
      <P><b><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">Non Standard Business</font><br>
        </b><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">There are a number 
        of types of commercial business that we are unable to put onto the quotation 
        system because of the type of information required or they need to be 
        individually underwritten. Quotation request forms can be accessed from 
        the system and these include hotels and guesthouses, property owners, 
        commercial combined, restaurants, liability, shops and offices. Theses 
        forms are for businesses that are refer trades on the quotation system 
        or fall outside the normal scope. We have an excellent panel of insurers 
        for commercial insurance including Axa, CGU, Lloyds, Norman, NIG Skandia, 
        Norwich Union and Zurich. Most quotations will be provided within 48 hours 
        but more complicated risks might take longer. Other areas we can cover 
        include motor traders, surgeries, contractors, care homes and some of 
        the more unusual ones include weddings, weather cover and special events.</font></P>
      <P><b><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">Payment</font><br>
        </b><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">We can accept payment 
        by credit card, cheque or direct debit where the premium exceeds £100.</font></P>
