									<p><font face="Arial,Helvetica,Geneva,Swiss,SunSans-Regular" size="2"><b>THUMBNAIL</b></font></p>
									<p><font face="Arial,Helvetica,Geneva,Swiss,SunSans-Regular">JobsInMotorsports.com is an Internet job bank that specializes in the motorsports industry. Its services are free to racing teams, motorsports sanctioning bodies, race tracks, motorsports suppliers, sponsors and the motorsports media. It is uniquely structured to make their staffing job simpler and more convenient.</font></p>
									<p><font face="Arial,Helvetica,Geneva,Swiss,SunSans-Regular">For candidates, JobsInMotorsports.com provides not only a forum to display their resumes to employers on-line, it also enables them to actually send their resume directly to the staffing people at racing teams, motorsports sanctioning bodies, race tracks, motorsports suppliers and sponsors. In addition, candidates can and apply directly for motorsports jobs they post.</font></p>
									<p><font face="Arial,Helvetica,Geneva,Swiss,SunSans-Regular">This JobsInMotorsports.com website is the result of months of brainstorming and talking to candidates and employers to develop services that would truly be effective for both candidates and employers involved in motor racing of all kinds. </font></p>
