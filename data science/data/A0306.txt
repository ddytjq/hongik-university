function days_to_euro()
{
today=new Date();
today_day_of_week=today.getDay()
today_day=today.getDate()
today_month=today.getMonth()+1
today_year=today.getYear()

today_year=today_year-parseInt(today_year/100)*100

if ((today_year!=1)||
(today_month<1)||
(today_month>12)||
(today_day<1)||
(today_day>31))
{ nbr_days=0;
}
else
{ nbr_days=days_to_go[today_month]-today_day;
}
if (nbr_days<0)
nbr_days=0;

if (nbr_days==0)
{ working_days=0;
}
else
{ if (nbr_days<2)
working_weeks=0;
else
working_weeks=parseInt((nbr_days-1)/7);

if ((today_day_of_week==0)||(today_day_of_week==6))
days_this_week=0;
else
days_this_week=5-today_day_of_week;

if (nbr_days>1)
working_days=working_weeks*5+days_this_week+1;
else
working_days=0;

if (nbr_days>=148)
working_days=working_days-1;
if (nbr_days>=64)
working_days=working_days-1;
if (nbr_days>=7)
working_days=working_days-3;
if (nbr_days==6)
working_days=working_days-2;
if (nbr_days==5)
working_days=working_days-1;
}

d1=parseInt(nbr_days/100)
nbr_days=nbr_days-d1*100
d2=parseInt(nbr_days/10)
d3=nbr_days-d2*10

document.digit_1.src='/new/images/digit_'+d1+'.gif'
document.digit_2.src='/new/images/digit_'+d2+'.gif'
document.digit_3.src='/new/images/digit_'+d3+'.gif'

d1=parseInt(working_days/100)
working_days=working_days-d1*100
d2=parseInt(working_days/10)
d3=working_days-d2*10

document.wdigit_1.src='/new/images/digit_'+d1+'.gif'

}

<BR CLEAR="LEFT"><H4> EMU Overview </H4>
<P>In accordance with the terms of the Maastricht Treaty,
Stage 3 of EMU started on 1 January 1999.

</P>
<P>The decision to proceed, and the choice of Member States to
participate in the 'first wave was taken by the Heads of the
15 EU governments in May 1998.

</P>
<P>The participants were decided on the basis of whether each
country had achieved the required degree of economic convergence
and was based on reports prepared by the EU Commission and the
European Central Bank (ECB) based, in turn, on the economic
criteria set out in the Maastricht Treaty.

</P>
<P>The original 11 participating Member States are Austria, Belgium, 
Finland, France, Germany, Holland, Ireland, Italy, Luxembourg, Portugal
and Spain. Since 1 January 1999 their currencies are linked
to each other and to the euro on the basis of irrevocably fixed
conversion rates. Since 1 January 2001, Greece is now one of the 
participating Member States.
