            <p class="MsoNormal" style="mso-layout-grid-align:none;text-autospace:none"><br>
            <b><font face="Verdana" size="2"><span style="mso-ansi-language: EN-US">Strategy</span></font></b></p>
            <p class="MsoNormal" style="mso-layout-grid-align:none;text-autospace:none"><font face="Verdana" size="2"><span style="mso-ansi-language: EN-US">The
            Directors believe that there is significant potential for growth in
            the UK non-conforming mortgage market and that there are good
            prospects for Kensington&#146;s continued growth in this market.
            Kensington&#146;s strategy is to continue to focus on its core market
            and to build on its competitive strengths, which include:<o:p>
            </o:p>
            </span></font></p>
            <ul>
              <li>
                <p class="MsoNormal" style="mso-layout-grid-align:none;text-autospace:none"><font face="Verdana" size="2"><span style="mso-ansi-language: EN-US">its reputation as a leader and innovator in the non-conforming
            mortgage market;<o:p>
                &nbsp;
            </span></font></li>
            </ul>
            <o:p>
            <ul>
              <li>
                <p class="MsoNormal"><font face="Verdana" size="2"><span style="mso-ansi-language: EN-US">&nbsp;its well-established multi-channel distribution network;<o:p>
                &nbsp;
            </span></font></li>
            </ul>
            <o:p>
            <ul>
              <li>
                <p class="MsoNormal" style="mso-layout-grid-align:none;text-autospace:none"><font face="Verdana" size="2"><span style="mso-ansi-language: EN-US">&nbsp;the strength and experience of its management team;<o:p>
                &nbsp;
            </span></font></li>
            </ul>
            <o:p>
            <ul>
              <li>
                <p class="MsoNormal" style="mso-layout-grid-align:none;text-autospace:none"><font face="Verdana" size="2"><span style="mso-ansi-language: EN-US">the depth of its underwriting experience working within the
            framework of flexible and disciplined underwriting criteria and
            controls;<o:p>
                &nbsp;
            </span></font></li>
            </ul>
            <o:p>
            <ul>
              <li>
                <p class="MsoNormal" style="mso-layout-grid-align:none;text-autospace:none"><font face="Verdana" size="2"><span style="mso-ansi-language: EN-US">its outsourcing strategy which minimises fixed costs,
                facilitates rapid growth, provides access to the skills of its
                external service providers and enables management to concentrate
                on the issues that are central to the success of the business;
                and</span></font></li>
            </ul>
            <ul>
              <li>
                <p class="MsoNormal" style="mso-layout-grid-align:none;text-autospace:none"><font face="Verdana" size="2"><span style="mso-ansi-language: EN-US">its
                good relationships with regulators and participation in
                initiatives to raise industry standards and improve practices.<o:p>
                </o:p>
                </span></font></li>
            </ul>
              <p class="MsoNormal" style="mso-layout-grid-align:none;text-autospace:none"><font face="Verdana" size="2"><span style="mso-ansi-language: EN-US">In
              support of the above strategy, Kensington&#146;s management will be
              concentrating on the following initiatives:<o:p>
              </o:p>
              </span></font></p>
            <ul>
              <li>
                <p class="MsoNormal" style="mso-layout-grid-align:none;text-autospace:none"><font face="Verdana" size="2"><span style="mso-ansi-language: EN-US">customer
                retention, through cross-selling of additional products such as
                insurance, selling further advances to existing borrowers and
                testing other retention strategies;</span></font></li>
            </ul>
            <ul>
              <li>
                <p class="MsoNormal" style="mso-layout-grid-align:none;text-autospace:none"><font face="Verdana" size="2"><span style="mso-ansi-language: EN-US">forming
                relationships with traditional lenders to review their declined
                applications:</span></font></li>
            </ul>
            <ul>
              <li>
                <p class="MsoNormal" style="mso-layout-grid-align:none;text-autospace:none"><font face="Verdana" size="2"><span style="mso-ansi-language: EN-US">developing
                additional distribution channels to target other segments of the
                non-conforming market; and</span></font></li>
            </ul>
            <ul>
              <li>
                <p class="MsoNormal" style="mso-layout-grid-align:none;text-autospace:none"><font face="Verdana" size="2"><span style="mso-ansi-language: EN-US">reviewing
                the business processes of Kensington (including those activities
                outsourced to third party service providers) to improve
                cost-effectiveness and speed.<o:p>
                </o:p>
                </span></font></li>
            </ul>
              <p><font face="Verdana" size="2"><span style="mso-fareast-font-family: Times New Roman; mso-bidi-font-family: Times New Roman; mso-ansi-language: EN-US; mso-fareast-language: EN-US; mso-bidi-language: AR-SA">The
              Directors believe that Kensington is also well positioned to
              benefit from external changes in the mortgage market as a result
              of consolidation and the increased participation of traditional
              lenders seeking commission income through referring non-conforming
              applicants to leading specialist lenders such as Kensington</span></font></p>
