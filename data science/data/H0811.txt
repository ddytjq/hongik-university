<b>Product Description:</b><br>
The PowerLab/4SP is a high-performance recording unit for both Life and Physical Sciences. It is powerful and flexible enough to perform a wide range of experiments. 
<p>
The high-performance PowerLab/4SP features:
<ul><Li>4 general-purpose analog input channels 
<li>single-ended and differential inputs 
<li>2 independent stimulator outputs 
<li>external trigger input and signal triggering 
<li>a wide range of high-pass and low-pass filters, and 
<li>4 pod connectors. </ul>
The PowerLab/4SP connects via SCSI or USB to computers running Windows or the MacOS, and can record at up to 200 000 samples per second. 
<p>
The PowerLab/4SP is shipped with PowerLab software (Chart, Scope, and Chart Extensions) and associated manuals and cables.
<p>
The strength of this system lies in its power and versatility. If lower performance is all you require, the PowerLab 20 Series is recommended.
