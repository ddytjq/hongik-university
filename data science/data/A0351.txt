        <p><span class="bodyBold">our rebuilding cost guarantee </span><br / >
          We'll pay the cost of work carried out in repairing or replacing the 
          damaged parts of the buildings. However, providing the qualifying conditions 
          shown below are met we'll pay the full rebuilding cost, subject to the 
          deduction of any applicable excess, even if it's more than the buildings 
          sum insured. </p>
        <span class="bodyNormal">
        <ul>
          <li>The home must be a house, not a flat or maisonette </li>
          <li>The home must either have walls constructed of brick, stone or concrete 
            and roofs made of tiles, slates, asbestos or concrete or have a modern 
            timber frame clad in brick and roofed in tile, slate or asbestos </li>
          <li>The buildings sum insured must be based on a professional insurance 
            valuation by a surveyor or valuer dated on or after 1st January 1981</li>
        </ul>
        </span>
        <p>The buildings sum insured at the beginning of the guarantee must not 
          exceed £250,000. It must also be set at an amount not less than the 
          estimated full rebuilding cost of the home according to the <span class="bodyItalic"> 
          Guide to house rebuilding costs for insurance valuations </span>prepared 
          by the Building Cost Information Service of the Royal Institution of 
          Chartered Surveyors or an acceptable alternative. The buildings sum 
          insured shall thereafter be adjusted in line with the House Rebuilding 
          Costs for Insurance Valuations, or similar index. If at any time the 
          home is extended, modified or improved in a way that will increase the 
          full rebuilding cost, we must be given full details and the buildings 
          sum insured adjusted accordingly. </p>
