 Engine Ver 0.31</H4><H1 class="entry">Preston North End</H1><H2 class="entry">Deepdale</H2><table class="entry" cellspacing=0 width="100%"><tr><td><H3 class="entry">The Ground</H3><table class="entry" cellspacing=0><TR><TD class="entry"><P><TABLE><TR><TD ALIGN="CENTER"><A HREF="/cgi-bin/ag.pl?club=disppic&pfname=/images/PNE-Ground1-Full.jpg" TARGET="_new"><IMG SRC="/images/PNE-Ground1-Thumb.jpg" ALT="Main Entrance" WIDTH="100" HEIGHT="75" BORDER="0" ALIGN="TEXTTOP"></A></TD></TR><TR><TD ALIGN="CENTER" class="entry"><B>Main Entrance</B></TD></TR><TR><TD ALIGN="CENTER" class="entry">Click for bigger picture</TD></TR></TABLE></P></TD><TD class="entry"><P><TABLE><TR><TD ALIGN="CENTER"><A HREF="/cgi-bin/ag.pl?club=disppic&pfname=/images/PNE-Ground2-Full.jpg" TARGET="_new"><IMG SRC="/images/PNE-Ground2-Thumb.jpg" ALT="Behind Ground" WIDTH="100" HEIGHT="75" BORDER="0" ALIGN="TEXTTOP"></A></TD></TR><TR><TD ALIGN="CENTER" class="entry"><B>Behind Ground</B></TD></TR><TR><TD ALIGN="CENTER" class="entry">Click for bigger picture</TD></TR></TABLE></P></TD><TD class="entry"><P><TABLE><TR><TD ALIGN="CENTER"><A HREF="/cgi-bin/ag.pl?club=disppic&pfname=/images/PNE-Legends-Full.jpg" TARGET="_new"><IMG SRC="/images/PNE-Legends-Thumb.jpg" ALT="Legends
" WIDTH="100" HEIGHT="75" BORDER="0" ALIGN="TEXTTOP"></A></TD></TR><TR><TD ALIGN="CENTER" class="entry"><B>Legends
</B></TD></TR><TR><TD ALIGN="CENTER" class="entry">Click for bigger picture</TD></TR></TABLE></P></TD><TD></TD></TR></TABLE><p><P>The ground is currently undergoing redevelopment and visiting fans are allocated the Pavilion Paddock and part of the Pavilion Stand 
until the ground improvements are finished. 
The Pavilion Paddock is all open terracing with a tier of seats behind (away fans are allocated some seating in this area
but not all of it)
and the front of the terrace is very close to the dug outs. Due to the redevelopment away fans are normally allocated between 1800 and 2500 tickets. 
</P><P>After the Town End has been rebuild it is expected to be the new home of visiting fans with an allocation of just under 6000 seats.
</P><P>There is food available from a single kiosk and while the pies are nothing remarkable the food is cheaper than most grounds. 
There is no alcohol available and the toilets are Portaloo's and in short supply.
</P></P><p>Click <a class="entry" href="http://uk.multimap.com/p/browse.cgi?pc=PR16RU&Title=Deepdale+(Preston+North+End)&scale=25000&cat=loc" target="_new">here</a> to see a map of the surrounding area</P></td></tr></table><BR><table class="entry" cellspacing=0 width="100%"><tr><td><H3 class="entry">Disabled</H3><p>5 wheelchair places are available on a specially built platform in the Tom
Finney in addition to places in front of the stand. The ground also has
toilets and refreshment facilities for the disabled. 
</P><P>Disabled visitors can park on 'Legends' outside the ground for &pound; 2.
</P></p></td></tr></table><BR><table class="entry" cellspacing=0 width=100%><tr><td><H3 class="entry">Pubs</H3><p><P>There are a few options around the ground for away fans to get a drink
before the game. The general opinion is that the town centre is less than
welcoming to away fans so its best to head to the ground area. 
</P><P>The Legends night club is situated in front of the ground and whilst they admit away fans for some games
its not recommended for larger games.
</P></p><TABLE><TR></TR></TABLE><H5 class="entry">The Garrison
</H5><P ALIGN=LEFT></P><TABLE><TR><TD><P><TABLE><TR><TD ALIGN="CENTER"><A HREF="/cgi-bin/ag.pl?club=disppic&pfname=/images/PNE-Garrison1-Full.jpg" TARGET="_new"><IMG SRC="/images/PNE-Garrison1-Thumb.jpg" ALT="The Garrison" WIDTH="100" HEIGHT="75" BORDER="0" ALIGN="TEXTTOP"></A></TD></TR><TR><TD ALIGN="CENTER"><B>The Garrison<B></TD></TR><TR><TD ALIGN="CENTER">Click for bigger picture</TD></TR></TABLE></P></TD><TD><P><TABLE><TR><TD ALIGN="CENTER"><A HREF="/cgi-bin/ag.pl?club=disppic&pfname=/images/PNE-Garrison2-Full.jpg" TARGET="_new"><IMG SRC="/images/PNE-Garrison2-Thumb.jpg" ALT="The Garrison
" WIDTH="100" HEIGHT="75" BORDER="0" ALIGN="TEXTTOP"></A></TD></TR><TR><TD ALIGN="CENTER"><B>The Garrison
<B></TD></TR><TR><TD ALIGN="CENTER">Click for bigger picture</TD></TR></TABLE></P></TD></TR></TABLE><p>The Garrison is situated fairly close to the ground. From the ground cross
Blackpool Road and carry on up Sir Tom Finney Way. The Garrison is on your
left at the second set of traffic lights. The Garrison lets both home and
away fans in and gets very busy on matchdays. Basic food, i.e. pie and
chips is available on matchdays and the pub also has pool and sky sports.
There is no beer garden as such but there are benches outside next to the
car park where fans can go if its a nice day. Expect the police to pop in
the check everythings ok from time to time.
</P></p><p>Click <a class="entry" href="http://uk.multimap.com/p/browse.cgi?pc=PR2 8AE 
&Title=The+Garrison
&scale=25000&cat=pub" target="_new">here</a> to see a map of the surrounding area</P><H5 class="entry">The Sumners
</H5><P ALIGN=LEFT></P><TABLE><TR><TD><P><TABLE><TR><TD ALIGN="CENTER"><A HREF="/cgi-bin/ag.pl?club=disppic&pfname=/images/PNE-Sumners-Full.jpg" TARGET="_new"><IMG SRC="/images/PNE-Sumners-Thumb.jpg" ALT="Sumners
" WIDTH="100" HEIGHT="75" BORDER="0" ALIGN="TEXTTOP"></A></TD></TR><TR><TD ALIGN="CENTER"><B>Sumners
<B></TD></TR><TR><TD ALIGN="CENTER">Click for bigger picture</TD></TR></TABLE></P></TD></TR></TABLE><p>The Sumners has is situated across the
road from The Garrison. Generally gets very busy with home fans on match
days but away fans are permitted for most games. Food is available (and
comes highly recommended) and there is a beer garden. There is a big police presence outside the pub.
</P></p><p>Click <a class="entry" href="http://uk.multimap.com/p/browse.cgi?pc=PR2 8AB 
&Title=The+Sumners
&scale=25000&cat=pub" target="_new">here</a> to see a map of the surrounding area</P><H5 class="entry">Hesketh Arms
</H5><P ALIGN=LEFT></P><TABLE><TR><TD><P><TABLE><TR><TD ALIGN="CENTER"><A HREF="/cgi-bin/ag.pl?club=disppic&pfname=/images/PNE-Hesketh1-Full.jpg" TARGET="_new"><IMG SRC="/images/PNE-Hesketh1-Thumb.jpg" ALT="The Hesketh Arms" WIDTH="100" HEIGHT="75" BORDER="0" ALIGN="TEXTTOP"></A></TD></TR><TR><TD ALIGN="CENTER"><B>The Hesketh Arms<B></TD></TR><TR><TD ALIGN="CENTER">Click for bigger picture</TD></TR></TABLE></P></TD><TD><P><TABLE><TR><TD ALIGN="CENTER"><A HREF="/cgi-bin/ag.pl?club=disppic&pfname=/images/PNE-Hesketh2-Full.jpg" TARGET="_new"><IMG SRC="/images/PNE-Hesketh2-Thumb.jpg" ALT="The Hesketh Arms
" WIDTH="100" HEIGHT="75" BORDER="0" ALIGN="TEXTTOP"></A></TD></TR><TR><TD ALIGN="CENTER"><B>The Hesketh Arms
<B></TD></TR><TR><TD ALIGN="CENTER">Click for bigger picture</TD></TR></TABLE></P></TD></TR></TABLE><p>The Hesketh Arms is situated at the top of Blackpool Road, towards the M6
junction 31 (if exiting at this junction you will drive past it to get to 
the ground). This pub always welcomes away fans but is a long walk from
the ground. There is a car park and food is served.
</P></p><p>Click <a class="entry" href="http://uk.multimap.com/p/browse.cgi?pc=PR1 4SY
&Title=Hesketh+Arms
&scale=25000&cat=pub" target="_new">here</a> to see a map of the surrounding area</P><H5 class="entry">The White Hart
</H5><P ALIGN=LEFT></P><TABLE><TR></TR></TABLE><p>The White Hart is on Watling St Road, a short way past The Garrison & The
Sumners. Away fans are welcome and food is available.
</P></p><p>Click <a class="entry" href="http://uk.multimap.com/p/browse.cgi?pc=PR2 8AD
&Title=The+White+Hart
&scale=25000&cat=pub" target="_new">here</a> to see a map of the surrounding area</P></td></tr><table><BR><table class="entry" cellspacing=0 width=100%><tr><td><H3 class="entry">Food</H3><p><P>Food is limited outside the ground with no chippies or fast food outlets
near to the ground though there are some takeaways on Deepdale Road
towards the town centre. There is also a cafe (Clare's Pantry) on Deepdale
Road which is recommended by one Preston fan.  There are usually a couple
of burger vans outside the ground or alternatively eat in the nearby pubs. There is also a chippie on Watling
Street Road, close to The Garrison and a Sainsburys's between the ground and The Garrison.
</P><P>There is also now a cafe built into the Football Museum which can get very busy on match days.
</P></p></td></tr><table><BR><table class="entry" cellspacing=0 width=100%><tr><td><H3 class="entry">Cash Machines</H3><p><P>There is a Halifax Link cash machine at the ground.
</P></p><p>Click <a class="entry" href="http://link.infonow.net/bin/findNow?CLIENT_ID=LINK_ATM" target="_new">here</A> to go to <a class="entry" HREF="http://www.link.co.uk"  TARGET="_NEW">LINKs</a> locate a cash machine page. The local postcode is PR1 6RU</P></td></tr><table><BR><table class="entry" cellspacing=0 width=100%><tr><td><H3 class="entry">Bookmakers</H3><p>Online betting is always available through <a class="entry" 
href="https://www.ladbrokes.co.uk/bet?AFF_ID=121"  TARGET="_NEW">Ladbrokes</a> by
clicking <a class="entry" href="https://www.ladbrokes.co.uk/bet?AFF_ID=121"  TARGET="_NEW">here</a>.</P></td></tr><table><BR><table class="entry" cellspacing=0 width=100%><tr><td><H3 class="entry">Directions</H3><p>If approaching from the North exit the M6 at junction 32. Keep in the left
hand lane (the right hand lane joins the M55) to exit the motorway and
then turn left at the roundabout onto the A6 Preston. Travel down here
for approx 2 miles and then turn left onto Blackpool Road (A5085) at the
set of traffic lights with a big park in front of you to the left. Take
the first right off Blackpool Road onto Sir Tom Finney Way (formerly
Deepdale Rd) and the ground is just on your left.
</P><P>If approaching from the South exit the M6 at junction 31. Follow the A59
towards Preston. After going up the hill take the second exit at the
roundabout (by the Hesketh Arms) onto Blackpool Road. After approx 1.5 
miles turn left at the traffic lights (with a large park in front of you
to the left) onto Sir Tom Finney Way and the ground is on your left.
</P></p></td></tr><table><BR><table class="entry" cellspacing=0 width=100%><tr><td><H3 class="entry">Parking</H3><p>There is very limited parking available at the ground so parking on
nearby streets is recommended.
</P></p></td></tr><table><BR><table class="entry" cellspacing=0 width=100%><tr><td><H3 class="entry">Train</H3><p>Preston is a mainline station with direct trains to London, Manchester and
Leeds. The station is quite a long way from the ground so its best to get
a taxi or a bus. If you want a bus the bus station is a ten minute walk up
Fishergate.
</P></p><p>The nearest station is Preston
. Click <a class="entry" href="http://212.87.65.227/bin/newquery.exe/en" target="_new">here</a> to check train times</p></td></tr><table><BR><table class="entry" cellspacing=0 width=100%><tr><td><H3 class="entry">Accommodation</H3><p>There are a few B &amp; B's on Garstang road (A6) which aren't to far from
the ground and about 1/2 hours walk to the town centre. The town centre 
has a large Forte hotel (The Crest) next to the bus station.
</P></p><p>Click 
<a class="entry" href="http://www.smoothhound.co.uk/preston.html"  TARGET="_NEW">
here</a> to see the local accomodation available from 
<a class="entry" href="http://www.smoothhound.co.uk"  TARGET="_NEW">  Smoothhound</a>.</p></td></tr><table><BR><table class="entry" cellspacing=0 width=100%><tr><td><H3 class="entry">Local Fans</H3><p>The majority of fans are friendly and trouble is rare although the town centre may get
a little hostile (this however is not due to the football fans in particular).
</P><P>Local rivals include Blackburn, Burnley and Bolton, but probably most disliked are neighbours
Blackpool.
</P></p></td></tr><table><BR><table class="entry" cellspacing=0 width=100%><tr><td><H3 class="entry">Local Police And Stewards</H3><p><P>The police have come in for some criticism on matchdays from local fans
and publicans. We have however received comments reporting that the stewards are
friendly and helpful. 
</P></p></td></tr><table><BR><table class="entry" cellspacing=0 width=100%><tr><td><H3 class="entry">Chants</H3><p>One our last visit the fans where noticeably loud, helped along by the acoustics 
of the two new stands, however nothing particularly unique was heard.
</P></p></td></tr><table><BR><table class="entry" cellspacing=0 width=100%><tr><td><H3 class="entry">After The Game</H3><p>Preston is a very good night out with plenty of pubs/clubs to choose from. Colours are
not advised in the town centre. The Fishergate area has many of the larger chain pubs and
the popular Tokyo Jo's night club.
</P></p></td></tr><table><BR><table class="entry" cellspacing=0 width=100%><tr><td><H3 class="entry">Local Attractions</H3><p>Preston North End is of course home to the new <a class="entry" href="http://www.nationalfootballmuseum.com">National Football Museum</a>.
</P><P>Preston town centre has a fairly decent shopping centre with a large
outdoor covered market and two indoor shopping centres, St Georges &amp;
Fishergate.
</P></p><P>You can also use the following to search for events in the area</p><BR><CENTER>
