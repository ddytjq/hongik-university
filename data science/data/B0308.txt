        <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2"><b>Standard Variable 
          Mortgage Rate of 5.05% (5.21% APR)</b></font></p>
        <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">Our standard variable 
          mortgage interest rate is currently one of the lowest in the country 
          and has been for at least the last 5 years. It is important to look 
          at the true cost of your mortgage over the whole term (the &quot;APR&quot;), 
          rather than focusing on just the headline rate.</font></p>
        <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2"><b>&pound;500 Cash 
          Bonus</b></font></p>
        <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">On completion of 
          your mortgage we will give you a &pound;500 cash bonus. This is designed 
          to help reimburse your legal costs but you can use it as you wish. If 
          you are remortgaging from another lender, you have a choice. We can 
          give you the &pound;500 cash bonus and you arrange your solicitor and 
          pay the legal fees separately, or the legal work can be carried out 
          by a solicitor on our 'Jubilee Special' remortgage panel and we will 
          pay the costs. We have agreed a special rate of &pound;300 for the legal 
          fees involved, so when your mortgage completes you will still receive 
          a &pound;200 cash bonus. Please ask for details of this service when 
          you apply (also see 'Terms'). Loans under &pound;50,000 do not qualify 
          for the cash bonus or free legal service.</font></p>
        <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2"><b>Capital Repayments 
          at no charge</b></font></p>
        <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">As long as you do 
          not fully repay your mortgage, you can make as many capital repayments 
          or monthly overpayments as you like to your mortgage without charge. 
          See Charges for details of the early repayment charge applied when your 
          mortgage is repaid in full.</font></p>
        <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2"><b>Daily Interest</b></font></p>
        <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">Interest is charged 
          daily, which means that you benefit immediately from lower interest 
          charges when making your minimum monthly payment and any overpayments.</font></p>
        <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2"><b>No Additional 
          Security Fee</b></font></p>
        <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">If the loan to value 
          on your mortgage is more than 75%, we will take out a mortgage indemnity 
          policy but we will pay the additional security (mortgage indemnity) 
          fee for you. This is of particular benefit to First Time Buyers who 
          tend to have a lower deposit and a tighter budget.</font></p>
        <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2"><b>No Valuation 
          Fee</b></font></p>
        <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">We will carry out 
          a standard valuation of the property and providing your mortgage completes, 
          we will pay for it. We will also give you a copy of the valuation report.</font></p>
        <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2"><b>Available for 
          House Purchase and Remortgage</b></font></p>
        <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">As long as you are 
          buying or remortgaging your main residence, then the 'Jubilee Special' 
          mortgage is available to you. This is, of course, subject to status, 
          valuation and our standard lending policy. Check with a Mortgage Adviser 
          to ensure that you qualify.</font><br>

          mortgage in full, within the first year of the mortgage completing. 
          This charge reduces to 3% of the original loan amount in year two; 2% 
          in year three and 1% in year four of the mortgage term.</font>
        <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">Partial capital

          apply for a mortgage you must be aged 18 or over. All loans are subject 
          to status and independent valuation. </font>
        <p align="left"><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">You 
          should ask your tax office if 'Jubilee Special' mortgage benefits are

          the 5 year discounted period, the interest rate will revert to the Society's 
          then standard variable mortgage rate of interest. Interest is calculated 
          daily.</font>
        <p align="left"><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">The 
          &pound;500 cash bonus will be sent to you within two weeks of your mortgage 
          completing. If you are remortgaging and choose our fixed price legal 
          service instead of the &pound;500 cash bonus, you will receive a cash 
          bonus of &pound;200.</font>
        <p align="left"><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">If 
          you are remortgaging and use our fixed price legal service, you need 
          to be aware that the service excludes costs incurred from registering 
          unregistered land/property or telegraphic transfer fees. If these apply, 
          you will need to meet the additional cost. If your mortgage does not 
          complete you are responsible for repaying the legal fees in full.</font>

          our Valuer.</font>
