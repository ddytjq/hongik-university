world champions with his 22nd goal for France after a jinking run through the
defence. Instead, Iceland had France on the rack early in the second half with
two goals in nine minutes, the first a superb free kick by captain Eyjoflur
Sverrisson from 30 metres into the top corner of Bernard Lama's net. Substitute
striker David Trezeguet scored France's winner when the match could have gone
either way, knocking the ball home from close range after Marcel Desailly's
shot was blocked by goalkeeper Birkir Kristinsson. </FONT></P>
