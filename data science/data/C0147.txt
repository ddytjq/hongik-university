		<td colspan="2" height="82" bgcolor="#fff8dc"><font face="Verdana,geneva,Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif" size="3"><b>Conditions</b></font>
			<ol>
				<li><font face="Verdana,geneva,Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif" size="2">This Policy of Insurance and the Schedule shall be read together as one contract and any word or expression to which a specific meaning has been attached in any part of this Policy or of the Schedule shall bear such specific meaning wherever it may appear.</font>
				<li><font face="Verdana,geneva,Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif" size="2">The Insured shall take all reasonable precautions for the safety of the Property.</font>
				<li><font face="Verdana,geneva,Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif" size="2">The Insured shall be resident in the United Kingdom and the Property shall normally be kept in the United Kingdom unless otherwise agreed. The Insured shall notify the Insurer prior to departure on any journey outside the United Kingdom which is expected to last for more than three weeks. The Insurer may as a consequence impose special terms or decline to provide cover for the duration of that journey.</font>
				<li><font face="Verdana,geneva,Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif" size="2">The Insured shall, as soon as is reasonably practicable after the happening of any loss or damage to the Property give notice thereof in writing to the Insurer before repairers are instructed and shall at his own expense deliver to the Insurer a claim in writing with such details, particulars and proofs as may be reasonably required and shall take all practicable steps for the recovery of any article claimed for. In the event of theft or accidental loss, immediate notice shall be given to the Police.</font>
				<li><font face="Verdana,geneva,Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif" size="2">If any claim shall be in any respect fraudulent or any fraudulent means or devices are used by the Insured or anyone acting on his behalf to obtain any benefit under this Policy all benefits thereunder shall be forfeited.</font>
				<li><font face="Verdana,geneva,Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif" size="2">If at the time of loss, damage or destruction there shall be any other insurance covering the same loss, damage or destruction the Insurer shall not be liable to pay or contribute more than the rateable proportion of such loss, damage or destruction.</font>
				<li><font face="Verdana,geneva,Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif" size="2">The Insurer shall be entitled if it so desires to prosecute in the name of the Insured for its own benefit any claim for indemnity and shall have full discretion in the conduct of any proceedings and the Insured shall give such information and assistance to the Insurer as it may require.</font>
				<li><font face="Verdana,geneva,Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif" size="2">The Insurer may cancel this Policy by sending notice by recorded delivery letter to the Insured at his last known address and in such event the Insured shall become entitled to the return of a proportionate part of the premium corresponding to the unexpired period of insurance. A notice sent by recorded delivery letter in pursuance of this condition shall be deemed to be received at the time it should have been delivered in the ordinary course of post.</font>
				<li><font face="Verdana,geneva,Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif" size="2">If any difference shall arise as to the amount to be paid under this Policy (liability being otherwise admitted) such difference shall be referred to an arbitrator to be appointed by the parties in accordance with the statutory provision in that behalf for the time being in force. Where any difference is by this condition to be referred to arbitration the making of an award shall be a condition precedent to any right of action against the Insurer.</font>
				<li><font face="Verdana,geneva,Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif" size="2">The due observance and fulfilment of the terms, provisions and conditions of this Policy in so far as they relate to anything to be done or complied with by the Insured shall be conditions precedent to any liability of the Insurer to make any payment under this Policy.</font>
				<li><font face="Verdana,geneva,Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif" size="2">Any mis-statement concealment of any material fact, mis-representation or mis-description in any material particular renders this Policy void.</font>
				<li><font face="Verdana,geneva,Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif" size="2">This Policy is not transferable. </font>
				<li><font face="Verdana,geneva,Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif" size="2">The Insured shall notify the Insurer immediately of any change of address at which the Property is normally kept and shall provide details of security precautions at the new address.</font>
			</ol>
