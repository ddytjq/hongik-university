          FOR CASH ISAs<BR>

          <BR>

          </B>The Government has issued CAT standards for CASH ISAs. They set

          minimum standards so that you can be sure that a CAT standard ISA has

          reasonable charges, easy access and fair terms.<BR>

          <BR>

          <B>The CAT standards for CASH ISAs are:</B></FONT> </P>

        <BLOCKQUOTE>

          <P><FONT SIZE="2" FACE="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" COLOR="000000"><B>Charges</B><BR>

            No one-off or regular charges of any kind, for example, no charge

            for withdrawls or for any regular service (such as use of ATMs), except

            that charges for replacements (such as duplicate statements, lost

            cards) are permitted.<BR>

            <B><BR>

            Access</B><BR>

            Minimum transaction size no greater than &pound;10. Withdrawls within

            seven working days or less.<BR>

	<B><BR>Terms</B> <BR>

	Interest rate no lower than two percentage points below base rate, which is 
	currently 4.00%.

	Upward interest rate changes to reflect base rate movements within
	a calendar month. Downward changes may be slower. No other conditions,
	for example, no limits on frequency of withdrawals.</FONT></P>

        </BLOCKQUOTE>
