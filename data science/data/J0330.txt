<H1>Oval Tracks</H1>
<P>
This section is just starting out; be patient with us. There are a great
many oval tracks in the US (especially dirt/clay ovals),
and thus a great deal of work to be done.
</p>
<p>
5/3/01 Just added: Weather Channel Links for the
Southeastern and Northeastern Paved Ovals.
Be patient, the rest will follow.
</p>

cup racing at the <A href="http://www.nascar.com/TRACKS/">official NASCAR web
site</A>.
