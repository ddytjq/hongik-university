      <h1>Acorns Children's Hospice Trust</h1>
      <p>Acorns is a children's hospice that cares for life limited children and 
        their families across the West Midlands region, although they'll welcome 
        your help no matter where you are. Acorns offers both practical help and 
        emotional support to families at home, 24 hours a day, as well as respite 
        and palliative care at one of the hospices, in Walsall and Selly Oak.</p>
      <p><img src="graphics/acorns.gif" width="186" height="69" align="left" hspace=10>A 
        registered charity, Acorns is largely dependant on donations. Acorns has 
        helped over 800 families since it was established in 1988. It is thought 
        that up to 1,000 families in the West Midlands may need help at any one 
        time.</p>
      <p>&nbsp;</p>
      <p align="center"><a href="http://www.acorns.org.uk" target="_blank">You 
        can find out more about the Acorns Children's Hospice by visiting their 
        website.</a></p>
      <h6 align="center">Acorns Children's Hospice Trust is a Registered Charity, 
        Number 70859</h6>
