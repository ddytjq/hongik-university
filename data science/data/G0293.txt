<h4>
<font face="Arial,Helvetica"><font size=+2>Assembly</font></font>
<hr WIDTH="100%"></h4>
Before flying balloon-borne around Antarctica, <b>B</b><font size=-1>OOMERANG</font>
required preparation. After instrument construction, <a href="http://www.wff.nasa.gov/pages/scientificballoons.html">NASA's
Scientific Balloon Program</a> requires that the instrument be flown in
a North American test flight before going to Antarctica.&nbsp; Assembly
and testing of the instrument took place at the <a href="http://192.149.107.70/fred/index.html">National
Scientific Balloon Facility in Palestine, Texas</a>.
<p>Here are some pictures from <b>B</b><font size=-1>OOMERANG's</font>
highly successful 1997 North American test flight.&nbsp; The information
obtained during this flight resulted in science papers available on our
<a href="../../../../papers.html">publications
page</a>.
