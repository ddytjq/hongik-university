<B>Striped Bass</B> <BR ALIGN=Center>
<I>[Morone saxatilis]</I>
<P ALIGN=Left>
&nbsp; &nbsp; This large bass, which can grow to over 6ft. long can be found
throughout Long Island. It migrates to freshwater to spawn during the late
spring and early summer. It will take baits such as eel, squid, clams, worms
and a variety of lures. This fish can be trolled, drifted, from the surf,
anchored, surface or bottom fished. It is a pleasure to catch and very tasty
to eat. That is why this species is over fished. I've been very successful
with this species using a rig that I slow troll with.
