      <p>There are two types of insurance you will need to protect your home </p>
      <p><span class="bodyBold">buildings</span><br>
        Protects the structure of your property, permanent fixtures and fittings
        such as baths, fitted kitchens, fitted cupboards, and decorations such
        as wallpaper, and structures on your property such as patios,
        swimming pools, walls, fences and so on. </p>
      <p><span class="bodyBold">contents</span><br>
        Protects items in your house, furniture, furnishings, household goods,
        kitchen equipment, food and drink, TVs, video, personal effects, such
        as clothes and books, and valuables up to a certain limit specified by
        the customer. Motor vehicles, boats, aircraft, caravans will have to be
        insured separately</p>
      <p>You can choose either buildings cover, contents cover, or both. You can
      	also add accidental damage to your buildings or contents cover and include
      	personal belongings on your contents cover.</p>
      <p>And, when you take out your policy you will also be protected by our <a href="../insurance/insurance_home_faq.html#emergency">Home Emergency</a> and
	  <a href="../insurance/insurance_home_faq.html#legal">Legal Expenses</a> cover free of charge for the first year.</p><br>
