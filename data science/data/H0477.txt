<TR> <TD></TD> <TD> <OL>

<P><LI> Applied concepts   from information theory   and communication
theory to theoretically  address the problem  of neural coding, or how
neurons in the brain encode and transmit information effectively.

<P><LI> Analytically characterized  the different sources of noise  in
neurons and  compared the theoretical predictions against experimental
data.

<P><LI>  Carried   out detailed  numerical  simulations  to extend the
theoretical formalism to study neurons with complicated geometries and
morphologies  and  make falsifiable predictions for  neural processing
which can be easily compared against experimental data.

<P><LI> Assessed the functional   advantage of adaptive processing  in
limited dynamic    range   systems from     an   information-theoretic
perspective, in an  attempt  to understand the  role  of adaptation in
biological systems.
