    <td width="200%" colspan="2"><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">The Adelaide
    Magic Millions Sale was held on February 18th and 19th for the Select Sessions and
    February 20th for the Summer Session.</font><p><font face="Arial" color="#008000" size="4"><strong>Select
    Sessions</strong></font></p>
    <p><img src="003.jpg" alt="003.jpg (14187 bytes)" align="right" border="2" hspace="15" WIDTH="250" HEIGHT="262"><strong><font face="Arial" color="#008000" size="3">Lot 3 - son of the mighty OSSIE COSSIE <br>
    </font><font color="#008000" face="Viner Hand ITC" size="5">Timber Country - Ossie Cossie</font></strong></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">This colt was offered by Erinvale
    Thoroughbreds as agent for DHK Investments. He is by Timber Country (by Woodman) who won
    over US$1 million on the track including the Breeders' Cup Juvenile (G1). </font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">At stud Timber Country has sired
    stakewinners such as Mugamuchu and Admire Touch and in Australia his first crop are now
    only 2yos. </font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Ossie Cossie was a speedy grey mare who
    recorded 19 wins including the Listed Tatt's Lightning Hcp. She was trained in country NSW
    but travelled extensively in NSW, Victoria and SA including running third in the Group 2
    AJ Moir Stakes. Ossie Cossie is a half sister to Force of Destiny dam of the stakesplaced
    winner Grey Regent. </font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">This chestnut colt was purchased by Doug
    Harrison of Victoria for $42,000.</font></p>
    <p><img src="010.jpg" alt="010.jpg (12764 bytes)" align="left" border="2" hspace="15" WIDTH="250" HEIGHT="225"><strong><font face="Arial" color="#008000" size="3">Lot 10 <br>
    </font><font color="#008000" face="Viner Hand ITC" size="5">Fuji Kiseki - Palace Spirit</font></strong></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Sired by Fuji Kiseki this chestnut colt was
    offered by Oakford Thoroughbred Farm as agent. </font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Fuji Kiseki is a son of legendary sire
    Sunday Silence and was the champion 2yo colt in Japan. At stud he has sired multiple
    stakeswinners in Japan including Daitaku Riva, Tosho Andre and Tenshino Kiseki. His first
    Australian crop are only aged two but are showing plenty of promise. </font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Palace Spirit was a winner and has
    previously produced one foal to race for a winner. Palace Spirit is from the stakesplaced
    Blithe Spirit who has produced two other winners in the stakesplaced Lauriston Lass and
    Nikos both by Rubiton. The fourth dam of this colt is Kingston Rose who produced the Group
    1 stakeswinners Rose Of Kingston (dam of Kingston Rule) and Spirit of Kingston. </font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">The purchaser of this colt was Grandway
    Racehorse Company of Hong Kong for $47,500.</font></p>
    <p><img src="14.jpg" alt="14.jpg (13125 bytes)" align="right" border="2" hspace="15" WIDTH="250" HEIGHT="251"><strong><font face="Arial" color="#008000" size="3">Lot 14&nbsp; - half sister to SMYZTER'S TRISH<br>
    </font><font color="#008000" face="Viner Hand ITC" size="5">King of Kings - Patronella</font></strong></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">A member of the second crop of King of
    Kings this filly was offered by McBryde's Summerset Park Stud in South Australia and was
    purchased by Wattle Gully Stud of New South Wales for $60,000. </font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">King of Kings, a son of Sadlers Wells, was
    a multiple Group One winning champion during his racing career and his first two-year-olds
    are now racing well throughout Australia. Many of the leading trainers are among the
    buyers of youngsters by King of Kings and it's no real surprise as most are lovely types
    and are showing early signs of promise.</font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Patronella has produced three foals to race
    and all have won including the stakeswinners Smytzer's Trish and Stella Artois.</font></p>
    <p><strong><img src="15.jpg" alt="15.jpg (12455 bytes)" align="left" border="2" hspace="15" WIDTH="250" HEIGHT="228"><font face="Arial" color="#008000" size="3">Lot 15 - half brother to Group One
    winner SPARTACUS <br>
    </font><font color="#008000" face="Viner Hand ITC" size="5">Keltrice - Pazzihi</font></strong></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">A bay colt, this yearling is the 11th foal
    of Pazzhi of which 8 have raced all winners. These include the multiple Group 1
    stakeswinner and promising sire Spartacus, speedy filly Mavournae a stakeswinner of 5
    races and dam of stakesplaced winner Mavourtrice (by Keltrice), Manitaeha winner of 9
    races, Stately Bay (10 wins), Maniziah (9 wins) and The Real Deal (10 wins). </font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">He is sired by Keltrice who has produced
    stakeswinners such as Deprave, Dashing Granada, Northeast Sheila, etc and the stakesplaced
    winners Lezayre, Mavourtrice and Zarek plus winners such as Deltrice, Barossa Broadway
    Keltrita, Trimaha etc. </font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Offered by Collingrove Stud as agents this
    yearling was purchased by John Moore's Olympia Bloodstock of Hong Kong.</font></p>
    <p><img src="18.jpg" alt="18.jpg (13754 bytes)" align="right" border="2" hspace="15" WIDTH="250" HEIGHT="233"><strong><font face="Arial" color="#008000" size="3">Lot 18 - full brother to Group winner KEN CHEVAL <br>
    </font><font color="#008000" face="Viner Hand ITC" size="5">Kenvain - Perfect Idea</font></strong></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Offered by Eagle Ridge as agents and
    purchased by Stuart Gower for $44,000 this grey colt is by Kenvain from Perfect Idea. </font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Kenvain by Kenmare and out of a Vain mare
    was an out and out sprinter on the racetrack winning 6 races including the Group 1
    Oakleigh Plate. At stud he has stakeswinning sprinters and stayers including Rain Gauge
    (MV Cup), sprinter Romilada and Ken Cheval plus Sedation, Dallaeni and the ill fated Work
    and Turn. </font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Perfect Idea won 6 races and has produced 3
    foals to race for 2 winners. This colt was a full brother to the Christmas Handicap (G3)
    winner Ken Cheval.</font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">The purchaser Stuart Gower has had a great
    deal of success with the stock of Kenvain including Pray in Vain, Always Aspire, Princess
    Nicky and of course Niavnek.</font></p>
    <p><img src="26.jpg" alt="26.jpg (12952 bytes)" align="left" border="2" hspace="15" WIDTH="250" HEIGHT="226"><strong><font face="Arial" color="#008000" size="3">Lot 26 - three quarter sister to LIBERTY ROSE <br>
    </font><font color="#008000" face="Viner Hand ITC" size="5">Royal Academy - Portrait
    Collector</font></strong></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Offered by Collingrove Stud this bay filly
    is by Royal Academy who has sired stakeswinners such as Oscar Schindler, Ali Royal,
    Kenwood Melody, Honour the Name, Sarson Trail, Quays, Regal Kiss etc while her dam is the
    Sir Tristram mare Portrait Collector. </font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Portrait Collector has produced 3 foals to
    race for two winners including Final Exhibit (by Rory's Jester). </font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">The second dam of this filly is the
    stakeswinner Lady Liberty who has produced the stakeswinners Freedom Fields (who in turn
    foaled stakesplaced Liberty Hall and Cherry Garden dam of stakeswinner Liberty Rose) and
    Equal Rights (by Royal Academy). </font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">The purchaser of this filly was R Davis and
    S Yip of Queensland for $85,000.</font></p>
    <p><img src="39.jpg" alt="39.jpg (14237 bytes)" align="right" border="2" hspace="15" WIDTH="250" HEIGHT="195"><strong><font face="Arial" color="#008000" size="3">Lot 39 <br>
    </font><font color="#008000" face="Viner Hand ITC" size="5">Danzero - Pure Bright</font></strong></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Sired by Danzero this brown colt was part
    of the Rothwell Park Throughbreds as agent draft. </font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Danzero is the sire of numerous
    stakeswinners including the recently retired Fairway, Danglissa, All Courage, Miss
    Bussell, etc. </font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Pure Bright has produced 3 foals but none
    have raced as yet but she is from the Sir Tristram mare Convent who produced the
    stakeswinner Simply Believe ( dam of stakeswinner Faith Hill) and the stakesplaced Truly
    Believe. Convent was from the group 1 winner Pure Of Heart (by Godswalk) who produced 8
    winners including the stakesplaced Glowing Tribute and Lion Hunter (now promising sire). </font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">He was purchased by Rick Worthington of New
    South Wales for $82,500.</font></p>
    <p><img src="46.jpg" alt="46.jpg (15300 bytes)" align="left" border="2" hspace="15" WIDTH="250" HEIGHT="227"><strong><font face="Arial" color="#008000" size="3">Lot 46 <br>
    </font><font color="#008000" face="Viner Hand ITC" size="5">Encosta De Lago - Quisera</font></strong></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Offered by Flower Hill Stud &amp; Dr C A
    Gliddon this bay colt was purchased by Melbourne based trainer Rick Hore-Lacy for $60,000.
    </font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">He's by Encosta De Lago whose oldest
    progeny are now 3yos but he has already produced stakeswinners such as Chong Tong,
    Dragila, Gold Lottey and is the sire of this years Adelaide Magic Millions Classic winner
    Milkshake. </font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Quisera is the dam of two foals including
    this yearling and has not had any reach the track as yet. She is from Show Gallery who in
    turn is from the Showdown mare Show Day. </font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Show Day is the dam of stakeswinner
    Showmeran and of Deep Show dam of the stakeswiners Cheval Place, Snippet's Girl, Snippets'
    Show and stakesplaced Deep Snip and Show Em Sadie.</font></p>
    <p><img src="71.jpg" alt="71.jpg (13215 bytes)" align="right" border="2" hspace="15" WIDTH="250" HEIGHT="226"><strong><font face="Arial" color="#008000" size="3">Lot 71 <br>
    </font><font color="#008000" face="Viner Hand ITC" size="5">Spartacus-Ruby Dream</font></strong></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Sired by Spartacus this bay colt is the
    first foal from the Rubiton mare Ruby Dream. </font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Spartacus is doing a great job with his
    early runners throughout the country. Spar Power recently gave the sire his first
    metropolitan winner when he bolted in at Moonee Valley under the lights.</font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Ruby Dream is an unraced foal of Dream of
    Kingston (by Biscay) who is turn is from Kingston Rose dam of Rose of Kingston, Spirit of
    Kingston and Prince of Kingston. </font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Offered by Mooroduc based Fulmen Park as
    agent this colt was sold to Don Baertschiger a leading trainer in Singapore for $62,500.</font></p>
    <p><img src="77.jpg" alt="77.jpg (11850 bytes)" align="left" border="2" hspace="15" WIDTH="250" HEIGHT="208"><strong><font face="Arial" color="#008000" size="3">Lot 77 <br>
    </font><font color="#008000" face="Viner Hand ITC" size="5">Danewin - Sassie Lassie</font></strong></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">A brown filly this lot was offered by
    Emirates Park Stud Victoria and was purchased by Graeme Rogerson Bloodstock of New South
    Wales for $80,000. </font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">She is sired by Danewin who is already the
    sire of horses such as Kootoomootoo, Haste Ye Back and Bushra Gem. </font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Sassie Lassie was a winner over 1400m and
    this foal is her third though none have yet raced. </font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Sassie Lassie is from the stakeswinner Pert
    Lassie who also produced stakeswinner Cheeky Trot (dam of SW Dancer's Choice who in turn
    produced SW Catango).</font></p>
    <p><img src="88.jpg" alt="88.jpg (15267 bytes)" align="right" border="2" hspace="15" WIDTH="250" HEIGHT="241"><strong><font face="Arial" color="#008000" size="3">Lot 88 - the first foal of Group winner SHE'S
    DISCREET <br>
    </font><font color="#008000" face="Viner Hand ITC" size="5">Distorted Humor - She's
    Discreet</font></strong></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Offered by David Peacock this bay colt is
    the first foal of stakeswinner She's Discreet. </font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">She's Discreet is from Halo Again who in
    five foals also produced the stakeswinners Be Discreet, Gilded Angel, My Advocator and
    lightly raced winner Oh So Discreet. </font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Halo Again is from Cassie who also foaled
    stakeswinner Oriental Ruler and Lady Chancery dam of Group 1 winner Firing Range. </font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Distorted Humor only stood one season in
    Australia to produce this crop. He was a multiple group winner on the racetrack and his
    first foals are only just starting careers in the US. </font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">This colt was purchased by Aquanita Racing
    for $60,000.</font></p>
    <p><img src="89.jpg" alt="89.jpg (13065 bytes)" align="left" border="2" hspace="15" WIDTH="250" HEIGHT="235"><strong><font face="Arial" color="#008000" size="3">Lot 89 <br>
    </font><font color="#008000" face="Viner Hand ITC" size="5">Red Ransom - Shiveen</font></strong></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Purchased by Mark Kavanagh for $60,000 this

    Ransom's first Australian crop. </font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Red Ransom has produced numerous
    stakeswinners in the Northern Hemisphere including Perfect Sting, Sri Pekan, Intikhab,
    Petrouchka, Ruby Ransom, etc. </font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">This colt is from Vain mare Shiveen who has
    had one foal race for one winner and is from the Soy Numero Uno (by Damascus) mare Aveen.
    Aveen is from My Nord who is best known is Australia as the dam of At Talaq, Melbourne Cup
    winner and successful sire, but she also produced the stakeswinner of 12 races Annoconnor.</font></p>
    <p><img src="106.jpg" alt="106.jpg (13626 bytes)" align="right" border="2" hspace="15" WIDTH="250" HEIGHT="240"><strong><font face="Arial" color="#008000" size="3">Lot 106 <br>

    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Offered by New South Wales' Wakefield Stud
    this bay colt is by Woodman who has sired stakeswinners such as Hector Protector, Timber
    Country, Way of Light, Bosra Sham etc though his local progeny are only currently
    three-year-olds. </font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Stellare has produced one foal to race for
    a winner and is from Shamayil who has also produced the Group 1 stakeswinner Shame dam of
    stakesplaced 3yo Evander. </font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">This yearling was purchased by Computer
    Source Australia for $32,500.</font></p>
    <p><img src="111.jpg" alt="111.jpg (14108 bytes)" align="left" border="2" hspace="15" WIDTH="250" HEIGHT="206"><strong><font face="Arial" color="#008000" size="3">Lot 111 <br>

    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">A chestnut colt like his sire this colt is
    from Canadian mare Sweet Prancer. </font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Sweet Prancer has produced one foal to race
    who is a multiple winner in the US. The fourth dam of this colt is the Buckpasser mare Sex
    Appeal who produced champion UK gallopers El Gran Senor and try My Best plus the
    stakeswinning filly Solar amongst her 7 winners. </font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Ashkalani is a Group 1 stakewinners whose
    oldest Australian progeny are only 3yos but has produced stakeswinners such as Askaleta,
    Potemkin and Strawberry Blonde. </font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Offered by Greg Willis' Chatswood Stud this
    colt was purchased by J Hambrook of Queensland for $42,000.</font></p>
    <p><img src="120.jpg" alt="120.jpg (13217 bytes)" align="right" border="2" hspace="15" WIDTH="250" HEIGHT="210"><strong><font face="Arial" color="#008000" size="3">Lot 120 <br>

    the first crop of General Nediym who was the champion 3yo sprinter of his year. </font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">General Nediym first came to attention with
    victory in the Gold Coast Magic Millions and he then won numerous Group races including
    the Group 1 sprints VRC Lightning Stakes and VRC Newmarket Handicap. </font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Thunderbowl (by Palace Music) is an unraced
    foal of stakeswinner Myra's Best who has also produced stakeswinners in Forest Express and
    Waky Na (dam of Waky Nao winner of 10 races in Germany). </font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Offered by Ballymore Thoroughbreds this

    unraed dam of Australian stakeswinner Pushy Dancer. </font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">This brown colt was purchased by S
    Quinlivan of Western Australia for $47,500.</font></p>

    <p><img src="142.jpg" alt="142.jpg (11823 bytes)" align="left" border="2" hspace="15" WIDTH="250" HEIGHT="229"><strong><font face="Arial" color="#008000" size="3">Lot 142 <br>

    Wooden Star). </font></p>

    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Bailey was in form at Cheltenham Park on
    the Saturday preceeding the sale with a winning double including the Group Two Swettenham
