            <p class="contentsubhead">Protection for you and your family</p>
			<p>Nobody knows what the future will bring, but we can supply a range of cover through National Australia Life to offer you and your family peace of mind.</p>
			<p class="contenttextbold">Life Cover*</p>
			<p>The family is the most important thing in most peoples' lives and a high priority should be placed on making sure that your family will be looked after if the worst should happen to you. The right type of cover is needed. </p>
			<p>Your Yorkshire Bank financial planner can help you to decide what you need. For example, you can take out insurance on your own life, your partner on their life, or both of you together.</p>
			<p class="contenttextbold">Critical Illness Protection*</p>
			<p>A serious illness can place enormous financial strain on your family if your main source of income is stopped. With no money coming in, bills may go unpaid, while additional funds may be needed to pay for special care, equipment or alterations to your home. To protect you and your family, your financial planner can help to arrange Critical Illness Cover*** which will provide you with a lump sum should you contract one of the specific critical illnesses.</p>
			<p class="contenttextbold">Tailored Mortgage Protection</p>
			<p>If you have a mortgage you are no doubt working hard to put a roof over your family's head. So you should also consider cover to make sure it stays there.</p>
			<p>We offer the main mortgage payer(s) a range of mortgage protection options which:</p>
			<ul>
			<li>aim to repay the outstanding mortgage in the event of death or critical illness*</li>
			<li>cover up to a maximum of 125% of the monthly mortgage payments should you be unable to work due to accident, sickness or unemployment.**</li>
			</ul>
