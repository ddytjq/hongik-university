      <h1>Charity and Community at Birmingham Midshires</h1>

      <p><img src="../../stickpic/winning.gif" width=150 height=150 align="right">At 
        Birmingham Midshires we are committed to putting something back into the 
        communities in which our people and customers live.</p>
      <p>We have a number of on-going alliances with charitable and community 
        based organisations across the United Kingdom - from the National Trust 
        sponsored events, through to our work with the British Heart Foundation. 
        Linking with these organisations we offer savings accounts, mortgages 
        and insurance products that benefit both them and their supporters.</p>
      <p>If you are involved with a charitable or community organisation and would 
        like to discuss how Birmingham Midshires could assist you, please email 
        Stephen G. Derges, Head of Alliances at <a href="mailto:%20alliances@birminghammidshires.co.uk">alliances@birminghammidshires.co.uk</a>. 
        You can also telephone him on 01902 302733.</p>
