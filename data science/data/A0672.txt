 <tr><td><p class="PageTitle">Partnerships and sponsorships
      <p class="bodytext">Barclays participates in - and helps to 
    sponsor - many external strategic groups and networks concerned 
    with environmental management. We do this both to learn and 
    to contribute - for example, by providing speakers at seminars. 
    We are committed to a number of external bodies, programmes 
    and projects, including: 
      <ul>
    <li><span class="bodytext"><a href="http://www.cpi.cam.ac.uk/" class="bodylink">Business 
      and the Environment</a> - corporate sponsors and participants 
      in the training programme for senior executives developed 
      by the University of Cambridge Programme for Industry 
    <li><span class="bodytext"><a href="http://www.bba.org.uk/" class="bodylink">British Bankers' Association</a>   - founder of and ongoing commitment to the Environmental Issues   Advisory Panel and the Corporate Social Responsibility Advisory   Panel
   <li><span class="bodytext"><a href="http://www.business-in-environment.org.uk/bieframe.html" class="bodylink">Business   in the Environment</a> - active participation by Barclays Chairman   and Environmental Director in the Leadership Team and Practitioners'   Forum respectively
   <li><span class="bodytext"><a href="http://www.earthwatch.org/aboutew/corporate.html" class="bodylink">Earthwatch   Institute</a> - founder member of the Corporate Environmental   Responsibility Group
   <li><span class="bodytext"><a href="http://www.iccwbo.org/home/environment/charter.asp" class="bodylink">International   Chamber of Commerce</a> - signatory to the Business Charter for   Sustainable Development
   <li><span class="bodytext"><a href="http://www.macc2.org.uk/" class="bodylink">Making A Corporate Commitment   2</a> - Barclays is a signatory to this Government-sponsored scheme.   This commits us to improvement in the areas of CO<sub>2</sub> emissions (relating   to electricity and gas usage and business travel) and water consumption.   Each year we report our progress in meeting our targets to the   Government (<a href="progress_report_2001.doc" class="bodylink">download report</a> [23k Microsoft Word document])
   <li><span class="bodytext"><a href="http://www.abi.org.uk/forge" class="bodylink">The Forge Group</a> -   participant in joint government-industry initiative to provide   guidance on environmental management and reporting and in the   near future on Corporate Social Responsibility, specifically for   the financial services sector
   <li><span class="bodytext"><a href="http://www.unepfi.net/" class="bodylink">UN Environment Programme</a>   - signatory to the financial institutions' statement on the environment   and sustainable development, and active member of the Steering   Committee
   <li><span class="bodytext">WWF-UK - Sustainability Action Network - an active participant in this Network of individuals, not-for-profit organisations and businesses, to explore innovations and solutions for corporate sustainability that can - on a practical basis - be adopted by business
   <li><span class="bodytext"><a href="http://www.yptenc.org.uk/yeya.htm" class="bodylink">Young Environmentalist   of the Year Awards 2001</a> - one of a number of companies running   competitions for employees' children (UK based).
   <li><span class="bodytext"><a href="http://www.digital-eu.org/" class="bodylink">Digital Europe</a> - Corporate   Sponsors of this flagship pan-European study into the social and   environmental effects of ebusiness and information communication   technology
   <li><span class="bodytext"><a href="http://www.cbi.org.uk/home.html" class="bodylink">Confederation of   British Industry</a> - Ongoing commitment to the Environmental   Affairs Committee and the Environmental Liability Working Group
   <li><span class="bodytext"><a href="http://www.iema.net/" class="bodylink">Institute of Environmental Management   and Assessment (IEMA)</a> - active participant in the Institute's   professional standards panels</ul>
</td>
