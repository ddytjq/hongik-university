<td><font SIZE="+1" COLOR="#000000" FACE="ARIAL"><b>Carl Jones, </b><font SIZE="+0"><i>Crew Chief</i>
<hr SIZE="2">
<font SIZE="-1">
Carl is Crew Chief and part owner of the #20 CJ's Racing NASCAR Modified race team.
He has many years experience as crew chief for both the NASCAR Modifieds and Late Model Stock Cars.<br>
<br>
Starting his racing career as a driver, he competed in his Troyer Modified for several years.  
Some of the racetracks include Dorney Park Speedway (PA), Evergreen Raceway (PA), 
Wall Stadium (NJ), Mahoning Valley Speedway (PA), Flemington Speedway (NJ), 
Shangri-La Speedway (now, Tioga Speedway, NY), and New Smyrna Speedway (Daytona, FL).<br>
<br>
As a certified welder, Carl knows all about proper fabrication and techniques.  
Carl is also head engine man in the CJ's Racing organization.  
With assistance from a local machine shop, Carl assembles, freshens, and is responsible for all the engine packages used by CJ's Racing.<br>
<br>
As an owner of a successful local business, Carl understands the needs and desires of his sponsors and has always been flexible in helping both team and sponsor reach their goals.

</td></tr>
