<h2>
Lesson 9: Looping</h2></center>
Visual Basic allows a procedure to be repeated as many times as long as
the processor could support. This is generally called&nbsp; looping .
<br>&nbsp;
<h3>
9.1&nbsp; Do Loop</h3>
The format are
<p>a)&nbsp;&nbsp; Do While condition
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Block
of one or more VB statements
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Loop
<p>b)&nbsp;&nbsp; Do
<br>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
Block of one or more VB statements
<br>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Loop While condition
<p>c)&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Do Until condition
<br>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
Block of one or more VB statements
<br>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Loop
<p>d)&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Do
<br>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
Block of one or more VB statements
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Loop Until condition
<p>Example 9.1
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Do while counter &lt;=1000
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
num.Text=counter
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
counter =counter+1
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Loop
<p>* The above example will keep on adding until counter >1000.
<p>The above example can be rewritten as
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Do
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
num.Text=counter
<br>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
counter=counter+1
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Loop until counter>1000
<br>&nbsp;
<h3>
9.2&nbsp; For....Next Loop</h3>
The format is:
<br>&nbsp;
<blockquote><font color="#000099">For</font> counter=startNumber to endNumber
(Step increment)
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; One or more VB statements
<p><font color="#000099">Next</font>
<br><font color="#000099"></font>&nbsp;</blockquote>
<font color="#000000">Example:</font><font color="#000000"></font>
<p><font color="#000000">(a)&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; For&nbsp;
counter=1 to 10</font><font color="#000000"></font>
<p><font color="#000000">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
display.Text=counter</font>
<br><font color="#000000">&nbsp;</font>
<br><font color="#000000">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
Next</font><font color="#000000"></font>
<p><font color="#000000">(b)&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; For counter=1
to 1000 step 10</font><font color="#000000"></font>
<p><font color="#000000">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
counter=counter+1</font>
<br><font color="#000000">&nbsp;</font>
<br><font color="#000000">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
Next</font><font color="#000000"></font>
<p><font color="#000000">(c)&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; For counter=1000
to 5 step -5</font><font color="#000000"></font>
<p><font color="#000000">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
counter=counter-10</font><font color="#000000"></font>
<p><font color="#000000">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
Next</font>
<br><font color="#000000">&nbsp;</font>
