<p><font size="2" face="Times new roman"><B>
What is a Fixed Rate Tessa-Only ISA?</B><BR><BR>
A&nbsp; 'Tax Free' account fixed at 5.2% until 5 April 2005 for matured TESSA savings.&nbsp; The Ipswich
TESSA-Only ISA is not a CAT standard product
<BR><BR><B>
Withdrawal:</B>   No withdrawals are allowed during the Fixed Rate period.&nbsp;
Closure will only take place in the event of the death of the account holder.</font></p>
<p><font size="2" face="Times new roman"><b>Transfers: </b>You can transfer your
TESSA-Only ISA funds to the Ipswich Fixed Rate TESSA-Only ISA subject to the
conditions imposed by the ISA manager.&nbsp; As this is a fixed rate account we
are unable to accept transfers out.
<BR><BR><B>
Minimum investment:</B>   £500
<BR><BR><B>
Maximum investment:</B>  All or part of the capital from your maturing TESSA
into the TESSA-Only ISA. No additional investments are allowed once the account
has been opened.
<BR><BR><B>
Interest: </B>Paid on the 5 April each year and added to the ISA. Interest&nbsp;
is not subject to Personal Income Tax or Capital Gains Tax.
<BR><BR>
Our staff will be delighted to open an account for you.</font></p>
