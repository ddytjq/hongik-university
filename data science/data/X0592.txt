                  <td width="100%">
              <img border="0" src="../images/begcanoeing.gif" width="300" height="28"><BR><font face="Verdana,arial" size="2">Canoeing
              is a sport that can be enjoyed by all ages, race and ability.</font>
              <p><img border="0" src="../images/kit.gif" width="120" height="28"><BR><font face="Verdana,arial" size="2">The
              basic kit consists of a canoe, buoyancy aid and paddle.<Br>You
              can add to your kit as you move further on in canoeing (and get
              more advanced)</font></p>
              <p><img border="0" src="../images/start.gif" width="110" height="28"><BR><font face="Verdana,arial" size="2">Really you should go to your local club to start off, because there should never be more then 3 people on the water when you are canoeing because of safety. They can also provide the right equipment to start you off.<br>
              Also you could injure yourself while trying the sport unless a qualified instructor teaches you.</font></p>
              <p><img border="0" src="../images/basic.gif" width="110" height="28"><BR><font face="Verdana,arial" size="2">The basics in canoeing are as follows: -</font></p>
              <ol style="font-family: Verdana, arial; font-size: 10pt; color: #000000; font-weight: bold">
                <li><font face="Verdana,arial" size="2">	Being able to get in and out of the canoe on water and on dry land.</font></li>
                <li><font face="Verdana,arial" size="2">	Going in a straight line on the water.</font></li>
                <li><font face="Verdana,arial" size="2">	Turning the canoe while going in a straight line on the water.</font></li>
                <li><font face="Verdana,arial" size="2">	Being able to capsize, meaning, "Getting out if the canoe while upside down (under water)."</font></li>
              </ol>
              <p><font face="Verdana,arial" size="2">It's as easy as that.</font></p>
              <p><img border="0" src="../images/bcu.gif" width="180" height="28"><BR><font face="Verdana,arial" size="2">Once you have mastered the basics and want to progress in the sport there are a series of star tests you can gain from the British Canoe Union (BCU).
Each star test will teach you more manoeuvres (skills) in the sport starting from the very basics getting up to a more advance level of canoeing.
There are five stars tests in total.</font>
</p>
              <p><img border="0" src="../images/tricks.gif" width="200" height="28"><BR><font face="Verdana,arial" size="2">As you progress through the sport there are a number of tricks you will pick up that may help you.
The main trick which we at 'Watersports-extreme" think you will fine useful is a 'roll'. A roll stops you from falling out of the canoe if you end up underwater as you can learn to roll back up again.</font>
</p>
