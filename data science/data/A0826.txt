<b>Savings</b></span>
<ul class="BodyText">
 <li>an external bank account through which you can transfer money to and from your Standard Life Bank account</li>
 <li>your current bank details to hand</li>
 <li>to be at least 16 years old to apply for a savings account</li>
 <li>to have a joint external bank account if you wish to open a joint personal savings account</li>
 <li>to be a permanent resident in the UK</li>
</ul>
<p><span class="bodytext">Online applications are currently only available for Direct Access & 50 Day Notice personal savings accounts.</span></p>
<span class="bodytext"><b>Mortgages</b></span>
<ul class="BodyText">
 <li>to be at least 18 years old to apply for a mortgage</li>
 <li>to be an existing Freestyle Mortgage holder to apply for Freestyle Cash Reserve</li>
 <li>to call us once you’ve completed your initial online application so we can talk through the final stages of your application</li>
</ul>
