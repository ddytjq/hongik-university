				<td class=body colspan=2>
<P>
The Scottish Bond offers a tried and tested way of building your savings and your payout is made up of two parts. Firstly, a cash sum that is
guaranteed when your Bond matures, giving you security and automatic life cover. <P>
Secondly, your main long term growth comes from the potential addition of both annual bonuses and a final bonus on maturity. These are based on the profits of Scottish Friendly. Because we're a mutual organisation,
there are no shareholders to take a cut - so all our profits go to our policyholders. Whilst bonuses cannot be guaranteed in advance, once a bonus is added, it cannot be taken away.

The Scottish Bond includes an element of life cover.  The level of cover you receive when you take out your Scottish Bond depends on your age. And of course, the benefit of life cover means that if you die, your dependants will receive a cash sum to help them through a difficult time. <P>
