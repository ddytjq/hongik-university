<h1>Phoenix International Raceway</h1>
<h2>Introduction</h2>
<p>
Phoenix International Raceway is a 1 mile oval with a 1.51 mile
road course
that uses part of the oval, located at Baseline Road and 115th Avenue
near Phoenix, Arizona. The banking is 11 Degrees in turns 1 and 2,
and 9 degrees in turns 3 and 4.
</p>

<h2><a name="HistoricalNote">Historical Note</a></h2>
<p>
Phoenix International Raceway opened in 1964.
</p>
<p>
A number of configurations of road course have been used over the years
at PIR; the current one is entirely infield,
and the outfield sections are no longer in use.
</p>
