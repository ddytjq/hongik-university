	<P><H3>The Format For The Day</H3>

	<IMG SRC="44engines.JPG" WIDTH=282 HEIGHT=350 BORDER=0 ALIGN="LEFT" HSPACE="8" ALT="Introducing the technical aspects of the race car">
	
	<P>Each ride day is conveniently arranged into two sessions, morning and afternoon, allowing even busy people to experience hot laps around Sydney's racetracks.</P>
	<P>Each session starts with a briefing in the pit lane, introducing the technical specifications of the V8 Supercar. We then explain the safety aspects pertaining to the ride and you get to try on our custom overalls and helmets.
	Once you're suited up ready for your ride, we take photographs of you with Mal and the car and again when you're strapped in ready to go.
	Then the engine is fired up and the experience of almost 600 horsepower acceleration kicks in! The awesome braking and cornering of the V8 Supercar usually comes as huge surprise, as there is nothing in normal driving experience that can prepare you for the abilities of a purebred racecar.</P>

	<P>We welcome spectators, so be sure to invite your friends along to share in your excitement.</P>

	<P>Lunch is provided for both sessions, and is included in the price for participants.</P>

	</P>

	<H3>Frequently Asked Questions</H3>
	<IMG SRC="frontstrs.JPG" WIDTH=250 HEIGHT=328 BORDER=0 ALIGN="RIGHT" ALT="The front straight at Oran Park">
	<H6>How fast will we go?</H6>
	With a car capable of almost 300km/h, on the Oran Park South circuit, you will hit around 230km/h down the front straight in 6th gear.
	<H6>How many revs does the car do?</H6>
	V8 Supercars are limited to 7,500 rpm under acceleration (in accordance with the regulations) but on downchanges can reach 9,000 rpm or more! 
	<H6>What sort of lap times will we do?</H6>
	On the Oran Park South circuit, a typical lap time is 42.5* seconds. On the GP circuit (full track configuration), the times are around 1 minute 10.2 seconds.
	<BR><BR><FONT SIZE="-2">* These lap times are indicative only and do not take into account passengers, tyre compounds or weather and track conditions. At Oran Park, only the South circuit is used for V8 Supercar rides.</FONT>
	</P>
