          <td valign="top" width="100%" colspan="3"> 
            <p><b><span class="engfanz">Kevin Phillips</span></b><span class="engfanz"> 
              said: "I enjoyed it. A lot has been made of my not being in earlier 
              squads.&nbsp;<br>
              <br>
              "I got the chance here and I was delighted to take it and get my 
              first appearance under Sven. I was only told this morning that I 
              was in. I did a bit of team play yesterday and then I found out 
              I was playing at lunchtime.<br>
              <br>
              "No doubt I will be judged by my not scoring. It is my job to score 
              but I only got 60 minutes. I felt I played fairly well. I am happy 
              with myself."<br>
              <br>
              <b>Trevor Sinclair</b> said: "I was delighted my first start has 
              been a long time coming. I am happy to get my first cap. I thought 
              I had the beating of my full-back in the first half but I was disappointed 
              that I gave away the free-kick for their goal"<br>
              <br>
              <b>England captain David Beckham</b> said: "I suppose it was harder 
              to get ourselves up for this game because of the last one. It was 
              a carnival atmosphere out there, I was surprised by how good the 
              fans were, there was no tension and people were just enjoying themselves.<br>
              <br>
              "The manager said to us beforehand that he wanted to win the game 
              so much, and we tried to."<br>
              <br>
              The England boss professed himself satisfied with the performances 
              of the fringe players brought into the squad, but said he had not 
              seen enough of Phillips to convince him he was worth a place in 
              the World Cup squad.<br>
              <br>
