 <tr><td>    <p class="PageTitle">Independent review</p>    <p class="PageTitle"><b class="bodytext">To the Executive Committee   of Barclays PLC</b>    
      <p class="bodytext">The 2001 Social and Environmental Report 
    (the 'Report')relates to the performance of the operations 
    of Barclays PLC(the 'Group') for the period January to December 
    2001. The Report is the responsibility of, and has been approved 
    by the Executive Committee and the Board. We have specifically 
    been asked to review and report to management on the statements 
    in the Report on pages 23 to 26 relating to: 
      <ul>
    <li><span class="bodytext">The governance arrangements in place 
      for environmental management and reporting at Executive 
      level within the Group and Business Units; 
    <li><span class="bodytext">The scope of Group Policies relating 
      to management of environmental risk in commercial lending 
      in the context of the Group environmental risk profile; 
    <li><span class="bodytext">The existence of internal management 
      processes and controls which implement these Policies across 
      the parts of the Group to which they are relevant; 
    <li><span class="bodytext">The activity of BGI Ltd in respect of 
      the emerging SRI guidelines and policies; 
    <li><span class="bodytext">The implementation of the Environment, 
      Social and Ethical (ESE) Supply Chain Management Tool in 
      Barclays Sourcing Network; and 
    <li><span class="bodytext">The consistency of the information contained 
      in the Environment section of the Report based on the work 
      we have completed.</span></li>
      </ul>
      <p class="bodytext">There are no generally accepted international 
    standards for reporting or review of environmental performance 
    data or of processes to measure environmental performance. 
    We have therefore adopted a review approach that reflects 
    emerging best practice and the underlying principles within 
    international standards for assurance engagements.
      <p class="bodytext">Our review comprised:
<ul>
    <li><span class="bodytext">Interviews (15) with management at Group and business 
      level and review of available supporting documentation; 
    <li><span class="bodytext">Review and sample testing of Group lending 
      environmental risk assessment processes; 
    <li><span class="bodytext">Review of the work completed by Group 
      Internal Audit on the Group environmental governance arrangements; 
    <li><span class="bodytext">Review and sample testing of Group and 
      business level processes for the management of environmental 
      risk in the Sourcing Network; and 
    <li><span class="bodytext">Review of the final content of the environmental 
      section of this Report to assess consistency with the overall 
      findings of our work.
      </ul>
<b class="bodytext">Review Findings</b>
      <p class="bodytext">The findings of our review are consistent 
    with the information presented in the Report, concerning: 
      <ol type="a">
    <li><span class="bodytext">the status of the environmental governance structure 
      in place at Group level (page 23); 
    <li><span class="bodytext">the processes in place for managing environmental 
      risk in commercial lending (page 24); 
    <li><span class="bodytext">the adoption of SRI within BGI Ltd (page 25); and
 <li><span class="bodytext">the implementation of the ESE Supply Chain Management</span></li></ol></td>
