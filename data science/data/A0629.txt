            <p class="contentsubhead">Asset Finance</p>
            <p> 
            Good capital investment planning is a common feature of all successful 
            businesses. Profitability and growth are more likely when care is 
            taken in planning capital expenditure projects. </p>
            <p> Asset finance can 
              often be the most appropriate solution, allowing your business the 
              opportunity of acquiring capital equipment without the financial 
              restriction of outright purchase. </p>
            <p> Clydesdale Bank 
              Asset Finance* can help you acquire a diverse range of key business 
              assets including: </p>
            <ul>
              <li>Cars and light 
                commercials - from a single vehicle to an entire fleet 
              <li>Trucks and Plant 
                - from a tractor unit to an excavator 
              <li>Buses and Coaches 
                - from a minibus to a luxury coach 
              <li>Production equipment 
                - from a lathe to a printing press 
              <li>Business equipment 
                - from a photocopier to a computer network 
              <li>Farming equipment 
                - from a tractor to a harvester 
            </ul>
            <p>* provided through 
            Clydesdale Bank's subsidiary Clydesdale Bank Asset Finance Limited. 
            (1) </p>
            <p> Flexible funding 
              packages are available to meet your needs, with a choice of attractive 
              options: </p>                
            <ul>
              <li><a href="#hire" class="link">Hire 
                Purchase</a>, 
              <li><a href="#lease" class="link">Leasing 
                Facilities</a> or 
              <li><a href="#contract"  class="link">Contract 
                Hire</a> and a choice of; 
              <li>fixed or 
              <li>variable interest 
                rates. 
            </ul>
            <p>Asset Finance allows you to: </p>
            <ul>
              <li>Purchase new 
                or used capital equipment 
              <li>Improve your 
                cash flow and profitability by spreading payments 
              <li>Have full use 
                of an asset while you are paying for it 
              <li>Choose between 
                hire purchase and leasing or contract hire 
              <li>Enhance affordability 
                with a flexible initial outlay and attractive fixed and variable 
                interest rates or contract hire 
              <li>Have a tax efficient 
                package 
            </ul>
            <p>Repayments can be structured to take account of: </p>
            <ul>
              <li>cash flow 
              <li>tax position 
                
              <li>VAT arrangements 
                
              <li>capital allowances; 
                and 
              <li>capital expenditure 
                programmes. 
            </ul>
            <p>(1) Clydesdale Bank 
            Asset Finance Limited is a wholly owned subsidiary of Clydesdale Bank 
            PLC. </p>
            <p> <a name="hire" class="link"> <span class="contenttextbold">Hire 
              Purchase</span></a><br />
              A flexible product which allows the asset to be purchased over a 
              pre-determined period. This form of finance lets you spread the 
              cost of the purchase, thus preserving your cash flow for use in 
              the business. Payments can also be matched to income generated. 
              During the repayment period, you have sole use of the asset and 
              you can take full advantage of any accounting and tax benefits. 
              Ownership and title pass at the end of the repayment period when 
              you complete regular payments and exercise the option to purchase. 
              </p>
            <p> <a name="lease"><span class="contenttextbold">Leasing</span></a><br />
              Under this arrangement, Clydesdale Bank Asset Finance offers you 
              the opportunity to acquire assets without the financial constraints 
              of outright purchase. You can in many cases finance up to 100% of 
              the asset cost. Ownership of the asset never passes to you, although 
              the rentals are normally tax allowable as a business expense </p>
            <p> All lease rentals 
              are subject to VAT and at the end of the primary rental period, 
              you can continue to lease through a secondary period. Alternatively 
              with our agreement, you can dispose of the goods at the market value 
              and receive a percentage rebate of rentals in line with the sale 
              proceeds. </p>
            <p> <a name="contract"><span class="contenttextbold">Contract 
              Hire</span></a><br />
              Contract hire has become an increasingly popular method of financing 
              company car fleets and light commercial vehicles. The hire of the 
              vehicle is for an agreed term and mileage, and at the end of the 
              period the finance company disposes of it. You never actually own 
              the vehicle as ownership remains with the finance company throughout 
              the agreement. </p>
            <p> Clydesdale Bank Asset Finance Limited offer 
              contract hire through a sister company - <a href="http://www.customfleet.co.uk" target="_blank" class="link">Custom Fleet</a> - which will 
              also manage your vehicle fleet for you if you choose. Fleet management 
              services are tailored to each customer and can include Vehicle Excise 
              Duty, or taking care of scheduled servicing, maintenance, replacement 
              tyres or even arranging membership to one of the motoring organisations.
			</p>
