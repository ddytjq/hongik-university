<A NAME="brachium"><H2 ALIGN=CENTER>Brachium Musculature</H2>
<p><p align=center><A HREF="#page_top"><IMG SRC="images/colorbar_up.gif" WIDTH=504 HEIGHT=13 BORDER=0 ALT=
"------------------------====================------------------------"></A>


<BR>
<P><A NAME="coracobrachialis"><FONT SIZE=+1>Coracobrachialis</FONT>
<UL><LI>Origin: coracoid process of the scapula
<LI>Insertion: medial shaft of the humerus at about its middle
<LI>Action:
	<OL><LI>flexes the humerus
	<LI>assists to adduct the humerus</OL>
<LI>Blood: muscular branches of the brachial artery
<LI>Nerve: musculocutaneous nerve, C5,6,(C7)</UL>


<BR>
<P><A NAME="biceps brachii"><FONT SIZE=+1>Biceps brachii</FONT>
<UL><LI>Origin:
	<OL><LI>long head- supraglenoid tubercle and glenohumeral labrum
	<LI>short head- tip of the coracoid process of the scapula</OL>
<LI>Insertion:
	<OL><LI>radial tuberosity
	<LI>bicipital aponeurosis</OL>
<LI>Action:
	<OL><LI>flexes the forearm at the elbow (when supinated)
	<LI>supinates forearm from neutral
	<LI>stabilizes anterior aspect of shoulder
	<LI>flexes shoulder (weak if at all)</OL>
<LI>Blood: muscular branches of brachial artery
<LI>Nerve: musculocutaneous nerve, C5,6</UL>


<BR>
<P><A NAME="brachialis"><FONT SIZE=+1>Brachialis</FONT>
<UL><LI>Origin:
	<OL><LI>lower 1/2 of anterior humerus
	<LI>both intermuscular septa</OL>
<LI>Insertion:
	<OL><LI>ulnar tuberosity
	<LI>coronoid process of ulna slightly</OL>
<LI>Action: elbow flexion (major mover)
<LI>Blood:
	<OL><LI>muscular branches of brachial artery
	<LI>radial recurrent artery</OL>
<LI>Nerve: musculocutaneous nerve, C5,6</UL>


<BR>
<P><A NAME="triceps brachii"><FONT SIZE=+1>Triceps brachii</FONT>
<UL><LI>Origin:
	<OL><LI>long head - infraglenoid tubercle of the scapula
	<LI>lateral head - upper half of the posterior surface of the shaft of the humerus, and the upper part of the lateral intermuscular septum
	<LI>medial head - posterior shaft of humerus, distal to radial groove and both the medial and lateral intermuscular septum (deep to the long & lateral heads)</OL>
<LI>Insertion:
	<OL><LI>posterior surface of the olecranon process of the ulna
	<LI>deep fascia of the antebrachium</OL>
<LI>Action:
	<OL><LI>long - adducts the arm, extends at the shoulder, and a little elbow flexion
	<LI>lateral - extends the forearm at the elbow
	<LI>medial - extends the forearm at the elbow</OL>
<LI>Blood:
	<OL><LI>muscular branches of the brachial artery
	<LI>superior ulnar collateral artery
	<LI>profunda brachii artery</OL>
<LI>Nerve: radial nerve, C6,7</UL>


<BR>
<P><A NAME="anconeus"><FONT SIZE=+1>Anconeus</FONT>
<UL><LI>Origin: posterior surface of the lateral epicondyle of the humerus
<LI>Insertion: lateral aspect of olecranon extending to the lateral part of ulnar body
<LI>Action:
	<OL><LI>extends the forearm at the elbow
	<LI>supports the elbow when in full extension</OL>
<LI>Blood: middle collateral artery from the profunda brachii artery
<LI>Nerve: radial nerve, C7,8</UL>



<BR>

<P><BR>
<A NAME="antibrachiumflex"><H2 ALIGN=CENTER>Antibrachial Flexor Musculature</H2>
<p><p align=center><A HREF="#page_top"><IMG SRC="images/colorbar_up.gif" WIDTH=504 HEIGHT=13 BORDER=0 ALT=
"------------------------====================------------------------"></A>


<BR>
<P><A NAME="pronator teres"><FONT SIZE=+1>Pronator teres</FONT>
<UL><LI>Origin:
	<OL><LI>humeral head:
		<OL TYPE=a><LI>upper portion of medial epicondyle via the CFT (common flexor tendon)
		<LI>medial brachial intermuscular septum</OL>
	<LI>ulnar head - coronoid process of ulna
	<LI>antebrachial fascia</OL>
<LI>Insertion: lateral aspect of radius at the middle of the shaft (pronator tuberosity)
<LI>Action:
	<OL><LI>pronates forearm (during rapid or forced pronation)
	<LI>weakly flexes the elbow</OL>
<LI>Blood:
	<OL><LI>muscular branches of ulnar artery
	<LI>muscular branches of radial artery</OL>
<LI>Nerve: median nerve, C6,7</UL>


<BR>
<P><A NAME="flexor carpi radialis"><FONT SIZE=+1>Flexor carpi radialis</FONT>
<UL><LI>Origin:
	<OL><LI>medial epicondyle via the CFT (common flexor tendon)
	<LI>antebrachial fascia</OL>
<LI>Insertion: base of the 2nd and sometimes 3rd metacarpals
<LI>Action:
	<OL><LI>flexes the hand at the wrist
	<LI>radially deviates the wrist
	<LI>may assist to pronate the forearm</OL>
<LI>Blood: muscular branches of radial artery
<LI>Nerve: median nerve, C6,7</UL>


<BR>
<P><A NAME="palmaris longus"><FONT SIZE=+1>Palmaris longus</FONT>
<UL><LI>Origin:
	<OL><LI>medial epicondyle via the CFT (common flexor tendon)
	<LI>antebrachial fascia</OL>
<LI>Insertion:
	<OL><LI>central portion of the flexor retinaculum
	<LI>superficial portion of the palmar aponeurosis</OL>
<LI>Action: flexes the hand at the wrist
<LI>Blood: muscular branches of ulnar artery
<LI>Nerve: median nerve, C6,7</UL>


<BR>
<P><A NAME="flexor carpi ulnaris"><FONT SIZE=+1>Flexor carpi ulnaris</FONT>
<UL><LI>Origin:
	<OL><LI>humeral head - medial epicondyle via the CFT (common flexor tendon)
	<LI>ulnar head:
		<OL TYPE=a><LI>medial aspect of olecranon
		<LI>proximal 3/5 of dorsal ulnar shaft
		<LI>antebrachial fascia</OL></OL>
<LI>Insertion:
	<OL><LI>pisiform & hamate bones (via the pisohamate ligament)
	<LI>base of  the 5th metacarpal (via the pisometacarpal ligament)</OL>
<LI>Action:
	<OL><LI>flexes the hand at the wrist
	<LI>ulnarly deviates the wrist
	<LI>stabilizes wrist to permit powerful thumb motion</OL>
<LI>Blood: muscular branches of ulnar artery
<LI>Nerve: ulnar nerve, C8,T1</UL>


 <BR>
<P><A NAME="flexor digitorum superficialis"><FONT SIZE=+1>Flexor digitorum superficialis</FONT>
<UL><LI>Origin:
	<OL><LI>humeral-ulnar head:
		<OL TYPE=a><LI>medial epicondyle via the CFT (common flexor tendon)
		<LI>medial boarder of base of coronoid process of ulna
		<LI>medial (ulnar) collateral ligament
		<LI>antebrachial fascia</OL>
	<LI>radial head: oblique line of radius along its upper anterior boarder</OL>
<LI>Insertion: both sides of the base of each middle phalanx of the 4 fingers
<LI>Action:
	<OL><LI>flexes the proximal and middle phalanges
	<LI>flexes the wrist if fingers are extended</OL>
<LI>Blood:
	<OL><LI>muscular branches of ulnar artery
	<LI>muscular branches of radial artery</OL>
<LI>Nerve: median nerve, C7,8,T1</UL>


<BR>
<P><A NAME="flexor digitorum profundus"><FONT SIZE=+1>Flexor digitorum profundus</FONT>
<UL><LI>Origin:
	<OL><LI>anterior & medial surface of upper 3/4 ulna
	<LI>adjacent interosseous membrane</OL>
<LI>Insertion: distal phalanx of medial 4 digits (through FDS tunnel)
<LI>Action:
	<OL><LI>flexes the distal IP joints and in so doing flexes the proximal and middle IP joints
	<LI>flexes the wrist if fingers are extended</OL>
<LI>Blood:
	<OL><LI>muscular branches of the ulnar artery
	<LI>muscular branches of the radial artery
	<LI>anterior interosseous artery (from ulnar artery)</OL>
<LI>Nerve:
	<OL><LI>medial portion - ulnar nerve, C8,T1
	<LI>lateral portion - anterior interosseous branch of median nerve, C8,T1</OL></UL>


<BR>
<P><A NAME="flexor pollicis longus"><FONT SIZE=+1>Flexor pollicis longus</FONT>
<UL><LI>Origin:
	<OL><LI>middle anterior surface of the radius
	<LI>interosseous membrane
	<LI>(may also originate from lateral boarder of coronoid process
	<LI>or medial epicondyle)</OL>
<LI>Insertion: palmar aspect of base of the distal phalanx of thumb (deep to flexor retinaculum)
<LI>Action:
	<OL><LI>flexes the distal phalanx of the thumb (IP joint)
	<LI>flexes the other joints to the wrist (McP, CMc and weakly at the wrist)</OL>
<LI>Blood:
	<OL><LI>muscular branches of radial artery
	<LI>anterior interosseous artery</OL>
<LI>Nerve: anterior interosseous branch of median nerve, C8,T1</UL>


<BR>
<P><A NAME="pronator quadratus"><FONT SIZE=+1>Pronator quadratus</FONT>
<UL><LI>Origin: distal 1/4 anteriomedial surface of ulna
<LI>Insertion: distal 1/4 anteriolateral surface of radius
<LI>Action: pronates the forearm and hand
<LI>Blood:
	<OL><LI>anterior interosseous artery
	<LI>muscular branches of the radial artery</OL>
<LI>Nerve: anterior interosseous branch of median nerve, C8,T1</UL>


<P><BR>
<A NAME="antibrachiumextens"><H2 ALIGN=CENTER>Antibrachial Extensor Musculature</H2>
<p><p align=center><A HREF="#page_top"><IMG SRC="images/colorbar_up.gif" WIDTH=504 HEIGHT=13 BORDER=0 ALT=
"------------------------====================------------------------"></A>

<BR>

<BR>
<P><A NAME="brachioradialis"><FONT SIZE=+1>Brachioradialis</FONT>
<UL><LI>Origin:
	<OL><LI>upper lateral supracondylar ridge of humerus (between the triceps and brachialis muscles)
	<LI>lateral intermuscular septum of humerus</OL>
<LI>Insertion:
	<OL><LI>superior aspect of styloid process of radius
	<LI>lateral side of the distal 1/2 to 1/3 of the radius
	<LI>antebrachial fascia</OL>
<LI>Action:
	<OL><LI>flexes the forearm at the elbow
	<LI>pronates the forearm when supinated
	<LI>supinates the forearm when pronated</OL>
<LI>Blood: radial recurrent artery
<LI>Nerve:
	<OL><LI>radial nerve, C5,6 OR
	<LI>deep branch of the radial nerve</OL></UL>


<BR>
<P><A NAME="extensor carpi radialis longus"><FONT SIZE=+1>Extensor carpi radialis longus</FONT>
<UL><LI>Origin:
	<OL><LI>lower lateral supracondylar ridge (below the brachioradialis)
	<LI>lateral intermuscular septum of humerus</OL>
<LI>Insertion: base of 2nd metacarpal
<LI>Action:
	<OL><LI>extends the hand at the wrist
	<LI>radially deviates the hand at the wrist
	<LI>weakly flexes the forearm at the elbow
	<LI>weakly supinates the forearm</OL>
<LI>Blood: radial recurrent artery
<LI>Nerve:
	<OL><LI>radial nerve, C5,6 OR
	<LI>deep branch of the radial nerve</OL></UL>


<BR>
<P><A NAME="extensor carpi radialis brevis"><FONT SIZE=+1>Extensor carpi radialis brevis</FONT>
<UL><LI>Origin:
	<OL><LI>lateral epicondyle via the CET (common extensor tendon)
	<LI>radial collateral ligament
	<LI>antebrachial fascia</OL>
<LI>Insertion: base of 3rd metacarpal
<LI>Action:
	<OL><LI>extends the hand at the wrist
	<LI>radially deviates the hand at the wrist</OL>
<LI>Blood: radial recurrent artery
<LI>Nerve: deep branch of the radial nerve, C6,7</UL>


<BR>
<P><A NAME="extensor digitorum"><FONT SIZE=+1>Extensor digitorum</FONT>
<UL><LI>Origin:
	<OL><LI>lateral epicondyle via the CET (common extensor tendon)
	<LI>antebrachial fascia</OL>
<LI>Insertion:
	<OL><LI>base of middle phalanx of each of the four fingers (central band)
	<LI>base of distal phalanx of each of the four fingers (2 lateral bands)</OL>
<LI>Action:
	<OL><LI>extends the four medial digits
	<LI>extends the wrist if fingers flexed
	<LI>abducts the digits (spreads the digits as it extends them)</OL>
<LI>Blood: posterior interosseous artery
<LI>Nerve: posterior interosseous nerve of the radial nerve, C6,7,8</UL>


<BR>
<P><A NAME="extensor digiti minimi"><FONT SIZE=+1>Extensor digiti minimi</FONT>
<UL><LI>Origin:
	<OL><LI>lateral epicondyl via the CET (common extensor tendon)
	<LI>antebrachial fascia
	<LI>ulnar aspect of extensor digitorum</OL>
<LI>Insertion:
	<OL><LI>base of middle phalanx of the 5th digit (central band)
	<LI>base of distal phalanx of the 5th digit (2 lateral bands)</OL>
<LI>Action:
	<OL><LI>extends the 5th digit
	<LI>abducts the 5th digit</OL>
<LI>Blood: posterior interosseous artery
<LI>Nerve: posterior interosseous nerve of the radial nerve, C6,7,8</UL>


<BR>
<P><A NAME="extensor carpi ulnaris"><FONT SIZE=+1>Extensor carpi ulnaris</FONT>
<UL><LI>Origin:
	<OL><LI>1st head - lateral epicondyle via the CET (common extensor tendon)
	<LI>2nd head - posterior body of ulna
	<LI>antebrachial fascia</OL>
<LI>Insertion: medial side of base of the 5th metacarpal
<LI>Action:
	<OL><LI>extends the hand at the wrist
	<LI>ulnarly deviates the hand at the wrist</OL>
<LI>Blood: posterior interosseous artery
<LI>Nerve: posterior interosseous nerve of the radial nerve, C6,7,8</UL>


<BR>
<P><A NAME="supinator"><FONT SIZE=+1>Supinator</FONT>
<UL><LI>Origin:
	<OL><LI>lateral epicondyle of humerus
	<LI>supinator crest of ulna
	<LI>radial collateral ligament
	<LI>annular ligament
	<LI>antebrachial fascia</OL>
<LI>Insertion: proximal portion of anteriorlateral surface of the radius
<LI>Action: supinates the forearm
<LI>Blood: radial recurrent artery
<LI>Nerve: deep branch of the radial nerve, C6</UL>


<BR>
<P><A NAME="abductor pollicis longus"><FONT SIZE=+1>Abductor pollicis longus</FONT>
<UL><LI>Origin:
	<OL><LI>posterior surfaces of ulna and radius
	<LI>interosseous membrane
	<LI>antebrachial fascia</OL>
<LI>Insertion: lateral aspect of base of 1st metacarpal
<LI>Action:
	<OL><LI>abducts the 1st metacarpal
	<LI>assists to extend & rotate the thumb
	<LI>radially deviates the hand at the wrist
	<LI>flexes the hand at the wrist</OL>
<LI>Blood: posterior interosseous artery
<LI>Nerve: posterior interosseous nerve of the radial nerve, C6,7,(C8)</UL>


<BR>
<P><A NAME="extensor pollicis brevis"><FONT SIZE=+1>Extensor pollicis brevis</FONT>
<UL><LI>Origin:
	<OL><LI>posterior surfaces of radius (below abductor pollicis longus)
	<LI>interosseous membrane
	<LI>antebrachial fascia</OL>
<LI>Insertion: base of proximal phalanx of thumb (often a slip inserts into extensor pollicis longus tendon)
<LI>Action:
	<OL><LI>extends the proximal phalanx and 1st metacarpal of the thumb
	<LI>radially deviates the hand at the wrist</OL>
<LI>Blood: posterior interosseous artery
<LI>Nerve: posterior interosseous nerve of the radial nerve, C6,7,(C8)</UL>


<BR>
<P><A NAME="extensor pollicis longus"><FONT SIZE=+1>Extensor pollicis longus</FONT>
<UL><LI>Origin:
	<OL><LI>posterior surface of ulna
	<LI>interosseous membrane
	<LI>antebrachial fascia</OL>
<LI>Insertion: distal phalanx of thumb
<LI>Action:
	<OL><LI>extends distal phalanx of thumb
	<LI>extends proximal phalanx of thumb
	<LI>assists to extend the hand at the wrist (if fingers flexed)</OL>
<LI>Blood: posterior interosseous artery
<LI>Nerve: posterior interosseous nerve of the radial nerve, C6,7,8</UL>


<BR>
<P><A NAME="extensor indicis"><FONT SIZE=+1>Extensor indicis</FONT>
<UL><LI>Origin:
	<OL><LI>posterior surface of ulna (distal to extensor pollicis longus)
	<LI>interosseous membrane
	<LI>antebrachial fascia</OL>
<LI>Insertion: base of middle and distal phalanx of the index finger
<LI>Action:
	<OL><LI>extends the 2nd digit (McP & IP joints)
	<LI>adducts the 2nd digit
	<LI>assists to extend the hand at the wrist
	<LI>stabilizes McP joint for flexion of IP solely</OL>
<LI>Blood: posterior interosseous artery
<LI>Nerve: posterior interosseous nerve of the radial nerve, C6,7,8</UL>


<P><BR>
<A NAME="hand"><H2 ALIGN=CENTER>Hand & Wrist Musculature</H2>
<p><p align=center><A HREF="#page_top"><IMG SRC="images/colorbar_up.gif" WIDTH=504 HEIGHT=13 BORDER=0 ALT=
"------------------------====================------------------------"></A>

<BR>


<BR>
<P><A NAME="abductor pollicis brevis"><FONT SIZE=+1>Abductor pollicis brevis</FONT>
<UL><LI>Origin:
	<OL><LI>distal border of flexor retinaculum
	<LI>trapezium (may be variable)</OL>
<LI>Insertion:
	<OL><LI>lateral aspect of base of proximal phalanx of the thumb
	<LI>may also send a slip to the tendon of extensor pollicis longus</OL>
<LI>Action:
	<OL><LI>abducts thumb (at the McP joint)
	<LI>participates to flex the thumb (at the McP joint)
	<LI>if attached to extensor pollicis longus, it might assist to extend the thumb</OL>
<LI>Blood: superficial palmar branches of radial artery
<LI>Nerve: recurrent branch of median nerve, C8,T1</UL>


<BR>
<P><A NAME="flexor pollicis brevis"><FONT SIZE=+1>Flexor pollicis brevis</FONT>
<UL><LI>Origin:
	<OL><LI>superficial head:
		<OL TYPE=a><LI>distal border of flexor retinaculum
		<LI>trapezium</OL>
	<LI>deep head:
		<OL TYPE=a><LI>floor of carpal tunnel
		<LI>indirectly to scaphoid & trapezium</OL></OL>
<LI>Insertion:
	<OL><LI>base of proximal phalanx of thumb
	<LI>can also attach to the lateral sesamoid bone at the McP joint</OL>
<LI>Action: powerfully flexes the thumb (at the McP joint)
<LI>Blood: superficial palmar branches of radial artery
<LI>Nerve:
	<OL><LI>superficial head - recurrent branch of median nerve,  C8,T1
	<LI>deep head - deep branch of ulnar nerve, C8,T1</OL></UL>


<BR>
<P><A NAME="opponens pollicis"><FONT SIZE=+1>Opponens pollicis</FONT>
<UL><LI>Origin:
	<OL><LI>distal border of flexor retinaculum
	<LI>trapezium</OL>
<LI>Insertion: lateral aspect of the 1st metacarpal
<LI>Action: opposes the thumb to the fingers
<LI>Blood: superficial palmar branches of radial artery
<LI>Nerve: recurrent branch of median nerve, C8,T1</UL>


<BR>
<P><A NAME="adductor pollicis"><FONT SIZE=+1>Adductor pollicis</FONT>
<UL><LI>Origin:
	<OL><LI>transverse head: 3rd metacarpal
	<LI>oblique head:
		<OL TYPE=a><LI>base of 1st, 2nd and 3rd metacarpals
		<LI>floor of carpal tunnel</OL></OL>
<LI>Insertion:
	<OL><LI>medial aspect of the base of proximal phalanx
	<LI>medial sesamoid at McP</OL>
<LI>Action:
	<OL><LI>adducts the thumb
	<LI>may assist to flex the thumb (at the McP joint)</OL>
<LI>Blood: superficial palmar branches of radial artery
<LI>Nerve: deep branch of ulnar nerve, C8,T1</UL>


<BR>
<P><A NAME="palmaris brevis"><FONT SIZE=+1>Palmaris brevis</FONT>
<UL><LI>Origin: medial margin of palmar aponeurosis
<LI>Insertion:
	<OL><LI>skin of ulnar border of palm
	<LI>may insert on the pissiform</OL>
<LI>Action: tenses the skin on the ulnar side, which is used in a grip action
<LI>Blood: superficial palmar branches of ulnar artery
<LI>Nerve: superficial branch of ulnar nerve, C8,T1</UL>


<BR>
<P><A NAME="abductor digiti minimi"><FONT SIZE=+1>Abductor digiti minimi</FONT>
<UL><LI>Origin: pisiform & tendon of flexor carpi ulnaris
<LI>Insertion:
	<OL><LI>medial aspect of the base of proximal phalanx of the 5th digit
	<LI>may send a slip to the ulnar side of the dorsal expansion</OL>
<LI>Action:
	<OL><LI>abduct 5th digit (requires pisiform stabilized by FCU)
	<LI>assists to flex the 5th digit (at McP)
	<LI>may assist in extension of 5th digit (at IP due to slips to extensor digitorum)</OL>
<LI>Blood: deep palmar branches of ulnar artery
<LI>Nerve: deep branch of ulnar nerve, C8,T1</UL>


<BR>
<P><A NAME="flexor digiti minimi brevis"><FONT SIZE=+1>Flexor digiti minimi brevis</FONT>
<UL><LI>Origin:
	<OL><LI>distal border of flexor retinaculum
	<LI>hook of the hamate</OL>
<LI>Insertion: medial aspect of the base of proximal phalanx
<LI>Action: flexes the 5th digit (at the McP joint)
<LI>Blood: deep palmar branches of ulnar artery
<LI>Nerve: deep branch of ulnar nerve, C8,T1</UL>


<BR>
<P><A NAME="opponens digiti minimi"><FONT SIZE=+1>Opponens digiti minimi</FONT>
<UL><LI>Origin:
	<OL><LI>distal border of flexor retinaculum
	<LI>hook of the hamate</OL>
<LI>Insertion: medial aspect of the 5th metacarpal
<LI>Action:
	<OL><LI>opposes the 5th digit with the thumb
	<LI>assists to "cup" the palm</OL>
<LI>Blood: deep palmar branches of ulnar artery
<LI>Nerve: deep branch of ulnar nerve, C8,T1</UL>


<BR>
<P><A NAME="palmar interossei"><FONT SIZE=+1>Palmar interossei</FONT>
<UL><LI>Origin: from the side of the metacarpal that faces the midline - to adduct them
<LI>Insertion:
	<OL><LI>on the base of the proximal phalanx of the digit of origin (same side toward the midline)
	<LI>extensor hood of the same digit(s)</OL>
<LI>Action:
	<OL><LI>adducts the fingers (hint: PAD)
	<LI>flexes the fingers (at the McP while IP joints are extended)</OL>
<LI>Blood: palmar metacarpal artery of deep palmar arch
<LI>Nerve: deep branch of ulnar nerve, C8,T1</UL>


<BR>
<P><A NAME="dorsal interossei"><FONT SIZE=+1>Dorsal interossei</FONT>
<UL><LI>Origin: between each metacarpal
<LI>Insertion:
	<OL><LI>directly distal to the origin on the base of the proximal phalanx closest to the midline (to abduct them.)
	<LI>extensor hood of the same digit(s)</OL>
<LI>Action:
	<OL><LI>abducts the fingers (hint: DAB)
	<LI>flexes the fingers (at the McP while IP joints are extended)</OL>
<LI>Blood: palmar metacarpal artery of deep palmar arch
<LI>Nerve: deep branch of ulnar nerve, C8,T1</UL>


<BR>
<P><A NAME="lumbricals"><FONT SIZE=+1>Lumbricals</FONT>
<UL><LI>Origin:
	<OL><LI>tendon of flexor digitorum profundus
	<LI>1 & 2 have a single head of origin (from radial aspect of tendon)
	<LI>3 & 4 have two heads of origin (each head from an adjacent tendon)</OL>
<LI>Insertion: extensor hood of digits 2-5
<LI>Action:
	<OL><LI>flexes the fingers (at the McP joints)
	<LI>extend IPs</OL>
<LI>Blood: palmar metacarpal arteryof deep palmar arch
<LI>Nerve:
	<OL><LI>1 & 2 - median nerve, C8,T1
	<LI>3 & 4 - deep branch of ulnar nerve, C8,T1</OL></UL>
