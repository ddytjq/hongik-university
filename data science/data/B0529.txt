<p><font size="2" face="Times new roman"><B>
What is the Premier Plus?</B><BR><BR>
A joint investment product offered by Ipswich Building Society in conjunction
with Norwich Union.&nbsp; A delicate balance between high growth potential and a
secure building society investment with a guaranteed bonus. The rates reflect our 2 Year
Term account plus a 2% BONUS in the first year.
<BR><BR><B>
Withdrawal:</B>   Up to £500 per day in cash or
£30,000 by cheque subject to 180 days loss of interest.
<BR><BR><B>Combined Minimum investment:</B>     £10,000.&nbsp;<BR>Minimum of 65% of your investment will
be placed in the Norwich Union Portfolio Bond, the remainder in our 2 Year Term account
subject to a minimum of £2,500.
<BR><BR><B>
Maximum investment:</B>  £250,000
<BR><BR><B>
Interest: </B>Paid on the 1 December each year into the account where it
immediately starts earning more interest.
<BR><BR>
To open an account, visit your local branch or telephone to arrange an
appointment with a Norwich Union cons</font><font size="2" face="Times new roman">ultant who will be able to discuss the
account with you in more detail.</font></p>
