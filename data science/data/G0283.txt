            <p><font face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">&#9;This 
              resource guide is designed to help astronomy instructors, students, 
              historians, and scholars delving into the contributions of women 
              to our culture. It is a directory of written and web-based sources 
              on the contributions of women to the science of astronomy, both 
              in the past and today. It contains both general references on the 
              topic of women&#146;s contributions to the exploration of the cosmos, 
              and resources on the lives and work of 36 specific women astronomers. 
              </font></p>
            <p><font face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">&#9;The 
              selection of the 36 women who are included (especially when it comes 
              to women working today) is somewhat arbitrary. Since one main aim 
              is to provide resources that students trying to do a term paper 
              could find with relative ease, we picked mostly on the basis of 
              the availability of at least one popular-level article, book, or 
              web-site. </font></p>
            <p><font face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">&#9;Suggestions 
              and additions are most welcome. </font></p>
