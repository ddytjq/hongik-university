    <td VALIGN="TOP"><!--mstheme--><font face="arial, helvetica, geneva"><font face="Arial, helvetica">The
      International Motor Racing Research Center at Watkins Glen holds approximately
      2500 rare and reference books; hundreds of periodical titles;
    programs; manuscript collections; historic scrapbooks; posters; historic videos, fine art,
    and many other items relating to motorsport. Individual guides and lists itemize these
    holdings.<p>A list of our <a HREF="collections/accessions.htm" target="main"><b> Holdings</b></a>
    is available, as are a list of <b>Programs </b>(sorted by <a href="collections/PROGRAMSbytitle.htm" target="main">title</a>,
      <a href="collections/PROGRAMSbyvenue.htm" target="main">venue</a>, or <a href="collections/PROGRAMSbydate.htm" target="main">date</a>)
      and of titles in
    our <b>Video Collection </b>(sorted by <a href="collections/VIDEObytitle.htm" target="main">title</a>,
      <a href="collections/VIDEObyvenue.htm" target="main">venue</a> or <a href="collections/VIDEObydate.htm" target="main">date</a>). The <b>Book List</b> is under
    development; it will be a Web searchable database. Rare films are always
      available for showing.</p>
    <h4><!--mstheme--><font color="#000000">A Note on Collection Development:<!--mstheme--></font></h4>
    <p>The Research
    Center is
    actively seeking additional documentation to enrich its material holdings. We are always
    glad to receive rare and reference books, periodicals (especially complete runs and rare
    titles), programs, prints, films, and
    posters. We are especially interested in manuscript items such as drivers' diaries; the
    research notes of motor racing journalists; club, sanctioning body, corporate, and team
    records; scrapbooks; and other unique items that express the enthusiasm and virtuosity of
    the people who have shaped motorsport. We would also appreciate historical videos from any
    genre of motorsport.</p>
