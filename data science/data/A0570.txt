which a cardholder could make over the 
6 month period from the date of account 
opening on purchases and or balance transfers. 
<BR>
Savings assume an interest rate of <B>1.9% PA
</B> is applicable for the first six months 
from the date the account is opened and 
<B>18.9% APR</B> (variable)
from the end of this 
six month period. The table assumes that 
the credit card account balance remains 
constant over the period specified and 
assumes that the minimum payment is made 
on time each month during the period specified. 
<BR>
The table also assumes that interest 
on the full balance is chargeable from 
the date the account is opened.
<BR>
The calculation of savings also assume 
that all other cards standard purchase 
rates indicated apply in the 6 month period 
when the Ulster Bank introductory offer 
applies and exclude any short term or spend 
related offers that may be on offer from 
the other financial institutions indicated. 
Comparative data as at 4 April 2002. 
Different cards have different benefits.
