FACE="arial,helvetica,sans-serif" SIZE=2>The <I>together connections</I> mortgage gives you a secured loan of up to 95% of your property's value.<P>It gives you the flexibility to pay off your mortgage early. If you'd like to pay some extra each month or if you've had a bonus or windfall and you'd prefer to overpay with a lump sum, then you can without any Early Repayment Charge.<P>Interest is calculated daily so that credits to your mortgage will immediately reduce the interest payable and shorten the term of your mortgage.<P>You have the option of taking payment holidays or of underpaying when you're short of cash (subject to our approval).<P><B>AN INTEREST RATE GUARANTEE</B><P>How can you be sure you won't be paying over the odds?<P>Because, each month, for a set period, we'll review the <I>together connections</I> rate against the Bank of England Base Rate.<P>For full details of the guarantee and how long it applies, please refer to our "<A HREF="./tcon_20whatcost.asp">What's it going to cost</A>" page.<P>What's more, after the Bank of England Base Rate guarantee, <I>together connections</I> will always be below our standard variable mortgage base rate for existing borrowers.<P><B>AS WELL AS COMPLETE PEACE OF MIND</B><P>You can also cover yourself with our payment protection insurance. This cover isn't compulsory but we do offer the first six months' premiums free.<P><B>PRODUCT OPTIONS</B><P>With <I>together connections</I>, you're free to choose any combination of the following products.<P><B>AN UNSECURED LOAN OF UP TO &#163;30,000</B><P>This is a great opportunity to pay for the holiday you've always wanted to take, to do up the house, build an extension... whatever you want.<P>Say the value of your property is &#163;100,000. You could get a <I>together connections</I> mortgage of up to &#163;95,000. And have the facility to borrow up to &#163;30,000 (up to 30% loan to value with a maximum limit of &#163;30,000) with an unsecured loan to use for anything you like.<P>You can draw on this &#163;30,000 unsecured loan as it suits you. So you might need &#163;10,000 now and hold &#163;20,000 for later. You only pay interest on the part of the loan you have actually used.<P>You don't have to work out in advance how much you'll need, or even when you'll need it. We will agree with you a limit for your cash reserve and that initial limit will always be available to you (subject to conditions).<P>By connecting the unsecured loan to the mortgage, you only pay the same competitive rate as the mortgage.        <P> <B>A SAVINGS ACCOUNT (SAVE DIRECT CONNECTIONS)</B>        <P>The Save Direct Connections Account is easy to use and makes the most of your savings. The main features of this telephone account are:<P><UL><LI>You can get round the clock information via the automated Save Direct telephone service.<LI>You can make direct transfers to your chosen bank or building society account.<LI>You can make up to six penalty free withdrawals per calendar year.<LI>We'll keep in touch with you and give you regular statements. <LI>Interest can be paid annually at the same rate as the <I>together connections</I> mortgage.</UL><P><B>A CURRENT ACCOUNT</B><P>You get a complete banking service and instant access to your money with this account.<P><UL><LI>You can withdraw cash from any one of over 19,900 LINK machines countrywide, or at any of our branches.<LI>You can use your Visa Delta payment card as an electronic cheque or as a cheque guarantee card.<LI>You'll receive regular statements.<LI>You can arrange Direct Debits/Standing Orders.<LI>And you can arrange an overdraft (subject to status).</UL><P><B>HOW DO <I>together connections</I> PRODUCTS WORK?</B><BR><B>When you choose to connect your Savings Account (Save Direct Connections) to your <I>together connections</I> borrowing there are two options:</B><P><UL><LI><B>Connections Benefit</B>. We calculate the average monthly cleared funds in your Savings Account. The average cleared funds for the month will be set against the capital balance upon which the mortgage and any unsecured loan interest is charged.<BR>The result is that you reduce the amount you pay in interest on your borrowing.<P><LI><B>Connections Interest</B>.Traditionally, you pay a higher rate of interest on your borrowing than you receive on your savings. Now, you can choose to receive annual interest on your Savings Account at the same rate as the <I>together connections</I> mortgage. Please note that this is subject to the deduction of basic rate tax, where appropriate.</UL><P><B>When you choose to connect your Current Account to your <I>together connections</I> borrowing:</B><P>You can receive Connections Benefit. This works in the same way as the  Savings Account. We calculate the average monthly cleared credit funds in your Current Account. The average cleared credit funds for the month will be set against the capital balance upon which the mortgage and unsecured loan interest is charged. The result is that you reduce the amount you pay in interest on your borrowing.<P>If you choose to use the balance in your Savings Account and/or your Current Account, against the equivalent mortgage balance and any connected unsecured loan, you can make a substantial reduction in the interest you'll be paying on your mortgage and any unsecured loan you've connected to it. You can potentially pay off your mortgage early without increasing your monthly payments and with Connections Benefit, there's no tax to pay on your savings because you agree not to receive interest.</FONT></TD></TR></TABLE><!-- Linear sequence navigation & copyright... --><TABLE WIDTH=595 BORDER=0 CELLSPACING=0 CELLPADDING=5><TR WIDTH=595>  <TD WIDTH=198 ALIGN=left>  <FONT FACE="arial,helvetica,sans-serif" SIZE=1><A HREF="./index.asp">back</A></FONT></TD>  <TD WIDTH=199 ALIGN=center>  <FONT FACE="arial,helvetica,sans-serif" SIZE=1><A HREF="#top">top of page</A></FONT></TD>  <TD WIDTH=198 ALIGN=right>  <FONT FACE="arial,helvetica,sans-serif" SIZE=1><A HREF="./tcon_02howwork.asp">next</A></FONT></TD></TR><TR WIDTH=595>  <TD COLSPAN=3 ALIGN=center>  <CENTER><FONT FACE="arial,helvetica,sans-serif" SIZE=1>&copy; Northern Rock plc 2002</FONT></CENTER></TD></TR></TABLE></CENTER></BODY></HTML>
