                <h2>For Homeowners</h2>
                <p>Prepare for the unpredictable. Protect your home, furniture 
                  and personal possessions by choosing from our range of insurance 
                  policies that will give you full peace of mind during the term 
                  of your mortgage with us.</p>
                <p>If you would like to view some frequently asked questions <a href="../FAQ/Insurance_FAQs.asp">click 
                  here</a>.</p>
                <p><a href="statement_of_cover.asp">Click here</a> to view the 
                  Cheshire's sample Home and Contents Insurance Statement of Cover 
                  and important notes.</p>
                <p>The annual cost of your cover can be paid monthly and added 
                  to your mortgage payment at no extra cost. Simply choose the 
                  cover that best suits your needs from one of the following, 
                  either by applying by post or at one of our branches.</p>
                <h3><a name="homebandc"></a>Buildings and Contents</h3>
                <p>The Society has developed specifically for our borrowers a 
                  wide range of cover within one single policy protecting your 
                  home, contents and personal possessions. </p>
                <ul>
                  <li> 
                    <p><a href="Slp.asp">Safelink Plus</a> - <i>one of the 
                      most comprehensive covers available, with the added benefit 
                      of accidental damage cover</i></p>
                  <li> 
                    <p><a href="Sls.asp">Safelink Standard</a> - <i>standard 
                      cover for your home, contents and personal possessions</i></p>
                  <li> 
                    <p><a href="Sep.asp">Safelink Economy Plus</a> - <i>cover 
                      for your home and contents, including accidental damage 
                      cover </i></p>
                  <li> 
                    <p><a href="Se.asp">Safelink Economy</a> - <i>essential 
                      home and contents cover at a realistic price</i></p>
                </ul>
                <h3><a name="homeb"></a>Buildings Only</h3>
                <p>Alternatively, you may already have cover for your contents 
                  and simply require buildings only insurance. </p>
                <ul>
                  <li> 
                    <p><a href="Bp.asp">Buildings Plus</a> - <i>with the benefit 
                      of accidental damage cover</i></p>
                  <li> 
                    <p><a href="Bs.asp">Buildings Standard</a> - <i>standard 
                      cover for your home</i></p>
                </ul>
                <h3><a name="homec"></a>Contents Only</h3>
                <p>Contents Only insurance is also available if you are unable 
                  to arrange your own buildings insurance because there is a leasehold 
                  restriction preventing you from doing this. This is often the 
                  case, for example, if you own a flat.</p>
                <ul>
                  <li> 
                    <p><a href="Cp.asp">Home Contents Plus</a> - <i>cover within 
                      a single policy protecting your home contents and personal 
                      possessions with the benefit of accidental damage cover</i></p>
                  <li> 
                    <p><a href="Cs.asp">Home Contents Standard</a> - <i>all 
                      the benefits of Home Contents Plus with the exception of 
                      accidental damage cover</i></p>
                </ul>
                <h3><a name="homeasu"></a>Accident, Sickness & Unemployment</h3>
                <p>Protect yourself and your family from the worry of accident, 
                  sickness or unemployment by taking out one of our policies. 
                </p>
                <ul>
                  <li> 
                    <p><a href="Mcfull.asp">Mortgage Care Payment Protection</a> 
                      - <i>designed to provide a monthly sum towards your mortgage 
                      payments if you are unable to work through accident, sickness 
                      or unemployment</i></p>
                    <br>
                </ul>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
                <h2>For Landlords</h2>
                <p>If you are a landlord, it is equally important that your property 
                  and any contents are protected and adequately insured. The Society 
                  has specifically developed a choice of cover tailored to suit 
                  the needs of our Buy to Let mortgage customers.</p>
                <p>The annual cost of your cover can be paid monthly and added 
                  to your mortgage payment at no extra cost. Simply choose the 
                  cover that best suits your needs from one of the following, 
                  either by applying by post or at one of our branches whilst 
                  completing your mortgage application:</p>
                <h3><a name="landbandc"></a>Buildings and Contents</h3>
                <p>There is a wide range of cover within the one policy to protect 
                  your buildings and contents. </p>
                <ul>
                  <li> 
                    <p><a href="btlbcec.asp"> Buildings and Contents (Extra Cover)</a> 
                      - <i>one of the most comprehensive covers available, with 
                      the added benefit of cover for accidental damage, theft 
                      and malicious damage caused by the tenant and a choice of 
                      content cover limits</i></p>
                  <li> 
                    <p><a href="btlbcsc.asp">Buildings and Contents (Standard 
                      Cover)</a> - <i>cover for your home and contents with a 
                      choice of contents cover limits</i></p>
                </ul>
                <h3><a name="landb"></a>Buildings Only</h3>
                <p>If your property is let unfurnished and you only require buildings 
                  cover, the following will be suitable for you.</p>
                <ul>
                  <li> 
                    <p><a href="btlboec.asp">Buildings Only (Extra Cover)</a> 
                      - <i>with the added benefit of cover for accidental damage, 
                      theft and malicious damage caused by the tenant</i></p>
                  <li> 
                    <p><a href="btlbosc.asp">Buildings Only (Standard Cover)</a> 
                      - <i>standard cover for your home</i></p>
                </ul>
                <p>Buildings and Contents insurance is provided by Royal & Sun 
                  Alliance Insurance PLC, whilst Accident, Sickness and Unemployment 
                  insurance is provided by Norwich Union.</p>
                <p>For more information on any of our insurance services get in 
                  touch by using our <b>Contact Us</b> facility in the top navigation.</p>
                <h3>What to do in the event of a complaint</h3>
                <p>Please contact our Mortgage Services Department in the first 
                  instance, either by telephone on 01625 613612 or email us by 
                  using our <a href="../Contact/contact1.asp">Contact Form</a>.</p>
