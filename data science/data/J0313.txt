1977 Dodge Monaco - 2nd Derby - It Still Lives
<BR>
360 c.i. engine
<BR>
After fixing the bad ground that failed me last year, the car was still running good.
<BR>
The upper "A" arm was bent on the driver's side, but still pretty sturdy.  I did
<BR>
some welding to strengthen it up.  I won my heat and went to the finals.  I ended up
<br>
getting 7th place.  Both rear tires went flat and the bumper broke off on one
<br>
side and started digging into the mud.  I got $99 for 7th place and sold the car
<br>
to the salvage yard for $50.
