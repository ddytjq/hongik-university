<b>An easy route to stockmarket investment</b>
<p>
Our Scottish Investment Bond offers an easy route to stockmarket investment providing professional investment management, access to your investment and a choice of funds.
<p>
It is a capital investment bond which uses our professional investment expertise to pool your money with the investments of a great number of people. This means you benefit from investments spread across a wide range of stocks and shares, cash deposits and fixed interest securities.
<p>
A building society account may be suitable for short term investments where you need access to your money but for the longer term, the stock market offers the potential of greater returns than from a building society.

<P>
We offer five investment funds designed and managed by our Investment team to help you make the most of your money varying from low to high risk investments.
<P>
<span class="body">
You should remember that the value of investments in the investment-linked funds depends directly on the value of underlying assets and can, therefore, go down as well as up.As a result there is no guarantee that your initial investment will be returned.
</span>

Managed Funds are the most popular investment linked funds.  Your money will go into a broadly based fund investing as appropriate in UK and overseas equities, fixed interest stocks, index linked securities and cash deposits.  Our managers - after fully considering the prospects for each market - decide on the mix of investment that is most likely to balance good returns with security.  They then regularly review the mix of investments to maximise efficiency while attempting to minimise the risk involved through a wide portfolio spread.</span>
