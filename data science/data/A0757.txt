So, if you’re at a stage in your life when you’d rather know exactly how much you’ll be paying on your mortgage, then a Freestyle Fixed Rate Mortgage is the option for you.</span>

<br><br>
<span class="bodytext">Just take a look at the range of features and benefits Freestyle can offer:</span>
<br><br>

We’ll contribute up to £200 towards your legal fees on completion<br />
There’s no Mortgage Indemnity Guarantee premium to pay<br /><br /></li>

<li><b>Easy access to further funds</b><br />
To find out how you can get easy access to further funds to buy anything you like at mortgage rates, just <a href="/page.php?id_link=619">click here to find out about our Freestyle Cash Reserve facility</a>.<br /><br /></li>

You can choose to have an interest only mortgage, repayment or a combination of both.</li>

When finances are tight, you can take up to two mortgage payment holidays per year, subject to our terms and conditions.<br /><br /></li>
	
<li><b>Choice of fixed or variable rates</b><br />
You can choose from our range of Freestyle fixed or variable rates or even a combination of both.  At the end of your fixed period, you can either choose another fixed rate (as long as we’re offering a fixed rate at that time), or your could change to our highly competitive Freestyle Standard Variable Rate.<br /><br /></li>
	
<li><b>Daily interest calculation</b><br />
Interest is calculated daily - allowing immediate benefit from each repayment<br /><br /></li>
</ul>
