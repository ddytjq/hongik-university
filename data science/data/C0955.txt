                                    <h1><font color="#003399" size="5" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">BIBA
                                    IT Forum launches software<br>
                                    directory for brokers</font><span lang="EN-GB" style="font-size:22.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:
normal"><o:p>
                                    </o:p>
                                    </span></h1>
                                    <h1><font face="Arial" size="4"><span style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.0pt" lang="EN-GB">First
                                    broker software directory helps brokers
                                    through maze of offerings </span></font><span style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.0pt" lang="EN-GB"><font size="3"><o:p>
                                    </o:p>
                                    </font></span></h1>
                                    <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.0pt; font-family: Arial; mso-bidi-font-family: Times New Roman; mso-bidi-font-weight: normal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.0pt; font-family: Arial; mso-bidi-font-family: Times New Roman"><font size="3">A
                                    new software directory, developed by Garol
                                    Ltd in conjunction with the BIBA IT Forum,
                                    contains the first comprehensive overview of
                                    the capabilities and functionality of most
                                    of today’s broking software offerings. It
                                    was compiled by sending a detailed
                                    questionnaire to 16 software houses
                                    providing systems to brokers, covering 18
                                    different offerings.</font></b></span><span style="font-size:11.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Arial;mso-bidi-font-family:
&quot;Times New Roman&quot;"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight:normal"><o:p>
                                    </o:p>
                                    </b></span></p>
                                    <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-size:11.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;
font-family:Arial;mso-bidi-font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">The
                                    directory</span><span style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.0pt; font-family: Arial; mso-bidi-font-family: Times New Roman"><font size="3">
                                    gives brokers</font></span><span style="font-size:11.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;
font-family:Arial;mso-bidi-font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;"> the
                                    opportunity to compare different systems and
                                    their capabilities against the needs of
                                    their own businesses. Each entry is divided
                                    into the following broad sections:<o:p>
                                    </o:p>
                                    </span></p>
                                    <p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-top:4.0pt;margin-right:0in;margin-bottom:0in;
margin-left:17.85pt;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-indent:-17.85pt;mso-list:l0 level1 lfo1;
tab-stops:list .25in"><span style="font-size:11.0pt;

                                    </span></span><span style="font-size:11.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;
font-family:Arial;mso-bidi-font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">An overview
                                    of the system, including who developed it,
                                    who to contact for more information and the
                                    broad features and benefits of the system;<o:p>
                                    </o:p>
                                    </span></p>
                                    <p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-top:4.0pt;margin-right:0in;margin-bottom:0in;
margin-left:17.85pt;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-indent:-17.85pt;mso-list:l0 level1 lfo1;
tab-stops:list .25in"><span style="font-size:11.0pt;

                                    </span></span><span style="font-size:11.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;
font-family:Arial;mso-bidi-font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">Client
                                    management information, such as what
                                    information is stored, how it is used and
                                    what searching capabilities exist;<o:p>
                                    </o:p>
                                    </span></p>
                                    <p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-top:4.0pt;margin-right:0in;margin-bottom:0in;
margin-left:17.85pt;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-indent:-17.85pt;mso-list:l0 level1 lfo1;
tab-stops:list .25in"><span style="font-size:11.0pt;
mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Wingdings;color:red">§<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    </span></span><span style="font-size:11.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;
font-family:Arial;mso-bidi-font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">The
                                    functionality of the system, covering
                                    support for EDI and reporting features;<o:p>
                                    </o:p>
                                    </span></p>
                                    <p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-top:4.0pt;margin-right:0in;margin-bottom:0in;
margin-left:17.85pt;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-indent:-17.85pt;mso-list:l0 level1 lfo1;
tab-stops:list .25in"><span style="font-size:11.0pt;
mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Wingdings;color:red">§<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    </span></span><span style="font-size:11.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;
font-family:Arial;mso-bidi-font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">An overview
                                    of how the system handles data transfer,
                                    including the impact on your business;<o:p>
                                    </o:p>
                                    </span></p>
                                    <p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-top:4.0pt;margin-right:0in;margin-bottom:0in;
margin-left:17.85pt;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-indent:-17.85pt;mso-list:l0 level1 lfo1;
tab-stops:list .25in"><span style="font-size:11.0pt;
mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Wingdings;color:red">§<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    </span></span><span style="font-size:11.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;
font-family:Arial;mso-bidi-font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">Accounting
                                    functionality, such as support for GISC
                                    practices, payment options and commissions;<o:p>
                                    </o:p>
                                    </span></p>
                                    <p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-top:4.0pt;margin-right:0in;margin-bottom:0in;

tab-stops:list .25in"><span style="font-size:11.0pt;
mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Wingdings;color:red">§<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    </span></span><span style="font-size:11.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;
font-family:Arial;mso-bidi-font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">Information
                                    on the level of support the vendor supplies,
                                    including training, response times and
                                    disaster recovery;<o:p>
                                    </o:p>
                                    </span></p>
                                    <p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-top:4.0pt;margin-right:0in;margin-bottom:0in;

tab-stops:list .25in"><span style="font-family:Wingdings;

                                    </span></span><span style="font-size:11.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;
font-family:Arial;mso-bidi-font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">Technology,
                                    which includes hardware, software and
                                    Windows integration;</span><span style="font-family:Arial;mso-bidi-font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;"><o:p>
                                    </o:p>
                                    </span></p>
                                    <p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-top:4.0pt"><span style="font-size:11.0pt;
mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Arial;mso-bidi-font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;"><br>
                                    Mike Williams, BIBA’s chief executive,
                                    said: “This CD software directory will
                                    prove invaluable to members who are
                                    inundated with the various offerings. It is
                                    often very difficult to work one’s way
                                    through the maze of systems in the market.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp;
                                    </span>We believe this will help brokers to
                                    find the best system for their business, and
                                    we are grateful to the software houses for
                                    their support and for supplying information
                                    to help us to get this project up and
                                    running. It’s the first time that all the
                                    systems have been put to the test, and we
                                    are pleased that BIBA is leading the way
                                    with this initiative.“ </span><span style="font-family:Arial;mso-bidi-font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;"><o:p>
                                    </o:p>
                                    </span></p>

                                    </span></h2>

                                    inclusive of<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp;
                                    </span>VAT and is supplied on CD ROM (please
                                    allow 7 days for delivery). For those
                                    brokers without CD reading facilities, BIBA
                                    can arrange for a paper copy to be printed
                                    and delivered (at a total cost of £150.00
                                    inclusive of VAT including post &amp;
                                    packing). Please allow 28 days for delivery.
                                    Any update information received from the
                                    Suppliers will be dealt with quarterly.<o:p>
