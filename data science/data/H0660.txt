        <p align="center"><b><font color="#0000FF" face="Comic Sans MS" size="4">Introduction:&nbsp; Why
Employ an Agent?</font>
</b></p>
        <p><font face="Comic Sans MS" color="#0000FF" size="2">
Do you ever feel like the resume you submitted to a company never actually made it to the appropriate
person?&nbsp; Most likely the hiring manager, the most important person to
review your resume, never
even saw it.&nbsp; Well, it is often
because HR departments do not possess the appropriate knowledge to differentiate
between qualified and unqualified applicants especially for highly technical jobs.&nbsp; The best companies rely heavily on hiring agents with expertise
in these fields to bring in appropriately qualified people.&nbsp; Often the jobs
are never even posted and companies routinely use agents to find the most
qualified candidates.&nbsp;&nbsp; In addition an agent can often find you the best job,
help you with the presentation of your resume and get you the best salary offer and
even in some cases where appropriate they will help you in negotiating such benefits
like hiring bonuses.<br>
        <br>
        Start with an agent we know and trust - MS People.&nbsp; Good luck in
        your search.</font></p>
