        <table border="0" cellpadding="2" cellspacing="1" width="100%">
          <tr>
            </b></font><td width="22%" class="dark" valign="top"><strong><b><font face="Arial" size="1"><a name="A" class="bookmark">All Risks</a></font></b></strong></td>
            <td width="78%" class="light" valign="top"><span class="light"><font face="Arial" size="1">Wider
            cover than given under a normal property insurance policy. Covers any loss or damage apart
            from exclusions stated in the policy. </font></span></td>
            <b><font FACE="Arial" SIZE="2">
          </tr>
          <tr>
            </font></b><td width="22%" class="dark" valign="top"><strong><b><font face="Arial" size="1">Annual Premium</font></b></strong></td>
            <td width="78%" class="light" valign="top"><span class="light"><font face="Arial" size="1">See
            &quot;Yearly Premium&quot;.</font></span></td>
            <b><font FACE="Arial" SIZE="2">
          </tr>
          <tr>
            </font></b><td width="22%" class="dark" valign="top"><strong><b><font face="Arial" size="1"><a name="B" class="bookmark">Buildings insurance</a></font></b></strong></td>
            <td width="78%" class="light" valign="top"><span class="light"><font face="Arial" size="1">A
            policy covering the structure of a house or other building against a number of different
            risks.</font></span></td>
            <b><font FACE="Arial" SIZE="2">
          </tr>
          <tr>
            </font></b><td width="22%" class="dark" valign="top"><strong><b><font face="Arial" size="1"><a name="C" class="bookmark">Car insurance</a></font></b></strong></td>
            <td width="78%" class="light" valign="top"><span class="light"><font face="Arial" size="1">Covers
            legal liabilities arising from the use of a motor vehicle. Comprehensive policies also
            cover damage to the vehicle.</font></span></td>
            <b><font FACE="Arial" SIZE="2">
          </tr>
          <tr>
            </font></b><td width="22%" class="dark" valign="top"><strong><b><font face="Arial" size="1">Certificate</font></b></strong></td>
            <td width="78%" class="light" valign="top"><span class="light"><font face="Arial" size="1">Document
            issued by insurers as evidence that insurance is in force to meet the requirements of the
            law (notably for car insurance).</font></span></td>
            <b><font FACE="Arial" SIZE="2">
          </tr>
          <tr>
            </font></b><td width="22%" class="dark" valign="top"><strong><b><font face="Arial" size="1">Claim</font></b></strong></td>
            <td width="78%" class="light" valign="top"><span class="light"><font face="Arial" size="1">When
            a policyholder or beneficiary seeks payment or settlement under the terms of a policy.</font></span></td>
            <b><font FACE="Arial" SIZE="2">
          </tr>
          <tr>
            </font></b><td width="22%" class="dark" valign="top"><strong><b><font face="Arial" size="1">Comprehensive insurance</font></b></strong></td>
            <td width="78%" class="light" valign="top"><span class="light"><font face="Arial" size="1">A
            policy covering a number of types of loss or damage. The name is used mainly in car
            insurance.</font></span></td>
            <b><font FACE="Arial" SIZE="2">
          </tr>
          <tr>
            </font></b><td width="22%" class="dark" valign="top"><strong><b><font face="Arial" size="1">Condition</font></b></strong></td>
            <td width="78%" class="light" valign="top"><span class="light"><font face="Arial" size="1">Part
            of a policy stating that certain rules must be followed, for example, the duty to take
            reasonable care to protect property, or to report claims to the insurance company
            promptly.</font></span></td>
            <b><font FACE="Arial" SIZE="2">
          </tr>
          <tr>
            </font></b><td width="22%" class="dark" valign="top"><strong><b><font face="Arial" size="1">Contents Policy</font></b></strong></td>
            <td width="78%" class="light" valign="top"><span class="light"><font face="Arial" size="1">A
            policy covering the contents of a home or other building against a number of different
            risks. </font></span></td>
            <b><font FACE="Arial" SIZE="2">
          </tr>
          <tr>
            </font></b><td width="22%" class="dark" valign="top"><strong><b><font face="Arial" size="1">Cover Note</font></b></strong></td>
            <td width="78%" class="light" valign="top"><span class="light"><font face="Arial" size="1">A
            document giving temporary evidence of cover while the policy and certificate are being
            prepared.</font></span></td>
            <b><font FACE="Arial" SIZE="2">
          </tr>
          <tr>
            </font></b><td width="22%" class="dark" valign="top"><strong><b><font face="Arial" size="1"><a name="E" class="bookmark">Endorsement</a></font></b></strong></td>
            <td width="78%" class="light" valign="top"><span class="light"><font face="Arial" size="1">A
            written amendment to an insurance policy which becomes part of it.</font></span></td>
            <b><font FACE="Arial" SIZE="2">
          </tr>
          <tr>
            </font></b><td width="22%" class="dark" valign="top"><strong><b><font face="Arial" size="1">Excess</font></b></strong></td>
            <td width="78%" class="light" valign="top"><span class="light"><font face="Arial" size="1">An
            amount of money which the policyholder has to pay towards the cost of a claim, for
            example, the first £50.</font></span></td>
            <b><font FACE="Arial" SIZE="2">
          </tr>
          <tr>
            </font></b><td width="22%" class="dark" valign="top"><strong><b><font face="Arial" size="1">Exclusion</font></b></strong></td>
            <td width="78%" class="light" valign="top"><span class="light"><font face="Arial" size="1">Specified
            property, person or event which the policy does not cover.</font></span></td>
            <b><font FACE="Arial" SIZE="2">
          </tr>
          <tr>
            </font></b><td width="22%" class="dark" valign="top"><strong><b><font face="Arial" size="1"><a name="G" class="bookmark">Green Card</a></font></b></strong></td>
            <td width="78%" class="light" valign="top"><span class="light"><font face="Arial" size="1">A
            document issued to policyholders motoring abroad as evidence that they have the minimum
            insurance cover required by the law of the country visited. Not essential for European
            travel, because minimum legal cover is automatically included in UK policies.</font></span></td>
            <b><font FACE="Arial" SIZE="2">
          </tr>
          <tr>
            </font></b><td width="22%" class="dark" valign="top"><strong><b><font face="Arial" size="1"><a name="I" class="bookmark">Insurance Premium Tax</a></font></b></strong></td>
            <td width="78%" class="light" valign="top"><span class="light"><font face="Arial" size="1">A
            tax imposed on most non-life insurance premiums.</font></span></td>
            <b><font FACE="Arial" SIZE="2">
          </tr>
          <tr>
            </font></b><td width="22%" class="dark" valign="top"><strong><b><font face="Arial" size="1">Insured</font></b></strong></td>
            <td width="78%" class="light" valign="top"><span class="light"><font face="Arial" size="1">A
            person covered by an insurance policy.</font></span></td>
            <b><font FACE="Arial" SIZE="2">
          </tr>
          <tr>
            </font></b><td width="22%" class="dark" valign="top"><strong><b><font face="Arial" size="1"><a name="L" class="bookmark">Legal Expenses insurance</a></font></b></strong></td>
            <td width="78%" class="light" valign="top"><span class="light"><font face="Arial" size="1">Covers
            the cost of legal proceedings in circumstances defined in the policy. </font></span></td>
            <b><font FACE="Arial" SIZE="2">
          </tr>
          <tr>
            </font></b><td width="22%" class="dark" valign="top"><strong><b><font face="Arial" size="1">Liability</font></b></strong></td>
            <td width="78%" class="light" valign="top"><span class="light"><font face="Arial" size="1">Legal
            responsibility for causing loss to someone else by injuring them or damaging their
            property.</font></span></td>
            <b><font FACE="Arial" SIZE="2">
          </tr>
          <tr>
            </font></b><td width="22%" class="dark" valign="top"><strong><b><font face="Arial" size="1">Loss Adjuster </font></b></strong></td>
            <td width="78%" class="light" valign="top"><span class="light"><font face="Arial" size="1">A
            person, independent of an insurance company but engaged and paid by it, who checks that a
            claim is covered and negotiates with the policyholder the amount payable for a claim.</font></span></td>
            <b><font FACE="Arial" SIZE="2">
          </tr>
          <tr>
            </font></b><td width="22%" class="dark" valign="top"><strong><b><font face="Arial" size="1"><a name="N" class="bookmark">New-For-Old</a></font></b></strong></td>
            <td width="78%" class="light" valign="top"><span class="light"><font face="Arial" size="1">Cover
            for property where an item lost or destroyed would be replaced with a brand new one, with
            no deduction for wear and tear. Also called &quot;replacement as new&quot;.</font></span></td>
            <b><font FACE="Arial" SIZE="2">
          </tr>
          <tr>
            </font></b><td width="22%" class="dark" valign="top"><strong><b><font face="Arial" size="1">No Claim Discount (or Bonus)</font></b></strong></td>
            <td width="78%" class="light" valign="top"><span class="light"><font face="Arial" size="1">A
            reduction in a renewal premium to reflect a claim-free record; used most often in motor
            insurance.</font></span></td>
            <b><font FACE="Arial" SIZE="2">
          </tr>
          <tr>
            </font></b><td width="22%" class="dark" valign="top"><strong><b><font face="Arial" size="1"><a name="P" class="bookmark">Policy</a></font></b></strong></td>
            <td width="78%" class="light" valign="top"><span class="light"><font face="Arial" size="1">The
            document providing full details of the contract between the insurer and the policyholder.</font></span></td>
            <b><font FACE="Arial" SIZE="2">
          </tr>
          <tr>
            </font></b><td width="22%" class="dark" valign="top"><strong><b><font face="Arial" size="1">Policyholder</font></b></strong></td>
            <td width="78%" class="light" valign="top"><span class="light"><font face="Arial" size="1">Person
            or organisation to whom the insurer issues the policy. Normally the person to whom
            benefits are payable.</font></span></td>
            <b><font FACE="Arial" SIZE="2">
          </tr>
          <tr>
            </font></b><td width="22%" class="dark" valign="top"><strong><b><font face="Arial" size="1">Premium</font></b></strong></td>
            <td width="78%" class="light" valign="top"><span class="light"><font face="Arial" size="1">The
            amount paid by the policyholder for insurance.</font></span></td>
            <b><font FACE="Arial" SIZE="2">
          </tr>
          <tr>
            </font></b><td width="22%" class="dark" valign="top"><strong><b><font face="Arial" size="1"><a name="R" class="bookmark">Renewal Notice</a></font></b></strong></td>
            <td width="78%" class="light" valign="top"><span class="light"><font face="Arial" size="1">Notice
            sent to the policyholder inviting him/her to renew a policy for a further period and
            stating the premium payable. </font></span></td>
            <b><font FACE="Arial" SIZE="2">
          </tr>
          <tr>
            </font></b><td width="22%" class="dark" valign="top"><strong><b><font face="Arial" size="1"><a name="S" class="bookmark">Sum Insured</a></font></b></strong></td>
            <td width="78%" class="light" valign="top"><span class="light"><font face="Arial" size="1">The
            amount for which property is insured, and the maximum amount which the insurance company
            will pay for any claim. In life insurance, the amount which is guaranteed to be paid and
            to which bonuses may be added.</font></span></td>
            <b><font FACE="Arial" SIZE="2">
          </tr>
          <tr>
            </font></b><td width="22%" class="dark" valign="top"><strong><b><font face="Arial" size="1"><a name="T" class="bookmark">Third Party</a></font></b></strong></td>
            <td width="78%" class="light" valign="top"><span class="light"><font face="Arial" size="1">Someone
            involved in a claim who is neither the policyholder nor the insurer.</font></span></td>
            <b><font FACE="Arial" SIZE="2">
          </tr>
          <tr>
            </font></b><td width="22%" class="dark" valign="top"><strong><b><font face="Arial" size="1"><a name="U" class="bookmark">Underwriter</a></font></b></strong></td>
            <td width="78%" class="light" valign="top"><span class="light"><font face="Arial" size="1">Person
            who decides whether to accept a risk and calculates the premium to be charged.</font></span></td>
