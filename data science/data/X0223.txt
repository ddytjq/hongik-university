<CENTER><P><B><I><FONT COLOR="#008080"><FONT SIZE=+1>Cricket</FONT></FONT></I></B></P></CENTER>

<CENTER><P><B><I><FONT SIZE=+1><FONT COLOR="#008080">The game of Cricket
differs from </FONT><FONT COLOR="#FF0000">&quot;301&quot;</FONT><FONT COLOR="#008080">,</FONT><FONT COLOR="#FF0000">&quot;501&quot;</FONT><FONT COLOR="#008080">
and </FONT><FONT COLOR="#FF0000">&quot;1001&quot;</FONT><FONT COLOR="#008080">
since there is an element of strategy against the opponent rather than
just individual scoring. </FONT></FONT></I></B></P></CENTER>

<CENTER><P><B><I><FONT SIZE=+1><FONT COLOR="#FF0000">1.</FONT><FONT COLOR="#008080">
The idea of the game is to </FONT><FONT COLOR="#FF0000">&quot;close&quot;</FONT><FONT COLOR="#008080">
the numbers </FONT><FONT COLOR="#FF0000">20</FONT><FONT COLOR="#008080">,</FONT><FONT COLOR="#FF0000">19</FONT><FONT COLOR="#008080">,</FONT><FONT COLOR="#FF0000">18</FONT><FONT COLOR="#008080">,</FONT><FONT COLOR="#FF0000">17</FONT><FONT COLOR="#008080">,</FONT><FONT COLOR="#FF0000">16</FONT><FONT COLOR="#008080">,</FONT><FONT COLOR="#FF0000">15</FONT><FONT COLOR="#008080">
and bullseye by scoring </FONT><FONT COLOR="#FF0000">3 hits</FONT><FONT COLOR="#008080">
in each number. These numbers including </FONT><FONT COLOR="#FF0000">bullseye</FONT><FONT COLOR="#008080">
may be closed in any order. A score in the </FONT><FONT COLOR="#FF0000">triple
</FONT><FONT COLOR="#008080">or </FONT><FONT COLOR="#FF0000">double</FONT><FONT COLOR="#008080">
area </FONT><FONT COLOR="#FF0000">scores 3</FONT><FONT COLOR="#008080">
and </FONT><FONT COLOR="#FF0000">2</FONT><FONT COLOR="#008080"> respectively
for that number.</FONT></FONT></I></B></P></CENTER>

<CENTER><P><B><I><FONT SIZE=+1><FONT COLOR="#FF0000">2.</FONT><FONT COLOR="#008080">
When </FONT><FONT COLOR="#FF0000">3 hits</FONT><FONT COLOR="#008080"> are
</FONT><FONT COLOR="#FF0000">&quot;scored&quot;</FONT><FONT COLOR="#008080">
in the number, the number is </FONT><FONT COLOR="#FF0000">&quot;closed&quot;</FONT><FONT COLOR="#008080">
and </FONT><FONT COLOR="#FF0000">additional darts score points</FONT><FONT COLOR="#008080">
only if the other player has not yet </FONT><FONT COLOR="#FF0000">scored</FONT><FONT COLOR="#008080">
his three. </FONT></FONT></I></B></P></CENTER>

<CENTER><P><B><I><FONT SIZE=+1><FONT COLOR="#FF0000">3.</FONT><FONT COLOR="#008080">
Your opponet cannot recieve points on a number you have closed, but can
prevent you from scoring additional points when he has scored three hits
and closed it. </FONT></FONT></I></B></P></CENTER>

<CENTER><P><B><I><FONT SIZE=+1><FONT COLOR="#FF0000">4.</FONT><FONT COLOR="#008080">
To win the game, all the numbers </FONT><FONT COLOR="#FF0000">20 through
15</FONT><FONT COLOR="#008080"> and </FONT><FONT COLOR="#FF0000">bullseye</FONT><FONT COLOR="#008080">
must be </FONT><FONT COLOR="#FF0000">closed </FONT><FONT COLOR="#008080">before
your opponent does. However, if a point deficit exists, it must be made
up by scoring points in his open numbers. </FONT></FONT></I></B></P></CENTER>
