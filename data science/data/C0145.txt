				<td class=body colspan=2>
<b>Tax-free savings</b> <br>
The Scottish Bond provides an easy and straightforward way to build a tax-free&#42; cash lump sum.
<p>
The Scottish Bond not only gives peace of mind for your client in the knowledge that they are saving for their future but with built in life cover they can also be sure they are providing that little bit extra in the event of their death to help dependants through a difficult time.
<p>
<span class="terms">*Tax free means free
of Income and Capital Gains Tax.  In addition, tax credits will be received
until 5 April 2004.  On maturity, proceeds are free of tax.</span>
