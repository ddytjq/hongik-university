  England slipped up by drawing only 0-0 with Portugal.&nbsp; In our final home

  but Mick Channons goal wasn't enough as the home team won 2-1.&nbsp; England
  still topped the group by one point&nbsp; but we had played one extra game,
  after we both drew 1-1 in Portugal, the Czechs only had win in Cyprus to top
  the group and they did it convincingly, 3-0.&nbsp; After that win, they went
  on to beat the USSR in the two-legged quarter finals, Holland in the semi's
  and the Germans in the final to lift the trophy.</font></p>
