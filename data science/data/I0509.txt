   <td bgcolor="#FFFFFF"><span class="newsBody"><P>Outside of the UEFA Administration based in Nyon, Switzerland, many components from all four corners of Europe make up the UEFA 'Football Family'. The directory provides a searchable list of these groups, together with information about UEFA's continental counterparts. </P>
<P><B>Associations</B><BR>From its inception in 1954, UEFA's footballing family has more than doubled from its 30 initial members to a current tally of 51 member associations. Information about all&nbsp;of the&nbsp;UEFA member associations can be accessed in the associations section. This section records the statistical profiles of each of these associations - from foundation dates to personnel and from communication information to player lists - who as member associations have all contributed to making football Europe's most popular sport. </P>
<P><B>Clubs</B><BR>The clubs section contains comprehensive information about all of the clubs currently playing in each First Division in Europe. To access the data, which includes names of officials; dates of foundation; honours won; various competition records and communication details, please use the search option in the right navigation by using the country-by-country dropdown box. </P>
<P><B>Match agents</B><BR>To easily obtain details about licensed match agents, please select a country from the right navigation to access address, telephone, fax and e-mail information. </P>
