</P>


<H2>State Representatives</H2>

<P>
IMBA State Representatives are voluteers who provide guidance and advice regarding
access issues for members and advocates within a geographical region. Reps
cultivate working relationships with land managers, public agencies, local
mountain bike advocates and other trail user groups.
</P>

<P>
View the <A HREF="near_you/state_reps.html">complete State Representatives list</A>.
</P>

<H2>IMBA National Mountain Bike Patrols</H2>

<P>
IMBA's National Mountain Bike Patrol (NMBP) program organizes and supports
bike patrols throughout the United States and around the world.
</P>

<P>
View the <A HREF="../nmbp/links.html">complete National Mountain Bike Patrol list.</A>
</P>
