<CENTER>
1977 Mercury Marquis
<BR>
400 c.i.
<br>
I put a new transmission in it and got it running fine.
<br>
I ran a figure 8 race with it.  I won my heat and advanced to the finals.
<br>
I was doing pretty good in the finals, until I got stuck on a tire and it took a little while to get off.
<br>
I ended up finishing 7th and got my first trophy.
<br>
This was the first year that the fair gave out trophys, or I'd a had a half dozen or so.
<br>
The car was in pretty good shape still, so I ran a derby with it a month later.
<br>
I ended finishing 4th in my heat, but only the top 3 advanced to the finals.
<br>
Something went in the electrical system.  I almost sold it after the derby, but ended up bringing it back home.
<br>
It is really trashed.  I will get it running again and figure 8 it again
<br>
