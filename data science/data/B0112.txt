    <td><p><font face="Verdana" size="2"><strong><a name="Product Information Sheet">Mortgage Product 
            Information</a></strong>  
                        </font></p>
            <p><font face="Verdana" size="2"><strong>Kensington Variable Rate (KVR) </strong>is the rate of interest set by us from
            time to time to be our standard variable rate.  We can vary KVR at any time, however it will never be more than 3% above 3 month LIBOR rounded up to the nearest 0.05% on the date on which it is set.  The interest charged is KVR plus the product margin shown for each product.</font></p>
      <p><font face="Verdana" size="2"><strong>Minimum age </strong>18 years at time of
      application on all products except mortgage products FFF2, FF2 and F2 (buy
      to let), which are only available to applicants aged 25 and over.</font></p>
            <p><font face="Verdana" size="2"><strong>No MIG (Mortgage Indemnity Guarantee) 
            </strong>is payable on any Kensington Mortgage Company 
            product.</font></p>
            <p><font face="Verdana" size="2"><strong>Exclusions </strong>– mortgages will not be considered on:
            shared ownership properties; freehold flats and maisonettes; flats
            above commercial properties; some properties of concrete construction; properties of 100% timber construction; properties deemed defective under the Housing Defects Act 1984 and Housing Act 1995; properties containing
            mundic block materials;
            properties with
            agricultural restrictions; high rise flats and studio flats; leasehold properties with less than 35 years remaining at the end of the mortgage term.</font></p>
 <p><font face="Verdana" size="2"><strong>CCJ's 
            </strong>will not affect product choices where 
            they are:<br>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;1&nbsp; up to £100, satisfied or not.&nbsp; 
            <br>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;2&nbsp; 
            satisfied more than 12 months ago.&nbsp; <br>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;3&nbsp; 
            registered more than 2 years ago, satisfied or not.</font><font face="Verdana" size="2">     
                <br>
            Please however ensure a 
            <strong>full</strong> explanantion of <strong>all</strong> CCJ's, <STRONG>excluding</STRONG>  
             those listed above, are submitted with the 
            application.</font></p>
            <p><font face="Verdana" size="2"><strong>Minimum income </strong>for a single applicant 
            is £10,000 p.a. Where principal applicant's income is less 
            than £10,000 p.a., a minimum joint income of £16,000 p.a. is required. 
            </font></p>
            <p><font face="Verdana" size="2"><strong>Minimum term </strong>is 5 years. Maximum 
            term is 30 years. </font></p>
      <p><font face="Verdana" size="2"><b>Applicant status</b> - mortgage products
      FFF2, FF2, F2, RIM1, RIM2 and RIM3 are not available for letting to DSS or student tenants.</font></p>
            <p><font face="Verdana" size="2"><strong>Redemption terms - </strong>generally<strong>
            </strong>5%, 4%, 3% of the amount redeemed in years 1, 2, 3 respectively
            for standard products. Thereafter, interest will be payable until the end of the month
            of redemption. Special terms apply to discounted and fixed rate
