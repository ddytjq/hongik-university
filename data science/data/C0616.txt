  <p align="center" class="body"><span class="body">The POIS Savings Bond is a 
    unit-linked savings plan for customers who have used up their tax-free savings 
    opportunities. And although the fund is taxed, if you invest for 10 years 
    or more, there are no taxes to pay on the benefits, no matter what your tax 
    rate is. </span></p>
  <p align="center" class="body"><span class="body">The Savings Bond gives you 
    an easy way to save for your family&#146;s future. With a wide range of premium 
    choices, from &pound;15 a month, it&#146;s an ideal way to save for a special 
    event. </span></p>
  <p align="center" class="body"><span class="body">Premiums are payable for a 
    minimum of 10 years with an initial maximum of 25 years. At the end of the 
    initial term, you will have the option to continue paying premiums for a further 
    10 years. This option will be repeated until the policy is encashed or there 
    is less than 10 years until the member&#146;s 85th birthday.<br>
    <br>
    </span></p>
