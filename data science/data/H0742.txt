Three major RNA polymerases			can be extracted from nuclei of cells of humans and other eukaryotes.			Single RNA polymerases can be extracted from the mitochondria			and one or two from <A HREF="MG2211.html">chloroplasts</A>. 			<LI>These enzymes vary in their sensitivity to inhibitors. (see Table)						<LI>Rat liver was perfused with tritiated uridine, an RNA precursor,			and various concentrations of a-amanitin. After several hours,			total RNA was prepared. The RNA was separately <A HREF="../MGW4/MG423.html">hybridized</A> to filters containing albumin cDNA, 5S rDNA, and 18S rDNA. The			results are plotted in the graph. 		</UL>		<CENTER>			<P><IMG SRC="i2211.gif" WIDTH="272" HEIGHT="200" NATURALSIZEFLAG="3"> </P>		</CENTER>		<UL>			<LI>The sensitivity of albumin mRNA synthesis to amanitin was also			observed with other mRNAs and with hnRNA. 			<LI>tRNA synthesis showed a sensitivity similar to that of 5S rRNA.						<LI>28S rRNA synthesis inhibition by amanitin resembled that of 18S			rRNA synthesis. 		</UL>		<CENTER>			<P><A HREF="#Facts">Facts</A> | <A HREF="#Interp">Interpretations</A> | <A HREF="#Info">Further Info.</A> | <A HREF="#bottom">Other Pages</A></P>		</CENTER>		<H4>Interpretations</H4>		<UL>			<LI><A NAME="Interp"></A>The three nuclear polymerases function in the synthesis of different			RNA molecules. RNA pol.I catalyzes the synthesis only of the 18			and 28S <A HREF="MG2323.html">rRNAs</A>. RNA pol. II makes the precursors to mRNA. RNA pol. III synthesizes			a variety of small RNAs including <A HREF="MG2322.html">tRNA</A> and 5S rRNA. 			<LI>Eukaryotic RNA polymerases are specialized, while the prokaryotic			enzyme is versatile. 			<LI>Sensitivity to inhibition by amanitin or rifampicin can be used			to identify the polymerase responsible for the synthesis of a			given RNA. 			<LI>That mitochondrial and bacterial RNA polymerases share an antibiotic			sensitivity profile supports the <A HREF="../MGW1/MG1378.html">endosymbiont hypothesis</A>. 		</UL>		<CENTER>			<P><A HREF="#Facts">Facts</A> | <A
