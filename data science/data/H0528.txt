<font face="Benguiat Bk BT"><a name="Course Description">Course Description</font><br>

<br>

This two day seminar and wet lab will focus on new and important issues surrounding

antimicrobial susceptibility testing. International and national speakers will present on

pharmocodynamics, antibiotic utilization, molecular methodologies, troubleshooting

specific antibiotic-microorganism combinations, external quality assessment and new

developments in antifungal and mycobacterial testing. The laboratory component will stress

screening tests, problems of detection of resistance and reporting practices.</p>
