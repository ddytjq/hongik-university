<h2>Some Parting Thoughts</h2>

<blockquote>
<i>Rules of Optimization:<br>
Rule 1: Don't do it.<br>
Rule 2 (for experts only): Don't do it yet.</i><br>
- M.A. Jackson<br>
<br>
<i>"More computing sins are committed in the name of efficiency (without

blind stupidity."</i><br>
