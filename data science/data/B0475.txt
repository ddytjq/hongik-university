      <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2"><b>TERMS AND CONDITIONS 
        OF SHARE ACCOUNTS - OPEN ISSUES</b></font> </p>
      <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2"><b>YOUNG PEOPLES &quot;OWN&quot; 
        SHARE</b></font></p>
      <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">1. Accounts may be 
        opened for anyone up to the age of 18. Young People aged 7 and over may 
        open and operate their own account. Accounts for Young People under the 
        age of 7 may be opened by adults.</font></p>
      <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">2. The minimum investment 
        shall be &pound;1.</font></p>
      <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">3. On reaching the 
        age of 18 the balance of the account must be withdrawn or transferred 
        to another type of account.</font></p>
      <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">4. Withdrawals may 
        be made on demand in accordance with the Rules of the Society and subject 
        to the normal branch limits.</font></p>
      <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">5. Interest will be 
        credited to accounts half-yearly on 31st May and 30th November.</font></p>
      <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">6. The rate of interest 
        on these shares may vary from time to time. The Society will only reduce 
        the rate of interest for one or more of the following reasons:</font></p>
      <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2"> (i) because the Society 
        needs to reduce costs, to enable it to reduce interest rates payable to 
        it by its current and new borrowers or otherwise to maintain and develop 
        the Society's lending business;<br>
        <br>
        (ii) because the Society's administrative costs and charges have increased 
        or the Society reasonably believes they are likely to do so in the near 
        future;</font></p>
      <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2"> (iii) because the 
        cost to the Society of borrowing funds has increased or the Society reasonably 
        believes it is likely to do so in the near future;</font></p>
      <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">(iv) because the Society 
        needs to reduce a rate or rates of interest payable by it in order to 
        achieve and maintain prudent levels of liquidity, profitability and/or 
        financial strength having regard to current economic circumstances and 
        the Society's expectations of future economic trends.</font></p>
      <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2"> Any variation will 
        be publicised in the Society's offices, and will be advertised in selected 
        newspapers as advised in the leaflet &quot;Guide to Savings&quot;. 
        </font></p>
      <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">7. The terms and conditions 
        of these shares may be varied from time to time by giving not less than 
        30 days prior written notice to the investor.</font></p>
      <hr width="75%" size="1">
      <p align="CENTER"><b><font face="Arial" size="7"><a name="bline"></a><font size="5">b_line</font></font></b></p>
      <font size="4"> </font> 
      <p align="CENTER"><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="4">A special 
        account for young people in Derbyshire</font></p>
      <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">We have designed the 
        b_line account specifically for all young people in Derbyshire who hold 
        the County Council&#146;s b_line card.</font></p>
      <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">You can open an account 
        with as little as £1 by calling in at our office and completing an application 
        form. You will need to bring your b_line card with you and some other 
        form of identification such as your bus pass or birth certificate. You 
        can invest up to £5,000 with us.</font></p>
      <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">You can withdraw at 
        any time from your account without any penalty; subject to our normal 
        branch limits. Details can be found in our Guide to Savings leaflet.</font></p>
      <p align="JUSTIFY"><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">We 
        will add interest to your account each year on 31 May. The rate of interest 
        may vary from time to time. The rate of interest will be linked to Bank 
        of England base rate and will not be less than 2% below that rate (except 
        when the Bank of England base rate changes, in which case we will amend 
        the rate within one calendar month of that change).</font></p>
      <p align="JUSTIFY"><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">Until 
        31 December 2001, we promise that the rate will not be lower than the 
        Bank of England base rate.</font></p>
      <p align="JUSTIFY"><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">Please 
        notify us immediately if you change your address.</font></p>
      <p align="JUSTIFY"><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">* 
        The b_line account is operated by Clay Cross Building Society. The offer 
        to b_line cardholders by the Society should not be taken as Derbyshire 
        County Council either recommending or endorsing the product.</font></p>
      <p align="CENTER"><b><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">TERMS 
        AND CONDITIONS</font></b></p>
      <ol>
        <li><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">Accounts may be 
          opened by anyone holding a valid b_line Derbyshire Youth Card.</font></li>
        <li><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">The minimum investment 
          is £1 and the maximum is £5,000.</font></li>
        <li><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">Upon reaching the 
          age of 18, the balance of the account must be withdrawn or transferred
