<TD><P class=body08><strong>F.A. Funding</strong>: The F.A. will invest Â£45 million via the Football Foundation into facility improvement projects over the next 3 years.

</P></TD>
</TR>
<TR>
<TD><P class=body08>&nbsp;</P></TD>
</TR>
<TR>
<TD><P class=body08><strong>Register of English Football Facilities</strong> (REFF): a national survey of all footballing facilities in England is currently underway. This will help to assess and prioritise pitch and facilities investment.

</P></TD>
</TR>
<TR>
<TD><P class=body08>&nbsp;</P></TD>
</TR>
<TR>
<TD><P class=body08><strong>Local Football Partnerships</strong>: County-based forums have been set up, led by County F.A.s, to bring together football and other partners to create and prioritise football facility and development plans in their area.

</P></TD>
</TR>
<TR>
<TD><P class=body08>&nbsp;</P></TD>
</TR>
