These offerings mirror the entire value chain – from the initial conceptual stage to the final series manufacture of market-ready products. With its cutting-edge technology and first-hand knowledge of the key embedded industries, ProSyst can provide its clients with support and expertise every step of the way. 

professional services 
Gain invaluable business insight from ProSyst – a leader in the embedded field, with extensive experience and technology partnerships with key players in the residential, healthcare, automotive and industrial automation markets.

ProSyst can be relied on to help realize customer strategies through professional services: training, on- and off-site support and technology transfer. Critical software development can be outsourced to ProSyst’s team of 100+ in-house professionals. 

gateway software products 
Advanced embedded services become a reality using the OSGi-compliant mBedded Server. This platform enables devices on local area networks to communicate intelligently with each other and with the Internet – and is open to an unlimited range of technologies. Development is a breeze using the mBedded Builder, ProSyst’s state-of-the-art embedded IDE. 

remote management software products
ProSyst’s unrivalled mPower Remote Manager allows gateway operators or device manufactures to easily control multiple networks and supports functions such as configuration, diagnostics, update and billing.
