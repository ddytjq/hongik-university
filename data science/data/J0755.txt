	<P>The formation of The Supercar Experience has come about as a result of repeated requests from the public to feel for themselves the exhilaration of the raw V8 horsepower they watch on television or at the track. The noise, the acceleration, the braking, the grip on cornering is something that can only be fully appreciated from within the car.</P>
	<P>With the fitting of a race seat with 4 point harness to engineers specifications, this rare experience is one that, until now, has been reserved for corporate sponsors, competition winners or race car crew only. Now, due to overwhelming demand from the public, Mal has established The Supercar Experience to give <B>you</B> this opportunity to be there in "the office" of a full blown race car.</P>
	<P>We have an enthusiastic and friendly team on hand to ensure that your Supercar Experience is a memorable and enjoyable one.</P>
	<P>The parent company, Rinist Pty. Ltd. has been around since 1989, and Mal has been running these V8 rides since 1996 for select groups, and many more than satisfied customers have come back year after year for a fresh adrenalin rush.</P>
