<B>Labels</b>
</font>
</p>

<p align="left">
<font face="tahoma">
<B>Labels</b> are very simple to understand. Basically they just hold text that appears on the Form.
For example, if you had a picture of your dog on the Form, in the label you would write
something like "My Dog" to describe the pic. Many programmers use labels around textboxes
so the user knows what to put in the textbox... 
</p>

<p align=center>
<img src="http://www.angelfire.com/ny3/visualbasic/images/vblab.jpg"
</p> 

<p align="left">
<font face="tahoma">
<font color="blue">
Label properties:
</font>
</p>

<p align="left">
<font face="tahoma">
<font color="red">
Alignment</font> - Determines if the label's text is in the middle, towards the right or the left<Br>
<Br>
<font color="red">
AutoSize</font> - Sizes the label to fit the text in it perfectly<Br>
<Br>
<font color="red">
BackStyle</font> - Determines if the Label is transparent or not<Br>
<Br>
<font color="red">
BorderStyle</font> - Determines how the border of the label looks like<Br>
<Br>
<font color="red">
Caption</font> - The label's caption<Br>
<Br>
<font color="red">
Font</font> - The label's font type<Br>
<Br>
<font color="red">
ForeColor</font> - Determines what color the font is<Br>
<Br>
<font color="red">
TabIndex</font> - What order the label gets focus<Br>
<Br>
<font color="red">
ToolTipText</font> - Holds text that will pop up when the mouse is over the label<Br>
<Br>
<font color="red">
Visible</font> - Determines if the control is visible at run time or not<Br>
</p>
