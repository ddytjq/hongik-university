        <td width="100%"><span><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#0099ff"><br>
        <strong>Personal Finances<br>
        </font><font face="Arial" size="3">Buy to Let Mortgages</font></strong></span><p><font face="Arial,Helvetica" size="2" color="#000000"><!--Start Content--></font></p>
        <font face="Arial" size="2"><b><p>If you want to buy a property to rent out and you need
        to borrow £25,000 or more choose our Buy To Let Mortgage.</p>
        <p>Benefits:</b><ul>
          <li>You can buy five properties to let out in addition to your main residence</li>
          <li>You can borrow up to 80% of the valuation or purchase price of the property (whichever
            is lower)</li>
          <li>Choice of several different mortgage types</li>
          <li>Low arrangement fee (0.5%) </li>
        </ul>
        <b><p>Plus:</b><ul>
          <li>Choose how to repay - Capital Repayment, Endowment, Pension Plan or Individual Savings
            Account</li>
          <li>Mortgage term can range from 3 years to retirement or age 70, whichever is earlier</li>
        </ul>
        <blockquote>
          <ul>
            <b>
            <li>Variable Rate Buy To Let </b></li>
          </ul>
        </blockquote>
        <p>This package is ideal if you want a variable rate and the flexibility to make extra
        payments into your mortgage. There is an arrangement fee of £295 for mortgages below
        £35,000.</p>
        <blockquote>
          <ul>
            <b>
            <li>Fixed Rate Buy To Let</b></li>
          </ul>
        </blockquote>
        <p>We offer a choice of 2, 3 or 5 year fixed rates, giving you the security of knowing
        that no matter what happens to general interest rates, your payments will not change
        during the fixed period. </p>
        <p></font><font face="Arial" size="1">There is an arrangement fee of £295 payable when
        booking a fixed or capped rate. This secures the rate you have chosen for a period of 3
        months. This means that even if we change the fixed rate or it is withdrawn from sale, we
        will still honour the rate agreed with you. This fee is non-refundable.</font></p>
      <p><font face="Arial" size="1">Fixed rate mortgages are subject to
      availability of funds. We would advise that, where a rate is chosen, funds
      are booked as soon as possible. The maximum amount for a fixed rate
      mortgage is normally £500,000, however applications in excess of this
      amount will be considered on an individual basis.</font></p>
        <p><font face="Arial" size="1">Typical example: based on a capital 
        repayment mortgage of £75,000 (property valued at £100,000). For each 
        example there are 300 monthly repayments, the arrangement fee of 0.5% of 
        the loan, booking fee of £295 in the case of a fixed rate, solicitors 
        and valuation fees of £470, other Bank fees of £100 and life assurance 
        premiums of £10.71 per month. The early repayment will be incurred if the
        mortgage is repaid in full of if a different Royal Bank mortgage is switched to during the
        period shown. At the end of the fixed rate the Bank's standard variable Mortgage Rate will
        apply, which is currently 5.95%. No account has been taken of any future changes in the
        Bank's mortgage rate which we may change at any time. The maximum early repayment charge
        based on these examples is £300.</font></p>
