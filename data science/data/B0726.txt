	        		<B>INTEREST</B><BR>
					*Interest will be paid or credited after deduction of income tax at the lower rate of 20% or, subject to the required certification, gross.<BR><BR>
**AER stands for Annual Equivalent Rate and illustrates what the interest rate would be if interest was paid and added each year.  (As every advertisement for a savings product which quotes an interest rate will contain an AER, you will be able to compare more easily what return you can expect from your savings over time.)  Please note that, if monthly interest is not added to your account, then the AER will not be achieved.<BR><BR>
      			
	        		<B>GENERAL TERMS AND CONDITIONS</B><BR>
					These terms and conditions (and those contained in the Scarborough Building Society general investment conditions) together with the Scarborough Building Society Memorandum and Rules set out the contractual relationship between Scarborough Building Society ('the Society'), and you, the member.<BR><BR>
These conditions contain important information and should be read by you before you open an account with the Society.  These conditions are available on this site by clicking on <U><B>Other Information.</U></B> <BR><BR><BR><BR>
      			
	        		<B>OPENING AN ACCOUNT</B><BR>
					The minimum investment required to open a Scarborough First Post Plus Account is £10,000.<BR><BR>
The maximum amount which may be invested in a Scarborough First Post Plus Account upon opening and during the life of the account is £250,000. This includes any interest which is credited to the account.<BR><BR>
The Society reserves the right to close your Scarborough First Post Plus Account if the balance falls below £10,000.<BR><BR>

The initial payment and any subsequent payments to your Scarborough First Post Plus Account will only be accepted in the form of a cheque or transfer from an existing Society investment account.<BR><BR>

This is a share account and on opening the account, the account holder(s) will become an investing member(s) of the Society, in accordance with its Rules.
<BR><BR>
      			
	        		<B>PROOF OF IDENTITY</B><BR>
					Proof of identity and verification of address are required for all applicants.  Two original documents, not more than three months old are required for each investor, photocopies are not acceptable.  The documents will be returned to you and should be either:<BR><BR>
<UL>
<LI>  Utility bill (e.g.  Gas/Electricity)</LI><BR>
<LI>  Bank/Building Society Statement</LI><BR>
<LI>  Current Council Tax/Notice of Tax Coding</LI>
</UL><BR>and MUST show the name and address of the investor as it appears on the application form.  Further verification checks may be carried out by the Society.<BR><BR>
If you already have an account with the Society bearing your current address, identification is not required.<BR><BR>
Unless proof of identity is provided, and all appropriate sections of the application form have been completed, including date of birth, we will return your investment to you.
<BR><BR>
      			
	        		<B>INTEREST</B><BR>
					If the balance of your Scarborough First Post Plus Account exceeds £10,000, interest may, on your written instructions be paid monthly, either to a designated bank or building society account, or added to the balance of your Scarborough First Post Plus Account.  If the balance of your Scarborough First Post Plus Account falls below £10,000, or you do not request to have your interest paid monthly, interest will be paid annually on 1 May.  The Society reserves the right to change the date on which interest is credited to your account.<BR><BR>

Interest which is payable monthly will be credited to your Scarborough First Post Plus Account or your designated bank or building society account on the first working day of each calendar month.<BR><BR>
If the balance of your Scarborough First Post Plus Account falls below £10,000, the interest rate payable for the period that the balance remains below £10,000 will be the Society's variable base savings rate applicable at the time.  Details are available on request.<BR><BR>
All rates are variable, and the gross monthly rates do not normally compound to the full annual rates shown.
<BR><BR>
      			
	        		<B>WITHDRAWALS</B><BR>
					Your Scarborough First Post Plus Account cannot be operated through any of the Society's branches.<BR><BR>

Subject to available funds, withdrawals from your Scarborough First Post Plus Account can be made at any time, after providing written notification to the Society.<BR><BR>

This account does not offer cheque withdrawals payable to a third party.  All cheques will be made payable to one or all of the account holders and sent to the last address on record.<BR><BR>

Within 24 hours of receipt of instructions, the Society, wherever possible, will process all cheque withdrawals and transfers to other accounts subject to account terms and conditions.
