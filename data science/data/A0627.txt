			<P>



			Now you don't have to commit your spare business cash for months on end to make it work harder for you. With a Bank of

			Scotland 30-Day Reserve Account you could benefit from:

			<P>

			<B>High Rates of Interest - guaranteed&#042;</B>

			<BR>

			make your business cash work harder.

			<P>

			<B>Free withdrawals after 30 day's notice</B>

			<BR>your working capital is never far from reach when you need it.

			<P>

			<B>One Free early withdrawal per year</B>

			<BR>We have built in this valuable facility just in case you need your business cash unexpectedly.<BR>

			Should you require more than one instant withdrawal per calendar year, this can easily be arranged upon payment of a small fee.&#042;&#042;



			<P>

			<B>Initial minimum opening deposit of &#163;50,000</B>

			<BR>

			once your account is opened there is no minimum or maximum balance.

			<P>

			<B>Dedicated Relationship Managers</B>

			<BR>

			Business Managers with knowledge and experience of the industry sector in which you operate will be at hand during

			the hours of business to answer your questions and queries. (8am-6pm)

			<P>



		<SPAN CLASS="terms">&#042; For all balance bands, the gross interest rates are guaranteed to be no less

		than the current links to Bank of Scotland Base Rate until the end of August 2002. The current links are:<BR>

		<B>30 Day Reserve Account:</B><BR>

		1.10&#037; under base rate on balances between &#163;50,000 and &#163;99,999;

		0.70&#037; under base rate on balances between &#163;100,000 and &#163;249,999;

		0.25&#037; under base rate on balances between &#163;250,000 and &#163;499,999;

		0.20&#037; under base rate on balances between &#163;500,000 and &#163;999,999;

		0.15&#037; under base rate on balances of &#163;1,000,000 and above.

		<P>

		&#042;&#042; Currently &#163;10 per &#163;10,000 withdrawn, or part thereof, subject to a minimum fee of &#163;10.
