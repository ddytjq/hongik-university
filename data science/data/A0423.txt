  <p><nobr><a target="_top" href="../insurance/insurance_travel_glossary_single.html" class="nav4levelOn">&gt;&nbsp;single trip</a> &nbsp;|&nbsp;</nobr> <nobr><a target="_top" href="../insurance/insurance_travel_glossary_annual.html" class="nav4levelOff">&gt;&nbsp;annual multi-trip</a> &nbsp;|&nbsp;</nobr> <nobr><a target="_top" href="../insurance/insurance_travel_glossary_extended.html" class="nav4levelOff">&gt;&nbsp;extended trip</a></nobr> <nobr><a href="#" class="nav4levelOff"></a></nobr></p>
  

<!-- #EndEditable --> <!-- #BeginEditable "Main%20Text" --> 
      <p>Here are the standard definitions for single trip cover.</p>
      <table width="430" border="0" cellspacing="0" cellpadding="0">
        <tr valign="top"> 
          <td width="138" class="bodyBold">Accident</td>
          <td width="292">A sudden, unexpected, specific, violent, external event 
            which occurs at a single identifiable time and place, but shall also 
            include exposure resulting from mishap to conveyance in which the 
            <span class="bodyBold">Insured Person</span> is travelling.</td>
        </tr>
        <tr> 
          <td colspan="2">&nbsp;</td>
        </tr>
        <tr valign="top"> 
          <td width="136" class="bodyBold">Baggage</td>
          <td width="294">Each of <span class="bodyBold">Your</span> suitcases 
            (or containers of a similar nature) and their contents, articles <span class="bodyBold">You</span> 
            are wearing or carrying which are owned by <span class="bodyBold">You</span>, 
            including <span class="bodyBold">Your Valuables</span>.</td>
        </tr>
        <tr> 
          <td colspan="2">&nbsp;</td>
        </tr>
        <tr valign="top"> 
          <td width="136" class="bodyBold">Bodily Injury</td>
          <td width="294"> 
            <p>Bodily injury (other than where directly or indirectly caused by 
              illness or disease) which is caused solely and directly by accidental 
              means, which within twelve months from the date of such <span class="bodyBold">Accident</span> 
              shall result in the death, total loss of sight or limbs or <span class="bodyBold">Permanent 
              Total Disablement</span> of the <span class="bodyBold">Insured Person</span>.</p>
          </td>
        </tr>
        <tr> 
          <td colspan="2">&nbsp;</td>
        </tr>
        <tr valign="top"> 
          <td width="136" class="bodyBold">Child</td>
          <td width="294">An unmarried and dependent person, aged 3-15 years, 
            living with an insured adult (unless agreed with Sainsbury's Bank 
            &reg; Travel Insurance Centre) and named on the <span class="bodyBold">Policy 
            Schedule</span>. Individuals aged between 16 and 22, who are in full 
            time education, named on the <span class="bodyBold">Policy Schedule</span> 
            and travelling with an insured parent, will also be classed as children.</td>
        </tr>
        <tr> 
          <td colspan="2">&nbsp;</td>
        </tr>
        <tr valign="top"> 
          <td width="136" class="bodyBold">Claims Office</td>
          <td width="294"> 
            <p>Sainsbury's Bank Travel Insurance Claims Centre (Telephone 0845 
              300 3192<sup>&#134;</sup>).</p>
          </td>
        </tr>
        <tr> 
          <td colspan="2">&nbsp;</td>
        </tr>
        <tr valign="top"> 
          <td width="136" class="bodyBold">Close Business Colleague</td>
          <td width="294">An associate in the same employment as <span class="bodyBold">You</span> 
            in the <span class="bodyBold">United Kingdom</span>, whose absence 
            from work or place of employment, as certified by a senior manager 
            or principal of the business, necessitates <span class="bodyBold">Your</span> 
            return to the <span class="bodyBold">United Kingdom</span>.</td>
        </tr>
        <tr> 
          <td colspan="2">&nbsp;</td>
        </tr>
        <tr valign="top"> 
          <td width="136" class="bodyBold">Emergency & Medical Assistance Centre</td>
          <td width="294">Sainsbury's Bank Travel Insurance Assistance Centre 
            (Telephone +44 (0)1273 765 305<sup>&#134;</sup>)</td>
        </tr>
        <tr> 
          <td colspan="2">&nbsp;</td>
        </tr>
        <tr valign="top"> 
          <td width="136" class="bodyBold">Europe</td>
          <td width="294">The continent of <span class="bodyBold">Europe</span>, 
            the Channel Islands, Isle of Man, Republic of Ireland, Iceland, the 
            Mediterranean islands, the Canary Islands, Madeira, Russia west of 
            the Urals and all those countries bordering the Mediterranean except 
            Libya, Egypt and Albania. </td>
        </tr>
        <tr> 
          <td colspan="2">&nbsp;</td>
        </tr>
        <tr valign="top"> 
          <td width="136" class="bodyBold">Extension of Insurance</td>
          <td width="294">If a public transport delay means <span class="bodyBold">You</span> 
            are unable to return <span class="bodyBold">Home</span> by the date 
            the <span class="bodyBold">Policy Schedule</span> expires, then <span class="bodyBold">Your</span> 
            cover is extended for up to one week. If <span class="bodyBold">Your 
            Trip</span> extends beyond the maximum <span class="bodyBold">Trip 
            Duration</span> indicated on <span class="bodyBold">Your Policy Schedule</span>, 
            due to <span class="bodyBold">Your</span> death, injury or illness 
            or that of anyone covered by this insurance, Your cover will be automatically 
            extended for the additional days necessary, provided the <span class="bodyBold">Emergency 
            &amp; Medical Assistance Centre</span> are contacted at the time.</td>
        </tr>
        <tr> 
          <td colspan="2">&nbsp;</td>
        </tr>
        <tr valign="top"> 
          <td width="136" class="bodyBold">Geographical Limit</td>
          <td width="294">The geographical limits specified on the <span class="bodyBold">Policy 
            Schedule</span>.</td>
