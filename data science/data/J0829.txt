<h1>
<font face="Arial,Helvetica"><a href="../../uk_karting.html">Kart <font color="#FF0000">racing</font></a></font></h1></center>
<font face="Arial,Helvetica"><b><font size=+2>W</font></b>here aspiring
race drivers should start. World Champions Mika Hakkinen, Nigel Mansell,
Alain Prost, Ayrton Senna and Michael Schumacher were all successful kart
racers. This is the nearest you can get to motor racing on the cheap!</font>
<br>&nbsp;
<p><font face="Arial,Helvetica">Although karts look much alike to the uninitiated,
there are many classes, from 60cc Cadets to 160mph Formula E. Some of the
smaller classes feature fixed-price equipment. Karts are divided into two
basic categories: those with direct drive (which race on short circuits),
and those with gearboxes (which race on both the long and short circuits).</font>
<br>&nbsp;
<center>
<h2>
<font face="Arial,Helvetica">Major <font color="#FF0000"><font size=+3>UK
events</font></font></font></h2></center>

<ul>
<li>
<font face="Arial,Helvetica">Annual - Five British Championships (Long
Circuit, Formula A, Junior, Cadet and Endurance)</font></li>
</ul>

<center>
<h2>
<font face="Arial,Helvetica">Minimum <font color="#FF0000"><font size=+3>requirements</font></font></font></h2></center>
<font face="Arial,Helvetica">Any kart complying with MSA regulations; a
crash helmet and overalls. You must be at least 8 years old and hold a
<a href="licenses.html">Competition
Licence</a>. Except for Endurance races, you may also need a Medical Certificate.
Before you can even apply for a licence, you must do the following:</font>
<ol>
<li>
<font face="Arial,Helvetica">Obtain a "Starting Karting" pack from the
MSA. This costs &pound;31 (including post and packing) and contains a video,
the <i>MSA Kart Race Handbook, the MSA Motor Sports Yearbook, </i>a Fixture
List, a list of organising clubs, and useful advice on driving techniques.
<a href="http://www.msauk.org/Go_motorsport/noviceKart.htm">Click
here</a> to download order form.</font></li>

<li>
<font face="Arial,Helvetica">If over 18 and if applying for a "normal"
(not Endurance) licence, you must obtain a medical certificate (the form
is in the "<a href="http://www.msauk.org/Go_motorsport/noviceKart.htm">Starting
Karting</a>" pack).</font></li>

<li>
<a href="http://www.arks.co.uk/"><img SRC="../images/arks.gif" ALT="Association of Racing Kart Schools" BORDER=0 height=95 width=100 align=ABSCENTER></a><font face="Arial,Helvetica">Complete
a half-day MSA assessment for <a href="novice_karting.html">novice kart
racers</a>, run&nbsp; by members of the <a href="http://www.arks.co.uk/">Association
of Racing Kart Schools (ARKS)</a>. It costs &pound;53.28 per person (not
including hire of kart and/or racewear).</font></li>
</ol>
