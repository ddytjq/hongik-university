		<SPAN CLASS=header>Direct Business Cheque Account</SPAN>

		<P>

		<SPAN CLASS=bold>Your business can benefit from free banking forever...</SPAN>

		<BR>
	With a Direct Business Cheque Account you could bank for free, not just now, but forever, when you write fewer than 100 Cheques a month and maintain a balance of £5000 or more. Branch services are excluded from this offer.
 

		<P>
	If you do write more than 100 cheques per month or let your balance fall below £5,000, we will only charge you 40p per cheque.
		<P>

		<B>With complete flexibility...</B>
		<BR>
You're free to draw from your deposit at any time. When your deposit balance falls below £5000 you won't incur high bank charges, the only charge is 40p per cheque. As soon as your balance returns to the £5000 or more, your banking is free again.

		<P>

		<B>Earning higher rates of interest...</B>
		<BR>
		You will automatically earn interest as standard on your account, no matter what your balance is.
		<P>

		<B>Overdraft with no arrangement fees...</B>
		<BR>
Arranged quickly and easily for your convenience, without any initial or annual fees. Your overdraft and interest rates are negotiable, and you will only pay interest on your overdraft if you use it.

		<P>

		<B>Access to three dedicated relationship managers.</B>
		<BR>

To ensure there will always be someone on hand to meet your business banking needs, you will have three named relationship managers to call upon.  You can choose to primarily deal with one relationship manager, and will always have two other named contacts to call upon if needed.<br><br>

Your relationship managers make it their business to understand your business needs. They will be on hand between 8am and 6pm to help you with any questions and queries you may have.

		<P>


		<B>You will also benefit from:
</B>
<ul>
	<li><b>Free Internet Banking Service</b><br /> 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.<br><br>
		</li>

	<li><b>Telephone Banking Service</b><br /> 24 hours a day, 7 days a week at lo-call rates.<br><br>
	</li>


<li><b>Direct Cash service</b><br>
	Providing you with a cheap and easy way to pay cash into your account by post. By using special envelopes provided, you can send us a maximum of £2500 in each, and we will credit your account the next working day. The cost per envelope is £6. Each envelope is insured up to the value of £2500.
<br><br></li>


<li><b>Fee Free Business Visa</b><br>
	You won't have to pay a fee for the first 12 months and you'll get up to 41 days interest free credit.</li>

<br><br>

</ul>

		<P>

		<b>	Easy to Join</b><br>

Switching business accounts used to be complex and time consuming. Well, not anymore. Moving to Bank of Scotland is quick and easy thanks to our unique 'Easy to Join' service.<br><br>

Bank of Scotland is the first major UK bank to introduce a comprehensive account switching service for Small and Medium-sized Enterprises (SMEs). Should you wish to take advantage of this service, you would be allocated your own named coordinators who would oversee the whole transfer process including direct debits, standing orders, CHAPS and BACS payments as well as credit card, merchant services and international transfers - leaving you to concentrate on your own business.<br><br>


For more information, call us on 0845 300 1956.<br><br>
