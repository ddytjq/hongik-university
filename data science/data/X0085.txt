Clutch catching       by brothers John and Bill Begoske and a flaming backhand throw of Dave Young       propelled the Bandits past the team of Ron Arvo, Ron Blau, Rick LaRue, and       Scott Stewart.<br>      &nbsp;&nbsp;Sunny skies, 80 degree temperatures, and constant breezes greeted       the guts players, who dined on bratwurst, hot dogs, and burgers between       matches. <br>      &nbsp;&nbsp;The Buskaboyz from Hancock and Chicago took third, and fourth       place went to Guido, Grumpu, and Minnow from Hancock and Utah.<br>      &nbsp;&nbsp;Guts Frisbee matches two teams fourteen meters apart hurling       the orange Professional Frisbee at speeds near 80 mph. Players must catch       the Frisbee one-handed.
