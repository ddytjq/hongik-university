      <p><b><font size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">Require an urgent 
        schedule or proof of cover?</font><br>
        </b><font size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">If you need proof 
        of cover urgently (perhaps because of a house sale completion) then we 
        can provide a priority Internet and Fax service. Compete the details as 
        normal, but click on the EDI button to send the proposal details directly 
        to us via the Internet. We will complete a schedule and fax it to you 
        or the Solicitor on a priority basis. If you do not have access to the 
        Internet then you can fax the proposal, and quote breakdown to us for 
        an urgent case.</font></p>
      <p><b><font size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">Rates</font><br>
        </b><font size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">The rates are updated 
        by disk, usually every month, depending upon the rate changes from the 
        Insurers. Your system will continue to quote if it is out of date, but 
        you won&#146;t be able to print proposals until you have loaded the update 
        disk.</font></p>
      <p><font size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">We usually only send 
        disks to active members, so if you have not placed any business with us 
        for a month or so, you may not receive an update disk. You can always 
        call us for an update, or go to the download area on this site if you 
        need an update urgently.</font></p>
      <p><b><font size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">Quotes are valid 
        for 30 days.</font></b><font size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif"><br>
        If you are quoting for a case where the on risk date is more that 30 days 
        ahead, we may have to re-quote the business prior to it being placed on 
        risk. There may be rate or IPT changes which will invalidate you quote 
        if it is outside the 30 day rule.</font></p>
      <p><b><font size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">Payment<br>
        </font></b><font size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">We can accept 
        payment by Credit Card, Cheque or Direct Debit.</font></p>
      <p><font size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">Credit card: please 
        collect the clients Credit Card details (Card Issuer, Card Number, Name 
        on the card, Statement address, Valid from, Valid to and if appropriate, 
        issue number). We will authorise the card and collect payment. If the 
        credit card is not authorised we will inform the client, but we can only 
        hold cover for 14 days whilst alternate payment arrangements are made.</font></p>
      <p><font size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">Cheque: Please ensure 
        your client makes a cheque payable to <b>CETA Limited</b>. We will bank 
        the cheque. If the cheque is returned, we will inform the client, but 
        we can only hold cover for 14 days whilst alternate payment arrangements 
        are made.</font></p>
      <p><font size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">Direct Debit: Please 
        ensure that your client makes a cheque payable to <b>CETA Limited</b> 
        for the 20% deposit and then completes and signs the Direct Debit mandate. 
        We will process the mandate as quickly as possible. If there are problems 
        with any of the payments, we will contact the client directly and give 
        them time to make alternate payment arrangements.</font></p>
      <p><b><font size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">Non Standard business<br>
        </font></b><font size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">There are 
        number of types of household business that we are unable to put onto the 
        quotations system because of either the type of information required, 
        or they need to be individually underwritten.</font></p>
      <p><font size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">Please contact us 
        with any queries, for most risks you will be required to complete a quotation 
        request form that can be faxed to you and then fax it back.</font></p>

      <p><b><font size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">Problems / Advice<br>
        </font></b><font size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">If you have

      <p><font size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">Staff in our household 
        department are happy to offer advice and deal with your enquiries regarding 
        Insurance business, either by email, fax or telephone. They cannot provide 
        training over the telephone, or software support. All telephone calls 
        are queued so that your call will be answered in strict rotation.</font></p>
      <p><font size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">Every proposal that 
        we receive is checked by our staff before the schedule is produced. We 
        will contact you if we come across a problem, however, please help us 
        by ensuring you follow the new business checklist when placing business 
        and letting us know in good time of the commencement date of the policy.</font></p>
      <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2"><b>Renewals<br>
        </b></font><font size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">We will 
        automatically re quote every case when it falls due for renewal. If a 
        more competitive premium is available from another Insurer then we will 
        offer this to the client otherwise we will renew the policy with the existing 
        Insurer.</font></p>
      <p><font size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">The sums insured will 
        be index linked so that they keep pace with inflation. Where a client 
        is already paying by Direct Debit, we will automatically continue with 
        the Direct Debit, spreading the renewal premium over the next 12 months.</font></p>
      <p><b><font size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">Keeping the business</font></b><font size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif"><br>
        We will do everything we can to ensure that your client continues with 
        the policy thereby protecting your commission income. We do this by offering 
        the best schemes at the most attractive rates and by providing the ability 
        to spread payments over 12 months.</font></p>
      <p><font size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">One of the best ways 
        of keeping the business is to ensure that the client pays by Direct Debit. 
        Because they need do nothing at renewal they do not often lapse at this 
        time.</font></p>
