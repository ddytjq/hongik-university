  <p align="center" class="headers">Safeguard your family&#146;s future</p>
  <p align="center" class="body">If you have a Capital and Interest repayment 
    mortgage you may need the security of life assurance cover for the money you 
    have borrowed. </p>
  <p align="center" class="body">A Mortgage Protection policy is a straightforward 
    and affordable way to ensure that if you, or your partner if a joint policy, 
    should die during the policy term, your family receives a cash sum big enough 
    to pay off the mortgage amount outstanding.</p>
  <p align="center" class="body">As long as you keep up with monthly payments 
    on your mortgage and your policy, you will have a sufficient level of cover 
    to repay what you owe in full (assuming mortgage interest rates do not go 
    above 10%). </p>
  <p align="center" class="body">You decide how much cover you need (the balance 
    of your mortgage), and how long you need cover for (usually the mortgage term 
    outstanding). We then calculate a premium level to give you this amount of 
    cover. </p>
  <p align="center" class="body">And what&#146;s more, the death benefit received 
    will be tax-free, so more money goes to your family when it is needed most.<br>
    <br>
  </p>
  <p align="center" class="subheads">Mortgage Protection</p>
  <ul>
    <li class="body">Life assurance protection throughout the policy term.<br>
      <br>
    </li>
    <li class="body"> Available for everyone aged 16 to 75 next birthday.<br>
      <br>
    </li>
    <li class="body"> Choose a policy term from 10 to 30 years.<br>
      <br>
    </li>
    <li class="body"> All death benefits will be free of tax.<br>
      <br>
    </li>
    <li class="body"> Easy to arrange, with a simple application form and weekly 
      or monthly deductions from your pay, where available. </li>
  </ul>
