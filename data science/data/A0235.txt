<h1>The Answers</h1>
<h2>MoneyBack Credit Card</h2>
<a name="IntroductoryPurchaseRate">&nbsp;</a>
<p class="faqquestion">Q. Will I get a special Introductory Purchase Rate?</p>	
<p class="faqanswer">A. Yes, it's just 1.9% 
  interest charged for 6 months.</p>

<a name="after6months">&nbsp;</a>
<p class="faqquestion">Q. Can you offer me a competitive rate after 6 months?</p>	
<p class="faqanswer">A. Our Standard Purchase APR is 16.5% 
  (variable).</p>

<a name="interestfreeperiod">&nbsp;</a>
<p class="faqquestion">Q. Is there an interest-free period?</p> 
<p class="faqanswer">A. This will be detailed in your card literature.</p>
<a name="TravelAccidentInsurance">&nbsp;</a>
<p class="faqquestion">Q. Do I get any Travel Accident Insurance? </p>
<p class="faqanswer">A. You can get up to £100,000 of travel insurance when you 
  purchase travel services with your card.</p>

<a name="VisaCard">&nbsp;</a>
<p class="faqquestion">Q. Is it a Visa Card?</p>
<p class="faqanswer">A. We give you a choice of Visa or MasterCard.</p>

<a name="anyotherbenefits">&nbsp;</a>
<p class="faqquestion">Q. Can I look forward to any other benefits?</p>	
<p class="faqanswer">A. There's 0.5% MoneyBack on purchases up to &pound;19,999.99 
  and double your MoneyBack at partner companies. You even earn 10% MoneyBack 
  on interest you pay.</p>
<p><a href="#"><img src="/media/images/but-backtotop.gif" width="120" height="20" border="0" /></a></p>
<a name="howmuchmoneyBack">&nbsp;</a>
<p class="faqquestion">Q. How much MoneyBack can I expect to earn?</p>
<p class="faqanswer">A. Our cardholders typically earn over £35 in MoneyBack over 
  a year and in total we have paid back over £74.3 million to our customers since 
  1997.</p>

<a name="whendoigetmymoneyback">&nbsp;</a>
<p class="faqquestion">Q. When do I get my money back with the MoneyBack card?</p>
<p class="faqanswer">A. You will receive a cheque in January for the total amount 
  of money back earned for you to spend as you wish. Makes a nice post Christmas 
  present!</p>

<a name="incertainplaces">&nbsp;</a>
<p class="faqquestion">Q. Will I benefit if I use my MoneyBack Card in certain 
  places? </p>
<p class="faqanswer">A. You could get even more MoneyBack when you shop with our 
  High Street Shops, travel, entertainment, motoring or utilities Partners.</p>

<a name="whatbalances">&nbsp;</a>
<p class="faqquestion">Q. What balances can I transfer?</p>
<p class="faqanswer">A. You can transfer balances from any credit or store cards 
  up to your available credit limit. Transferred balances must be a minimum of 
  £100 and cannot be from another Alliance & Leicester Group account or where 
  your repayments are in arrears.</p>

<a name="morethanonebalance">&nbsp;</a>
<p class="faqquestion">Q. Can I transfer more than one balance?</p>
<p class="faqanswer">A. Yes. You can transfer as many balances from as many cards 
  as you wish, up to your credit limit.</p>

<a name="arrangeabalancetransfer">&nbsp;</a>
<p class="faqquestion">Q. How do I arrange a balance transfer?</p>
<p class="faqanswer">A. Transferring balances from your other cards is easy. You 
  will receive a balance transfer application when you are accepted for an Alliance 
  & Leicester credit card or alternatively you can call our customer representatives 
  and complete the transfers over the phone.</p>  

<p><a href="#"><img src="/media/images/but-backtotop.gif" width="120" height="20" border="0" /></a></p>

<a name="annualfee">&nbsp;</a>
<p class="faqquestion">Q. Will I have to pay an annual fee?</p>	
<p class="faqanswer">A. No. Unlike some cards we don't charge an annual fee.</p>

<a name="balanceeachmonth">&nbsp;</a>
<p class="faqquestion">Q. Do I have to pay off the balance each month?</p>	
<p class="faqanswer">A. No. You can just pay 2% of your balance or £5 minimum, 
  whichever is greater, and choose to fix your payment on any day of the month 
  to suit your needs.</p>

<a name="cashcaniwithdraw">&nbsp;</a>
<p class="faqquestion">Q. How much cash can I withdraw? </p>
<p class="faqanswer">A. Up to £300 a day all around the world at an interest rate 
  of 18.2% (includes a 1.5% 
  or £2 minimum charge).</p>
  

<a name="feeforcash">&nbsp;</a>
<p class="faqquestion">Q. Is there a fee for cash advances?</p>
<p class="faqanswer">A. There is a fee of 1.5% of the cash advance (minimum £2) 
  plus interest.</p>

<a name="goodcreditlimit">&nbsp;</a> 
<p class="faqquestion">Q. Will I have a good credit limit?</p>	
<p class="faqanswer">A. It starts at a minimum of £500, but can be as high as 
  £10,000.</p>

<a name="worldwide">&nbsp;</a>
<p class="faqquestion">Q. Will my card be accepted worldwide?</p>	
<p class="faqanswer">A. Your card will be accepted wherever you see the Visa or 
  MasterCard sign at over 19 million outlets worldwide.</p>

<p><a href="#"><img src="/media/images/but-backtotop.gif" width="120" height="20" border="0" /></a></p>

<a name="familymembers">&nbsp;</a>
<p class="faqquestion">Q. Can I get additional cards for family members?</p>	
<p class="faqanswer">A. You can add up to 3 additional cardholders onto your account.</p>



<a name="successful">&nbsp;</a>
<p class="faqquestion">Q. How long will it take to hear if my application has 
  been successful?</p>
<p class="faqanswer">A. It will take up to 10 days to receive a decision, and 
  you will be informed of the decision by post.</p>



<a name="speaktosomeone">&nbsp;</a>
<p class="faqquestion">Q. Can I speak to someone when I need to?</p>  
<p class="faqanswer">A. Our team are on hand 24 hours a day, 365 days a year, 
  for you to pay bills, request a PIN and make account enquiries. Simply go to 
  our Contact Us section for further details.</p>


<a name="lostorstolen">&nbsp;</a>
<p class="faqquestion">Q. If my Alliance & Leicester Card is lost or stolen, who 
  do I contact? </p>
<p class="faqanswer">A. As soon as you know that your Alliance & Leicester card 
  is missing you must advise us by telephone on 0500 31 32 33 (or +44 151 928 
  4033 if you are calling from overseas).</p>



<a name="protectmycard">&nbsp;</a>
<p class="faqquestion">Q. Can I protect my card purchases or payments?</p> 
<p class="faqanswer">A. Our Purchase Protection Insurance offers a refund on most 
  worldwide purchases over £50 for up to 100 days (if they're not already covered 
  on any existing insurance policy).</p>
<p class="faqanswer">Our Payment Protection Plan covers payments in case of accident, sickness, unemployment or death. Plus our Card Protection Plan protects all your cards from loss or theft.</p>



<a name="doesthechip">&nbsp;</a>
<p class="faqquestion">Q. What does the chip in my Alliance & Leicester credit 
  card do?</p>
<p class="faqanswer">A. The chip is a fairly new technology introduced in the 
  UK, but has been used successfully in other countries for a while now. The chip 
  helps to prevent fraudulent activity at point of sale, and will become standard 
  issue by credit card providers in the UK over the next few years.</p>



<p><a href="#"><img src="/media/images/but-backtotop.gif" width="120" height="20" border="0" /></a></p>
<p><small>*1.90% is a fixed 
  monthly interest charge, equivalent to 0.316% 
  per month over 6 months.</small></p>
