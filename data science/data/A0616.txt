<span class="bodytext"><b>Accommodation and layout</b> 
<p>
<b>Entrance (hall) </b><br> 
<ul>
<table border=0 width=100% cellpadding=1 cellspacing=1>
<tr>
<td valign=top><INPUT TYPE="CHECKBOX"></td>
<td valign=top><span class="bodytext">is there an outer porch (as protection against weather, for delivery of parcels)?</span></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td valign=top><INPUT TYPE="CHECKBOX"></td>
<td valign=top><span class="bodytext">is there space for parking a pram, pushchair, bicycle, shopping trolley?</span></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td valign=top><INPUT TYPE="CHECKBOX"></td>
<td valign=top><span class="bodytext">is there anywhere to hang coats, store wellington boots, gardening shoes, umbrellas?</span></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td valign=top><INPUT TYPE="CHECKBOX"></td>
<td valign=top><span class="bodytext">is it well lit?</span></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td valign=top><INPUT TYPE="CHECKBOX"></td>
<td valign=top><span class="bodytext">which direction does the front door face?</span></td>
</table>
</ul>
<br><br>
<b>Living-room(s)</b><br>  
<ul>
<table border=0 width=100% cellpadding=1 cellspacing=1>
<tr>
<td valign=top><INPUT TYPE="CHECKBOX"></td>
<td valign=top><span class="bodytext">are they adequate in size? (in an irregular-shaped room or one with a bay window, take the exact measurements)</span></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td valign=top><INPUT TYPE="CHECKBOX"></td>
<td valign=top><span class="bodytext">is the dining-room or dining area convenient for the kitchen? are there steps up or down from the kitchen? (making a trolley impracticable, carrying trays hazardous)</span></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td valign=top><INPUT TYPE="CHECKBOX"></td>
<td valign=top><span class="bodytext">is there space for the children to play?</span></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td valign=top><INPUT TYPE="CHECKBOX"></td>
<td valign=top><span class="bodytext">is there space for the piano? (preferably not against a neighbour's adjoining wall)</span></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td valign=top><INPUT TYPE="CHECKBOX"></td>
<td valign=top><span class="bodytext">is there space for your furniture (large sofas can cause problems if access is restricted)?</span></td>
</tr>
<tr><td valign=top><INPUT TYPE="CHECKBOX"></td>
<td valign=top><span class="bodytext">are the windows in convenient places?</span></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td valign=top><INPUT TYPE="CHECKBOX"></td>
<td valign=top><span class="bodytext">where are the electrical sockets and are there enough of them?</span></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td valign=top><INPUT TYPE="CHECKBOX"></td>
<td valign=top><span class="bodytext">where are the lights and switches?</span></td>
</tr>
</table>
</ul>
<br><br>
<b>Kitchen </b><br> 
<ul>
<table border=0 width=100% cellpadding=1 cellspacing=1>
<tr>
<td valign=top><INPUT TYPE="CHECKBOX"></td>
<td valign=top><span class="bodytext">is it large enough, particularly if you want to be able to eat in it or work in it?</span></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td valign=top><INPUT TYPE="CHECKBOX"></td>
<td valign=top><span class="bodytext">does it lead off the hall/living-room/dining-room? how convenient is it for carrying food and shopping to, rubbish from?</span></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td valign=top><INPUT TYPE="CHECKBOX"></td>
<td valign=top><span class="bodytext">is there an outside door into the garden, for access to the garage or dustbin?</span></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td valign=top><INPUT TYPE="CHECKBOX"></td>
<td valign=top><span class="bodytext">is there a back porch?</span></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td valign=top><INPUT TYPE="CHECKBOX"></td>
<td valign=top><span class="bodytext">what does the window look on to? which way does it face? (if south or west, it may get too hot); could you keep an eye on a child in the garden?</span></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td valign=top><INPUT TYPE="CHECKBOX"></td>
<td valign=top><span class="bodytext">is the window easy to open? is there an air extractor system?</span></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td valign=top><INPUT TYPE="CHECKBOX"></td>
<td valign=top><span class="bodytext">does the layout of the worktop and sink area suit your way of working?</span></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td valign=top><INPUT TYPE="CHECKBOX"></td>
<td valign=top><span class="bodytext">is it well lit?</span></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td valign=top><INPUT TYPE="CHECKBOX"></td>
<td valign=top><span class="bodytext">what storage is there for food? is there a larder? is there space for your refrigerator? for your freezer? for your dishwasher?</span></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td valign=top><INPUT TYPE="CHECKBOX"></td>
<td valign=top><span class="bodytext">will your existing equipment fit?</span></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td valign=top><INPUT TYPE="CHECKBOX"></td>
<td valign=top><span class="bodytext">is there enough cupboard space, drawers, shelves? a tall cupboard for the vacuum cleaner, brooms?</span></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td valign=top><INPUT TYPE="CHECKBOX"></td>
<td valign=top><span class="bodytext">is the floor covering in good condition? comfortable to stand on? likely to be easy to clean?</span></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td valign=top><INPUT TYPE="CHECKBOX"></td>
<td valign=top><span class="bodytext">is gas laid on? (check particularly whether the present owner has an electric cooker)</span></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td valign=top><INPUT TYPE="CHECKBOX"></td>
<td valign=top><span class="bodytext">where and how many electric sockets are there? is there one for a cooker?</span></td>
</tr>
</table>
</ul>
<br><br>
<b>Utility room</b><br>  
<ul>
<table border=0 width=100% cellpadding=1 cellspacing=1>
<tr>
<td valign=top><INPUT TYPE="CHECKBOX"></td>
<td valign=top><span class="bodytext">is there one? or what facilities are there for laundry?</span></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td valign=top><INPUT TYPE="CHECKBOX"></td>
<td valign=top><span class="bodytext">indoors: is there space for a washing machine/tumble dryer? ironing area?</span></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td valign=top><INPUT TYPE="CHECKBOX"></td>
<td valign=top><span class="bodytext">outdoors: is there space for hanging clothes to dry? are there any restrictions on hanging washing outside?</span></td>
</tr>
</table>
</ul>
<p>
<b>Staircase </b><br> 
<ul>
<table border=0 cellpadding=1 cellspacing=1>
<tr>
<td valign=top><INPUT TYPE="CHECKBOX"></td>
<td valign=top><span class="bodytext">is it easy to climb (for small children or elderly people)?</span></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td valign=top><INPUT TYPE="CHECKBOX"></td>
<td valign=top><span class="bodytext">are the treads wide enough?</span></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td valign=top><INPUT TYPE="CHECKBOX"></td>
<td valign=top><span class="bodytext">risers not too steep?</span></td> 
</tr>
<tr>
<td valign=top><INPUT TYPE="CHECKBOX"></td>
<td valign=top><span class="bodytext">are there awkward curves or bends? are there handrails?</span></td> 
</tr>
<tr>
<td valign=top><INPUT TYPE="CHECKBOX"></td>
<td valign=top><span class="bodytext">is it well lit (top and bottom)?</span></td> 
</tr>
<tr>
<td valign=top><INPUT TYPE="CHECKBOX"></td>
<td valign=top><span class="bodytext">will you be able to get your furniture upstairs?</span></td>
</tr>
</table>
</ul>
<p>
<b>Bedrooms</b><br>  
<ul>
<table border=0 cellpadding=1 cellspacing=1>
<tr>
<td valign=top><INPUT TYPE="CHECKBOX"></td>
<td valign=top><span class="bodytext">how many? are they large enough? for double bed/twin beds/four-poster/bunk beds?<br>
will other furniture (chest of drawers, wardrobe, for instance) fit in? (measure, do not guess)</span></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td valign=top><INPUT TYPE="CHECKBOX"></td>
<td valign=top><span class="bodytext">is there built-in furniture?</span></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td valign=top><INPUT TYPE="CHECKBOX"></td>
<td valign=top><span class="bodytext">is there a washbasin? (if so, fill it up and let it drain, and do the same with any others – they can be noisy and react to the plumbing elsewhere in the house)</span></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td valign=top><INPUT TYPE="CHECKBOX"></td>
<td valign=top><span class="bodytext">are the rooms convertible to nursery/playroom/study/bedrooms? (consider future needs as well as current ones)</span></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td valign=top><INPUT TYPE="CHECKBOX"></td>
<td valign=top><span class="bodytext">do they face a noisy road or a bright street light?</span></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td valign=top><INPUT TYPE="CHECKBOX"></td>
<td valign=top><span class="bodytext">how soundproof are the walls between rooms?</span></td>
</tr>
</table>
</ul>
<br><br>
<b>Bathroom(s) </b><br> 
<ul>
<table border=0 cellpadding=1 cellspacing=1>
<tr>
<td valign=top><INPUT TYPE="CHECKBOX"></td>

</tr>
<tr>
<td valign=top><INPUT TYPE="CHECKBOX"></td>

</tr>

<td valign=top><span class="bodytext">is there a separate WC?</span></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td valign=top><INPUT TYPE="CHECKBOX"></td>
<td valign=top><span class="bodytext">plumbing: can another bathroom/extra basin/shower/WC be installed easily? (check where the water supply and waste piping run in the building) does everything work?</span></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td valign=top><INPUT TYPE="CHECKBOX"></td>
<td valign=top><span class="bodytext">is there an airing cupboard/linen cupboard or space to make one?</span></td>
</tr>
</table>
