              <h4 class="font">Syndicate Forecasts and Current Trading</h4>
              <p class=MsoBodyText3 style='text-align:justify'><span class="font">The 
                Directors of Chaucer Holdings PLC announce initial syndicate forecasts 
                for the 2000 year of account and updated forecasts for the 1999 
                year of account based on statistics as at 30 June 2001.  The 2000 
                forecasts confirm an improving trend across all managed syndicates.  
                Rates are continuing to harden in all major parts of the Group’s 
                business and prospects for 2002 are good.  Syndicate 1096 has 
                strengthened reserves in respect of US casualty business but is 
                unaffected by US Directors and Officers insurance which it reduced 
                to minimal levels from the 1999 year and only had low exposure 
                prior to that.</span></p>

              <h4 class="font">Capacity Offers</h4>
              <p class="font">ALIT (No. 1) Limited, the wholly-owned corporate 
                member of Chaucer Holdings PLC, is to make announced auction offers 
                in the first Lloyd’s auction this September to acquire all of 
                the prospective participations on syndicates 587 and 1096 for 
                the 2002 year of account other than those to which it and its 
                associates are already entitled.  The offer prices will be 0.1p 
                per £1 in respect of £23.5 million of capacity on syndicate 587 
                and 1.5p per £1 in respect of £6.9 million of capacity on syndicate 
                1096.  In order to buy-out non-accepting holders of capacity in 
                each respective syndicate, Chaucer would need Lloyd’s approval 
                and to purchase 57.5% of syndicate 587 capacity for which the 
                offer is made and 50% in respect of syndicate 1096.</p>
