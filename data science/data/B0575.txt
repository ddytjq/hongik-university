                  Board has established an Audit Committee, a Remuneration
                  Committee and a Nomination Committee. The Audit Committee is
                  chaired by Mr Fink and its other members are Mr Eckert and Mr
                  Birch. The Audit Committee is required by the Combined Code to
                  consist exclusively of non-executive directors, the majority
                  of whom are independent of management and free from any
                  business or other relationship which could materially affect
                  the exercise of their independent judgement.<o:p>
                  </o:p>
                  </font></span></p>
                  <p class="MsoNormal" style="mso-layout-grid-align:none;text-autospace:none"><font face="Verdana" size="2"><span style="mso-ansi-language: EN-US">This

                  and reviewing the Company&#146;s annual and interim reports and
                  accounts and the involvement of the Company&#146;s auditors in
                  that process, focusing particularly on compliance with legal
                  requirements and accounting standards and the requirements of
                  the UK Listing Authority and the London Stock Exchange. The
                  ultimate responsibility for reviewing and approving the
                  Company&#146;s annual and interim reports and accounts is with
                  the Board.<o:p>
                  </o:p>
                  </span></font></p>
                  <p class="MsoNormal" style="mso-layout-grid-align:none;text-autospace:none"><font face="Verdana" size="2"><span style="mso-ansi-language: EN-US">The
                  Remuneration Committee is chaired by Mr Fink and the other
                  member is Mr Eckert. The Combined Code states <i>inter alia </i>that
                  a Remuneration Committee of a UK public company should consist
                  exclusively of non-executive directors who are independent of
                  management and free from any business or other relationship
                  which could materially affect the exercise of their
                  independent judgement. The Remuneration Committee makes
                  recommendations to the Board within agreed terms of reference,
                  on the Company&#146;s framework of executive remuneration and
                  determines on the Board&#146;s behalf specific remuneration
                  packages for each of the executive directors, including
                  pension rights and any compensation payments.<o:p>
                  </o:p>
                  </span></font></p>

                  to fill Board vacancies and for making recommendations on
                  Board composition and balance. This committee is chaired by Mr
                  Birch and its other members are Mr Fink, Mr Finegold and Mr
                  Maltby. A majority of the members of the Nomination Committee

                  either the chairman of the Board or another non-executive
