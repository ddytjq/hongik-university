<p class=MsoNormal align=center style='text-align:center'><b><span
style='font-family:Arial'>THE GREAT RACE 2002<o:p></o:p></span></b></p>

<p class=MsoNormal align=center style='text-align:center'><b><span
style='font-family:Arial'>X-CUP REGULATIONS <br>
(Revised 3-02)<o:p></o:p></span></b></p>

<p class=MsoNormal align=center style='text-align:center'><b><span
style='font-family:Arial'><o:p>&nbsp;</o:p></span></b></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-family:Arial'>I. SPONSORSHIP<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-family:Arial'>1.<span
style='mso-spacerun:yes'>   </span>Greatrace, L.L.C. reserves the right to sell
overall (title) sponsorship <span class=GramE>of<span
style='mso-spacerun:yes'>  </span>X</span>-Cup. X-Cup teams will be required to
display event signage and X-Cup signage on their uniforms, support vehicle and
competing vehicles. <o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-family:Arial'>2.<span
style='mso-spacerun:yes'>   </span>X-Cup teams are responsible for selling
local sponsorship to cover travel and vehicle expenses.<span
style='mso-spacerun:yes'>  </span>Team sponsors may not conflict with Event
Sponsors and must conform to all Event <span class=GramE>Sponsorship<span
style='mso-spacerun:yes'>  </span>rules</span>.<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-family:Arial'>3.<span
style='mso-spacerun:yes'>    </span>Each X-Cup entry will be required to
display X-Cup title sponsor signage on the competing vehicle, support vehicle
and team uniforms in addition to the required Event Signage. <o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-family:Arial'><o:p>&nbsp;</o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-family:Arial'>II. VEHICLES<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-family:Arial'>Vehicles must conform to
Great Race Vehicle Rules and Authorized Modifications.<span
style='mso-spacerun:yes'>  </span>Vehicles may be loaned to the students by
companies or individuals. In the event individuals wish to make a tax
deductible donation of a vehicle to the X-Cup project, the vehicle may be
donated to The Great Race Automotive Hall of Fame, a 501 c3 corporation. The
vehicle will be loaned to students designated by the donor for race preparation
which may include modification and restoration. The vehicle will be available
to the students for use in the Great Race for a specified time. At the
conclusion of the project, the vehicle will be returned to the Hall of Fame
where it may be displayed, traded or sold.<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-family:Arial'><o:p>&nbsp;</o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-family:Arial'>III. Level of Restoration<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-family:Arial'>The Level of Restoration is
based upon three levels of vehicle restoration to be performed in the current
school term by students as outlined.<span style='mso-spacerun:yes'>   
</span>This information is useful in media releases, etc<span class=GramE>..</span><o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-family:Arial'><o:p>&nbsp;</o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-family:Arial'>Level 1&#8209; a body-off
restoration including dismantling and re&#8209;assembly of the engine,
transmission,<span style='mso-spacerun:yes'>         </span><o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-family:Arial'><span
style='mso-spacerun:yes'>              </span><span class=GramE>rear</span> end
and brakes. <o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><span class=GramE><span style='font-family:Arial'>Level 2&#8209;
dismantling and re&#8209;assembly of the engine, transmission and brakes.</span></span><span
style='font-family:Arial'><span style='mso-spacerun:yes'>  </span><o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-family:Arial'><o:p>&nbsp;</o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><span class=GramE><span style='font-family:Arial'>Level 3&#8209;
less than Level 2.</span></span><span style='font-family:Arial'><span
style='mso-spacerun:yes'>  </span><o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-family:Arial'><o:p>&nbsp;</o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-family:Arial'>IV. DOCUMENTATION OF
RESTORATION<span style='mso-spacerun:yes'>      </span><o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-family:Arial'>Written continuing progress
reports, supported by a <b>minimum of 4 photographs</b> labeled on the reverse
with date, car number, names of high school and students, and work in progress,
are due at Great Race Headquarters by the <b><u>10th of each month</u></b> to
qualify for participation.<span style='mso-spacerun:yes'>  </span>No exceptions
please!<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-family:Arial'><o:p>&nbsp;</o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-family:Arial'>V. TO QUALIFY, TEAMS MUST:<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-family:Arial'>1.<span
style='mso-spacerun:yes'>     </span><span class=GramE>be</span> from an
accredited high school or secondary level educational institution which is
defined <o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-left:22.5pt'><span class=GramE><span
style='font-family:Arial'>as</span></span><span style='font-family:Arial'>
a<span style='mso-spacerun:yes'>  </span>magnet school, technical or vocational
high school, career center, traditional high school, exploratory high school or
other institution that awards a high school diploma or equivalency, herein
called High School<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-family:Arial'>2.<span
style='mso-spacerun:yes'>     </span><span class=GramE>be</span> endorsed by
the Board of Directors of the participating institution <o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-family:Arial'>3.<span
style='mso-spacerun:yes'>     </span><span class=GramE>be</span> managed by a
shop or vocational class instructor herein referred to as the Team Leader<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-family:Arial'>4.<span
style='mso-spacerun:yes'>     </span><span class=GramE>enter</span> a pre&#8209;1951
vehicle or pre-1960 sports car or race car or motorcycle.<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-family:Arial'>5.<span
style='mso-spacerun:yes'>     </span><span class=GramE>raise</span> necessary
funding for the restoration/preparation of the vehicle and travel expenses <o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal>6.<span style='font-family:Arial'>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
X-Cup entrants must have <b>three or four different navigators</b>, each
running a minimum of <o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-family:Arial'><span
style='mso-spacerun:yes'>        </span><span class=GramE>two</span>
stages.&nbsp; <b>Each X-Cup team leader must furnish its driver/navigator
schedule for the <o:p></o:p></b></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><b><span style='font-family:Arial'><span
style='mso-spacerun:yes'>        </span><span class=GramE>6 qualifying stages
at Registration, Thursday, June 13.</span></span></b><span style='font-family:
Arial'>&nbsp; Also, X-Cup entrants must have <o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-family:Arial'><span
style='mso-spacerun:yes'>        </span><span class=GramE>a</span> rookie
navigator on <u>at least one of the last two days of the Championship Run</u>.<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='background:#F3F3F3'><span style='font-family:Arial'>7.<span
style='mso-spacerun:yes'>     </span>Furnish certificate of ownership on the vehicle
and current certificate of insurance naming <o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='background:#F3F3F3'><span style='font-family:Arial'><span
style='mso-spacerun:yes'>        </span><span class=GramE>the</span>
competition vehicle and valid through </span><st1:date Year="2002" Day="23"
Month="6"><span style='font-family:Arial'>June 23, 2002</span></st1:date><span
style='font-family:Arial'><span style='mso-spacerun:yes'>  </span>to
Headquarters by </span><st1:date Year="2002" Day="1" Month="6"><b><span
 style='font-family:Arial'>June 1, 2002</span></b></st1:date><span
style='font-family:Arial'>.<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='background:#F3F3F3'><span style='font-family:Arial'><span
style='mso-spacerun:yes'> </span><o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-family:Arial'><o:p>&nbsp;</o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-family:Arial'><o:p>&nbsp;</o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><span class=GramE><span style='font-family:Arial'>VI.
PARTICIPATING</span></span><span style='font-family:Arial'> STUDENTS &#8209;
REQUIREMENTS:<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-family:Arial'>The instructor/teacher of each
participating team must forward a brief profile of Student Team<span
style='mso-spacerun:yes'>  </span>Members (hereinafter, STMs) including:<span
style='mso-spacerun:yes'>  </span>Social Security #, birth date, current
addresses, phone number, e-mail (if applicable), a transcript of each STM's
grades, interests, date of graduation and future plans and a notarized parental
consent form and release for each team member by March 30.<span
style='mso-spacerun:yes'>  </span>Each STM must:<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-family:Arial'>1.<span
style='mso-spacerun:yes'>     </span><span class=GramE>be</span> enrolled in a
class of an accredited school as defined in V.1. <o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-family:Arial'>2.<span
style='mso-spacerun:yes'>     </span><span class=GramE>be</span> less than 20
years old <o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-family:Arial'>3.<span
style='mso-spacerun:yes'>     </span><span class=GramE>be</span> involved in the
restoration/preparation of the competing vehicle and fund raising for the<span
style='mso-spacerun:yes'>  </span><o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-family:Arial'><span
style='mso-spacerun:yes'>        </span><span class=GramE>project</span><o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-family:Arial'>4.<span
style='mso-spacerun:yes'>     </span><span class=GramE>have</span> maintained a
2.0 grade average in academic subjects for the 2002 school year.<span
style='mso-spacerun:yes'>  </span><o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-family:Arial'>5.<span
style='mso-spacerun:yes'>     </span><span class=GramE>navigate</span> a
minimum of<span style='mso-spacerun:yes'>  </span>2 stages.<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-family:Arial'><o:p>&nbsp;</o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-family:Arial'>VII. EVENT OPERATIONS<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-family:Arial'>To assist in offsetting the
expense of complimentary entry fees, each X-Cup team will be required to supply
assistance in event operations on a rotating basis throughout the event. <o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-family:Arial'><o:p>&nbsp;</o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><b><span style='font-family:Arial'>X-CUP STUDENTS QUALIFYING
FOR SCHOLARSHIP FUNDS IN PREVIOUS EVENTS<o:p></o:p></span></b></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-family:Arial'><o:p>&nbsp;</o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-family:Arial'>VII. SCHOLARSHIP FUND <o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-family:Arial'>A. Management<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-family:Arial'>The Great Race Automotive
Hall of Fame administers the X-Cup Scholarship Fund. The funds are deposited in
an interest bearing account as received and interest earned is retained by the
Great Race Automotive Hall of Fame to partially offset cost of administrating
the Fund.<span style='mso-spacerun:yes'>  </span>Disputes or questions
regarding the administration, distribution or maintenance of the Scholarship
Fund are under the jurisdiction of the Board of Directors of the Great Race
Automotive Hall of Fame, whose decisions will be final. <span style='mso-tab-count:
1'> </span><o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-family:Arial'>B. Use<span
style='mso-spacerun:yes'>    </span><o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-family:Arial'>Scholarship Funds must be
applied toward post-secondary training in the automotive field at a trade or
technical school or post-secondary course work that will apply to an associate
or bachelor degree at an accredited college or university. <o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-family:Arial'>C. Allocation<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-family:Arial'>The Team Leader must furnish
a sworn affidavit confirming the names, addresses, phone numbers, social
security numbers and dates of graduation of all STMs at Registration.<span
style='mso-spacerun:yes'>  </span>Funds are allocated to teams based upon their
final finishing positions in the following percentages:<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-family:Arial'><span
style='mso-spacerun:yes'>      </span><span style='mso-tab-count:1'>      </span>1.&#8209;
30%<span style='mso-tab-count:2'>             </span>3.&#8209; 15%<span
style='mso-tab-count:2'>             </span><span style='mso-spacerun:yes'> 
</span>5.&#8209; 8%<span style='mso-tab-count:2'>             </span> 7.&#8209;
5%<span style='mso-spacerun:yes'>                   </span>9.&#8209; 2%<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-family:Arial'><span
style='mso-spacerun:yes'>      </span><span style='mso-tab-count:1'>      </span>2.&#8209;
20%<span style='mso-tab-count:2'>             </span>4.&#8209; 10%<span
style='mso-tab-count:2'>             </span><span style='mso-spacerun:yes'> 
</span>6.&#8209; 7%<span style='mso-tab-count:2'>             </span> 8.&#8209;
3%<span style='mso-spacerun:yes'>        </span><span style='mso-tab-count:
3'>                              </span><o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-family:Arial'>D. Distribution<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-family:Arial'>Each winning STM will have
up to one year following graduation to apply for his or her share of the
Scholarship Fund.<span style='mso-spacerun:yes'>  </span>If the funds are not
requested within five years of the finish of the Race in which the STM won the
funds, the funds become the property of The Great Race Automotive Hall of Fame,
a 501 c3 corporation.<span style='mso-spacerun:yes'>   </span><o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-family:Arial'>E.<span
style='mso-spacerun:yes'>  </span>To Request Scholarship Funds<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-family:Arial'>1.<span
style='mso-spacerun:yes'>  </span>Team leader will furnish a certified list of
STMs eligible for scholarship funds by July 31. <o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-family:Arial'>2.<span
style='mso-spacerun:yes'>  </span>STMs will be reimbursed for expenses paid to
a qualifying institution for tuition and/or book <o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-family:Arial'><span
style='mso-spacerun:yes'>     </span><span class=GramE>expenses</span> upon the
receipt of a paid invoice from the qualifying institution, or funds may be<span
style='mso-spacerun:yes'>          </span><o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-family:Arial'><span
style='mso-spacerun:yes'>     </span><span class=GramE>paid</span> directly to
the qualifying institution based on a bill in the STM’s name.<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-family:Arial'>3.<span
style='mso-spacerun:yes'>  </span>STMs should apply to Great Race Headquarters
with Item No. 2, name of X-Cup team and <o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-family:Arial'><span
style='mso-spacerun:yes'>     </span><span class=GramE>date(s)</span> of Great
Races in which he/she participated.<o:p></o:p></span></p>
