<H1>Policy Changes</H1>
<HR>

<P>
<font size=+1>During the term of the policy there may be changes in the cover
you require. You may move house, want to add items on to your
policy etc.
<P>
It is important that we know about these changes so that you remain
properly covered.
<P>

<p>



If you want to notify us of any changes email us at <A HREF=mailto:info@mail.iicl.co.uk> info@mail.iicl.co.uk</A>
telling us your name, address, and your policy number. Give as
many details of the changes as you think relevant. If you are
not sure what is relevant, tell us anyway.
<P>
Do not notify us of <i>claims</i> using this email box.  You should notify us of a claim at <a href="mailto:claims@mail.iicl.co.uk">claims@mail.iicl.co.uk</a>.
<P>
