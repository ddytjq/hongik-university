<p>A new French Record was set by Antoine Hernandez. Congratulations !!
He thereby improved the old World Record of Michel Dufayard (149.12 m),
which was set in Shrewsbury GB in 1992, and now the Records of the French
and German are <a href="natrec.htm">tied at 166 m</a> ! Antoine also left
behind the American Record (158 m). Christophe Taboureux (154 m) was above
the old record as well. Most of other throwers had personal bests as well,
due to the excellent conditions.
<p>Unfortunately, there was no Swiss participation...
<p>I'm sure we will witness a French-German-American(-Australian?) battle
for distance records in the years to come.
