<h1 align="center"><font color="#FFFFFF" size="4">Uninsured Loss Recoveries 
Limited</font></h1>
<p align="center"><font color="#FFFFFF">Personal Legal Protection Policy</font></p>
<p align="center"><font color="#FFFFFF">The period of insurance is concurrent 
with the Insured Person's Householders Insurance as arranged by the Insured 
Person's Intermediary.</font></p>
<blockquote>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">This document is a legally binding 
  contract of insurance between You (the person insured) and Us (Equity Red Star 
  Motor \Policies at Lloyd's). Equity Red Star Motor Policies at Lloyd's has 
  agreed to insure you under the terms, conditions and exceptions contained in 
  this document.</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">Signed for and on behalf of Equity Red 
  Star Motor Policies at Lloyd's</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">This insurance will only apply if</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">1 The Insured Incident happens within 
  the territorial limit and <br>
  2 the time of occurrence is within the Period of Insurance and <br>
  3 any legal proceedings will be under the jurisdiction of a court or as 
  authorised by Us within the Territorial Limit and <br>
  4 reasonable prospects always exist for the recovery of damages or other 
  remedy or for a successful defence</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">Meaning of words</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">1 We, Us, Our</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">Equity Red Star Motor Policies at 
  Lloyd's</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">2 Equity Red Star Motor Policies at 
  Lloyd's is made up of the Lloyd's Underwriters who have insured you under this 
  contract. Each Underwriter is only liable for their own share of the risk and 
  not for any other share. You can ask us for the names of the Underwriters and 
  the share of the risk each has taken on.</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">3 Scheme provider</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">Uninsured Loss Recoveries Limited, 
  Station House, Station Road, Knebworth, SG3 6HL, Herts, telephone 01438 814533</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">4 You, Your</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">The Person to whom the policy has been 
  issued</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">5 Insured</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">You and members of your family 
  permanently living with you provided that any person claiming under this 
  policy has your agreement to claim.</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">6 Appointed Lawyer</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">The Lawyer or other suitably qualified 
  person appointed under condition 2 to act for an Insured.</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">7 Legal Costs</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">A reasonable amount of all costs 
  reasonably incurred by the appointed Lawyer on a standard basis and the costs 
  incurred by opponents in civil cases where the Insured is made liable to pay 
  them or pay them with our agreement.</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">8 Territorial limits</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">United Kingdom, Northern Ireland, the 
  Isle of Man and the Channel Islands.</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">9 Period of Insurance</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">The period not exceeding 12 months for 
  which we have agreed to accept and you have paid the premium</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">10 Time of occurrence</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">a) In civil cases the date at which the 
  cause of action first occurred.</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">b) In criminal cases the time at which 
  the Insured commenced or was alleged to have commenced to violate the criminal 
  law in question.</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">Insured Incident</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">1 Personal Injury</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">Any event which causes the death of or 
  bodily injury to an Insured</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">2 Contract Disputes</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">Any contractual dispute arising out of 
  an agreement entered into or allegedly entered into for:- <br>
  the sale, purchase or hire of any goods</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">obtaining services</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">The Insured must have entered or 
  allegedly entered into the agreement during the Period of Insurance.</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">The amount in dispute must be over £100.</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">3 Prosecution Defence</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">Anything done or not done (or alleged to 
  have been done or not done) by the Insured in the Insured's normal paid 
  employment which leads to:</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">The Insured being prosecuted in a 
  criminal court or civil action being taken against the Insured under the Race 
  Relations Act 1976 or the Sex Discrimination Act 1975 or</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">An Improvement Notice or Prohibition 
  Notice being served on the Insured under the Health and Safety at Work etc. 
  Act 1974 or the Food Safety Act 1990 or an Enforcement Deregistration or 
  Transfer Prohibition Notice under the Data Protection Act 1984 or</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">The serving of an Order of Enforcement 
  under the Fair Employment (Northern Ireland) Act 1989 or</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">Civil action being taken against the 
  Insured for compensation under Section 22 and/or 23 of the Data Protection Act 
  1984.</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">4 Employment</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">The cost of representation in respect of 
  an application to an Industrial Tribunal for unfair dismissal as defined by 
  the Employment Rights Act 1996 or the equivalent provisions in Northern 
  Ireland arising from the Insured's normal salaried or waged employment 
  provided that the Insured has sought and followed legal advice from the scheme 
  provider.</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">5 Tax disputes</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">An investigation by the Inland Revenue 
  into the Insured's personal tax affairs arising out of the Insured's normal 
  salaried or waged employment.</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">What is covered</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">Following an Insured Incident, We/Scheme 
  Provider will negotiate a claim to:</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">1 Pursue the legal rights of an Insured 
  or</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">2 Defend an Insured against civil or 
  criminal actions.</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">3 Appeal or defend an appeal against the 
  impositions of the terms of a notice specified under Insured Incident (3) 
  Prosecution Defence</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">4 Appeal or defend an appeal against 
  judgement, conviction or sentence of the court or tribunal and if a lawyer is 
  appointed We will pay the Legal Costs incurred.</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">The most we will pay in Legal Costs 
  including Legal Costs incurred in claims related by time or by cause is 
  £25,000.</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">What is not covered</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">We will not give cover for claims 
  arising from or relating to:</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">1 Claims reported to Us or Scheme 
  Provider more than 180 days from the Insured Incident</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">2 Legal Costs, including any costs of 
  appeals, incurred before We agree to pay them</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">3 Any sickness, disease or naturally 
  occurring condition or degenerative process</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">4 Contracts regarding the Insured's 
  trade, business, profession other than a claim relating to an employment 
  dispute</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">5 Any matters concerning the advice, 
  design, specification, construction, conversion or extension on any land or of 
  any buildings other than the normal maintenance or normal repair of Your 
  principle home.</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">6 A lease, tenancy or licence to occupy.</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">7 Dishonesty or violence by the Insured.</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">8 Ownership of use other than as a 
  passenger of a motor vehicle or a contract for sale, purchase, hire, 
  insurance, adaptation, repair, service or test of any motor vehicle or of its 
  parts or accessories.</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">9 Fines or other penalty or damages 
  which the Insured is ordered to pay by the relevant court</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">10 A prosecution deliberately or 
  intentionally solicited by the Insured</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">11 A contract of Insurance in so far as 
  the dispute is in respect of the sum of money or other compensation payable 
  under such a contract</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">12 Any claim caused by or contributed to 
  by or arising from ionising radiations or contamination by radioactivity from 
  an irradiated nuclear fuel or the toxic, explosive or other hazardous 
  properties of any nuclear component of it.</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">13 Any consequence of war, invasion, act 
  of foreign enemy, hostilities (whether war be declared or not), civil war, 
  rebellion, revolution, insurrection, military or usurped power.</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">14 Loss, destruction or damage caused by 
  pressure waves made by an aircraft and other aerial devices travelling at 
  sonic or supersonic speeds.</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">15 Any dispute with Us not dealt with 
  under condition 7.</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">16 Where the Insured: Takes legal action 
  without Our agreement or the Appointed Lawyers agreement or causes delay or 
  fails to give reasonable evidence to Us/Scheme Provider or the Appointed 
  Lawyers.</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">17 Parking or obstruction offences</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">18 Any claim under insured incident 4 
  Employment where the Insured is or has been a director, partner or sole owner 
  of the employer involved in the dispute.</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">19 Any claim in respect of employment 
  that occurs within 90 days of this policy coming into force.</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">Conditions</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">1 The Insured must:</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">a) Keep to the terms and conditions of 
  this policy.</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">b) Take reasonable steps to minimise any 
  amount We have to pay and attempt to prevent any incident which may cause a 
  claim.</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">c) Send every notice or communication he 
  or she has to give Us or Scheme Provider in writing.</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">d) Give us or Scheme Provider details in 
  writing of any claim and provide us with any information needed by Us or 
  Scheme Provider.</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">2 a) We can take over and conduct in the 
  name of the Insured any claim or proceedings at any time before proceedings 
  are issued for which a lawyer or other suitably qualified person is required. 
  We or Scheme Provider can negotiate any claim on behalf of the Insured.</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">b) If We or Scheme Provider think that 
  legal proceedings should be issued for which a lawyer or suitably qualified 
  person is required or if there is a conflict of interest, the Insured may 
  choose to nominate an Appointed Lawyer by immediately providing Us or Scheme 
  Provider with the name and address of the nominated Appointed Lawyer in 
  writing. We or Scheme Provider may refuse this nomination in exceptional 
  circumstances. If there is a dispute over the choice of Appointed Lawyer, this 
  can be referred to arbitration under Condition 7.</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">c) before the Insured exercises his/her 
  choice of lawyer ((b) above) We or Scheme Provider may instruct an Appointed 
  Lawyer.</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">d) The Appointed Lawyer will be 
  appointed by Us or the Scheme Provider in the name of and on behalf of the 
  Insured.</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">e) We and the Scheme Provider will have 
  direct access to the Appointed Lawyer and the Insured must co-operate fully 
  with Us/Scheme Provider and the Appointed Lawyer in all respects and keep 
  Us/Scheme Provider fully informed of all recent developments in the claim.</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">The Insured must give any instructions 
  to the Appointed Lawyer that We or Scheme Provider require.</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">3 a) The Insured must tell Us or Scheme 
  Provider of any offer made to settle the claim.</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">b) No negotiations or agreement to 
  settle the claim must be made without Our or Scheme Providers approval.</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">c) If the insured does not accept an 
  offer to settle the claim no further Legal Costs will be paid by Us unless We 
  agree.</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">d) We reserve the right to reimburse the 
  Insured for the damages sought in place of pursuing or defending legal 
  proceedings.</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">4 a) If We or Scheme Provider ask the 
  Insured must instruct the Appointed Lawyer to have legal costs taxed, assessed 
  or audited.</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">b) The Insured must take every step to 
  recover Legal Costs from opponents that We have to pay and these must be paid 
  to Us.</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">5 If the Appointed Lawyer refuses to 
  continue acting for the Insured or the Insured withdraws the claim from the 
  Appointed Lawyer, Our cover will cease immediately, unless We or Scheme 
  Provider agree to instruct another Appointed Lawyer.</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">6 If the Insured withdraws from a case 
  without Our or Scheme Provider's agreement, no Legal Costs and Expenses will 
  be covered by the Policy.</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">7 If We or Scheme Provider and the 
  Insured disagree about the choice of the Appointed Lawyer, the acceptance, 
  rejection, presentation or ending of a claim, a lawyer (chosen jointly by Us 
  and the Insured) will decide the matter, as long as We both ask for this in 
  writing.</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">If there is no agreement between Us and 
  the Insured, We will ask the President of a relevant Law Society to nominate 
  the lawyer. All costs of resolving the difference must be paid by the party 
  who loses.</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">8 We may cancel this policy at any time 
  by telling You 7 days beforehand.</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">9 We will not pay any claim covered 
  under any other policy, or which would have been covered if this policy did 
  not exist.</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">Legal Advice Help Line</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">These services are provided 24 hours a 
  day 7 days a week and provide legal advice over the telephone in respect of 
  personal legal problems that you may have including: employment problems, 
  consumer disputes, divorce, separation, maintenance and custody, disputes with 
  neighbours, faulty workmanship, insurance disputes, landlord and tenancy 
  problems, state benefit and wills. We cannot accept responsibility for a 
  breakdown in this service which is beyond our control. Details for contacting 
  this service will be provided on taking out this insurance.</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">Problems</font></p>
  <p align="left"><font color="#FFFFFF">We or Scheme Provider aim to provide a 
  quality service at all times. If however You feel that We or Scheme Provider 
  have not provided a quality service, please contact the Managing Director, 
  Uninsured Loss Recoveries Limited, Station House, Station Road, Knebworth, SG3 
  6HL Herts. If you are unhappy with the way things are dealt with you may write 
  to the Chief Executive, Equity Red Star Motor Policies at Lloyd's, Library 
  House, New Road, Brentwood, Essex CM14 4GD. If You are still unhappy, You can 
  contact The Manager, Complaints &amp; Advisory Department, Lloyd's of London 1 
  Lime Street, London EC3M 7HA, telephone number 0171 623 7100.</font></p>
