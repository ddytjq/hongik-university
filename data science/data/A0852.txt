		  <p class="contentsubhead">How much does it cost?</p>
		  <p>Our minimum portfolio value is £75,000 and we charge an annual fee for our management service on a sliding scale related to the amount managed on your behalf.  </p>
		  <p>In addition we charge commission on share dealing undertaken during the management of your portfolio and this is also on a sliding scale dependent on the sums involved.</p>
		  <p>Typically your total fees, including commissions, will range between 0.75% to 1.5% per annum dependent upon the amount we manage for you and the level of dealing activity undertaken on your behalf during the year.  We believe this compares very favourably with the charges levied by other investment managers, including unit trusts, whilst still delivering the benefit of a tailored portfolio, created and managed just for you.</p>
		  <p>Details of our actual fee and commission scales are given below. Fees are subject to Value Added Tax at the standard rate and are payable half yearly in advance, based on the value of your portfolio at time of charge.</p>
