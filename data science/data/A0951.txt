                              <p>Charges can be complex as they vary from fund 
                                to fund and from provider to provider. However, 
                                one of the advantages of buying your ISA through 
                                FundsNetwork is that you can benefit from the 
                                savings that are made through volume discounts. 
                                What's more Citibank don't take any commission 
                                from the initial charge (to set up your account) 
                                which means more of your money is invested on 
                                you behalf. All we take is a proportion of the 
                                annual charges which apply thereafter (0.5% of 
                                the fund value each year, paid to us by Fidelity 
                                out of their share of the annual management fee). 
                              </p>
                              <p>If you go to a fund provider direct you can expect 
                                to pay as much as 5% to set up an account, and 
                                an annual charge of around 1%. As you will see 
                                when you choose your individual funds, ours can 
                                be considerably lower*. </p>

                                The majority of funds offered through FundsNetwork 
                                can be purchased for an initial charge of <b>2.00% 
                                and under</b>, considerably lower than the average 
                                initial charge for ISA funds bought directly from 
                                the provider. For example, a £7,000 Maxi ISA investment 
                                at 1.25% initial charge rather than 3.25% will 
                                <b>save you over £130</b>. </p>
                              <p>There are some of the funds that are available 
                                at <b>no initial charge</b> - these are 'no-load'; 
                                you pay nothing up front. </p>

                                fee. You'll find these lists within the "<a href="fundlist/index.htm">Who

                                Each fund carries an annual fee, which covers 
                                the cost of professional management of your money 
                                by the fund provider. Through FundsNetwork, you 
                                pay the standard annual fee for each fund you 
                                purchase; these are detailed in the funds section 
                                of <a href="#" onClick="MM_openBrWindow('jump_fi.htm','jump','status=yes,scrollbars=yes,resizable=yes,width=780,height=530'); return false;">Fidelity's

                                charges tend to be around 1% to 1.5%, but can 
                                be slightly less or more. </p>
										
                              <p><b>Switching charges</b><br>
                                Switching between funds or fund providers could 
                                hardly be simpler or cheaper. All dealing is online

                                move between funds offered by different fund companies.
