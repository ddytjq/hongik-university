  <ul>
    <li><a href="#Overview">Overview</a>

    <li><a href="#Levels of Service">Levels Of Service</a></li>
  </ul>
  
  <hr size=0 noshade color="#ffffff">
        
  <p><a name="Overview"></a><b>Overview</b></p>
  <p>Business On Line - Bank of Ireland's new internet-based banking service for business - has been developed in order to give you more control and flexibility in carrying out financial transactions with us.</p>
  <p>Using the very latest internet technology, Business On Line provides on-line access to accounts and offers many additional <a href="#Benefits of Business On Line">benefits</a> to previous electronic banking systems.</p>
  <p>Business On Line is a highly advanced service and is unique in the Irish market. It responds to individual business requirements by offering three levels of banking service. </p>

  <hr size=0 noshade color="#ffffff">

  <p><a name="Benefits of Business On Line"></a><b>Benefits of Business On Line </b></p>
  <p>Business On Line offers many benefits. It:

  <ul>
    <li>offers the first six months service charge free to any business customer who registers before 30 September 2000 
    <li>gives you complete cash management control at your fingertips 
    <li>provides easy, secure access to your accounts anywhere, anytime 
    <li>enables you to make payments easily and efficiently anywhere in the world 
    <li>provides up-to-date information to help you spot windows of opportunity and maximise the value of funds under your management <A href="#footnote"><sup>1</sup></a> 
    <li>provides the latest financial information at the touch of a button</li>
  </ul>
  
  <p>To operate the service all you need is MS Internet Explorer (Ver 4). This is one of the most widely used browsing tools and comes pre-installed on most new PCs. Bank of Ireland can provide you with a copy of MS Internet Explorer if required. </p>
  <p><font face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">This service is continually enhanced and upgraded. All updates are sent to customers online.</p>
  <p>Business On Line is open 365 days a year.* </p>
  <p><font face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica" size="1">*Occasionally we will carry out essential maintenance outside normal business hours. </p>
		
  <p><font face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica" size="1"><a name="footnote"><sup>1</sup></a>Account balances quoted as at close of business on the previous banking day. In the case of retail accounts, items which have been presented on the banking day of access are also shown for information purposes.  </p>
	
 
  <hr size=0 noshade color="#ffffff">
  
  <p><a name="Levels of Service"></a><b>Levels of Service</b></p>
  <p> The functions on Business On Line are grouped together logically. Users will receive functionality depending upon the level of service chosen. The&nbsp;three service levels accommodate users with different banking requirements, from standard domestic banking needs (Level 1) through Domestic and Cross-Border Payments (Level 2) to a Comprehensive electronic banking and treasury service (Level 3).</p>
  <p>Level 1 Standard Domestic Banking Needs</p>
  <p>Level 2 Cross-Border Banking</p>
