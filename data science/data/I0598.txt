Blades seal deal for Ten Heuvel
10 Jul<br>
			<SPAN Class='gentext' ><P>Sheffield United have completed the signing of Dutch striker Laurens Ten Heuvel from Telstar.</P><P>The 26-year-old forward has signed a three-year deal at Bramall Lane and has moved to the club in a six-figure deal.</P><P>Ten Heuvel has some experience of the English game having signed for Barnsley in 1996 but he failed to establish himself in the first team at Oakwell and played in only eight League games for the club in three difficult seasons.</P><P>He spent several years in Austrian football before signing for Telstar last season and he went on to score 20 goals in just 31 games for the Dutch club.</P><P>"I've been tracking Laurens for some months now and am really happy that we have managed to do the deal," Blades boss Neil Warnock told the club's official website.</P><P>"He brings us something different because of his style of play and while there is now real competition for places in attack his signing increases our options.</P><P>"He will have to fight for his place with the rest but is a good addition to our squad."</P>
