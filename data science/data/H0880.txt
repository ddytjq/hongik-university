      <td width="463" height="21"><font color="#000080">Fast Atom Bombardment.
        Technique used to ionize a compound usually in a glycerol matrix by
        bombarding it with xenon atoms or other neutral atoms.&nbsp; The
        ionization is accomplished in a vacuum with the sample placed on a
        probe, although flow FAB at low flow rates is sometimes practiced.&nbsp;
        Most often used in conjunction with a high resolution magnetic sector
        mass spectrometer.&nbsp; A very good description of FAB can be found at&nbsp;</font></td>
