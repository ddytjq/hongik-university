      <p><font face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2"><b>This is 
        a summary of cover</b> <br>
        Membership is subject to acceptance of your application by BUPA, the rules 
        and table of benefits of the scheme and any special conditions that may 
        apply. Full details of general terms and conditions are available on request 
        and will be sent to you when you join. Precise terms of cover available 
        will depend on an assessment of your individual circumstances. </font> 
      <p> <font face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">Local HospitalCare 
        offers lower cost private medical cover at a network of around 180 specially 
        selected BUPA partnership network hospitals. All that&#146;s excluded 
        is the cost of seeing a consultant privately before you go into hospital 
        (which you can do via your GP). This enables costs to be kept down, without 
        the quality of care you receive in hospital being compromised.</font> 
      <p><font face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">You wil have 
        cover for eligible treatment:</font>

          of BUPA's partnership network hospitals</font></li>
        <li><font face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">For private 
          day-case and in-patient treatment</font></li>
        <li><font face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">For out-patient 
          care directly related to &#150; and following &#150; your hospital stay</font></li>
        <li><font face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">the opportunity 
          to reduce your subscriptions by selecting to pay an excess</font></li>
      </ul>
