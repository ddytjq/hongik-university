<h2 align="center">WFDF Member Status The following are the
flying disc sport association members of the World Flying Disc
Federation. </h2>

<h2 align="center">WFDF was founded in 1985.</h2>

<p>WFDF membership dues for Regular Members are $150 US annually.
Associate Membership is $75 US annually. Provisional membership
has no fee. Payment is due on January 31 of each year. Click <a
href="membership.html">here</a> for details of membership. For
payment information or if your national association is not a member of WFDF, please
contact the WFDF Executive Director Dan Roddick at <a
href="mailto:cyberstork@earthlink.net">cyberstork@earthlink.net</a> for
information about how to join. </p>

<p><font size="2"><strong>(updated 7/01/02)</strong></font></p>
