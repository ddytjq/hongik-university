</TD></TR>
<TR><TD COLSPAN="4">&nbsp;</TD></TR>
<TR><TD COLSPAN="4"><H3>Guidance Notes for Assured/Insured Person(s)</H3>
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD COLSPAN="4">This Policy contains important conditions, exclusions and 
limitations relating to the attachment and duration of cover in 
respect of each and every Period of Travel to be insured hereunder; 
particular reference should be made to the General Exclusions 
following, the General Provisions and Conditions within, and the 
Other Conditions, Exclusions and Limitations of Liability towards the 
end of this document.
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD COLSPAN="4">&nbsp;</TD></TR>
<TR><TD COLSPAN="4"><H2>General Exclusions</H2>
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD COLSPAN="4">Except where the Underwriters' specific prior agreement has been 
obtained, no cover shall attach under this Insurance in respect of, 
and for the entire duration of, any trip that is:
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD>&nbsp;</TD><TD VALIGN="TOP">(a)</TD><TD COLSPAN="2">expected at the date of its booking or commencement to exceed 31 
days in duration, or
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD>&nbsp;</TD><TD VALIGN="TOP">(b)</TD><TD COLSPAN="2">booked or commenced during convalescence following serious injury 
or illness, or
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD>&nbsp;</TD><TD VALIGN="TOP">(c)</TD><TD COLSPAN="2">expected at the date of its booking or commencement to involve 
hazardous or non-conventional holiday or business activities, or
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD>&nbsp;</TD><TD VALIGN="TOP">(d)</TD><TD COLSPAN="2">booked or undertaken by an Insured Person aged under 18 years at 
its commencement date unless, for the entire duration of such trip, 
he is to be accompanied by another person insured hereunder and aged 
over 18 years at the said date.
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD>&nbsp;</TD><TD VALIGN="TOP">(e)</TD><TD COLSPAN="2">Undertaken for the purposes of business travel.
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD COLSPAN="4">&nbsp;</TD></TR>
<TR><TD COLSPAN="4">Premium quotations for such excluded trips may be obtainable from 
Underwriters; full details should be submitted for their 
consideration. Examples of activities considered hazardous and/or 
non-conventional are:
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD VALIGN="TOP"><IMG SRC="../images/nv_blob.gif" width="10" height="14"></TD><TD COLSPAN="3">international overland journeys in Asia, Africa or South America 
(other than by rail)
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD VALIGN="TOP"><IMG SRC="../images/nv_blob.gif" width="10" height="14"></TD><TD COLSPAN="3">motorcycle touring
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD VALIGN="TOP"><IMG SRC="../images/nv_blob.gif" width="10" height="14"></TD><TD COLSPAN="3">professional entertaining
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD VALIGN="TOP"><IMG SRC="../images/nv_blob.gif" width="10" height="14"></TD><TD COLSPAN="3">manual work for gain or reward
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD VALIGN="TOP"><IMG SRC="../images/nv_blob.gif" width="10" height="14"></TD><TD COLSPAN="3">scuba diving
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD VALIGN="TOP"><IMG SRC="../images/nv_blob.gif" width="10" height="14"></TD><TD COLSPAN="3">other occupational, sporting, training or recreational activity 
carrying a generally recognised risk of serious injury.
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD COLSPAN="4">&nbsp;</TD></TR>
<TR><TD COLSPAN="4">Any medical condition you or an immediate relative or travelling 
companion have at the time of purchasing this insurance which has 
not been reported to us and been agreed and confirmed in writing.
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD COLSPAN="4">&nbsp;</TD></TR>
<TR><TD COLSPAN="4"><H2>Section 1: Medical, Repatriation and Other Expenses</H2>
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD COLSPAN="4"><B>To pay up to a maximum limit of &#163;3 million in respect of the 
following expenses, incurred within the Period of Insurance and the 
twelve months' period subsequent to its expiry:</B>
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD VALIGN="TOP">(a)</TD><TD COLSPAN="3"><B><I>Medical and Repatriation Expenses</I></B>
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD>&nbsp;</TD><TD COLSPAN="3"><B>Medical, hospital and treatment expenses (including associated travel 
expenses) incurred outside the United Kingdom and additional hotel 
and repatriation expenses</B> necessarily incurred as the result of the 
Insured Person becoming ill or sustaining accidental bodily injury 
during the Period of Travel, including compulsory quarantine and 
emergency dental treatment.
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD VALIGN="TOP">(b)</TD><TD COLSPAN="3"><B><I>Air Ambulance Charter</I></B>
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD>&nbsp;</TD><TD COLSPAN="3"><B>The charter of an air ambulance or the special use of air transport</B> 
(including qualified attendants) if agreed by prior 
consultation between the Insured Person's attending physicians 
and the Underwriters appointed advisors as being 
necessary for the transportation repatriation of the Insured 
Person.
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD VALIGN="TOP">(c)</TD><TD COLSPAN="3"><B><I>Escorting Relative or Friend</I></B>
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD>&nbsp;</TD><TD COLSPAN="3"><B>Reasonable travel and accommodation expenses of a relative or friend</B> 
(not necessarily an Insured Person) if he becomes severely 
incapacitated during the Period of Travel.
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD VALIGN="TOP">(d)</TD><TD COLSPAN="3"><B><I>Hospital Inconvenience Benefit</I></B>
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD>&nbsp;</TD><TD COLSPAN="3"><B>&#163;10 for each completed 24 hour period</B> that the Insured Person 
spends as a hospital in-patient outside the United Kingdom as the 
result of his sustaining accidental bodily injury or becoming ill 
during the Period of Travel, up to a maximum of &#163;600 in all, 
payable in addition to the indemnity under Subsection 1(a).
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD VALIGN="TOP">(e)</TD><TD COLSPAN="3"><B><I>Emergency Return to the United Kingdom</I></B>
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD>&nbsp;</TD><TD COLSPAN="3"><B>Additional repatriation expenses</B> necessarily incurred by the Insured 
Person consequent upon any of the following occurrences during the 
Period of Travel:
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD>&nbsp;</TD><TD VALIGN="TOP">(i)</TD><TD COLSPAN="2">the death, serious illness or injury of the Insured Person's 
relative, fianc&eacute;(e) or business colleague necessitating the 
Insured Person's presence in the United Kingdom.
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD>&nbsp;</TD><TD VALIGN="TOP">(ii)</TD><TD COLSPAN="2">the death, serious illness or injury of an accompanying Insured 
Person, or the repatriation of such person as provided for in 
paragraph (i).
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD VALIGN="TOP">(f)</TD><TD COLSPAN="3"><B><I>Alteration of Itinerary</I></B>
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD>&nbsp;</TD><TD COLSPAN="3"><B>Reasonable additional travel and accommodation expenses</B> incurred by 
the Insured Person consequent upon his being the victim of hijack, 
kidnap or the like terrorist or criminal act, or upon the 
cancellation of publicly licensed passenger transport services caused 
by strike, industrial action, hijack, terrorist act, criminal act, 
bomb scare, riot, civil commotion, fire, flood, earthquake, 
landslide, avalanche, adverse weather conditions, accident or 
mechanical breakdown, provided that these occur or commence during 
the Period of Travel.
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD VALIGN="TOP">(g)</TD><TD COLSPAN="3"><B><I>Funeral Expenses</I></B>
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD>&nbsp;</TD><TD COLSPAN="3"><B>Funeral expenses</B> and/or the cost of transporting the remains or ashes 
of the Insured Person to his former place of residence in the United 
Kingdom if he dies during the Period of Travel.
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD COLSPAN="4">&nbsp;</TD></TR>
<TR><TD COLSPAN="4"><H2>Section 2: Trip Cancellation and Disruption</H2>
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD VALIGN="TOP">(a)</TD><TD COLSPAN="3"><B><I>Cancellation, Curtailment and "Get you There" Expenses</I></B>
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD>&nbsp;</TD><TD COLSPAN="3"><B>To pay up to &#163;2,500 for irrecoverable loss of unused travel and 
accommodation expenses</B> paid in advance or for which there is a 
contractual liability, consequent upon the cancellation or 
curtailment of the pre-arranged Period of Travel; or if the Period of 
Travel is not cancelled.
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD>&nbsp;</TD><TD COLSPAN="3"><B>To pay up to &#163;500 in all in respect of reasonable additional 
travel and accommodation expenses</B> incurred by the Insured Person in 
fulfilling his pre-booked travel and accommodation commitments 
(following an occurrence covered under Sub-sections (i) to (iv) or in 
using equivalent alternative local accommodation (following an 
occurrence covered under Sub-section (v);
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD>&nbsp;</TD><TD COLSPAN="3"><B>Consequent upon any of the following occurrences:</B>
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD>&nbsp;</TD><TD VALIGN="TOP">(i)</TD><TD COLSPAN="2">the Insured Person sustaining bodily injury or becoming ill.
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD>&nbsp;</TD><TD VALIGN="TOP">(ii)</TD><TD COLSPAN="2">the death, injury or illness of the Insured Person's relative, 
fianc&eacute;(e) or business colleague or of any person with whom the 
Insured Person had arranged to travel or reside, or of the relative, 
fianc&eacute;(e) or business colleague of such person.
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD>&nbsp;</TD><TD VALIGN="TOP">(iii)</TD><TD COLSPAN="2">the Insured Person or any person with whom he had arranged to 
travel or reside being:
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD COLSPAN="2">&nbsp;</TD><TD VALIGN="TOP">(a)</TD><TD>quarantined or called for witness or jury service.
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD COLSPAN="2">&nbsp;</TD><TD VALIGN="TOP">(b)</TD><TD>made redundant, provided that such redundancy qualifies for 
payment under the United Kingdom's Redundancy Payments Acts.
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD COLSPAN="2">&nbsp;</TD><TD VALIGN="TOP">(c)</TD><TD>called for emergency duty as a member of the armed forces, the 
defence or civil administration, the police force, or the fire, 
rescue, public utility or medical services.
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD COLSPAN="2">&nbsp;</TD><TD VALIGN="TOP">(d)</TD><TD>required to be present at his home or place of business in the 
United Kingdom following burglary or major damage.
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD>&nbsp;</TD><TD VALIGN="TOP">(iv)</TD><TD COLSPAN="2">The cancellation or delayed departure for 24 hours or more of an 
aircraft, sea vessel or other publicly licensed form of passenger 
transport in which the Insured Person had previously booked to 
travel, resulting from any of the following contingencies: strike, 
industrial action, hijack, criminal act, bomb scare, 
riot, civil commotion, fire, flood, earthquake, landslide, avalanche, 
adverse weather conditions, accident or mechanical breakdown provided 
always that such contingency had not occurred, commenced or been 
announced before the booking was made in respect of the flight, 
voyage or journey thus affected.
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD>&nbsp;</TD><TD VALIGN="TOP">(v)</TD><TD COLSPAN="2">major damage rendering uninhabitable the accommodation in which 
the Insured Person had previously booked to reside during the Period 
of Travel, excluding any waterborne vessel or craft.
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD VALIGN="TOP">(b)</TD><TD COLSPAN="3"><B><I>Missed Departure and Transport Diversion</I></B>
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD>&nbsp;</TD><TD COLSPAN="3"><B>To pay up to &#163;500 in all in respect of reasonable additional 
travel accommodation expenses</B> incurred by the Insured Person in 
fulfilling his pre-booked travel and accommodation commitments:
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD>&nbsp;</TD><TD VALIGN="TOP">(i)</TD><TD COLSPAN="2">if at the commencement of the Period of Travel he misses his 
pre-booked international travel connection from the United Kingdom 
through disruption of his journey to the United Kingdom departure 
point occurring as the direct result of:
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD COLSPAN="2">&nbsp;</TD><TD VALIGN="TOP">(a)</TD><TD>a fellow passenger or crew member of the conveyance in which he 
is travelling sustaining bodily injury or becoming ill after such 
journey is commenced, or
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD COLSPAN="2">&nbsp;</TD><TD VALIGN="TOP">(b)</TD><TD>any of the contingencies specified in Subsection 2(a)(iv), 
provided always that such contingency had not occurred, commenced or 
been announced before the international travel booking was made if 
the United Kingdom journey is by scheduled public transport services, 
or before the United Kingdom journey commenced if such journey is by 
non-scheduled transport.
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD>&nbsp;</TD><TD VALIGN="TOP">(ii)</TD><TD COLSPAN="2">if at any time during the Period of Travel an aircraft, sea 
vessel or other publicly licensed passenger conveyance in which he is 
travelling has to be diverted from its pre-arranged destination as 
the result of:
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD COLSPAN="2">&nbsp;</TD><TD VALIGN="TOP">(a)</TD><TD>a fellow passenger or crew member sustaining bodily injury or 
becoming ill, or
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD COLSPAN="2">&nbsp;</TD><TD VALIGN="TOP">(b)</TD><TD>any of the contingencies specified in Subsection 2(a)(iv), 
provided always that such contingency had not occurred, commenced or 
been announced before the booking was made in respect of the flight, 
voyage or journey thus affected.
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD VALIGN="TOP">(c)</TD><TD COLSPAN="3"><B><I>Travel Delay Inconvenience Benefit</I></B>
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD>&nbsp;</TD><TD COLSPAN="3"><B>To pay as follows if the Insured Person is delayed</B> because of the 
late departure of an aircraft, sea vessel or other publicly licensed 
form of passenger transport in which he had previously booked to 
travel occurring as the result of any of the contingencies specified 
in subsection 2(a)(iv):
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD>&nbsp;</TD><TD VALIGN="TOP">(i)</TD><TD COLSPAN="2">Outward journey at commencement of the Period of Travel &#163;10 
for the first completed 12 hour period that departure is delayed and 
&#163;5 for each subsequent 12 hour period, up to a maximum of 
&#163;50 in all, and/or
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD>&nbsp;</TD><TD VALIGN="TOP">(ii)</TD><TD COLSPAN="2">All subsequent journeys during the Period of Travel Payment as 
in paragraph (i) up to a maximum of &#163;50 in all, provided always 
that such contingency had not occurred, commenced or been announced 
before the booking was made in respect of the flight, voyage or 
journey thus affected.
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD COLSPAN="4">&nbsp;</TD></TR>
<TR><TD COLSPAN="4"><B><I>Note:</I></B> <I>Cover in respect of travel and accommodation rearrangement is 
also provided under the Section 1 Sum Insured - see subsection 1(f).</I>
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD COLSPAN="4">&nbsp;</TD></TR>

</TD></TR>
<TR><TD COLSPAN="4"><B>To pay according to the following schedule of benefits</B> if at any time 
during the Period of Travel the Insured Person sustains bodily injury 
caused by an accident which solely and independently of any other 
cause results in his death or disablement as specified within twelve 
calendar months from the date of such accident:
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD COLSPAN="4">&nbsp;</TD></TR>
<TR><TD VALIGN="TOP"><B>1.</B></TD><TD COLSPAN="3"><TABLE WIDTH="100%"><TR><TD WIDTH="80%"><B>Death</B></TD><TD WIDTH="18%" VALIGN="BOTTOM"><B>&#163;25,000</B></TD></TR></TABLE>
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD VALIGN="TOP"><B>2.</B></TD><TD COLSPAN="3"><TABLE WIDTH="100%"><TR><TD WIDTH="80%"><B>Loss of one limb or one eye</B></TD><TD WIDTH="18%" VALIGN="BOTTOM"><B>&#163;25,000</B></TD></TR></TABLE>
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD VALIGN="TOP"><B>3.</B></TD><TD COLSPAN="3"><TABLE WIDTH="100%"><TR><TD WIDTH="80%"><B>Loss of two limbs or both eyes or one limb and one eye</B></TD><TD WIDTH="18%" VALIGN="BOTTOM"><B>&#163;25,000</B></TD></TR></TABLE>
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD VALIGN="TOP"><B>4.</B></TD><TD COLSPAN="3"><TABLE WIDTH="100%"><TR><TD WIDTH="80%"><B>Permanent Total Disablement</B></TD><TD WIDTH="18%" VALIGN="BOTTOM"><B>&#163;25,000</B></TD></TR></TABLE>
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD COLSPAN="4">&nbsp;</TD></TR>
<TR><TD COLSPAN="4">For children under 16 years of age the death benefit is limited to 
&#163;2,500.
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD COLSPAN="4">&nbsp;</TD></TR>
<TR><TD COLSPAN="4">The Underwriters shall only be liable:
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD VALIGN="TOP">(i)</TD><TD COLSPAN="3">under item 1 of the Schedule of Benefits if death occurs within twenty-four

</TD></TR>
<TR><TD VALIGN="TOP">(ii)</TD><TD COLSPAN="3">under items 2 and 3 of the Schedule of Benefits if the loss of

calendar months of the date of the accident.
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD VALIGN="TOP">(iii)</TD><TD COLSPAN="3">under item 4 of the Schedule of Benefits if the period of

</TD></TR>
<TR><TD COLSPAN="4">&nbsp;</TD></TR>
<TR><TD COLSPAN="4"><B>Disappearance Clause</B> If after a reasonable period of time has elapsed 
and all available evidence examined there is reason to presume the 
death of the Insured Person as the result of an occurrence covered by 
this Section of the Insurance, the disappearance of the Insured 
Person shall be deemed a claim hereunder. If at any time after 
payment of such claim the Insured Person shall be found to be living, 
all sums so paid shall be refunded to the Underwriters.
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD COLSPAN="4">&nbsp;</TD></TR>
<TR><TD COLSPAN="4"><H2>Section 4: Baggage and Personal Effects</H2>
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD COLSPAN="4"><B>To indemnify the Insured Person for loss of or damage to baggage and 
personal effects which are his property or responsibility occurring 
during the Period of Travel, up to &#163;1,500 in all.</B>
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD COLSPAN="4">Claims 
settlements for articles lost or destroyed will be based on the cost 
price of comparable new articles, less an appropriate allowance for 
age and condition. The Underwriters' liability for any single item is 
limited to &#163;250, a pair or set of articles being deemed a single 
item.
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD COLSPAN="4">&nbsp;</TD></TR>
<TR><TD COLSPAN="4"><B><I>Delayed Baggage</I></B>
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD COLSPAN="4"><B>To pay up to &#163;100 in all in respect of the cost of immediate 
necessities purchased or hired by the Insured Person</B> if on arrival at 
his outward destination he is deprived of his travel baggage for more 
than 12 hours because of temporary loss or misdirection by the 
Carriers, provided always that any amounts thus paid, other than hire 
charges, shall be deducted from the total of any claim becoming 
payable under this Section if the said baggage proves to be 
permanently lost.
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD COLSPAN="4">&nbsp;</TD></TR>
<TR><TD COLSPAN="4"><B><I>General Average and Salvage</I></B>
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD COLSPAN="4"><B>To indemnify the Insured Person up to &#163;500 in all in respect of 
any General Average and Salvage Charges</B> levied by Carriers under 
Maritime Law and/or Contract of Carriage and paid by the Insured 
Person to secure the release of his accompanied baggage if at any 
time whilst he is engaged on a journey by sea during the Period of 
Travel a situation of peril arises and the Carriers incur expenses 
and liability in the interest of the common safety of their 
passengers and cargo as a whole.
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD COLSPAN="4">&nbsp;</TD></TR>

</TD></TR>

</TD></TR>

</TD></TR>
<TR><TD>&nbsp;</TD><TD COLSPAN="3"><B>Loss of money, travellers' cheques, passports, driving licences,

</TD></TR>
<TR><TD VALIGN="TOP">(b)</TD><TD COLSPAN="3"><B><I>Fraudulent Use of Lost Credit Card</I></B>
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD>&nbsp;</TD><TD COLSPAN="3"><B>Loss resulting from the fraudulent use of any credit card, charge

</TD></TR>
<TR><TD COLSPAN="4">Cover in respect of the money 
and travel documents specified in paragraph (a) shall commence at the

<TR><TD COLSPAN="4">&nbsp;</TD></TR>
<TR><TD COLSPAN="4"><B><I>Business Documents and Records</I></B>
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD COLSPAN="4"><B>To indemnify the Insured Person up to &#163;100 in all</B> in respect of 
the cost of replacing or restoring business documents and records the 
property of the Assured or the Insured Person, following loss or 
damage during the Period of Travel.
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD COLSPAN="4">&nbsp;</TD></TR>

</TD></TR>
<TR><TD VALIGN="TOP">(a)</TD><TD COLSPAN="3"><B><I>Legal Expenses</I></B>
</TD></TR>

</TD></TR>
<TR><TD VALIGN="TOP">(b)</TD><TD COLSPAN="3"><B><I>Personal Liability</I></B>
</TD></TR>

accidents and in all inclusive of associated legal expenses,
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD>&nbsp;</TD><TD COLSPAN="3">Excluding the following:
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD>&nbsp;</TD><TD VALIGN="TOP">(i)</TD><TD COLSPAN="2">Liability for bodily injury to the Insured Person's employees or 
to any member of his family or household.
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD>&nbsp;</TD><TD VALIGN="TOP">(ii)</TD><TD COLSPAN="2">Liability for damage to property owned by, or in the care, 
custody or control of the Insured Person or any member of his family 
or household, except for damage to the structure or contents of any

</TD></TR>
<TR><TD>&nbsp;</TD><TD VALIGN="TOP">(iii)</TD><TD COLSPAN="2">Liability arising out of the ownership, possession, custody or 
use of:
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD COLSPAN="2">&nbsp;</TD><TD VALIGN="TOP">(a)</TD><TD>any aircraft
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD COLSPAN="2">&nbsp;</TD><TD VALIGN="TOP">(b)</TD><TD>any mechanically propelled or horse drawn vehicle, other than 
golf buggies
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD COLSPAN="2">&nbsp;</TD><TD VALIGN="TOP">(c)</TD><TD>any caravan or vehicular trailer
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD COLSPAN="2">&nbsp;</TD><TD VALIGN="TOP">(d)</TD><TD>any waterborne craft, other than sailboards, surfboards, canoes, 
rowing dinghies, foot or hand-propelled paddle boats and inflatable 
sailing dinghies
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD COLSPAN="2">&nbsp;</TD><TD VALIGN="TOP">(e)</TD><TD>any firearm
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD COLSPAN="2">&nbsp;</TD><TD VALIGN="TOP">(f)</TD><TD>any animal, other than horses hired for hacking only
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD COLSPAN="2">&nbsp;</TD><TD VALIGN="TOP">(g)</TD><TD>any land or building or permanently or seasonally sited property 
of any kind.
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD>&nbsp;</TD><TD VALIGN="TOP">(iv)</TD><TD COLSPAN="2">Employer's liability.
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD>&nbsp;</TD><TD VALIGN="TOP">(v)</TD><TD COLSPAN="2">Contractual liability.
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD>&nbsp;</TD><TD VALIGN="TOP">(vi)</TD><TD COLSPAN="2">Liability arising out of or incidental to the practice of a 
profession or occupation or to the supply of goods or services.
</TD></TR>
<TR><TD>&nbsp;</TD><TD VALIGN="TOP">(vii)</TD><TD COLSPAN="2">Liability that is covered under any other insurance, except for
