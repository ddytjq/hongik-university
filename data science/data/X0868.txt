<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>First-year Kent State coach Stan Heath is used to the Big Ten. He's used to Michigan State. He's used to Final Fours.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<?xml:namespace prefix = o ns = "urn:schemas-microsoft-com:office:office" /><o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>What he's not used to, though, is winning 20-plus games, dominating a conference&nbsp;... and having to wonder about his NCAA Tournament status.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoBodyText style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>"This is something of an unknown for me," Heath said Thursday morning from his office on the Ohio campus. "In the Big Ten, if you finish fifth, you're pretty much in. So I'm kind of like a little pup. But the only thing I'm telling my team is that we're still in the process of building our resume."</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>A resume that's already pretty darn good.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>Heath's Golden Flashes are 21-5 overall and 14-1 in the Mid-American Conference. Their RPI stands at 41. They clinched the MAC's regular-season championship with a&nbsp;-- ready for this score?&nbsp;-- 116-76 home win over Marshall.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>Think this team is clicking right now?</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>"We've gotten better and better and better as the year has gone on," the coach said. "I'm really surprised that we are where we are."</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>Especially considering Heath and his players are still getting used to each other. The 36-year-old Detroit native left his slot as an assistant under Tom Izzo in East Lansing, Mich., and took the Kent job last spring after former Flashes coach Gary Waters went to Rutgers.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>But apparently no one told Heath that the MAC isn't a league teams dominate. It just isn't. There are too many teams (13), too much travel (from Mount Pleasant, Mich., to Buffalo, N.Y., to Huntington, W.Va.) and too much talent for any one team to roll.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>Ask Matt Doherty and his Tar Heels about MAC star Brandon Hunter. How good is he? The Ohio strongman helped the Bobcats manhandle UNC Wednesday night at the Smith Center.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>Or just ask those within the conference itself. The MAC folks have a definitive answer: Ohio is good enough to be in third place in the East Division.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>At the top of that chart, though, is Kent State. The Golden Flashes next play on Saturday against Hunter and Ohio. But Heath's troops haven't exactly followed the league's standard means of earning a bit of national media love.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>Typical MAC darlings knock off a big-name team or two early in the season&nbsp;-- see: State, Ball&nbsp;-- and then begin to slowly fade from memory while riding on a bus somewhere in between Toledo and Kalamazoo.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>The Flashes, on the other hand, lost to Hofstra Nov. 23, Kentucky Nov. 28, Youngstown State Dec. 15 and Xavier Dec. 20.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>"We had ups and downs in the preseason part of the year," Heath said. "After we lost to Youngstown, we hit rock bottom. We were really down."</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>It didn't last long. Kent went on the road and got a combined 30 points from veteran guards Trevor Huffman and Andrew Mitchell in beating Illinois State. The Flashes topped St. Bonaventure on New Year's Eve.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>They opened MAC play two days later with a resounding victory over Ball State. Their only loss since then was at league doormat Buffalo, believe it or not, and it came on a buzzer-beating runner with less than a second to go.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>KSU now has won 12 in a row, and most of the games in the last month haven't even been that close.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>That 14-1 conference ledger? It could be 15-0. And that's akin to going 15-1 in the ACC (like, perhaps, Maryland) or running the table in the Big 12 (like, perhaps, Kansas).</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>All those team breakfasts in the preseason, the one-on-one meetings with Heath in his office, the group pow-wows at his house&nbsp;-- that stuff's kicking in right now.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>"Just to build a personal relationship," Heath said. "That was the biggest thing."</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>That, of course, and having four seniors (including three fifth-years), a defense that ranks among the MAC's finest and a nucleus that now has won 53 conference games (and counting) in the last four seasons.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>And want balance? Mitchell, a 5-foot-11 senior from Detroit, leads the team with 15.6 points per game. Huffman, a 6-1 junior from Petoskey, Mich., averages 15.5. So does redshirt junior Antonio Gates.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>Nine Flashes have scored in double figures in at least one game this winter.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>"It just took us a while," Heath said. "It took time for them to learn a new system and to look at a different guy on the sidelines."</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>And now they're looking at a potential march in March.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<H1 style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>YALE BULLDOG TOUGH</FONT></H1>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>Yale's season almost fell apart down in (of all places) South Carolina's Upstate. The Bulldogs lost on back-to-back late December days at a Furman-hosted tournament to Division I provisional Gardner-Webb and DIII Macalester.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>Not good. Not good at all. Especially for a team that already had a win at Penn State and a crazy overtime loss at George Washington on its resume.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>But James Jones' Bulldogs spent the next 72 hours in the Palmetto State practicing twice a day in preparation for their Jan. 2 date at Clemson.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>"We got back to the basics," junior T.J. McHugh said Wednesday night during a study break. "There was no more taking days off. This was our season."</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>And the turning point. Yale won 68-65 at Littlejohn. The Bulldogs have lost just once since then&nbsp;-- Jan. 19 to Brown&nbsp;-- and embark on this weekend's Princeton-and-Penn road trip with a 9-1 Ivy League record and a chance to clinch at least a tie for the conference championship.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>"We're excited about this opportunity," Jones said from his office earlier this week. "Sold-out arenas&nbsp;... people calling for your head&nbsp;... that's fun. That excites me as a coach, and that excites our players."</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>The P's&nbsp;-- Princeton and Penn, that is, winners of the last 13 Ivy titles&nbsp;-- are out for revenge. Yale beat both the Tigers and the Quakers two weekends ago in New Haven in front of packed crowds of 3,100 at the Lee Amphitheater.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>"It was unmatched," Jones said. "You'd be hard-pressed to find a basketball venue that was more impressive than ours that weekend. It was the most unbelievable environment I've ever been in."</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoBodyText style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>Even the on-campus academics got caught up in the sudden hysteria. John Loge, Dean of Yale's Timothy Dwight College, discussed the basketball success in his weekly letter to the student body.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>"When things go right, as they did on Friday and Saturday, all things seem right indeed," he wrote. "Prospects are back. Hope and optimism are back. And I take all of them personally, floating in the air with my feet on the ground."</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><SPAN style="mso-spacerun: yes"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3></FONT></SPAN>&nbsp;</P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>The players felt the same way.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>"That was the great part about it," McHugh said. "The students were there, they were into the games, they were standing the whole time, and I couldn't believe they actually rushed the floor. It was insane."</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>But this is the new and hip version of Yale hoops. Three years removed from a 4-22 disaster, the Bulldogs are on top of the Ivy League, and Jones has gotten them there in what has turned into the Ancient Eight's best basketball season ever.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>The conference&nbsp;-- non-scholarship, remember&nbsp;-- is No. 14 (out of 32) overall in the most recent RPI rankings.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>"From top to bottom, this is probably the best it's ever been," James explained, "and we have a chance to be the best team in that league. We're on top right now, and hopefully we'll be at the top in two weeks."</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>And on their way to the NCAAs.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<H1 style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>AROUND AND ABOUT</FONT></H1>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>*** The Atlanta Tipoff Club released a list of its 25 finalists for Naismith College Basketball Coach of the Year. The list (on your mark&nbsp;... get set ...) goes something like this: <B>Henry Bibby</B>, Southern Cal; <B>Perry Clark</B>, Miami; <B>Tom Crean</B>, Marquette; <B>Mike Davis</B>, Indiana; <B>Billy Donovan</B>, Florida; <B>Mark Few</B>, Gonzaga; <B>Pete Gillen</B>, Virginia; <B>Mark Gottfried</B>, Alabama; <B>Jim Harrick</B>, Georgia; <B>Ben Howland</B>, Pittsburgh; <B>Bob Huggins</B>, Cincinnati ...</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>(... deep breath ...)</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman"><B>Ernie Kent</B>, Oregon; <B>Bobby Knight</B>, Texas Tech; <B>Mike Krzyzewski</B>, Duke; <B>Mike Montgomery</B>, Stanford; <B>Jim O'Brien</B>, Ohio State; <B>Lute Olson</B>, Arizona; <B>Skip Prosser</B>, Wake Forest; <B>Kelvin Sampson</B>, Oklahoma; <B>Herb Sendek</B>, N.C. State; <B>Bill Self</B>, Illinois; <B>Tubby Smith</B>, Kentucky; <B>Eddie Sutton</B>, Oklahoma State; <B>Gary Williams</B>, Maryland; <B>Roy Williams</B>, Kansas.</FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>Take Gillen and Self the heck off that list, add Rutgers' <B>Gary Waters</B> and Kent State's <B>Stan Heath</B>, and go from there. The winner will be honored April 5 at Atlanta's Cobb Galleria Centre.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>*** Tulsa is the No. 3 basketball school in what is quite clearly a football-first state. What does that leave for the Golden Hurricane? Not much love. Not much love at all. But <B>Bill Phillips</B>' squad is 22-4 heading into Thursday night's critical WAC showdown at Hawaii. Tip is set for 7 p.m. Island time.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>Perhaps TU hoop should be known for something other than its status as an uncanny breeding ground for coaching talent. (<B>Nolan Richardson</B>, <B>Steve Robinson</B>, <B>Buzz Peterson</B> and Self are among the "names" that used the Oklahoma city as a career springboard.) Perhaps the Golden Hurricane should be known for, say, ridiculously consistent success.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>Recognize: In the last three years, in fact, only Duke has more wins than TU. Duke has 87; Tulsa has 79. Cincinnati, with 77, is No. 3. The WAC squad has won 20-plus games eight times in the last nine seasons. Didn't exactly know that, huh?</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>*** <B>Chris Marcus</B> is officially back. The Western Kentucky center&nbsp;-- out for 17 games earlier this season with a stress fracture in his foot&nbsp;-- had 32 points and 15 rebounds in the No. 24 Hilltoppers' 100-63 win Tuesday night over Division II Kentucky State. Marcus made 12 of his 14 field-goal attempts in 24 minutes&nbsp;-- the most he's played in the five games since he's returned.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>"It feels good to come out here and play some minutes in a long stretch," he told the Associated Press. "My condition is starting to come back and my shot is starting to come back."</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>*** <B>Juan Dixon</B> scored his 2,000th point Wednesday night in Maryland's win at Clemson, becoming the first player in the history of college basketball to score that many while also compiling 300 steals and 200 threes.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>Such composite records that use three-point stats are usually sort of dubious&nbsp;-- the three, after all, is only, what, 15 or so years old?&nbsp;-- but the point still stands: Dixon is really, really good.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>*** <B>Zack McMillin</B> of the Memphis Commercial Appeal raised a valid point this week. Why, he wondered, has Duke's <B>Jason Williams</B> all but been crowned national player of the year, when Cincinnati guard <B>Steve Logan</B> boasts better numbers in virtually every meaningful statistical category?</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>And, while on this topic, what about <B>Drew Gooden</B>? The Kansas big man is averaging a double-double&nbsp;-- a double-double on 'roids, actually&nbsp;-- and his Jayhawks are the No. 1 team in the nation. Not saying Williams shouldn't be in the hunt. Not even saying he shouldn't be the frontrunner at this point. Just saying this balloting shouldn't be closed. Not just yet.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>*** Huge game Thursday night in the Mid-Continent Conference: <B>Homer Drew</B>'s Valparaiso squad, in its customary spot atop the MCC standings, is 10-2 in league action. But 9-3 Oakland is threatening the Crusaders. The two teams are set to meet at Valpo.</FONT></P></DIV>
