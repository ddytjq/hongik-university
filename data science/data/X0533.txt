<STRONG>Ryder Cup Standings</STRONG>
<br><font size="-1"><b>FINAL STANDINGS</b></font>

<p><b>Related Links:</b>
<br><a href="/Tour/stats/ryder_index.html">Past Ryder Cup Standings</a>
<br><a href="/Tour/PGAofAmerica/rydercup01/index.html">News from the 2001 Ryder Cup Matches</a>

<p>The 2001 U.S. Ryder Cup Team was chosen on the basis of points compiled by The PGA of America, which players accumulate from Jan. 9, 2000, through August 19, 2001 at The 83rd PGA Championship at Atlanta Athletic Club in Duluth, Ga.  The top 10 finishers on the points list qualified for the 12-member team, and <a href="/tour/pgaofamerica/rydercup01/strange_index.html">U.S. Captain Curtis Strange</a> selected the final two players.

<p><a href="/Events/Tickets/index.html#tickets">The 34th Ryder Cup Matches</a> will be held September 27-29, 2002 at The Belfry in Sutton Colfield, England.
