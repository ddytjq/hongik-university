<h1 align="center"><u>Open Clusters</u></h1>
<p>Stars are sometimes concentrated into tight clusters a few light years
in diameter, usually because the stars all formed in the same nebula.
These clusters usually disperse over millions of years although there
are some that well over a billion years old.
</p>

galaxy.  Note that because of the relatively large distances to these clusters,
that they all tend to have a low galactic latitude.
