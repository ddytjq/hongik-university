					<h3>1 - 10 of 28</h3><p><strong><a href="http://www.aebc.gov.uk/">Agriculture and Environment Biotechnology Commission (AEBC)</a></strong><br />The new Agriculture and Environment Biotechnology Commission (AEBC) offers the UK government independent strategic advice on biotechnology issues. It focuses primarily the impact of developments in biotechnology on agriculture, environment and health care.</p>
<p><strong><a href="http://www.absa.org/">American Biological Safety Association (ABSA)</a></strong><br />The American Biological Safety Association (ABSA) aims to foster recognition of Biological Safety as a distinct scientific discipline, to promote biological safety and to establish a forum for the continued exchange of information on biological safety. </p>
<p><strong><a href="http://biosafety.ihe.be/">Belgian Biosafety Server</a></strong><br />The Belgian Biosafety Server informs about the different European regulations of the release and marketing of GMO's.</p>
<p><strong><a href="http://bioline.bdt.org.br/by/">Bioline International - BioSafety Journal</a></strong><br />Bioline's electronic journal with publications, letters and a discussion forum on science and technology, focusing the aspects of biosafety. </p>
<p><strong><a href="http://www.iisd.ca/biodiv/excop/">Biosafety Agreement</a></strong><br />The "Biosafety Protocol" to the United Nations Convention on biological diversity was adopted by 130 countries in Montreal on 29 January. It is the first global treaty regulating trade in genetically modified products world-wide and provides rules intended to protect the environment from genetically altered plants, animals and bacteria. 
Pictures and summary of the meeting.
   </p>
<p><strong><a href="http://binas.unido.org/binas/">Biosafety Information Network and Advisory Service (BINAS)</a></strong><br />Homepage of The Biosafety Information Network and Advisory Service (BINAS), a service of the United Nations Industrial Development Organization. BINAS monitors global developments in regulatory issues in biotechnology. Latest news, bulletin, library and list of events.</p>
<p><strong><a href="http://www.buwal.ch/stobobio/biotechnologie/">Biotechnology Services by the SAEFL</a></strong><br />The Biotechnology Services of the SAEFL evaluate the impact on the environment of the use of genetically modified and pathogenic organisms (GMO, PO) and informas about its consequences in terms of biosafety, biodiversity and ethical considerations. Very useful. 
</p>
<p><strong><a href="http://www.oecd.org/ehs/bioabout.htm">BioTrack Online</a></strong><br />BioTrack Online, a website by the OECD, focuses on information related to the regulatory oversight of products of biotechnology which is used by governments, industry and other stakeholders.</p>
<p><strong><a href="http://www.monsanto.com/monsanto/biotechnology/background_information/99june21_Monarch.html">Butterflies and Bt corn pollen - Lab research and field realities</a></strong><br />On this site, Monsanto summarizes recent developments in monarch butterfly research related to Bt corn pollen.   </p>
<p><strong><a href="http://www.cabi.org/BIOSCIENCE/switz.htm">CAB Bioscience Switzerland</a></strong><br />CAB International is a global non-profit organisation delivering practical knowledge solutions in applied life sciences by utilizing its expertise in publishing and biological sciences. The CABI Bioscience Centre Switzerland in Delémont has developed a substantial programme of weed and arthropod biological control.</p>
