				<td class=body><B>A safer, more powerful way to save tax free</B><P>
The Bonus ISA is our mini insurance ISA. It offers you security, growth potential, flexibility and freedom from tax&#42;. It grows through the addition of bonuses which, once added, cannot be taken away.
<p>
Your Bonus ISA is designed to be a medium to long term (5-10 years) investment plan with the aim of providing a consistently better performance than a typical bank or building society. And remember that as banks' and building societies' interest rates have been going down, it's more important than ever to find an investment that adds value to your savings.
<p>
<SPAN CLASS="terms">&#42;Income generated by your ISA will be free from UK Income and Capital Gains Tax. In addition, we will be able to reclaim tax credits of 10&#37; on UK dividends until 5.4.04.</span>

					<td class=body colspan=2><P>
Your money is invested in the ISA With Profits fund. Of all the ways you can invest with ISAs, it gives you maximum potential returns but with minimum risk. <P>
This is because as a with profits plan, your money grows by the addition of bonuses. What's more, these are tax-free. These bonuses are your share of the society's profits. As a mutual society we do not have any shareholders so our profits are distributed among our policyholders in the form of bonuses. They add security to your savings because once a bonus is added it cannot be taken away*. So your profits are safe. The current bonus rate is <B>5.0%</B> p.a.# <P>
When you finally cash in your Bonus ISA, you could well be in for a pleasant surprise with a final bonus. Add this to all the other bonuses you have accrued over the years and your tax-free Bonus ISA could be a highly profitable investment.
This final bonus isn't guaranteed but the longer you keep your Bonus ISA, the more likely you are to get one. <P>
