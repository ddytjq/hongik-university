<B><CENTER>Week 16 RaceLink Winners</CENTER></B>
<P>
Congratulations to Petty Ent. who scored 1267 points and earns $50 in first place prize money.  Fun & Gun Racing finished in second place with 1249 points and will receive $35. Light Speed finished in third place with 1229 points and will receive $20.  The maximum possible score for Week 16 was 1316 points.
<P>
<B><CENTER>1997 RaceLink Champions </CENTER></B>
<P>
Bust A Nut Racing finishes the season in first place with 17,126 points.  Clay-Con Racing remains in second place with 16,953 points, only  173 points behind.  Team Goldring, 49er Motorsports and  Brickyard Racing finish in third, fourth and fifth respectively
<P>
These five teams earned $1,000, $500, $300, $100, $100 in Championship Awards.  These awards will be mailed out to the winners on July 22, 1997.  Teams that finished 6th (Fat Pig Racing) to 25th (Star Wars Racing) receive a free entry into the Ô97 RaceLink Fall Challenge.

<CENTER><B>Stock Car Fall Challenge</B></CENTER>
The RaceLink Stock Car Fall Challenge begins on July 5, 1997 with the Pepsi 400 at Daytona Beach, Florida.  See the reverse side of this newsletter for the entry form.  Total prize money is $10,000 which is paid to 78 winners.  The Grand Prize is $5,000.  The cost to enter is $99.95 - checks only please.  The game is played the same way as the Stock Car Challenge - Spring Season.  The only change is to make each unused driver change worth 20 points instead of 10 points.  Recommend a rookie stock car player(s) and receive a $10.00 referral fee(s).  I need your help to have 200+ players to enter.
<P>
For teams that finished in the 6th to 25th positions in the RaceLink Spring Season, please submit your entry form.
