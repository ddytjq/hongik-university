<h1>1997 SCCA Club Racing Schedule</h1>
<p>
This file contains information on Club Racing schedules in the
various SCCA Divisions for 1997.
Schedules that appear this early in the year are <em>always</em>
subject to change.
</p>
<p>
In cases where both a National and a Regional are listed at the
same time and place, the Regional is often restricted to classes
that do not have National status (e.g. IT, Club Ford, ASR.)
</p>
<p>
This schedule may from time to time contain information about
races sanctioned by bodies other than the SCCA; these races will
primarily be those of interest to SCCA members, such as Pro races
that depend heavily on SCCA workers and on Club races that SCCA
Drivers may wish to compete in (such as EMRA in the Northeast, the
RCCA, etc.)  These schedules should not be taken as gospel;
changes occur right up to the last minute, and such changes
may not be be reflected here.
</p>

<h3><a name="Runoffs">1997 SCCA Valvoline&REG Runoffs</a></h3>
<p>
The 1997 SCCA Valvoline Runoffs will be held on October 6th-12th at
<a href="/Tracks/MidOhio.html">Mid-Ohio Sports Car Course</a>.
The detailed schedule will be posted here when it becomes available
during the summer of 1997.

Results from the Valvoline Runoffs will be posted on the
Valvoline web site devoted to the Runoffs.
</p>
