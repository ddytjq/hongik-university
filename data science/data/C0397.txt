                                    <b> </b> </font> </td>
                                  <td background width="393" height="100%">
                                    <span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:11.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:
10.0pt;font-family:Arial;mso-bidi-font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">Friday
                                    19th October, 2001<o:p>
                                    </o:p>
                                    </span>
                                    <p class="MsoBodyText"><font color="#003399" size="5" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif"><b>ADVICE
                                    TO BUSINESSES<br>
                                    ON CHEMICAL OR BIOLOGICAL ATTACK<br>
                                    </b></font><span lang="EN-GB"><font face="Arial" size="2"><br>
                                    BIBA has received from the City of London
                                    Police the following advice to businesses on
                                    chemical or biological attack.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">
                                    </span>The police have agreed that the
                                    advice can be given the widest possible
                                    circulation.<br>
                                    <br>
                                    </font><font face="Arial" size="2">Members
                                    may wish to circulate the information to
                                    their clients and to their own staff,
                                    although it is vital to stress that, at the
                                    time of writing the government assessment of
                                    the threat to Great Britain is 'Low'.<br>
                                    <br>
                                    </font><font face="Arial" size="2">BIBA is
                                    also reviewing the current position with
                                    regard to any insurance protection available
                                    under existing insurance policies of all
                                    classes.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes"> </span>At
                                    present we are unable to offer definitive
                                    guidance on this, although initial
                                    indications are that little, if any cover is
                                    offered by current policies.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">
                                    </span>We will publish advice on this issue
                                    following the process of consultation we are
                                    currently undertaking involving brokers,
                                    insurers and other interested parties.<br>
                                    <br>
                                    </font></span><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-GB" style="layout-grid-mode: line"><b>1.<span style="mso-tab-count:1">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    </span>Introduction</b><br>
                                    <br>
                                    </span></font><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-GB" style="layout-grid-mode: line">1.1<span style="mso-tab-count:1">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    </span>This paper gives advice on how to
                                    prevent or to mitigate the<br>
                                    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    effects of a chemical or biological attack
                                    against buildings.<br>
                                    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    The advice is precautionary and follows
                                    recent cases of<br>
                                    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    anthrax in the US. It is stressed that there
                                    is no evidence to<br>
                                    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    suggest that any terrorist group is
                                    currently planning a<br>
                                    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    chemical or biological attack in the UK.<br>
                                    <br>
                                    </span></font><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-GB" style="layout-grid-mode: line"><b>2.<span style="mso-tab-count:1">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    </span>The Threat</b><o:p>
                                    </o:p>
                                    </span></font></p>
                                    <p class="MsoNormal"><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-GB" style="layout-grid-mode: line">&nbsp;</span></font><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-GB" style="layout-grid-mode: line">2.1<span style="mso-tab-count:1">&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    </span>The current government assessment of
                                    the threat to Great<br>
                                    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    Britain from the use chemical or biological
                                    material is 'LOW',<br>
                                    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    and this should be borne in mind when
                                    considering the<br>
                                    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    remainder of this advice.<br>
                                    <br>
                                    </span></font><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-GB" style="layout-grid-mode: line"><b>3.<span style="mso-tab-count:1">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    </span>Prevention Advice</b><o:p>
                                    </o:p>
                                    </span></font></p>
                                    <p class="MsoNormal"><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-GB" style="layout-grid-mode: line"><b>Air
                                    Conditioning Plant Room</b></span></font><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-GB" style="layout-grid-mode: line"><br>
                                    <br>
                                    </span></font><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-GB" style="layout-grid-mode: line">3.1<span style="mso-tab-count:1">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    </span>Prevent unauthorised access to main
                                    AC plant. Ensure all<br>
                                    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    existing keys are accounted for. Change
                                    locks and reissue<br>
                                    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    keys on a controlled basis if necessary.
                                    Alarm all points of<br>
                                    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    access to the plant room and cover with CCTV
                                    for alarm&nbsp;&nbsp;<br>
                                    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    verification. Verify identity of any
                                    contractors working on the<br>
                                    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    air conditioning and supervise whilst
                                    present on the premises.<br>
                                    <br>
                                    </span></font><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-GB" style="layout-grid-mode: line"><b>Air
                                    Conditioning Intakes<br>
                                    <br>
                                    </b>3.2<span style="mso-tab-count:1">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    </span>Identify them all.<br>
                                    <br>
                                    </span></font><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-GB" style="layout-grid-mode: line">3.3<span style="mso-tab-count:1">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    </span>Prevent unauthorised access, secure
                                    access routes and<br>
                                    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    alarm with CCTV coverage for verification.<br>
                                    <br>
                                    </span></font><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-GB" style="layout-grid-mode: line"><b>On
                                    Site Water Storage Tanks<br>
                                    <br>
                                    </b>3.4&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Prevent
                                    unauthorised access, secure access routes
                                    and<br>
                                    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    alarm with CCTV coverage for verification.<o:p>
                                    </o:p>
                                    </span></font></p>
                                    <p class="MsoNormal"><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-GB" style="layout-grid-mode: line"><b>Review
                                    Access Control Systems<br>
                                    <br>
                                    </b>3.5&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Ensure
                                    access control is working effectively for
                                    staff, visitors<br>
                                    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    and contractors. Passes should be worn
                                    visibly and a<br>
                                    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    'challenge culture' adopted, to ensure only
                                    authorised people<br>
                                    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    are on the premises.<br>
                                    <br>
                                    <b>Post Room Procedures<br>
                                    <br>
                                    </b></span></font><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-GB" style="layout-grid-mode: line">3.6<span style="mso-tab-count:1">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    </span>Review procedures for dealing with
                                    all mail in accordance with<br>
                                    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    the advice contained in 'Bombs - Protecting
                                    People and<br>
                                    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    Property', and in particular:<br>
                                    <br>
                                    </span></font><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-GB" style="layout-grid-mode: line">3.7<span style="mso-tab-count:1">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    e</span>nsure that all incoming mail,
                                    despatch, lorry, van and<br>
                                    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    motorcycle courier deliveries, or hand
                                    delivered items, are<br>
                                    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    processed at one single location i.e. the
                                    post room;&nbsp;<br>
                                    <br>
                                    </span></font><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-GB" style="layout-grid-mode: line">3.8<span style="mso-tab-count:1">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    i</span>f available, scan or x-ray all mail.<br>
                                    <br>
                                    </span></font><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-GB" style="layout-grid-mode: line">3.9<span style="mso-tab-count:1">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    </span>Check all mail for suspicious signs:<br>
                                    <br>
                                    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    </span></font><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-GB" style="layout-grid-mode: line">Point
                                    of Origin e.g. from postmark or name of
                                    sender, if given.<br>
                                    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    If from an unusual point or sender.<br>
                                    <br>
                                    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</span></font><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-GB" style="layout-grid-mode: line">
                                    Manuscript of Sender<span style="mso-tab-count:1">
                                    </span>if this indicates, for example, a
                                    foreign<br>
                                    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    style of writing, and if such is not usually
                                    received.<br>
                                    <br>
                                    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    Flap of Envelope<span style="mso-tab-count:1">
                                    - </span>usually there is an un-gummed gap
                                    of<br>
                                    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    about 3-5mm; if the flap of the envelope is
                                    stuck down<br>
                                    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    completely.<br>
                                    <br>
                                    </span></font><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-GB" style="layout-grid-mode: line">3.10<span style="mso-tab-count:1">&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    </span>Do not open, or continue to open any
                                    package deemed<br>
                                    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    </span></font><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-GB" style="layout-grid-mode: line">suspicious.<br>
                                    <br>
                                    <b>4.<span style="mso-tab-count:1">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    </span>Dealing with an Incident<br>
                                    <br>
                                    </b></span></font><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-GB" style="layout-grid-mode: line">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    The following four examples will help when
                                    developing your<br>
                                    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    contingency plans:<br>
                                    <br>
                                    4.1<span style="mso-tab-count:1">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    </span>Suspicious package in the post room<br>
                                    <br>
                                    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    </span></font><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-GB" style="layout-grid-mode: line">Do
                                    not open it. Do not pass it around.<br>
                                    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    If unopened, immediately place the
                                    suspicious package in<br>
                                    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    clear sealable plastic bag, for larger
                                    objects use a clear&nbsp;<br>
                                    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    sealable plastic container.<br>
                                    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    If opened leave it in situ.<br>
                                    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<br>
                                    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    Shut windows in the post room;<br>
                                    <br>
                                    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    Evacuate the room, closing the door;<br>
                                    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    Switch off the air conditioning system;<br>
                                    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    Evacuate all the floors below the post room,
                                    the floor on<br>
                                    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    which the post room is located and the floor
                                    directly above;<br>
                                    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    and Dial 999 for the police, making sure you
                                    state you<br>
                                    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    believe chemical or biological material is
                                    involved.&nbsp;<br>
                                    <br>
                                    4.2<span style="mso-tab-count:1">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    </span>In addition to the above, take the
                                    following action if any<br>
                                    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    material may have been released in the post
                                    room:<br>
                                    <br>
                                    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</span></font><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-GB" style="layout-grid-mode: line">
                                    Close all fire doors in the building - where
                                    premises have<br>
                                    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    automated closing fire doors this may be
                                    achieved by<br>
                                    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    re-configuring the fire alarm system to
                                    provide an additional<br>
                                    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    function of automatically closing the fire
                                    doors without the fire<br>
                                    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    alarm sounding;<br>
                                    <br>
                                    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    Close all windows in the rest of the
                                    building;<br>
                                    <br>
                                    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    Evacuate the building - where evacuation is
                                    not possible, for<br>
                                    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    example where the only evacuation route is
                                    through the<br>
                                    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    contaminated area, move staff up the
                                    building to await<br>
                                    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    instructions from the emergency services.<br>
                                    <br>
                                    4.3<span style="mso-tab-count:1">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    </span>Device outside the building:<br>
                                    <br>
                                    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    </span></font><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-GB" style="layout-grid-mode: line">Switch
                                    off the air conditioning system;<br>
                                    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    Close all fire doors in the building;<br>
                                    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    Close all windows in the building;<br>
                                    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    Move all occupants located on the ground and
                                    below ground<br>
                                    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    floors up the building to the first floor
                                    and above to await<br>
                                    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    instructions from the emergency services;
                                    and<br>
                                    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    Dial 999 for the police, making sure you
                                    state you believe<br>
                                    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    chemical or biological material is
                                    involved.&nbsp;<br>
                                    <br>
                                    4.4<span style="mso-tab-count:1">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    </span>Internal device in the entrance foyer
                                    (This assumes that the<br>
                                    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    </span></font><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-GB" style="layout-grid-mode: line">existing
                                    access control procedures limit unauthorised
                                    access<br>
                                    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    to the entrance foyer only)<br>
                                    <br>
                                    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    Switch off the air conditioning system -
                                    this may require the<br>
                                    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    installation of an isolation switch within
                                    the Security Control<br>
                                    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    Room;<br>
                                    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    Close all fire doors in the building;&nbsp;<br>
                                    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    Isolate the foyer - close all doors that
                                    give access to the foyer<br>
                                    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    and close off the lifts;<br>
                                    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    Close all windows in the rest of the
                                    building;<br>
                                    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    Evacuate the building; and<br>
                                    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    Dial 999 for the police, making sure you
                                    state you believe<br>
                                    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    chemical or biological material is
                                    involved.&nbsp;<br>
                                    <br>
                                    <b>5.<span style="mso-tab-count:1">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    </span>Contaminated Persons<br>
                                    <br>
                                    </b></span></font><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-GB" style="layout-grid-mode: line">5.1<span style="mso-tab-count:1">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    </span>The following action should be taken
                                    for any person&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<br>
                                    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    suspected of being in contact with a
                                    suspected chemical or<br>
                                    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    biological device, prior to the arrival of
                                    the emergency<br>
                                    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    services:<br>
                                    <br>
                                    </span></font><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-GB" style="layout-grid-mode: line">5.2<span style="mso-tab-count:1">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    </span>Wash hands in a weak bleach solution
                                    (one teaspoonful of<br>
                                    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    domestic bleach to one litre of water), and
                                    then rinse with<br>
                                    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    ordinary soap and water;&nbsp;<br>
                                    <br>
                                    </span></font><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-GB" style="layout-grid-mode: line">5.3<span style="mso-tab-count:1">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    </span>Keep the person finding the device
                                    separate from others and<br>
                                    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    </span></font><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-GB" style="layout-grid-mode: line">available
                                    for immediate attention from the emergency<br>
                                    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    services;<br>
                                    <br>
                                    <o:p>
                                    </o:p>
                                    5.4<span style="mso-tab-count:1">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    </span>Try to ascertain how many other
                                    people may have been<br>
                                    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    exposed to the material - keep a detailed
                                    list including<br>
                                    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    details of their family doctor; and<br>
                                    <br>
                                    </span></font><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-GB" style="layout-grid-mode: line">5.5<span style="mso-tab-count:1">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    </span>Keep all suspected contaminated

                                    &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    others.<br>
