<h2>Messier Object Data as estimated by Don Machholz</h2>

While a more or less homogeneous data collection of the Messier objects is
hardly available from professional sources, amateur Don Machholz has estimated
apparent visual magnitudes and apparent angular diameters of all Messier 
objects under about the same conditions, with a 6-inch reflector. It is 
remarkable that according to his estimate, all objects ar brighter than 
apparent magnitude 10, which might explain that Messier could observe them all. 
His estimates are compared here to the values used in our database.
