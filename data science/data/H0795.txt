<center><b><font color="#FF0000"><font size=+1>Derivation of <font face="Symbol">D</font>q
for a Haploid Organism</font></font></b></center>

<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <font face="Symbol">D</font>q<b> </b>= q<sub>n+1</sub>
- q<sub> n</sub>
<br>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <font face="Symbol">D</font>q = [(1 - s)*q<sub>
n</sub>]/[1 - s*q<sub> n</sub>] - q<sub> n</sub>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
(Substituted the equation of q<sub>n+1</sub> for q<sub>n+1</sub>.)
<br>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <font face="Symbol">D</font>q = [(1 - s)*q<sub>
n</sub> - (1 - s*q<sub> n</sub>)*q<sub> n</sub>] / [1 - s*q<sub> n</sub>]&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
(Brought q into the fraction by cross multiplication.)
<br>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <font face="Symbol">D</font>q = [q<sub> n</sub>&nbsp;
- s*q<sub> n</sub> - q<sub> n</sub> - s*q<sub> n</sub> *q<sub> n</sub>]
/ [ 1 - s*q<sub> n</sub>]
<br>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <font face="Symbol">D</font>q = [-s*q<sub> n</sub>
-s*q<sub> n</sub> *q<sub> n</sub>]/ [1 - s*q<sub> n</sub>]
<br>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <font face="Symbol">D</font>q = -s*(q<sub> n</sub>
- q<sub> n</sub> *q<sub> n</sub>) / [ 1 - s*q<sub> n</sub>]
<br>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <font face="Symbol">D</font>q = -s*q<sub> n</sub>
*(1 - q<sub> n</sub>) / [ 1 - s*q<sub> n</sub>]
<br>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Because p<sub> n</sub> + q<sub> n</sub> = 1, 1 -
q<sub> n</sub> = p<sub> n</sub>; therefor:
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <font face="Symbol">D</font>q = -s*p<sub> n</sub>
*q<sub> n</sub> /[ 1 - s*q<sub> n</sub>]
<p>Summary : The change in q due to 1 generation of selection is called
delta q (<font face="Symbol">D</font>q) is given by the equation:
<br><font face="Symbol">&nbsp;&nbsp; D</font>q = -s*p<sub> n</sub> *q<sub>
n</sub> /[ 1 - s*q<sub> n</sub>]
<br>where s is the selection coefficient, p<sub> n</sub> is the frequency
of the more fit (increasing) allele, and q<sub> n</sub> is frequency of
the less fit (decreasing) allele.
