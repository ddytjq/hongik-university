			<H2>V8 Lites - Oran Park - June 18th, 2000.</H2>
			<IMG SRC="lite01s.JPG" WIDTH=400 HEIGHT=289 BORDER=0 ALIGN="LEFT" HSPACE="8" VSPACE="2" ALT="Mal at the recent Oran Park V8 Lites round">
			Mal's return to V8 Supercar competition began recently at Round 3 of the Konica Lites Series.
			<P>Straight away, Mal set the pace for the weekend at what would have to be considered his home track - Oran Park. Mal's time to beat was a 1 minute 10 second lap. This was also his first taste of the new 2000 Bridgestone control tyre, a concept where no-one has an advantage with different rubber.</P>
			<P>A three race format similar to what is run at the parent Shell Series was the challenge for The Supercar Experience driver. Mal ran competitively in all three heats, the highlight being a photo finish in race 2 between Victorian Matt White with a result of what could have been Mal's first win for 2000 elluded him by just 0.0142 seconds!</P>
			<P>A minor mechanical hiccup in race three saw Mal withraw The Supercar Experience VS Commodore and yet still finish 4th overall on the day.</P>
			<P>The team is hoping to source extra funding to compete in the remaining two rounds at Lakeside, QLD and Mallala, S.A.</P>
			<BLOCKQUOTE><I>"I haven't raced competitively since Bathurst '98 when I won the privateers Cup" said Mal. Things haven't changed much. Every time I put the helmet on, the mongrel comes out in me. "Winning is everything".</I></BLOCKQUOTE>
