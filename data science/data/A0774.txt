            <td> 
              <p><span class="introtitleredbold">Investment funds</span></p>
              <p> <span class="body">In today's world, it makes sense to put something 
                aside for your future. But you want to be sure that your money 
                will have the opportunity to work hard for you.</span></p>
              <p><span class="body"> Our investment funds can help. They offer 
                you an easy way to invest on the stock market. Before investing, 
                it's worth taking some time to identify your investment aims and 
                decide what you want your money to achieve.</span></p>
              <p><span class="body"> All the funds on this site are sub-funds 
                of our Open-Ended Investment Companies (OEICs). Each fund is divided 
                into shares of equal value. When you invest, your money is used 
                to buy shares. When you cash in your investment, you sell the 
                shares back to us.</span></p>
              <p><span class="body">We offer four main types of funds. </span></p>
