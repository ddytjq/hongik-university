		<P>
		<a href="corpdeposits.html"><span class=bold>Corporate Deposit Services</span></a>
		<P>
		<span class=body>When you have surplus cash, you require competitive rates and the assurance of dealing with one of the UK's premier financial institutions. But when you need the cash back to work in the business, easy accessibility is essential. With dedicated service from a specialist Corporate Deposit team, in your area, on standby to respond instantly to your requests, Bank of Scotland brings you the ultimate cash management solution, giving you absolute control of your sterling funds. </span>
<p>
<a href="clientbanking.html"><span class="bold">Client Banking Cash Management Services</span></a>
<p>
<span class="body">Bank of Scotland's Client Banking Cash Management Service is designed to help businesses manage their corporate and client accounts more efficiently. The service provides personal support from a dedicated Relationship Manager combined with a unique PC-based electronic banking system, to provide an effective and flexible cash management service.</span>
<p>
<a href="payment.html"><span class=bold>Payment Services</span></a>
<P>
<span class=body>
Bank of Scotland offers a range of payment services allowing customers to transfer funds to third parties electronically, both in the UK and worldwide, to settle suppliers invoices, make commission payments, pay wages, salaries and expenses, and undertake other transactions. </span>
<P>
<a href="electronicbank.html"><span class=bold>Electronic Banking Services</span></a>
<P>
<span class=body>
Our electronic banking services give you control and flexibility over the management of your money, providing full electronic banking from your home and office via PC. The range of services includes balance checks of all your bank, credit cards and foreign currency accounts, funds transfer and the payment of bills or salaries by BACS (Bankers' Automated Clearing System). CORPORATE HOBS&#174; also provides same-day CHAPS (Clearing Houses Automated Payment System) payments and International payments and drafts.
</span>
<P>
<a href="specialistservices.html"><span class=bold>Specialist Services</span></a><P>
<span class=body>
Bank of Scotland offers a range of tailored services to suit your specific business requirements, for example, Business Financial Planning, Business Insurance and Payroll and Human Resource Service.
</span>
<P>
<a href="assetfinance.html"><span class=bold>Asset Finance </span></a>
<P>
<span class=body>Bank of Scotland Asset Finance offers individual packages designed to allow you to acquire the assets your business needs with a finance package that is cash flow-friendly and tax-efficient. </span><BR><BR>
