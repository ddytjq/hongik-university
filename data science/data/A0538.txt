				 <p>
				<span class='body'>

					HSBC welcomes you to this web page, which is devoted to Continuous Linked Settlement (CLS™), the industry's initiative to remove settlement risk from Foreign Exchange transactions. Within this page you will find information concerning HSBC’s CLS service propositions, a demonstration of our on-line information delivery system and news of the latest industry and internal issues impacting this important initiative.
				</span>
					 </p> 
				

				
				 <p>
				<span class='body'>

					HSBC has been a strong supporter of CLS since its inception and is a fully paid up shareholder of CLS Services Ltd. It has been closely involved with the development of the process and has gained extensive and detailed knowledge of the inner workings of CLS through its Beta Testing of the Member Application software.
				</span>
					 </p> 
				

				
				 <p>
				<span class='body'>

					HSBC intends to settle all its eligible trades through CLS and will be one of the largest volume providers when the system becomes operational, which is scheduled for August 2002. HSBC* will provide enhanced nostro payment services to other Settlement Members to enable them to meet the timed payment demands of the CLS process and will provide a full settlements service to institutions which do not qualify for direct CLS Membership or which choose not to make the investment.
				</span>
					 </p> 
				

				
				 <p>
				<span class='body'>

					We hope that you will find the information contained here useful and informative and would welcome the opportunity to discuss any aspect of CLS with you. Details of how to contact us can be found by clicking on "CLS Contacts".
				</span>
					 </p>
