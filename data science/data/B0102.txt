      <h1 style="text-align: justify; text-indent: -.25in; mso-list: l11 level1 lfo3; tab-stops: list .25in; line-height: 100%; margin-left: .25in"><font face="Arial" size="2"><b><span lang="EN-US">1.<span style="font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; font-weight: normal">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
      </span>INTRODUCTION</span></b><span lang="EN-US">&nbsp;<o:p>
      </o:p>
      </span></font></h1>
      <p class="MsoBodyText" style="text-align: justify; line-height: 100%"><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-US">An
      ISA is an Individual Savings Account which is available from 6 April 1999
      onwards. The scheme provides tax-free investment and will run for at least
      ten years. The favourable tax treatment of ISAs is given by the Government
      who could change this at any time.</span></font><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-US">&nbsp;<o:p>
      </o:p>
      </span></font></p>
      <p class="MsoBodyText" style="text-align: justify; line-height: 100%"><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-US">This
      leaflet describes the types of ISA available from us (Clay Cross Building
      Society) as well as some detail about the ISA scheme generally. For more
      information about ISAs generally please ask for the leaflet, <i style="mso-bidi-font-style:normal">Individual
      Savings Accounts and Building Societies</i>.</span></font><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Arial" size="2">&nbsp;<o:p>
      </o:p>
      </font></span></p>
      <p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: justify; line-height: 100%"><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Arial" size="2">The
      Government has issued CAT standards for ISAs. To meet the CAT standards
      our ISA must be simple, clear and fair. CAT standards are explained more
      fully in section 8 of this leaflet. This ISA meets the Government’s CAT
      standards.</font></span><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Arial" size="2">&nbsp;<o:p>
      </o:p>
      </font></span></p>
      <h1 style="text-align: justify; text-indent: -.25in; mso-list: l11 level1 lfo3; tab-stops: list .25in; line-height: 100%; margin-left: .25in"><b><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-US">2.<span style="font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; font-weight: normal">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
      </span>WHAT ARE THE QUALIFYING INVESTMENTS FOR ISAs?</span></font></b><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Arial" size="2">&nbsp;<o:p>
      </o:p>
      </font></span></h1>
      <p class="MsoBodyText" style="text-align: justify; line-height: 100%"><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-US">There
      are three groups (or components) of qualifying ISA investments:</span></font><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Arial" size="2">&nbsp;<o:p>
      </o:p>
      </font></span></p>
      <p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: justify; text-indent: -.35in; mso-list: l0 level1 lfo4; tab-stops: list 49.5pt; line-height: 100%; margin-left: .7in"><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-US">(i)<span style="font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; font-weight: normal">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
      </span></span><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight:normal"><span lang="EN-US">cash
      </span></b><span lang="EN-US">(the cash component), which includes
      building society and bank accounts</span></font><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Arial" size="2">&nbsp;<o:p>
      </o:p>
      </font></span></p>
      <p style="text-align: justify; text-indent: -.35in; mso-list: l0 level1 lfo4; tab-stops: list 49.5pt; line-height: 100%; margin-left: .7in"><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-US">(ii)<span style="font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; font-weight: normal">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
      </span></span><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight:normal"><span lang="EN-US">stocks
      and shares </span></b><span lang="EN-US">(the stocks and shares
      component), including equity shares, unit trusts and UK Government
      securities</span></font><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Arial" size="2">&nbsp;<o:p>
      </o:p>
      </font></span></p>
      <p style="text-align: justify; text-indent: -.35in; mso-list: l0 level1 lfo4; tab-stops: list 49.5pt; line-height: 100%; margin-left: .7in"><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-US">(iii)<span style="font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; font-weight: normal">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
      </span></span><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight:normal"><span lang="EN-US">insurance
      </span></b><span lang="EN-US">(the insurance component), namely certain
      single premium life insurance policies</span></font><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight:
normal"><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Arial" size="2">&nbsp;<o:p>
      </o:p>
      </font></span></b></p>
      <h1 style="text-align: justify; text-indent: -.25in; mso-list: l1 level1 lfo5; tab-stops: list .25in; line-height: 100%; margin-left: .25in"><b><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-US">3.<span style="font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; font-weight: normal">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
      </span>WHAT TYPES OF ISA ARE THERE?</span></font></b><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Arial" size="2">&nbsp;<o:p>
      </o:p>
      </font></span></h1>
      <p class="MsoBodyText" style="text-align: justify; line-height: 100%"><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-US">There
      are three types of ISA:</span></font><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Arial" size="2">&nbsp;<o:p>
      </o:p>
      </font></span></p>
      <p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: justify; text-indent: -.35in; mso-list: l2 level1 lfo6; tab-stops: list 49.5pt; line-height: 100%; margin-left: .7in"><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-US">(i)<span style="font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; font-weight: normal">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</span><b style="font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; font-weight: normal">
      </b><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight:normal">maxi</b></span><span lang="EN-US">,
      which comprises one or more components but including at least the stocks
      and shares component</span></font><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Arial" size="2">&nbsp;<o:p>
      </o:p>
      </font></span></p>
      <p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: justify; text-indent: -.35in; mso-list: l2 level1 lfo6; tab-stops: list 49.5pt; line-height: 100%; margin-left: .7in"><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-US">(ii)<span style="font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; font-weight: normal">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
      </span></span><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight:normal"><span lang="EN-US">mini</span></b><span lang="EN-US">,
      which is made up of a single component</span></font><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Arial" size="2">&nbsp;<o:p>
      </o:p>
      </font></span></p>
      <p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: justify; text-indent: -.35in; mso-list: l2 level1 lfo6; tab-stops: list 49.5pt; line-height: 100%; margin-left: .7in"><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-US">(iii)<span style="font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; font-weight: normal">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
      </span><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight:normal">TESSA-only</b></span><span lang="EN-US">,
      a special type of ISA which allows investment of matured TESSA&nbsp;&nbsp;
      funds in a cash component</span></font><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Arial" size="2">&nbsp;</font></span></p>
      <p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: justify; text-indent: -.35in; mso-list: l2 level1 lfo6; tab-stops: list 49.5pt; line-height: 100%; margin-left: .7in"><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Arial" size="2">&nbsp;<o:p>
      </o:p>
      </font></span></p>
      <p class="MsoBodyText" style="text-align: justify; text-indent: -.25in; mso-list: l1 level1 lfo5; tab-stops: list .25in; line-height: 100%; margin-left: .25in"><b><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-US">4.<span style="font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; font-weight: normal">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
      </span>WHAT TYPE OF ISA CAN I INVEST IN AT CLAY CROSS BUILDING SOCIETY?</span></font></b><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Arial" size="2">&nbsp;<o:p>
      </o:p>
      </font></span></p>
      <p class="MsoBodyText" style="text-align: justify; line-height: 100%"><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-US">We
      offer the cash component of a mini ISA and, for investors whose TESSA is
      maturing, a TESSA-only ISA.</span></font><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Arial" size="2">&nbsp;<o:p>
      </o:p>
      </font></span></p>
      <p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: justify; line-height: 100%"><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Arial" size="2">You
      can subscribe to our mini ISA if you are aged 16 or over and live in the
      UK. You must not subscribe to another cash mini ISA or subscribe to any
      component of a maxi ISA in the tax year in which you subscribe to your
      mini ISA with us. By opening a cash<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>mini
      ISA you limit the amount of tax-free savings in equities you can make, if
      you do not already have a mini stocks and shares or insurance ISA.&nbsp;<o:p>
      </o:p>
      </font></span></p>
      <p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: justify; line-height: 100%"><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Arial" size="2">You
      cannot have an ISA jointly with anyone else.</font></span><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Arial" size="2">&nbsp;<o:p>
      </o:p>
      </font></span></p>
      <p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: justify; line-height: 100%"><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Arial" size="2">Special
      rules apply to TESSA-only ISAs. These are described in section 9.<o:p>
      </o:p>
      </font></span></p>
      <p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: justify; line-height: 100%"><span lang="EN-US" style="color: lime"><font face="Arial" size="2">&nbsp;<o:p>
      </o:p>
      </font></span></p>
      <p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: justify; text-indent: -14.15pt; mso-list: l8 level1 lfo7; tab-stops: list .25in; line-height: 100%; margin-left: 14.15pt"><b><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Arial" size="2">5.<span style="font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; font-weight: normal">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
      </span>HOW MUCH CAN I INVEST IN MY ISA?</font></span></b><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Arial" size="2">&nbsp;<o:p>
      </o:p>
      </font></span></p>
      <p class="MsoBodyText" style="text-align: justify; line-height: 100%"><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-US">To
      open an ISA with us you will need to invest at least £10. After that you
      can invest any amount up to the limit allowed.&nbsp;<o:p>
      </o:p>
      </span></font></p>
      <p class="MsoBodyText" style="text-align: justify; line-height: 100%"><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-US">In
      each of the tax years ending on 5 April until the year 2006 the limit is
      £3,000 in the cash component of your mini ISA with us. &nbsp;<o:p>
      </o:p>
      </span></font></p>
      <p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: justify; line-height: 100%"><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Arial" size="2">Special
      rules apply to TESSA-only ISAs. These are described in section 9.<o:p>
      </o:p>
      </font></span></p>
      <p class="MsoBodyText" style="text-align: justify; line-height: 100%"><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-US"><span style="mso-spacerun:
yes">&nbsp;</span><o:p>
      </o:p>
      </span></font></p>
      <p class="MsoBodyText" style="text-align: justify; text-indent: -14.15pt; mso-list: l8 level1 lfo7; tab-stops: list .25in; line-height: 100%; margin-left: 14.15pt"><b><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-US">6.<span style="font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; font-weight: normal">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
      </span>CAN I MAKE WITHDRAWALS FROM MY ISA?</span></font></b><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-US">&nbsp;<o:p>
      </o:p>
      </span></font></p>
      <p class="MsoBodyText" style="text-align: justify; line-height: 100%"><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-US">Yes.
      You can withdraw any amount up to the value of your account, subject to
      our normal branch limits, details of which are contained in our <i style="mso-bidi-font-style:normal">Guide
      to Savings </i>leaflet. Interest will continue to be payable free of tax.</span></font><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-US">&nbsp;<o:p>
      </o:p>
      </span></font></p>
      <p class="MsoBodyText" style="text-align: justify; line-height: 100%"><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-US">Withdrawals
      have no effect on the subscription limits. Once you reach the limit in any
      tax year you cannot make any further subscriptions in that tax year.</span></font><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-US">&nbsp;<o:p>
      </o:p>
      </span></font></p>
      <p class="MsoBodyText" style="text-align: justify; line-height: 100%"><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-US">For
      example, if you subscribe £3,000 in May 2002 and withdraw £2,000 in June
      2002 your account balance will be £1,000. But you cannot make any further
      subscription in the tax year because you have already reached your
      subscription limit of £3,000. You can, however, make further
      subscriptions from 6 April 2003, the following tax year.&nbsp;<o:p>
      </o:p>
      </span></font></p>
      <p class="MsoBodyText" style="text-align: justify; line-height: 100%"><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-US">If
      you withdraw all of your investment and close your ISA, you cannot open
      another cash component of a mini ISA in the same tax year.</span></font><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-US">&nbsp;<o:p>
      </o:p>
      </span></font></p>
      <p class="MsoBodyText" style="text-align: justify; line-height: 100%"><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-US">You
      can also transfer funds from your ISA to any other ISA we may offer from
      time to time or to another ISA manager. If the transfer is to be of
      current year subscriptions, the whole of those subscriptions must be
      transferred. For future years, funds in an ISA from previous year
      investments may be transferred in whole or in part.</span></font><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-US">&nbsp;<o:p>
      </o:p>
      </span></font></p>
      <p class="MsoBodyText" style="text-align: justify; line-height: 100%"><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-US">You
      cannot, though, transfer from your cash component to the insurance
      component or to the stocks and shares component.&nbsp;</span></font></p>
      <p class="MsoBodyText" style="text-align: justify; line-height: 100%"><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-US">&nbsp;<o:p>
      </o:p>
      </span></font></p>
      <p class="MsoBodyText" style="text-align: justify; text-indent: -14.15pt; mso-list: l8 level1 lfo7; tab-stops: list .25in; line-height: 100%; margin-left: 14.15pt"><b><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-US">7.<span style="font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; font-weight: normal">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
      </span>HOW IS INTEREST PAID?</span></font></b><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-US">&nbsp;<o:p>
      </o:p>
      </span></font></p>
      <p class="MsoBodyText" style="text-align: justify; line-height: 100%"><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-US">Interest
      will be added to your account each year on 5 April. No tax will be
      deducted. The rate of interest may vary from time to time. The rate of
      interest will be linked to Bank of England base rate and will not be less
      than 2% below that rate (except when the Bank of England base rate
      changes, in which case we will amend the rate within one calendar month of
      that change).</span></font></p>
      <p class="MsoBodyText" style="text-align: justify; line-height: 100%"><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-US">&nbsp;<o:p>
      </o:p>
      </span></font></p>
      <p class="MsoBodyText" style="text-align: justify; text-indent: -14.15pt; mso-list: l8 level1 lfo7; tab-stops: list .25in; line-height: 100%; margin-left: 14.15pt"><b><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-US">8.<span style="font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; font-weight: normal">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
      </span>WHAT ARE CAT STANDARDS?</span></font></b><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-US">&nbsp;<o:p>
      </o:p>
      </span></font></p>
      <p class="MsoBodyText" style="text-align: justify; line-height: 100%"><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-US">The
      Government has issued CAT standards for ISAs. To meet the CAT standards
      our ISA must be simple, clear and fair, and you must not be required to
      buy some other product. We are committed to continuing to provide a CAT
      standard ISA for those customers who have one.</span></font><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-US">&nbsp;<o:p>
      </o:p>
      </span></font></p>

      ISA meets the CAT standards in the following ways:</span></font><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-US">&nbsp;<o:p>
