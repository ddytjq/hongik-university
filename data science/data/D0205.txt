<h2>
Tutorial Overview</h2>
This tutorial teaches the EJB (Enterprise Java Beans) technology.
<p><i>Prerequisites:</i>&nbsp; The tutorial requires a good knowledge of
Java.&nbsp; The more advanced topics also require knowledge of JDBC.&nbsp;
Access to a database is also required for many of the exercises (though
even without a database, the first few tutorial steps may be covered by
using the included MS/Acces file.)
<p><i>Approach:&nbsp;</i> This tutorial is meant for developers and is
completely designed to be <i>"hands-on</i>".&nbsp; The assumption is that
you will learn most by doing, not by reading.&nbsp; The tutorial is a guided
approach to doing things in a manner that will lead you into learning the
EJB concepts very quickly.&nbsp; For best progress, it is recommended that
you actually get all the examples working.&nbsp; By doing this, you will
gain familiarity with the actual practical issues.&nbsp; Also spend some
time on the exercises, these are designed to solidify your grasp of the
material.
