<H2>Host at the neutral venue</H2>

<p>
<p> <b>FK Crvena zvezda Beograd (Red Star Belgrade)</b>, Champions' Cup winner 
  for season 1990/91, was eight times host at the neutral venue due "the unstable 
  political situation in Yugoslavia" and this is probably the record in Europe.</p>

<PRE>
17.09.1991. EC1 Crvena zv. - Portadown         4:0 (2:0) [in Széged]
23.10.1991. EC1 Crvena zv. - Apollon Limassol  3:1 (1:1) [in Széged]
12.12.1991. EC1 Crvena zv. - Anderlecht        3:2 (1:1) [in Budapest]
18.03.1992. EC1 Crvena zv. - Panathinaikos     1:0 (0:0) [in Sofia]
01.04.1992. EC1 Crvena zv. - Sampdoria         1:3 (1:2) [in Sofia]
20.10.1998. EC3 Crvena zv. - Olympique Lyon    1:2 (0:0) [in Bukarest]
14.09.1999. EC3 Crvena zv. - Montpelier        0:1 (0:1) [in Sofia]
28.09.2000. EC3 Crvena zv. - Leicester City    3:1 (1:1) [in Wien]

Total: 8  5 0 3  16:10 (+6) 
</PRE>
