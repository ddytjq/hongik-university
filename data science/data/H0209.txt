<td bgcolor="#FFFFFF" width="100%" align="left" valign="top"><p><b><span style="font-family: 'Arial'; font-size: 14pt">NAS Colloquium</span></b></p><p><b><i><span style="font-family: 'Arial'; font-size: 14pt">Science and Conservation Colloquium: Farm Bill Opportunities and Challenges</span></i></b></p><p><span style="font-size: 10pt">The goal of this two-day colloquium was to engage scientists and policymakers in a dialogue on the science that should be considered in implementing conservation programs.  The program included discussions of ecosystem status and needs (wetland and riparian, forest, and grassland ecosystems); issues concerning endangered, migratory, and invasive species; the interface between agriculture and the environment (pesticides, nutrient loss, soil erosion, carbon sequestration, and the urban-rural interface); and socioeconomic issues.  </span></p><p><b><span style="font-family: 'Arial'; font-size: 14pt">Date: Thursday, April 25, and Friday, April 26, 2002</span></b></p><p><span style="font-size: 10pt">                                                 Location:</span></p><p style="text-align: center"><span style="font-size: 10pt">USDA South Building</span></p><p style="text-align: center"><span style="font-size: 10pt">Jefferson Auditorium</span></p><p style="text-align: center"><span style="font-size: 10pt">1400 Independence Avenue, SW</span></p><p style="text-align: center"><span style="font-size: 10pt">Washington, D.C. 20250</span></p><p><span style="font-size: 10pt">For more information, please contact Michael Kisielewski by e-mail, </span><span style="font-size: 10pt; color: #000066"><a href="mailto:mkisiele@nas.edu" target="_top">Mike Kisielewski</a></span><span style="font-size: 10pt">, or by phone, 202-334-3062.  To view a speaker&#8217;s presentation (if available), please click on the speaker&#8217;s name.</span></p><p style="text-align: center"><b><span style="font-size: 10pt">NATIONAL RESEARCH COUNCIL</span></b></p><p style="text-align: center"><b><span style="font-size: 10pt">Board on Agriculture and Natural Resources</span></b></p><p style="text-align: center"><b><span style="font-size: 10pt">Water Science and Technology Board</span></b></p><p style="text-align: center"><b><i><span style="font-size: 10pt">Science and Conservation Colloquium: Farm Bill Opportunities and Challenges</span></i></b></p><p><b><u><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman'; font-size: 12pt">Thursday, April 25, 2002</span></u></b></p><p><b><span style="font-size: 10pt">USDA South Building</span></b></p><p><b><span style="font-size: 10pt">Jefferson Auditorium</span></b></p><p><b><span style="font-size: 10pt">1400 Independence Avenue, SW</span></b></p><p><b><span style="font-size: 10pt">Washington, D.C. 20250</span></b></p><p>
