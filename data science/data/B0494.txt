                              <div align="left"> 
                                <p><font face="ms sans serif, arial, geneva" size="-1"> 
                                  <font face="Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif">If 
                                  you are thinking about buying a property in 
                                  Southern Spain to use as a main residence, retirement 
                                  home or a holiday property, we can help you 
                                  every step of the way. When buying a property 
                                  abroad it is important that you find the right 
                                  lender. We can lend on properties located along 
                                  the Costa del Sol, from Gibraltar to Motril.</font></font></p>
                                <div align="left">
                                  <div align="left">
                                    <p><font face="Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif" size="-1" color="#006699"><b>Key 
                                      Features</b></font></p>
                                  </div>
                                </div>
                                <ul>
                                  <li> 
                                    <div align="left"><font face="Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif" size="-1">No 
                                      compulsory insurances</font></div>
                                  </li>
                                  <li> 
                                    <div align="left"><font face="Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif" size="-1">You 
                                      will need to be a resident or expatriate 
                                      of the UK, or a permanent resident of Gibraltar</font></div>
                                  </li>
                                  <li> 
                                    <div align="left"><font face="Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif" size="-1">We 
                                      cannot offer mortgages on properties which 
                                      are intended for long-term or regular letting 
                                      purposes</font></div>
                                  </li>
                                  <li> 
                                    <div align="left"><font face="Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif" size="-1">The 
                                      minimum property / valuation that we will 
                                      lend on is &pound;40,000 </font></div>
                                  </li>
                                  <li> 
                                    <div align="left"><font face="Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif" size="-1">You 
                                      can borrow up to 75% of the sterling equivalent 
                                      of the valuation of the property</font></div>
                                  </li>
                                  <li> 
                                    <div align="left"><font face="Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif" size="-1">All 
                                      mortgages are advanced in sterling</font></div>
                                  </li>
                                  <li> 
                                    <div align="left"><font face="Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif" size="-1">Arrangement 
                                      fee on some schemes</font></div>
                                  </li>
                                  <li> 
                                    <div align="left"><font face="Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif" size="-1">If 
                                      you are a single applicant you could borrow 
                                      up to 3 times your income</font></div>
                                  </li>
                                  <li><font face="Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif" size="-1">If 
                                    it is a joint application you could borrow 
                                    up to 2.5 times your joint income </font><br>
                                  </li>
                                  <li><font face="Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif" size="-1">Maximum 
                                    repayment period is 20 years.</font></li>
                                </ul>
                                <p><font face="Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif" size="-1">You 
                                  will be sent a mortgage statement once a year. 
                                  The statement will show the payments made by 
                                  you together with any debits, including interest, 
                                  charged by us and details of the rates of interest 
                                  charged to your account during the year. You 
                                  can choose which address you would like this 
                                  to be sent to.</font></p>
                                <p><font face="Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif" size="-1">Interest 
                                  will be calculated on a daily basis and will 
                                  be charged to your account at the end of each 
                                  calendar month. This means that every payment 
                                  you make will affect the amount of interest 
                                  charged to your account. If you make an overpayment, 
                                  this will reduce the balance on which interest 
                                  is charged. If you make an underpayment or miss 
                                  a payment, this will increase the balance on 
                                  which your interest is charged. If any fees, 
                                  charges, insurance premiums or other sums are 
                                  charged to your mortgage account these will 
                                  increase the balance on which interest is charged. 
                                  </font></p>

                                  will be charged on your loan by reference to 
                                  the average variable base lending rates of the 
                                  UK banks named in the offer of advance and mortgage 
                                  deed. The method of calculating interest will 
                                  be fully explained in the offer and mortgage 
                                  deed. You will be told of any changes to the 
                                  interest rate charged on your loan by letter.</font></p>
