  <p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:100%;font-family:
  Arial;font-weight:bold;language:EN'>Granbury, TX – June 24, 2002</span><span
  style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:100%;font-family:Arial;language:EN'> – 23 
  cities across four states in the Southwest rolled out the red carpet for the 
  20th</span><span style='font-size:12.0pt;
  line-height:100%;font-family:Arial;language:EN'> Anniversary Great Race, the
  world’s longest running and richest vintage car race.  All host cities
  competed for the hotly contested Great American City Awards, a $10,000 prize
  package shared by the libraries of the three winning communities. Cities compete
  in their own categories:  overnight, lunch and pit stops.  A mix of Great
  Racers and staff vote nightly on hospitality, theme, enthusiasm, size of
  crowd, and refreshments provided.  The ballots are totaled and winners are
  announced at the awards ceremony.</span></p>
  <p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:100%;font-family:
  Arial;language:EN'>&nbsp;</span></p>
  <p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:100%;font-family:
  Arial;language:EN'> Clovis, NM edged out Anaheim, CA in one of the closest
  votes in the Great American City Award competition history.  For the second
  time, Clovis secured $5,000 and the title of best overnight city.  Locals
  themed the Great Race visit around a comprehensive Fiesta style
  celebration.   Racers arrived on Main Street to the tunes of mariachis and
  the swirls and twirls of flamenco dancers in festive garb. Hosts assigned to
  each racing team smothered the competitors with unique Clovis hospitality: 
  individual parking stalls decorated with brightly colored piñatas and tissue
  paper flowers, grills sizzling with fajitas right on the spot and 6,000+
  colorfully dressed spectators circulating the packed Main Street for five
  blocks.</span></p>
  <p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:100%;font-family:
  Arial;language:EN'>&nbsp;</span></p>
  <p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:100%;font-family:
  Arial;language:EN'> Racers and staff voted Prescott, AZ the best of seven
  lunch stops, earning the library a $2,500 check. In the first Race visit ever
  to Prescott, Great Racers rolled down historic Whiskey Row among a throng of
  cheering local Race devotees.  The Prescott stop featured a refreshing dining
  twist.  The restaurants surrounding the Courthouse plaza chipped in on the
  Race experience by feeding the participants.  Rather than eating in a central
  location, Racers and staff spread out around the plaza to lunch with the
  regular crowd in the sponsoring eateries.</span></p>
  <p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:100%;font-family:
  Arial;language:EN'>&nbsp;</span></p>
  <p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:100%;font-family:
  Arial;language:EN'> Eastland, TX, famous for ‘Old Rip’, the horned toad
  encased in a marble and glass sarcophagus in the 1928 courthouse, captured
  the prize as best pit stop and scored $2,500 for the local library.  Streets
  were lined with American flags, balloons, and black and white checkered flag
  rope, setting the stage for a rousing, Lone Star state welcome.  Refreshments
  featured horned toad cookies, home-baked goods and fresh fruit.</span></p>
  <p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:100%;font-family:
  Arial;language:EN'>&nbsp;</span></p>
  <p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:100%;font-family:
  Arial;language:EN'> “Our host cities are a cornerstone of the Great Race
  experience,” noted Race CEO and founder Tom McRae.  “Year after year, the
  hospitality extended to us while traveling the route means so much to the
  Racers.  The course is grueling, the heat sometimes sweltering and the cars
  un-predicable, but we can always count on the enthusiasm and generosity of
  our host stops for rejuvenation.”</span></p>
  <p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-size:12.0pt;line-height:100%;font-family:

  language:EN'>For more information contact: Greatrace, LLC:  800-989-7223</span></p>
