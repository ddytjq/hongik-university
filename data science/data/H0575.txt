					</div>

						<li><a href="Transforming_Plants.html" target="_top">Transforming Plants - Basic Genetic Engineering Techniques</a> - How to get new genes into plants.

					<ul>
						<li><a href="vTours/agri/toc/toc.html">Agricultural Biotechnology, a Virtual Tour: &nbsp; &quot;Look Ma, No Seeds in my Watermelon&quot;</a> - Building the perfect watermelon - a case-study from research to market.

						<li><a href="http://www.ed.gov/databases/ERIC_Digests/ed402152.html">Biotechnology Education and the Internet</a> - A 1996 review of biotechnology information resources available on the Internet and current links to <a href="http://ericir.syr.edu/ithome/digests/EDO-IR-1999-06.html">&quot;Ask an Expert&quot;</a>
