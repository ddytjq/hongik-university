    <div align="center"><center><table border="0" cellpadding="3" width="95%">
      <tr>
        <td>
          <h2 align="center"><font size="1">Five-Star Basketball</font></h2>
          <h2 align="center"><font size="1">Individual Workout Drills For Post
          Players</font></h2>
        <h3 align="center"><font color="#000000"><small>by Stu Lash<br>
        </small></font></h3>
        <font size="2"><p align="center"></font><font color="#000000" size="1"><a
        href="http://www.five-starbasketball.com/"><img
        src="../../../../images/Graphics/Five-Star_banner.gif"
        alt="Five-Star_banner.gif (12378 bytes)" align="center" border="0" WIDTH="397" HEIGHT="85"></a></font><br>
        </p>
        </td>
      </tr>
      <tr>
        <td width="65%" style="border: thin ridge rgb(0,0,128); padding: 2px" valign="top"
        align="left"><h3><font size="1"><br>
          <font color="#000080">
          The Coach: Stu Lash<br>
          Recruiting Director<br>
          Five-Star Basketball Camps<br>
          Yonkers, New York</font></font></h3>
          <p><font color="#000000" size="1">Stu is the currently the Director of
          Recruiting for Five-Star Basketball Camps.&nbsp; Five-Star has
          traditionally brought in and developed some of the top talents in both
          America and the world each year as part of one of the most successful
          and productive camps systems in existence over the last 35 years.<br>
          </font>
        </td>
      </tr>
    </center>
    </table>
      <p align="left"><font color="#CC3300"><b>Introduction </b> </font> </p>
          <p align="left"><font size="1" color="#000000">This is the workout program we have
          used at Five-Star Instructional Basketball Camps which has been
          developed over the 35 plus years we have been in existence.&nbsp; This
          program should help you to organize both your time and skill workouts
          to get the maximum benefit as a guard or wing-forward player.&nbsp;
          Join us next month when I will provide you the post player workouts.</font><font size="1"></o:p></font><span style="font-weight: normal; font-style: normal; text-decoration: none; text-underline: none"><font size="1"><o:p>&nbsp;
          The time it takes to complete the program will depend upon your skill
          level and the efficiency of your workout program.&nbsp; Remember there
          is no getting around putting in repetitions to automate your perimeter
          skills.</font></span></p>
      <p class="MsoNormal" align="left"><font size="1" color="#000080"><b>Ball
      Slaps/Mikan's/Taps</b></font></p>
      <p class="MsoNormal" align="left"><font size="1">2
      minutes each</font></p>
      <p class="MsoNormal" align="left"><font size="1"><font color="#000080"><b>Five-Spot
      Shooting</b></font></font></p>
      <blockquote>
      <p class="MsoNormal" align="left"><font size="1"><font color="#008080"><b>Makes five shots (or 1 1/2 minutes) from each of the five spots</b></font><br>
      <i>
      Go around and back-Make a total of 50 shots</i><br>
      All Shots should be in the 5-10 foot range.</font></p>
      </blockquote>
      <p class="MsoNormal" align="left"><font size="1" color="#008000"><b>Shoot
      15 Foul Shots</b></font></p>
      <p class="MsoNormal" align="left"><font size="1" color="#000080"><b>Footwork</b></font></p>
      <blockquote>
        <p class="MsoNormal" align="left"><font size="1"><b><font color="#008080">This
        is a time drill- (30 seconds)- Go from one drill to the next, repeat the
        series 3 times.</font></b><br>
        <i>Jumping Drill</i>- Place your feet together and jump on the balls of
        your feet- moving left to right<br>
        <i>Toe-Touch Drill</i>- Place your feet apart and jump upward and touch
        your toes<br>
        <i>Heel- Touch Drill</i>- <span style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal; mso-bidi-font-style: normal">Place
        your feet apart and jump upward and touch your heel<br>
        <i>Heel and Toe Touch Drill-</i> Place your feet apart and jump upward
        and alternate touching your heels and toes.</span></font></p>
      </blockquote>
      <p class="MsoNormal" align="left"><font size="1" color="#008000"><span style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal; mso-bidi-font-style: normal"><b>Shoot
      15 Foul Shots</b></span></font></p>
      <p align="left"><font size="1"><span style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal; mso-bidi-font-style: normal"><b><font color="#000080">Drop
      Step Moves</font></b></span></font></p>
      <blockquote>
        <p align="left"><font size="1"><span style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal; mso-bidi-font-style: normal"><font color="#008080"><b>Make
        5 shots off of each move</b></font><br>
        First start on the right side with the right hand and after all moves
        are completed move to the left side with the left hand.&nbsp; Always
        have a strong base in the post-established position and feel the
        defender.<br>
        <i>Drop Step Power Dribble- to the outside of the paint- straight up
        lay-up or dunk<br>
        Drop Step Power Dribble- to the inside of the paint- straight up lay-up
        or dunk<br>
        Drop Step Power Dribble to the outside of the paint- jump hook<br>
        Drop Step Power Dribble to the inside of the paint- jump hook<br>
        Drop Step Power Dribble to the outside of the paint- ball-fake, straight
        up lay-up or dunk<br>
        Drop Step Power Dribble to the inside of the paint- ball-fake, straight
        up lay-up or dunk<br>
        Drop Step Power Dribble to the outside of the paint- ball-fake, up and
        under move<br>
        Drop Step Power Dribble to the inside of the paint- ball-fake, up and
        under move</i></span></font></p>
      </blockquote>
      <p align="left"><font size="1"><span style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal; mso-bidi-font-style: normal"><b><font color="#008000">Shoot
      15 Foul Shots</font></b><br>
      <br>
      <font color="#000080"><b>Back to the Basket Moves without the Drop Step</b></font></span></font></p>
      <blockquote>
        <p align="left"><font size="1"><span style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal; mso-bidi-font-style: normal"><b><font color="#008080">Make
        5 shots off of each move</font></b><br>
        First start on the right side with the right hand and after all moves
        are completed move to the left side with the left hand.&nbsp; Always
        have a strong base in the post-established position- gathering yourself
        prior to each move and feel the defender.<br>
        <i>Catch the Ball- feel the defender- Jump Hook<br>
        Catch the Ball- feel the defender- Crab Move<br>
        Catch the Ball- feel the defender- Turn and Face- Shoot the Jump Shot<br>
        Catch the Ball- feel the defender- Turn and Face- Ball fake, one
        dribble- attack the rim</i></span></font></p>
      </blockquote>
      <p align="left"><font size="1"><span style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal; mso-bidi-font-style: normal"><b><font color="#008000">Shoot
      15 Foul Shots</font></b><br>
      <br>
      <font color="#000080"><b>Back to the Basket Moves without the Drop Step-
      Adding the Re-Post</b></font></span></font></p>
      <blockquote>
        <p align="left"><font size="1"><span style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal; mso-bidi-font-style: normal"><b><font color="#008080">Make
        5 shots off of each move</font></b><br>
        First start on the right side with the right hand and after all moves
        are completed move to the left side with the left hand.&nbsp; Always
        have a strong base in the post- establish position- gather yourself and
        feel the defender.<br>
        <i>Catch the Ball- feel the defender- Jump Hook<br>
        Catch the Ball- feel the defender- Crab Move<br>
        Catch the Ball- feel the defender- Turn and Face- Shoot the Jump Shot<br>
        Catch the Ball- feel the defender- Turn and Face- Ball fake, one
        dribble- attack the rim<br>
        </i></span><i style="mso-bidi-font-style: normal; mso-bidi-font-weight: normal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal; mso-bidi-font-style: normal"></o:p>
        </b></i></font></p>
      </blockquote>
      <p align="left"><font size="1"><span style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal; mso-bidi-font-style: normal"><b><font color="#008000">Shoot
      15 Foul Shots</font></b><br>
      <br>
      <font color="#000080"><b>Agility and Flash Cuts</b></font></span></font></p>
      <blockquote>
        <p align="left"><font size="1"><span style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal; mso-bidi-font-style: normal"><b><font color="#008080">These
        are time drills (1 minute) -Go through each drill 3 times</font></b>)<br>
        Place one ball on each block<br>
        <i>Go Block to Block- Drop Step-Power Dribble- attack the rim</i><br>
        Place one ball on each elbow<br>
        <i>Flash to each elbow from the block- Turn and Face- Jump Shot</i>
        (also good to work on for a possible high-low pass situation)</span></font></p>
      </blockquote>
      <p align="left"><font size="1"><span style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal; mso-bidi-font-style: normal"><b><font color="#008000">Shoot
      15 Foul Shots<br>
      <br>
      </font><font color="#000080">Combination Drills</font></b></span></font></p>
      <blockquote>
        <p align="left"><font size="1"><span style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal; mso-bidi-font-style: normal"><b><font color="#008080">Three
        Spot Post Move Series</font></b><br>
        This drill is tough to do unless you have at least 2-3 passers<br>
        If you have 3 passers- passer A goes to the right wing- passer B goes to
        the top of the key, Passer C goes to the left wing.<br>
        <font color="#008080"><b>(2 minutes on the clock) do the drill 3 times<br>
        </b></font>Player starts under the rim, player flashes to each spot, can
        not use a move back to back<br>
        <i>Moves<br>
        Drop Step Lay-up<br>
        Drop Step Ball Fake Lay-Up<br>
        Jump Hook<br>
        Jump Shot<br>
        Up and Under Move<br>
        Crab Move<br>
        Turn and Face Up- one dribble attack the rim</i></span></font></p>
      </blockquote>
      <p align="left"><span style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal; mso-bidi-font-style: normal"><font size="1"><b><font color="#008000">Shoot
      25 Foul Shots<br>
      <br>
      program completed!</font></b></font></span></p>
