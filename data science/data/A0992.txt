		<P>
		If your company is facing an unusual situation, it can be frustrating when all that's on offer from some banks are standardised solutions, none of which quite work. As is having to explain your business from scratch to yet another new face who understands nothing more about your organisation than a row of figures on a page.
		<P>
At Bank of Scotland, we solve these problems before they happen with one simple philosophy - relationship banking. Long term relationships are central to the ethos of our Corporate Banking Division. With this strong relationship culture as our foundation, we can create innovative and individualised solutions to the often complex banking needs of our corporate customers. We keep our internal lines of communication as short as possible, to give you quick, constructive responses to your requirements.
<P>
As a customer you are assigned a skilled and experienced director who can handle your day to day banking requirements, as well as acting as a hub for any specialist services you may want to call on. We encourage a creative approach to problem-solving and by working closely with you, your key Bank of Scotland contacts can build up a real knowledge and understanding of the unique aspects of your business and your markets.
<P>
As well as providing information on any specialist services required, your relationship director can advise on a full range of corporate banking products. With the support of your director, a suite of products tailored to suit your own particular financial requirements could be put in place.

		<a href="corpdeposits.html"><span class=bold>Corporate Deposit Services</span></a>
		<P>

		<span class=body>When you have surplus cash, you require competitive rates and the assurance of dealing with one of the UK's premier financial institutions. But when you need the cash back to work in the business, easy accessibility is essential. With dedicated service from a specialist Corporate Deposit team, in your area, on standby to respond instantly to your requests, Bank of Scotland brings you the ultimate cash management solution, giving you absolute control of your sterling funds. </span>
<p>
<a href="payment.html"><span class=bold>Payment Services</span></a>
<P>
<span class=body>
Bank of Scotland offers a range of payment services allowing customers to transfer funds to third parties electronically, both in the UK and worldwide, to settle suppliers invoices, make commission payments, pay wages, salaries and expenses, and undertake other transactions. </span>
<P>
<a href="electronicbank.html"><span class=bold>Electronic Banking Services</span></a>
<P>
<span class=body>
Our electronic banking services give you control and flexibility over the management of your money, providing full electronic banking from your home and office via PC. The range of services includes balance checks of all your bank, credit cards and foreign currency accounts, funds transfer and the payment of bills or salaries by BACS (Bankers' Automated Clearing System). CORPORATE HOBS&#174; also provides same-day CHAPS (Clearing Houses Automated Payment System) payments and International payments and drafts.
</span>
<P>
<a href="specialistservices.html"><span class=bold>Specialist Services</span></a><P>

</span>
