    <td width="100%" align="center" colspan="2"><b>Portman&#146;s 2002 Calendar &#150; A recipe for success!</b></td>
  </tr>
  <tr>
    <td width="100%" colspan="2"><br>
<p>CLIC (Cancer and Leukaemia In Childhood) and ten regional charities will 
benefit from Portman&#146;s 2002 Calendar, which features twelve distinct features or 
landmarks from Portman&#146;s operating area along with twelve traditional recipes.
</p>
<p>The Society is seeking a donation of at least &pound;1 per calendar from customers 
and hopes to raise over &pound;20,000 for charitable causes. The calendars are 
available from any of Portman&#146;s 113 branches. </p>
<p>&#147;The Portman calendar has been popular with our customers for many years and 
since 1997, when we began to use it as a fundraising tool, over &pound;100,000 has 
been passed on to good causes&#148;, says Mike Dobson, Portman&#146;s Group Manager, 
Communications.</p>
<p>&#147;Once again, we have produced a calendar of high quality. In addition to 
helping people plan for 2002 and remember important dates we hope that it will 
encourage people to try out at least one or two of those traditional recipes!&#148; 
he added.</p>
<p><b>Notes to Editors:</b></p>
<p>CLIC will receive 50% of the donations made for Portman&#146;s Calendar. CLIC is a 
national charity based in Bristol, providing services for all children affected 
by cancer and Leukaemia. CLIC provides for the whole family and concentrates on 
three specific areas &#150; treatment, care and research. </p>
