    <TD ALIGN="LEFT" COLSPAN="2">
      <FONT FACE="Arial, Helvetica">
      This scheme is a  mortgage or remortgage for landlords who are 
looking to borrow below our minimum loan (£15,001 to £25,000).  This scheme has a 1% loading and, reflecting the market costs of wholesale money, moves in line with general trends of financial markets. This rate is fixed at 6.35% for 1 year and then reverts to 2.5% above 3 month LIBOR (London Inter Bank Offered Rate) which is reset quarterly in January, April, July & October.  The current LIBOR rate applicable is 6.70%.

      <FONT FACE="Arial, Helvetica" SIZE="+1">
      Early Redemption Charge
      </FONT><BR>
      <FONT FACE="Arial, Helvetica">
      <BLOCKQUOTE>
      3 month's interest if the loan is repaid within the first 3 years. If a similar mortgage is continued on a new property of the same (or higher) amount, consideration will be given to carrying over the redemption charges.
      </BLOCKQUOTE>
      </FONT>

  <TR>
    <TD ALIGN="LEFT" COLSPAN="2">
      <FONT FACE="Arial, Helvetica" SIZE="+1">
      Income Requirements
      </FONT><BR>
      <FONT FACE="Arial, Helvetica">
      <BLOCKQUOTE>
      Non Portfolio & Investment Applications: 

<P>For let properties, up to 50% rental income may be added to the primary applicants annual income provided the amount does not exceed 20% of the individuals total income.<BR>
<BR>
As a general rule, the maximum loan available will be assessed by applying the appropriate multiple(s) (see Mortgage Income Requirement Guidelines) to the annual income(s) determined and the resultant amount must cover the proposed mortgage loan and any other mortgages.<BR>
<BR>
<U>Portfolio Applications:</U> <BR>
Where the applicant owns three or more let properties and has more than 12 months lettings experience, then the maximum loan may be assessed on the basis that the gross rental income from the property must be equal or exceed 150% of the interest only payment calculated at the prevailing Lettings Variable Rate.      </BLOCKQUOTE>
      </FONT>
    </TD>
  </TR>

  <TR>
    <TD ALIGN="LEFT" COLSPAN="2">
      <FONT FACE="Arial, Helvetica" SIZE="+1">
      Additional Requirements
      </FONT><BR>
      <FONT FACE="Arial, Helvetica">
      <BLOCKQUOTE>
      Three months bank statements are required with the application together with three most recent payslips, or last P60 (3 years accounts if self-employed) and two most recent annual mortgage statements.  If future rental income is to be taken into account we will require a letter from a local letting agent confirming the estimated income.
<BR>
<BR>
Property insurance is required. Paragon do offer Paragon Privilege, a 2nd Property insurance covering buildings &amp; contents, rental income cover and appliance breakdown.<BR>
* Full Homelet  - £34.95 per property.  We are not able to offer just buildings insurance alone.<BR>
* Homelet excluding Buildings Insurance - £29.95<BR>
* Full Payment Protection Insurance - protects against accident, sickness and redundancy @ £4.85 per month per £100 of your monthly mortgage payment.<BR>
Life insurance is at the discretion of the applicants and their advisers.      </BLOCKQUOTE>
      </FONT>
    </TD>

      Acceptable Properties
      </FONT><BR>
      <FONT FACE="Arial, Helvetica">
      <BLOCKQUOTE>
      The property will be valued on the basis of a single family dwelling, and valuation will include a deduction for any conversion costs.
<BR>
<BR>
There is no limit on height/storey for flats, subject to valuation.<BR>
<BR>
If a property is split into more than 4/5 units, we must refer to our insurers for approval, and there could be an issue with valuation, as conversion costs may be very high.       </BLOCKQUOTE>

    <TD ALIGN="LEFT" COLSPAN="2">
      <FONT FACE="Arial, Helvetica" SIZE="+1">
      Solicitors
      </FONT><BR>
      <FONT FACE="Arial, Helvetica">
      <BLOCKQUOTE>
      Paragon Mortgages will normally instruct the applicants' solicitors to act on the Company's behalf in respect of both the conveyance and completion of the Company's requirements with regard to letting the property, provided the firm:
<UL><LI>Has a minimum of two partners.<LI>The solicitor acting holds a current practicing certificate.<LI>Is shown in the current edition of &quot;Solicitors &amp; Barristers Directory and Diary&quot;.<LI>Has been in business for at least 3 years.<LI>Has commercial premises, from where business is conducted.</UL>
<P><B>Note:</B> If the above requirements cannot be met, Paragon Mortgages may instruct its own solicitors to act for the Company.  Applicants will be responsible for costs incurred which will be deducted from the advance.  Applicants may instruct the Company's solicitors to act on their behalf should they wish.
<P>Paragon Mortgages will not instruct sole practitioners or licensed conveyancers.  Please check that the solicitor you would like to use is acceptable to Paragon Mortgages in advance of submission.      </BLOCKQUOTE>
      </FONT>
