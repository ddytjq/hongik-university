            <p class="contenttextbold">Have you ever considered how much of your 
              business' time is taken up dealing with banking - much of it routine 
              ?</p>
            <p>TeleBank&reg; Business, our PC banking package, 
              can assist by carrying out a wide range of financial operations 
              from your office personal computer without the need to visit your 
              Bank. </p>
            <p>By using TeleBank&reg; Business you can access 
              your bank accounts 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. </p>
            <p class="contenttextbold">How your business can benefit </p>
            <ul>
              <li><strong>Saves your business time and money</strong></li>
              <p>Reduces time spent visiting your bank allowing 
                you and your employees to manage your time more effectively. </p>
              <p>Reduces administration time and costs associated 
                with paperwork. </p>
              <li><strong>Freedom to access your Bank account</strong>
              </li>
              <p>Provides up-to-date information on your accounts 
                24 hours a day 7 days a week. </p>
              <li><strong>Easy to Use</strong></li>
              <p>Runs under Microsoft&reg; Windows with the 
                advantages of being easy to install and quick to learn. Should 
                you require any assistance, on-line help is available on your 
                PC and there is also a Help Desk you may contact. </p>
              <li><strong>Security</strong></li>
              <p>Information is encrypted ensuring data is 
                protected. </p>
              <p>Gives you full control of any employee's 
                access to functions on the PC and provides a full audit log of 
                all activities. </p>
            </ul>
            <p class="contenttextbold">Range of Services</p>
            <p>You can tailor your TeleBank&reg; system to 
              meet your business' individual financial needs through choosing 
              the relevant modules from the following selection :- </p>
            <p><strong>Module 1 : Balance Reporting</strong></p>
            <ul>
              <li class="contenttext">Provides balance and transaction details 
                on your bank accounts up to close of business each evening and 
                also provides real time updates throughout the day. </li>
              <li class="contenttext">Enables you to transfer funds between your 
                accounts and keep surplus money in a TeleBank&reg; Investment 
                Account. </li>
              <li class="contenttext">Allows a small number of payments to pre-registered 
                beneficiaries. </li>
              <li class="contenttext">The system allows you to restrict reports 
                as required - you can group accounts for balance reporting, look 
                at all paid cheques or even search to see if a specific payment 
                has been received. </li>
              <li class="contenttext">Turns Bank data into manageable information 
                with the ability to be exported to other software, such as a spreadsheet 
                program to enable you to produce tailored management reports. 
              </li>
              <li class="contenttext">The system can be set up to accept download 
                files overnight so that your reports are ready each morning. </li>
              <li class="contenttext">Offers electronic mail facility for your 
                employees to communicate between each other and with the bank. 
              </li>
            </ul>
            <p><strong>Module 2 : CHAPS and SWIFT</strong></p>
            <p>CHAPS is the standard UK payment system for 
              making same-day payments to any UK beneficiary. </p>
            <p>SWIFT is the worldwide payment system for making 
              payments outside the UK in a variety of different currencies.</p>
            <ul>
              <li class="contenttext">Makes electronic payment simple and convenient. 
              <li> 
              <li class="contenttext">Regular payee details can be registered 
                with the Bank so all that requires updating is the amount. </li>
              <li class="contenttext">Payments can be prepared off-line and uploaded 
                to the Bank in one &quot;update&quot; with confirmation being 
                returned down the line. This process assists in minimising communication 
                costs. </li>
              <li class="contenttext">Generates PC reports, giving a summary and 
                details of payment transactions </li>
            </ul>
            <p><strong>Module 3 : BACS</strong></p>
            <p>Allows automated payments to be made simply, 
              effectively and cheaply. This is the cheapest form of making higher 
              volume UK payments.</p>
            <ul>
              <li class="contenttext">Saves a large amount of administration time 
                and costs. </li>
              <li class="contenttext">Facilitates preparation of payroll, trade 
                payments or direct debits using a database on your PC. </li>
              <li class="contenttext">All the relevant information can be stored 
                in advance with only the payment amounts requiring updating. </li>
              <li class="contenttext">Option of submitting instructions up to 
                30 days in advance of the due date. </li>
              <li class="contenttext">Unlike Bank Giro Credits, funds remain in 
                your account for a further 2 days after you confirm the instructions. 
              </li>
              <li class="contenttext">Generates PC reports, giving a summary and 
                details of payment transactions </li>
            </ul>
