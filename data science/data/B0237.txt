<P><B>BASE RATE TRACKER MORTGAGE - 1.00% ABOVE BANK BASE RATE</B></p>

<P><B>APR 5.70%</B></p>

<P>Assumes that the current variable base rate tracker mortgage of 5.00% remains in place for five years from completion after which the mortgage will revert to the Society's Variable Residential Rate (currently 5.25%) for the remaining term of the mortgage and a buildings insurance premium of £7.94 per month as reflected in the total amount payable and APR, a capital and interest repayment mortgage of £50,000 over 25 years and a purchase price of £70,000.</p>
