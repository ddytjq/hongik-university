<h1>
<b>B<font size=+2>OOMERANG </font>Instrumentation</b></h1></center>
<font face=""><font size=+1>B</font><font size=-1>OOMERANG</font>, </font>designed
to measure anisotropies in the Cosmic Microwave Background (CMB), consists
of an array of detectors cooled to 0.28 Kelvin mounted at the focus of
a 1.3 meter telescope.&nbsp; The instrument is flown on a gondola beneath
a NASA/NSBF high altitude balloon.&nbsp;

flight, and scans back and forth repeatedly over the selected observing
field to map the CMB.</td>

sun from interfering with our measurements.</td>
