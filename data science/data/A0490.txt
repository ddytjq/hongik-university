							<p><a name="valid"><span class="bodybold">Q.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;How long is my credit card valid for?</span></a>
							<br><span class="body">A.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Your credit card will be valid for three years after it is issued.  The expiry date is shown on your card.</span>
							<p><a href="#top"><img src="../../common/up.gif" align="right" width="14" height="13" border="0" alt="Click here to go to the top of the page"><br>
                            <div align="right" span class="tablered1">Back to top</div></a></p>
							
			  				<p><a name="free"><span class="bodybold">Q.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;	How do I get free Travel Accident Insurance?</span></a>
							<br><span class="body">A.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; All you have to do is pay for all or part of your travel with a credit card from us (including the cost of a commuter season ticket loan or car hire). You will benefit from free automatic cover for death or permanent disability anywhere in the world while you are travelling to and from your destination</span></p>
							<p><a href="#top"><img src="../../common/up.gif" align="right" width="14" height="13" border="0" alt="Click here to go to the top of the page"><br>
                            <div align="right" span class="tablered1">Back to top</div></a></p>
							
			  				<p><a name="cover"><span class="bodybold">Q.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;	What does this cover me for?</span></a>
							<br><span class="body">A.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Travel Accident Insurance will cover you for up to £50,000 in the event of death, loss of sight, or permanent and total disablement. Personal Liability Insurance will cover you for up to £1 million for accidental injury to other people and accidental loss of, or damage to, other people's property. You should refer to your insurance certificate for more information</span></p>
							<p><a href="#top"><img src="../../common/up.gif" align="right" width="14" height="13" border="0" alt="Click here to go to the top of the page"><br>
                            <div align="right" span class="tablered1">Back to top</div></a></p>
							
			  				<p><a name="extra"><span class="bodybold">Q.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;	Do I need to take out extra holiday insurance?</span></a>
							<br><span class="body">A.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Yes. You need to take out a full holiday insurance policy in addition to our Travel Accident Insurance. This should cover you for events such as  cancellation, loss of luggage etc.</span></p>
							<p><a href="#top"><img src="../../common/up.gif" align="right" width="14" height="13" border="0" alt="Click here to go to the top of the page"><br>
                            <div align="right" span class="tablered1">Back to top</div></a></p>
							
			  				<p><a name="minimum"><span class="bodybold">Q.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;	What is meant by minimum repayment by Direct Debit?</span></a>
							<br><span class="body">A.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;You can make arrangements to avoid the inconvenience of making a manual payment each month. The minimum amount that is due for repayment will be automatically debited to your bank account. This will amount to £5 per month or 3% of the balance, whichever is greater.</span></p>
							<p><a href="#top"><img src="../../common/up.gif" align="right" width="14" height="13" border="0" alt="Click here to go to the top of the page"><br>
                            <div align="right" span class="tablered1">Back to top</div></a></p>
							
			  				<p><a name="transfer"><span class="bodybold">Q.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;	How do I transfer money owed on other credit and store cards?</span></a>
							<br><span class="body">A.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Details on transferring your other balances can be obtained by telephoning <span class="bodytextredboldtext">08456 043 114</span>. Lines are open from 9am to 9pm Monday to Friday and from 9am to 1pm on Saturday. Calls may be monitored or recorded for quality purposes. You can also apply online, simply <a href="https://hsss.hsbc.co.uk/bankinguk/creditcards/cardsbalancetransfer/move-your-balance-to-us.htm">click here</a> and complete the details.</span>
							<p><a href="#top"><img src="../../common/up.gif" align="right" width="14" height="13" border="0" alt="Click here to go to the top of the page"><br>
                            <div align="right" span class="tablered1">Back to top</div></a></p>
							
			  				<p><a name="waiting"><span class="bodybold">Q.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;	Why am I sometimes kept waiting when I use my card to make a purchase? </span></a>
			  				<br><span class="body">A.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;From time to time you may experience slight delays or be asked security questions when using your card to make purchases. The retailer will occasionally make a call to authenticate a transaction. The process is designed to reduce fraudulent transactions on lost or stolen cards. Please be assured that this is a fraud prevention measure as we continually work to prevent unauthorised use of your card/s.</span></p>
							<p><a href="#top"><img src="../../common/up.gif" align="right" width="14" height="13" border="0" alt="Click here to go to the top of the page"><br>
                            <div align="right" span class="tablered1">Back to top</div></a></p>
							
							<p><a name="cashuk"><span class="bodybold">Q.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;	How can I obtain cash using my credit card in the UK?</span></a>
			  				<br><span class="body">A.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;You can use your credit card to obtain cash from machines just as you would to withdraw money from your normal bank account, provided you know your Personal Identification Number (PIN).  If you have a combined MasterCard/Visa account, you should use the same PIN number for both cards.</span>
							<br><span class="body">A cash handling fee of 1.5% (minimum £1.50) is charged for all credit cards.</span></p>
							<p><span class="body">You can also use your card to obtain cash over the counter at banks and building societies displaying the Visa or MasterCard symbols.</span></p>
							<p><span class="body">N.B. You will be required to show additional identification such as your passport or driving licence.</p></span></p>
							<p><a href="#top"><img src="../../common/up.gif" align="right" width="14" height="13" border="0" alt="Click here to go to the top of the page"><br>
                            <div align="right" span class="tablered1">Back to top</div></a></p>
							
							<p><a name="cash"><span class="bodybold">Q.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;	How can I obtain cash using my card while I am abroad?</span></a>
			  				<br><span class="body">A.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;You can obtain cash while you are abroad using your card in cash machines or over the counter at banks, building societies and travel agents just as you would in the UK. Your card will be acceptable wherever you see the Visa or MasterCard Symbols. Some local restrictions may apply to the amount of cash you are able to withdraw at any one time.</span></p>
							<p><a href="#top"><img src="../../common/up.gif" align="right" width="14" height="13" border="0" alt="Click here to go to the top of the page"><br>
                            <div align="right" span class="tablered1">Back to top</div></a></p>
							
							<p><a name="interest"><span class="bodybold">Q.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;	Will I be charged more interest for withdrawing cash?</span></a>
			  				<br><span class="body">A.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;No. Interest for cash withdrawn is charged in exactly the same way as for purchases. Contrary to what you may believe, interest is only charged when you <span class="bodybolditalic">do not </span>repay the full balance showing on your statement by the payment date.</span></p>
							<p><a href="#top"><img src="../../common/up.gif" align="right" width="14" height="13" border="0" alt="Click here to go to the top of the page"><br>
                            <div align="right" span class="tablered1">Back to top</div></a></p>
							
							<p><a name="receive"><span class="bodybold">Q.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;	How often will I receive a credit card statement?</span></a>
			  				<br><span class="body">A.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;You will receive a statement approximately every month. The precise day on which you receive your statement will vary slightly from month to month depending on how many weekends or bank holidays there are in the month.</span></p>
							<p><a href="#top"><img src="../../common/up.gif" align="right" width="14" height="13" border="0" alt="Click here to go to the top of the page"><br>
                            <div align="right" span class="tablered1">Back to top</div></a></p>
							
							<p><a name="limit"><span class="bodybold">Q.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;	Can I increase my credit limit at any time?</span></a>
			  				<br><span class="body">A.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;If you feel that your present limit is insufficient, contact HSBC Card Services on <span class="bodytextredboldtext">08457 404 404 </span> and we will try to meet your requirements for a higher limit. Lines are open 8am to 10pm. every day except Christmas Day. Calls may be monitored or recorded for quality purposes.</span></p>
							<p><a href="#top"><img src="../../common/up.gif" align="right" width="14" height="13" border="0" alt="Click here to go to the top of the page"><br>
                            <div align="right" span class="tablered1">Back to top</div></a></p>
							
							<p><a name="protect"><span class="bodybold">Q.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;	What cover is available to protect my payments if I lose my job?</span></a>
			  				<br><span class="body">A.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Our Cardholder Repayment Protector insurance costs just a few pence a day, and covers your repayments in the event of accident, sickness, unemployment or death.</span></p>
							<p><a href="#top"><img src="../../common/up.gif" align="right" width="14" height="13" border="0" alt="Click here to go to the top of the page"><br>
                            <div align="right" span class="tablered1">Back to top</div></a></p>
							
							<p><a name="difficult"><span class="bodybold">Q.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;	What should I do if I experience payment difficulties?</span></a>
							<br><span class="body">A.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;You should contact us at the earliest opportunity to discuss your situation. The sooner we know the problem, the sooner we can help you.</span></p>
