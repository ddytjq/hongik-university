<p><font face="Arial" size="2"><b>Business First - Spring 2002</b><br>
<br>
The latest issue of Business First, the magazine produced by The Royal Bank 
of Scotland for senior business managers in the mid-corporate sector is now 
available.

You can download the entire issue or order a print version from <a href="mailto:teresa.fitzgibbons@rbs.co.uk"> Teresa
Fitzgibbons</a>.&nbsp;<br>
<br>

This issue features the following articles:<br>
<br>
<b><a href="spring2002.pdf">Business focus</a></b> &#150; Coverage of upcoming events, sources of help and industry news<br>
<br>
<b><a href="spring2002.pdf">The euro and you</a></b> &#150; Richard Chalk and Jeremy Peat of The Royal Bank of Scotland consider the implications of the introduction of euro notes and coins<br>
<br>
<b><a href="spring2002.pdf">Path to creativity</a></b> &#150; Profile customer John Varney and his Centre for Creative Management<br>
<br>
