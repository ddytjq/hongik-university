	It provides a high level of personal service, covering almost all major areas of personal finance.</p>

<p>With a record of over 100 years business in Beverley, the Society is a major financial institution in the town and has served generations of families in the area.</p>

<p>Today the Society provides a comprehensive range of services in addition to its core business of mortgages and investments. These include personal and home insurance and a wide range of pensions and general investment advice.</p>

<p>The Society prides itself on its confidential professional advice service, which is provided free of charge and without any obligation, to all.</p>
