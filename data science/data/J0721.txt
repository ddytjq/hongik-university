SHEPHERD CAPTURES THIRD MESA MARIN VICTORY
<br><br>
BAKERSFIELD, Calif. (June 8) -- Dick Shepherd, of Bakersfield, notched his third NASCAR Late Model feature victory of the season by capturing the Bruce's Travel Plaza 75 at Mesa Marin Raceway on Saturday night. Bakersfield rookie Tom Fry recorded a season best second place finish while Pacific Coast Region and track point leader Sean Woodside, of Saugus, maintained a perfect top five record posting a third. Sixteen year old Bakersfield rookie Jason Small and Donnie Davis, of Valencia, grabbed season high finishes at fourth and fifth respectively.
<br><br>
In a season strong 18-car field, Rod Johnson won the 30-lap Grand American Modified main event over Rip Michaels and Dave Blankenship. Jeff Drummen captured the 15-lap Super Street Stock feature over T.K. Karvasek.
