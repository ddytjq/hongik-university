<p>
<B><font size="+1">Car Rental</font></B>
</p>
<blockquote>
<p>
All major rental car companies have outlets in Albuquerque and Santa Fe. The  car rental agencies are now in a separate facility reached by free shuttle from the Albuquerque airport. We have negotiated new  excellent corporate rates with Enterprise.   To make a reservation,  call  1-800-736-8227 or use the website at <a href="http://www.enterprise.com">http://www.enterprise.com</a>.  Go  into the "Corporate Class Program" section of the website and enter the Id # 3400179  and the first three letters of company name "SAN".   Make your reservation for the Albuquerque Airport location.   Since our arrangement with Enterprise doesn't cover insurance,  we recommend that you accept supplemental coverage at the desk ONLY if your credit card or personal insurance policy does not cover rental car insurance.
</p>
</blockquote>
<p>
<B><font size="+1">Shuttle Service from the Albuquerque Airport</font></B>
</p>
<blockquote>
<p>
You must check in at the Ground Transportation Desk across from the Southwest Airlines baggage claim area.
</p>
<p>
Due to road construction in Albuquerque, each trip is approximately 1 hour and 20 - 25 minutes. Reservations are reccomended to ensure a seat.
</p>
<p>
Note: Since the shuttle schedules change periodically, we've decided to link to the bus companies' web sites instead of trying to accurately mirror the schedule here.
</p>
