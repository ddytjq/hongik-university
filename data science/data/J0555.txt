    <td><!--mstheme--><font face="arial, helvetica, geneva"><h4><!--mstheme--><font color="#000000"><strong><a name="Hours" target="main"><br>
      Hours</a> of Operation:</strong><!--mstheme--></font></h4>
    <p>9 am - 5 pm Monday through Saturday.&nbsp;<br>
    Also open 9-5 on some race Sundays. Evening hours can be arranged.&nbsp;</p>
      <p> The
    Research Center is available for private receptions, meetings, and other
    social events. Contact <a href="mailto:glenda@racingarchives.org" target="main">glenda@racingarchives.org</a>.</p>
    <h4><!--mstheme--><font color="#000000"><strong><a name="Location" target="main">Location</a> of the Center:</strong><!--mstheme--></font></h4>
    <p>The Center is located at 610 S. Decatur Street in
    Watkins Glen, New York, in the heart of the state's Finger Lakes region. The Schuyler
    County Chamber of Commerce has provided <a href="http://www.schuylerny.com/direct.html" target="main">directions
    and information</a> about this region. Or call the Center at (607)535-9044.</p>
    <h4><!--mstheme--><font color="#000000"><strong><a name="Mission" target="main">Mission</a> Statement:</strong><a href="images/sitephotos/98wide.jpg" target="main"><img src="images/sitephotos/98wides.jpg" alt="98wides.jpg (29856 bytes)" align="right" border="0" WIDTH="300" HEIGHT="186"></a><!--mstheme--></font></h4>
    <p>The mission of the International Motor
    Racing Research Center at Watkins Glen is to be the world-class leader in the collection of materials representing the
    documentary heritage of amateur and professional motor racing, highlighting Sports Car,
    Formula 1, NASCAR, and vintage and Historic racing. The Research Center maintains
    archives for the personal papers of drivers, journalists and authors; rare books, serial
    publications, and reference books pertaining to drivers, cars, races and the history of
    motor racing; the historical records and newsletters of clubs and motor racing teams and
    corporations; and historical photographs, movie films, videos, oral histories, and
    memorabilia. Operating according to professional archival standards of preservation and
    bibliographic cataloguing, the Research Center is open to all for primary research,
    offers extensive reference and other public services, and makes the wealth of gathered
    information available worldwide through computer technology.</p>
