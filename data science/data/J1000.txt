<h1>Shannonville Motorsport Park</h1>
<h2>Introduction</h2>
<p>
Shannonville Motorsport Park is a road course complex located
near Belleville, between Kingston and Toronto in Ontario, Canada.
</p>
<p>
Shannonville offers a variety of different road course circuits,
including the 4.03Km Long track, the 2.47 Km Pro track, the
2.23 Km Fabi Circuit, and the 1.80 Km Nelson Circuit.  In addition
to the road course complex, there is a drag strip and a variety of
Karting facilities.
</p>
<p>
On Monday nights, owners of bikes and street cars may take laps for
a modest fee.
</p>
<p>
Shannonville has their own web site at
<a href="http://www.shannonville.com/">http://www.shannonville.com/</a>.
<p>
The Hotel/Motel information was supplied by Mark Holle.
</p>

<h2><a name="GettingThere">Getting to Shannonville Motorsport Park</a></h2>
<p>
Shannonville is 2 hours east of Toronto and 3 1/2 hours west of Montreal,
just off of Highway 401.
</p>
<h3>From Toronto</h3>
<p>
From Highway 401, take exit 556, Shannonville Road.
Take Shannonville Road south 2 miles to Highway 2, turn left onto
Highway 2, and after 3 miles, the track is on the left.
</p>
<h3>From Montreal</h3>
<p>
From Highway 401, take exit 566, Marysville Road/Highway 49.
Go south on Marysville Road for 1/2 mile to Highway 2,
turn right onto Highway 2, and after 2 miles, the track will be on
your right.
</p>
