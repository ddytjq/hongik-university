              <br>
              <a name="3"></a>3) What sort of security features do I need for 
              my equipment to be covered in my car?<b><br>
              </b></font></b></font><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2" color="#FFFFFF">Theft 
              of the insured equipment from any unattended vehicle is only covered 
              if you have taken out the 'in vehicle' extension on the Comprehensive 
              policy (or with Flexicover), but only when access to the vehicle 
              has been effected by forcible and violent means provided that:<br>
              a) </font><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">all 
              doors, windows and other openings are left closed, securely locked 
              and properly fastened;<br>
              b) the security devices installed in the vehicle are in operation;<br>
              c) the Property Insured is stored out of sight in a Locked Luggage 
              Compartment;<br>
              d) in respect of a claim for any one item of Property Insured to 
              the value of &pound;3,000 or more and in respect of a claim for 
              items of Property Insured to a total value of over &pound;10,000, 
              the vehicle shall be fitted with either a factory fitted immobilizer 
              meeting the current requirement of the Motor Industry Repair Association 
              Research Centre (Thatcham) as a Category 2 immobilizer, or Category 
              1 immobilizer and alarm, and if not factory fitted, that has been 
              installed by a member of the Vehicle Security Installation Board, 
              and that evidence of ownership of such immobilizer can be provided 
              in the event of a claim, otherwise the insured value will be limited 
              to a maximum of &pound;3,000 for any one article and up to a maximum 
              of &pound;10,000 for composite items.</font></p>

              1.10 &quot;Locked Luggage Compartment&quot;, in respect of hatchback, 
              estate or four wheeled drive car, trailer, van or minibus; then 
              cover is deemed to apply only where the Insured strictly complies 
              with the foregoing alarm and/or immobilizer requirements. Any equipment 
              not in a luggage compartment as defined must be covered over out 
              of sight. Where there is no single item of Property Insured of &pound;3,000 
              or more, or no composite items to a total value of over &pound;10,000 
              or more, then cover is deemed to apply by the fitting of a mechanical 
              immobilizer (steering lock) meeting the requirements of Thatcham 
              Category 3, and that evidence of ownership of such immobilizer can 
              be provided in the event of a claim.</font></p>

              of the United Kingdom - where it is impossible to comply with the 
              security requirements of the Motor Industry Repair Association Research 
              Centre (thatcham) ie; for loan, hire or borrowed vehicles outside 
              of the United Kingdom ,Theft cover is only provided if the property 
              insured is kept in a locked luggage compartment and the vehicle 
              is fitted with a factory fitted alarm and immobilizer and that evidence 
              can be provided of its existence in the event of a claim. </font></p>
