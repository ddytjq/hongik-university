<FONT COLOR="#FFFFFF" face="sans-serif">Get the orchestral music.  I think his best stuff is the Accord CD (which may be out of print) with Hurqualia, Hymnos and Chukrum.  They're all heavy duty monstrously static and ecstatic pieces.  I also like the orchestral/choral piece  Konx-Om-Pax.  <BR><BR>His chamber music is a bit hard for me to get into as he often relies on immense numbers of things just barely fluctuating to get his movement.  <BR><BR>Also, I haven't been getting that much into Scelsi recently, (probably overkill) but man oh man am I into the new Xenakis Complete Orchestra Music by the Luxembourg Philharmonic on Timpani.  I had to buy it through the web at <!-- BBCode auto-link start --><a href="http://www.jpc.de" target="_blank">www.jpc.de</a><!-- BBCode auto-link end --> but Jonichaaes (I'll never learn to play it) is the most incredible orchestra piece I've heard in a long long time.
