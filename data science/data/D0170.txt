This 4-space object has 24 vertices and 96 edges.
Its 4-dimensional volume is bounded by 24 3-space octahedra.
I learned how to construct it by reading
<a href="http://www.uccs.edu/~eswab/">Eric Swab's</a> stuff.
Follow the links on Eric's <a href="http://www.uccs.edu/~eswab/hyprspac.htm">hyperspace page</a>
for a good explanation of this object.
