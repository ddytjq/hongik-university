		<TD CLASS=body WIDTH=75%><B><A NAME="secure">Is it secure&#063;</A></B>

		<P>

		 All possible measures to secure your information are in place. The key to security is your user name and

		 password. It is essential these are not disclosed to anyone. If at any time you suspect that these have

		 been compromised then you should change them immediately. If you have any difficulty with this then

		 contact the Internet Banking help desk who can prevent further access to your accounts and explain the

		 process of re-activating the system.

		 <BR>

		 Information passed between your computer and the Bank is encrypted and in the unlikely event of any

		 third party seeing a message it is unreadable. Although many other security measures are present in the

		 system for your protection the Bank will not reveal any further details regarding these.

		</TD>
