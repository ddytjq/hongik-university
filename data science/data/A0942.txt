			      <p class="contentsubhead"><a name="Q1"> How Much Can I Borrow?</a></p>
			      <p> With a Personal Loan, you can apply to borrow from &pound;500 up to
			        &pound;25,000. Once you have decided how much you would like to borrow,
			        we will be able to help you choose the term to suit you best. Terms range
			        from 6 months up to 84 months.</p>
			      <p align="right"><a href="#top"><img src="../g_images/but_top.gif" alt="Back to Top" border="0" /></a></p>
			      <p class="contentsubhead"><a name="Q2">How Long Can I Have to Repay the Personal Loan?</a></p>
			      <p> As part of our flexible approach to banking, we can give you a choice
			        as to how quickly you pay back the loan, making sure it is over a period
			        you feel comfortable with. It can vary from a minimum of 6 months up to
			        84 months.</p>
			      <p align="right"><a href="#top"><img src="../g_images/but_top.gif" alt="Back to Top" border="0" /></a></p>
			      <p class="contentsubhead"><a name="Q4">Is my online application safe?</a></p>
			      <p>When you send us an online application, it is sent over a secure connection.
			        This means your personal information is encrypted to help prevent it being
			        accessed by anyone except us. This is far more secure than many things
			        we do every day and take for granted, such as paying for goods and services
			        over the phone with credit cards.</p>
			      <p align="right"><a href="#top"><img src="../g_images/but_top.gif" alt="Back to Top" border="0" /></a></p>
			      <p class="contentsubhead"><a name="Q5">What happens to my online application when I submit it?</a></p>
			      <p>Once you submit an online application form, we will then process it and
			        reply to you with a decision by telephone. If successful, we will send
			        you the relevant documents which you then sign and return along with any
			        supporting documentation (eg wage slips). Once we have received this,
			        the money is released into your account. As the Consumer Credit Act requires
			        that you sign a copy of your loan agreement, we send out the documentation
			        rather than issue it online.</p>
			      <p align="right"><a href="#top"><img src="../g_images/but_top.gif" alt="Back to Top" border="0" /></a></p>
			      <p class="contentsubhead"><a name="Q6">How quickly can I find out if my online application has
			      been successful?</a></p>
			      <p>We can usually let you know within one working day.</p>
