					<TD><ul><li>We calculate interest on a daily basis on the balance
outstanding on your account.
<li>You pay the interest as part or all of your monthly
mortgage payment, depending upon whether you
have a repayment or an interest only mortgage.
<li>Interest accrued during any month will be added to
the outstanding balance on your account at the end
of that month.
<li>We calculate the amount of your monthly payment
assuming that it is paid in full during the month, so
that any interest accruing during that month will be
paid as part of that months' payment and will not
be added to the balance outstanding on your loan.
<li>We usually send a cheque for the loan to your solicitor
on day one. On day four we start to charge interest.
<li>If we send the loan to your solicitor by telegraphic transfer
on day one we will start to charge interest on day one.
<li>Interest will be payable on all expenses and insurance
premiums as soon as they are added to the
balance outstanding on your account. You can avoid
such interest accruing by paying any such expenses
or insurance premiums as they arise.
