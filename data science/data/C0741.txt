				<td class=body><B>Scottish Friendly is proud to support The Scottish Book Trust</B>
				<P>
<b>Curl up with a book</b>
<P>
Scottish Friendly has a longstanding commitment to supporting reading and literacy in communities throughout Scotland. For the past three years it has sponsored Scottish Book Trust, the independent organisation that promotes books and reading in Scotland with the partnership winning awards from Arts and Business Scotland.
<P>
For the last two years we have sponsored their touring programme - Words on Wheels - in which authors and illustrators are taken in their specially converted bright yellow van into primary schools to meet the children while Scottish Book Trust staff stage a Book Bonanza, introducing children to all kinds of different books. It was such a success that we are sponsoring it again for another year.
