		  <td class="content"> 
            <p class="contentsubhead">Longer Term</p>
			<p class="contenttextbold"> Longer term savings.</p>
            <p>If you don't need 
              to get at your savings straightaway, you may want to consider our 
              Notice Account options and enjoy the benefits of our attractive 
              interest rates.With this kind of account you can leave your savings 
              to grow, adding to them whenever you want. However, you have the 
              reassurance that if you need money unexpectedly, you can access 
              your savings whenever you want. All you will lose is the equivalent 
              of interest on the notice period of your account. </p>
            <p>Depending on how 
              comfortable you feel about saving over longer periods of time, you 
              can choose from one-month, three-month and six-month notice periods. 
              Simply work out how much you can afford to save, and how quickly 
              you might need it. Remember that the less access you have to the 
              money in your account, the higher the rate we can offer you - and 
              the more interest it will earn. </p>
            <p>Like all our savings accounts, the interest 
              is tiered - so when the balance reaches the next tier, the new rate 
              covers the total amount. If you prefer, your interest can be paid 
              directly into another Yorkshire Bank account, giving you a regular 
              income without touching your initial capital. </p>

            <ul>
              <li>
            <p class="contenttextbold">Three Month Premium </p>
            <p>To open a Three Month Premium Account, all 
              you need is an <strong>initial investment of £500</strong>, or, even easier, 
              <strong>a monthly standing order of £50</strong>. Your interest is calculated 
              twice a year, so you will earn interest on your interest as well 
              as your original amount. </p>
            <p>Should you need to access your money in a hurry, 
              you can make one penalty-free instant access withdrawal of up to 
              £500 a month -and any additional withdrawals only require three 
              months' notice. And don't worry, if you require your money urgently, 
              you may withdraw it without notice, only losing the equivalent of 
              three months' interest.</p>
            <p>You may need 
                  to access your money sooner but still want the benefits of a 
                  longer term savings account. Our One Month Premium account has 
                  all the benefits of our Three Month account except a monthly 
                  income option. So while you still benefit from earning interest 
                  you also have access sooner. </p>
            <li><p class="contenttextbold">Premium Plus Account</p>
            <p>If you have 
                  a larger sum to put away, a Premium Plus Account may be right 
                  for you. With a <strong>minimum investment of £5,000</strong>, you can 
                  commit to a <strong>six-month notice</strong> period and earn an attractive 
                  rate of interest which is paid annually. </p>
            <p>Six months 
                  can be a long time - so we've included the option of one free 
                  instant access withdrawal a year, in case you need cash unexpectedly. 
                  Should you need it, you can withdraw up to a quarter of your 
                  balance, excluding any accrued interest. </p>
            <p>Additional withdrawals require six months' 
              notice, and as with all our notice accounts, you always have the 
              option to withdraw your money without notice.All you will lose is 
              the equivalent of six months' interest.</p>
            <li><p class="contenttextbold">Monthly Income Interest Option.</p>
            <p>For the Three Month and Premium Plus Account 
              there is an interest option worth considering. If you would like 
              some extra cash on hand every month, or require your savings to 
              provide additional income, you can choose our monthly income option, 
              where interest is paid into your account on a monthly basis. The 
              interest you have earned can be transferred directly into your regular 
              bank account, on the 28th day of each month for the Three Month 
              and the 4th of each month on the Premium Plus Account.Without touching 
              your original capital you can benefit from your savings. </p>
             </ul>
              
            <p class="contenttextbold">Guaranteed interest.</p>
              <p>If you feel you don't need access to your 
                money - and are able to put your money away for longer - our Term 
                Deposits offer great rewards. Because the interest you earn is 
                fixed for the full period, you know exactly how much you'll get 
                at the end of the term - which means that you can plan ahead with 
                complete confidence. </p>
              <p>There's more than just an attractive rate 
                to discover with a Yorkshire Bank Term Deposit. To start, you 
                have a range of term lengths between 3, 6, 12 and 18 months to 
                choose from, so you decide how long you want to save for. Then 
                there's the promise that the rate of interest you're offered when 
                you open the account, will be the rate you get for the full term 
                your money is deposited. So, no matter what happens to interest 
                rates, you'll know exactly how much you will receive and when. 
              </p>
              <p>What's more, you can open a Term Deposit 
                with anything from £2,000 to a maximum of £250,000. During the 
                Fixed Term you may only make one withdrawal which must be for 
                the whole balance in your account. Such withdrawals will be subject 
                to a charge and the stated return will not be achieved. </p>
              <p>If you would like to take advantage of everything 
                the account has to offer, simply ask at any branch for full details, 
                or call free anytime from 8am to 8pm Monday to Friday on 0800-0281806. 
                But don 't leave it too long. The sooner you start seeing guaranteed 
                returns on your savings, the better. </p>
