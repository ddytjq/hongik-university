<P>
C++Builder4 Unleashed is written by Kent Reisdorph, Charlie Calvert, Bruneau Babet, and a host of
other well known programmers. BCB4 unleashed is probably the most advanced C++Builder book on the
market. It covers topics that most other books tend to shy away from, such as threads, NT service
programs, the TRichEdit control, COM, how to automate Word and Excel, C++ templates, and internet
programming. The resumes of the people who produced BCB4 unleashed are impressive. The contributors
to BCB4 Unleashed include 3 TeamB members, 5 present or past employees of Borland, 4 authors of
previous Delphi or C++Builder books, and 5 programmers who present seminars at the annual Borland
Conference.
</P>
<P>
BCB4 Unleashed is without a doubt, an advanced level book. Beginning C++Builder programmers
may want to read the Teach Yourself books from Kent Reisdorph before reading BCB4 Unleashed.
</P>
