<P> <B>Our basic Buy to Let lending criteria</B> 
<P> 
<UL>
  <LI>Our Buy to Let mortgages are for private individuals only. Up to four people, 
    can make joint applications. 
  <LI>They are available to both new and existing Northern Rock customers. 
  <LI>All applicants must be at least 21 years old. 
  <LI>Mortgages are available up to a maximum of 75% of the property's value. 
  <LI>The minimum value of the property you're buying must be &#163;40,000 and 
    the maximum loan per property &#163;250,000. We'll lend on up to four properties 
    (and up to a total value of &#163;1million). Please talk to our Commercial 
    Finance Department if you want to buy more than four properties or purchase 
    a property worth over &#163;250,000. 
  <LI>As a borrower intending to rent out your property, you must have an Assured 
    Shorthold Tenancy Agreement for each property. 
  <LI>You should also consider the office of Fair Trading Guidance on 
    unfair terms in Assured Shorthold Tenancy Agreements before you finalise your 
    agreement. 
</UL>
<P> <B>Your income and the rent you charge</B> 
<P> The rental income from the property must be equal to or greater than your 
  monthly mortgage repayment*. Please note that whichever mortgage option you 
  choose, we'll make this calculation based on a capital and interest basis over 
  a 25 year term at a nominal interest rate of 8.00%. You'll also need to have 
  a minimum annual income of &#163;25,000 (excluding your rental income).
