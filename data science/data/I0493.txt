</B></TD></TR><TR><TD ALIGN="CENTER" class="entry">Click for bigger picture</TD></TR></TABLE></P></TD><TD></TD></TR></TABLE><p><P>Away fans are given the choice of standing on the Christie Avenue Stand or sitting in the Playing
Fields Stand. Both stands are covered and have catering facilities, but only the Playing Fields Stand has a bar.
</P><P>Fans are not normally segregated and there is no bookmakers on site and possibly not one within
walking distance.
</P></P><p>Click <a class="entry" href="http://uk.multimap.com/p/browse.cgi?pc=LA45TJ&Title=Christie+Park+(Morecambe+AFC)&scale=25000&cat=loc" target="_new">here</a> to see a map of the surrounding area</P></td></tr></table><BR><table class="entry" cellspacing=0 width=100%><tr><td><H3 class="entry">Pubs</H3><p><P>Visiting supporters have plenty of options for a pre-match beer. Both JB's Bar and Morecambe Supporters club which are at the ground welcome visiting fans. A short walk away from Christie Park there are the York Hotel and the Shrimp. The York is popular with both home and away fans while the Shrimp is a standard chain eating house.
</P><P>Morecambe town centre is about  1/2 hours walk away. The Chieftain on Peddar Street in the town centre has become popular for a pre match pint recently. The Chieftain is a traditional pub with a good selection of real ales.
</P></p><TABLE><TR></TR></TABLE><H5 class="entry"> JB's Bar
</H5><P ALIGN=LEFT></P><TABLE><TR><TD><P><TABLE><TR><TD ALIGN="CENTER"><A HREF="/cgi-bin/ag.pl?club=disppic&pfname=/images/Morecambe-JBs-Full.jpg" TARGET="_new"><IMG SRC="/images/Morecambe-JBs-Thumb.jpg" ALT="Outside JBs

the ground) they openly welcome away fans and their
families, with Sky Sports, pool, good beer and a good atmosphere its the best bet for a drink
before the game.Thankfully inside is better than the picture above of the
outside.
</P></p><H5 class="entry">Morecambe Supporters Club
</H5><P ALIGN=LEFT></P><TABLE><TR><TD><P><TABLE><TR><TD ALIGN="CENTER"><A HREF="/cgi-bin/ag.pl?club=disppic&pfname=/images/Morecambe-SC-Full.jpg" TARGET="_new"><IMG SRC="/images/Morecambe-SC-Thumb.jpg" ALT="Morecambe AFC SC

Club welcomes away fans in.
</P></p><H5 class="entry"> The York Hotel
</H5><P ALIGN=LEFT></P><TABLE><TR><TD><P><TABLE><TR><TD ALIGN="CENTER"><A HREF="/cgi-bin/ag.pl?club=disppic&pfname=/images/Morecambe-York-Full.jpg" TARGET="_new"><IMG SRC="/images/Morecambe-York-Thumb.jpg" ALT="The York Hotel
" WIDTH="100" HEIGHT="75" BORDER="0" ALIGN="TEXTTOP"></A></TD></TR><TR><TD ALIGN="CENTER"><B>The York Hotel
<B></TD></TR><TR><TD ALIGN="CENTER">Click for bigger picture</TD></TR></TABLE></P></TD></TR></TABLE><p>The York Hotel is located a 10 minutes walk from the ground on Lancaster Rd (towards Morecambe)
and is frequented by a lot of Morecambe Supporters. Away fans are usually welcomed and mix happily with the home supporters. The York is known for good beer and atmosphere and also has cheap accommodation available.
</P></p><H5 class="entry">The Shrimp
</H5><P ALIGN=LEFT></P><TABLE><TR><TD><P><TABLE><TR><TD ALIGN="CENTER"><A HREF="/cgi-bin/ag.pl?club=disppic&pfname=/images/Morecambe-Shrimp-Full.jpg" TARGET="_new"><IMG SRC="/images/Morecambe-Shrimp-Thumb.jpg" ALT="The Shrimp
" WIDTH="100" HEIGHT="75" BORDER="0" ALIGN="TEXTTOP"></A></TD></TR><TR><TD ALIGN="CENTER"><B>The Shrimp
<B></TD></TR><TR><TD ALIGN="CENTER">Click for bigger picture</TD></TR></TABLE></P></TD></TR></TABLE><p>A Brewers Fayre eating house located on roundabout at the start of Lancaster Rd it is 5 minutes walk to the ground. The Shrimp has the standard decor/Fayre of a chain pub but the food and service is highly rated.
</P></p></td></tr><table><BR><table class="entry" cellspacing=0 width=100%><tr><td><H3 class="entry">Food</H3><p><P>The Shrimp pub above does great food, but you also have Atkinsons Chippie
on Lancaster Rd right outside the ground, and a couple minute walk away
from the ground (towards Morecambe) is 'Harrys'
Chippie. A little further along Lancaster Rd is The Rice Bowl Chinese
take-way. There are also mobile catering vans inside and outside the ground.
</P><P>A drive-through McDonalds is just over a mile away on Morecambe Rd as you
approach the ground from Lancaster.
</P></p></td></tr><table><BR><table class="entry" cellspacing=0 width=100%><tr><td><H3 class="entry">Cash Machines</H3><p><P>No cash machines are located close to the ground, the nearest are in
Morecambe centre and in the nearby village Torrisholme.
</P></p><p>Click <a class="entry" href="http://link.infonow.net/bin/findNow?CLIENT_ID=LINK_ATM" target="_new">here</A> to go to <a class="entry" HREF="http://www.link.co.uk"  TARGET="_NEW">LINKs</a> locate a cash machine page. The local postcode is LA4 5TJ</P></td></tr><table><BR><table class="entry" cellspacing=0 width=100%><tr><td><H3 class="entry">Bookmakers</H3><p>Online betting is always available through <a class="entry" 
href="https://www.ladbrokes.co.uk/bet?AFF_ID=121"  TARGET="_NEW">Ladbrokes</a> by
clicking <a class="entry" href="https://www.ladbrokes.co.uk/bet?AFF_ID=121"  TARGET="_NEW">here</a>.</P></td></tr><table><BR><table class="entry" cellspacing=0 width=100%><tr><td><H3 class="entry">Directions</H3><p>Leave the M6 at Junction 34 and head towards Lancaster A683. Follow signs for Morecambe & Heysham
and once in the one way system stay in the right hand lane. On the bridge move to the left hand lane
and follow for 3 miles over 2 roundabouts, at the 3rd head over a mini-roundabout towards the
town centre (signposted B5321 Town Centre & Morecambe FC). The ground is
located on the left in about 3/4 of a mile.
</P></p></td></tr><table><BR><table class="entry" cellspacing=0 width=100%><tr><td><H3 class="entry">Parking</H3><p>Limited parking is available at the ground, however street parking is normally available.
</P></p></td></tr><table><BR><table class="entry" cellspacing=0 width=100%><tr><td><H3 class="entry">Train</H3><p>Morecambe station is located on the just off the sea front and is a long
walk from the ground, a taxi should cost around £2.00. Closer to the
ground is Bare Lane station, from the station head down South Road
along side the railway way track, just over a mile later, when you reach
Lancaster Rd (at the York Hotel) turn left towards the ground
which is one the right in just less than a mile.
</P></p><p>The nearest station is Bare Lane
. Click <a class="entry" href="http://212.87.65.227/bin/newquery.exe/en" target="_new">here</a> to check train times</p></td></tr><table><BR><table class="entry" cellspacing=0 width=100%><tr><td><H3 class="entry">Accommodation</H3><p>Whilst Morecambe has many hotels and B&Bs to choose from <a class="entry" 
href="http://www.lothersdale.com">The Lothersdale Hotel</a> is highly recommended and is often
used by not only away fans, but away teams as well. Very friendly staff, great service and
excellent value for money. 
</P><P>Other accommodation can be found by contacting Morecambe Tourist Information Centre on 01524 582808
</P></p><p>Click 
<a class="entry" href="http://www.smoothhound.co.uk/morecamb.html"  TARGET="_NEW">
here</a> to see the local accomodation available from 
<a class="entry" href="http://www.smoothhound.co.uk"  TARGET="_NEW">  Smoothhound</a>.</p></td></tr><table><BR><table class="entry" cellspacing=0 width=100%><tr><td><H3 class="entry">Local Fans</H3><p>The local fans are a friendly bunch and trouble is uncommon.
</P></p></td></tr><table><BR><table class="entry" cellspacing=0 width=100%><tr><td><H3 class="entry">Local Police And Stewards</H3><p><P>Police presence is normally low and whilst stewards are laid back they swiftly eject abusive fans.
</P></p></td></tr><table><BR><table class="entry" cellspacing=0 width=100%><tr><td><H3 class="entry">After The Game</H3><p>Lancaster is a good night out, it can get very busy especially during University term time. 
Lots of nationally known pubs are represented (Walkabout, Blob Shop, Firkin, Varsity, Wetherspoons etc.)
Very little trouble in Lancaster and away colours should not be a problem (other than for places with dress codes).
</P><P>Morecambe town centre and sea front has a huge amount of choice , many offering drinks promotions and most pubs on
the promenade have late licences. Check Out Oscars Bar on Victoria Street and the attached Crystal Ts nightclub.
Trouble is rare out of (holiday) season, but watch out for trouble during the holidays as as well as the tourists other traveling away fans sometimes visit Morcambe's town centre after other Lancashire & Cumbrian games.
</P></p></td></tr><table><BR><table class="entry" cellspacing=0 width=100%><tr><td><H3 class="entry">Local Attractions</H3><p>Check out <a class="entry" href="http://www.morecambe.co.uk">http://www.morecambe.co.uk</a> for a huge range of tourism ideas.
</P></p><P>You can also use the following to search for events in the area</p><BR><CENTER>
