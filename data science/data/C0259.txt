      <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2"><b>Please ensure you read 
        the following important notes before you send the registration form.</b></font></p>
<p><font size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">To register as a CETA member 
  there are certain rules that everyone must follow to make the system work and 
  also to protect the good name that CETA members have in the market place. If 
  any member is found to not be following the rules set out, they will be unable 
  to place any business with CETA in the future.</font></p>
<ul>
  <li><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">You must read and follow 
    the ABI on selling general insurance.</font> 
  <li><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">Your client must be given 
    a copy of the quotation and summary of cover quote breakdown.</font> 
  <li><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">Your client must be told 
    to read through the proposal form and to ask them that they fully understand 
    what is being covered.</font> 
  <li><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">If your client is paying 
    by payment plan, you must leave the client with their copy.</font> 
  <li><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">All monies are payable 
    to CETA for all products sold through the CETA membership.</font> 
  <li><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">All cheques, postal orders 
    and money orders must be made payable to CETA Ltd.</font> 
  <li><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">Payments must accompany 
    proposal forms sent to CETA.</font> 
  <li><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">You cannot issue, bind 
    or confirm cover on behalf of CETA.</font> 
  <li><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">You must send completed 
    documents to CETA at the earliest time possible.</font> 
  <li><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">If the policy is cancelled 
    for whatever reason, you are responsible to refund the proportionate amount 
    of the commission received.</font> 
  <li><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">Your membership of CETA 
    is not assignable, unless authorised by CETA.</font> 
  <li><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">CETA undertakes not to 
    use any information acquired in the course of this agreement to solicit your 
    clients directly for any business.</font> 
  <li><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">CETA undertakes to pay 
    all commissions on time and to notify you of any changes to the commission 
    structure at the earliest possible time.</font></li>
</ul>
