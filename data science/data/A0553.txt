<h1>Birmingham Midshires in the Community</h1>

<img src="../../stickpic/news2.gif" width=150 height=150 align="right">

<p><b>Birmingham Midshires believes that healthy communities means healthy business and the company is committed to putting something back into the communities in which our staff and customers live and work.</b></p>

<p>Through a combination of business partnerships, charitable giving and 'hands on' grass roots support Birmingham Midshires contributes to the life and soul of the country - investing in the community so the community can invest in us.</p>

<p>This programme is further enhanced by branch staff at local level who are encouraged to underpin this as active members of the local community.</p>
