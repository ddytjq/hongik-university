		<P>
		<span class="body">Client Banking Cash management software is called BOSLink. This is a secure Windows&#174; based, fully automated system.</span>
<p>
<span class="body">BOSLink provides the facility for Client Banking customers to manage a large number of individual interest-bearing accounts, offering the ability to open and close accounts and to transfer funds between these accounts.</span>
<p>
<span class="body">BOSLink also allows customers to track the status of accounts. The information available includes balances, transactional history, tax information and daily interest accruals.</span>
<p>
<span class="body">In addition, BOSLink can be integrated directly with other COM compliant software to allow 'straight-through' processing.</span>
<p>
<span class="body">In order to run BOSLink effectively, we recommend the following system requirements:</span>
