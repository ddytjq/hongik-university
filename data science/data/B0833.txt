      <p><font face="Arial" size="2">As in previous years during November the

      shop kindly donated one of their teddies. A customer drew the winner’s
      names out of a hat. The mole’s name was Digger and was won by Charlie
      Barnes and the dog’s name was Sluppy and was won by Mr David Edinburgh.</font></p>

      <p class="MsoNormal"><font face="Arial" size="2">The competition has
      raised £35.00 and will be sent to ‘BBC Children in Need’, along with

      <p class="MsoNormal"><font face="Arial" size="2">Clay Cross Building
      society has recently achieved the <a href="http://www.iipuk.co.uk/" target="_blank">‘Investors
      in People’</a> award.<o:p>
      </o:p>
      </font></p>
      <p class="MsoNormal"><font face="Arial" size="2">In order to get the award
      the employers had to prove their abilities to look after their staff and
      provide for their training and for certain needs.<o:p>
      </o:p>
      </font></p>
      <p class="MsoNormal"><font face="Arial" size="2">Developed in 1990 by the
      National Training Task Force in partnership with leading national
      businesses, then supported by the employment department it proves a great
      standard of achievement.&nbsp;</font></p>
