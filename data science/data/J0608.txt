<h2>
<font face="Arial,Helvetica">A Dictionary of Road Rally Terminology</font></h2></center>

<center>
<h3>
<font face="Arial,Helvetica">Introduction</font></h3></center>
<font face="Arial,Helvetica">This is a small glossary of terminology associated
with <a href="../rallying_uk_road.html">road rallying</a> and other club
activities. The original text was written by David Crooke and Andrew Cooper
of <a href="http://www.ed.ac.uk/~eumsc/">Edinburgh University Motor Sport
Club</a> and has been converted to HTML format by <i>John Upham</i> of
<a href="../hartmc/index.html">Hart
Motor Club</a>. There are some definitions including references to <a href="http://www.windsorcarclub.co.uk/">Windsor
Car Club</a> and Hart Motor Club phenomena for which I can only apologise!</font>
<p><font face="Arial,Helvetica">Last update: Sunday, December 30<sup>th</sup>
2001</font>

<font face="Arial,Helvetica">Dictionary</font></h3></center>

<ul>
<li>
<b><font face="Arial,Helvetica">A</font></b></li>

<ul>
<li>
<font face="Arial,Helvetica"><b>AA </b><i>abbrev.</i> Automobile Association

<li>
<font face="Arial,Helvetica"><b>all-nighter</b><i> n.</i> A full length

</ul>

<li>

<font face="Arial,Helvetica"><b>Bowring</b><i> n.</i> purveryors of insurance</font></li>
