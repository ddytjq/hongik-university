<h1>Messier Object Astronomy Pictures of the Day</h1>
</center>

Since June 16, 1995, 
<a href=""http://antwrp.gsfc.nasa.gov/htmltest/rjn.html>
Robert Nemiroff</a>,
<a href="http://antwrp.gsfc.nasa.gov/htmltest/jbonnell/www/bonnell.html">
Jerry Bonnell</a> and <a href="mailto:jpn@grossc.gsfc.nasa.gov">Jay Norris</a>
provide <a href="http://antwrp.gsfc.nasa.gov/apod/">Astronomy Picture
of the Day (APOD)</a>, an online presentation of a selected astronomy
image, updated daily. Among these images are of course many Messier object
images - also see their
<a href="http://antwrp.gsfc.nasa.gov/apod/messier.html">pictorial Messier 
index</a> and their search engine created
<a href="http://antwrp.gsfc.nasa.gov/apod/lib/messier.html">Messier list</a>.
Below we list the result of an overall scan of the APOD images.
<p>
<a href="mailto:spider@seds.org">Please email me any errors and omissions</a> ..
