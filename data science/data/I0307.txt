          <h2>Answered questions</h2>
        </CENTER>
        <p>Q: (Ciprian Turturean) I need the result of the mach Hamburg S.V.-Dinamo 
          Bucuresti (Cup Winners' Cup 1983-1984) with the name of players then 
          scored and the minutes of goals.</p>
        <p>A:( Bernd Timmermann)<br>
          EC I:<br>
          Dinamo Bucuresti - Hamburger SV 3-0 (1-0) 19.10.83<br>
          1-0 Augustin (28')<br>
          2-0 Multescu (62')<br>
          3-0 Oraz (73') </p>
        <p>Hamburger SV - Dinamo Bucuresti 3-2 (1-0) 02.11.83<br>
          1-0 Jakobs (45') <br>
          2-0 Jakobs (54')<br>
          3-0 von Heesen (63')<br>
          3-1 Talna (82')<br>
          3-2 Multescu (88')</p>
        <p>Q: (P. Degyse) Who scored in the 87-88 game between Bor. Dortmund and 
          Club Bruges where the final score was 5_0; especially the last two goals 
          in the extra time!</p>
        <p>A: (Guy De Dekker)<br>
          9. Ceulemans Jan 1-0<br>
          48. Van der Elst Leo 2-0<br>
          82. Van der Elst Leo (pen) 3-0<br>
          99. Van der Elst Franky 4-0<br>
          106. Van der Elst Leo (pen) 5-0 </p>
        <p>Q: (Belli Riccardo)<br>
          a. 1956-58 Fairs Cup - Why Cologne XI withdraw ?<br>
          b. 1968-69 European Cups - Why all teams of East Europe withdraw ? Why 
          team of East Europe had only partecipated in Fairs Cup ? <br>
          c. 1971-72 Why Hallescher FC Chemie withdraw after first match with 
          PSV ? </p>
        <p>A: (John Beuker and Guy De Dekker)<br>
          a. They couldn't get a representative team together.<br>
          b. It was due to the Soviet intervention in Czechoslovakia.<br>
          c. There was a hotel fire just before the second leg. One Halle player 
          was killed, several injured.</p>
        <p>Q: ( G.J.Kuiper) Where was the Final of the Cup Winners Cup in the 
          season 92/93? </p>
        <p>A: The Final of the Cup Winners Cup was played on 12th May, at Wembley 
          Stadium (London, att 37393).</p>
        <p>Q: (Kirsten) HAS ANY TEAM WON THE EUROPEAN CUP WITHOUT WINNING A GAME. 
          I.E. WINNING ON AWAY GOALS OR PENALTIES?</p>
        <p>A: No, something like that haven't happened, but the closest to that, 
          by number of lost points, were Steaua (85/86, EC1, 4w,3d,2l), PSV (87/88, 
          EC1, 3w,5d,1l), Manchester United (1998/99, EC1, 6w,7d,0l), FC Barcelona 
          (81/82, EC2, 5w,1d,3l), Eintracht Frankfurt (79/80, EC3, 6w,1d,l5) and 
          Real Madrid (84/85 and 85/86, EC3, 6w,1d,l5)</p>
        <p>Q: (Iris) What club win most times the uefa cup?</p>
        <p>A: There are two clubs who won Uefa Cup three times: Internazionale 
          and Juventus.</p>
        <p>Q:(Merijntje v. Acht) Who scored the 5-3 in the EuroCup for Champions 
          in 1962. I know the two teams who competed (Benfica-Real Madrid). Could 
          you tell me who made the 5-3 (the fifth or third goal) and how did this 
          goal take place (what happend)?????</p>
        <p>A: (Bernd Timmerman)<br>
          A hard shot after a free kick from Coluna.</p>
        <p>Q: (Cambré Marc) In the season 1964/'65 Liverpool (GB) played against 
          Anderlecht (B) in the second round of the Champions' cup. What was the 
          date of this matches and who scored the goals.</p>
        <p>A: (Bernd Timmerman) 64/11/25 and 64/12/16<br>
          Ian St. John, Roger Hunt, Yeates and Roger Hunt.</p>
        <p>Q: (Turab.Hussain and Agustin Jimenez) Is it possible to get the listing 
          of players who took place in the European Cup Finals over time?</p>
        <p>A:Yes, it is,<a href="http://www.zfe1.demon.co.uk/ecuparch/"> James 
          Ross's archive</a>.</p>
        <p>Q: (Gyuri Vergouw) FC Cologne - FC Liverpool 1964-1965 0-0 0-0 and 
          2-2. How did the lotery take place???</p>
        <p>A: (Bernd Timmerman) They used a wooden chip, one side red (Liverpool), 
          one side white (Cologne). After the first throw the chip landed on the 
          muddy grass and stuck vertical. The second try Liverpool won. Besides 
          it was a great third match with a referee, who made a mistake by not 
          countingthe 3-2 for Cologne and with Cologne getting their two goals 
          against them during injury time for Weber, who was off the field for 
          some twenty minutes. After the match they noticed, that he has played 
          the second half and the extra time with an almost broken leg (he had 
          a fracture). </p>
        <p>Q: (Daniel Lister) Please could you tell me the cities which have had 
          two clubs reach the finals or semi-finals of the European Cup (Champions 
          League as was).</p>
        <p>A: (Neil Macaskill) There are three cities with two clubs participated 
          Champions' Cup or Champions League finals: Belgrade (FK Crvena zvezda 
          and FK Partizan), Madrid (Real and Atletico) and Milan (AC and Internazionale). 
          Additionally, Glasgow has one finalist and one semifinalist (Celtic 
          FC and Rangers FC) and Dundee has two semifinalists (Dundee FC and Dundee 
          United). Milan is the only city to have two European Champion clubs.</p>
        <P>Q: (Nenad Poljak) What club have lost the most finals?</P>
        <P>A: Juventus FC have lost 8 finals (one of those in Super Cup).</P>
        <P>Q: (Yiannos Hadjidemetriou) The scorers of Juventus and the scorer 
          of AEK in the match between the two teams in the UEFA cup semi-final 
          of 1976/77 season.</P>
        <P>A: Juventus Turin - AEK Athens 4:1 (1:1) <br>
          (Cuccureddu 18, Bettega 59, 83, Causio 67 - Papadopoulos 3)</P>
        <p>Q: (P. Kuystermans) Which player passed the ball to arnold Muhren who 
          then passed the ball to Marco van Basten to score a goal in the final 
          of the european Championship cup? </p>
        <P>A: (Ronald Nieuwenhuizen) That was Adri van Tiggelen (then playing 
          for Anderlecht), who intercepted a Russian pass and gave the ball to 
          Muhren.</P>
        <P>Q: (Steve Murphy) The FC Zimbru Chisniau team (and substitutes) that 
          appeared at White Hart Lane against Tottenham Hotspurs in last season's 
          UEFA Cup.</P>
        <P>A: (George Chilvers) Zimbru: Romanenco, Telesnenco, Catinsus, Ghilazev, 
          Kulyk, Oprea, Miterev (Gusila 83), Epureanu, Dodul (Fistican 46), Borets, 
          Tropanet (Robu 46).</P>
        <P>Q: (George Chilvers) Do you know what colour shirts were worn by Eintracht 
          Frankfurt in the 1960 European Cup final?</P>
        <P>A: (Gonzalo San Martín) Red, with white sleeves.</P>
