          <td valign="top" width="100%" colspan="3"> 
            <p><b><span class="engfanz">Sven-Goran Eriksson said:</span></b> <span class="engfanz">&quot;We 
              played 10 games last year but because we were in the middle of World 
              Cup qualification there was never time to try something different.<br>
              <br>
              &quot;We did not want to go into the World Cup with one system. 
              It is not the right way. We did much better today than we did in 
              August against the Dutch.<br>
              <br>
              &quot;It was always in mind to play a system using three attackers 
              and three central midfielders.&quot;<br>
              <br>
              &quot;It was a good football game. Holland had more possession but 
              we created more. It was a fair result but with a bit of luck we 
              could have won the game. I was more satisfied than I was in August.<br>
              <br>
              &quot;Vassell was excellent for his first game in the senior team. 
              He did exactly what we asked of him and was not nervous at all.<br>
              <br>
              &quot;Michael Ricketts and Wayne Bridge were both very good. Bridge 
              had a toe problem and had to go off and Ricketts did a difficult 
              job well. He was on his own at times but came out with credit.<br>
              <br>
              &quot;I like to see young players coming in against a very good 
              team. You always have your ideas about the squad but you can always 
              change those ideas. We still have a lot of time before the World 
              Cup.&quot;</span></p>
            <p><span class="engfanz">Aston Villa's <b>Darius Vassell </b>who marked 
              his England debut with a stunning goal said: &quot;It's been a really 
              big week for me. I don't know how to describe it - I'm over the 
              moon. I just hope I can go back to my club and keep my feet on the 
              ground.<br>
              <br>
              &quot;There was a man in front of me, and I just gambled. It missed 
              his head and fortunately it went in.<br>
              <br>
              &quot;All through the build-up you get nerves, but the lads were 
              good to me. When you get the opportunity you just try to do your 
              best - and luckily it worked out for me.<br>
              <br>
              &quot;I was absolutely knackered by 60 minutes, but it's all part 
              of my progression.<br>
              <br>
              &quot;I'd say we had more chances than them, and we could have won 
              the game.&quot;</span></p>
