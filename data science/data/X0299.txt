<P><FONT FACE="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" SIZE="2">
<B><FONT SIZE="3">About PGA Today:</FONT></B></FONT>

<P><FONT FACE="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" SIZE="2">"The PGA Today" is a television series spanning The PGA of America's programs, personalities and events. The series debuted in 2000 and appears monthly on The Golf Channel.</FONT>

<P><FONT FACE="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" SIZE="2">Brian Hammons and Kelly Tilghman host "The PGA Today," which is co-produced by The Golf Channel and The PGA of America. The programs will originate from PGA Village in Port St. Lucie, Fla., along with such venues as the PGA Merchandise Show, Valhalla Golf Club in Louisville, Ky., and other championship sites. </FONT><P><FONT FACE="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" SIZE="2">Each edition of the program covers a wide range of subjects of great interest to golfers of all types. Cover stories explore such topics as how to learn to play golf and the hot new equipment at the PGA Merchandise Show. And every show offers special instruction from Rick Martino, the PGA's Director of Instruction, or other top PGA Professionals, as well as features on youth golf, behind-the-scenes looks at PGA tournaments, and more.
