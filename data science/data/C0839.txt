<TD colSpan="3"><P><FONT face="Verdana" size="1"><BR>You may not be insured for this unless you take out additional cover for dangerous sports and pastimes such as abseiling or hand gliding.</P></FONT></TD></TR>
<TR>
<TD colSpan="3"><P><FONT face="Verdana" size="1"><B><BR>Would my insurance replace a lost camera?</B></FONT></P></TD></TR>
<TR>
<TD colSpan="3"><P><FONT face="Verdana" size="1"><BR>In most cases, claims are paid at the respective value of the goods at the time when they were lost. For instance a brand new camera that is lost a day later would only have a second hand value. Claims are not paid at replacement cost and you may have to pay towards the cost of the claim (excess) and you will subject to a maximum upper limit per article.<br>When taking expensive items on excursions you're advised to check that you have significant cover. This may even be provided under your home contents policy.<br><br></P></FONT></TD></TR>
