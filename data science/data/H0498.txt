					<h3>1 - 10 of 32</h3><p><strong><a href="http://www.iia.msu.edu/absp/">Agricultural Biotechnology Support Project (ABSP), Michigan</a></strong><br />Presentation of the Agricultural Biotechnology Support Project (ABSP) of the Institute of International Agriculture at the Michigan State University. The project aims to help developing countries to use and manage biotechnology.</p>
<p><strong><a href="http://www.adb.org/">Asian Development Bank (ADB)</a></strong><br />The Asian Development Bank (ADB) is a multilateral development finance institution that promotes the social and economic progress of the Asia-Pacific region. It gives special attention to the needs of smaller or less-developed countries, and to regional and national projects and programs.
</p>
<p><strong><a href="http://www.bioinfo.com/">Biotechnology Information Institute</a></strong><br />The Biotechnology Information Institute in Rockville, USA, offers consulting in biotechnology and pharmaceutical information resources development and use. A lot of links.</p>
<p><strong><a href="http://www.cabi.org/BIOSCIENCE/switz.htm">CAB Bioscience Switzerland</a></strong><br />CAB International is a global non-profit organisation delivering practical knowledge solutions in applied life sciences by utilizing its expertise in publishing and biological sciences. The CABI Bioscience Centre Switzerland in Delémont has developed a substantial programme of weed and arthropod biological control.</p>
<p><strong><a href="http://www.cde.unibe.ch/">Centre for Development and Environment (CDE), Universität Bern</a></strong><br />The Centre for Development and Environment (CDE) is concerned with development and environment in the South and the North, with a special emphasis on sustainable management of natural resources. It is a division of the Institute of Geography of the University of Berne and is involved in bilateral and worldwide networks with institutions and specialists.</p>
<p><strong><a href="http://www.cgiar.org/centers.htm">Consultative Group on International Agricultural Research (CGIAR)</a></strong><br />Worldwide network of 16 research centers with the aim of contributing to food securitiy in developing countries. Information on sustainable agricultural development based on the environmentally sound management of natural resources.</p>
<p><strong><a href="http://www.sandec.ch/index.html">Department of Water and Sanitation in Developing Countries (SANDEC)</a></strong><br />SANDEC is the Department of Water and Sanitation in Developing Countries at the Swiss Federal Institute for Environmental Science and Technology (EAWAG) in Duebendorf, Switzerland. Its activities centre on problems of sustainable development in economically less developed countries.</p>
<p><strong><a href="http://www.ecos.ch/index.html">ecos.ch</a></strong><br />Ecos.ch develops, supports and evaluates projects and products that harmonize the needs of business, the environment and society. </p>
<p><strong><a href="http://www.gefweb.org/">Global Environment Facility (GEF)</a></strong><br />The Global Environment Facility (GEF) is a multilateral financing mechanism created to address global environmental threats, including biological diversity. Most of its resources support efforts by developing countries to implement the climate change and biodiversity conventions.  
</p>
