<font face="Arial,Helvetica">Weight: 3.5 ounces</font>

<br><font face="Arial,Helvetica">Range: 70 yards+</font>

<font face="Arial,Helvetica">Onado</font></h3>
<font face="Arial,Helvetica">Weight: 3.75 ounces</font>
<br><font face="Arial,Helvetica">Span: 16.5 inches</font>
<br><font face="Arial,Helvetica">Range: 60 yards+</font>
<br><font face="Arial,Helvetica">Wind speed: 5-15mph</font>
<br><font face="Arial,Helvetica">The "Onado" is an open "V" shape, with
gracefull curving blades. Designed from my "Duocurve" boomerang, the "Onado"
has a low flight path and, like the Duocurve, stands up well in windy conditions,
has a large circular flight.&nbsp;</font>

<h3>
<font face="Arial,Helvetica">Tigarang</font></h3>
<font face="Arial,Helvetica">Weight: 2.75 ounces</font>
<br><font face="Arial,Helvetica">Span: 14 inches</font>
<br><font face="Arial,Helvetica">Range: 50 yards+</font>
<br><font face="Arial,Helvetica">Wind speed: 3-12 mph</font>
<br><font face="Arial,Helvetica">This model is the original of the so called
"Trapezoid" shape boomerangs, and the fine little boomerang which I designed
from wood in 1974. The Tigarang is an attractive three sided boomerang
with a most pleasing circular flight pattern. TIGA is the Malay word for
number 3, hence the name "Tigarang". A nice easy boomerang to throw and
catch, and can be weighted for distances up to 90 yards plus.&nbsp;</font>
<h3>
<font face="Arial,Helvetica">Valiant</font></h3>
<font face="Arial,Helvetica">Weight: 1.75 ounces</font>

<br><font face="Arial,Helvetica">Range: 50 yards+</font>
<br><font face="Arial,Helvetica">Wind speed: 0-10 mph</font>
<br><font face="Arial,Helvetica">A petit V-shaped boomerang, being an easy
model to throw and to catch. A good performer in windless conditions, where
some other boomerangs have a tendency to fall short.&nbsp;</font>

<font face="Arial,Helvetica">Veebo</font></h3>
