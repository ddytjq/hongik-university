	<H5>CAREER HISTORY - The '80's</H5>
		<TABLE CELLSPACING="0" CELLPADDING="4">
		<TR>
		    <TD VALIGN="top" WIDTH="48"><B>1983-84</B></TD>
		    <TD>
			<UL>
			<LI>Raced self prepared GTR-XU1 Torana in Sports Sedan category.
			<LI>Nineteen races resulted in nine firsts, five seconds and five thirds.
			</UL>
			</TD>
		</TR>
		<TR>
		    <TD VALIGN="top"><B>1984-86</B></TD>
		    <TD>
			<UL>
			<LI>Each year contested in six hour race at Amaroo, winning two, second once, and covering some 150 laps single handed.</TD>
			</UL>
		</TR>
		<TR>
		    <TD VALIGN="top"><B>1985</B></TD>
		    <TD>
			<UL>
			<LI>Contested the Goodyear Laser series for two firsts, one second, one third and two fourths.
			</UL>
			</TD>
		</TR>
		<TR>
		    <TD VALIGN="top"><B>1985-91</B></TD>
		    <TD>
			<UL>
			<LI>Winton 300 production endurance race. Six consecutive years in five different cars.
			</UL>
			</TD>
		</TR>
		<TR>
		    <TD VALIGN="top"><B>1986</B></TD>
		    <TD>
			<UL>
			<LI>Contested in the first endurance Championship with the late Mike Burgmann in a VK Group A Commodore at Amaroo, Sandown and Bathurst.
			</UL>
			</TD>
		</TR>
		<TR>
		    <TD VALIGN="top"><B>1987</B></TD>
		    <TD>
			<UL>
			<LI>Contested first Group E Australian Championship in a VK 5 Litre Commodore, finishing sixth outright.
			<LI>Contested the Oran Park Commodore Classic series, with front row starts in five consecutive races.
			<LI>Also copmpeted in the Calder Park Super Series, with pole position and a second placing.
			<LI>The inaugural Tooheys 1000 Group E support race, qualifying fourth in a non-turbo car.
			</UL>
			</TD>
		</TR>
		<TR>
		    <TD VALIGN="top"><B>1988-90</B></TD>
		    <TD>
			<UL>
			<LI>Thunderdome Auscar Series in a VL V8 Commodore. Nine races for one first, one second, one third, one fourth, two fifths and one sixth.
			</UL>
			</TD>
		</TR>
		</TABLE>
