              <p class="plaintext"><a name="thislittlepiggysbuyingitshome"></a></p>
              <p class="plaintext">&nbsp;</p>
              <p class="biggreentext">this little piggy's buying its home&#133;</p>
              <p class="boldtext">&#133; pay your mortgage off quicker and for 
                less</p>
              <ul>
                <li class="plaintext">Our offset account allows you to combine 
                  your mortgage and your savings to make your money work harder.<br>
                </li>
                <li class="plaintext">The mortgage interest rate you pay is the 
                  Bank of England base rate plus 0.75% for five years (current 
                  rate of 4.75% <span class="boldtext">APR 5.4%</span>).<br>
                </li>
                <li class="plaintext">1% cashback is paid into your offset savings 
                  account upon completion (to a maximum of &pound;2,000).<br>
                </li>
                <li class="plaintext">There are no arrangement fees.<br>
                </li>
                <li class="plaintext">Borrow up to 95% of the value of your property.<br>
                </li>
                <li class="plaintext">There is no Mortgage Risk Payment, even 
                  at 95%.<br>
                </li>
                <li class="plaintext">There are flexible features such as daily 
                  interest charging, overpayment, underpayment and payment holidays.<br>
                </li>
                <li class="plaintext">Offset is available for house purchase, 
                  re-mortgage and existing customer transfers.</li>
              </ul>
              <p class="plaintext"><br>
                <a href="#top">Back to the top of the page</a></p>
              <p class="plaintext"><a name="thislittlepiggygetsagreatrate"></a></p>
              <p class="plaintext">&nbsp;</p>
              <p></p>
              <p class="biggreentext">this little piggy gets a great rate&#133;</p>
              <p class="plaintext">&#133;<span class="boldtext">earn</span> the 
                equivalent to<span class="boldtext"> at least 5.94%*</span> and 
                <span class="boldtext">up to 7.92%**</span> on your savings.</p>
              <ul>
                <li> <span class="plaintext">Rather than earning interest on your 
                  savings, the money in your offset savings account will be used 
                  to reduce the interest charged on your offset mortgage.</span></li>
                <li class="plaintext">This results in an equivalent rate considerably 
                  higher than that available on other typical savings accounts.<br>
                </li>
                <li class="plaintext">You don't need a large amount of savings 
                  to make offset work for you. Investing just &pound;1 per day 
                  could save you over &pound;8,500 in mortgage interest***. Have 
                  a look at some <a href="#examples">examples</a> of how much 
                  mortgage interest could be saved.<br>
                </li>
                <li class="plaintext">You can set up direct debits and standing 
                  orders from your savings account for regular payments.<br>
                </li>
                <li class="plaintext">And you don't need to pay in salary credits 
                  or go to the hassle of opening a new current account.</li>
              </ul>
              <p class="smalltext">* Standard rate tax payers<br>
                ** Higher rate tax payers<br>
                *** Based on an average &pound;75,000 mortgage over 25 years</p>
              <p class="plaintext"><br>

              <p class="plaintext"><a name="thislittlepiggypaysnone"></a></p>
              <p class="plaintext">&nbsp;</p>
              <p class="biggreentext">this little piggy pays none&#133;</p>
              <p class="boldtext">&#133;tax free savings with instant access</p>
              <ul>
                <li> <span class="plaintext">Because you are not being paid any 
                  interest on your offset savings (the benefit you gain is a reduction 
                  in your mortgage interest), you won't pay any tax on them.<br>
                  </span></li>
                <li class="plaintext">For higher rate tax payers this is an especially 
                  valuable benefit.<br>
                </li>
                <li class="plaintext">You will also receive a Cashcard linked 
                  to your account that allows instant access to your savings free 
                  of charge at most cashpoints.</li>
              </ul>
