<B>UCI Juniors Track Nations Cup 2002:</B><P>

The UCI Juniors Track Nations Cup 2002 will be held across all the continents with the aim of supporting the promotion and development of junior track cycling (for men and women). 

<OL TYPE=A>

<LI><B>GENERAL CONDITIONS APPLICABLE TO ALL CONTINENTS:</B><P>

<B>Team travel expenses:</B> Teams' expenses (travel and accommodation) will not be paid by the organisers, but by each of the National Federations concerned.<P>

<B>Race programmes, rules and prizes: </B>Race programmes, rules and prizes (to be provided by the UCI) will be identical in each continent and will meet the following criteria:<P>


<OL TYPE=1>
