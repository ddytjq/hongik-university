      <p><font face="Arial" size="4"> <b><font size="3">Protecting your self-build 
        property</font></b></font></p>
      <p><font size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">It is important that 
        you have the correct insurance to protect you and your self-build property 
        during construction.</font><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif"> 
        </font></p>
      <font face="Arial"><p><font size="2">If your builder is not registered with NHBC we recommend 
        that you arrange a suitable policy with an insurer such as Zurich Municipal. 
        In exceptional cases we will accept architect’s certificates issued at 
        appropriate stage during the construction.</font></p>
      <p><font size="2">We strongly recommend that you have third party liability 
        insurance. If your house is being constructed by a building contractor 
        we will need to see a copy of an appropriate Contractor’s All Risks Policy.</font></p>
      <p><font size="2">If you constructing the property on a self-build basis, 
        you should arrange a Self-Build Policy. This should cover damage from 
        subsidence, storm, impact, vandalism, fire and theft.</font></p>
