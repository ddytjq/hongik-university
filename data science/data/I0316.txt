<H1>uhlsport United Counties League</H1>
<H2>Founded: 1895</H2>
<P>The league was formed as the Northamptonshire League and changed its name to
the United Counties League in 1934 in order to be able to accept clubs from a
wider area.</P>
<H3>Founder Members</H3>
<P>Earls Barton, Finedon Town Revellers Reserves, Higham Ferrers, Kettering
Church Institute, Kettering Crusaders, Kettering Victoria, Midland Cottages
(Wellingborough), Northampton Victoria, Rothwell Town Swifts, Rushden Town
Reserves, Wellingborough Town Reserves.</P>
<H2>Area Covered</H2>
<P>The United Counties League in centered around Northamptonshire and covers
areas of adjoining counties, West Cambridgeshire, South Lincolnshire, North
Bedfordshire, North Buckinghamshire and North Herfordshire.</P>
<H2>Structure</H2>
<P>2 divisions, 38 clubs<BR><A HREF="#uclp">Premier Division</A> (20 clubs)<BR><A
HREF="#ucl1">Division One</A> (18 clubs)</P>
