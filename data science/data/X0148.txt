<H1>Conservation Actions Performed By Mountain Bikers</H1>

<P> Mountain bikers are conservation-minded. We enjoy bicycling in
scenic, natural places and we work to protect these areas from harmful
development. We value clean air and water and abundant wildlife. We
revere narrow trails in roadless areas. We are committed to maintaining
and enhancing these natural areas around the world. </P>

<P> Here are a few examples of the community conservation projects that
mountain bicyclists have supported. </P>
