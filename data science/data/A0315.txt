		<P>
		Many customers use HOBS only to keep track of their accounts but could be doing much more with the system. Business HOBS and Corporate HOBS now provide a very comprehensive electronic banking software package..... are you benefiting from them to the extent you could be?   Over the following pages we highlight some of the main facilities and benefits and encourage you to consider if you could use these to your advantage.  (Only brief details of available facilities are provided here but references are given for the section of the User Manual that provides full information).
		<P>

		<span class="topnav">Payments (See pages 36/51 in the User Guide for Details)   </span>
		<P>
		Using any of the payment facilities is more cost effective than sending cheques as they are available at a fraction of the cost of making manual payments.
		<P>
		<span class="topnav">Beneficiary Management</span>
		<P>
		Beneficiary details are maintained locally on your own database. You can add, amend or delete as required, when required. No need to contact the Bank or fill in forms!
		<P>
		<span class="topnav">Domestic Payments</span>
		<P>
		Mainly used for one off payments e.g. to suppliers, with the beneficiary name appearing on your statement as a record of payment. <P>
		<span class="topnav">BACS Direct</span>
		<P>
		Mainly used for Payroll purposes. This facility allows for a single debit to an account in respect of a batch of payments. Using BACS Direct payments can reduce your charges further as the cost for this payment type is only 8p per credit item. A further aid is the "Template" which you can use to store/recall regular payments.
<P>


		<span class="topnav">Updates (See pages 21/24 in the User Guide for Details)</span><p><span class="body">In order to see up to the minute position, individual accounts can be updated at any time. This function is particularly useful should you be waiting for a CHAPS payment to be received. </span>
		<P>
		<span class="topnav">Group (See pages 26/27 in the User Guide for Details)</span><p><span class="body">HOBS provides the facility to place accounts into groups. This is particularly useful for separating Purchase Ledger/Payroll Accounts.</span>
		<P>
		<span class="topnav">Exporting & Importing Data  (See pages 30/35 in the User Guide for Details)</span><p><span class="body">These facilities allow you to export balance and transaction information into accounting software such as Pegasus or Farmplan or to a spreadsheet for analysis. You can also import payment instructions from your accounting software avoiding the need to re-key payments. This is particulary useful for Wages & Payroll.</span>
		<P>
		<span class="topnav">Finding transactions  (See pages 28/29 in the User Guide for Details)</span><p><span class="body">
		This lets you search for a specific transaction or a range of transactions using certain criteria: - amounts, dates and transaction types, etc.</span>
		<P>
		<span class="topnav">Printing/Summary reporting</span><p><span class="body">
		This includes the facilities for printing statements (you choose the dates), and payment batches prior to transmission.</span>

<span class=topnav>Corporate HOBS Users (See pages 43/47 in the User Guide for Details)</span><p><span class=body>
CHAPS Payments are used where your customer requires funds to be credited to their account on the same day*.
<P>
Are you aware that the Bank charges &#163;3 less for CHAPS payments you make through Corporate HOBS, compared to manual CHAPS payments?
<P>
Are you still using your Branch for making International Payments?
<P>
Corporate HOBS is ideal for this type of payment, you can set up regular beneficiaries on your database and transmit payments at your convenience, safe in the knowledge that they have been received by the bank without the need to send a fax or hope that it gets there in the post!*
<P>
You can also order drafts in a Foreign Currency. These will be sent to you for onward transmission to your customer!*
<P>
* You can access any or all of these facilities only when you subscribe to Corporate HOBS.
