    service to customers, banks and other professionals.&nbsp; The Bank's services include the
    provision of attractive and competitive deposit and savings accounts, flexible and
    imaginative secured lending, factoring and other private banking facilities and
    operations which are offered throughout the United Kingdom.&nbsp; The Bank has developed a Professional
    Services division which provides banking, accounting and legal advice to other
    professionals as well as banking and financial institutions.</font></p>
