<B>Winter Flounder</B> <BR ALIGN=Center>
<I>[Pseudopleronectes americanus]</I>
<P ALIGN=Left>
<I>&nbsp; &nbsp; </I>The Winter Flounder is one of the most common flatfish
in the Long Island area. The reason they call it Winter &nbsp;Flounder is
because that's when you'll find this fish around the area. This fish is very
valuable to commercial and recreational fisherman. Flounder is delicious
and when fished for are usually kept. These fish are capable of reaching
8lbs.but are usually in the 2-3lb. class. They have very small mouths, so
if you are going to let this fish go please cut your hook if you are having
a problem getting the hook out.
