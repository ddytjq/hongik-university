      <p><font face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2"><b>Policy 
        details</b></font>
      <p><font face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2"><b>This is 
        a summary of cover</b> <br>
        Membership is subject to acceptance of your application by BUPA, the rules 
        and table of benefits of the scheme and any special conditions that may 
        apply. Full details of general terms and conditions are available on request 
        and will be sent to you when you join. Precise terms of cover available 
        will depend on an assessment of your individual circumstances. </font>
      <p><font face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">BUPACare 
        offers you extensive cover - from the first time your GP refers you to 
        a consultant, right the way through to hospital and after-care treatment. 
        It is BUPA's most comprehensive scheme for individual members and offers 
        cover for eligible treatment:</font> 
      <ul>
        <li><font face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">At any 
          one of the participating hospitals in BUPA's national network</font></li>
        <li><font face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">Including 
          private day-case and in-patient treatment</font></li>
        <li><font face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">For complimentary 
          medicine - acupuncture, chiropractic, osteopathy and homeopathy</font></li>
        <li><font face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">Including 
          out-patient consultations with a specialist</font></li>
        <li><font face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">For psychiatric 
          treatment after two years' membership</font> </li>
      </ul>
      <p><font face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">It also offers:</font> 
      <ul>
        <li> <font face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">Cash 
          benefit if you should have NHS in-patient treatment.</font></li>
        <li> <font face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">The opportunity 
          to reduce your subscriptions by selecting to pay an excess.</font></li>
        <li> <font face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">Three 
          scales of cover, so there&#146;s a scheme to suit every budget.</font></li>
        <li> <font face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">No medical 
          examination required, making it easier to join.<br>
          </font></li>
      </ul>

        <li><font face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">Cover
