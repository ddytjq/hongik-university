<B>Title:</B> CETA AND PREMIER PROTECTION JOIN FORCES</a> |   <B>Release Date:</B> 26/09/2001<hr color="#505050" align="left"><br>The UK’s largest general insurance network, CETA, is joining forces with Premier Protection one of the fastest growing non-regulated life networks to provide its members with a full range of general insurance products, training and compliance support.<br>
<br>
Through the new partnership Premier’s members will also gain access to CETA’s tailor made insurance quotation and administration software system, which can be easily interfaced with existing web sites to provide a full on-line general insurance marketing capability.<br>
<br>
The agreement will give Premier’s members access to CETA’s market leading household, ASU, commercial, liability, legal, travel, pet and motor insurance products.  The two partners also intend to develop a range of innovative products which will be exclusively available to Premier’s members.<br>
<br>
David Quick, Managing Director of CETA comments: ‘This is exciting news for both CETA and Premier Protection.  By gaining access to CETA’s general insurance products Premier’s members will be in a better position to protect their relationships with their clients.  By offering a complete range of products clients will not have to look elsewhere for their insurance needs and Premier’s members will be able to build a substantial new income stream’.<br>
<br>
Michael Cooke, Premier Protection’s Managing Director added ‘Our aim is to provide our members with an excellent service and give them access to the best products the market can offer.  We believe we have achieved this through our partnership with CETA.  They have a good panel of insurers, their products are innovative and their technological and administration support is first class’.<br>
Based in Southampton, Premier Protection was established in March 2001 to provide a fast,  efficient and profitable way for intermediaries such as mortgage brokers and IFAs to write term, ASU, PMI and other non-investment business.  Recent developments in the industry have created the opportunity to provide a more comprehensive service through market leading partners such as CETA<br>
<br>
General insurance intermediaries and IFAs interested in finding out more about CETA should call 01608 647650.<br>
