        </b> <font size="2">Credit facilities are available. Typical example assumes 

        borrowers take our variable rate of 6.59%. <u>APR 7.4% (variable)</u> 

        based on 1% above our basic variable rate of 5.59% for the duration of 

        the account. Rates correct as at 20 November 2001. Based on a &pound;42,000 

        repayment mortgage over 15 years on a purchase price of &pound;129,000. 

        180 monthly repayments of &pound;374.40 will be required. Total amount 

        payable &pound;68,494.05 includes solicitors costs of &pound;117.50 (assuming 

        the same solicitor acts for both of us), a valuation fee of &pound;265, 

        an administration fee of &pound;210, a deeds release fee of &pound;50,a 

        sealing fee of &pound;40 paid at the time of redemption and accrued interest 

        of &pound;115.33 paid at the time of the first repayment. Mortgages are 

        subject to status, valuation and security, they are available to those 

        aged 18 or over only. The product shown above is subject to availability 

        and may be withdrawn without notice at any time. Written quotations available 

        on request from Clay Cross Building Society, Eyre Street, Clay Cross, 

        Chesterfield. S45 9NS. Clay Cross Building Society is a member of the 

        Building Societies Ombudsman Scheme and subscribes to the Banking and 

        Mortgage codes, copies of which are available on request.</font></font></p>
