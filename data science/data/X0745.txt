      <a name="overview"></a> 
      <h3><img src="images_grandisland/deer-hunting-11-19-01.jpg" width="250" height="208" align="right" border="1">Overview</h3>
      <p>We specialize in affordable, all-inclusive guided hunting and fishing 
        trips in the Southern Tier of New York State. Our operation is geared 
        towards small groups of 2 to 4 people but we can handle up to 6. We encourage 
        our clients to include their children so that they too may have a wonderful 
        outdoor experience. Special rates apply to children under 14 and non-hunting 
        spouses and significant others.</p>
      <p>For whitetail deer hunts (archery, shotgun and muzzleloader seasons), numerous stand locations have been set including tree and ground blinds.  We continually scout for the best locations to see trophy animals.  Situated in the heart of Steuben County in Western NY (which perennially leads the state in total deer and turkey take), we have thousands of acres of land available between leases and state lands.</p>  
      <p>We also provide guided hunting opportunities for bear, wild turkey (Spring 
        and Fall), varmints (woodchuck, red fox, gray fox and coyote) as well 
        as small game (grouse, woodcock, rabbit and squirrel). Guided river canoe 
        fishing trips are available for walleye, chain pickerel and bass. Half 
        and full-day charters on the Upper Niagara River and Lake Erie are also 
        available on a 24' boat for scenic tours, scuba diving and fishing for 
        walleye, bass and muskys.</p>

<img src="images_grandisland/lodge.jpg" width="300" height="200" border="1" alt="Cozy, private cabins with outstanding home cooked meals in the main cabin kitchen, including packed lunches" align="right"><a name="accommodations"></a><h3>Accommodations</h3> 
<p>All clients stay in cozy, private cabins with outstanding home cooked meals in the main cabin kitchen, including packed lunches.  Amenities include hot showers, refrigerator, microwave oven and TV.  Local phone service is available.</p>
<br><br>
<a name="licenseinfo"></a>
      <h3>License information</h3> 
      <p>Specifics regarding licensing can be viewed by clicking on the New York 
        State Dept. of Environmental Conservation's website (<a href="http://www.dec.state.ny.us/website/dfwmr/nrlicsl3.html" target="_blank">http://www.dec.state.ny.us/website/dfwmr/nrlicsl3.html</a>) 
        or by calling the NYSDEC @ (518) 457-3521. Hunting and fishing licenses 
        are available over the counter at our local Walmart. Hunting and Fishing 
        licenses can also be purchased through the US Mail. Download the license 
        application from the above NYSDEC website and send the completed application 
        along with the appropriate payment to the specified address.&quot; </p>
      <a name="directions"></a> <h3>Directions</h3>
<p>Rochester International Airport is approximately 60 miles away and all major car rentals are available.  Specific driving directions are available when you book your hunt.</p>
