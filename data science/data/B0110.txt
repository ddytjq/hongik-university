    <a name="Top"><big><big><u><font face="Arial" color="#0000FF">Mortgage Guide</font></u></big></big></a></p>
    <p><small><font face="Arial">How many times have you read that buying a house is one of
    the most stressful events that will happen in your lifetime?</font></small></p>
    <p><small><font face="Arial">Well we do not disagree, although there is one sure way to
    reduce the level of stress significantly, and that is to arrange your borrowing through
    the Holmesdale Building Society.</font></small></p>
    <p><small><font face="Arial">With our single office in Reigate, we can proudly boast that
    spanning over 146 years we have helped many people to buy or improve their homes.</font></small></p>
    <p><small><font face="Arial">This page is only intended as a guide, for more detailed
    information about the terms and conditions of our mortgage business please contact the
    Society. You are strongly recommended to refer to the Society's Rules and Mortgage
    Conditions.</font></small></p>
    <p align="center"><u><strong>YOUR HOME IS AT RISK IF YOU DO NOT KEEP UP REPAYMENTS ON A
    MORTGAGE OR OTHER LOAN SECURED ON IT</strong></u></p>

    </center></div><p><a name="Where do I start?"><font face="Arial" color="#0000FF"><strong>Where
    do I start?</strong></font></a></p>
    <p><small><font face="Arial">Whether you are looking to set up home for the first time,
    hoping to move to a larger house, looking to improve your existing house, or even looking
    for a new lender because you are unhappy with your existing one, you need to establish how
    much you can borrow, and how much it will cost you every month.</font></small></p>
    <p><font face="Arial"><a href="mortgages.htm#Top">Back </a>to the top</font></p>
    <p><a name="So how much can I borrow?"><strong><font face="Arial" color="#0000FF">So how
    much can I borrow?</font></strong></a></p>
    <p><small><font face="Arial">The amount we may be able to lend you will depend on the
    level of your regular income and of course, the value of the property to be mortgaged.
    However to give you an idea, we are able to lend you up to 3.49 times your basic annual
    salary if you are buying on your own or, if there are two of you; we are able to lend up
    to 2.74 times your combined salaries on a loan to value of up to 75%. On a loan to value
    of over 75% we can offer you 3 times your single income and 2.5 times your combined
    incomes. In addition, if either you or your partner receive overtime, commission or
    bonuses, we can take this into account, but only if this other income can be shown to be
    either guaranteed or a regular feature of your monthly pay.</font></small></p>
    <p><small><font face="Arial">Please note the above is only a guideline and the income
    multiples are subject to the Society's discretion.</font></small></p>
    <p><font face="Arial"><a href="mortgages.htm#Top">Back </a>to the top</font></p>
    <p><a name="How much deposit will I need?"><font face="Arial" color="#0000FF"><strong>How
    much deposit will I need?</strong></font></a></p>
    <p><small><font face="Arial">Unless you are purchasing as a sitting tenant and are
    therefore paying quite a lot less for your property than its current value, you will find
    that the Holmesdale Building Society does not consider 100% mortgages.</font></small></p>
    <p><small><font face="Arial">The most we will lend you - as long as you can afford it of
    course - is 90% of the lower of the purchase price or valuation and therefore you will
    need a deposit of at least 10%.</font></small></p>
    <p><font face="Arial"><a href="mortgages.htm#Top">Back </a>to the top</font></p>
    <p><a name="Affordability"><strong><font face="Arial" color="#0000FF">Affordability</font></strong></a></p>
    <p align="left"><small><font face="Arial">Whilst you may fit the Society's criteria in
    respect of the size of mortgage you are considering, when applying for a mortgage you
    should complete a budget checklist. This will take into account all of your regular
    outgoings and anticipated purchase/moving costs so that you can be sure that you can
    afford the mortgage instalments. Consideration also needs to be given to any increase in
    mortgage interest rates.</font></small></p>
    <p><font face="Arial"><a href="mortgages.htm#Top">Back </a>to the top</font></p>
    <p><a name="Right to redeem"><font face="Arial" color="#0000FF"><strong>Right to redeem</strong></font></a></p>
    <p align="left"><small><font face="Arial">The borrower(s) may exercise this without prior
    notice in accordance with the Society's Mortgage Conditions clause 20 subject to clause
    5(f) and Rule No 15 (see below).</font></small></p>
    <p><font face="Arial"><a href="mortgages.htm#Top">Back </a>to the top</font></p>
    <p><a name="Redemption fee"><font face="Arial" color="#0000FF"><strong>Redemption Fee
    (Rule No 15)</strong></font></a></p>
    <p align="left"><small><font face="Arial">The Society reserves the right to charge up to 3
    months interest on either full or partial redemptions within the first 5 years. This is
    reduced by one fifth for each complete year that the mortgage has been in force or is
    waived if a new mortgage is taken out with this Society. Further the value of any
    discounted rate concession or benefit in lieu of the concession will be recovered, if
    applicable to the individual account. Redemption interest will not be charged in the event
    of early repayment of the mortgage for all mortgage offers issued after 26th November
    2001.</font></small></p>
    <p><font face="Arial"><a href="mortgages.htm#Top">Back </a>to the top</font></p>
    <p><a name="Right of sale"><font face="Arial" color="#0000FF"><strong>Right of sale
    (Possession)</strong></font></a></p>
    <p align="left"><small><font face="Arial">The Society's power arises from the Mortgage
    Conditions clauses 13 and 14 and should this action be taken the Society will close the
    account but without prejudice to any liability under the borrower's Personal Covenant.</font></small></p>
    <p><font face="Arial"><a href="mortgages.htm#Top">Back </a>to the top</font></p>
    <p><a name="Personal covenant"><font face="Arial" color="#0000FF"><strong>Personal
    covenant</strong></font></a></p>
    <p align="left"><small><font face="Arial">The borrower is personally liable for all sums
    due to the Society whether or not any security held by the Society is sufficient to meet
    that liability.</font></small></p>
    <p><font face="Arial"><a href="mortgages.htm#Top">Back </a>to the top</font></p>
    <p><a name="Cases of hardship"><font face="Arial" color="#0000FF"><strong>Cases of
    hardship</strong></font></a></p>
    <p align="left"><small><font face="Arial">The Society will wherever possible give
    sympathetic consideration in cases of hardship and would encourage borrowers who find
    themselves in financial difficulty to contact the Society immediately.</font></small></p>
    <p><font face="Arial"><a href="mortgages.htm#Top">Back </a>to the top</font></p>
    <p><a name="Guarantees"><font face="Arial" color="#0000FF"><strong>Guarantees and third
    party security</strong></font></a></p>
    <p align="left"><small><font face="Arial">Any person willing to act as Guarantor must be
    aware that they are equally as liable for the debt as the borrower and that the potential
    liability is unlimited. Independent Legal advice is strongly recommended before entering
    into the undertaking to act as a Guarantor.</font></small></p>
    <p><font face="Arial"><a href="mortgages.htm#Top">Back </a>to the top</font></p>
    <p><a name="Data protection"><font face="Arial" color="#0000FF"><strong>Data protection
    act 1998</strong></font></a></p>
    <p align="left"><small><font face="Arial">Borrowers have the right of access to their
    personal details held on our computer files and within manual files, although we may wish
    to make a reasonable charge for providing this.</font></small></p>
    <p><font face="Arial"><a href="mortgages.htm#Top">Back </a>to the top</font></p>
    <p><a name="Confidentiality"><font face="Arial" color="#0000FF"><strong>Confidentiality</strong></font></a></p>
    <p align="left"><small><font face="Arial">The Society will at all times exercise strict
    confidentiality regarding your personal details and conduct of your account, except as
    authorised in the mortgage application form.</font></small></p>
    <p><font face="Arial"><a href="mortgages.htm#Top">Back </a>to the top</font></p>
    <p><a name="Marketing"><font face="Arial" color="#0000FF"><strong>Marketing</strong></font></a></p>
    <p align="left"><small><font face="Arial">Please confirm in writing if you do not wish to
    receive advertising or general material relating to the services the Society or an
    associated body can provide.</font></small></p>
    <p><font face="Arial"><a href="mortgages.htm#Top">Back </a>to the top</font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" color="#0000FF"><strong>Internal <a name="Complaints">Complaints
    Procedure</a></strong></font></p>
    <p><small><font face="Arial">We hope that you are fully satisfied with the service you
    receive from the Society. However, if you are not, we have an internal complaints
    procedure (ICP) which is designed to help resolve your complaints in a fair and speedy
    manner.</font></small></p>
    <p><strong><small><font face="Arial">Making a complaint - ICP procedure</font></small></strong></p>
    <p><small><font face="Arial">You may make a complaint by any reasonable means, for
    example, by letter, telephone, e-mail or in person. All complaints should be directed to
    the Chief Executive and confirmed in writing.</font></small></p>
    <p><small><font face="Arial">We will send a written acknowledgement of your complaint
    within five working days (working days exclude weekends and bank holidays).</font></small></p>
    <p><small><font face="Arial">Within four weeks of receiving your complaint, the Society
    will either send you a final response or a holding response, which will explain:-</font></small><ul>
      <li><small><font face="Arial">why we are not yet in a position to resolve the complaint, and</font></small></li>
      <li><font face="Arial"><small>indicate when the Society will make further contact, which
        will be no later then eight weeks after the complaint was received.</small></font></li>
    </ul>
    <p><font face="Arial"><small>By the end of eight weeks after receiving your complaint, the
    Society will send you either:-</small></font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial"><small>a final response which</small></font><ul>
      <li><font face="Arial"><small>accepts the complaint and, where applicable, offers redress,</small></font></li>
      <li><font face="Arial"><small>offers redress without accepting the complaint, or</small></font></li>
      <li><font face="Arial"><small>rejects the complaint and gives reasons for doing so, and
        contains information about the right to complain to the Financial Ombudsman Service or,</small></font></li>
    </ul>
    <p><font face="Arial"><small>a response which</small></font><ul>
      <li><font face="Arial"><small>explains that the Society is still not in a position to make a
        final response, give reasons for the further delay and indicates when the Society expects
        to be able to provide a final response and</small></font></li>
      <li><font face="Arial"><small>informs the complainant that he/she may refer the complaint to
        the Financial Ombudsman Service if he/she is dissatisfied with the delay, and enclose a
        copy of the Financial Ombudsman Service explanatory leaflet relating to complaints.</small></font></li>
    </ul>
    <p><font face="Arial"><small>If you decide to pursue your complaint through the Financial
    Ombudsman Service you must do so within six months of the date of the final response.</small></font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial"><small>Further information relating to the current Financial
    Ombudsman Service complaints scheme is available from this Society or direct from
    Financial Ombudsman Service, South Quay Plaza, 183 Marsh Wall, London, E14 9SR, telephone
    number 0845 080 1800; e-mail: <a href="mailto:enquiries@financial-ombudsman.org.uk">enquiries@financial-ombudsman.org.uk</a>
    or website <a href="http://www.financial-ombudsman.org.uk" target="_blank">www.financial-ombudsman.org.uk</a>.
    </small></font></p>
