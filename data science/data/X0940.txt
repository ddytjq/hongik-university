    Venture with Lineage wide on the track with Annihilation and Prime Monarch next. By the
    1800m Lineage moved up outside Gianni followed by Risky Venture and Tawtrice then Prime
    Monarch. Little changed until the 1100m where Risky Venture and Gianni took over the front
    running from Lineage and Tawtrice followed by Prime Monarch then King Royale. Approaching
    the turn Lineage moved up to make a line of three in front from Prime Monarch and
    Tawtrice. On straightening Lineage briefly hit the front as Hobart moved up outside Prime
    Monarch with Tawtrice making a run closer to the rail. By the 200m Tawtrice led from
    Hobart then Prime Monarch. In the run to the line Tawtrice easily held Hobart winning by
    3/4 length under hands and heels with Eastman just grabbing third from Annihilation. </small></font></p>
    <p align="left"><small><font face="Arial" color="#004000">Tawtrice is a 5yo chestnut
    gelding by Tawriffic from Acetrim and was ridden by Travis Creek. He is trained by Richard
    Jolly for RC Dennett, IJ Donchi, BP Frazzetto, JL Bertalli, PD Constance, PE Quinn, LW
    Ross and JP McNamara. </font></small></p>

    as Jamatre started to run on late. In the run to the line Jetway ridden under hands and

    for third. </small></font></p>
