              <p><span class="introtitleredbold">Money Market</span><br>
                <br>
                <span class="body">Our Money Market accounts provide a flexible 
                way of depositing money with interest rates linked to the London 
                Money Market.</span><br>
                <br>
                <span class="body"><span class="bodytitle">How it works</span><br>
                <br>
                Money Market deposits are very flexible. Fixed term, fixed rate 
                deposits can be arranged for periods from overnight to five years. 
                Notice deposits, with variable interest rates, can be arranged 
                with immediate, 7 or 14 day, one, three or six months' notice 
                of withdrawal. </span><br>
