          <h2><font face="Times New Roman, Times, serif">Statistics and Clubs</font></h2>
        </center>
        <p><font size="+2" face="Times New Roman, Times, serif">T</font><font face="Times New Roman, Times, serif">his 
          method uses the same principles as UEFA does to calculate its association 
          coefficients: <br>
          Every match played (MP) in every UEFA club competition (Champion Clubs' 
          Cup (or Champions League), Cup Winners' Cup and UEFA Cup (or Inter-Cities 
          Fairs' Cup), but not Supercup and Intertoto cup) is taken into account: 
          Each club receives 2 points for a win (W), 1 point for a draw (D) and 
          no point for a lost match (L).<br>
          Moreover, participating in some rounds yields bonus points (B): 1 point 
          for the quarter-finals, 1 point for the semi-finals and 1 point for 
          the final. Participating in the Champion Clubs' Cup league stage yields 
          2 bonus points for the 1991/92 and 1992/93 seasons and 1 point for the 
          next ones.<br>
          The ranking is then set up according to the highest number of points 
          (Pts), then (if needed) to the best goal difference (gd) between goals 
          for (gf) and goals against (ga), and then (if needed) to the best "goals 
          for" amount.<br>
          <a href="Stats/clubslist.html">The all-time table (from 1955/56 to 2001/02)</a><br>
