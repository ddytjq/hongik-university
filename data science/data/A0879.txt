      <p class="PageTitle">Training and communications
      <p class="bodytext"><b>Environmental objectives:</b>
<ul>
   <li><span class="bodytext"><b> To ensure that all employees are informed and trained -   where appropriate - to facilitate implementation of the organisation's   environmental policy and Environmental Management System.</b></span></li>
   <li><span class="bodytext"><b> To raise staff awareness of the environmental impacts associated   with particular activities (and thereby improve environmental   performance).</b></span></li></ul>
<p class="bodytext">Appropriate employee training and communications are a vital element in the successful implementation of the Group's environmental policies and Environmental Management System (EMS).<p class="bodytext">On-line environmental training became a reality in 2001 after the development of two bespoke training modules which are available on-line or on cd-rom to Barclays staff. Although these modules have been principally aimed at those staff with specific environmental roles and responsibilities (who are required to complete them), they are accessible to all staff to improve general environmental awareness. Each of the modules which are entitled 'The key environmental issues for Barclays' and 'Impact management and minimisation' finish with a short quiz, again specific to Barclays.<p class="bodytext">Communications during 2001 included regular environmental articles in staff publications aimed at increasing awareness of our environmental programme. In particular we have focussed internal communication on our environmental performance targets, and progress towards achieving them. These paper communications were supplemented by an Energy Efficiency Roadshow, run in partnership with WWF, which visited four of our largest sites.<br></td>
