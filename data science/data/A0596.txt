</span><span class="bodytext">The Abbey National e-banking service allows you to access your Abbey National Bank
Account, Instant Plus Account, Student Bank Account, Direct Saver, Savings ISA, eSaver,
Flexible Saver, Instant Saver, Graduate Account and 16-21 Account as long as they are
linked to the card you have registered for e-banking.<br>
<br>
Most transactions will be done instantly, for example moving money between accounts. If
you are asking us to set up any new facilities, or request any amendments to existing
facilities on your account, we will undertake to carry out your request by the end of the
next working day. <br>
<br>
You will receive a unique transaction reference number after you send a transaction to us.
If you do not receive this for any reason, you know we have not received your request.<br>
<br>
For help and information on using the Abbey National e-banking service, the on-line user
guide within the e-banking service covers all the sections within the service and should
answer all your questions.<br>
<br>
</span><span class="blockheading"><b>Useful Contact Numbers</b><br>
</span><span class="bodytext">If you have any further questions relating to your accounts, please call us on <span class="bodytext"><b>08459 724 724</b></span> <span class="bodytext">8.00am to 9.00pm Monday to Saturday</span>.<br>
<br>
If you have any technical problems relating to the e-banking service please call the Abbey
National e-commerce Support line on <span class="bodytext"><b>0845 600 4388</b></span>. <span class="bodytext">Lines are open 7 days a week, 7.00am to 11.00pm. (Dial + 44 161 9516659 from abroad.)</span>.<br>
<br>
To help us improve our service we may monitor phone calls.<br>
<br>
</span><span class="blockheading"><b>Frequently Asked Questions</b><br>
</span><span class="bodytext"><b>If I have a joint account can we both use e-banking?</b><br>
Yes, both account holders can use the e-banking service, provided you both have a
Multifunction, Electron or an Abbeylink card. A separate e-banking registration number and
passcode needs to be requested for each card. You can register for e-banking at any
Abbeylink Machine, by phoning us on <span class="bodytext"><b>08457 654321</b></span>or by visiting your local branch. If you apply on-line for any of our card based accounts
you will be automatically registered for e-banking when you apply.<br>
<br>
<b>Will I get a higher rate of interest if I access my account via the e-banking service?</b><br>
The e-banking service will make it easier for you to manage your existing accounts, but
our existing channels will still be available to you. Our Flexible Saver account does vary
it's rate of interest depending on which channel you use, and our eSaver account, which is
only available on-line, also offers an attractive rate of interest.<br>
<br>
<b>Is there any charge for using Abbey National's e-banking Support Line?</b><br>
There is no charge for using the Abbey National e-banking Support Line other than the cost
of a telephone call. Calls made via a land line are charged at a local rate. Mobile phone
tariffs may differ. The Abbey National e-banking service is also free, there are no set up
charges or annual fees to pay. However you may have charges applied by your Internet
Service Provider or telephone supplier for being on-line.<br>
<br>
<b>What happens if I make a mistake on the Internet?</b><br>
It is very difficult to make a mistake as all transactions require you to check and
confirm the details you have input. You will also be given a reference number after every
transaction. Please remember we will not have received your transaction unless you are
given this number.If you have any questions about transactions on your account, please
contact us <span class="bodytext"><b>08459 724 724</b></span>.<br>
<br>
<b>Can I have a demonstration of how this works?</b><br>
You can find a demonstration of the service by clicking <a href="javascript:window.open('ebanking_demo_check.htm','ebankingdemo','width=635,height=560,top=30,left=30,menubar=0,toolbar=0,location=0,directories=0,scrollbars=0,status=0,resizable=0');void(0);" class="link">here</a>.<br>
<br>
<b>What transactions can I make once I am on-line?</b><br>
Once registered and logged in, you will be able to view the balances, and last 40
transactions of all the accounts linked to your e-banking registered card. You can also
make bill payments and personal payments from your main banking account.Other transactions
include the setting up, amending and cancelling of payments, standing orders and regular
transfers, as well as applying for or amending an existing overdraft.Some facilities are
not available on all types of account, please check individual accounts for facilities
offered.<br>
<br>
<b>Is Abbey National an Internet Service Provider?</b><br>
Abbey National is not currently an Internet Service Provider.<br>
<br>
<b>How secure is Abbey National's e-banking?</b><br>
At Abbey National we take security very seriously and place the highest of priorities on
protecting customer information. The identity of customers using e-banking is confirmed
through the dual use of e-banking passcodes and registration numbers, which have been
designed in accordance with financial industry standards and best practice. All
information passed between Abbey National and customers using e-banking is sent using
strong industry standard encryption technology. What's more, we will cover any losses from
e-banking fraud - guaranteed. Just as long as you have taken the necessary safeguards to
keep your passcode and registration number secret and haven't deliberately involved
yourself in fraud. We recommend that you update your browser's cipher strength to 128bit
SSL. This could prevent any problems caused when viewing secure sites. If you have any
questions about this, please contact our e-banking helpdesk on <span class="bodytext"><b>0845 600 4388</b></span> <span class="bodytext">Lines are open 7 days a week, 7.00am to 11.00pm. (Dial + 44 161 9516659 from abroad.)</span>.<br>
<br>
<b>Can I download information from Abbey National's e-banking into applications such as
Microsoft Money?</b><br>
At present our e-banking service does not support this. However, we will be continually
updating e-banking and we will let you know when extra services are introduced.<br>
<br>
<b>How long will it take to process my transaction request?</b><br>
You are told at the end of each transaction when it will be processed by Abbey National<br>
