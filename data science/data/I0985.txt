        <tr>
          <td valign="top" width="100%" colspan="3" class="engfanz">
            <p>World Cup warm up time and England started with a game against 
              co-hosts South Korea in the impressive Seogwipo Stadium in South 
              Korea. England started the game well are were on top for most of 
              the first 45 minutes and lead through a 26th minute goal from skipper 
              Michael Owen, the Liverpool striker following in to scramble the 
              ball home after Paul Scholes effort was saved. As appear to be the 
              norm in friendlys now Sven Goran Eriksson made six changes at half-time 
              and England were very poor second half. South Korea drew level from 
              a close range header from Park Ji-Sung on 52 minutes. England came 
              through the game with no injuries.&quot;</p>
