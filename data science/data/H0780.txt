<table>

    <td valign="middle"> 
      <p><font face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">There is still controversy 
        over speciation and pathogenicity of Blastocystis hominis. </font></p>
      <p><font face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif"><br>
        These organisms are now considered to be a protozoan. </font></p>
      <p>&nbsp;</p>

      <p><i><font face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">B. hominis</font></i><font face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif"> 
        in formalin-fixed wet mount typically shows a spherical cyst-like stage 
        ranging in size from 6-35 
<font face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif"></font><font face="Symbol">&#109;</font><font face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">m</font>. </font></p>
      <p><font face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">They contain a large 
        central body (resembling a vacuole) with a narrow rim of cytoplasm that 
        contains nuclei. </font></p>
      <p><font face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">They tend to be refractile. 
        </font></p>
