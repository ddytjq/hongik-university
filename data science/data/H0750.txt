<center><b><font color="#FF0000"><font size=+1>Natural Selection Observed

<br>from <a href="EvoEvidence.html" target="_top">The Evolution Evidence
Page</a></center>

<p>&nbsp;<a href="http://www.pnas.org/cgi/content/full/98/16/9157">Strength
and tempo of directional selection in the wild&nbsp;</a>&nbsp; (Proceedings
of the National Academy of Sciences, USA, Vol. 98, Issue 16, 9157-9160,
July 31, 2001) - This article documents <b>993</b> measurements of <a href="http://www.ultranet.com/~jkimball/BiologyPages/E/Evolution.html#DirectionalSelection">directional
selection</a> in the wild.&nbsp; The authors have provided a database of
instances of natural selection in the wild, as reported in Kingsolver et
al (2001) The American Naturalist 157(3): 245-261: <a href="http://www.bio.unc.edu/faculty/kingsolver/lab/Kingsolveretal.SelnDatabase.norepdur.3.2001.xls">http://www.bio.unc.edu/faculty/kingsolver/lab/Kingsolveretal.SelnDatabase.norepdur.3.2001.xls</a>
. Note that these studies all deal with <a href="http://www.ultranet.com/~jkimball/BiologyPages/E/Evolution.html#DirectionalSelection">directional
selection</a>, not purifying or <a href="http://www.ultranet.com/~jkimball/BiologyPages/E/Evolution.html#StabilizingSelection">stabilizing
natural selection,</a> which creationists sometimes falsely claim is the
only kind of natural selection that occurs in nature.
<p>John Endler's classic book "<a href="http://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/0691083878/qid=999011437/sr=1-1/ref=sc_b_1/102-0222640-9274541">Natural
Selection in the Wild</a>" (1986, published by Princeton Univ Pr; Call
Number: QH375 .E53 1986 ; ISBN: 0691083878 )&nbsp; can be found in most
university libraries and is availiable from Amazon.com.
<p><a href="http://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/019512183X/qid=999011116/sr=1-1/ref=sc_b_1/102-0222640-9274541">"Adaptive
Genetic Variation in the Wild"</a> (1999, published by Oxford University
Press; Call Number: QH401 .A395 2000 ; ISBN: 019512183X) by Timothy A.
Mousseau (Editor), Barry Sinervo (Editor), John A. Endler is also recommended.
<p>"<a href="http://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/019506352X/qid=999012710/sr=1-1/ref=sc_b_1/102-0222640-9274541">Selection
in Natural Populations</a>" by Jeffry B. Mitton.(1997, published by Oxford
University Press, Call Number: QH371 .M68 1997 ; ISBN: 019506352X)
