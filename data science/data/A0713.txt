      <p><b><a name="internationalpayments"></a>International Payments<br>
        <br>
        </b>In the business of exporting or importing, making and receiving international 
        payments is crucial to the success of your company. We use up to date 
        international payment facilities to enable your payments to reach their 
        destination on time and right first time. International payments can be 
        made using: </p>
      <ul>
        <li>Electronic payments via SWIFT (Society for World-wide Inter Bank Financial 
          Communications Telecommunications) technology </li>
        <li>Telex </li>
        <li>Foreign drafts </li>
        <li>Irish Pound and Euro cheques</li>
        <li> Electronic payments via ServiceLink - our Windows™ based electronic 
          banking technology </li>
      </ul>
      <p><b><a name="documentaryproducts"></a>Documentary Products<br>
        <br>
        </b>As emerging markets become more lucrative for corporate business, 
        there is increasing demand for documentary products to help offset global, 
        financial and country risk. Our extensive expertise in this area will 
        provide you with appropriate financing mechanisms for your trade activities. 
      </p>
      <ul>
        <li>48 hour turnaround time</li>
        <li>Documentary collections</li>
        <li>Documentary letters of credit</li>
        <li>Confirmed letters of credit</li>
        <li>Cheque collections</li>
      </ul>
      <p align="right"><a class="content" style="text-decoration:none;" href="#top">top 
        ^</a></p>
      <p><b><a name="foreigncurrencyaccounts"></a>Foreign Currency Accounts<br>
        <br>
        </b>Managing the exchange rate risk can be a concern for any business 
        involved in international trade. We can help you to minimise that risk. 
        Open a foreign currency account in any major currency and see the results 
        when sales and purchases are made in the same currency.</p>
      <ul>
        <li>Available in all major currencies</li>
        <li>May be deposit or current accounts</li>
        <li> Balance and transaction information can be accessed via ServiceLink</li>
        <li> Irish Pound cheque books and foreign drafts are available</li>
      </ul>
      <p><b><a name="foreigncurrencyloans"></a>Foreign Currency Loans<br>
        <br>
        </b>Cost-effective Currency loans combine the possibility of lower interest 
        rates and the elimination of the exchange rate risk, where income is the 
        currency borrowed. Fixed or variable interest rate Overdrafts are available 
        in all major currencies for extra flexibility on international business 
        transactions.</p>
      <p align="right"><a class="content" style="text-decoration:none;" href="#top">top 
        ^</a></p>
      <p><b><a name="tradefinanceexportfinance"></a>Trade Finance-Export Finance<br>
        <br>
        </b>As International deals become more sophisticated and trade boundaries 
        widen into unchartered territory, so too does our expertise. Our latest 
        range of Trade Finance services will help you meet these challenges, maximise 
        your business potential and assist you in maintaining a steady cashflow.</p>
      <ul>
        <li>Bill Discounting<b> </b>- At the request of the exporter, the Bank 
          can discount Bills of Exchange to help enhance cash flow.</li>
        <li>Finance under Export Letter of Credit - Exporters who trade under 
          the term Letter of Credit can avail of our discounting facilities thus 
          enhancing cash flow.</li>
        <li>Discounting on Export Credit Insurance Facilities - If you have trade 
          debts insured, you may avail of a discounting facility on these debts 
          in conjunction with Export Credit Insurance policies.</li>
        <li>Forfaiting - Available for exporters of capital or semi-capital goods, 
          this short to medium term finance may be provided by the Bank of deferred 
          trade debt - usually by way of Bills of Exchange or Promissory Notes.</li>
      </ul>
      <p><b><a name="bondsandguarantees"></a>Bonds and Guarantees<br>
        <br>
        </b>In certain countries, companies are required to provide Bonds and 
        Guarantees when tendering for contracts. We can provide you with a range 
        of Bonds and Guarantees, including Performance Guarantees for contracts 
        already awarded.</p>
      <p><b><a name="internationalliaisondesk"></a>International Liaison Desk<br>
        <br>
        </b>Offering personal and corporate customers access to banking facilities 
        and information from anywhere in the world.</p>
      <ul>
        <li>Advice on overseas banking practice (local products, clearing cycles, 
          cut-off times, etc).</li>
        <li>Information on international bank tariffs.</li>
        <li>Assistance in opening accounts with subsidiaries of the Ulster Bank 
          and The Royal Bank of Scotland Group and other correspondent banks worldwide.</li>
        <li>Advice on Trade Products</li>
      </ul>
      <p align="right"><a class="content" style="text-decoration:none;" href="#top">top 
        ^</a></p>
      <p><b><a name="electronicbanking"></a>Electronic Banking Services<br>
        <br>
        </b>Ulster Bank customers can avail of a range of electronic banking services, 
        internationally renowned. Using ServiceLink, our Windows TM electronic 
        banking package you can: </p>
      <ul>
        <li>Have access to currency accounts, currency loans and overdraft balances 
          and transactions on screen</li>
        <li>Transfer funds from currency accounts into interest bearing deposit 
          accounts</li>
        <li>Make third party payments in Sterling or any foreign currency.</li>
        <li>Have access to Treasury services and daily market commentary including 
          foreign exchange rates (Spot and Forward), and Sterling deposit rates.</li>
      </ul>
      <p>For further information on ServiceLink, view our <a class="content" href="/ni/business/businessecommerce.asp">Business 
        eCommerce</a> page. </p>
      <p><b>Contact us!</b></p>
      <p>Whatever your business, wherever you are, it could be well be worth talking 
        to us.</p>

        McConkey</a> at Ulster Bank Markets - International Trade Services for 
        a confidential quotation for any part of your international banking business. 
      </p>
