	 <P><FONT FACE="Verdana" SIZE="1"><U>englandfans</U><BR><BR> On 2nd July
		2001, the Football Association launched their new official England supporters
		club: englandfans. Below are brief details.</FONT></P> 
	 <P><FONT FACE="Verdana" SIZE="1">Joining englandfans is the only way that
		match tickets for overseas games can be bought. Tickets for home games can be
		bought by anyone - whether members of englandfans or otherwise, but englandfans
		members that have travelled to an away game get priority. <BR>Here is the FA's
		quick overview of how it works and some of the benefits:</FONT></P> 
	 <UL> 
		<LI><FONT FACE="Verdana" SIZE="1">englandfans is the only way you can buy
		  tickets for away games - but you're guaranteed home tickets if you do go to
		  away games</FONT></LI> 
		<LI><FONT FACE="Verdana" SIZE="1">A new vetting process which supports
		  genuine fans</FONT></LI> 
		<LI><FONT FACE="Verdana" SIZE="1">Closer links with travel operators,
		  who'll guarantee a certain standard of service</FONT></LI> 
		<LI><FONT FACE="Verdana" SIZE="1">Special members' website with advanced
		  ticket and travel news, and a chat forum</FONT></LI> 
		<LI><FONT FACE="Verdana" SIZE="1">Home tickets will be on general
		  sale</FONT></LI> 
	 </UL> 
	 <P><FONT FACE="Verdana" SIZE="1"><U>Cost of membership</U><BR><BR>&pound;20
		a year for adults, &pound;25 for adults who live overseas and &pound;10 for
		under 16s. Membership runs until 30th June 2002, or immediately after the end
		of the World Cup if you attend.</FONT></P>
