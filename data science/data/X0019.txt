<h2><a name="LeagueDefs"><b>Leagues Referenced</b></a></h2>

<h3><b><i>Leagues officially recognized as &quot;Major
Leagues&quot;</i></b></h3>

<ul>
    <li><b>AA</b> - American Association 1882-1884, 1887-1891 </li>
    <li><b>AL</b> - American League 1901-&gt; </li>
    <li><b>FL</b> - Federal League 1914-1915 </li>
    <li><b>NA</b> - National Association of Professional Baseball
        Players 1871-1875, 1879-1880 </li>
    <li><b>NL</b> - National League 1876-&gt; </li>
    <li><b>PL</b> - Players League 1890 </li>
    <li><b>UA</b> - Union Association 1884 </li>
</ul>

<h3><b><i>Leagues included just because I wanted to!</i></b></h3>

<ul>
    <li><b>AAGPBL</b> - All American Girls Professional Baseball
        League 1943-1954 </li>
    <li><b>ANL</b> - American Negro League 1929 </li>
    <li><b>ECL</b> - Eastern Colored League 1923-1928 </li>
    <li><b>NAL</b> - Negro American League 1937-1950 </li>
    <li><b>NEWL</b> - Negro East West League 1932 </li>
    <li><b>NNL</b> - Negro National League 1920-1948 </li>
    <li><b>NSL</b> - Negro Southern League 1932 </li>
    <li><b>CL</b> - Continental League 1960 (Announced but never
        played) </li>
    <li><b>UBL</b> - United Baseball League 1996?-&gt;
        (Announced) </li>
    <li><b>SPBA</b> - Senior Professional Baseball Association
        1989-1990 </li>
    <li><b>WA</b> - Western Association 1894-1900 (became the
        American League 1901) </li>
    <li><b>WBL</b> - Women's Baseball League 1994-&gt; </li>
</ul>
