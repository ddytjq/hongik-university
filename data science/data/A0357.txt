<h1>The Answers</h1>
<h2>Cash Cards</h2>
<a name="ccardsoverdrawn">&nbsp;</a>
<p class="faqquestion">Q. Is there any chance that I will go overdrawn?</p>
<p class="faqanswer">A. No, you can rest assure that with a U18 Cashcard Account 
  you will not go overdrawn. If you are over 16 year's old you can apply for a 
  Visa Electron on-line debit card. The Visa Electron card automatically checks 
  your balance each time you use it which prevents you from spending more than 
  you have in your account.</p>
<p><a name="ccardsdifference">&nbsp;</a></p>
<p class="faqquestion">Q. What's the difference between a savings account and 
  a bank account?</p>
<p class="faqanswer">A. Anyone in receipt of a regular income - pocket money, 
  regular part-time or temporary work pay or simply birthday or Christmas cash 
  presents is likely to find a bank "current" account the safest and most practical 
  way of handling and moving everyday money. You will still be able to earn interest 
  on the money in your account and you'll also have instant access to your cash 
  should you need it at over 35,000 cash machines and 24-hour telephone banking.</p>
<a name="ccardsiamunder16">&nbsp;</a>
<p class="faqquestion">Q. I am under 16 - where can I use my U18 Cashcard?</p>
<p class="faqanswer">A. If you are under 16 you will receive a Cashcard which 
  can be used to withdraw cash at any cash machine.</p>

<a name="ccardscashmachines1">&nbsp;</a>
<p class="faqquestion">Q. Using cash machines</p>
<p class="faqanswer">A. There are over 35,000 cash machines around the country 
  where you can take out up to £250 a day in cash, 24 hours a day (provided you 
  have the money in your account).</p>

<a name="ccardsabroad1">&nbsp;</a>
<p class="faqquestion">Q. Using your card abroad</p>
<p class="faqanswer">A. Your Cashcard lets you withdraw local currency when you're 
  abroad, wherever you see cash machines with the PLUS sign*.</p>
<p><a href="#"><img src="/media/images/but-backtotop.gif" width="120" height="20" border="0" /></a></p>
<a name="ccardsbranches1">&nbsp;</a>
<p class="faqquestion">Q. Cash from Alliance & Leicester branches</p>
<p class="faqanswer">A. You may withdraw up to £250 a day in cash over the counter 
  at any of our branches, provided that the funds are available in your account.</p>

<a name="ccardsotherservices">&nbsp;</a>
<p class="faqquestion">Q. Other services from Alliance & Leicester cash machines</p>
<p class="faqanswer">A. As well as withdrawing cash, you can also use most Alliance 
  & Leicester cash machines for the following:</p>
<ul>
<li>	Paying in cash and cheques</li>
<li>	Checking your balance</li>
<li>	Requesting a mini statement showing your last 10 transactions</li>
<li>	Changing your PIN to one of your own choice</li>
</ul>

<a name="ccardswherecani">&nbsp;</a>
<p class="faqquestion">Q. I am over 16 - where can I use my U18 Cashcard</p>
<p class="faqanswer">A. If you are 16 or over you will receive a Visa Electron 
  card. This can be used to withdraw cash at any cash machine or to make debit 
  payments where you see the Visa Electron sign.</p>  

<a name="ccardscashmachines">&nbsp;</a>
<p class="faqquestion">Q. Using cash machines</p>
<p class="faqanswer">A. There are over 35,000 cash machines around the country 
  where you can take out up to £250 a day in cash, 24 hours a day (provided you 
  have the money in your account).</p>

<a name="ccardsmealsorother">&nbsp;</a>
<p class="faqquestion">Q. To pay for shopping, meals or other purchases.</p>
<p class="faqanswer">A. The Visa Electron card can be used in around 360,000 shops, 
  stores and restaurants in the UK and almost 7.5 million outlets worldwide. It's 
  an electronic debit card that automatically checks your balance each time you 
  use it. (This saves you from accidentally spending more than you have in your 
  account). Simply hand over your card, check the amount on the sales slip, sign 
  it, and the amount is debited from your account. Details of the transaction 
  appear on your statement.</p>
<p><a href="#"><img src="/media/images/but-backtotop.gif" width="120" height="20" border="0" /></a></p>
<a name="ccardsabroad">&nbsp;</a>
<p class="faqquestion">Q. Using your card abroad</p>
<p class="faqanswer">A. Your Cashcard lets you withdraw local currency when you're 
  abroad, wherever you see cash machines with the PLUS sign*.</p>

<a name="ccardsbranches">&nbsp;</a>
<p class="faqquestion">Q. Cash from Alliance & Leicester branches</p>
<p class="faqanswer">A. You may withdraw up to £250 a day in cash over the counter 
  at any of our branches, provided that the funds are available in your account.</p>

<a name="ccardscashmachines">&nbsp;</a>
<p class="faqquestion">Q. Other services from Alliance & Leicester cash machines</p>
<p class="faqanswer">A. As well as withdrawing cash, you can also use most Alliance 
  & Leicester cash machines for the following transactions: 
<ul>
<li>	Paying in cash and cheques</li>
<li>	Checking your balance</li>
<li>	Requesting a mini statement showing your last 10 transactions</li>
<li>	Changing your PIN to one of your own choice</li>
</ul>

<a name="ccardsfreemagazine">&nbsp;</a>
<p class="faqquestion">Q. Once I have applied for the U18 Cashcard Account how 
  do I claim my free magazine subscriptions?</p>
<p class="faqanswer">A. You will receive a magazine subscription claim form in 
  your welcome pack. All you need to do is fill in the form, indicating which 
  magazine you would like, and pop it in the post. After that, we'll make sure 
  you get your magazines absolutely free.</p>

<p>*When used abroad, there is a conversion fee of 2.75% of the value of the transaction. 
  Cash withdrawals made at Visa machines which are not part of the LINK network 
  or VISA bank branches at home or abroad are subject to a 1.5% (min £1.50) charge 
  in addition to the conversion fee.</p>

<h3>Alliance Current Account</h3>

<a name="ca50welcomegift">&nbsp;</a>
<p class="faqquestion">Q. When do I receive my £50 Welcome Gift in my account?</p>
<p class="faqanswer">A. To qualify for the £50 Welcome Gift you must credit your 
  new account with a minimum of £500 for three consecutive months. You will also 
  need to complete and return the £50 Welcome Gift claim form (which is contained 
  in the Welcome Pack). The £50 will then be credited into your account within 
  28 days of the third consecutive months' salary being cleared into the account. 
  This offer is subject to terms and conditions, detailed in the <a href="/currentaccounts/index.asp?page=current-account">Current 
  Account</a> section. </p> 

<a name="caoverdraft">&nbsp;</a>
<p class="faqquestion">Q. How do I apply for an overdraft?</p>
<p class="faqanswer">A. On the application form you will be asked if you wish 
  to be considered for an overdraft on your account. If you tick yes you will 
  be notified of your agreed overdraft limit in your Welcome Pack.</p>

<h2>Terms & Conditions </h2>

<a name="tcmonthlycurrentaccountcharge">&nbsp;</a>
<p class="faqquestion">Q. Will there be a monthly current account charge on the 
  Premier Current Account?</p>
<p class="faqanswer">A. No. Unlike some other banks, we won't charge you a monthly 
  fee for running your account. And we offer you free banking if you remain in 
  credit, so you won't be charged for everyday transactions.</p>

<a name="tcavailabletome">&nbsp;</a>
<p class="faqquestion">Q. Which other Alliance & Leicester financial services 
  will be available to me at a preferential rate?</p>
<p class="faqanswer">A. As an Premier Current Account customer you'll be able 
  to enjoy access to a range of preferential offers on our other financial services. 
  These offers aren't limited promotions, but long-term offers and are available 
  on the following products:</p>
<ul>
<li>	MoneyBack Gold Credit Card</li>
<li>	PhoneSave Premier Account</li>
<li>	Personal loans</li>
<li>	Mortgages</li>
<li>	Motor Insurance</li>
<li>	Buildings and Contents Insurance</li>
</ul>

<a name="tc50welcome">&nbsp;</a>
<p class="faqquestion">Q. After opening a Premier Current Account when do I receive 
  my £50 Welcome Gift?</p>
<p class="faqanswer">A. The £50 Welcome Gift will be credited automatically to 
  your linked PhoneSave Premier account within 28 days of your first salary reaching 
  your Premier Current Account provided this happens within 3 months of the Account 
  being opened. This offer is subject to terms and conditions, detailed in the 
  <a href="/currentaccounts/index.asp?page=current-account">Current Account</a> 
  section. </p>
