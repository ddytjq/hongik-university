<p>
<nobr>Microsoft Visual C++® .NET</nobr> Standard Edition is available for the hobbyist, student, or beginner programmer looking to learn the Visual C++ 
language by constructing applications for Microsoft Windows® and the Web. 
</p>
<p>
Professional C++ developers looking to construct complex data-driven or server-based solutions, including XML Web services, should get 
<a href="/vstudio/howtobuy/choosing.asp"><nobr>Visual Studio .NET</nobr></a>, available in Professional, Enterprise Developer, and Enterprise Architect editions.  
</p>
<p>
Compare the following features to see which edition best meets your development needs.
