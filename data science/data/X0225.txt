<B><I>Would you like your tournaments to have more of an international flavor, or just simply to give them more exposure?</I></B>
<BR><BR>
This web site is dedicated to promoting tournament participation between teams from all
over the world. The objective is to help bring together those with a common 
desire - to play field hockey with athletes from around the globe and to have 
fun doing it.
<BR><BR>
<A HREF="#search"><B>Search for Events</B></A> - Our searchable listing of tournaments and festivals will give your event worldwide 
exposure and help you achieve your goal of international participation.<BR>
<FONT FACE="verdana,helvetica" SIZE="-2">
<B>Note</B>: FIH tournament information is currently entered by FHT. The information shown is as accurate as possible and is based on information we gather from them.  Dates & venues often change and because they do not keep us posted on those changes, finding out about them is quite a task and time consuming, resulting in our falling behind in updating our database.  Therefore, we recommend that you always double check dates/venues with them.
<BR><BR>
<FONT FACE="verdana,helvetica" SIZE="-1">
<A HREF="register.html"><B>Submit Your Event</B></A> for inclusion in our database,  and 
teams from across town or from other countries can then contact your club to inquire 
about participation. To date, <b>over 900 tournaments</b> have already been entered.
<BR><BR>
Visit our <A HREF="links.html"><B>Links</B></A> page.  With over 400 links and still growing, you will surely find another hockey site you'd like to visit!  This page also includes phone/fax numbers &amp email addresses of hockey clubs around the world.
