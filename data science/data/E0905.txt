<h2>Contributors to GCC</h2>

<p>The GCC project would like to thank its many contributors.  Without them the
project would not have been nearly as successful as it has been.  Any omissions
in this list are accidental.  Feel free to contact
<a href="mailto:law@redhat.com">law@redhat.com</a> if you have been left out
or some of your contributions are not listed.  Please keep this list in
alphabetical order.

<p>Some projects operating under the GCC project maintain their own list
of contributors, such as
<a href="http://gcc.gnu.org/libstdc++/">the C++ library</a>.
