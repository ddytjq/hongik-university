<p><font face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">The National Centre for 
  Mycology was established at the Provincial Laboratory of Public Health for Northern 
  Alberta in Edmonton in April, 1983 under the auspices of Health and Welfare 
  Canada and operates in conjunction with the Laboratory Centre for Disease Control 
  in Ottawa. </font>
<p><font face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">The Centre provides a variety 
  of diagnostic tests, both routine and esoteric testing, which are not performed 
  at all provincial health laboratories and also provides consultation, continuing 
  education and research. </font></p>
<p><font face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif"><b>Services:</b></font></p>
<ul>
  <li><font face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">Isolation and identification 
    of causative agents of cutaneous, subcutaneous and systemic mycoses </font>
  <li><font face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">Examination of histopathological 
    preparations of infected tissue </font>
  <li><font face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">Exoantigen tests to confirm 
    identification of agents associated with infections in immunocompromised hosts 
    </font>
  <li><font face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">DNA probes to confirm 
    the identity of <i>Coccidioides immitis, Blastomyces dermatitidis</i>, and 
    <i>Histoplasma capsulatum</i> </font>
  <li><font face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">Serological diagnosis 
    </font>
  <li><font face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">Susceptibility testing 
    of yeast and moulds according to NCCLS established or developing methods </font>
</ul>
<p><font face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif"><b>Research Programs:</b></font></p>
<ul>
  <li><font face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">Correlation of clinical 
    outcomes of patients treated with antifungal agents and susceptibility test 
    results </font>
  <li><font face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">Evaluation of commercial 
    panels for yeast susceptibility testing </font>
  <li><font face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">Preparation of in-house 
    antisera to fungal agents, especially to develop agents to perform serology 
    for <i>Fusarium</i> infections </font>
  <li><font face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">Evaluation and standardization 
    of methodologies for susceptibility testing of <i>Nocardia</i> species </font>
  <li><font face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">Evaluation of advanced 
    identification methodologies </font>
</ul>
<p><font face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif"><b>Continuing Education 
  Programs:</b></font></p>
<ul>
  <li><font face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">In keeping with the public 
    health mandate, the Centre is committed to continuing education programs</font>
</ul>
