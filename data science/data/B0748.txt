      <p class="blocktext"><span class="blocktext"><b>Specialist Reports</b></span><b><span class="blocktext"><br>
        </span></b><span class="blocktext">From time to time a Valuer may recommend 
        specialist reports such as:</span></p>
      <ul>
        <li class="lefttext11">Structural Engineers Report</li>
        <li class="lefttext11">Timber/Damp Proof Report</li>
        <li class="lefttext11">Electrical Contractors Report on Wiring</li>
        <li class="lefttext11">Roofing Contractors Report</li>
      </ul>
      <p class="blocktext">The need for the reports will be highlighted in the 
        Valuation Report.<br>
        Whilst the reports will incur additional costs, they are requested as 
        protection for both yourself and the Society against possible defects 
        in the property, before you exchange contracts and inherit problems not 
        of your own making.<br>
        Costs of works are taken into account by the Society and a decision will 
        be taken to either:-</p>
      <ul>
        <li class="centretext11">
          <div align="left"><span class="lefttext11">make a retention (hold back 
            some of the advance funds until evidence is presented by you that 
            the work has been satisfactorily completed)</span></div>
        </li>
        <li class="lefttext11">
          <div align="left"> request an undertaking (that you will undertake to 
            complete the works within a period of time, say 3 to 6 months)</div>
        </li>
      </ul>
      <p class="blocktext" align="left">These reports may also enable you to negotiate 
        a price reduction with the vendor.</p>
      <p class="blocktext">&nbsp;</p>

            <p><span class="blocktext"><b>The Structural Survey </b></span><b><span class="blocktext"></span></b><span class="blocktext"><br>
              This type of survey is carried out at your request and is used to 
              ensure that the property is structurally sound. As these reports 
              do not give either the value of the property or the insurance replacement 
              value, they should be read in conjunction with either a Valuation 
              Report or a Homebuyers Report. <br>
              Fees will vary according to the age, size and type of property but 
              will be more expensive than the Report and Valuation for Homebuyers.</span></p>
