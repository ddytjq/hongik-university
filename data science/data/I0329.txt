<P><Font Size = 3><b>World Cup 2002 UEFA Qualifying<br><br>13 or 14 teams to qualify + France</b><br>
The UEFA qualifying competition consists of four groups of five teams and five
groups of six teams, playing a home-and-away round robin. The nine group
winners qualify directly for the final competition.<br><br>

<b>Play-offs UEFA/AFC</b><br>
The nine runners-up of UEFA will be put into one pot. Four European play-off pairings
(home and away) will be drawn. The winners qualify for the final competition. The
remaining European runner-up will contest a home-and-away play-off against the
third-placed AFC team. That winner will also qualify for the final competition.</font><br><br>
