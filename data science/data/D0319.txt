      <h1><font face="Arial Narrow">Upcoming Conferences/Workshops</font></h1>
      <p>This page lists upcoming conferences and workshops for the year 2000 
        and beyond. If you want to see what happened at the very end of the 1900's, 
        please scroll down.</p>
      <hr>
      <p>&nbsp;</p>
      <h2><font face="Arial Narrow" size="5">PA EXPO 2000 Final Call for Papers</font> 
      </h2>
      <p><a href="http://www.practical-applications.co.uk/Expo2000/">http://www.practical-applications.co.uk/Expo2000/</a> 
      </p>
      <p><b>Submission Deadline</b> : December 6th 1999 </p>
      <p>The principal aim of PA EXPO 2000 is to bring together researchers, developers 
        and business users to examine and discuss the organizational and technical 
        challenges posed by various emerging technologies. PA EXPO provides a 
        unique opportunity for professionals involved in various parts of the 
        IT industry to meet, discuss and share ideas. PA EXPO 2000 will be held 
        from the 10th - 14th April 2000 at the Crowne Plaza Midland Hotel, Manchester, 
        UK.</p>
