<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>WALL TOWNSHIP, N.J.&nbsp;-- Terrence Roberts took a bit of a break this week in between his recent USA Basketball trip to Colorado Springs and the upcoming ABCD Camp up in Hackensack.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<?xml:namespace prefix = o ns = "urn:schemas-microsoft-com:office:office" /><o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>So where was he Thursday afternoon? In a gym here on the Jersey Shore, of course, working as a counselor, refereeing games and playing pickup at the Bob Hurley Basketball Camp.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>Some vacation for the 6-foot-8 power forward from Hurley's Jersey City juggernaut at St. Anthony High School.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>"I've been here since Monday," Roberts said during a brief break at the Atlantic Club. "The next few days, I'm going to chill out at home and maybe go to a couple open gyms, and then I'll go over to ABCD on Sunday."</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>The adidas All-American camp&nbsp;-- just like Nike's counterpart this week in Indianapolis and travel team tournaments all over the country throughout the coming month&nbsp;-- yields big talents and new names. Each and every year.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>And don't be surprised if Roberts emerges as one of this summer's biggest stock-boosters.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>"He doesn't quite have the body to bang yet," the legendary Hurley said. "But he's very, very quick, and he has great athleticism and agility."</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>Which is why veteran talent evaluator Brick Oettinger has Roberts at No. 46 in his pre-summer national rankings in the Prep Stars Recruiter's Handbook.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>And why national recruiting expert Dave Telep puts him at No. 43 in his mid-summer list on TheInsidersHoops.com.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>"To get where he is now has been a great leap," Telep said. "His ceiling is really high&nbsp;-- especially considering he was a complete unknown a year ago."</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>Roberts missed his entire sophomore season while rehabbing an injured knee. He sat on the sidelines and watched his teammates win New Jersey's Tournament of Champions state crown.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>As a junior, he averaged 11 points and three blocks a game in helping the Friars to a 29-1 record, another TOC title and the second slot in USA Today's national boys basketball poll. But he played a secondary role on a backcourt-oriented squad.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>Three Division I guards&nbsp;-- St. John's-bound McDonald's All-American Elijah Ingram, St. Joe's signee Dwayne Lee and Seton Hall pledge Donald Copeland&nbsp;-- dominated the scoring.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>"But you could always see that something was there," said Danny Hurley, Bob's son, the coach of his own up-and-coming prep power at St. Benedict's in Newark.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>"He was raw as a freshman&nbsp;-- but long and athletic. And those are the guys that can project if they're in a good program."</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>Obviously, Roberts is getting good training under the elder Hurley's tutelage, and he joined coach Jimmy Salmon's talent-packed Tim Thomas Playaz and pieced together a solid spring on the grassroots circuit.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>Syracuse, Florida, Villanova, UConn, Illinois and Maryland started recruiting him even before the spring, and Roberts' performances of late have done nothing to change the lofty level of his recruitment.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>"I think Terrence is still developing as a prospect," Telep said. "To take the next step, though, he's got to do it consistently and announce his presence.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>"A little bit of consistency will go a long way with him. He does some things so well that you want him to do them more of the time."</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>Who knows? That might happen this week at ABCD. Or the week after that in Hempstead, N.Y., at the adidas Three Stripes Classic. Or later this month in Las Vegas at the Big Time.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>Somewhere it'll happen.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>Just watch. Somewhere -- whether it be North Jersey, Long Island or Vegas&nbsp;-- Roberts'll pick a specific venue and strut his stuff.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>And he'll show exactly what he is&nbsp;-- a mobile power forward with plenty of face-up skills. He'll become a bona fide big-time talent in the Class of 2003.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>"I can turn and face the basket," Roberts said, "and I can get out and run. I'm just looking to work on my outside game and my mid-range, and I want to progress on my inside game, too."</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>And then carry this summer into next year at St. Anthony&nbsp;-- his year.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>"We'd really like to increase his role," Hurley said. "It's going to take us a while to learn how to get him the kind of touches he got last year&nbsp;-- defenses were so occupied with the perimeter last season&nbsp;-- and he's going to have to make quicker moves in the post before the double teams come.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>"Once the guards improve, though, his numbers should double."</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>As will Roberts' rep.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<H1 style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>TOUGH-AS-NAILS NARDI</FONT></H1>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>Mike Nardi wasn't built to sit on any bench. So imagine how frustrating this past year must have been for the gritty kid from Jersey.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>The 6-1 point guard from perennially powerful St. Patrick High School broke a bone in his foot in December. He returned in February for two games&nbsp;-- only to miss the rest of the season with a bruised bone in his big toe.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>"I really couldn't do anything," Nardi said earlier this week, "except ride the stationary bike and lift weights."</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>So that's what he did. And he returned this spring on the summer circuit stronger&nbsp;-- physically and mentally&nbsp;-- even if he was a little rusty.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>But he was also a bit more seasoned. A bit more crafty. A bit better.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>He began to get back into a rhythm in Charlottesville, Va., in mid-May with super swingman Luol Deng and the Jersey Demons.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>He was better yet on North Carolina's Tobacco Road with the New Jersey Celtics&nbsp;-- the off-season version of St. Pat's&nbsp;-- during the Memorial Day Tournament of Champions.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>He led St. Pat's to the Metro Classic championship last month at Seton Hall and therefore helped the Celtics earn one of the two East Coast berths in the first annual And1 High School National Championship later this month at the Palestra in Philadelphia.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>And he capped his comeback last week in Colorado Springs, Colo., playing for the East Team and averaging 12.3 points, 4.3 assists and three rebounds an outing&nbsp;-- not bad at all for a group that also featured rising junior playmaker Sebastian Telfair and Wake Forest commitment Chris Paul.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>"It took him a while," St. Pat's coach Kevin Boyle said of his star's recovery process. "He had the ability to shoot the ball, but he couldn't stop and go and change directions for a couple months, and a lot of basketball is cutting."</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>Especially Mike Nardi basketball.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>The Steve Blake look- and play-a-like dictates tempo. He pushes the ball. He makes jumpers. He gets passes to teammates at the right spots at the right times. What he does, really, is play the point as if he were meant to do nothing else in the whole wide world.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>"He's a pure point guard who sees the floor, and he's always taken on a strong leadership role," NJHoops.com's Jay Gomes said. "He'll make the spectacular pass as well as the old-school bounce pass on the break."</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>And much, much more.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>"He's a blue-collar kid," PrepStars.com guru Rob Harrington said. "He doesn't stand out in warm-ups, for sure, but he's a better athlete than people think. He's the type of guy that grows on you."</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>He's certainly grown on a number of high-major suitors on the East Coast. N.C. State is on top of a current list that also includes UConn, Villanova, Pittsburgh, Rutgers, Seton Hall and Florida State.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>None of his professed top three choices&nbsp;-- State, Pitt and UConn&nbsp;-- have offered a scholarship at this point heading into the pair of 10-day open recruiting periods in July.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>Due to his injuries, none of the three head coaches have seen Nardi this year, which makes his play at this week's Nike All-America Camp and throughout the rest of this month all the more important.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>"I'm pretty excited to get out to these events and showcase my play," Nardi said after a workout Wednesday morning in Elizabeth, N.J. "I just have to go out and play."</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>And then collect the inevitable scholarship offers that'll come along&nbsp;-- and go with one of them toward the end of the summer or early in the fall.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>So why Raleigh, N.C., and the Wolfpack for such a Northeastern floor general? Why is State the leader to this point?</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>"Playing time, really," Nardi explained. "It's all about what team has the better situation. I don't necessarily have to start as a freshman, but I'd like to play a significant amount."</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>Of course he would. Because Nardi's had enough of that bench.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<H1 style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>AROUND AND ABOUT</FONT></H1>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>*** Florida picked up a rock-solid commitment Friday morning when in-state banger <B>Chris Richard</B> called <B>Billy Donovan</B> to give the Gators his pledge. The 6-8 power forward from Lakeland Kathleen is a tailor-made big man for today's college game. He's a bit undersized&nbsp;-- that 6-8 is probably a tad generous&nbsp;-- but attacks the glass and uses his mobility and athleticism to get things done on the block and around the basket. "When you're talking about the prototypical 21-century big guy, you're talking about a big guy who can run the floor like a guard," Kathleen coach <B>Drew Taylor</B> said. "And Chris can do that." Richard averaged 16 points, 12 rebounds, six blocks and three assists per game as a junior. He's playing his summer ball right now for <B>John Adkins</B>' Tallahassee Wildcats.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>*** <B>LeBron James</B>&nbsp;-- broken wrist and all&nbsp;-- has to love reading stuff like this. Baton Rouge, La., power forward <B>Brandon Bass</B> told PrepStars.com's <B>Thom Jones</B> the following: "My goal is to be the No. 2 player in the nation. I know that I can't take the No. 1 spot away from LeBron, but I'm reaching for the top five." After being dubbed "The Chosen One" by Sports Illustrated&nbsp;-- not to mention his appearance last month on the cover of Slam&nbsp;-- LeBron's top billing is nothing short of untouchable.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>*** Folks are waiting with baited breath to watch <B>Kendrick Perkins</B> this week at ABCD. And rightfully so: The rising senior big man from Beaumont, Texas, spent the spring&nbsp;-- with the exception of one travel team tourney in Houston in mid-April&nbsp;-- apparently doing day-long workouts near his home. <B>Pete Thamel</B> featured him in the latest ESPN The Magazine. <B>Mike DeCourcy</B> of The Sporting News penned a similar column earlier this week. Which is all dandy, of course, but what about <B>Charlie Villanueva</B>? Everyone's just assuming Perkins is the guy who's going to fill the LeBron hype vacuum. Yet Villanueva&nbsp;-- a 6-9-plus perimeter player from Blair, N.J.&nbsp;-- is a lock to be an NBA player as well.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>*** Many of Kentucky's top high school teams are scheduled to participate in the Derek Smith Shootout July 11-13 at three separate venues in Louisville. Among the top contenders for the mid-summer championship: Lexington Catholic (the defending state champs in the Bluegrass), Lexington Lafayette and Louisville powers Ballard, Male and Eastern. Manual coach <B>David Zuberer</B> is running the show.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>*** St. Pat's and Charlotte Vance open quarterfinal play with a 9:30 a.m. tip July 13 at the inaugural And1 High School National Championship in Philly. The other three quarters: Stone Mountain (Ga.) Redan against Maywood (Ill.) Proviso East; Putnam City (Okla.) High against Los Angeles Loyola; Hyattsville (Md.) DeMatha against Mount Tahoma (Wash.) High. Semis are scheduled for Saturday night. Sunday afternoon's championship game is slated for a Fox Sports telecast. The Hoop Group's <B>Dennis Gregory</B> is the man in charge.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>*** Belmont Shore AAU boss <B>Dinos Trigonis</B> appears to have quite the talented haul set to show at his fifth annual Fullcourt Press All-West Camp July 12-14 in Cypress, Calif., at the Warrior Center. Among the top Californian prospects expected to attend: <B>Mohamed Abukar</B>, the 6-9 Georgia commitment from San Diego; <B>Wesley Washington</B>, a 6-3 shooting guard from Santa Ana; <B>Wendell White</B>, a 6-5 wing from Redondo Beach; <B>Marcus Williams</B>, the Oak Hill-bound lefty point from Los Angeles; <B>Nick Stiggers</B>, a 6-7 prep schooler from Van Nuys; <B>Ray Ray Reed</B>, a 6-1 point guard from Inglewood; and <B>DeMarcus Nelson</B>, the touted Class of 2004 Duke commitment from Vallejo.</FONT></P>
