      <p align="left"><b>Fault Reporting and making a Claim</b></p>
      <p align="left">If you suspect your hardware has failed, we will be able 
        to help you more efficiently if you follow the procedure below:</p>
      <ol>
        <li><b> Try and save your time and money!<br>
          </b>Make sure that: 
          <ol type="a">
            <li> The equipment is plugged in properly, switched on and correctly 
              interconnected with any other equipment </li>
            <li>All controls are properly set </li>
            <li>YThere isn't a software, configuration or data fault </li>
          </ol>
        </li>
        <li> <b>Be ready to quote</b> 
          <ol type="a">
            <li>Your policy number</li>
            <li> The processor serial number </li>
            <li>A description of the fault symptoms</li>
          </ol>
        </li>
        <li><b>Place your fault call with Burnetts Call Control on 023 8044 4345 
          </b></li>
        <li><b>The engineer will then arrange to call on site and carry out the 
          necessary repairs </b></li>
        <li> <b>Sign the Engineers visit report </b><br>
          On completion of the repairs to your satisfaction, sign the engineers 
          visit report, checking that the details of the call are correct.</li>
        <li><b>Retain your copy</b> </li>
        <li> <b>Claims</b> <br>
          The repairer will submit his invoice direct to the administrators for 
          settlement </li>
