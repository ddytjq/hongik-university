          <td valign="top" width="100%" colspan="3"> <b class="newsheading">GERMANY 
            1 ENGLAND 5</b> 
            <p class="engfanz">Every now and then you witness an outstanding England 
              performance - tonight was one of them as they destroyed Germany 
              on their own patch. England were outstanding after going a goal 
              behind.</p>
            <p class="engfanz">England - Germany games are usually tight chess 
              like affairs but the opening half hour of this game was fast and 
              furious with both sides scoring early goals and spurning good chances.</p>
            <p class="engfanz">Germany who many thought would sit back and allow 
              England more of the ball made all the early running and England 
              looked nervous. Germany took the lead on six minutes after poor 
              defending let in Jancker to slot past Seaman. England stunned by 
              the early goal finally kicked into life and Michael Owen drew the 
              score level on 13 minutes. Nick Barmby beat the offside trap and 
              headed the ball perfectly to Owen who finished with ease.</p>
            <p class="engfanz">Daisler missed an absolute sitter on 21 minutes 
              slicing wide with only Seaman to beat. Michael Owen felt he deserved 
              a penalty on 22 minutes after bursting into the German net. England 
              were now playing well with Owen in particular causing problems up 
              front.</p>
            <p class="engfanz">England had a great chance on the half-hour when 
              Kahn picked up a backpass giving England a indirect freekick near 
              the penalty spot. With every German player lined up on the goal 
              line David Beckham's effort was well blocked on the line and Gary 
              Neville ballooned the rebound over the bar.</p>
            <p class="engfanz">Germany were still coming forward and forced four 
              corners in-a-row from a nervous looking England defence. Bohme also 
              went close with a swerving volley that was just wide.</p>
            <p class="engfanz">Both sides looked good coming forward and scrappy 
              at the back as more goals looked likely.</p>
            <p class="engfanz">David Seaman pulled off a brilliant save after 
              a one-two between Jancker and Bohme on the edge of the box produced 
              a skimming shot from Bohme that was darting towards the bottom corner 
              of the net before the Arsenal keeper intervened to push the ball 
              away.</p>
            <p class="engfanz">Just as the referee was about to blow the half-time 
              whistle England were awarded a free-kick out by the corner flag 
              after a foul on Beckham. The England skipper's first effort was 
              cleared back to him and his second cross was headed out by Rio Ferdinand 
              to Steven Gerrard who smashed an unstoppable shot low past Kahn.</p>
            <p class="engfanz">England made a fantastic start to the second half 
              with Michael Owen scoring his second goal of the night. Beckham 
              did very well out on the right to get his cross in and Heskey cushioned 
              a beautiful header to Owen who finished with ease.</p>
            <p class="engfanz">After the third goal England pulled back and Germany 
              surged forward. Jancker went close with a diving header.</p>
            <p class="engfanz">Real Madrid's Steve McManaman replaced Nicky Barmby 
              on 65 minutes. And almost immediately England scored again. Man 
              of the match Michael Owen was released by Gerrard splitting the 
              German defence and he again finished expertly to complete his hat-trick.</p>
            <p class="engfanz">Germany were stunned and England made it five on 
              74 minutes Beckham found Scholes who found Heskey and the Liverpool 
              striker beat Kahn with ease.</p>
            <p class="engfanz">Without doubt one of the greatest England performances 
              of all time, certainly the best I've seen since Holland at Euro 
              1996.</p>
            <p><span class="engfanz">England are now back in control of Group 
              9 and wins over Albania on Wednesday and Greece next month should 
              see them winning the group and automatic qualification for next 
              years World Cup.</span><br>
