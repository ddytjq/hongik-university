<p> <img border="0" src="diamond.gif" width="15" height="15">      <a href="http://www.chemcalc.org/">ChemCalc</a>
- a chemical calculator written entirely in Java by Michal Krompiec. Calculates
isotopic distribution, elemental composition, molecular mass, exact mass and
molecular formula. </p>

<p align="left"><img border="0" src="diamond.gif" align="left" width="15" height="15"> <a href="http://www.bi.umist.ac.uk/users/mjfrbn/buffers/makebuf.asp">Buffers
for pH control.</a> Use a java calculator to design a buffer with specified pH,
concentration, and ionic strength.</p>
<p align="left"><img border="0" src="diamond.gif" align="left" width="15" height="15"> <a href="http://www.proteome.med.umich.edu/tools/esd/">Electrospray
Deconvolution calculator</a></p>

<p> <img border="0" src="diamond.gif" width="15" height="15"> <a href="http://sx102a.niddk.nih.gov/">NIH
Structural Mass Spectrometry Laboratory</a>&nbsp; <b>Calculate:</b> the
molecular mass from a formula, a molecular formula based on a mass, the
molecular weight from two adjacent ESI peaks, the charge states of a molecule,
calculate the molecular weight of an oligonucleotide.&nbsp; They also have
assorted protein and peptide on-line software. </p>

<p> <img border="0" src="diamond.gif" width="15" height="15">       <a href="http://www.caesar.de/searchxlinks/">SearchXLinks</a>&nbsp;
SearchXLinks, a program for analyzing mass spectrometric experiments carried out
on modified, cross-linked and digested proteins.&nbsp; This includes
identification of peptides linked via disulfide bridges.&nbsp; They provide a
fully functional free web based version and they also sell a standalone version.<br>

electron accountancy, VSPER, MLXZ. </p>
