    Listed CS Hayes Memorial Cup</font></strong><p align="left"><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">A bumper crowd turned out for the second ever Adelaide Magic Millions
    Classic Race Day, this year held at Cheltenham Park. In a great result the first two
    placegetters in the classic, Milkshake and Rachael's Pride, were both bargain buys from
    the Adelaide Sale twelve months earlier.</font></p>
    <p align="left"><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Later in the afternoon a
    classy field of mares clashed in the $272,000 Swettenham Stud Stakes and the Victorian
    visitor Suzy Grey scored a great win over the local favourite Regal Kiss.</font></p>
    <p align="left"><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">The day also paid tribute to
    the late and great Colin (CS) Hayes with the running of the listed CS Hayes Memorial Cup.
    Recent Tokyo City Cup winner Border Time grabbed another feature win when he proved too
    classy.</font></p>
    <p align="left"><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">The first race winner Dorfli
    was sure to win. He was picked out, purchased and part owned by Magic Millions' Victorian
    bloodstock representative and auctioneer Simon Vivian.</font></td>
  </tr>

    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Dorfli settled in front from I Ken then
    Hostile Lad and Jeune's Mark. By the 800m Dorfli led Hostile Lad then I Ken and Bad Boy
    Buck. On straightening Dorfli kicked away from Hostile Lad and Jeune's Mark then I Ken. In
    the run to the line Dorfli came away to win easily from Happy Family with Jeune's mark
    third.</font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Dorfli is a 5yo chestnut gelding by Grand
    Lodge from Mayenfeld and was ridden by Andrew Finlay. He is trained by Alan Bailey for B
    Quail, P Tighe, K MacDonald, C Vivian, T Thornhill and A Ward.</font></td>

    WIN ROCKETS HOME<br>
    </font><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Race 3 - Open Hcp 1000m</font></strong></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Corps Commander was restless in the barrier
    and missed the start as Mint Of Gold led Sunbury and Bluestone Villa then Peat Bog and
    Aptego. Around the turn Mint of Gold raced to a clear lead from Sunbury then Flurry's Gem
    with Bluestone Villa pocketed on the rails. By the 200m Flurry's Gem challenged Mint Of
    Gold from Corps Commander and Sunbury as Noble WIn started to run on through the field.
    Flurry's Gem hit the front by the 50m but Noble Win powered late to win bya half length
    from Flurry's Gem with Mint of Gold third and Corps Commander fourth.</font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Noble Win is a brown 4yo gelding by Blevic
    from Confessor's Halo and was ridden by Michael Hoppo. He is trained by David Jolly for
    himself, SJ Morris, MA Brake, GW Butcher, DC Cousins, MJ Turner &amp; Home and Away
    Syndicate.</font></td>

    <td width="50%"><p align="center"><font face="Arial" color="#008000" size="3"><strong>MILKSHAKE
    TAKES THE MILLIONS</strong></font></p>
    <p align="center"><strong><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Race 4 - Magic
    Millions 1250m</font></strong></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Wyndam Glory and Rachael's Pride led with a
    gap to Flawed Logic then two lengths to Milkshake, Red Labelle and Snowblast. Around the
    turn Wyndam Glory led Rachael's Pride with Snowblast and Flawed Logic next as Dark Horizon
    and Red Labelle started runs with We Will Rock You the widest runner. By the 200m
    Rachael's Pride joined Wyndam Glory then Dark Horizon, Red Labelle and Flawed Logic as
    Milkshake moved out wide to put in a run. With 100m left Wyndam Glory fought back as
    Rachael's Pride challenged with Milkshake appearing to drift in and check Dark Horizon as
    he made a final charge. Inside the final 50m Milkshake hit the front from Rachael's Pride
    as Red Labelle appeared to be struck over the nose by the latter's whip with Wyndam Glory
    on the inside. Milkshake raced away to win by 2 1/2 lengths from Rachael's Pride with
    Wyndam Glory nosing out Red Labelle for third.</font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Milkshake is a 2yo grey colt by Encosta De
    Lago from Bourbazam Lass and was ridden by Peter Mertens. He is trained by Colin Little
    for J Petch, J Maycock, D Dragojevich, M Everest, M Woodburne, D Facchino, Lucrativa Synd,
    GD Reynolds, J Little &amp; EC Dimech.</font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Milkshake was a $20,000 bargain buy at the
    2001 Adelaide Magic Millions Yearling Sale at Morphettville. He was selected by his
    current day trainer Colin Little.</font></td>

    3/4 length from Butkus with Devil In Disguise third.</font></p>

    Umaline and Raja Lane.</font></p>

    Switch In Time from La Busette and was ridden by Paul Gatt in his third straight win for

    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">She's Posh led Dinaar and Bricklin's
    Brother then Aquilane and Kurt. Little changed until straightening where Dinaar hit the
    front from Bricklin's Brother as Kurt waited for a run with Trimaha and Ikaros starting to
    run on. By the 200m Dinaar was holding Bricklin's Brother but soon Kurt and Ikaros started
    their challenges. Inside the final 50m Kurt hit the front with Ikaros chasing hard. Kurt
    found something to hold off Ikaros and win by a head with All Cheer third. Bricklin's
    Brother was fourth while Newtown Jet a US import from the Strawberry Road mare Strawberry
    Girl finished a fast finishing fifth at his first Australian start. </font></p>
