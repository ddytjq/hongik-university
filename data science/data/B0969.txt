      <span class="blocktext"><br>
      </span><span class="lefttext12bold">Product Description</span><span class="blocktext"><br>
      <br>
      House prices are continuing to rise and more and more retired home owners 
      are finding themselves extremely well off. If you own your own property 
      or have a small mortgage and want to release some of the value to improve 
      your lifestyle a home equity release mortgage can be a tax efficient way 
      of tapping into a property's value.<br>
      If you are a retired person over 50 and have sufficient income to meet interest 
      only payments you can release up to 75% of the value of your property. There 
      is no mortgage term but the amount outstanding must be repaid on the sale 
      of the property.<br>
      Your family must also be aware and agree to an equity release mortgage and 
      you should also consider how the loan will be repaid if you or your partner 
      dies.<br>
      <br>
      </span>
