Dan Thornton</FONT></P>      <P><FONT FACE="Helvetica">2. Thom Marusich</FONT></P>      <P><FONT FACE="Helvetica">July 26, 1998,</FONT></P>      <P><FONT FACE="Helvetica">Lansing, Michigan- Dan Thornton wins 9th   Worlds Guts Frisbee Championship.</FONT></P>      <P><FONT FACE="Helvetica">He earns the title of Sir Dan Thornton,   as a winner of thy Guts Grail. Dan defeated last years champion,   Sir Thomas "frisbee jones" Marusich, in games of 19-21, 21-17 and   21-17. Both players were in last years tournament finals, which   was part of last years Inflight Open. Hail to thy Grail and Hail   to Dan Thornton. Long live thy Grail. </FONT></P>      <P><FONT FACE="Helvetica">Winners in other divisions were,   Womens-Mary Thornton, 3 time defending champion, Kids- Makenna   Marusich, also 3 time defending champion, Amateur- Steven   McGovern.</FONT></P>      <P><FONT FACE="Helvetica">The weekend started out Saturday night,   for all that paid their registion fees, with check in at the   Radisson Hotel downtown Lansing. It was then to dinner in the   restaurant, a walk down to Oldsmoible Park to watch the Lansing   Lugnuts play baseball. After the ballgame, it was off on a boat   ride down the Grand River on the Lansing Queen. On the boat was a   jazz band and more food.</FONT></P>      <P><FONT FACE="Helvetica">Sunday morning started with breakfast at   the Radisson, The weather was fair with sunny skies, play was   great all day. Congratulations to Sir Dan Thornton on winning the   9th Worlds One on One Guts Frisbee Championship. Thanks to all   that played, see you nexts year.</FONT></P>      <P><FONT FACE="Helvetica">Thomas Marusich<BR>   5841 Haverhill Drive<BR>   Lansing, Michigan 48911-4809</FONT></P></BLOCKQUOTE><P><FONT FACE="Helvetica">Call 517-882-1187 or e-mail</FONT><A HREF="mailto:%20dmarusich@aol.com"><FONT FACE="Helvetica">dmarusich@aol.com</FONT></A><FONT FACE="Helvetica"><BR><IMG SRC="gifs/MARBLEGY.GIF" WIDTH=512 HEIGHT=6 ALIGN=bottom naturalsizeflag=3></FONT></P><P><A HREF="http://www.sas.it.mtu.edu/~dkwalika/frisbee/newhistory.html"><FONT FACE="Helvetica">Backto History Page</FONT></A></P><P><A HREF="http://www.sas.it.mtu.edu/~dkwalika/frisbee/"><FONT FACE="Helvetica">Backto Guts Home Page</FONT></A></P></BODY></HTML>
