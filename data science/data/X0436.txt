<B>GENERAL INFORMATION:</B>
<UL><LI>TRACK : covered in wood, of 250 metres, 7,12 m in width, with an inclination of a minimum of 13,01° and a maximum of 44,4°
<LI>CLIMATE : maritime temperate, in September - average temperature between a min. of 5° C and a max. of 15°C
<LI>VOLTAGE : 220V : European standard plug
<LI>CURRENCY : DKK - Danish Crown : 100 DKK = 19,85 CHF / 13,47 €
<LI>VISA : For European citizens, a valid passport or identity card. For all other nationalities, please contact your embassy.
</UL>
