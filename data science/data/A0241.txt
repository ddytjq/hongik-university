					<SPAN CLASS=header>Business Bonus Account</SPAN>
			<p />		
			The Business Bonus Account is a unique account that offers you not one but three distinct sets of interest rates. Leave your funds untouched for 30 days or 90 days and you'll automatically earn bonus rates of interest.  What's more your funds are always accessible, which means you can make withdrawals at any time - your balance will simply revert to the starting rate of interest.
			<p />
			<b>Features and benefits</b>
			<br />
			There are six bands of interest available within each set of rates, dependent on how much you deposit. And with an initial minimum deposit of only £1, the account is available to businesses of any size.
			<p />
			Interest is calculated daily on cleared balances and added to your Business Bonus Account on the last business day of each month. There are no charges payable on your account, and you will be issued with a monthly statement.
			<p />
			<b>How it works</b>
			<br />
			Your initial deposit earns interest at the Instant Bonus Rate. If your funds are left untouched for 30 days, your balance automatically starts earning at the 30-Day Bonus Rate.
			<p />
			If your funds are then left for a further 60 days (making 90 days in total), your balance automatically starts earning interest at the 90-Day Bonus Rate.
			<p />
			You can withdraw without notice both easily and free of charge, and your remaining balance will resume earning interest at the Instant Bonus Rate.
			This remaining balance will be eligible for bonus rates once again as soon as the required number of days have elapsed without you making a withdrawal.
			<p />
