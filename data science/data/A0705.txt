            <p class="introtitle">How do I set up a Stakeholder Pension?</p>
            <table width="100%" border="0" cellspacing="0" cellpadding="5">
              <tr> 
                <td width="61%" height="355"> 
                  <p class="body">We are offering our Stakeholder Pension product 
                    to you online. You can apply for and maintain your Stakeholder 
                    Pension from anywhere you can access a computer that has internet 
                    connection, by following the step-by-step guide on this page. 
                    If you want to print it out, just click on the 'print' icon 
                    above.</p>
                  <p class="body">You will receive free access to our Personal 
                    Internet Banking service once you have completed the online 
                    application for our Stakeholder Pension. Personal Internet 
                    Banking will enable you to manage your HSBC Stakeholder Pension 
                    online. If you are an existing HSBC customer, you can also 
                    manage all your other HSBC products via this service (e.g. 
                    bank accounts, credit cards etc).</p>
                </td>
                <td width="39%" valign="top" align="right" height="355"> 
                  <table width="200" border="2" cellspacing="0" cellpadding="0" bgcolor="#CCCCCC">
                    <tr> 
                      <td class="bodybold" height="302"> 
                        <div align="center"> 
                          <p>NOTE FOR EMPLOYEES:</p>
                          <p>You should only apply for a Stakeholder Pension using 
                            this site if you are doing so independently of your 
                            employer, or if you are self-employed or not employed. 
                          </p>
                          <p>If your employer is offering a Stakeholder Pension 
                            through HSBC you do not need to do anything. Your 
                            employer will process your membership and you will 
                            receive notification that your Stakeholder Pension 
                            has commenced as well as registration to our Personal 
                            Internet Banking service. You will be able to access 
                            your Stakeholder Pension details using this service. 
                          </p>
                        </div>
                      </td>
                    </tr>
                  </table>
                </td>
              </tr>
            </table>
            <p class="bodytitle">Am I eligible for a Stakeholder Pension?<br>
              <span class="body">To establish whether you are able to have a Stakeholder 
              Pension, please <a href="javascript:popUp('../eligibility-questionnaire/default.htm')">click&nbsp;here</a>.</span></p>
            <p class="bodytitle">What decisions will I need to make if I apply 
              for a Stakeholder Pension?<br>
              <span class="body">Before applying for our Stakeholder Pension, 
              you will need to have made the following decisions:</span></p>
            <p class="body">If you have not already done so, you should use the 
              Decision Trees, which will help you make an informed decision about 
              whether to take out a Stakeholder Pension. <a href="../decision-tree.htm">Click 
              here</a> to go to our Decision Tree information page.</p>
            <ul>
              <li><span class="body"><span class="bodybold">Retirement age</span> 
                - you will need to specify a standard retirement age for your 
                Stakeholder Pension plan - this could be any age between 50 and 
                75 years.<br>
                </span></li>
            </ul>
            <ul>
              <li><span class="body"><span class="bodybold">Contribution amount</span> 
                - you will need to decide how much you wish to contribute to your 
                Stakeholder Pension plan. This can be a percentage of your earnings, 
                or a specified amount.</span></li>
            </ul>
            <ul>
              <li><span class="body"><span class="bodybold">Contribution frequency</span> 
                - you can choose how often you want to make payments to your Stakeholder 
                Pension plan. For example, monthly, six monthly or annually.</span></li>
            </ul>
            <ul>
              <li><span class="body"><span class="bodybold">Fund choice</span> 
                - you can choose from eight available funds to invest your contributions 
                in. There is an investment choice questionnaire to help you. However, 
                if you do not wish to make an investment decision, we will automatically 
                place your contributions into our &quot;default&quot; investment. 
                This means that we will invest your contributions on your behalf.</span></li>
            </ul>
            <p class="body">To assist you in making these decisions, please use 
              the decision trees. These are accessed by clicking on the "What's 
              right for me" button on the right hand side of this page.</p>
            <p class="body">You will also need the following information to hand 
              to enable you to complete our application:</p>
            <ul>
              <li><span class="body">Your bank details</span></li>
              <li><span class="body">Your National Insurance number</span></li>
            </ul>
            <p class="bodytitle"> How do I apply for a Stakeholder Pension? <br>
              <span class="body">Simply <a href="javascript:popUp('../eligibility-questionnaire/default.htm')">click 
              here</a> to proceed to our online application form. Once your application 
              has been accepted we will set you up as a HSBC Personal Internet 
              Banking customer, allowing you to maintain your Stakeholder Pension 
              online. </span></p>
            <p class="bodytitle">What happens when my application is accepted?<br>
              <span class="body">We will write to you confirming your application 
              for a Stakeholder Pension has been accepted.</span></p>
            <p class="body">You will receive the following information:</p>
            <ul>
              <li><span class="body">Personal Internet Banking Logon ID</span></li>
              <li><span class="body">Security number</span></li>
              <li><span class="body">Internet access details</span></li>
            </ul>
            <p class="body"> For security reasons, your security number and Personal 
              Internet Banking Logon ID will be sent separately.</p>
            <p class="body"> You will also receive a variety of documentation 
              which will include details of your application, the opportunity 
              to cancel your application and other documentation which will highlight 
              the key features and charges associated with your pension.</p>
            <p class="bodytitle">What features are available using the Stakeholder 
              Pensions service?<br>
              <span class="body">Our service includes the following user-friendly 
              features:</span></p>
            <ul>
              <li><span class="body">Ability to view and amend personal/contribution 
                details</span></li>
              <li><span class="body">Fund valuation</span></li>
              <li><span class="body">Retirement projections</span></li>
              <li><span class="body">Choice of investment funds (if required)</span></li>
              <li><span class="body">Fund switching</span></li>
              <li><span class="body">Nomination of beneficiaries</span></li>
              <li><span class="body">Details of past contributions</span></li>
            </ul>
            <p class="bodytitle">What if I have problems setting up my Stakeholder 
              Pension plan?<br>
              <span class="body">To assist your application and the future maintenance 
              of your plan, there is a help facility on each screen, as well as 
              an online user guide. If you require further assistance there is 
              a list of useful contact numbers online.</span></p>
