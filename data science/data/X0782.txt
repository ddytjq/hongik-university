    1 - Fillies and Mares Hcp 1300m</strong></font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Lola and Emerald Lake shared the lead from
    Pretty Edgy then Palagia and Countback. Passing the 600m Lola led Emerald Lake with
    Countback making ground inside Pretty Edgy. By the 300m Lola led but Countback was in
    pursuit as Pretty Edgy ran on from Emerald Lake and Twiggy Summit. At the 100m Countback
    joined Lola as Pretty Edgy, Nassi Doll and Palagia made ground out wider. In the final
    stages Countback was too good winning by a half length from Nassi Doll with Lola third.</font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Countback is a 4yo chestnut mare by Geiger
    Counter from Northford and was ridden by Simon Price. She is trained by Dan O'Sullivan for
    PA &amp; MA Crawford.</font></td>

    <td width="50%"><p align="center"><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000"><strong>Race
    2 - 2yo Hcp 1000m</strong></font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Flash Henry ran outwards at the start into
    Nancy Eleanor as Gold Anthem went to the lead from Thanks a Bundle then Mr Watermark and
    Directory. By the 300m Gold Anthem was well clear from Thanks a Bundle then Mr Watermark,
    Directory and Flash Henry with Cycles and Nancy Eleanor very wide. Inside the 200m Thanks
    a Bundle stormed after Gold Anthem with Nancy Eleanor running on hard then Verse Lago. By
    the 50m Thanks a Bundle hit the front to go on to win by a length from Nancy Eleanor with
    Gold Anthem third.</font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Thanks a Bundle is a 2yo chestnut filly by
    Danewin from Bundle of Thanks and is trained by Paul Seater for SJ Seater and Marant Group
    Syndicate. </font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Thanks a Bundle is her dams 7th living
    foal. All but one of the foals have, like Thanks a Bundle, been offered at the Adelaide
    Yearling Sale with Bundle of Thanks herself being offered through Adelaide as a yearling
    before winning the Listed Widden Stakes during her race career. </font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Nancy Eleanor and Gold Anthem were also
    offered for sale at the 2001 Magic Millions Adelaide Yearling Sale.</font></td>

    <td width="50%"><font SIZE="2"><p align="center"></font><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000"><strong>Race 3 - Open Hcp 1000m</strong></font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Exalted Lad led early from Stand By Me then
    Corps Commander followed by Bontarri. By the 600m Stand By Me took over as Corps Commander
    moved forward around Exalted Lad. With 500m left Exalted Lad tried to push out from behind
    Stand By Me as Corps Commander held his ground. Soon after Exalted Lad continued to push
    between the leaders as all three engaged in a bumping duel as Exalted Lad bounced between
    Corps Commander and Stand By Me with Bontarri changing direction sharply moving to the
    outside behind them. By the 200m Corps Commander edged ahead of Exalted Lad and Stand By
    Me with Bontarri making ground on the outside. In the run to the line Corps Commander came
    away to win by a length from Stand By Me with Exalted Lad third and Bontarri fourth with a
    gap to the other two runners.</font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Corps Commander is a 5yo chestnut gelding
    by Sanction from Rapide Pied and was ridden by Jason Holder. He is trained by Mark
    Kavanagh for IP Kavanagh, RJ Stuttley, P Booth, S Paproth, P Ryan, J Brilliant, J Miller,
    G Delsar, P Emes and J Cameron-Smith.</font></td>
  </tr>

    <td width="50%" align="center"><img src="4inflewinzer.jpg" alt="4inflewinzer.jpg (12665 bytes)" border="2" WIDTH="300" HEIGHT="205"><p><img src="4princenaseem.jpg" alt="4princenaseem.jpg (15024 bytes)" border="2" WIDTH="300" HEIGHT="245"></td>
    <td width="50%"><font SIZE="2"><p align="center"></font><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000"><strong>Race 4 - Open Hcp 2000m</strong></font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Inflewinzer and Happy Conquest led from
    Evans three wide then Astrolante and All The Action then Prince Naseem and Blue Ted with
    Deaconess last. By the 1500m Inflewinzer led from Evans then Happy Conquest followed by
    Astrolante with All The Action wide. </font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Little changed until well into the straight
    where Inflewinzer pulled away from Evans then All The Action and Mighty Conquest as Blue
    Ted started to run on and Deaconess was still back in last and not striding out well. In
    the run to the line Inflewinzer lengthened his lead to win easily from Evans with
    Astrolante third. </font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Deaconess was pulled up and unsaddled on
    the track. After being led back to the raceday stalls it was confirmed that she had badly
    injured her off foreleg and may be retired.</font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Inflewinzer is a 5yo chestnut gelding by
    Vitalic from My Wishes and was ridden by Wayne Kerford. He is trained by Mark Minervini
    for himself, HL Winzer, MJ Price and PR Fisher.</font></td>
  </tr>
  <tr>
    <td width="50%" align="center"><img src="5latte.jpg" alt="5latte.jpg (14725 bytes)" border="2" WIDTH="300" HEIGHT="245"></td>
    <td width="50%"><font SIZE="2"><p align="center"></font><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000"><strong>Race 5 - 3yo Hcp 1250m</strong></font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Smilingly raced away from Discounter then
    Amaclase and Latte then Moved. Smilingly straightened over 6 lengths in front from Latte
    and Discounter then Amaclase. Inside the final 250m Smilingly started to stop as Latte put
    in a determined challenge well clear of Amaclase and Discounter. In the final 50m Latte
    came away to win by 1 1/2 lengths from Smilingly with 2 lengths to Discounter third and
    Amaclase fourth then a gap to the others.</font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Latte is a 3yo brown filly by Anabaa from
    Latty and was ridden by Simon Price. He is trained by Tony McEvoy for AM Harcourt.</font></td>
  </tr>

    <td width="50%" align="center"><img src="6onthesnip.jpg" alt="6onthesnip.jpg (14046 bytes)" border="2" WIDTH="275" HEIGHT="240"></td>
    <td width="50%"><font SIZE="2"><p align="center"></font><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000"><strong>Race 6 - Class 6 Hcp 1450m</strong></font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">All Cheer led King Crow and Prince Ida then
    House Guest. By the 900m All Cheer led Kazoo then Prince Ida followed by Lady Vicki, On
    The Snip and King Crow. Well into the straight Prince Ida went to All Cheer then Kazoo
    with On The Snip and House Guest looking for clear runs. With 150m left On The Snip and
    House Guest got clear to join the leaders with King Crow. Inside the final 70m House Guest
    hit the front but On The Snip was running on well from King Crow as Nothing But Gold
    switched to the inside. In the final 50m On The Snip did best to win by a head from House
    Guest with King Crow just ahead of Nothing Like Gold.</font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">On The Snip is a 4yo chestnut horse by
    Snippets from Lisset and was ridden by James Nevin. He is trained by Dan O'Sullivan for
    Hands and Heels Pristine Racing Syndicate.</font></td>

  <tr>
    <td width="50%" align="center"><img src="5bellottoboy.jpg" alt="5bellottoboy.jpg (13610 bytes)" border="2" WIDTH="300" HEIGHT="246"></td>
    <td width="50%"><font SIZE="2"><p align="center"></font><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000"><strong>Race 7- 1MW Hcp 1800m</strong></font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Brownlow went to the front from a wide
    barrier then Growing and Dancerkov followed by Outside the Square. By the 800m Growing
    joined Brownlow then Dancerkov and Outside the Square with Reflexes next. On straightening
    Growing drifted out as Outside the Square took the run between the leaders. By the 300m
    Outside the Square hit the front from Growing then Made for Real and Ride The Storm. In
    the run to the line Outside the Square held on to beat Growing by a head with Ride The
    Storm and Made For Real fighting out third.</font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Outside the Square is a 4yo chestnut
    gelding by Jackson Square from Sovereign Wonder and was ridden by Simon Price in his third
    victory for the day. He is trained by Mick Huxtable for RW Huxtable, M Moore, R Davies, G
    Weidenhofer, G Jarrett and JA Leopold.</font></td>

    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Straight By led Splash Of Money from
    Mootiara and Undertone then Snipallah wide. Around the turn Straight By led Splash of Love
    as Mootiara fanned wide with Snipallah. By the 300m Splash of Love joined Straight By but
    Mootiara then Dimness followed by Henrysee's Gold started to run on hard down the outside.
    Inside the final 150m Mootiara and Dimness hit the front as Keymark finally got clear and
    started to charge with Henrysee's Gold making ground near the rail. Over the final 100m
    Dimness came away to win by 1 1/4 lengths from Keymark, Mootiara and Henrysee's Gold who
    hit the line together.</font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Dimness is a 5yo brown gelding by Varick
    from Belle Gonzesse and was ridden by David Carney. He is trained by Ken Sweeney for
