      <p><img src="images_deerhvn/title_licenses.jpg" width="262" height="34" alt="Licenses &amp; Stamp Fees"></p>
      <p>1. There is now single hunting license for residents, with three price 
        categories</p>
      <blockquote> 
        <p>a. Junior $10.50<br>
          b. Regular $24.50<br>
          c. Senior $5.00</p>
        <p>This license allows a person to hunt all legal game without need for 
          additional stamps, EXCEPT:</p>
        <p>a. Deer during bow season<br>
          b. Deer during muzzleloader season and<br>
          c. Migratory game birds.</p>
        <p>&nbsp;</p>
      </blockquote>
      <p>2. The resident bow stamp ($5) and resident muzzleloader stamp ($5) are 
        now required in addition to a hunting license for residents hunting deer 
        in these seasons.</p>
      <p>3. The Senior Lifetime Hunting License has been eliminated, but person 
        who already have this license are &quot;grandfathered&quot; and do not 
        need to purchase the Senior Hunting License, Bow or Muzzleloader Stamps. 
        However, they must buy a Bonus Antlered Deer Stamp to hunt a bonus antlered 
        deer or a Maryland Migratory Game Bird Stamp to hunt migratory game birds.</p>
      <p>4. The bonus deer stamp is now a Bonus Antlered Deer Stamp ($10 for residents 
        and $25 for nonresidents) and is only needed to hunt bonus antlered deer. 
        The Nonresident Bonus Deer Stamp - Federal Properties has been eliminated.</p>
      <p>5. The Maryland Migratory Waterfowl Stamp has been replaced by a Maryland 
        Migratory Game Bird Stamp ($9). This stamp must be purchased by any person 
        hunting migratory game birds (doves, rails, coots, snipe, woodcock and 
        waterfowl) in Maryland, without exception. Prior exemptions for landowners 
        hunting on their own property and seniors have been eliminated. The Harvest 
        Information Program (HIP) permit is now issued tin conjunction with the 
        Maryland Migratory Game Bird Stamp.</p>
      <p>6. The Nonresident Full Season License fee has increased to $130 for 
        persons who are residents of states other than Delaware, Pennsylvania, 
        Virginia, and West Virginia.</p>
      <p>7. Nonresidents participating in the deer Bow or Muzzleloader seasons 
        must purchase a Nonresident Bow Stamp ($25) or a Nonresident Muzzleloader 
        Stamp ($25).</p>
      <p>8. The fee for the Nonresident 3-Day Waterfowl and Small Game Hunting 
        License is now $45.</p>
      <p>9. The fee for the Regulated Shooting Area Hunting License is now $6.</p>
