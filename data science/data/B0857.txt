<p><font color="#FF0000" face="Times New Roman"><b>THE CODE OF MORTGAGE LENDING PRACTICE</b></font></p>

<p><font face="Times New Roman">This is a voluntary Code followed by lenders and mortgage
intermediaries in their relations with personal customers and sets out standards of good
lending practice. Under the Code, the Society can provide you with three types of service
which are as follows:</font></p>

<blockquote>
  <p><font color="#FF0000" face="Times New Roman">a) <u>Advice and recommendation</u></font></p>
  <blockquote>
    <p><font face="Times New Roman">We can provide information and advice on the various
    mortgage products which the Society offers and recommend which of our products is most
    suited to your needs,. When advising you, we will take care to help you to select a
    mortgage to fit your needs by asking for relevant information about your circumstances and
    objectives. Our advice will also depend on your particular requirements and on the
    mortgage conditions at the time. If you wish us to provide advice and to recommend one of
    our products, please contact us in order that we may arrange for you to attend an
    interview at the branch.</font></p>
  </blockquote>
  <p><font color="#FF0000" face="Times New Roman">b) <u>Provision of information on the
  different types of product which we offer</u>.</font></p>
  <blockquote>
    <p><font face="Times New Roman">We can provide you with information on the different types
    of mortgage product which we offer so that you can make an informed choice of which to
    take. Once you have had an opportunity to read through the mortgage information, please
    will you let us know which product you require in order that we may proceed with your
    application as soon as possible.</font></p>
  </blockquote>
  <p><font color="#FF0000" face="Times New Roman">c) <u>Information on a single product only</u>.
  </font></p>
  <blockquote>
    <p><font face="Times New Roman">If you have already made up your mind as to which product
    you require, once you have had an opportunity to read through the information or if we can
    only offer you one product, please will you contact us in order that we may proceed with
    your application as soon as possible.</font></p>
  </blockquote>

</blockquote>
