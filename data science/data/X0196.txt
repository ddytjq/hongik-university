<FONT size=+1>We are a non-profit, membership supported Museum dedicated to collecting, preserving, and exhibiting elements from the broad spectrum of ski history for the purpose of research, education, and entertainment. Since opening our doors in 1982, tens of thousands of visitors have toured our exhibits, enjoyed historic ski films in our theatre, and discovered unique gifts in our Museum Shop. Located at the base of Cannon Mountain in Franconia Notch, New Hampshire, the Museum is a popular stopping place for skiers and travelers throughout the year.<P>

The Museum houses the most extensive collection of historical ski equipment, clothing, film, photographs, literature, and artwork in the East. Our extensive archive is renowned by scholars and historians across the country. The New England Ski Museum is officially recognized by the United States Ski Association as a Regional Musuem, one of four in the country.<P>
