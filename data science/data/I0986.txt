ECONOMY<br></b></font><p><font size="2" face="Verdana" color="#231428">The Exchequer will receive an income of over &pound;40,000,000 each year from stadium related activity, not to mention the fact that:</font></p><ul style="list-style-type:circle;"><li><font size="2" face="Verdana" color="#231428">The Stadium will create some &pound;229 million of total visitor expenditure each year, both at the Stadium and from spending by event visitors in shops, bars, hotels and leisure facilities elsewhere in London. This is an increase of &pound;119 million over the annual expenditure attracted by the first Wembley Stadium. </font></li><li><font size="2" face="Verdana" color="#231428">This expenditure will produce some &pound;62 million of income annually, in the form of wages, salaries and business profits, in the London economy. This would be an increase of nearly &pound;38 million p.a. over that produced by the original Wembley Stadium. </font></li><li><font size="2" face="Verdana" color="#231428">The London Development Agency forecasts that up to 10,000 jobs will be created in one of the country’s most deprived areas. </font></li><li><font size="2" face="Verdana" color="#231428">On the back of the Stadium development over &pound;170m has been secured from both the private and public sector to regenerate the area. This includes funding for a state-of-the-art underground station capable of carrying up to 40,000+ people an hour. Two new roads are being built and the two train stations upgraded. </font></li><li><font size="2" face="Verdana" color="#231428">WNSL estimates that 6870 permanent job opportunities (the equivalent of 4280 full time jobs) will be created by economic activity directly related to the stadium. </font></li><li><font size="2" face="Verdana" color="#231428">The National Stadium will generate an estimated &pound;17.5 million of annual income in the north west London area. </font></li><li><font size="2" face="Verdana" color="#231428">Almost 4,000 person years will be spent building the new stadium. A further 1,000 jobs will be created off site.  This is an average of 1,670 jobs during the construction period. </font></li><li><font size="2" face="Verdana" color="#231428">The new stadium will create a major new charitable trust operating in the area. One percent of the Stadium's annual turnover will be provided to the Trust. </font></li></ul><p><font size="2" face="Verdana" color="#231428"><b>THE NEED FOR THE NEW STADIUM IN BRENT </b></font></p><p><font size="2" face="Verdana" color="#231428">The area in which the National Stadium is to be located suffers from high unemployment, over-reliance on manufacturing and distribution activities and declining local employment opportunities. Unemployment is twice the national average and the London Borough of Brent has under performed in the UK economy throughout the 1990's. Based on the DETR Index of Local Deprivation, Brent is the 20th most deprived of the 354 English local authorities, with pockets of severe deprivation close to Wembley.</font></p>									</td>								</tr>								<tr>									<td width="180" align="left" valign="bottom">										<div align="left">										  <a href="http://www.thefa.com/" target="_blank"><img src="../images/team/crest_nt_w.gif" border="0" hspace="52" vspace="8" width="59" height="70"></a><br><font size="1" face="Verdana" color="#3e2447">For England fixtures and ticket information</font><font size="1" face="Verdana" color="#9fe39f"> </font><a href="http://www.thefa.com/" target="_blank"><font size="1" face="Verdana">please click here...<br></font></a> 										</div>									</td>									<td width="77" align="left" valign="bottom"></td>									<td width="443" align="left" valign="bottom">										<div align="left">											<font size="2" face="Verdana" color="#3e2447">You are in <a href="main.htm">THE STADIUM</a> &gt; ECONOMIC BENEFITS &gt; <a href="#top">back to top</a></font></div>									</td>								</tr>							</table>							<br>							<font color="#d1bdd7"><br>							</font><font face="Verdana" color="#3e2447" size="1">dedication by</font><font face="Verdana" color="#d1c186" size="1"> </font><a href="http://www.zestmedia.com" target="_blank"><font face="Verdana" size="1">zestmedia.com</font></a><br>							<br>						</div>					</td>				</tr>			</table>		</center>	</body></html>
