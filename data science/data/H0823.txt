<p><FONT FACE="Verdana" SIZE="+2" COLOR="#FFCC00">Cyclospora species</font></p>
<table width="90%" border="0">
  <tr valign="top"> 
    <td width="90%" valign="middle"> 
      <p align="center"><font face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif"><b>Cyclospora 
        species</b><br>
        <font size="-1">Modified Kinyoun Stain x 1000</font></font></p>
      <p><font face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">This organism was 
        previously called CLB (cyanobacterium-like body) or coccidian like body. 
        </font></p>
      <p><font face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">It has been recently 
        identified as a new coccidian pathogen. </font></p>
      <p><font face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">On modified acid-fast 
        smears, the oocyst will stain from a light pink to a deep red (shown right). 
        </font></p>
      </td>

    <td width=66%> 
      <p>&nbsp;</p>
      <p><font face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">Some will contain

        left. </font></p>

    <td>
      <p><font face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">Many are unstained 
        and appear as glassy wrinkled sphere referred to as ghosts (see right). 
        </font></p>
      <p><font face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">There are two oocyst 
        of Cyclospora in this field, one shows the typical acid fast reaction 
        and the other is a ghost (not demonstrating acid-fastness).</font></p>
      <p><font face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">It is very important 
        to measure the oocysts of Cyclospora (8-10 <font face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif"></font><font face="Symbol">&#109;</font><font face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">m</font>) 
        because they can be easily confused with <a href="crypto.htm">Cryptosporidium</a> 
        oocysts which measure 4-6 <font face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif"></font><font face="Symbol">&#109;</font><font face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">m</font>. 
        </font></p>
    </td>

      <p align="center"><b><font face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">Phase 
        contrast </font></b></p>
      <p><font face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">On wet mounts, the 
        oocyst will appear as a non-refractile sphere. </font></p>
      <p><font face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">This picture shows 
        an immature cyst. It takes 5 days or more for the oocyst to mature. </font></p>

        contains two sporocysts, with two sporozoites.</font></p>
