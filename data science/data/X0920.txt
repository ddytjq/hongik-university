<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>CHAPEL HILL, N.C.&nbsp;-- Can Olu Famutimi take his man off the dribble? Sure thing. Can Olu Famutimi take over a game? No question. Can Olu Famutimi take out the trash? Absolutely.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<?xml:namespace prefix = o ns = "urn:schemas-microsoft-com:office:office" /><o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>Wait. What?</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>True story: The 6-foot-5 junior swingman from Flint, Mich., lives with Chris Grier&nbsp;-- his guardian and summer coach with the high-powered Michigan Hurricanes&nbsp;-- and it doesn't matter that he's one of the nation's best overall prospects in the Class of 2003.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>The kid's got chores. No ifs, ands or buts.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>"He washes the dishes," Grier said. "He cleans up his bedroom and his bathroom, he vacuums all the downstairs rooms, and he cuts the grass."</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>Big yard?</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>"Real big yard," Grier said with a smile.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>But yard work is hardly the only work Famutimi's doing. The Canadian native&nbsp;-- he was born to Nigerian parents in Toronto&nbsp;-- has spent the last couple years making the transition from Freakish Athlete to Actual Basketball Player.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>He showed that last weekend here at the Memorial Day Tournament of Champions.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>The Hurricanes didn't have their typically strong showing. After winning a handful of tournaments earlier this spring&nbsp;-- events in Houston, Columbus, Detroit and Cleveland&nbsp;-- they dropped two of their three pool play games at the TOC.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>But Famutimi showed off a growing shooting guard skill set. He hurt the Miami Tropics Friday night at the Smith Center by pulling from three, using improved ballhandling to create his own shot and elevating for good-looking jumpers.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>He averaged 17 points on the weekend.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>"I've been working on my dribbling skills," he said. "I need to play more like a guard."</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>So far so good.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>It's not like he couldn't do a bunch of good stuff before. He was doing something right this past winter when he averaged 22 points, 12 rebounds, four assists and two blocks a game for coach Dave Bush at Flint Northwestern.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>Which, of course, is why Duke, Louisville, Missouri, Michigan State and Arkansas are among the schools that are vying for his college commitment.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>"He's very personable, very athletic and extremely talented," Bush said earlier this week. "But most of the time he's so far ahead of his teammates that it's hard for him to get pushed."</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>Not with Grier it isn't.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>The 'Canes coach has been doing some scouting this year for the Portland Trail Blazers&nbsp;-- and he picked up some NBA workout tactics he's now using with his in-house hoops pupil.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>"I've been working him out as a prototypical two-guard," Grier explained. "That's what he projects as if he makes it to the NBA. We work out together once or twice a week and then I try to get him to do it on his own. I try not to be overbearing."</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>He tries not be an overbearing&nbsp;... dad.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>Grier and Famutimi have a brotherly sort of strain of a father-son relationship, a bond that's been growing ever since the two met at an AAU tournament in Lansing, Mich., three years ago.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>"We've gotten to know each other more and more," Famutimi said. "We're like father and son&nbsp;-- we've developed a brotherly love&nbsp;-- and that's what I need."</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>During down time. During work on the court. And even during work around the house.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><B><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>PERRY GOOD&nbsp;-- OR JUST PRETTY GOOD?</FONT></B></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>Kentucky boss Tubby Smith and Durham Hillside coach Chet Mebane go way back&nbsp;-- all the way back to their High Point, N.C., days&nbsp;-- so there was a bond there already.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>That bond bore fruit in the form of Bobby Perry's commitment to the Wildcats. The 6-7 Hillside junior decided he wanted to be a part of Big Blue Nation and gave the program his pledge earlier this spring.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>Smith had a big hand in that.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>"Coach Smith is a great person and a great coach," Perry said last weekend while playing with the North Carolina Red Storm at the Tournament of Champions. "We just hit it off."</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>Smith made sure of it. He was the driving force in Perry's recruitment. The UK head man watched two of his games this past season and made two additional trips to Durham just to chat.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>Wake Forest was involved, and so was Stanford, but ...</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>"Coach Smith was right there on me," Perry explained. "I could call him anytime. And Kentucky is a great school and probably the top program in the nation overall."</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>It remains to be seen, though, whether or not Perry's on-court marriage with Kentucky will be as smooth as his union with Smith.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>"Bobby Perry is very skilled, but he's not an elite athlete," PrepStars.com recruiting analyst Thom Jones said.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>"He'll be a blue-collar type of guy at Kentucky. He's not going to be someone who comes in and is a program-changer. He's going to be more of a blend-in guy. I like him&nbsp;-- but I don't love him."</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>No matter. Smith liked what he saw.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>Perry averaged 23 points and 11 rebounds as a junior for Mebane's 13-11 Hillside squad. The team finished second in conference and advanced to the second round of the state playoffs.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>And this summer?</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>"I'm going to be going pretty hard," Perry said. "I'll be in the gym all the time&nbsp;-- and on the track, too&nbsp;-- doing a whole bunch of running and shooting drills.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>"I want to improve all parts of my game, of course, but mainly I need to get in better physical condition. I'm concentrating on being able to go through whole games without losing a bit."</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<H1 style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>AROUND AND ABOUT</FONT></H1>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>*** Vallejo, Calif., sophomore <B>DeMarcus Nelson</B> committed to Duke Saturday night while in North Carolina's Triangle for last weekend's Tournament of Champions. But Duke didn't want the word to get out. Well it did&nbsp;-- shocker!&nbsp;-- so the 6-3 combo guard went ahead and said as much. "This is a firm commitment," Nelson told <B>Al Featherston </B>of the Durham Herald-Sun. "Honestly, down in my heart, I know that's where I'm going." He becomes the youngest player ever to commit to the Blue Devils in the <B>Mike Krzyzewski</B> era.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>*** <B>Tiki Mayben</B> is 14 years old. He just finished his freshman year of high school in Troy, N.Y., but Albany City Rocks coach <B>Jim Hart</B> gave the precocious youngster the starting point position on his 17-and-under team earlier this spring. Already the inimitable <B>Clark Francis</B> has crowned him the Hoop Scoop's No. 1 overall prospect in the Class of 2005. He hit a much-talked-about buzzer-beater to top the Arkansas Wings last month at the Houston Kingwood Classic. "Things like that are typical for him," Hart said. "He makes big plays at big times. It's like, 'Whoa, look at this guy.' I see him as a <B>T.J. Ford</B> type." Mayben played every minute of every game last weekend in Bloomington, Ind., at the Nike Memorial Day Classic. Next up: Rumble in the Bronx June 14-16 at Fordham.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>*** Good for <B>Harrison Schaen</B>. The 6-9, 205-pound combo forward from Santa Ana, Calif., scored a 1220 on his SAT. It only makes sense, then, that academics are going to play a big part in his decision. On his list heading into the summer: Stanford, Cal, USC and Arizona as well as Yale, Penn and Princeton, he told recruitnik <B>Jeff Goodman</B>. Schaen's a bit of a high-major tweener&nbsp;-- a tad too small to be a truly effective four right away in the Pac-10&nbsp;-- and his stock has taken some knocks in the last couple years. He averaged 11.6 points and 10 rebounds as a junior for coach <B>Gary McKnight</B> at perennial power Mater Dei.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>*** The Sonny Hill Seniors beat the Regional All-Stars in a game Thursday night at Villanova that served as a kickoff to Philly's annual summer rite that is the Sonny Hill League. <B>Artie Bowers</B>, the 6-3 shooting guard from Newark, N.J., led the Regionals with 12 points and five assists.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>*** It's not that <B>Troy Weaver</B> doesn't like high school coaches. On the contrary. But sometimes the summer circuit is necessary as well&nbsp;-- actually, all the time. "The new rules aren't bad for us, but I don't like them at all," the Syracuse assistant told BA contributor <B>Mike Waters</B>. "They don't give you a chance to outwork anyone or identify sleepers. Those are my biggest qualms."</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>*** Up and Down <B>Shannon Brown</B> was nothing but Up last weekend in Bloomington. The 6-3 shooting guard from Maywood, Ill., scored 28, 28 and 35 points for the Illinois Warriors in their final three games at the MDC. Is it any wonder coach <B>Larry Butler</B>'s squad won the event's 17-and-under championship?</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>*** <B>Vakeaton Wafer</B> didn't put forth the kind of performance he did late last month at the Kingwood Classic. But The Kid Called Von was pretty darn good for the Arkansas Wings in Bloomington. Some of the schools being bandied about with him: Texas, Florida, Indiana, Maryland, LSU, Kansas State, Louisiana Tech and Cincinnati.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>*** Coach <B>Bart Flener</B> helped coach Kentucky-bound guard <B>Brandon Stockton</B> to the state's coveted Mr. Basketball award this past winter. Now the spotlight is on him: The Glasgow High School coach took the open Lexington Catholic job at a Wednesday press conference. He replaces a legend, <B>Danny Haney</B>, who built LexCath into a powerhouse before taking a similar position in Coconut Creek, Fla., at North Broward Prep.</FONT></P>
