					<h3>1 - 10 of 60</h3><p><strong><a href="http://www.pi.csiro.au/">Australian research centre in the plant sciences (CSIRO)</a></strong><br />CSIRO is an Australian research centre in the plant sciences to promote profitable and sustainable agri-food and to improve natural resource management. Its main goal is genetic engineering for plant improvement.</p>
<p><strong><a href="http://www4.nas.edu/beyond/beyonddiscovery.nsf/">Beyond Discovery: The Path from Research to Human Benefit</a></strong><br />"Beyond DiscoveryTM: The Path from Research to Human Benefit" is a project of the National Academy of Sciences. It is a series of case studies of important recent technological and medical advances. Each case study reveals the crucial role played by basic science, the applications of which could not have been anticipated at the time the
original research was conducted.</p>
<p><strong><a href="http://www.bioinformatics.oupjournals.org/misc/about.shtml">Bioinformatics</a></strong><br />Bioinformatics, one of the Oxford Journals online, publishes scientific papers and review articles in the fields of computational molecular biology, biological databases and genome bioinformatics. Beside the current issue, you can subscribe or serach for articles.</p>
<p><strong><a href="http://bmerc-www.bu.edu/">Biomolecular Engineering Research Centre (BMERC), Boston</a></strong><br />The Biomolecular Engineering Research Centre (BMERC) at Boston, USA, supports research in the development and application of computational biology and bioinformatics methods in biology, including graduate and postdoctoral training programs.</p>
<p><strong><a href="http://www.bio.com/">BioOnline</a></strong><br />BioOnline offers a selection of software for chemical and biological research and an internet career centre for life sciences.</p>
<p><strong><a href="http://bio.perl.org/">Bioperl Project </a></strong><br />Bioperl Project is an international association of developers of public domain Perl tools for bioinformatics, genomics and life science research. Online resource for modules, scripts, and web links for developers of Perl-based software for life science research.</p>
<p><strong><a href="http://www.biores-irl.ie/">BioResearch Ireland (BRI)</a></strong><br />BioResearch Ireland (BRI) facilitates the commercialisation of opportunities arising from Irish Biotechnology research. News, events and career centre.
