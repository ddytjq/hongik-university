<h3 align="center"><!--mstheme--><font color="#330099">Barnet Fighting For Their Lives<!--mstheme--></font></h3>
<p>Barnet might be doing well at the top of the third division at the moment but
their future in the Football League is in serious doubt. Whether you agree with
the recommendations of the Taylor Report or not, the fact is that they have to
be adhered to. Unfortunately, Barnet's current ground, Underhill, is not a
viable option for growth so Barnet have been looking for a new home for several
years now. Three years ago it seemed a solution had been found when the club put
forward a scheme to relocate to Copthall Stadium in Mill Hill, North London but
the plan has recently been blocked by John Prescott. This ruling has left Barnet
is dire straights and they are in danger of being expelled from the Football
League due to their ground not being up to scratch. These are desperate times
for Barnet and any help that you can give would be gratefully appreciated. The
campaign Keep Barnet Alive is doing its best to keep their club afloat. To visit
their website <a href="http://www.geocities.com/colosseum/lodge/5529">click here</a>.</p>
