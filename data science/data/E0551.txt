<p class=MsoNormal><b><span style='font-size:12.0pt'>Basic Storyline:<o:p></o:p></span></b></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-size:12.0pt'><span style='mso-tab-count:
1'>            </span>Four alien races fight for domination over a newly
discovered planet in a distant solar system, which has the highest quantities
of bollium ever discovered.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">  </span>Bollium is
the main material used to fuel the space fold engines for their ships.<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-size:12.0pt'><span style='mso-tab-count:
1'>            </span>The zylex discovered the planet first, but the humans
just a few days later discovered the planet before a zylex mining colony could
be set up.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">  </span>A week later a human
colonization ship folded into the system to find out that a zylex colony ship
was in orbit already surveying possible landing locations!!!<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-size:12.0pt'><span style='mso-tab-count:
1'>            </span>For the last 20 years the four known races: humans,
zylex, ornons, and alsetti have been at a cautious peace, in the aftermath of a
huge war, in which the races were all so evenly matched that no one gained a
real foothold on any of the others.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">  </span>The
human central government, the United Planets, did not want to start another
war.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">  </span>A galactic meeting was called on
the UP headquarters starbase orbiting the human homeworld, Earth.<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-size:12.0pt'><span style='mso-tab-count:
1'>            </span>The four races decided that they did not want to start
another war, after coercing the warlike ornons, who as usual wanted to stupidly
blast away any thing, ship or asteroid or whatnot in their path and wanted to
start a new war.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">  </span>The wise alsetti
proposed that all races could share the planet and an interspecies colony would
be set up there.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">  </span>The arrogant humans
and the warlike ornons refused to share the planet.<span style="mso-spacerun:
yes">  </span>Instead, the zylex proposed that all races send a transforming
ship to the new, still unnamed solar system.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes"> 
</span>Upon defolding, the ship would transform into a fully automated base of
operations, and launch a single piloted fighter, which would engage the other
races.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">  </span>The winner would have undisputed
control of the planet.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">  </span>The ornons were
eager to fight and so agreed.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">  </span>The
humans were convinced that they could win this fight and so agreed.<span
style="mso-spacerun: yes">  </span>The zylex thought their superior technology
would come out on top of this battle and so agreed.<span style="mso-spacerun:
yes">  </span>The alsetti, who did not wish to fight almost declined to the
idea and thought about letting the other races have the planet but decided that
they badly needed the fuel and so reluctantly agreed.<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-size:12.0pt'>At the end of the galactic
conference the races brought up the point that the planet had not been named
yet.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">  </span>They discussed this topic and it
was a matter of irony that the planet was named after the Alsettis delegates
daughter, being as modest as they are.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes"> 
</span>The races then agreed on a month to prepare for the battle that was
ahead of them.<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-size:12.0pt'><span style='mso-tab-count:
1'>            </span>After a month the four races defolded in the new star
system and chose their orbits around the planet.<span style="mso-spacerun:
yes">  </span>As was decided a single piloted ship came out of the new
base.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">  </span>Each race chose its best fighter
design and its best pilot.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">  </span>The zylex
and humans decided to risk the battle with new designs that were quickly put
together.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">  </span>The designed where untested
but used state of the art technology.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes"> 
</span>The alsetti used their traditional, proven design.<span
style="mso-spacerun: yes">  </span>The ornons used the only ship design that
was intelligently built enough to face the other fighters one-for-one (although
some doubt the design is actually the ornons but instead designed by a human
firm, which was heavily paid in secret by the ornons.<span style="mso-spacerun:
yes">  </span>This is only a rumor, however).<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-size:12.0pt'><span style='mso-tab-count:
1'>            </span>Each race was very wary of this battle.<span
style="mso-spacerun: yes">  </span>The wise alsetti commander was especially
wary of the battle.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">  </span>She thought, given
the events in the past, would each race REALLY stick to their pact?<span
style="mso-spacerun: yes">  </span>Would the other races simply let this simple
battle decide everything and then allow the one race sole control over the
largest known deposit of bollium in the galaxy?!<span style="mso-spacerun:
yes">  </span>She decided to prepare their fleets and increase military
production just in case.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">  </span>Can the others
be trusted?<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">  </span>Only time will tell.<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-size:12.0pt'><![if !supportEmptyParas]>&nbsp;<![endif]><o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><b><span style='font-size:12.0pt'>The races:<o:p></o:p></span></b></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><b><span style='font-size:12.0pt'>Humans:<o:p></o:p></span></b></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-size:12.0pt'><span style='mso-tab-count:
1'>            </span>The humans always think they can survive and win the
fight.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">  </span>To some extent their arrogant
belief has some truth to it.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">  </span>The
well-balanced and adaptable humans, although somewhat weak in comparison to the
other races have found ways to survive situations that the other races thought
were impossible to survive in.<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-indent:.5in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt'>The
human ship uses an ultra-high speed machinegun array as its primary weapon. The
human ship can cloak, appearing translucent while it is firing and completely
dissapears when doing nothing. Other players can see the thrust comming out of
the cloaked ship.<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-indent:.5in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt'><![if !supportEmptyParas]>&nbsp;<![endif]><o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><b><span style='font-size:12.0pt'>Zylex:<o:p></o:p></span></b></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-size:12.0pt'><span style='mso-tab-count:
1'>            </span>The zylex are the most ingenious and technological known
race in the galaxy.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">  </span>They were the first
ones to discover that by folding space-time they could travel huge distances in
just a matter of days.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">  </span>Their ships have
the best propulsion systems but their military prowess is much less glamorous.<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-size:12.0pt'><span style='mso-tab-count:
1'>            </span>The zylex make the fastest ships in the galaxy.<span
style="mso-spacerun: yes">  </span>A laser serves as the primary weapon of this
ship, and as a special ability they can travel through asteroids without
injury, a system that was developed when it was discovered that Zylex pilots
flew so fast they could not avoid many obstacles.<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-size:12.0pt'><![if !supportEmptyParas]>&nbsp;<![endif]><o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><b><span style='font-size:12.0pt'>Alsetti:<o:p></o:p></span></b></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-size:12.0pt'><span style='mso-tab-count:
1'>            </span>The alsetti are considered by the other races to be the
wisest and the most conservative, and although they stick strongly to
tradition, their tradition is much more open, flexible, and less discriminatory
than the other races.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">  </span>For example they
do not discriminate based on gender (women roughly equal men in the armed
forces).<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">  </span>They are usually withdrawn and
shy, and would rather have peace than fight.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes"> 
</span>But even they know that sometimes fighting is inevitable and have
developed a strong military capable of keeping intruders out although not as
capable of invading.<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-indent:.5in'><span style='font-size:12.0pt'>The
alsetti ship has the infamous alsetti blaster as its main weapon. Its secondary
weapon is the parabolt that paralizes an enemy ship for a short time.<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-size:12.0pt'><![if !supportEmptyParas]>&nbsp;<![endif]><o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><b><span style='font-size:12.0pt'>Ornons:<o:p></o:p></span></b></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-size:12.0pt'><span style='mso-tab-count:
1'>            </span>The ornons are the most belligerent of the races, and also
the stupidest.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">  </span>Their solution to
everything is to destroy it, and the other races have a hard time of convincing
the ornons of anything else.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">  </span>They have
