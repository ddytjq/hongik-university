            <p align="center"><b>UNDERWRITING RESULTS OF SYNDICATES</b></p>

            </p>
            <p>Chaucer Holdings PLC announces today the results for the 1996 Lloyd&#146;s 
              year of account of the syndicates managed by Chaucer Syndicates 
              Limited. The total profit before personal expenses for the Chaucer 
              syndicates is &pound;16.1 million, representing a return on capacity 
              of 7.7 per cent before personal expenses and 5.3 per cent after 
              personal expenses (for individual members). </p>
