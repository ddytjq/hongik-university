            2000</font></b></p>

            read Racer Magazine.&nbsp; I had to dust off my very old photo albums

            hardest part was deciding which ones not to include.&nbsp; We hope
            you enjoy them.&nbsp; </font><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#FF0000">Click </font><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#000000">

        <font face="Arial" size="2">Mario's win in the 3/28/71 Questor GP at
        Ontario Motor Speedway was very popular. Competition Press &amp;
        Autoweek ran this headlines.&nbsp; Three weeks later it also carried him to two wins in the non-points paying Questor GP at the
        former Ontario Motor Speedway where he beat Jackie Stewart in both heats.&nbsp;
    In the first heat Mario passed Stewart on the final lap to take the
    win.&nbsp; In the second heat, Stewart got the jump on Mario at the start
    and led the first 7 or 8 laps. Mario then passed and pulled away for a
    dominating win.&nbsp;
        That race was dubbed the war of the worlds, pitting Americans in F-5000
        machines against the F-1 cars and stars.&nbsp; As expected the F-1 cars
        dominated that day, but the American fans had something to celebrate,
        Mario was American.</font></font></p>
