		  you would pay £215 when you placed your order and £625 (the rest of the deposit) nearer
		  to the time of delivery. You would then make 36 monthly payments of £159.00.
		  If you decided to purchase the vehicle at the end of the period, the option to buy price
		  would be £3,471.50, payable one month after your final normal monthly payment.

(This can be either as a result of an application for a product or service or by signing an explicit declaration as part of the application.
