<font face="Arial,Helvetica"></font><font face="Arial,Helvetica">Rapid
progress in genome science and a glimpse into its potential applications
have spurred observers to predict that biology will be the foremost science
of the 21st century. Technology and resources generated by the Human Genome
Project and other genomic research already are having major impacts on
research across the life sciences. Doubling in size between 1993 and 1999,
according to Ernst &amp; Young,<sup>1</sup> the biotechnology industry
generated 151,000 direct jobs and 287,000 indirect jobs. Revenues totaled
$20 billion directly and $27 billion indirectly.</font><font face="Arial,Helvetica"></font>
<p><b><font face="Arial,Helvetica"><font color="#800040"><font size=+2>Some
Current and Potential Applications of Genome Research</font></font></font></b><font face="Arial,Helvetica"></font>
<p><b><font face="Arial,Helvetica"><font color="#800040">Molecular Medicine</font></font></b>
<ul>
<li>
<font face="Arial,Helvetica">Improve diagnosis of disease</font></li>

<li>
<font face="Arial,Helvetica">Detect genetic predispositions to disease&nbsp;</font></li>

<li>
<font face="Arial,Helvetica">Create drugs based on molecular information</font></li>

<li>
<font face="Arial,Helvetica">Use gene therapy and control systems as drugs</font></li>

<li>
<font face="Arial,Helvetica">Design "custom drugs" based on individual
genetic profiles</font></li>
</ul>
<b><font face="Arial,Helvetica"><font color="#800040">Microbial Genomics</font></font></b>
<ul>
<li>
<font face="Arial,Helvetica">Rapidly detect and treat pathogens (disease-causing
microbes) in clinical practice</font></li>

<li>
<font face="Arial,Helvetica">Develop new energy sources (biofuels)</font></li>

<li>
<font face="Arial,Helvetica">Monitor environments to detect pollutants</font></li>

<li>
<font face="Arial,Helvetica">Protect citizenry from biological and chemical
warfare</font></li>

<li>
<font face="Arial,Helvetica">Clean up toxic waste safely and efficiently</font></li>
</ul>
<b><font face="Arial,Helvetica"><font color="#800040">Risk Assessment</font></font></b>
<ul>
<li>
<font face="Arial,Helvetica">Evaluate the health risks faced by individuals
who may be exposed to radiation (including low levels in industrial areas)
and to cancer-causing chemicals and toxins</font></li>
</ul>
<b><font face="Arial,Helvetica"><font color="#800040">Bioarchaeology, Anthropology,
Evolution, and Human Migration</font></font></b>
<ul>
<li>
<font face="Arial,Helvetica">Study evolution through germline mutations
in lineages</font></li>

<li>
<font face="Arial,Helvetica">Study migration of different population groups
based on maternal genetic inheritance</font></li>

<li>
<font face="Arial,Helvetica">Study mutations on the Y chromosome to trace
lineage and migration of males</font></li>

<li>
<font face="Arial,Helvetica">Compare breakpoints in the evolution of mutations
with ages of populations and historical events&nbsp;</font></li>
</ul>
<b><font face="Arial,Helvetica"><font color="#800040">DNA Identification</font></font></b>
<ul>
<li>
<font face="Arial,Helvetica">Identify potential suspects whose DNA may
match evidence left at crime scenes</font></li>

<li>
<font face="Arial,Helvetica">Exonerate persons wrongly accused of crimes</font></li>

<li>
<font face="Arial,Helvetica">Identify crime, catastrophe, and other victims</font></li>

<li>
<font face="Arial,Helvetica">Establish paternity and other family relationships</font></li>

<li>
<font face="Arial,Helvetica">Identify endangered and protected species
as an aid to wildlife officials (could be used for prosecuting poachers)</font></li>

<li>
<font face="Arial,Helvetica">Detect bacteria and other organisms that may
pollute air, water, soil, and food</font></li>

<li>
<font face="Arial,Helvetica">Match organ donors with recipients in transplant
programs</font></li>

<li>
<font face="Arial,Helvetica">Determine pedigree for seed or livestock breeds</font></li>

<li>
<font face="Arial,Helvetica">Authenticate consumables such as caviar and
wine</font></li>
</ul>
<b><font face="Arial,Helvetica"><font color="#800040">Agriculture, Livestock
Breeding, and Bioprocessing</font></font></b>
<ul>
<li>
<font face="Arial,Helvetica">Grow disease-, insect-, and drought-resistant
crops</font></li>

<li>
<font face="Arial,Helvetica">Breed healthier, more productive, disease-resistant
farm animals</font></li>

<li>
<font face="Arial,Helvetica">Grow more nutritious produce</font></li>

<li>
<font face="Arial,Helvetica">Develop biopesticides</font></li>

<li>
<font face="Arial,Helvetica">Incorporate edible vaccines into food products</font></li>

<li>
<font face="Arial,Helvetica">Develop new environmental cleanup uses for
plants like tobacco</font></li>
</ul>
<font face="Arial,Helvetica"><font size=-2><sup>1</sup><i>The Economic
Contributions of the Biotechnology Industry to the U.S. Economy</i>, May
2000, Ernst and Young.</font></font>
