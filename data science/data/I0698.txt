								  <TR> 
									 <TD COLSPAN="2" BGCOLOR="#FFFFFF">
										<P> Brazil's Ronaldinho burst down the left to
										  win a corner after 60 seconds but the kick came to nothing. Then Kleberson
										  cruised down the opposite flank but Oliver Kahn gathered in the German goal.
										  </P>
										<P>Marco Bode whipped in a low cross from the
										  left as Germany tried to up the pace but the ball was cleared. The German
										  defence were standing firm against Brazil's attacking triumvirate of Ronaldo,
										  Rivaldo and Ronaldinho. Kleberson then shot low after a mistake by Thomas Linke
										  but Kahn held. </P>
										<P>Edmilson intervened to deny Miroslav Klose
										  after a low cross from the right by Oliver Neuville before Dietmar Hamann had a
										  long-range drive blocked by a clutch of defenders in a spell of German
										  pressure. </P>
										<P>Ronaldinho almost unlocked the door with a
										  perfect through ball to Ronaldo but his left-footed effort trickled past Oliver
										  Kahn's far post after 18 minutes. Lucio strode forward through the middle and
										  tried to free Ronaldo but the steely German defence crowded him out. </P>
										<P>Tortsen Frings whipped in a high cross from
										  the right after 24 minutes which Miroslav Klose just failed to meet. </P>
										<P>Roberto Carlos tried to galvanise the
										  Brazilians with a run down the left but again a low cross hit a wall of German
										  white shirts. </P>
										<P>Marco Bode failed to make the most of a fine
										  crossfield ball as Cafu got back to clear. At the other end Ronaldinho's lob
										  sent Ronaldo away but Kahn raced out and smothered his effort. cw/gj</P>
										<P> Jens Jeremies came close to scoring in the
										  opening minutes of the second half but his header was blocked by Edmilson's
										  boot as the rain fell on the Yokohama International Stadium. </P>
										<TABLE WIDTH="100%">
										  <TR>
											 <TD>
												<P>Brazil keeper Marcos touched a booming
												  Oliver Neuville freekick from 35 yards against an upright as the Germans almost
												  took the lead four minutes after the interval.</P></TD>
											 <TD><IMG SRC="../../images/Final/WCF7.jpg"
												WIDTH="200" HEIGHT="127" BORDER="0"></TD>
										  </TR>
										</TABLE>
										<P> A cross from Roberto Carlos was met by
										  Gilberto Silva but keeper Oliver Kahn managed to smother and Silva poked the
										  rebound wide with Kahn obstructing his path to goal. Dietmar Hamann then
										  smacked a drive over the bar to leave the 69,029 crowd still waiting for a goal
										  after 56 minutes.</P>
										<TABLE WIDTH="100%">
										  <TR>
											 <TD><IMG SRC="../../images/Final/WCF11.jpg"
												WIDTH="200" HEIGHT="116" BORDER="0"></TD>
											 <TD>
												<P> Ronaldo gave Brazil the lead after 67
												  minutes when German keeper Oliver Kahn spilled an effort from Rivaldo, allowing
												  Ronaldo to follow up and sidefoot home for his seventh goal of the
												  finals</P></TD>
										  </TR>
										</TABLE>
										<P>Kahn had been superb for Germany throughout
										  the finals - but Ronaldo punished him for his first mistake.</P>
										<P> At the other end Bernd Schneider almost put
										  Oliver Neuville through after 62 minutes but the latter just failed to get on
										  the end of his through pass and the ball ran through to keeper Marcos.</P>
										<P> Jens Jeremies had to leave the action briefly
										  three minutes later after a clash with Cafu, who caught him on the outside of
										  his knee but the German midfielder came back on after treatment. </P>
										<P>After Ronaldo's goal the Germans redoubled
										  their efforts and Torsten Frings fired over before Jeremies blasted a low
										  effort just wide. </P>
										<P>With 17 minutes remaining veteran Oliver
										  Bierhoff came on for Miroslav Klose.</P>
										<TABLE WIDTH="100%">
										  <TR>
											 <TD>
												<P>Ronaldo put Brazil 2-0 up with 11
												  minutes left. Kleberson crossed from the right, Rivaldo let the ball carry and
												  Ronaldo drove home for his eighth goal of the tournament and second of the
												  match. </P></TD>
											 <TD><IMG SRC="../../images/Final/WCF12.jpg"
												WIDTH="200" HEIGHT="139" BORDER="0"></TD>
										  </TR>
										</TABLE>
										<P>Ronaldo was substituted by Denilson and
										  Juninho came on for Ronaldhino as the samba kings controlled posession while
										  the Germans threw more into attack.</P>
