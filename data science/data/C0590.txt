				<td class=body colspan=2>
<P>
The Prosperity Savings Plan is a with profits endowment savings plan. This means you'll be sharing in the performance of Scottish Friendly, one of the UK's leading friendly societies.
Because we're a mutual society, we don't have shareholders. Instead, we share out all the profits we make among our policyholders in the form of regular bonuses. Therefore each year you may receive a bonus and on maturity a terminal bonus may also be added. This offers you security and growth when you're saving, because once a bonus has been added to your policy, it cannot be taken away.
