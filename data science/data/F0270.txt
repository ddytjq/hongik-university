<h1><a name="_Toc445024333"><span style='mso-ansi-language:EN-US'>Which Record?</span></a><span
style='mso-ansi-language:EN-US'><o:p></o:p></span></h1>

<p class=MsoNormal>In the last section <!--[if supportFields]><span
style='mso-element:field-begin'></span><span style="mso-spacerun:
yes"> </span>XE &quot;records&quot; <![endif]--><!--[if supportFields]><span
style='mso-element:field-end'></span><![endif]-->we discussed obtaining data
from fields. But which record was providing the data? Were we on the first
record, the last or somewhere in between? Later we will open recordsets with
only one record, and we will also study how to find a specific record, but for
now we need to understand which record we are on. </p>

<p class=MsoNormal><![if !supportEmptyParas]>&nbsp;<![endif]><o:p></o:p></p>

<p class=MsoNormal>The first two methods to learn when shifting the focus, or
pointer, from one record to another are simple.</p>

<p class=MsoNormal><![if !supportEmptyParas]>&nbsp;<![endif]><o:p></o:p></p>

<p class=CodeForeground><span lang=EN-GB>oRSp.movefirst</span></p>

<p class=CodeForeground><span lang=EN-GB><![if !supportEmptyParas]>&nbsp;<![endif]><o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=CodeForeground><span lang=EN-GB>oRSp.MoveNext</span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><![if !supportEmptyParas]>&nbsp;<![endif]><o:p></o:p></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><span class=CodeinText><span style='font-size:9.0pt;
mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:"Courier New";mso-bidi-font-family:"Times New Roman";
color:black'>oRS.movefirst</span></span> jumps<!--[if supportFields]><span
style='mso-element:field-begin'></span> XE &quot;records:<span
class=CodeinText><span style='font-size:9.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;
font-family:"Courier New";mso-bidi-font-family:"Times New Roman";color:black'>MoveFirst</span></span>
method&quot; <![endif]--><!--[if supportFields]><span style='mso-element:field-end'></span><![endif]--><span
style="mso-spacerun: yes"> </span>the cursor to the first record. If we then
request data from a field we will get the data from the first record.<!--[if supportFields]><span
style='mso-element:field-begin'></span> XE &quot;MoveFirst method&quot; <![endif]--><!--[if supportFields]><span
style='mso-element:field-end'></span><![endif]--><!--[if supportFields]><span
style='mso-element:field-begin'></span><span style="mso-spacerun:
yes"> </span>XE &quot;<span class=CodeinText><span style='font-size:9.0pt;
mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:"Courier New";mso-bidi-font-family:"Times New Roman";
color:black'>MoveNext</span></span> method&quot; <![endif]--><!--[if supportFields]><span
style='mso-element:field-end'></span><![endif]--><!--[if supportFields]><span
style='mso-element:field-begin'></span><span style="mso-spacerun:
yes"> </span>XE &quot;<span class=CodeinText><span style='font-size:9.0pt;
mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Palatino;color:black'>records:</span></span><span
class=CodeinText><span style='font-size:9.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;
font-family:"Courier New";mso-bidi-font-family:"Times New Roman";color:black'>MoveNext</span></span>
method&quot; <![endif]--><!--[if supportFields]><span style='mso-element:field-end'></span><![endif]--><span
style="mso-spacerun: yes"> </span><span class=CodeinText><span
style='font-size:9.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:"Courier New";
mso-bidi-font-family:"Times New Roman";color:black'>MoveNext</span></span>
jumps the pointer down to the next record of the recordset. By dong a series of
MoveNext commands we can work our way through the entire record set. </p>

<p class=MsoNormal><![if !supportEmptyParas]>&nbsp;<![endif]><o:p></o:p></p>

<p class=MsoNormal>Note that in most tables you can <i style='mso-bidi-font-style:
normal'>not</i> assume that when you open a recordset that you will be on the
first record. In fact, you cannot be sure that you know what the first record.
You may think that the first record is determined by the alphabetical order of
the NameLast field, but the records may have actually been entered into the
database in chronological order and therefore the first person to be entered is
the oldest record, even if her last name is <span class=CodeinText><span
style='font-size:9.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:"Courier New";
mso-bidi-font-family:"Times New Roman";color:black'>Zyminski</span></span>.
Therefore, you should generally use a <span class=CodeinText><span
style='font-size:9.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:"Courier New";
mso-bidi-font-family:"Times New Roman";color:black'>RS.MoveFirst</span></span>
prior to starting a walk through the data.</p>

<div style='border:solid black 2.25pt;padding:1.0pt 1.0pt 1.0pt 1.0pt;
background:black;mso-shading:windowtext;mso-pattern:solid auto'>

<h5><a name="_Toc445024334"><span style='mso-ansi-language:EN-US'>Try It Out -
Moving Through Three Records</span></a><span style='mso-ansi-language:EN-US'> <o:p></o:p></span></h5>

</div>

<p class=MsoNormal><![if !supportEmptyParas]>&nbsp;<![endif]><o:p></o:p></p>

<p class=MsoNormal>In this example we'll write a script that <!--[if supportFields]><span
style='mso-element:field-begin'></span><span style="mso-spacerun:
yes"> </span>XE &quot;records:moving through records&quot; <![endif]--><!--[if supportFields]><span
style='mso-element:field-end'></span><![endif]-->produces a page that lists the
first three vendors from the Clothier database. For this exercise we can assume
that there will be at least three vendors in the database.</p>

<p class=MsoNormal><![if !supportEmptyParas]>&nbsp;<![endif]><o:p></o:p></p>

<p class=CodeForeground><span lang=EN-GB>&lt;TITLE&gt;2726-03-SimpleRS TIO Move
Through 3 Records&lt;/TITLE&gt;</span></p>

<p class=CodeForeground><span lang=EN-GB>&lt;/HEAD&gt;&lt;BODY&gt;</span></p>

<p class=CodeForeground><span lang=EN-GB>&lt;H1&gt;Chapter 3 Simple

<p class=CodeForeground><span lang=EN-GB>&lt;H3&gt;Try It Out - Moving Through
Three Records&lt;/H3&gt;</span></p>

<p class=CodeForeground><span lang=EN-GB>oRSv.MoveFirst</span></p>

<p class=CodeForeground><span lang=EN-GB><![if !supportEmptyParas]>&nbsp;<![endif]><o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=CodeForeground><span lang=EN-GB>Response.Write &quot;Next line walks

<h3><a name="_Toc445024336"><span style='mso-ansi-language:EN-US'>A Note on
Moving Through Recordsets</span></a><span style='mso-ansi-language:EN-US'><o:p></o:p></span></h3>

<p class=MsoNormal>Note here that different types of recordsets support <!--[if supportFields]><span
style='mso-element:field-begin'></span><span style="mso-spacerun:
yes"> </span>XE &quot;recordsets:methods&quot; <![endif]--><!--[if supportFields]><span
style='mso-element:field-end'></span><![endif]-->different types of methods. We
have been using the simplest of recordsets and by default that means that the
pointer can only go forward through the data or back all the way to the start.
Here are the key points to remember about the default cursor type of the
recordset that we've used here:</p>

<p class=MsoNormal><![if !supportEmptyParas]>&nbsp;<![endif]><o:p></o:p></p>

<p class=Bullets>You can use the <span class=CodeinText><span style='font-size:
9.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:"Courier New";mso-bidi-font-family:
"Times New Roman"'>MoveFirst</span></span> method, as well as <span
class=CodeinText><span style='font-size:9.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;
font-family:"Courier New";mso-bidi-font-family:"Times New Roman"'>MoveNext</span></span>
and <span class=CodeinText><span style='font-size:9.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:
10.0pt;font-family:"Courier New";mso-bidi-font-family:"Times New Roman"'>MoveLast</span></span></p>

<p class=Bullets>You can use these methods multiple times on one recordset.
However, once you have walked through a recordset (using <span
class=CodeinText><span style='font-size:9.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;
font-family:"Courier New";mso-bidi-font-family:"Times New Roman"'>MoveNext</span></span>),
you can't go through it again without first calling <span class=CodeinText><span
style='font-size:9.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:"Courier New";
mso-bidi-font-family:"Times New Roman"'>MoveFirst</span></span> again</p>

<p class=Bullets>You <i style='mso-bidi-font-style:normal'>cannot</i> use <span
class=CodeinText><span style='font-size:9.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;
font-family:"Courier New";mso-bidi-font-family:"Times New Roman"'>MovePrevious</span></span>,
bookmarks or other types of moving methods</p>

<p class=Bullets>The <span class=CodeinText><span style='font-size:9.0pt;
mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:"Courier New";mso-bidi-font-family:"Times New Roman"'>RecordCount</span></span>
property lacks meaning with the default cursor</p>

<p class=MsoNormal><![if !supportEmptyParas]>&nbsp;<![endif]><o:p></o:p></p>
