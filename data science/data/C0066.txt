        The policy is designed to incorporate all the removers non-motor insurance 
        requirements into a single, streamlined but focused policy to ease your 
        administration.</font></p>
      <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">This is further supported 
        by the absence of complicated proposal forms or need for regular declarations. 
        The scheme allows you to regulate the policy yourself and cuts out unnecessary 
        paperwork.</font></p>
      <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">The are four key sections 
        to the policy:</font></p>
      <ul>
        <li><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2"> Contractual and 
          Legal Liability for your customers goods.</font></li>
        <li><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2"> All Risks for 
          customers goods</font></li>
        <li><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2"> Employers, public 
          and products liability</font></li>
        <li><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2"> Combined Commercial 
          covers (buildings, contents, stock and business interruption)</font></li>
      </ul>
      <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">The policy even comes 
        with two separate schedules, one showing cover provided the other with 
        premium details, so you can safely assure customers of the level of cover 
        you have without having to release sensitive information.</font></p>
      <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2"><b>Focused Cover</b><br>
        Policy cover has been negotiated to the highest standards and largely 
        follows the latest British Association of Removers (BAR) contract terms 
        and conditions. However, it is not restricted to BAR members and can accommodate 
        non-BAR members and those who carry full liability.</font></p>
      <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">In addition to the 
        modernised standard wording, the scheme also offers very relevant extensions 
        which are not usually available to removal companies. Examples are &quot;New 
        for Old&quot; cover being incorporated as standard cover or on an &quot;as 
        and when&quot; basis to suit your business.</font></p>
      <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2"><b>Premium Savings</b><br>
        By combining together varying types of cover into one policy savings against 
        more fragmented policies are virtually guaranteed. In some cases savings 
        of up to 50% have been achieved.</font></p>
      <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2"><b>Other Features</b></font></p>
