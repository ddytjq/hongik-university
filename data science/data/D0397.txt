</a><font face="Arial">(Ronny Richardson <em>et al.</em>, Manning Publications, 1998, pp.
102-103).</font></p>

<p><font face="Arial">This applet is a simple text animation which plays a scrolling
marquee for which you can specify ticker text, font, type style, point size, and speed.
You can also specify background and foreground color.</font></p>

instance that is running on this page right now: </font></p>

<h2><font face="Arial">Background and Foreground Color Attributes </font></h2>

<p><font face="Arial">Most people will find the six basic colors sufficient. However, you
can set the background or foreground color of the applet to any one of the 16+ million
colors in the 24 bit RGB (Red-Green-Blue) spectrum. But first, you must calculate the
value of your custom color. To facilitate this, please use <a href="/java/rgb/">this
calculator</a>. </font></p>
