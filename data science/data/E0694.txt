<H3>Character Set, Arithmetic, Relational & Logical Operators</H3>

The <B>C</B> Programming Language <B>Character Set</B> is as follows:<P>
<UL>

<LI><B>A - Z</B>
<LI><B>a -z</B>
<LI><B>0 - 9</B>
<LI><B>(space) . , : ; ' $ "</B>
<LI><B># % & ! _ {} [] &lt; &gt; </B>|
<LI><B>+ - / * =</B>
</UL>

<A NAME="arithmetic"></A>
The <B>C</B> Programming Language <B>Arithmetic set</B>:<P>
<PRE>
<B>()	Brackets
++	Increment
--	Decrements
*	Multiplication
/	Division
+	Addition
-	Subtraction
%	Modulus
=	Assignment</B></PRE>

The <B>C</B> Programming Language has the following <B>Relational and Logical Operators</B>:<P>

<PRE>
<B>
Relational

&gt;	Greater than
&gt;=	Greater than or equal to
&lt;	Less than
&lt;=	Less than or equal to
==	Equal to
!=	Not equal to

Logical

&&	AND
||	OR
!	NOT
</B></PRE>
