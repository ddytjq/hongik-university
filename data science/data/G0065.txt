is comparable to GLAST but with the key difference that for EXIST these are
spectroscopic, not discriminator, channels.<span style="mso-spacerun:
yes">&nbsp; </span>The onboard data throughput and data processing needs for
GRB (and transient) source positions are more challenging than for Swift but
are not beyond projected data processing and power capabilities in the mission
timeframe.<o:p></o:p></span></p>
