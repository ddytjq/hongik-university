		Additional terms and conditions for Skipton Mini Cash ISA
<DIV class="textsmall">
		<ol>

<li>The account must not be a joint account or held on behalf of a person other than the account holder.
<li>All customer transactions must be made by cheque.
<li>The account holder must not subscribe to more than one Mini Cash ISA, or subscribe to a Maxi ISA in any one tax year.
<li>The application form including Declaration and Authorities must be completed and signed by the applicant.
<li>The minimum investment for the Mini Cash ISA is £500 and the maximum is £3,000 in any one tax year. The minimum operating balance is £500.

<li>You will have 14 days from receipt of your ISA acknowledgement letter in which to change your mind and request cancellation of your investment in writing only. This is subject to all necessary identification requirements having been met. Your cancelled ISA will not count as an ISA subscription. Interest will be paid gross for the cancellation period.
<li>The commencement date of the Skipton Mini Cash ISA will be the date of the first entry in your passbook.
<li>The account can only be put on notice for withdrawal, closure or transfer twice in any tax year.
<li>Withdrawals can be made on receipt of 30 days written notice. The minimum operating balance is £500. Withdrawals above this figure may be made by cheque for a minimum of £500. Investors should note however, that in accordance with ISA rules, investments in any tax year are limited to the amount permitted by the Inland Revenue (currently £3,000) regardless of the level of withdrawals made.
<li>Closure and transfer to another ISA provider are allowed but 30 days written notice is required subject to condition 10 above. For transfers to other ISA providers a fee of £30, to cover administration costs, is required and must be received at the same time as we receive your written notice.
