				<font class="news"><center><b>ZIMBABWE INNINGS</b><br>240 for 8 off 50 overs (4.8 an over)</center><br>The fourth ODI and second day/nighter is a must win for India, if Sourav Ganguly's team are to retain an interest in winning this five-match series. Down 2-1 to Zimbabwe, India needs a win to set up a deciding match in Guwahati on Tuesday. <br> Zimbabwe wins the toss and elects to bat - Andy Flower returns to the team after injury. Campbell and Ebrahim open the innings in bright sunlight. Ebrahim survives a very close call for a Dravid slip catch off Khan - the third umpire gives the batsman the benefit of doubt. <br>But Campbell (his first failure in four innings) is soon out caught by Dravid off Agarkar for 3. Friend follows three balls later caught off his gloves behind the wicket by Ratra. Agarkar is getting extra bounce out of the pitch. Ebrahim and Andy Flower lead the fightback - 50 partnership off 78 balls ... and more. <br>66-run partnership ends with Ebrahim holing out at deep midwicket to Dravid off Ganguly for 36 (79 for 3). Grant joins brother Andy in the middle, as 100 comes off 27 overs. Andy F reaches 50 off 75 balls (119 for 3). <br>150 off 35.4 overs as the siblings push the run rate above 4 an over. Mongia takes a running, diving catch at long on to get rid of Grant for 44 off Harbhajan (175 for 4) - partnership of 96 (107 balls). <br>Flower goes caught behind off Khan for 89. Streak joins Carlisle in the middle. 200 off 44.5 overs. Streak bowled by Khan for 10 (220 for 6). Carlisle joined by Marillier, who is caught at third man on the ropes for 1 - replaced by Taibu. Carlisle bowled Agarkar off the last ball as Zimbabwe close on 240 for 8. Agarkar finishes with 4 for 32 off 10 overs, Khan 2 for 58. <br><br><b>FALL of WICKETS</b><br>13 for 1 Campbell ct Dravid b Agarkar 3 (off 6 balls)<br>13 for 2 Friend ct Ratra b Agarkar 0 (off 3 balls)<br>79 for 3 Ebrahim ct Dravid b Ganguly 36 (off 74 balls 5x4) <br>175 for 4 G Flower ct Mongia b H Singh 44 (off 58 balls 4x4) <br>190 for 5 A Flower ct Ratra b Khan 89 (off 107 balls 6x4) <br>220 for 6 Streak b Khan 10 (off 13 balls) <br>223 for 7 Marillier ct Laxman b Agarkar 1 (off 3 balls) <br>240 for 8 Carlisle b Agarkar 40 (off 33 balls 3x4) <br>Taibu not out 3 (off 5 balls)<br>Zimbabwe 240 for 8 off 50 overs <br><br><b>RUN RATE</b><br>5 overs = 15 for 2<br>10 overs = 30 for 2<br>15 overs = 50 for 2<br>20 overs = 75 for 2<br>25 overs = 88 for 3<br>30 overs = 114 for 3<br>35 overs = 146 for 3 <br>40 overs = 176 for 4 <br>45 overs = 201 for 5<br>50 overs = 240 for 8<br><br><center><b>INDIA INNINGS</b><br>244 for 5 of 48.1 overs<br>Wins by five wickets</center><br>Mongia and Ganguly open under sparklingly bright floodlights and take 15 off Hondo's 2nd over (Mongia 3 fours in a row). Ganguly picked up at a cover by Grant Flower off Hondo for 7 (30 for 1). <br>Mongia caught low down at slip by Campbell off Friend for 30 (50 for 2). Dravid joins Laxman at the crease with a lot resting on their shoulders, but VVS (13) gets a legside tickle to Taibu off Friend (56 for 3). <br>Kaif enters the fray, as the Zimbabwe bowlers apply a stranglehold keeping the run rate at 4. 100 comes up in 24 overs, then a 50-run partnership in 81 balls. <br>Dravid unluckily plays on to G Flower for 32 (132 for 4). India's hopes are fast slipping away ... as 20-y-o Yuvraj Singh walks to the crease. Kaif reaches his 50 off 75 balls, then the 150 arrives off 36 overs. <br>As Yuvraj starts to play some great shots and with Kaif solid at the other end, India is left requiring 69 off last 10 overs. <br>The 50 partnership off 61 balls comes as India inches closer ... 200 up off 43 overs. ... 84 run partnership off 81 for Kaif and Yuvraj. Singh - who has set the match alight - catches and passes Kaif - 64 off 49 balls (7x4 and 1x6) in a remarkable innings that has swung the game back to the hosts. <br>Runs flow easily for both batsmen as the run rate drops to a most manageable 4.6 an over.<br> Zimbabwe urgently needs a wicket to haul themselves back into the match. And it arrives ... Kaif is run out failing to make his ground at the bowler's end - the third umpire's red light showing him the way back to the pavilion to a well-deserved rapturous reception. <br>Agarkar comes in with 15 needed and Yuvraj still confidently in command. <br>Another well-timed four through mid on from Yuvraj brings India to within 3 of victory. A leg glance for four by Agarkar seals the win and keeps the series alive. <br>And India has a new batting sensation to celebrate, man of the match Yuvraj ends with 80 off just 60 deliveries. <br><br> <b>FALL of WICKETS</b><br> 30 for 1 Ganguly ct G Flower b Hondo 7 (off 13 balls) <br>50 for 2 Mongia ct Campbell b Friend 30 (off 32 balls 5x4) <br>56 for 3 Laxman ct Taibu b Friend 13 (off 21 balls 2x4) <br>132 for 4 Dravid b G Flower 32 (off 56 balls) <br>226 for 5 Kaif run out 68 (off 101 balls 3x4) <br>Y Singh not out 80 (off 60 balls 8x4 1x6)<br>Agarkar not out 5 (off 3 balls 1x4)<br>India 244 for 5 off 48.1 overs <br><br><b>RUN RATE</b><br>5 overs = 25 for 0<br>10 overs = 45 for 1<br>15 overs = 62 for 3<br>20 overs = 77 for 3 <br> 25 overs = 104 for 3 <br>30 overs = 125 for 3 <br>35 overs = 146 for 4 <br>40 overs = 172 for 4 <br>45 overs = 217 for 4 <br><br><b>TEAMS</b><br>Kartik and Y Singh come in for Bangar and S Singh, while Andy Flower returns from injury for Zimbabwe in place of Wishart. <br>India: Ganguly, Mongia, Dravid, Laxman, Kaif, Ratra, Agarkar, H Singh, Khan, M Kartik, Y Singh.<br>Zimbabwe: Campbell, Carlisle, A Flower, Ebrahim, G Flower, Friend, Streak, Marillier, Taibu, Mbangwa, Hondo. <br><br><b>OFFICIALS</b><br>Umpires: Dr. MS Mahal & K Hariharan <br>Third Umpire: SC Gupta <br> Match Referee: JR Reid <br><br><b>PITCH REPORT</b><br>A warm day (35 deg) and 78% humidity - hot and sunny. The main area for concern with the pitch is texture. It's two-coloured with some areas a lot lighter than others. This may mean the ball will stay low and we could have some variable bounce. It is very dry and may turn later. So, whoever wins the toss should bat first to try and post a total of 250 then defend that total in the evening.<br><br>For further reports on this match go to <a href="http://www.wisden.com/scorecard/live/ms/#latest" title="Wisden.com" target="_blank"><i>Wisden.com</i></a></font><br>
