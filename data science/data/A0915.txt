<font color="#000033"><strong><b>

<p><font face="Arial" size="3">Foreign Exchange Hedging</font></b></strong></font></p>
<b>

<p><font face="Arial" size="2">Spot foreign Exchange</b> </font></p>

<p><font face="Arial" size="2">Spot foreign exchange is defined as the exchange of one
currency for another at an agreed rate of exchange for settlement in two business days<strong>
</font></p>

<p><font face="Arial" size="2">Useful If</strong> </font></p>

<p><font face="Arial" size="2">You have a currency requirement immediately<strong> </font></p>

<p><font face="Arial" size="2">Features </font>

<ul>
  </strong>
  <li><font face="Arial" size="2">Available in all major and most minor currencies </font></li>
  <li><font face="Arial" size="2">Agreed deal is legally binding on both yourself and the bank
    </font></li>
  <li><font face="Arial" size="2">The spot rate of exchange is determined by the rate quoted
    on the Interbank market </font></li>
</ul>
<strong>

<p><font face="Arial" size="2">Advantages</strong> </font>

<ul>
  <li><font face="Arial" size="2">Easy to arrange </font></li>
  <li><font face="Arial" size="2">Deal for immediate currency requirement </font></li>
</ul>
<strong>

<p><font face="Arial" size="2">Benefits</strong> </font>

<ul>
  <li><font face="Arial" size="2">Deal may be transacted to allow advantage to be taken on
    favourable exchange rate moves </font></li>
</ul>
<strong>

<p><font face="Arial" size="2">Charges</strong> </font>

<ul>
  <li><font face="Arial" size="2">There are no fees but a contingent liability limit is
    required </font></li>
</ul>
<b>

<p><font face="Arial" size="2">Foreign Exchange Contracts </font></b></p>

<p><font face="Arial" size="2">Allows you to fix an exchange rate now for payment or
receipt of foreign currency at a given time in the future<strong> </font></p>

<p><font face="Arial" size="2">Useful if </font>

<ul>
  </strong>
  <li><font face="Arial" size="2">You wish to eliminate the impact of adverse foreign exchange
    movements </font></li>
</ul>
<strong>

<p><font face="Arial" size="2">Features </font>

<ul>
  </strong>
  <li><font face="Arial" size="2">Currencies, amounts and settlement date are agreed at the
    start of the contract </font></li>
  <li><font face="Arial" size="2">The forward exchange rate is calculated by reference to
    interest rate differences between the two countries and involves either deducting a
    premium from or adding a discount to the spot rate of exchange </font></li>
  <li><font face="Arial" size="2">Agreed contract is legally binding on both you and the Bank
    and must be settled on maturity </font></li>
</ul>
<strong>

<p><font face="Arial" size="2">Advantages</strong> </font>

<ul>
  <li><font face="Arial" size="2">Easy to arrange </font></li>
  <li><font face="Arial" size="2">Removes uncertainty of future currency value </font></li>
  <li><font face="Arial" size="2">Allows you to lock into Cost/Profit </font></li>
</ul>
<strong>

<p><font face="Arial" size="2">Benefits</strong> </font>

<ul>
  <li><font face="Arial" size="2">Offers peace of mind in times of exchange rate volatility </font></li>
  <li><font face="Arial" size="2">Allows more accurate forward planning and budgeting </font></li>
</ul>
<strong>

<p><font face="Arial" size="2">Charges</strong> </font>

<ul>
  <li><font face="Arial" size="2">There are no fees but a contingent liability limit is
    required </font></li>
</ul>
<b>

<p><font face="Arial" size="2">Currency Options </font></b></p>

<p><font face="Arial" size="2">Purchasing a currency option gives you the right, but not
the obligation, to fix an exchange rate now for the payment or receipt of foreign currency
in the future </font></p>

<p><font face="Arial" size="2">A Currency Option provides specific exchange rate
protection whilst allowing benefit to be taken from any favourable movement in exchange
rates<strong> </font></p>

<p><font face="Arial" size="2">Useful if </font>

<ul>
  </strong>
  <li><font face="Arial" size="2">You wish to eliminate the impact of adverse foreign exchange
    movements , but wants to retain the ability to Take advantage of favourable movements </font></li>
  <li><font face="Arial" size="2">You have balance sheet exposures. </font></li>
</ul>
<strong>

<p><font face="Arial" size="2">Features </font>

<ul>
  </strong>
  <li><font face="Arial" size="2">If the exchange rate moves favourably, you have bought the
    right to walk away from the contract </font></li>
  <li><font face="Arial" size="2">If the exchange rate moves adversely, you can take up the
    option at the predetermined rate </font></li>
  <li><font face="Arial" size="2">Available in most major currencies for amounts in excess of
    £500,000 for periods of up to one year </font></li>
</ul>
<strong>

<p><font face="Arial" size="2">Advantages </font>

<ul>
  </strong>
  <li><font face="Arial" size="2">Removes uncertainty of future currency value </font></li>
</ul>
<strong>

<p><font face="Arial" size="2">Benefits</strong> </font>

<ul>
  <li><font face="Arial" size="2">Offers peace of mind in times of exchange rate volatility </font></li>
  <li><font face="Arial" size="2">Can be used in tender situations </font></li>
  <li><font face="Arial" size="2">Can be sold back to the Bank </font></li>
</ul>
<strong>

<p><font face="Arial" size="2">Charges</strong> </font>

<ul>
  <li><font face="Arial" size="2">An up front premium is payable based on various criteria at
    the time of the deal and the exchange rate chosen </font></li>
</ul>
