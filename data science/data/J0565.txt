									<p><font size="2" face="Arial,Helvetica,Geneva,Swiss,SunSans-Regular"><b>Body:</b></font></p>
									<p><font face="Arial,Helvetica,Geneva,Swiss,SunSans-Regular">JobsInMotorsports.com is an Internet job site connecting Motorsports employers and candidates. As an additonal service to the industry, JobsInMotorsports.com now offers a program to provide financial assistance to a wide variety of racing teams. The program is particularly designed to support low budget, grass-roots racing teams regardless of the series they participate in.</font></p>
								</div>
								<p>Based on the Internet &quot;Partner&quot; model, the JobsInMotorsports.com Agent Program is not strictly a sponsorship. Rather, it is a revenue sharing concept that enables JobsInMotorsports.com to contribute financially to a large number of teams without promising something it may not be able to follow through on.</p>
								<p>Racing teams &quot;recruit&quot; candidates and employers to register on and make use of the JobsInMotorsports.com website. The team receives a significant portion of the revenues resulting from the participation of those candidates and employers on our website. While JobsInMotorsports.com does not charge Motorsports employers for any of the employers services offered on the website, the revenue sharing formula actually provides incentives for registering both candidates and employers.</p>
								<p>Racing teams will have tremendous flexibility regarding how they display their participation in the JobsInMotorsport.com Agent Program and &quot;recruit&quot; candidates and employers.</p>
								<p>Racing teams interested in learning more about the program should call Rick Robins - Principal, JobsInMotorsports.com at (530) 292-9355.</p>
