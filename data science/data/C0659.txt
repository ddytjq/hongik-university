                                    <font color="#003399" size="5" face="Arial">BBC
                                    Radio 4 Wins Journalist of the Year Award<br>
                                    <b><font size="2">BIBA awards prizes for
                                    journalist excellence</font></b></font><b><br>
                                    </b>
                                    <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">Paul
                                    Charles and the BBC Radio 4 'Inside Money'
                                    team have won the 1999 British Insurance
                                    Brokers' Association Journalist of the Year
                                    award. The overall award was presented to
                                    the team for their &quot;imaginative,
                                    effective and original work which benefits
                                    the consumer.&quot;</font></p>
                                    <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">The
                                    annual event, now in its 20<sup>th</sup>
                                    year, made awards in seven categories. There
                                    were eight first prize winners, eight
                                    runners-up and five highly commended awards.
                                    BIBA received almost 100 entries.</font></p>
                                    <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">Mike
                                    Williams, BIBA chief executive, said:
                                    &quot;I am impressed by the commitment of
                                    the media to furthering the consumer's
                                    understanding about the world of general
                                    insurance. The quality and standard of all
                                    the entries was extremely high and BIBA is
                                    pleased to recognise the achievements and
                                    commitment of the journalists.&quot;</font></p>
                                    <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2"><b>Overall
                                    winner:</b> Paul Charles and BBC Radio 4
                                    'Inside Money' team.</font></p>
