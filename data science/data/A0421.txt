              about the tax paid on dividends? </b></font></td>
          </tr>
        </table>
        <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2"><b>A.</b> A tax 
          voucher is normally attached to your dividend cheque or shareholders 
          who have their dividends mandated to their bank or building society 
          account will receive a voucher at their registered address. The voucher 
          will detail tax paid. </font> 
        <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">Individual UK resident 
          shareholders will be entitled to a tax credit of one ninth of the dividend. 
          The tax credit will discharge the liability to income tax for individual 
          shareholders that are subject to UK income tax at the lower or basic 
          rate. Higher rate taxpayers will be able to offset the tax credit against 
          their liability to tax on the gross dividend (the value of the dividend 
          plus the tax credit), and will pay tax at the rate of 32.5% on the gross
