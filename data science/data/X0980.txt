<h2 align="center">What's new at HuntGuide.com</h2>

	<dl>
	<dt>12/01/99<dd>HuntGuide.com is under new management.&nbsp; Dennis Latsha and Kirk Fox become the new owners of HuntGuide.com.
</dl>
	<dl>
	<dt>7/12/99<dd>ProStaff announces trip to
Texas for upcoming video. This great opportunity puts us on a 12,000 acre ranch that has
not been commercially hunted since 1905. We will be taking our bows and our cameras to
visit our outfitter for what should be a great hunt!
</dl>

<dl>
	<dt>6/25/99<dd>Premier game call manufacturer Woods Wise Products has become a sponsor for HuntGuide.com's upcoming hunting video. Regarding the development, HuntGuide.com Founder Markus Muhlhauser stated, &quot;Having called in many bucks, Woods Wise calls have been a favorite call of mine for years, and we are confident that this new relationship between Woods Wise and HuntGuide.com will be a lasting and mutually beneficial one.&quot;<br><br>

He went on to say, &quot;With the expertise of folks like Jerry Peterson (President of Woods Wise Products)combined with the help of their excellent game calls, the 1999 season will hopefully provide some excellent on-camera calling and hunting footage for HuntGuide.com followers.&quot;
</dl>

<dl>
	    <dt>5/27/99
        <dd>ProStaff Announces addition of a fall Black Bear hunt for the 1999 season. ProStaffer Markus Muhlhauser and HuntGuide.com Videographer Allen Roe will be traveling to Maine the first week of September to hunt with Sportsman's Pride Outfitters. This is sure to be a great hunt a wonderful addition to the 1999 ProStaff Hunting Season Video.
</dl>

<dl>
	<dt>5/14/99<dd>Great News! By popular demand for all of you hunting fanatics, HuntGuide.com will be producing a video outlining the upcoming 1999 ProStaff Hunting Season. This professionally made video is currently scheduled to feature numerous hunts including:
<ul type="square">
	<li>Six Hunts in Canada for Woodland Caribou, Black Bear, and Moose - (Archery and Rifle)
	<li>Three Bow Hunts in the Northeast for Whitetails
	<li>One Midwest Bowhunt for Whitetails
	<li>One Pronghorn Bowhunt
	<li>Turkey Hunting in the Midwest and Texas
	<li>Three Exciting Monster Buck Bow Hunts in Louisiana
	<li>And last but not least, Texas Whitetail Hunting
	</ul>
	With so many exciting hunts caught on tape, this video is sure to be a winner. To be sent information on the video upon it's release, put yourself on our mailing list by <a href="mailto:DLatsha@aol.com">EMailing</a> Us.
</dl>

<dl>
	<dt>4/12/1999<dd>Olympic Gold Medalist &quot;Butch&quot; Johnson officially joins the HuntGuide.com ProStaff. We are honored to have Butch on board with us. With his detailed knowledge of archery, archery equipment, and bowhunting, Butch will certainly be a great asset to HuntGuide.com. Look for Butch in our upcoming season and well as articles from Butch. See his quick bio on the <a href="prostaff.htm">ProStaff Page</a>
</dl>
