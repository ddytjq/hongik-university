<p><font size="2" face="Times new roman"><B>
What is a Fixed Rate ISA?</B><BR><BR>
A 'Tax Free' account fixed at 5.2% until 5 April 2005 for those aged 16 or&nbsp;
over and resident in the UK (or serving member of the Armed Forces poster
overseas). The Ipswich Fixed Rate ISA is not a CAT standard product.</font></p>
<p><font size="2" face="Times new roman"><B>
Withdrawal:</B>   No withdrawals are allowed during the Fixed Rate period.&nbsp;
Closure will only take place in the event of the death of the account holder.
<BR><BR><B>
Minimum investment:</B>   £500 (Any existing Ipswich Building Society
Young Saver or Top Saver account holders reaching the age of 16 years may
open a Fixed Rate ISA account with just £100.)
<BR><BR><B>
Maximum investment:</B>  Up to £3000.&nbsp; No additional investments are
allowed once the account has been opened.
<BR><BR><B>
Interest: </B>Paid on the 5 April each year and added to the ISA. Interest&nbsp;
is not subject to Personal Income Tax or Capital Gains Tax.
<BR><BR>
Our staff will be delighted to open an account for you.</font></p>
