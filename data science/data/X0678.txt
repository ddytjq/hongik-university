<b>Saturday, 22 September 2001</b><p>

The new (and old) Swiss LD champion is... Manuel, of course. Congratulations ! He wins the tournement in Kloten for the fourth consecutive time: 1998 with 155m (World Record), 1999 with 238m (World Record), 2000 with 191m, this year with 160m "only". Read why further below.

Appalling wind conditions made it very difficult to get a valid return. It was dead f... calm most of the time. Moreover, it was very humid and rainy. A lot of people left the field with a zero score... Manuel had one valid throw only. With a very gently touch of wind, it barely crossed the line. Congratulations to Stephan, too. He came second with a personal best of 126m, achieved mainly with brute force (his throw is not as strong as Manuel's, though). David proved that he still is a respectable LD thrower with his score of 117 m, thrown with a Buzz Whip built by Manuel.<p>
