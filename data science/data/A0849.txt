<a name="#cover"></a><span class="bodytextbold">
What will my insurance cover me for? 
</span>

<br>

<span class="bodytext">
You can choose from our Contents Insurance, Buildings Insurance or both. Our Contents Insurance provides automatic cover of £35,000 and covers you for the main risks you may experience. Buildings Insurance covers you for a reinstatement value of £250,000 worth of damage. For full details of what’s included in your insurance, request our Home Insurance Information Leaflet or speak to one of our operators by calling 0845 600 1601. We also offer extra optional services for a small charge such as Accidental Damage Cover, Personal Possessions Cover, No Claim Discount Protection and Home Emergency Legal Protection.  
</span>

<a name="#claim"></a><span class="bodytextbold">
How do I make a claim? 
</span>
<br>

<span class="bodytext">
Once you’ve checked your policy and schedule carefully to make sure that the loss or damage is covered under this insurance, phone our claims department on 0845 600 2173 as soon as possible to report it. One of our operators will tell you what to do next. We will send you a claim form which you will need to check (and change if any of the details you have given us are not correct or complete) and sign. Return the form with any other information that we ask for. 
</span>

<a name="#havent"></a><span class="bodytextbold">
What if I haven’t made a claim in the last year or more? 
</span>

<br>

<span class="bodytext">
If you haven’t made a claim on your insurance in the last year or more, let us know and we’ll give you up to 30% No Claim Discount on your contents and buildings insurance. Also, for a small additional charge, your No Claim Discount can be protected.
</span>

<a name="#insurance"></a><span class="bodytextbold">
What is the difference between buildings and contents insurance?
</span>

<br>

<span class="bodytext">
Buildings insurance in general covers the building and related fittings and fixtures.  Contents is everything which is contained within your building.  For example,  this would include personal possessions and furnishings.  Details of the items covered by our buildings and contents insurance can be found in our policy documents.
</span>

<a name="#howmuch"></a><span class="bodytextbold">
How much should I insure my building and contents for?
</span>

<br>

<span class="bodytext">
Buildings - This should take into consideration the cost of completely rebuilding your home - including Building Costs, Architect, Surveyor and Legal Fees and any other associated costs.  To find out the value of rebuilding your home, you can find this on the valuation report or survey you had done when you took out your mortgage.  However, this figure may have changed due to increases in inflation but don’t worry, our policy is inflation linked so we’ll even cover you if the value of rebuilding your house has gone up.  You’ll also be covered for a wide range of accidental damage which is detailed in our policy documents.
<P>
Contents - as a general rule, you should include all your possessions and take into consideration how much it would cost to replace them today - remember, we replace everything on a new for old basis*.
<P>
* If you make a claim, we will replace any article (s) with one (s) of the same make and specification (if not available with one of a similar make and specification).  If the article that you are making a claim for is subject to wear and tear e.g. clothing, this will be taken into consideration and deductions may be made.

</span>

<a name="#valuable"></a><span class="bodytextbold">
What about valuable items?
</span>

<br>

<span class="bodytext">
Unlike many insurers the policies we offer do not make a distinction between valuable articles and everyday articles - as long as they have been included in the total value of your possessions, they’ll be covered by our policy. 
</span>

<a name="#computer"></a><span class="bodytextbold">
If I have a computer, will it be covered by my contents insurance?
</span>

<br>

<span class="bodytext">
Yes, your contents are covered up to £35,000 of automatic cover, which not only includes computer equipment but also TV, Video and Audio equipment.
</span>

<a name="#excess"></a><span class="bodytextbold">
Do I have to pay an excess if I make a claim?
</span>

<br>

<span class="bodytext">
Yes, we currently charge an excess of £50.00 so whether your claim is for £3,000 or £30,000 you will only ever have to pay £50.00 (unless you make a claim for subsidence which carries an excess of £1,000).
