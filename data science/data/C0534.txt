            <p class="font"><b>World Trade Center Losses</b></p>
            <p class=MsoBodyText3 style='text-align:justify'><span style="class=" font="font" class="font">On 
              20 September 2001, the Board of Chaucer Holdings PLC estimated that, 
              in respect of the events of 11 September, the Group could incur 
              a pre-tax loss of between £8 million and £12 million, </span><span lang="EN-US" class="font">equivalent</span><span class="font"> 
              to between 7p and 10p per share after tax.  It was stressed that 
              making an early estimate such as this was extremely difficult and 
              would be subject to change as further information became available.  
              The Chaucer Group has now submitted further returns to Lloyd’s and 
              is pleased to confirm that its estimates of potential Group pre-tax 
              losses from these events remain unchanged.</span></p>
