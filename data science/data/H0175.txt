        <h2 align="center" style="text-align:center"><a name="_Toc496587792"></a><a
        name="_Toc496587880"></a><a name="_Toc496587977"></a><a
        name="INSTRUCTIONS FOR COMPLETING THE FORMS"><span style="mso-bookmark:_Toc496587977"><span
        style="mso-bookmark:_Toc496587880"><span style="mso-bookmark:_Toc496587792"><span
        lang="EN-US" style="font-size:18.0pt;
mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt">INSTRUCTIONS FOR
        COMPLETING THE FORMS</span></span></span></span></a><span lang="EN-US"
        style="font-size:18.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt"><o:p></o:p></span></h2>
        <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US"><o:p>Standard WHO data forms will be provided on
        diskette to country teams, and forms will be customised and printed locally.<span
        style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>(May need to be in local language, in view of
        personnel collecting the data?)</span></p>
        <p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-top:3.0pt"><span lang="EN-US">The questionnaire should
        be administered to the mother in privacy by a trained attendant, at a time when she is
        rested and at leisure.</span></p>
        <h3><span lang="EN-US">Selection of participants</span></h3>
        <p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-top:3.0pt"><span lang="EN-US">All women delivering in
        the selected institution or geographical area will be invited to join the study.<span
        style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>If this is not possible, participants must be
        selected in a way that ensures they form a random sample, e.g. those delivering on certain
        days of the week.</span></p>
        <h3><span lang="EN-US">Consent</span></h3>
        <p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-top:3.0pt"><span lang="EN-US">All participants must
        give consent to join the study.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>If consent is
        refused the mother's wish must be respected. (But what if a mother who refuses
        consent has a malformed baby or stillbirth? Probably pregnancy outcome should be recorded
        in any case?)</span></p>
        <h3><span lang="EN-US">Identifiers</span></h3>
        <p class="ListBulletmr" style="margin-left:32.2pt;tab-stops:-72.0pt -36.0pt list 32.2pt"><span
        lang="EN-US" style="font-family:Symbol">·<span
        style="font:7.0pt "Times New Roman"">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span><span
        lang="EN-US"><i style="mso-bidi-font-style:normal">Epidemiological study:</i><span
        style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>Country code, institution code (when the study is
        hospital based) and mother's serial number are the necessary identifiers.<span
        style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span></span></p>
        <p class="ListBulletm"
        style="margin-left:32.2pt;text-indent:0cm;mso-list:none;
tab-stops:-72.0pt -36.0pt"><span
        lang="EN-US">When the study is community-based, identifiers should include postcode or
        equivalent.</span></p>
        <p class="ListBulletmr" style="margin-left:32.2pt;tab-stops:-72.0pt -36.0pt list 32.2pt"><span
        lang="EN-US" style="font-family:Symbol">·<span
        style="font:7.0pt "Times New Roman"">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span><span
        lang="EN-US"><i style="mso-bidi-font-style:normal">Follow-up study:</i><span
        style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>If a follow-up study is selected, identifiers must
        include exact name and address of the mother, and any other details necessary to ensure
        follow-up of the baby. A key element in ensuring effective follow-up in other long-term
        studies (e.g. the UK birth cohort studies) has been to send the parents / child a birthday
        card every year, with a request to reply, and reminder to report any change of address.
        (This incidentally provides an opportunity to test mothers' reading ability when
        there is any doubt, by asking the mother to read an example of a follow-up letter.)</span></p>
        <p class="ListBulletmr" style="margin-left:32.2pt;tab-stops:-72.0pt -36.0pt list 32.2pt"><span
        lang="EN-US" style="font-family:Symbol">·<span
        style="font:7.0pt "Times New Roman"">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span><span
        lang="EN-US"><i style="mso-bidi-font-style:normal">Use of existing hospital notes:</i><span
        style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>Study workers may take data from existing notes
        (this may speed parts of the questionnaire up), but should check the data with the mother
        in the course of the interview.<span style="mso-spacerun:
yes">&nbsp; </span>Use of the
        notes will allow entry of data such as mother's Rh and other blood groups,
        isoimmunisation etc.</span></p>
        <h2 align="center" style="text-align:center"><a name="_Toc496587796"></a><a
        name="_Toc496587881"></a><a name="_Toc496587978"></a><a
        name="MOTHER'S DATA COLLECTION SHEET 1"><span style="mso-bookmark:_Toc496587978"><span
        style="mso-bookmark:_Toc496587881"><span style="mso-bookmark:_Toc496587796"><span
        lang="EN-US">MOTHER'S DATA COLLECTION SHEET 1</span></span></span></span></a></h2>
        <h3><span lang="EN-US">Country code</span></h3>
        <p class="ListBulletm"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:
Symbol">·<span
        style="font:7.0pt "Times New Roman"">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span><span
        lang="EN-US">The official WHO country code will be printed on forms produced locally.</span></p>
        <h3><span lang="EN-US">Institution code (or name) </span></h3>
        <p class="ListBulletm"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:
Symbol">·<span
        style="font:7.0pt "Times New Roman"">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span><span
        lang="EN-US">This should appear on customised country forms printed locally.</span></p>
        <h3><span lang="EN-US">Serial numbers</span></h3>
        <p class="ListBulletmr"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:
Symbol">·<span
        style="font:7.0pt "Times New Roman"">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span><span
        lang="EN-US">Each mother's computer-generated serial number will be unique in each
        country.</span></p>
        <p class="ListBulletmr"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:
Symbol">·<span
        style="font:7.0pt "Times New Roman"">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span><span
        lang="EN-US">Each mother's form will have 1 correspondingly-numbered baby's form
        attached.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span></span></p>
        <p class="ListBulletm"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:
Symbol">·<span
        style="font:7.0pt "Times New Roman"">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span><span
        lang="EN-US">In case of multiple births a new un-numbered baby form must be produced, and
        numbered by the interviewer (see below for coding of baby forms).</span></p>
        <h3><span lang="EN-US">Mother's ID number</span></h3>
        <p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-top:3.0pt"><span lang="EN-US">In many countries adults
        have a unique social security number that allows them to be identified reliably.<span
        style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>For follow-up studies such an identifier should be
        recorded.</span></p>
        <h3><span lang="EN-US">Consent</span></h3>
        <p class="ListBulletmr"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:
Symbol">·<span
        style="font:7.0pt "Times New Roman"">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span><span
        lang="EN-US">This could be quite difficult, as many mothers may feel unable to give
        consent unless they have first discussed it with their husband.</span></p>
        <p class="ListBulletmr"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:
Symbol">·<span
        style="font:7.0pt "Times New Roman"">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span><span
        lang="EN-US">For an epidmemiological study, some participants favoured simply informing
        the mother that the study is taking place. If consent is refused, should mother's
        form finish here, or should outcome of this pregnancy be reported (to ensure extent of
        bias if any)?</span></p>
        <p class="ListBulletm"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:
Symbol">·<span
        style="font:7.0pt "Times New Roman"">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span><span
        lang="EN-US">Parents' consent and information for the parents will definitely be
        needed for a follow-up study. Experience will be needed.</span></p>
        <h3><span lang="EN-US">Parents' names<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span></span></h3>
        <p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-top:3.0pt"><span lang="EN-US">Mother's and
        father's names are always recorded on the data collection sheets. They are entered in
        the computer only for a named study.</span></p>
        <h3><span lang="EN-US">Mother's date of birth</span></h3>
        <p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-top:3.0pt"><span lang="EN-US">Day/month/year e.g. 01 01
        66.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>This will not always be known.<span
        style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>In this case an approximation should be obtained
        (may be based on year of marriage in some cases).</span></p>
        <h3><span lang="EN-US">Address</span></h3>
        <p class="ListBulletmr"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:
Symbol">·<span
        style="font:7.0pt "Times New Roman"">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span><span
        lang="EN-US">Will be entered on form, but not on computer (except for a named study).</span></p>
        <p class="ListBulletmr"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:
Symbol">·<span
        style="font:7.0pt "Times New Roman"">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span><span
        lang="EN-US">Discuss availability of post-codes or similar, in tracing families for
        follow-up studies.</span></p>
        <p class="ListBulletm"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:
Symbol">·<span
        style="font:7.0pt "Times New Roman"">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span><span
        lang="EN-US">Telephone number will not be recorded, except for a follow-up study.</span></p>
        <h3><span lang="EN-US">Primary health care unit attended in this pregnancy</span></h3>
        <p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-top:3.0pt"><span lang="EN-US">Necessary for a follow-up
        study.</span></p>
        <h3 align="left" style="text-align:left"><span lang="EN-US">Date and time of this
        baby's birth. Day, month, year (e.g. 01 08 96)</span></h3>
        <p class="ListBulletmr"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:
Symbol">·<span
        style="font:7.0pt "Times New Roman"">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span><span
        lang="EN-US">Time of birth.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>24 hour clock.
        Hour, minutes.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>e.g. 08 50.<span
        style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>or 23 10.</span></p>
        <p class="ListBulletm"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:
Symbol">·<span
        style="font:7.0pt "Times New Roman"">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span><span
        lang="EN-US">It is essential to enter time, for interpretation of Guthrie results.</span></p>
        <h3><span lang="EN-US">Date and time of interview (record as above)</span></h3>
        <h3><span lang="EN-US">Age at marriage to this husband:<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">
        &nbsp; </span>years</span></h3>
        <p class="ListBulletmr"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:
Symbol">·<span
        style="font:7.0pt "Times New Roman"">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span><span
        lang="EN-US">May be the most reliable indicator of maternal age in some cases. Though
        divorce is uncommon, widowhood is not. </span></p>
        <p class="ListBulletmr"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:
Symbol">·<span
        style="font:7.0pt "Times New Roman"">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span><span
        lang="EN-US">A check-list is needed for each country. It must include &#147;none&#148; and
        &#147;unknown&#148;.</span></p>
        <p class="ListBulletm"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:
Symbol">·<span
        style="font:7.0pt "Times New Roman"">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span><span
        lang="EN-US">Harmonisation may be achieved at first WHO workshop.</span></p>
        <h3><span lang="EN-US">Years of formal education.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span></span></h3>
        <p class="ListBulletmr"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:
Symbol">·<span
        style="font:7.0pt "Times New Roman"">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span><span
        lang="EN-US">Record separately for mother and father.</span></p>
        <p class="ListBulletm"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:
Symbol">·<span
        style="font:7.0pt "Times New Roman"">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span><span
        lang="EN-US">Code 0 - however many years of education.</span></p>
        <h3><span lang="EN-US">Able to read?</span></h3>
        <p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-top:3.0pt"><span lang="EN-US">It may be advisable to
        check the mother's literacy, especially if a long-term follow-up study is
        contemplated.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>This may be done by asking her
        to check a standard letter of the kind that may be sent to her to maintain contact.</span></p>
        <h3><span lang="EN-US">Occupation</span></h3>
