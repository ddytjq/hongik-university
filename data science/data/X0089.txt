<H1>Subaru/IMBA Trail Care Crew Schedule</H1>

<H3><I>New 2002 Schedule Online Now</I></H3>

<P> IMBA plans to arrange longer-lasting visits to each stop this year.
More days in a single location will facilitate tangible trailbuilding
accomplishments, strengthen partnerships with land managers and reduce
the wear-and-tear on our hardworking field leaders. We also plan to
schedule more high-profile Trailbuilding Schools. </P>

<P> <B>Here's a short list of our 2002 Trail Care Crew priorities:</B> </P>

<P> <OL><LI> National Parks (and other NPS-managed sites, including
National Recreation Areas). 
 <LI> "Hot Spots." IMBA is committed to improving riding
opportunities in urban areas (which by nature have a large population,
limited open space, intense user pressure, and as a result less-than
desirable mountain bike access). 
<LI> Bureau of Land Management, US Forest Service or Army Corps of
Engineers-managed areas. </ol> </P>


<P> Now beginning its sixth year, the Subaru/IMBA Trail Care Crew
program is more popular than ever. As much as we'd like to be able to
fulfill all visit requests, we can't. We thank you in advance for your
understanding, cooperation and continuing support. </P>
