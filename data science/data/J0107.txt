 2002</span></st1:date><span style='font-size:11.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt'><o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><b style='mso-bidi-font-weight:normal'><span

the event the world's richest vintage car competition. <o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-size:11.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt'><o:p>&nbsp;</o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><b style='mso-bidi-font-weight:normal'><span
style='font-size:11.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt'>Entrants:<span
style='mso-spacerun:yes'>  </span></span></b><span style='font-size:11.0pt;
mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt'>Road warriors range from a plumber to a NASCAR team
owner competing in four divisions: pro, expert or sportsman, while high school
student teams run in the X-Cup division. <o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-size:11.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt'><o:p>&nbsp;</o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><b style='mso-bidi-font-weight:normal'><span
style='font-size:11.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt'>The Cars:</span></b><span
style='font-size:11.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt'><span
style='mso-spacerun:yes'>  </span>A $3 million collection of vintage cars
including a 1917 Hudson Indy car, a rare 1949 VW convertible, 1934 Ford Indy
car, 1925 Pierce Arrow and a 1925 <span class=SpellE>Rickenbacker</span>. <br
style='mso-special-character:line-break'>
<![if !supportLineBreakNewLine]><br style='mso-special-character:line-break'>
<![endif]><o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><b style='mso-bidi-font-weight:normal'><span
style='font-size:11.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt'>The Stops:<span
style='mso-spacerun:yes'>  </span></span></b><span style='font-size:11.0pt;
mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt'>More than 20 cities host the Great Racers with a
celebrity-style welcome. Always free to the public, fans along the way enjoy an
up close and personal view of the vintage machines. Family-focused activities
include parades, local car shows, festivals, bands and coloring contests for
the kids. <o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-size:11.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt'><o:p>&nbsp;</o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><b style='mso-bidi-font-weight:normal'><span
style='font-size:11.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt'>City Awards:<span
style='mso-spacerun:yes'>  </span></span></b><span style='font-size:11.0pt;
mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt'>Communities pool resources to create special events
and compete for a share of Awards: The Great American City Awards, $10,000
total in cash prizes for the public libraries in winning cities. Award criteria
includes hospitality, crowd enthusiasm and venue decoration. <o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-size:11.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt'><o:p>&nbsp;</o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><b style='mso-bidi-font-weight:normal'><span
style='font-size:11.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt'>History:<span
style='mso-spacerun:yes'>   </span></span></b><span style='font-size:11.0pt;
mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt'>Great Racers logged more than 5,000,000 vintage
miles in 19 years, and been the center of free family celebrations in more than
800 cities on the North American continent. More than 300,000 spectators attend
Great Race stops each year. The total cash and prizes awarded to date exceed
$3,750,000. More than 300,000 fans turn out along the course each year to cheer
for the adventurers in their marvelous machines. Past stops include The Eclipse
at the White House, The Statue of Liberty Centennial Celebration, Disneyland,
Disney World, the <span class=SpellE>Reforma</span> in Mexico City, the Grand
Canyon and a parade down Broadway, New York. <o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><span style='font-size:11.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt'><o:p>&nbsp;</o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><b style='mso-bidi-font-weight:normal'><span
style='font-size:11.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt'>Who Qualifies:</span></b><span
style='font-size:11.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt'><span
style='mso-spacerun:yes'>   </span>The age limit includes pre-1951 vehicles and
pre-1960 sports cars, race cars and motorcycles. They may be stock or as
modified in their era of manufacture. Allowed modern modifications include
12-volt electrics, any point-type distributor, overdrive units, auxiliary
cooling systems and internal engine components. No electronic or digital
components of any type are allowed. High <span class=GramE>performance</span>
modifications avail able during the year of manufacture of the vehicle are
acceptable. Complete Authorized Modifications are included in the Entry Kit. <br>
