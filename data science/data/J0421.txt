1978 Chevy Impala Wagon
<BR>
350 c.i. engine (out of the 81 Malibu) - paid $00.00 (free car)
<p>
The previous owner of the car pulled out the engine, trans, radiator ect...
<br>
I pulled the motor out of last years Malibu and installed it in the wagon.  
<p>
Since the car was free, I spent my money on a new starter, new waterpump, new hoses, new belts and a new aircleaner.
<br>
I notched the frame and dented the roof to try and make sure the back end goes up.
<p>
I drew a tough heat and got creamed. In spite of my efforts to make the back end go up, it went down.
<br>
Ended up busting a driveshaft.  The back end was in such bad shape, that I sold the car for scrap ($30).
