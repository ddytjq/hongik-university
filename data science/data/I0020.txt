    <h2><!--mstheme--><font color="#330099">Targeting The Football Task Force:<b style="mso-bidi-font-weight:normal"><u><span style="font-size:14.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Arial;mso-bidi-font-family:

<h5><!--mstheme--><font color="#330099"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight:normal"><span style="font-size:14.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Arial;mso-bidi-font-family:
&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">Campaigning for a football regulator,
redistribution of wealth and a fair deal for fans.<u><o:p>
</o:p>
</u></span></b><!--mstheme--></font></h5>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align:justify"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;
font-family:Arial;mso-bidi-font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">The Football
Task Force (FTF) is our first and clearest opportunity to make our feelings
heard and have some real influence. The FTF was established by the Labour
Government to look into a number of concerns in football and to make
recommendations to the Minister for Sport about what action should be taken. It
has been in existence for two years and has consulted widely, including with
over 70 fans organisations. It has issued reports on racism, disabled access and
football in the community. The remaining areas it has to consider are:<o:p>
</o:p>
</span></p>
<!--mstheme--></font><!--msthemelist--><table border="0" cellpadding="0" cellspacing="0" width="100%">
  <!--msthemelist--><tr><td valign="baseline" width="42"><img src="_themes/blends-2/blebul1a.gif" width="15" height="15" hspace="13"></td><td valign="top" width="100%"><!--mstheme--><font face="Trebuchet MS, Arial, Helvetica">
    <p style="margin-left:14.15pt;text-indent:-14.15pt;mso-list:
l0 level1 lfo1"><span style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.0pt; mso-bidi-font-family: Times New Roman">Encourage
    greater supporter involvement in the running of clubs</span><!--mstheme--></font><!--msthemelist--></td></tr>
  <!--msthemelist--><tr><td valign="baseline" width="42"><img src="_themes/blends-2/blebul1a.gif" width="15" height="15" hspace="13"></td><td valign="top" width="100%"><!--mstheme--><font face="Trebuchet MS, Arial, Helvetica">
    <p style="margin-left:14.15pt;text-indent:-14.15pt;mso-list:
l0 level1 lfo1"><span style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.0pt; mso-bidi-font-family: Times New Roman">Encourage
    ticketing and pricing policies that are geared to reflect the needs of all,
    on an equitable basis, including for cup and international matches</span><!--mstheme--></font><!--msthemelist--></td></tr>
  <!--msthemelist--><tr><td valign="baseline" width="42"><img src="_themes/blends-2/blebul1a.gif" width="15" height="15" hspace="13"></td><td valign="top" width="100%"><!--mstheme--><font face="Trebuchet MS, Arial, Helvetica">
    <p style="margin-left:14.15pt;text-indent:-14.15pt;mso-list:
l0 level1 lfo1">Reconcile the potential conflict between the legitimate needs of
    shareholders, players and supporters where clubs have been floated on the
    Stock Exchange.<!--mstheme--></font><!--msthemelist--></td></tr>
  <!--msthemelist--><tr><td valign="baseline" width="42"><img src="_themes/blends-2/blebul1a.gif" width="15" height="15" hspace="13"></td><td valign="top" width="100%"><!--mstheme--><font face="Trebuchet MS, Arial, Helvetica">
    <p style="margin-left:14.15pt;text-indent:-14.15pt;mso-list:
l0 level1 lfo1">Encourage merchandising policies that reflect the needs of
    supporters as well as commercial considerations.<span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Arial;mso-bidi-font-family:
&quot;Times New Roman&quot;"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight:normal"><o:p>
    </o:p>
    </b></span><!--mstheme--></font><!--msthemelist--></td></tr>
<!--msthemelist--></table><!--mstheme--><font face="Trebuchet MS, Arial, Helvetica">
<p style="margin-left:14.15pt;text-indent:-14.15pt;mso-list:
l0 level1 lfo1" align="left"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight:normal"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Arial;mso-bidi-font-family:
&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp;<o:p>
</o:p>
</span></b><span style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.0pt; mso-bidi-font-family: Times New Roman">However,
this final, “commercial” report is also likely to cover the issue of the
need for <b style="mso-bidi-font-weight:normal">proper regulation</b> in
football, not least to ensure that Task Force proposals are carried out. It is a
golden opportunity to get the Government to accept the concerns of many football
fans and to have a real impact on how football is run in the future.</span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;
font-family:Arial;mso-bidi-font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;"><o:p>

<h2><!--mstheme--><font color="#330099"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;

</o:p>
</span>What you can do now...<b style="mso-bidi-font-weight:normal"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Arial;mso-bidi-font-family:
&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp;<o:p>

    <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight:normal"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Arial;mso-bidi-font-family:

<h3><!--mstheme--><font color="#330099"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;
font-family:Arial;mso-bidi-font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp;<o:p>
</o:p>
</span><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight:normal"><u><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Arial;mso-bidi-font-family:
&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">Content</span></u></b><u><span style="font-size:12.0pt;
mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Arial;mso-bidi-font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;"><o:p>
</o:p>
</span></u><!--mstheme--></font></h3>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;
font-family:Arial;mso-bidi-font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">You need to
highlight what you see as the problems and what you want done about them. Relate
these to the Task Force remit, but also the broader concerns of the CoFS
charter:<o:p>
</o:p>
</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;
font-family:Arial;mso-bidi-font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">Relate it to
your experience. In particular you should say why you or your group is concerned
that action is taken on these issues. For example, if ticket prices or
allocations are a problem, give some very brief details; if your club is
suffering because of the way TV money is redistributed - give brief details.
However, also highlight that your concern is not just motivated by self interest
but also for the good of the game as a whole.<o:p>
</o:p>
</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;
font-family:Arial;mso-bidi-font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">If your
organisation has given evidence to the FTF, say so and say what you argued for.<o:p>
</o:p>
</span></p>
<h5><!--mstheme--><font color="#330099"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;
font-family:Arial;mso-bidi-font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;"><o:p>
</o:p>
</span><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight:normal"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Arial;mso-bidi-font-family:
&quot;Times New Roman&quot;"><u>The core concerns need to be raised:</u><o:p>
</o:p>
</span></b><!--mstheme--></font></h5>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;
font-family:Arial;mso-bidi-font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">i)
meaningful fan role in decision making at every level in the game (elected fan
on board, elected fan at FA council etc.);<o:p>
</o:p>
</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;
font-family:Arial;mso-bidi-font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">ii) an end
to the spiraling rise in ticket prices, not to go up ahead of the rate of
inflation;<o:p>
</o:p>
</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;
font-family:Arial;mso-bidi-font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">iii)
restrictions on role of PLCs, a block to media companies owning significant
interests in clubs, controls over ‘fit and proper’ people owning clubs,
encourage supporter ownership;<o:p>
</o:p>
</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;
font-family:Arial;mso-bidi-font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">iv) better
redistribution of wealth;<o:p>
</o:p>
</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;
font-family:Arial;mso-bidi-font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">v) These
need enforcement - if the FA and Premier League won't or can't then the call
must be for the Government to act to protect the national game by setting up a
an independent and powerful football regulator<o:p>
</o:p>
</span></p>
<h5><!--mstheme--><font color="#330099"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;
font-family:Arial;mso-bidi-font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp;<o:p>
</o:p>
</span><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight:normal"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Arial;mso-bidi-font-family:
&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">Please note: <o:p>
</o:p></span></b><!--mstheme--></font></h5>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Symbol">·<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
</span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;
font-family:Arial;mso-bidi-font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">Say who you
are<o:p>
</o:p>
</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left:18.0pt;text-indent:-18.0pt;mso-list:l0 level1 lfo2"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Symbol">·<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
</span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;
font-family:Arial;mso-bidi-font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">Urge them to
back your and the CoFS agenda<o:p>
</o:p>
</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left:18.0pt;text-indent:-18.0pt;mso-list:l0 level1 lfo2"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Symbol">·<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
</span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;
font-family:Arial;mso-bidi-font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">Keep it
simple, firm and to the point<o:p>
</o:p>
</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left:18.0pt;text-indent:-18.0pt;mso-list:l0 level1 lfo2"><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Symbol">·<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
</span></span><span style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;
font-family:Arial;mso-bidi-font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">Ask them to
reply to you outlining their views and what they will do.<o:p>
</o:p>
