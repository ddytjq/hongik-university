      <p><b><font size="3" color="#000066"><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2"><a name="Introduction"></a></font></font><font size="3" color="#000066" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">Introduction</font></b></p>
      <p><font face="Arial" size="2">Buying your home is probably the biggest
      financial decision you will ever make. It can cause confusion and stress.
      So choosing the correct mortgage is important and why we want to help you
      to get it right.</font></p>
      <p><font face="Arial" size="2">The following sets out what you will have to
      do to obtain a mortgage from us, and answers your questions along the way.</font></p>
      <p><font face="Arial" size="2">You should read this document together with
      details of our products which are available on request.</font></p>
      <p><font face="Arial" size="2">We subscribe to the <a href="http://www.mortgagecode.org.uk/" target="_blank">Mortgage
      Code</a>, a copy of which is available to you on request. When you talk to
      one of our mortgage advisers you will be offered a choice of service
      available to you, namely:</font></p>
      <ul>
        <li>
          <p style="line-height: 100%"><font face="Arial" size="2">advice and a
          recommendation as to which mortgage is most suitable for you;</font></li>
        <li>
          <p style="line-height: 100%"><font face="Arial" size="2">information
          on the different types of mortgage product available so that you can
          make an informed choice of which to take;&nbsp;</font></li>
        <li>
          <p style="line-height: 100%"><font face="Arial" size="2">information
          on a single product only if you have already made up your mind.</font></li>
      </ul>
      <p><font face="Arial" size="2">Our mortgage advisers will help you choose
      the mortgage that is right for you.</font></p>
      <p><font face="Arial" size="2">A written quotation may be obtained on
      request.</font></p>
      <p><font face="Arial" size="2">Loans are not available for persons under
      18 years of age.</font></p>

      <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2"><b><font size="3" color="#000066"><a name="whatis"></a>What 
        is a Mortgage?</font></b></font></p>
      <p><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Arial" size="2">A mortgage is a legal
      agreement between you (the borrower) and us (the Lender to enable you to
      purchase your own home. Under the terms of the agreement we will lend you
      a certain sum of money and you will pay it back to us over an agreed
      number of years.</font></span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:&quot;Trebuchet MS&quot;"><o:p>
      </o:p>
      </span></p>
      <p align="center"><a href="#top"><img src="../images/backtotop.gif" alt="Back To Top" border="0" width="75" height="26"></a><br>
        <img src="../images/divider_mort.gif" width="500" height="38"></p>
      <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2"><b><br>
        <font size="3" color="#000066"><a name="choosing"></a>Choosing Your Home</font></b></font></p>
      <p><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-US" style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.0pt">If
      you are looking to buy a property you may want to contact local estate
      agents and ask them to send you information about suitable properties for
      you in your area. Clay Cross Building Society will lend money for most
      types of property so long as it is suitable for residential owner
      occupation.<o:p>
      </o:p>
      </span>
        </font></p>
      <p class="MsoNormal"><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-US" style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.0pt">However
      there are a few things you should consider when looking for a property,
      including:&nbsp;<o:p>
      </o:p>
      </span></font></p>
      <p class="MsoNormal"><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-US" style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.0pt">How
      much can you borrow?&nbsp;<o:p>
      </o:p>
      </span></font></p>
      <p class="MsoNormal"><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-US" style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.0pt">How
      much work needs to be done on the property?</span></font><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-US" style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.0pt">&nbsp;<o:p>
      </o:p>
      </span></font></p>
      <p class="MsoNormal"><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-US" style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.0pt">Is
      there a public transport service, if you need one?</span></font><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-US" style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.0pt">&nbsp;<o:p>
      </o:p>
      </span></font></p>
      <p class="MsoNormal"><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-US" style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.0pt">Is
      there a suitable local school for your children?</span></font><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-US" style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.0pt">&nbsp;<o:p>
      </o:p>
      </span></font></p>
      <p class="MsoNormal"><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-US" style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.0pt">You
      will spend a lot of time in this, your new home. You must ask yourself if
      you can see yourself living there? Will you be able to afford it, keep it
      maintained and be able to have reasonable quality of social life? Or will
      all of your income be spent on your home, leaving very little left? You
      will need to assess this and be sure that you can afford to buy your home
      and maintain it within your income.<o:p>
      </o:p>
      </span></font></p>
      <p>&nbsp;</p>
      <p align="center"><a href="#top"><img src="../images/backtotop.gif" alt="Back To Top" border="0" width="75" height="26"></a><br>
        <img src="../images/divider_mort.gif" width="500" height="38"></p>
      <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2"><b><br>
        <font size="3" color="#000066"><a name="howmuch"></a>How much can you 
        borrow?</font></b></font></p>
      <p class="MsoBodyText"><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Arial" size="2">The
      amount that we will lend to you is based on two main factors. Firstly your
      income and your ability to repay a loan, and secondly the value of the
      property you are buying.</font></span><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-US" style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.0pt">&nbsp;<o:p>
      </o:p>
      </span></font></p>
      <p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left:.5in;text-indent:-.5in;mso-list:l8 level1 lfo1;
tab-stops:list .5in"><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-US" style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.0pt">(a)<span style="font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; font-weight: normal">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
      </span></span><u><span lang="EN-US" style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.0pt">Your
      income</span></u><span lang="EN-US" style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.0pt">&nbsp;<o:p>
      </o:p>
      </span></font></p>
      <p class="MsoBodyText"><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Arial" size="2">We
      will normally lend up to three times your gross annual income. If you have
      a partner then we will add on their gross annual income, but only
      once.&nbsp;</font></span><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2"> <br>
        </font></p>

      </o:p>
      </span></font></p>
      <p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left:.5in;text-indent:-.5in;mso-list:l8 level1 lfo1;
tab-stops:list .5in"><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-US" style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.0pt">(b)<span style="font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; font-weight: normal">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
      </span><u>The valuation&nbsp;</u><o:p>
      </o:p>
      </span></font></p>
      <p class="MsoNormal"><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-US" style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.0pt">We
      will normally lend up to 90% of the purchase price (or the valuation if
      this is lower) of any property. So, if you are buying a house for £50,000
      we can lend you up to £45,000.</span></font><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:
10.0pt;font-family:&quot;Trebuchet MS&quot;"><o:p>
      </o:p>
      </span></p>
      <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2"> </font><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2"><br>
      <b>
        <font size="3" color="#000066"><a name="Mortgage Indemnity Guarantee"></a></font></b></font><b><font size="3" color="#000066" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">Mortgage
      Indemnity Guarantee</font></b></p>
      <p class="MsoNormal"><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-US" style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.0pt">MIG
      is a form of insurance which we may take for our protection in the
      unlikely event that you fall significantly behind with your mortgage
      payments and we have to repossess your property and sell it. If we sell
      your property for less than the amount you owe us we will claim on the MIG
      policy to reclaim some (or all) of the loss. The MIG is as protection for
      us. It is not additional security for you.</span></font><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-US" style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.0pt">&nbsp;<o:p>
      </o:p>
      </span></font></p>
      <p class="MsoNormal"><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-US" style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.0pt">We
      will normally arrange a MIG policy if we lend you 75% or more of the value
      of your property. In some cases we make a charge, which we call a high
      lending fee.</span></font><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-US" style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.0pt">&nbsp;<o:p>
      </o:p>
      </span></font></p>
      <p class="MsoNormal"><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-US" style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.0pt">If
      we arrange a MIG policy we will tell you and explain the policy and your
      rights and obligations in detail.</span></font><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-US" style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.0pt">&nbsp;<o:p>
      </o:p>
      </span></font></p>
      <p class="MsoNormal"><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-US" style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.0pt">You
      should also read the section on the valuation later.</span></font></p>

      <p><font size="3"><b><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif"><font color="#000066"><a name="whichtype"></a>Types of Mortgage</font></font></b></font></p>
      <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2"><b>Repayment Mortgage</b></font></p>
      <p class="MsoBodyText"><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Arial" size="2">With
      this type of loan you pay to us a monthly repayment.<span style="mso-spacerun:
yes">&nbsp; </span>This consists of part of the loan and all of the interest,
      which would be due that month.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>It
      can run for a maximum of 25 years.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>If
      the interest rate changes your monthly repayment will be recalculated so
      that the loan is paid off over the agreed term.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp;
      </span>You will be informed of any changes.</font></span><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-US" style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.0pt">&nbsp;<o:p>
      </o:p>
      </span></font></p>
      <p class="MsoNormal"><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-US" style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.0pt">During
      the early years of this type of mortgage your monthly repayment would
      mostly be made up of interest.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>As
      the term of the mortgage passes then a larger part of the repayment is
      used to pay off the loan.&nbsp;<o:p>
      </o:p>
      </span></font></p>
      <p class="MsoNormal"><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-US" style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.0pt">This
      type of mortgage makes sure that at the end of the term you are certain
      that the mortgage is repaid.</span></font><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:
10.0pt;font-family:&quot;Trebuchet MS&quot;"><o:p>
      </o:p>
      </span></p>
      <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2"><b>Interest Only Mortgage</b></font></p>
      <p class="MsoBodyText"><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-US">With
      this type of loan you pay to us a monthly repayment which consists of
      interest only.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>If the
      interest rate changes the monthly repayment will be amended to ensure that
      all interest is paid.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>The
      amount you borrow does not decrease during the term of your loan.</span><span lang="EN-US" style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.0pt">&nbsp;<o:p>
      </o:p>
      </span></font></p>
      <p class="MsoNormal"><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-US" style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.0pt">Normally
      a long term savings plan such as an endowment or an Individual Savings
      Account (ISA) is taken out in conjunction with this type of loan.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp;
      </span>A personal pension might be suitable in certain circumstances.<span style="mso-spacerun:
yes">&nbsp; </span>The funds saved up in the plan are ultimately used to repay
      the loan. &nbsp;<o:p>
      </o:p>
      </span></font></p>
      <p class="MsoNormal"><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-US" style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.0pt">This
      can be a good long term savings scheme.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp;
      </span>There is a chance of some cash surplus at the end of the agreed
      loan term. However, there is some risk element with this type of mortgage,
      as there is a possibility that the savings plan may not generate enough to
      pay off the agreed loan.&nbsp;<o:p>
      </o:p>
      </span></font></p>
      <font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-US" style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.0pt; mso-fareast-font-family: Times New Roman; mso-bidi-font-family: Times New Roman; mso-ansi-language: EN-US; mso-fareast-language: EN-US; mso-bidi-language: AR-SA">If
      you do not arrange a savings plan, or subsequently stop making payments
      into it, then the mortgage might not be repaid at the time you agreed. If
      you stop making payments into a savings plan or decide to surrender it</span>
      <span lang="EN-US" style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.0pt">before the agreed
      maturity date, you may not get back all of the money you have invested.
      You should take advice from an independent financial adviser about the
      consequences of stopping making payments into or surrendering a savings
      plan.</span><span lang="EN-US" style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.0pt">&nbsp;<o:p>
      </o:p>
      </span></font>
      <p class="MsoNormal"><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-US" style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.0pt">It
      is your responsibility to arrange a suitable savings plan. We will remind
      you each year of the need to make sure that an adequate savings plan is in
      place to repay your mortgage at the agreed time.&nbsp;</span></font><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:
10.0pt;font-family:&quot;Trebuchet MS&quot;"><o:p>
      </o:p>
      </span></p>
      <p class="MsoNormal"><font face="Arial" size="2"><span style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.0pt" lang="EN-US">We
      introduce to a local firm of independent financial advisers who will be
      pleased to advise you, without obligation, on suitable savings plans.</span></font><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:
10.0pt;font-family:&quot;Trebuchet MS&quot;">&nbsp;</span></p>
      <h1><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Arial" size="2">Variable Rate Mortgages</font></span><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Arial" size="2">&nbsp;<o:p>
      </o:p>
      </font></span></h1>
      <p><font face="Arial" size="2">The interest rate you will be charged on
      your mortgage will vary from time to time. Consequently, your monthly
      repayment to us will go up and down as interest rates change.&nbsp;</font></p>
      <h1><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Arial" size="2">Discounted Variable
      Rate Mortgages</font></span><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Arial" size="2">&nbsp;<o:p>
      </o:p>
      </font></span></h1>
      <p class="MsoBodyText"><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Arial" size="2">At
      the outset of your mortgage we may offer a discount from our standard
      variable rate for a certain period. This will reduce your repayment during
      that period but your repayment may still go up and down as interest rates
      change. At the end of the discount period, our standard variable rate
      normally applies and you should expect your monthly repayment to us to
      increase at that time.</font></span><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Arial" size="2">&nbsp;<o:p>
      </o:p>
      </font></span></p>
      <h1><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-US">Fixed Rate Mortgages<span style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.0pt">&nbsp;<o:p>
      </o:p>
      </span></span></font></h1>
      <p class="MsoBodyText"><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Arial" size="2">From
      time to time we offer mortgages with an interest rate which is fixed for a
      certain period.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>Your
      repayment to us will remain constant throughout that period. At the end of
      that fixed rate period the interest rate normally becomes variable, in
      line with our standard variable rate. Please ask for details of current
      offers.&nbsp;<o:p>
      </o:p>
      </font></span></p>
      <p class="MsoBodyText"><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-US"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight:normal">Capped
      Rate Mortgages</b>&nbsp;<o:p>
      </o:p>
      </span></font></p>
      <p class="MsoBodyText"><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Arial" size="2">From
      time to time we offer mortgages with an interest rate which is capped for
      a certain period. The interest rate on these mortgages cannot go above a
      certain rate but if our standard variable rate falls below that rate then
      the variable rate applies instead. Your monthly repayment to us will not
      exceed the amount we quote at the outset, during the period that the
      capped rate applies. At the end of the capped rate period the interest
      rate normally becomes variable, in line with our standard variable rate.
      Please ask for details of current offers.</font></span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family:&quot;Trebuchet MS&quot;">&nbsp;<o:p>
      </o:p>
      </span></p>
      <p class="MsoBodyText"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight:normal"><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Arial" size="2">Early
      Redemption</font></span></b><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Arial" size="2">&nbsp;<o:p>
      </o:p>
      </font></span></p>
      <p class="MsoBodyText"><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Arial" size="2">If
      you repay your mortgage in full (or make an extra repayment of £1,000 or
      more) within a few years after taking it out a fee may be payable to us.
      When we make our written mortgage offer to you we will tell you how much
      that fee (if any) will be and we will tell you when and in what
      circumstances it will be payable.</font></span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:
10.0pt;font-family:&quot;Trebuchet MS&quot;">&nbsp;<o:p>
      </o:p>
      </span></p>
      <h1><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Arial" size="2">Life Insurance</font></span><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Arial" size="2">&nbsp;<o:p>
      </o:p>
      </font></span></h1>
      <p class="MsoBodyText"><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Arial" size="2">We
      recommend that you have some form of life insurance in place to repay your
      loan to us in the event of your death.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp;
      </span>If you arrange an endowment to run in conjunction with your loan
      this will include full life cover.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>If
      you have another type of interest only loan or a repayment mortgage you
      should consider some form of term insurance.</font></span><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-US" style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.0pt">&nbsp;<o:p>
      </o:p>
      </span></font></p>
      <p class="MsoNormal"><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-US" style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.0pt">We
      introduce to a local firm of independent financial advisers who will be

      products.</span></font><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:

      <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="3"><b><font color="#000066"><a name="thevaluation"></a>The 
        Valuation</font></b></font></p>
      <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">There are two types 
        of valuation which we are able to offer:</font></p>
      <p><b><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">Standard Valuation</font></b></p>
      <p class="MsoBodyText"><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Arial" size="2">This
      tells us if the property is suitable for a mortgage.<span style="mso-spacerun:
yes">&nbsp; </span>We may not lend money on a property that is in very poor
      condition or appears overpriced.</font></span><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-US" style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.0pt">&nbsp;<o:p>
      </o:p>
      </span></font></p>
      <p class="MsoNormal"><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-US" style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.0pt">The
      valuer will recommend any repairs that need to be carried out and will
      provide a valuation of the property.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp;
      </span>He will also recommend the amount for which the property should be
      insured.&nbsp;<o:p>
      </o:p>
      </span></font></p>
      <p class="MsoNormal"><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-US" style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.0pt">The
      fee for the valuation (if any) will depend upon how much you are paying
      for the property.</span></font><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-US" style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.0pt">&nbsp;<o:p>
      </o:p>
      </span></font></p>
      <p class="MsoNormal"><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-US" style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.0pt">You
      must note that although we will instruct a valuer to survey the property
      you cannot assume that if any essential repairs are not mentioned in the
      valuation report the property is free from any faults.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp;
      </span>Also if the valuer does draw your attention to any essential
      repairs this does not mean that others do not exist.&nbsp;<o:p>
      </o:p>
      </span></font></p>
      <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight:normal"><font face="Arial" size="2"><span lang="EN-US" style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.0pt">To
      be satisfied as to the condition or value of the property, you must have
      an inspection and report of your own.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp;
      </span>Otherwise, you proceed entirely at your own risk.&nbsp;<o:p>
