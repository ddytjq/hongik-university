            <h2><a name="quality"></a> </h2>
            <h3><img src="images/images_eagleridge/bucks1.jpg" width="203" height="225" align="right">QUALITY</h3>
            <p> Eagle Ridge Outfitters is one of the highest quality hunting preserves 
              in the Mid-West.  We handle nothing but the finest caliber big game 
              animals, as well as wild turkey. </p>
            <p>Eagle Ridge Outfitters hunting season is 5 months, allowing ample 
              time to fit the most demanding schedule.  Bring your favorite rifle, 
              bow, or muzzleloader and let us show you the sights!  Sharpen your 
              aim at our shooting range before setting out for your guided hunt. 
              Eagle Ridge Outfitters is for the hunter who enjoys a challenge 
              and who loves and respects the great outdoors for it's primitive 
              candor. </p>
            <a name="location"></a>
            <h3>LOCATION</h3>
            <p> <img src="images/images_eagleridge/elk2.jpg" width="253" height="251" align="left" alt="Elk Hunting">Eagle 
              Ridge Outfitters preserves trophy bloodlines and genetics in a natural 
              environment. Our animals are free to roam and reproduce over hundreds 
              of acres of untouched habitat. Clear running streams, green hillsides 
              and tree-topped ridges make up the landscaping, as Eagles make this 
              their winter hide-away and are seen soaring over the Osage County 
              hillsides. </p>
            <p>Why spend half of your time and budget just getting to those crowded 
              hunting grounds, where successful hunts are few. Here at Eagle Ridge 
              Outfitters a successful hunt takes little planning and a lot less 
              time. </p>
            <p>In Central Missouri, we are only 90 minutes West of St. Louis, 
              off Hwy. 50. or only 30 minutes East of Jefferson City, off Hwy 
              50.</p>
            <a name="facilities"></a> 
            <h3><img src="images/images_eagleridge/lodge.jpg" width="280" height="172" alt="Lodging" align="right">FACILITIES</h3>
            <p> Come and enjoy a hunting experience you'll never forget. Our expert 
              guides will adapt to your hunting style, whether calling, still 
              hunting, stalking, or blinds. Our accommodations have all the amenities 
              of home, along with some of the most beautiful wildlife and scenery, 
              making a relaxing atmosphere all its own. Country cooked meals are 
              provided by Eagle Ridge Outfitters, also local meat care and professional 
              taxidermy services are available. </p>
