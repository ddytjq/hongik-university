                        Guardian name was established in 1821. Originally Guardian 
                        Fire &amp; Life, the Company was renamed as Guardian Assurance 
                        in 1902.

                          new base at Lytham St Annes in Lancashire for its expanding 
                          Life and Pensions business.</p>
                        <p>In 1968 the company merged with Royal Exchange Assurance 
                          to form the Guardian Royal Exchange Group (GRE).</p>
