     <p><p>The following income multiple calculations will normally apply as part of our affordability assessment:</p>

<ul>
<li>3.25 X main income plus 1 X secondary income (maximum number of applicants is 3), <i>OR</i>

<li>2.75 X joint income (maximum number of applicants is 2), <i>OR</i>

<li>2 X joint income (maximum number of applicants is 3)
</ul>

<p>Loans which exceed 90% of the purchase price / valuation (whichever is lower) may require additional security in the form of a High Percentage Loan Fee.</p>

<p>Mortgage security will be required and a life assurance policy may also be required.<br>
A written quotation on loans can be obtained on request.<br>
These offers are limited and may be withdrawn without notice.</p></p>
