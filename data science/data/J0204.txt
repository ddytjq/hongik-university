<p class=DefinitionTerm>97A1: Gene Dinkel Collection</p>

<p class=DefinitionList>John Castle’s Plaque Board; timing and scoring books,
entry lists, grid sheets, press kits, 1960s, 1970s; includes a computer
simulation of the Watkins Glen circuit by the Cornell Aeronautical Laboratory</p>

<p class=DefinitionTerm>97A2: William Coykendall Collection</p>

<p class=DefinitionList>Flagging paddles used in Watkins Glen Grand Prix,
1953-1954</p>

<p class=DefinitionTerm>97A3: Eugene and Constance Birdsey Family Collection<span
style='color:red'><o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=DefinitionList>Photographs, sketches, clippings, newsletters, and
other papers relating to sports car racing, northeast United States.<span
style="mso-spacerun: yes">  </span>Also, glass trophies.<br>
(Denise LeVan and D.J. Goebel, donors)</p>

<p class=DefinitionTerm>97A4: Ralph Miller Video</p>

<p class=DefinitionList>Depicting scenes at a countryside corner of the 1948
Watkins Glen Grand Prix</p>

<p class=DefinitionTerm>97A5: <i style='mso-bidi-font-style:normal'>Twenty Five
Years at Speed</i></p>

<p class=DefinitionList>Video derived from original print of a British
television documentary pertaining to the founding and history of the Watkins
Glen Grand Prix, narrated by Brock Yates<br>
(Cameron and Jean Argetsinger, donors)</p>

<p class=DefinitionTerm>97A6: Cameron and Jean Argetsinger Collection</p>

<p class=DefinitionList>Correspondence, photographs, clippings, and other
papers, documents, and other items. Also Nicholas Watts prints, autographed</p>

<p class=DefinitionTerm>97A7: Keith and Alison Harmer Collection</p>

<p class=DefinitionList><i style='mso-bidi-font-style:normal'>SAE Transactions</i>,
1929-1961;<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">  </span>Tournament of Champions
(film) [not converted to video]; also, program from the 1960 New York
International Automobile Show</p>

<p class=DefinitionTerm>97A8: Thomas A. Emdy Collection</p>

<p class=DefinitionList>German Grand Prix programs and press release,
1961-1965; Watkins Glen Classic Program, 1962; also books, periodicals, and
programs from other venues</p>

<p class=DefinitionTerm>97A9: Ian Marwick Photographic Slides, Programs</p>

<p class=DefinitionList>Watkins Glen original photography, c.1948-1955</p>

<p class=DefinitionTerm>97A10: William Burnett Programs and Tickets</p>

<p class=DefinitionList>Watkins Glen Grand Prix programs, and tickets to the
viewing area at Milliken’s Corner, 1948-1950</p>

<p class=DefinitionTerm>97A11: William McDougal Periodicals and Programs</p>

<p class=DefinitionList>Numerous titles covering European racing, Formula 1,
and stock car racing, c. 1968-1995</p>

<p class=DefinitionTerm>98A1: Arthur J. Peck Video</p>

<p class=DefinitionList>Watkins Glen Grand Prix, 1948-1953; derived from 16mm
film, depicting pre-race activities, races through downtown, festivities,
personalities, and race cars</p>

<p class=DefinitionTerm>98A2: Robert H. Basso Collection</p>

<p class=DefinitionList>1935 Monaco Grand Prix Souvenir program, printed 1979;
Collier Museum material including car inventory c.1989; also a copy of court
case Formula One Constructors Association v Watkins Glen Grand Prix Corporation</p>

<p class=DefinitionTerm>98A3: Herb Anastor Book Collection</p>

<p class=DefinitionList>Historical and reference books, Formula 1 and sports
car racing</p>

<p class=DefinitionTerm>98A4: Ledyard Pfund Collection</p>

<p class=DefinitionList>Rare and reference books and periodicals; ARCA and SCCA
newsletters, code books, and scrapbooks, 1935-1949<span style="mso-spacerun:
yes">  </span>(<i style='mso-bidi-font-style:normal'>Sportwagen/Sportscar</i>)</p>

<p class=DefinitionTerm>98A5: John Gorsline Collection</p>

<p class=DefinitionList>Press releases and team publicity packets, 1965-1990</p>

<p class=DefinitionTerm>98A6: Randy Malbone Collection</p>

<p class=DefinitionList>Rare and reference books chiefly concerned with Formula
1 drivers races, and history; European race posters, ephemera, calendars,
correspondence, tickets, press releases; slides; videos of F1, Indianapolis,
and other races and venues; also, elements from Formula 1 cars (Stewart and
Peterson)</p>

<p class=DefinitionTerm>98A7: Dick Knudson Collection</p>

<p class=DefinitionList>Volumes relating exclusively to the MG; also technical
drawings, correspondence, subject files, and photographs relating to the Kurtis
Kraft roadster; MG works drawings; oral history CD transcripts from Knudson’s
MG book; photographs; <i style='mso-bidi-font-style:normal'>Sacred Octagon</i>
and <i style='mso-bidi-font-style:normal'><span style="mso-spacerun:
yes"> </span>Safety Fast</i> periodicals.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes"> 
</span>Photographs of Jim Clark and Count Carel de Beaufort, and an essay on
the Colliers and “The Birth of the American Sports Car Culture: When Motor
Racing Was a Sport.”</p>
