                          <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">The
                            Mortgage Code provides protection for residential
                            mortgage borrowers.</font></p>
                          <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">It
                            sets out minimum standards which mortgage lenders
                            and intermediaries have to meet.</font></p>
                          <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">Cumberland
                            Building Society has adopted The Mortgage Code (our
                            registration number is 5384820) and will help you
                            to choose a mortgage by providing the level of service
                            most suited to your needs. The three levels of service
                            we offer are:</font></p>
                          <ul>
                            <li><b><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">advice
                              and a recommendation</font></b><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">
                              on which of the mortgages we provide is most suitable
                              for you; (we are only able to offer this level of service
                              to prospective borrowers who are able to visit one
                              of our branches for a personal interview)</font></li>
                            <li><b><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">information
                              on the different types of mortgage</font><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">
                              products</font></b><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2"> we offer so that you can make an informed
                              choice of which to take; or</font></li>
                            <li><b><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">information
                              on a single mortgage product only, </font></b><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">if
                              only one mortgage is available or if you have already
                              made up your mind.</font></li>
                          </ul>
                          <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">Further
                            details including an introductory leaflet '<a href="http://www.cml.org.uk/pdf/yaym.pdf" target="_blank">You
                            and your Mortgage - The Mortgage Code</a>' will automatically
                            be sent to you if you ask for information on our mortgage
                            offers. A copy of this leaflet can also be viewed
                            on the Council of Mortgage Lenders Website <a href="http://www.cml.org.uk/" target="_blank">www.cml.org.uk</a></font></p>
                          <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">Alternatively
                            you may obtain further details from any of our branches.</font></p>
