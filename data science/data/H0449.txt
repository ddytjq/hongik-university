<p><font color="#0000FF"><font size=-1>Ewens, W. J. 2000 "The Mathematical
Foundations of Population Genetics" <b>"Evolutionary Genetics"</b> Singh,
R.S. and Krimbas, C.B. (editors) Cambridge University Press, pp. 24-40.
See esp. pg. 32 where Ewens criticizes Haldane's concept of the cost of
natural selection as misguided due to inappropriate mathematical modeling.
Ewens also points out here that Mayr's well known arguments against "bean-bag
genetics" were directed specifically against Haldane's substitution cost
arguments (see Ewens, 1993 and Mayr 1963).</font></font>
