<H3>How to be a NAPCM Associate Member.</H3>

Associate members receive the Official NAPCM Associate Member T-shirts, window decals, monthly newsletter and the possibility of winning merchandise signed by pit crews and occasionally a guest spot on the NAPCM website giving us the fans perspective.
