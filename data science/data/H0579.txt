<p>
<span class="byline">POSTED 9/12/01 - </span>The <a href="../../sfi/BusNet/pastMeetings/annualMeeting01.html">agenda</a> for the November 2-3 Business Network Members Meeting and Fall Trustees Symposium is now available.
</p>
<p>
<span class="byline">POSTED 8/21/01 - </span>The online <a href="../../sfi/BusNet/pastMeetings/annual01form.html">registration form</a> for the <a href="../../sfi/BusNet/futureIndex.html">Business Network Annual Meeting and Fall Trustees Symposium</a> is now available. Please register by October 2, 2001.
</p>
<p>
<span class="byline">POSTED 8/16/01 - </span>The Business Network has recently attracted a financial management firm as its newest member.  <a href="http://www.bailliegifford.com/">Baillie Gifford</a>, based in Edinburgh, is one of the UK's leading independent investment management firms, and is the Network's first Scottish corporation. Their main lines of business are Pension Funds, both defined benefit and defined contribution (DC), Investment Trusts and Retail Financial Products (OEICs, Unit Trusts and ISAs).
</p>
<p>
<span class="byline">POSTED 7/26/01 - </span><a href="http://www.santafe.edu/sfi/BusNet/files/grldoc0.pdf_6.pdf">Where's the Bar?</a> is the latest report from Business Network member Michael Mauboussin and the U.S. Investment Strategy team at Credit Suisse First Boston.
</p>
<p>
<font face="helvetica" size="1" color="#000088">POSTED 4/12/01 - </font><a href="http://www.up.com/">Union Pacific</a> joins the Business Network! Under the direction of Mike Connolly, the primary operations division contact and John Cummings, the information technology division contact, Union Pacific hopes to look at complexity as it relates to these distinct but complementary areas of its organization. Both contacts are based out of Omaha, Nebraska.
</p>
<p>
<font face="helvetica" size="1" color="#000088">POSTED 4/4/01 - </font>We are very pleased to announce that <a href="http://www.pioneer.com/">Pioneer Hi-Bred</a> has rejoined the Business Network. Pioneer has been involved with the Santa Fe Institute since 1998 and we are glad to have them back on board.  Mark Cooper will be the main contact for their membership and he is based out of Pioneer's Johnston, Iowa office.
</p>
<p>
<font face="helvetica" size="1" color="#000088">POSTED 3/7/01 - </font>We are very pleased to welcome <a href="http://www.lanb.com/">Los Alamos National Bank</a> as SFI's newest member of the Business Network. This New Mexico community bank is a recent recipient of the prestigious <a href="http://www.nist.gov/public_affairs/releases/g00-187.htm">Malcolm Baldrige National Quality award</a>, the nation's premier award for performance excellence and quality achievement.
</p>
<p>
<font face="helvetica" size="1" color="#000088">POSTED 2/14/01 - </font><a href="http://www.insightpartners.com/">Insight Capital Partners</a>, a leading venture capital firm specializing in software companies developing technology-based solutions for enterprises in targeted business sectors, joins the Business Network!  Jerry Murdock, based out of Insight's Aspen office, will be the main contact.
</p>
<p>
<font face="helvetica" size="1" color="#000088">POSTED 1/5/01 - </font>We are happy to announce <a href="http://www.pwcglobal.com/gx/eng/main/home/">PricewaterhouseCoopers</a> has rejoined the Network. Formally Coopers & Lybrand, PWC's Global Thought Leadership Group has championed the new membership and Joel Kurtzman located in Boston, MA will serve as the main contact. Welcome aboard!
</p>
<p>
<font face="helvetica" size="1" color="#000088">POSTED 1/4/01 - </font>SFI's Business Network welcomes its first 2001 member, <a href="http://www.swissre.com/">SwissRe Investors</a>. John Sweeney, President and CEO of Swiss Re Investor's New York group, will be the main contact for the Network.
</p>
<p>
<font face="helvetica" size="1" color="#000088">POSTED 1/2/01 - </font>Michael Mauboussin's latest report entitled <a href="http://www.santafe.edu/sfi/BusNet/files/grldoc0_pdf_4.pdf">"Innovation and Markets:  How Innovation Affects Investing Process"</a> has been made available to Business Network members for download.
</p>
<p>
<font face="helvetica" size="1" color="#000088">POSTED 11/14/00 - </font>We've created a <a href="http://discuss.santafe.edu/busnetannmtg2000/">new website</a> to act as a platform for continuing the stimulating conversations from the Annual Business Network meeting, held November 3-4, 2001. And, for links to various talks and some PowerPoint presentations from the Annual Meeting and Fall Trustee Symposium.
</p>
<p>
<font face="helvetica" size="1" color="#000088">POSTED 11/9/00 - </font>We are pleased to announce that <a href="http://www.consilient.com/indexnoflash.html">Consilient</a>, a leading provider of peer-to-peer Internet infrastructure software, is the newest addition to the Business Network. Consilient is  based out of Berkeley, CA and Beau Roy, Executive Director, Market Strategy will be the Network's main contact.
</p>
<p>
<font face="helvetica" size="1" color="#000088">POSTED 9/27/00 - </font><a href="http://www.trilogyadvisors.com">Trilogy Advisors</a>, LLC is a New York-based investment advisory firm with a simple mission: to help its clients reach their financial goals through innovative research and disciplined investing. Trilogy's contact for the Business Network will be Stephen Waite.
</p>
<p>
<font face="helvetica" size="1" color="#000088">POSTED 9/19/00 - </font>Michael Mauboussin's latest report entitled <a href="http://www.santafe.edu/sfi/BusNet/files/Investment_Strategy.pdf">"It's the Ecology, Stupid"</a> has been made available to Business Network members for download.
</p>
<p>
<font face="helvetica" size="1" color="#000088">POSTED 9/7/00 - </font>The <a href="http://discuss.santafe.edu/busnetannmtg2000/stories/storyReader$3">agenda</a> for the Annual Business Network Members Meeting and Business Network and Trustees Symposium are now on our website.
</p>
<p>
<font face="helvetica" size="1" color="#000088">POSTED 8/4/00 - </font>We've just posted the newest U.S. investment strategy paper from Credit Suisse First Boston's Michael Mauboussin and Alexander Schay.  The paper is entitled:  <a href="http://www.santafe.edu/sfi/BusNet/files/grldoc0.pdf_3.pdf">"Still Powerful:  The Internet's Hidden Order."</a>
</p>
<p>
<font face="helvetica" size="1" color="#000088">POSTED 7/18/00 - </font><a href="http://www.commerce.net/">CommerceNet</a> joins the Business Network!  CommerceNet, a  non-profit group that gives research funding and angel funding in the area of e-commerce, will be the newest addition to SFI's corporate affiliates program.  Mark Resch,the President and CEO, will be the main contact and his office is located in Cupertino, CA.
</p>
<p>
<font face="helvetica" size="1" color="#000088">POSTED 5/17/00 - </font>The Santa Fe Institute announces yet another new Business Network Member! Welcome to <a href="http://ww4.janus.com/cgi-bin/gx.cgi/AppLogic+PowerPageA">Janus Funds</a> out of Denver, Colorado. The Business Network contact for Janus is David Decker.  A great addition to our Network!
</p>
<p>
<font face="helvetica" size="1" color="#000088">POSTED 5/16/00 - </font>Michael Mauboussin's latest report entitled <a href="http://www.santafe.edu/sfi/BusNet/files/GRL_268_0.pdf">"Network to Net Worth"</a> has been made available to Business Network members for download. Please be advised that this is a large (5.1 MB) PDF file.
</p>
<p>
<font face="helvetica" size="1" color="#000088">POSTED 4/19/00 - </font>The Santa Fe Institute Business Network proudly welcomes <a href="http://www.anl.gov/">Argonne National Laboratory</a> as our newest member. Argonne is one of the U.S. Department of Energy's largest research centers. It is also the nation's first national laboratory, chartered in 1946. Charles Macal, of the Illinois site, will be Argonne's contact for the Business Network. We thank Argonne for its support and welcome you to the SFI family.
</p>
<p>
<font face="helvetica" size="1" color="#000088">POSTED 3/31/00 - </font>We are proud to announce that <a href="http://www.trw.com">TRW Systems & Information Technology Group</a> has joined the Business Network!  TRW Inc. provides high-technology products and services to the automotive and space, defense & information systems markets. The financial results of the company's operations are reported in two core business segments: Automotive and Space, Defense & Information Systems. Christopher Wallace, in TRW's Albuquerque office, will be  SFI's main business contact. TRW has numerous offices nationally and internationally. Welcome!
</p>
<p>
<font face="helvetica" size="1" color="#000088">POSTED 3/21/00 - </font> The Business Network is pleased to announce <a href="http://www.zefer.com">ZEFER Corp.</a> as its newest member. ZEFER is a consulting firm headquartered in Boston with several other offices, including in London. The mission of this new company is help clients create innovative strategies and implement adaptive business models that thrive in the Internet economy.  ZEFER's main contact will be Francis Dudley,Vice President.
