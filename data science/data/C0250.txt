<b>What we provide - Scottish Investment Bond</b>
<p>
Scottish Friendly's Scottish Investment Bond offers an easy route to stockmarket investment providing professional investment management, access to your investment and a choice of funds.
<p>
It is a capital investment bond which uses our professional investment expertise to pool your client's money with the investments of a great number of people. This means the investment is spread across a wide range of stocks and shares, cash deposits and fixed interest securities.

<p>

You should remember that the value of investments in the investment-linked funds depends directly on the value of underlying assets and can, therefore, go down as well as up.
<br>
As a result there is no guarantee that the initial investment will be returned.
<p>
<b>Managed Fund</b>
<p>
Managed Funds are the most popular investment linked funds.  The money will go into a broadly based fund investing as appropriate in UK and overseas equities, fixed interest stocks, index linked securities and cash deposits.  Our managers - after fully considering the prospects for each market - decide on the mix of investment that is most likely to balance good returns with security.  They then regularly review the mix of investments to maximise efficiency while attempting to minimise the risk involved through a wide portfolio spread.
<p>
<b>UK Equity Fund</b>
<p>
This specialist Fund is designed to achieve long term capital growth from a carefully selected portfolio of UK equities. It will also hold cash deposits from time to time.
<p>
This Fund offers the best of both worlds - providing your client with investments in a spread of UK ordinary shares and/or convertible stocks, without the expense and bother of buying and selling shares.
<p>
<b>Scottish Investment Trusts Fund</b>
<p>
This is a specialist fund which invests with the top funds managed by Scottish investment trusts. Our job is to select a number of the best investment trusts and ensure these trusts perform to maximise growth of the fund and the investment. It will also hold companies deposits from time to time.
<p>
<b>Fixed Interest Fund</b>
<p>
This Fund is invested in British Government securities and/or other UK fixed interest stocks. It will also hold cash deposits from time to time. The Fund aim is to achieve secure medium term growth through careful management of a portfolio of fixed interest stocks.
<p>
<b>Cash Fund</b>
<p>
Through the Cash Fund, investments aim for maximum interest with guaranteed capital security. This is achieved through investments in short term bank deposits and/or similar cash investments.
