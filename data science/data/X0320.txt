          <h4><img src="images/images_starr/star.gif" width="15" height="15"> 
            Whitetail Deer Hunts</h4>
          <blockquote> 
            <p><img src="images/images_starr/ph5.jpg" width="200" height="173" align="right" alt="whitetail deer hunts">I 
              conduct these hunts on donnell private ranches totaling nearly 25,000 
              acres in LaSalle and McMullen counties in South Texas. These are 
              three-day and five-day hunts which include meals, lodging, and 4WD 
              transportation. Hunting is done primarily from tower blinds over 
              feeders. We also use high rack hunting vehicles, and we do some 
              horn rattling during the rut, which is predominantly around the 
              last two weeks of December.</p>
            <p>I have good game management programs implemented on these ranches 
              and also a year-round feeding program. I shoot only mature bucks. 
              Along with your buck, you may be able to shoot does, hogs, javelina 
              and predators at our discretion. (This is based on pre-season and 
              in-season populations.) I have some very nice bucks on these ranches. 
              You will see a lot of game! I take a very limited number of hunters 
              into each pasture, so call early to reserve a spot.</p>
            <p>A Texas nonresident hunting license is $250.00 and covers all of 
              the animals on this hunt. For those of you flying in, airport pickup 
              and delivery is available to and from San Antonio International 
              Airport ($100.00 per person).</p>
          </blockquote>
          <h4><img src="images/images_starr/star.gif" width="15" height="15"> 
            Wild Hog and Javelina Hunts</h4>
          <blockquote> 
            <p><img src="images/images_starr/ph3.jpg" width="225" height="175" align="right" alt="wild hog and javelina hunts">I 
              conduct these hunts on several ranches totaling almost 20,000 acres. 
              Ranchesare located in LaSalle and McMullen counties in South Texas. 
              This is a two-day package hunt which includes meals, lodging and 
              4WD transportation. Hunting is done from tower blinds over feeders, 
              driving ranch roads with high racked hunting vehicles, spot and 
              stalk around water holes and tripods around cattle feeders for archers. 
              You may kill predators, two javelina and all the hogs you want as 
              long as you take the meat home with you. <b><font color="#993300" size="4">That's 
              right - all the hogs you want!</font></b> I have way too many hogs. 
              Nonresidents will need to purchase a special 5-day hunting license 
              when they arrive ($35.00). For those of you flying in, airport pickup 
              and delivery is available to and from San Antonio International 
              Airport ($100.00 per person).</p>
          </blockquote>
          <h4><img src="images/images_starr/star.gif" width="15" height="15"> 
            Spring Turkey and Exotic Hunts</h4>
          <blockquote> 
            <p>I have too many exotic hunts to list, so please call for more information. 
              I hunt Spring Turkey (Rio Grande) in April and May. There is an 
              abundance of Turkey along the Frio River, which runs through some 
              of the pastures we hunt. Hunts are run by the day. I do include 
              meals, lodging and 4WD transportation. Hunt over bait or you call. 
              This is a great hunt to do in conjunction with Wild Hog and Javelina! 
              As with all our Texas hunts, airport pickup and delivery is available 
              to and from San Antonio International Airport ($100.00 per person).</p>
