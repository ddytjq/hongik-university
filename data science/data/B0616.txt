        <p><font size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif"><b>Investment Limits</b></font></p>

        <ul>

          <li><font size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">The minimum investment

            is &pound;1,000, but you can invest up to &pound;9,000 capital from

            a matured TESSA (excluding interest credited) into a TESSA ONLY ISA (2nd Issue) within six months of the TESSA maturity date. Amounts invested this

            way do not count towards the annual subscription limits and may be

            made at anytime during the six month period, which may straddle two

            tax years.<br>

            <br>

            This rule applies to TESSAs maturing at anytime after 5th January

            1999 (provided you have not opened a further TESSA before 6th April

            1999 - the proceeds of which may benefit from this rule on maturity

            in 2004).<br>

            <br>

            It also means that you can open a TESSA ONLY ISA (2nd Issue) and then subsequently

            open a FURNESS MINI CASH ISA, in the same tax year.</font></li>

        </ul>

        <p><font size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif"><b>Interest </b></font></p>

        <ul>

          <li><font size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">Interest is added

            to your TESSA-ONLY ISA (2nd Issue) on 5th April annually tax free and may be withdrawn

            without penalty or notice.</font></li>

        </ul>

        <p><font size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif"> <b>Withdrawals

          and Closure</b></font></p>

        <ul>

          <li><font size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">You can withdraw

            from or close your TESSA-ONLY ISA (2nd Issue) without penalty if you can give

            90 days notice, otherwise you will lose 90 days interest on the

            amount withdrawn or the closing balance.</font></li>

        </ul>

        <p><font size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif"><b>Who can open

          an ISA?</b></font></p>

        <ul>

          <li><font size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">To open an TESSA-ONLY

            ISA (2nd Issue) you must be:</font> <font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif"><br>

            <br>

            </font>

            <ul>

              <li><font size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">an individual

                aged 18 or over<br>

                <br>

                </font></li>

              <li><font size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">resident

                and ordinarily resident in the UK (subject to an exception for

                certain crown employees)<br>

                <br>

                </font></li>

            </ul>

          </li>

          <li><font size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">If you cease

            to meet the second condition you may not make further subscriptions

            to an ISA, but may keep the continuing benefit of the tax reliefs

            on the accumulated funds.</font></li>

          <li><font size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">You can only

            open a TESSA-ONLY ISA (2nd Issue) in your own name.</font></li>

        </ul>

        <p><font size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif"><b> <br>

          Transfers </b></font></p>

        <ul>

          <li><font size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">Transfers to

            other ISA providers are permitted subject to 90 days notice.</font></li>

        </ul>

        <p><font size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif"><b>Cancellation

          Period</b></font></p>

        <ul>

          <li><font size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">You may cancel

            your application to subscribe to a TESSA-ONLY ISA (2nd issue) during the first

            thirty days after the account was opened.<br>

            <br>

            </font></li>

          <li><font size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">Interest will

            be paid to you gross during the cancellation period.<br>

            <br>

            </font></li>

          <li><font size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">You may still

            subscribe to another ISA in the same year and for the full amount

            once your ISA has been cancelled.</font></li>

        </ul>

        <p><font size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif"><b>Transactions</b></font></p>

        <ul>

          <li><font size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">All TESSA-ONLY

            ISA (2nd issue) transactions must be either by cash or cheque.</font></li>

        </ul>

        <p><font size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif"><font size="1"><b><font size="2">Charitable

          Assignment Scheme</font></b></font></font></p>

        <ul>

          <li> <font size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">All new savers

            opening share accounts must sign a declaration agreeing to assign

            any future windfall rights to the Charities Aid Foundation. For more

            information please visit the Mutuality page of our web site or telephone

            Furness Direct on 0800 22 05 68. </font><font size="2"> </font></li>

        </ul>
