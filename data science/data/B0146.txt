	  <STRONG class="redtext">S7. Restricting Withdrawal Without Notice</STRONG><br>
      We can limit at any time without notice the amount which may be withdrawn 
        in respect of any account, if we reasonably consider it necessary in order 
        to protect the interest of our membership as a whole.

	  <STRONG class="redtext">S8. Recognition of Other Interest/Claims</STRONG><br>
      We do not have to recognise the interest or claim of any person other 
        than the account holder(s) in respect of money held in any account, nor 
        will we be liable in any way for failing to recognise such interest or 
        claim, except as required by law.
      <p></p>
	  <STRONG class="redtext">S9. Succession on Death</STRONG><br>
      On proof of the death of any joint account holder, we will recognise 
        only the survivor(s) as having any ownership of or interest in the account 
        and the survivor(s) shall be entitled to operate the account on the same 
        terms.
      <p></p>
	  <STRONG class="redtext">S10. Transferring Your Investment to Another</STRONG><br>
      You can transfer your investment to another person, subject to the conditions 
      applying to your account, our approval and the payment of any necessary 
      fee. No transfer will be valid unless made in a form approved by us and 
      until registered in our records. We may refuse to permit such a transfer 
      without giving any reason.

	  <STRONG class="redtext">S11. Rules</STRONG><br>
      You will, by opening a Share Account, become a Member of the Society 
        and your rights and obligations as a Member will be as set out in our 
        Rules. A copy of our Rules is available upon request.
      <p></p>
	  <STRONG class="redtext">S12. Indemnity, Retention and Set-Off</STRONG><br>
      In the event that you breach your obligations and investment contract 
        we will require you to indemnify us in respect of any loss we may suffer 
        as a result. If your account is held jointly then we may hold either of 
        you responsible to indemnify us in respect of any breach of your obligations. 
        We have the right to retain and set-off all money in any account you hold 
        with us against any money you may owe to us (whether under a mortgage, 
        loan, guarantee or otherwise) which is due for payment but which has not 
        been paid. We will give you notice in writing when we have done this. 
        Interest or bonuses will not be paid on any money after it has been used 
        in this way.
