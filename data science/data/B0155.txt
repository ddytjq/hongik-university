        <b>
        <p align="center"><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008200">HOW WOULD
        YOU PAY FOR YOUR MORTGAGE</font></p>
        <p align="center"><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008200">IF YOUR
        CIRCUMSTANCES SHOULD CHANGE?</font></p>
        </b>
        <p ALIGN="JUSTIFY"><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#000084">Many
        people worry about what would happen if they were unable to work and
        couldn't meet their monthly mortgage payment.</font></p>
        <p ALIGN="JUSTIFY"><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#000084">They
        worry that an unexpected accident or illness could hit them. They worry
        that they might be made redundant. They also know that in such times
        their expenses can increase and make things even more difficult</font></p>
        <b>
        <p ALIGN="JUSTIFY"><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008200">Mortgage
        Care</font></b><font face="Arial" size="2"><b> </b><font color="#000084">can
        help take away those worries by protecting you against the financial
        consequences of accident, sickness and unemployment</font></font></p>
        <p ALIGN="JUSTIFY"><b><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008200">ARE
        YOU ELIGIBLE FOR MORTGAGE CARE?</font></p>
        </b>
        <p ALIGN="JUSTIFY"><font face="Arial" size="2"><font color="#000084">Provided
        you are between the ages of 18 and 64 (cover ceases at age 65), have a
        mortgage, are resident in the UK and have been in work for the last 6
        months you are eligible to apply for</font> <font color="#008200"><b>Mortgage
        Care</b></font></font><b><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008200">.</font></b></p>
        <p ALIGN="JUSTIFY"><b><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008200">I'M
        SELF-EMPLOYED, AM I COVERED?</font></p>
        </b>
        <p ALIGN="JUSTIFY"><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#000084">If you
        work for yourself then <b>Mortgage Care</b> works for you. Naturally you
        are covered for accident &amp; sickness. You may also claim if you have
        involuntarily ceased to trade, declared this to the Inland Revenue and
        registered for Job Seeker's Allowance.</font></p>
        <p ALIGN="JUSTIFY"><b><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008200">WHAT
        ABOUT IF I AM WORKING ON A FIXED TERM</font></b><font color="#008200"> </font><b><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008200">CONTRACT?</font></p>
        </b>
        <p ALIGN="JUSTIFY"><font face="Arial" size="2">I<font color="#000084">f
        you have been working on a contract with the same employer for at least
        the last 24 months, or on a yearly contract which had been renewed at
        least once, and in either case you expected your contract to be renewed
        when taking </font><font color="#008200"><b>Mortgage Care</b></font><font color="#000084">
        cover, then we will treat you as if you are in full time employment</font></font></p>
        <p ALIGN="JUSTIFY"><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#000084">If you do
        not fall into the above category but have been with the same employer
        for at least 6 months and your contract has been renewed at least twice,
        then benefit will be paid in the event that your contract is terminated
        early until the expected expiry date of your contract.</font></p>
        <b>
        <p ALIGN="JUSTIFY"><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008200">JOINT
        COVER</font></p>
        </b>
        <p ALIGN="JUSTIFY"><b><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008200">Mortgage
        Care</font></b><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#000084"> gives you
        the option to cover both yourself and your partner at no extra cost. You
        should specify on the application form how you wish the cover to be
        divided between you.</font></p>
        <b>
        <p ALIGN="JUSTIFY"><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008200">HOW MUCH
        COVER CAN I APPLY FOR?</font></p>
        <p ALIGN="JUSTIFY"></b><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#000084">You
        can apply for cover sufficient to cover your mortgage and endowment,
        plus 25% for additional mortgage related expenses, including your </font><b><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008200">Mortgage
        Care</font></b><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#000084"> premium.</font></p>
        <p ALIGN="JUSTIFY"><b><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008200">HOW
        MORTGAGE CARE WORKS FOR ACCIDENT, SICKNESS &amp; UNEMPLOYMENT</font></p>
        </b>
        <p ALIGN="JUSTIFY"><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#000084">Provided
        your claim meets the criteria set down in the policy, if you're off work
        for more than 60 days then </font><b><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008200">Mortgage
        Care</font></b><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#000084"> begins to
        pay benefits until you return to work or until you have received the
        maximum of 12 monthly payments. To claim for an accident or an illness
        you must be under the care of a doctor. To claim for unemployment you
        must have registered for Job Seeker's Allowance</font></p>
        <b>
        <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008200">HOW MANY CLAIMS CAN I
        MAKE?</font></p>
        </b>
        <p ALIGN="JUSTIFY"><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#000084">There is
        no limit to the number of times you may claim. Each claim may not exceed
        a period greater than 12 months. To requalify for further unemployment
        benefits you must return to employment for at least 6 months. To
        requalify for further accident and sickness benefits you must return to
        work for at least 6 months to claim for the same condition or 1 month
        for a different condition.</font></p>
        <p ALIGN="JUSTIFY"><b><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008200">WHAT
        DOES MORTGAGE CARE COST?</font></p>
        </b>
        <p ALIGN="JUSTIFY"><b><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008200">Mortgage
        Care</font></b><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#000084"> aims to
        provide, as simply as possible, low cost protection for your home. The
        example table below shows that this sort of cover costs a lot less than
        you think. Premiums are calculated at 5.5% of your monthly mortgage
        repayment plus insurance premium tax. A monthly benefit of £100.00, for
        example, costs as little as £5.55 per month.</font></p>
