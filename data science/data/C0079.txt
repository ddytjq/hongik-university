      <p><b><font size="2" face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" color="#000066">1. 
        Who needs unemployment and/or disability protection insurance?</font></b><font size="2" face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" color="#000066"><br>
        </font></p>
      <p><font size="2" face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" color="#000066">Simply 
        consider the following information:</font></p>
      <ul>
        <li><font size="2" face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" color="#000066">Currently 
          90 families a day are having their homes repossessed. The majority due 
          to the financial problems associated with unemployment.<br>
          </font></li>
        <li><font size="2" face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" color="#000066">One 
          in three people aged between 25-34 have experienced unemployment for 
          a period in excess of one month.<br>
          </font></li>
        <li><font size="2" face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" color="#000066">Almost 
          one in five working age households (3.4 million) have someone who is 
          currently unemployed.<br>
          </font></li>
        <li><font size="2" face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" color="#000066">Today 
          in Britain there are nearly 1,000,000 persons who are registered as 
          unemployed<br>
          </font></li>
        <li><font size="2" face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" color="#000066">Every 
          day 500 people in the UK become unemployed. 60% of unemployed men and 
          45% of unemployed women will be out of work for six months or more.<br>
          </font></li>
        <li><font size="2" face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" color="#000066">Every 
          adult in Britain is five times more likely to suffer a serious disability 
          than die before the age of 60.<br>
          </font></li>
        <li><font size="2" face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" color="#000066">Today 
          in Britain, 2,900 people will start claiming state disability benefits<br>
          </font></li>
        <li><font size="2" face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" color="#000066">1,800,000 
          people in Britain are already disabled and have been unable to work 
          for 12 months or more.<br>
          </font></li>
      </ul>
      <p><font size="2" face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" color="#000066"><b>2. 
        How will this insurance help you?<br>
        </b><br>
        This insurance has been designed to overcome the consequences of unemployment 
        or disability and to provide the following unique benefits:<br>
        </font></p>
      <ul>
        <li><font size="2" face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" color="#000066">You 
          do not need to have a mortgage to be insured under this insurance. However, 
          being totally flexible it can cover a monthly mortgage payment, or rent, 
          loans, household bills or any other purpose you require.<br>
          </font></li>
        <li><font size="2" face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" color="#000066">Free 
          transfer and immediate cover for clients who switch from another insurer 
          (see section 4 below).<br>
          </font></li>
        <li><font size="2" face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" color="#000066">Highly 
          competitive premiums payable monthly by direct debit.<br>
          </font></li>
        <li><font size="2" face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" color="#000066">Up 
          to &pound;2,500 per month of tax free benefit.<br>
          </font></li>
        <li><font size="2" face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" color="#000066">Benefits 
          are payable for either 12 or 24 months after only 30 days excess period.<br>
          </font></li>
        <li><font size="2" face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" color="#000066">Unemployment 
          only cover, or disability only cover, or both combined.<br>
          </font></li>
        <li><font size="2" face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" color="#000066">The 
          self-employed, directors and proprietors of businesses are covered if 
          their business ceases to trade.<br>
          </font></li>
        <li><font size="2" face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" color="#000066">No 
          premium loading on the basis of age or gender.<br>
          </font></li>
      </ul>
      <p><font size="2" face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" color="#000066"><b>3. 
        Who is eligible for cover under this insurance?</b></font></p>
      <p><font size="2" face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" color="#000066"> 
        You can apply for this insurance if you:<br>
        </font></p>
      <ul>
        <li><font size="2" face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" color="#000066">are 
          aged between 18 and 64 and are permanently resident within the UK, Channel 
          Islands or Isle of Man,</font></li>
        <li><font size="2" face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" color="#000066">are 
          actively working for more than 20 hours per week and have been in continuous 
          employment for more than six months before the insurance start date. 
          You cannot obtain this insurance if you work in temporary or seasonal 
          employment,<br>
          </font></li>
        <li><font size="2" face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" color="#000066">are 
          in good health and know of no impending unemployment or disability.<br>
          </font></li>
      </ul>
      <p><font size="2" face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" color="#000066"><b>4. 
        When can you make a claim under this insurance?</b></font></p>
      <p><font size="2" face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" color="#000066">You 
        can make a claim under this insurance at any time during the period of 
        insurance providing you notify us as soon as reasonably possible. However, 
        unless you qualify for exclusion free transfer, there is a period of time 
        immediately following the insurance start date during which any occurrence 
        or notification of impending unemployment will not be insured. This is 
        known as the initial exclusion period and will depend on the monthly benefit 
        you select as detailed below:</font></p>

      <p></p>
      <p><font size="2" face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" color="#000066">To 
        qualify for exclusion free transfer you must transfer your existing monthly 
        unemployment and disability insurance (or unemployment only insurance) 
        from another insurer. Furthermore you must be able to prove that your 
        existing insurance has been in force for at least six months and that 
        you have not made a claim under that insurance.<br>
        </font></p>
      <p><font size="2" face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" color="#000066"><b>5. 
        How long are claim benefits paid for?</b></font></p>
      <p><font size="2" face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" color="#000066">You 
        can choose to receive up to a maximum of either 12 or 24 monthly benefit 
        payments for any separate unemployment or disability claim under this 
        insurance.</font></p>
      <p><font size="2" face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" color="#000066"><b>6. 
        When do you receive your first benefit payment?</b></font></p>
      <p><font size="2" face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" color="#000066">Subject 
        to the provisions of section 4, you will need to be continuously unemployed 
        or disabled for a period of 30 consecutive days before you are able to 
        claim benefit under this insurance. Thereafter payments will continue 
        monthly in arrears until the earliest of the following events:</font></p>
      <ul>
        <li><font size="2" face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" color="#000066">the 
          last consecutive day that you remain unemployed or disabled,<br>
          </font></li>
        <li><font size="2" face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" color="#000066">the 
          date you cease to provide evidence that you remain continuously unemployed 
          or disabled,<br>
          </font></li>
        <li><font size="2" face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" color="#000066">the 
          date on which you have received maximum benefit payments for any separate 
          period of claim,<br>
          </font></li>
        <li><font size="2" face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" color="#000066">the 
          finishing date of this insurance.<br>
          </font></li>
      </ul>
      <p><font size="2" face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" color="#000066"><b>7. 
        How much cover can you have?</b></font></p>
      <p><font size="2" face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" color="#000066">The 
        amount of cover you select is known as the monthly benefit. The maximum 
        monthly benefit available per applicant under this insurance is &pound;2,500 
        or 50% of gross monthly income* whichever is the lower.<br>
        <span class="small">* Gross monthly income means monthly salary plus the 
        average of any overtime, commission and/or bonus payments received in 
        the 12 months prior to the insurance start date.</span><br>
        </font></p>
