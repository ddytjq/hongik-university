            REPORT</b></td>
        </tr>
        <tr>
          <td valign="top" width="100%" colspan="3" class="engfanz"> 
            <p>England are out of the World Cup after losing 2-1 to Brazil. England 
              had a fantastic start to the game going in front through Michael 
              Owen but Brazil fought back just before half-time Rivaldo pulling 
              the score level. Brazil went in front on 50 minutes with Ronaldinho's 
              cross catching out David Seaman going over the Arsenal's keepers 
              head into the net. Ronaldinho was then sent off for a foul on Mills 
              but England looked jaded and could not get back into the game. A 
              disappointing exit because England did not do themselves justice, 
              maybe it was the heat or as Sven Goran Eriksson said his players 
              were tired England did not do enough to test the Brazilians up front.</p>
