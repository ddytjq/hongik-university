      <p>You may invest up to a maximum of &pound;500,000 provided that your total 
        investment in this and all other Chelsea accounts does not exceed &pound;1 
        million. </p>
      <p>You may add to your investment in your Chelsea 1 Year Fixed Rate Bond 
        (20th Issue) while it is available to new investors. </p>
      <p>Once the Bond has been withdrawn, further investments may only be made 
        after the end of the fixed rate period.</p>

        to your funds until 3rd September 2003, subject to 60 days' loss of interest 
        on the amount withdrawn.
      <p>Up to &pound;250 in cash or &pound;5,000 by cheque may be withdrawn immediately.
