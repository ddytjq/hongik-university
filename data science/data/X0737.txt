<div align="center"><h2>Membership at INBNews.com</h2></div>
<FONT FACE="Arial" SIZE="2">On February 20, 2001, Indiana Basketball News is switching to a new paid membership format.<p>
In an effort to provide an increasingly great service to our fans and users Indiana Basketball News has undertaken an extreme amount of financial burden.  The paid memberships will help to keep Indiana Basketball News at the top of its game!
<p>
<strong>The following areas of the site will continue to be free:</strong><br>
-Discussion Board<br>
-New Weekly News Area and Links to Online Resources<br>
-Access to all INBNews event information
<p>
<strong>The following areas of the site will become member only accessible:</strong><br>
<strong>News Subscribers will receive:</strong><br>
-Late breaking recruiting news<br>
-News happening in  the lengthy Spring/Summer Recruiting Season plus the Winter High School Season will be

-Access to all of the free areas of the site!
<p>
<strong>College Coach Subscribers will receive:</strong><br>

delivered online daily to all member subscribers. <br>
-Access to all of the free areas of the site!
