Among all boomerang events, Long Distance best conveys the spirit of the boomerang, in our humble view. Being able to throw a man-made object nearly out of sight, then watching it turn around and come all the way back to you is a thrilling experience.<p>
We are committed to promoting LD and encouraging throwers to participate in or organize LD events. If you have enquiries of any kind or seek assistance, we would be glad to offer our help. Just contact us at
