      <p><font face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif"><b>Introduction</b></font></p>
      <p><font size="2" face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">This <b>Certificate</b> 
        wording, <b>Your</b> current <b>Schedule</b> and any <b>Endorsements</b> 
        which the <b>Schedule</b> says apply are to be read together and any word 
        or expression used with a specific meaning in any of them has the same 
        meaning wherever it appears. These documents contain the terms of the 
        contract of insurance between <b>You</b> and <b>Us</b>. The contract is 
        based on<br>
        the proposal form and declaration which <b>You</b> signed. In this <b>Certificate</b> 
        wording. <b>We</b> refer to the contract as this and/or the <b>Certificate</b>.</font></p>
      <p><font size="2" face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif"><b>We</b> 
        will insure <b>You</b> subject to the terms of this <b>Certificate</b>, 
        against loss, damage or legal liability which may occur during any <b>Period 
        of Insurance</b> for which <b>We</b> have accepted <b>Your</b> premium.</font></p>
      <p><font size="2" face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif"><b>We</b> 
        may, at <b>Our</b> option, make a payment in money, repair, replace or 
        reinstate property lost or damaged. The maximum<br>
        amount <b>We</b> will pay for any one claim is the sum shown against the 
        item in the <b>Schedule</b>.</font></p>
      <p><font size="2" face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">Please read 
        the <b>Certificate</b> wording and the <b>Schedule</b> together with any 
        applicable <b>Endorsements</b> very carefully to<br>
        make certain that <b>You</b> have the protection <b>You</b> need. Please, 
        also ensure all conditions are strictly complied with, as non-compliance, 
        whether unintentional or not, could invalidate this <b>Certificate</b>.</font></p>
      <p><font size="2" face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif"><b>We</b> 
        hope that in the event of a claim, the matter will be dealt with swiftly 
        and efficiently. However, if <b>You</b> have any complaint about <b>Our</b> 
        service to <b>You</b>, please follow <b>Our</b> complaints procedure.<br>
        </font></p>

      <p><font face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif"><b>Definitions</b></font></p>
      <p><font size="2" face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">The definitions 
        tell you precisely what each term means where ever it is used. They are<br>
        emboldened throughout the <b>Certificate</b></font></p>
      <p><font size="2" face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif"><b>Certificate 
        holder/You/Your</b><br>
        The person or persons named as &quot;The Insured&quot; in the <b>Schedule</b>. 
        </font></p>
      <p><font size="2" face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif"><b>We/Our/Us</b><br>
        UK Insurance</font></p>
      <p> <font size="2" face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif"><b>Home</b><br>
        The private residence, garage and outbuildings within the <b>Premises</b> 
        shown as the risk address in the <b>Schedule</b> and used for domestic 
        purposes only.</font></p>
      <p><font size="2" face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif"><b>Excess</b><br>
        The first part of any claim which <b>You</b> have to bear.</font></p>
      <p><font size="2" face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif"><b>Unoccupied</b><br>
        If any <b>Home</b> is not being lived in by anyone with Your permission.</font></p>
      <p><font size="2" face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif"><b>Period 
        of Insurance</b><br>
        The period shown in the <b>Schedule</b> for which <b>We</b> have agreed 
        to accept and <b>You</b> have paid, or agreed to pay, a premium.</font></p>
      <p><font face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif"><b><font size="2">Premises</font></b><font size="2"><br>
        The <b>Buildings</b> and the land on which they stand, all within the 
        boundary belonging to them.</font></font></p>
      <p><font size="2" face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif"><b>Endorsement</b><br>
        A change or variation in the terms of the <b>Certificate</b>.</font></p>
      <p><font size="2" face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif"><b>Schedule</b><br>
        The document showing the details of the <b>Certificate holder</b>, the 
        property insured, the <b>Period of Insurance</b> and the sections of the 
        <b>Certificate</b> which apply.</font></p>
      <p><font size="2" face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif"><b>Buildings</b><br>
        The structure of the <b>Home</b> (including external radio and television 
        aerials or satellite receiving equipment up to &pound;1000) and:</font></p>
      <ul>
        <li><font size="2" face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">domestic 
          outbuildings, permanently constructed swimming pools (but not<br>
          swimming pool covers), paved terraces, patios, walls, gates, fences, 
          paths, drives, domestic<br>
          fixed fuel-oil tanks</font></li>
        <li><font size="2" face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">landlords' 
          fixtures and fittings on or in the <b>Home</b></font></li>
        <li><font size="2" face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">any common 
          parts to <b>Your Home</b>. All within the <b>Premises</b> shown in the 
          <b>Schedule</b>.<br>
          The main <b>Building</b> of the <b>Home</b> must be built of brick, 
          stone or concrete OR, if built during 1980 or after, timber-framed AND 
          roofed with slates, tiles, metal, asbestos, asphalt or concrete unless 
          agreed otherwise by special <b>Endorsement</b>.</font></li>
      </ul>
      <p><font size="2" face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif"><b>Contents</b><br>
        Domestic furniture and furnishings (excluding <b>Valuables</b>) belonging 
        to <b>You</b> or for which <b>You</b> are<br>
        responsible in any <b>Home</b> where <b>You</b> are providing furnished 
        accommodation other than for <b>Your</b> own use.</font></p>
      <p><font size="2" face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">The limit 
        on any one item is &pound;2500 or 10% of the sum insured, whichever the 
        greater BUT NOT including:</font></p>
      <ul>
        <li><font size="2" face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">Trees, 
          bushes, plants or shrubs</font></li>
        <li><font size="2" face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">Bonds, 
          bills of exchange, securities,<br>
          documents, manuscripts, money of any kind</font></li>
        <li><font size="2" face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">Property 
          in the open</font></li>
        <li><font size="2" face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">Any part 
          of the structure of the <b>Buildings</b>, ceilings, wallpaper or the 
          like.</font></li>
      </ul>
      <p> <font size="2" face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif"><b>Valuables</b><br>
        Items composed of precious metals or precious stones, jewellery, watches, 
        furs, curios and works of art.<br>
        </font></p>

      <p><font face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif"><b>Section 1 - Buildings</b></font></p>

          the <b>Premises</b> are insured in one amount on behalf of the individual 
          owners or lessees</font>.</li>

            at all times;</font></li>
          <li><font size="2" face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif"> advise 
            <b>Us</b> before any changes to the security at the <b>Home</b> are 
            made;</font></li>
          <li><font size="2" face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif"> put 
            all the security precautions into operation whenever the <b>Home</b> 
            is left <b>Unoccupied</b>.</font></li>
        </ol>

        </ol>
      </ol>

        <li><font size="2" face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">Removal

        <li><font size="2" face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">The cost 
          of complying with building regulations, local authority or other statutory 
          requirements except where notice of the need to comply was served upon 
          <b>You</b> before the damage occurred or these relate to undamaged parts 
          of the <b>Buildings</b>.</font></li>
      </ol>
      <p><font size="2" face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif"><b>We</b> 
        will NOT pay for fees incurred in preparing or furthering any claim under 
        this <b>Certificate</b>. </font></p>
      <p><font size="2" face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif"><b>We</b> 
        will pay the cost of repair or replacement less a deduction for wear, 
        tear or betterment:</font></p>

          the <b>Buildings</b> would be improved by the repair or replacement</font></li>
        <li><font size="2" face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">if the 
          <b>Buildings</b> have not been maintained in good repair</font></li>
        <li><font size="2" face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">if at 
          the time of any loss or damage the sum insured is less than the full 
          rebuilding cost.</font></li>
      </ul>
      <p><font size="2" face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">If repair 
        or replacement is not carried out <b>We</b> will, at <b>Our</b> option, 
        pay the reduction in market value resulting from the damage not exceeding 
        what it would have cost to repair the damage to <b>Your Home</b> if the 
        repair work had been carried out without delay.</font></p>
      <p><font size="2" face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">The maximum 
        amount payable in respect of any one claim under A-L is the sum insured 
        (less any <b>Excess</b>) shown in the <b>Schedule</b>.</font></p>
      <p><font size="2" face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">The sum insured 
        on <b>Buildings</b> will not be reduced following payment of a claim.</font><font face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif"><br>
        </font></p>
