  <p class="subheads">A Friendly Society you can trust</p>
  <p class="body">POIS Assurance is now part of Family Assurance Friendly Society 
    Limited, and as such brings its members the benefits and security of being 
    part of the UK&#146;s largest tax-exempt friendly society, with over 500,000 
    members and over &pound;1.5 billion of funds under management (as at February 
    2002).</p>
  <p class="body">POIS Assurance has a history of providing our members with good 
    value protection and secure and profitable savings. The partnership with Family 
    means that we can not only continue with this but also offer our customers 
    a wider choice of options for their financial planning and bring them value 
    for money, quality products. </p>
  <p class="body">We welcome the continued interest from employees of the Royal 
    Mail, BT and the Civil Service. </p>
  <p class="subheads">The benefits of Membership</p>
  <p class="body">Friendly Societies are &quot;mutual&quot; companies, which means 
    we do not have any shareholders - Members share directly in the performance 
    of the Society. And what&#146;s more your investments in our tax exempt savings 
    plans will benefit from being free of Income Tax and Capital Gains Tax. </p>
  <p class="subheads">Deductions from pay</p>
  <p class="body">If you wish, and provided your employer allows, your monthly 
    premium can be paid in weekly instalments by having deductions made automatically 
    from your pay or pension - a convenient benefit for many of our Members. </p>
