<h1 align="center"><u>Recent Changes</u></h1>
<p>This is a record of most of the changes and new additions that have been made
to the web page over the past few months.</p>

<p></p><center><b><u>22 Jun 2002</u></b><br />
On the <a href="50lys.html">list of Stars within 50 Light Years</a>, HR6094 is now
known to have a planet and Rho1 Cancri is now known to have 3 planets.
</center>

<p></p><center><b><u>16 Jun 2002</u></b><br />
I changed the <a href="darknebs.html">Dark Nebulae</a> page - I replaced 
the map with a <a href="darknebs.gif">new version</a> which includes southern 
hemisphere objects.  I changed the list next to it.  I also added a second diagram 
of <a href="mwclouds.gif">molecular clouds</a>.<br />
And I replaced the picture of the Pipe Nebula with a <a href="npipe.jpg">better version</a>.<br />
I added a few extra dark nebulae to <a href="2000lys.gif">this map</a>. 
</center>
<p></p><center><b><u>25 May 2002</u></b><br />
I revised the list of <a href="stars.html">The 200 Brightest Stars</a>, especially the
star names and the spectral classes.<br />
I also replaced the picture at the bottom of the page with a picture of Orion's belt.
</center>
<p></p><center><b><u>9 May 2002</u></b><br />
I added a plot of <a href="nebclust.html#temp">our part of the Galaxy within 20000 light years</a>.<br />
I also made a <a href="hr.html">Hertzsprung-Russell diagram</a> by plotting 23000 stars
from the Hipparcos and Gliese catalogues.
</center>
<p></p><center><b><u>18 April 2002</u></b><br />
I added one more star (LP 656-38) to the maps [<a href="20lys.gif">1</a>, <a href="w20lys.gif">2</a>] 
and <a href="nearstar.html">list</a> of stars within 20 light years.<br />
There are now 110 stars within 20 light years in 79 star systems.
</center>
<p></p><center><b><u>13 April 2002</u></b><br />
Aleksandar Zorkic translated this site into <a href="http://www.astronomija.co.yu/au/">Serbian!</a>
</center>
<p></p><center><b><u>11 April 2002</u></b><br />
I rearranged and partially rewrote the page on <a href="bigbang.html">The Expansion of the Universe</a>
to make it clearer.
</center>
<p></p><center><b><u>31 March 2002</u></b><br />
I created a page explaining all about <a href="bigbang.html">The Expansion of the Universe</a>.
</center>
<p></p><center><b><u>17 March 2002</u></b><br />
I added a map of the universe within <a href="200mill.html">200 million light years</a>.<br />
I also modified the description of the <a href="superc.html">Centaurus Supercluster</a>.
A3526 is the dominant cluster, not A3565.
</center>
<p></p><center><b><u>4 March 2002</u></b><br />
Norbert Rumiano translated this site into <a href="http://atunivers.free.fr/">French!</a>
</center>
<p></p><center><b><u>1 March 2002</u></b><br />
I rewrote some of the descriptions of <a href="virgo.html">nearby galaxy groups</a>.<br />
I also changed the picture caption at the bottom of <a href="galax200.html">this page</a>.
</center>
<p></p><center><b><u>16 February 2002</u></b></center>

<p></p><center><b><u>2 February 2002</u></b><br />
I completely revised the map of the <a href="galgrps.html">Nearest Galaxy Groups</a> and 
I also revised the list of nearby galaxies underneath it.  I also added a list of the 
<a href="galax200.html">200 Brightest Galaxies</a>.
</center>
<p></p><center><b><u>23 January 2002</u></b><br />
I created some slightly better <a href="stars.gif">wallpaper</a>. :)
</center>
<p></p><center><b><u>19 January 2002</u></b><br />
I replaced the <a href="250lys.html">250 Light Years map</a> (and 
<a href="w250lys.gif">this one</a>) with an actual plot of the 1500 most luminous 
stars within 250 light years, and I also rewrote the "About the Map" description, I
had to change my claim that the majority of the naked eye stars are within 250 light
years because it isn't actually true. :)<br />
I also added a map of <a href="250lys.html#temp">the Hyades star cluster</a>.
</center>
<p></p><center><b><u>12 January 2002</u></b><br />
I redrew the <a href="universe.html">Visible Universe map</a> 
(and <a href="wunivers.gif">this one</a>),
and I also replaced the <a href="hstdeep.jpg">Hubble Deep Field picture</a> with a 
larger version.<br />
I also modified the <a href="superc.html">1 billion light years map</a>
(and <a href="wsupercl.gif">this one</a>).
<p></p><center><b><u>5 January 2002</u></b><br />
I added this <a href="glossary.html">Glossary page</a>.
</center>
<p></p><center><b><u>1 January 2002</u></b><br />
I changed the <a href="20lys.html">20 light years map</a> 
(and <a href="w20lys.gif">this one</a>).  I added two new 'DENIS' stars to it
but removed the Wolf 630 system which probably lies at 21 light years.
I also revised the <a href="nearstar.html">List of the Nearest Stars</a>.<br />
I also added this <a href="nearstar.html#temp">picture of Proxima Centauri</a>.
</center>
<p></p><center><b><u>15 December 2001</u></b><br />
I made minor changes to the <a href="50lys.gif">50 light years map</a> 
(and <a href="w50lys.gif">this one</a>) and revised the 
<a href="50lys.html">list of the stars within 50 light years</a> (especially columns 6, 
11 and 12).<br />  I also added a picture of Capella at the bottom of the page.
</center>
<p></p><center><b><u>24 November 2001</u></b><br />
Redrew the <a href="5000lys.html">Orion Arm</a> and also added 
an <a href="galaxy.html">all-sky plot</a> of the 25000 brightest, whitest stars.<br />
Also created this Recent Changes page.
</center>
<p></p><center><b><u>2 November 2001</u></b><br />
Revised and redrew the other picture of <a href="galaxy.html">The Galaxy</a>. 
</center>
<p></p><center><b><u>1 November 2001</u></b><br />
Added <a href="stardist.html">this diagram</a> that zooms out from the Earth to the 
nearest stars. 
</center>
<p></p><center><b><u>13 October 2001</u></b><br />
I redrew the <a href="milkyway.html">Milky Way</a> yet again to make it even more 
accurate and incorporate new information about the shape of the central bar. 
</center>
<p></p><center><b><u>27 September 2001</u></b><br />
Completely revised and redrew the <a href="milkyway.html">Map of the Milky Way.</a> 
</center>
<p></p><center><b><u>26 August 2001</u></b><br />
New! <a href="2billion.html">A Map of Superclusters within 2 Billion Light Years!</a> 
</center>
<p></p><center><b><u> 4 August 2001</u></b><br />
Added a detailed map of the <a href="2000lys.html">Orion Arm within 2000 light years.</a> 
<p></p><center><b><u>30 June 2001</u></b><br />
Completely revised the map of the <a href="galgrps.html">nearest galaxy groups</a> and
added a list of the nearest bright galaxies. 
</center>
<p></p><center><b><u>23 June 2001</u></b><br />
Revised everything on <a href="superc.html">superclusters</a>.  Redrew the 1 billion 
light years map, rewrote all of the supercluster descriptions and revised the 
<a href="supercls.html">list</a> of the nearest superclusters. 
</center>
