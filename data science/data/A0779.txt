      <p>We've arranged a 1.5% discount off your interest rate for the first six
        months - especially helpful if you're buying your first house. If you're
        not moving home, you can also switch from your existing lender to a Sainsbury's
        Bank flexipay discount mortgage.</p>
      <p>We want to give you maximum flexibility when you're buying your own home.
      </p>
      <p>Here are some of your options. You can choose any of the options whenever
        you want as long as you borrow no more than your Home Loan Upper Limit.
      </p>
      <p>Certain restrictions apply - see '<a href="#exercising">exercising your
        options</a>' and '<a href="#home">home loan upper limit</a>' sections:</p>
      <p><a href="../mortgages/mortgages_products_discounted_1_1.html">option
        1</a><br>
        own your own home sooner and pay less interest </p>
      <p><a href="../mortgages/mortgages_products_discounted_1_2.html">option
        2 </a><br>
        make 10 repayments a year and take 2 months off</p>
      <p><a href="../mortgages/mortgages_products_discounted_1_3.html">option
        3 </a><br>
        borrow money against any equity in your home</p>
      <p><a href="../mortgages/mortgages_products_discounted_1_4.html">option
        4 </a><br>
        pay less each month and manage a tighter budget</p>
      <p><a href="../mortgages/mortgages_products_discounted_1_5.html">option
        5</a><br>
        take a repayment holiday of up to 6 months</p>
      <p class="bodyBold">exercising your options<a name="exercising"></a></p>
      <p>The flexible options are exercised by calling Sainsbury's Bank Customer
        Services. <br>
        A pre-set limit is placed on each mortgage account at the outset, equivalent
        to the original loan plus 5% of the valuation of the property. The 'overpayments'
        and '10 repayments a year' , flexible options may be exercised immediately,
        the other flexible options may be exercised after 3 or 24 monthly repayments
        depending on the original loan to value and provided there has been no
        breach of the mortgage terms and conditions. Underpayment can be made
        or further advances taken up to the pre-set limit. Payments can be stopped
        for a maximum of six monthly instalments or up to the pre-set limit, whichever
        is less. Any previous overpayments or lump sum payments into the account
        can also be withdrawn.<br>
      </p>
      <p class="bodyBold">home loan upper limit <a name="home"></a></p>
      <p>You can choose any of the options whenever you want as long as you don't
        borrow any more than your Home Loan Upper Limit. </p>
      <p>We'll agree your upper limit with you when you take out your flexipay
        discount . It will be equivalent to the original amount you borrowed,
        plus 5% of the value of your property. </p>
      <p>Property value = £50,000 </p>
      <p>You borrow £45,000 + 5% of property value £2,500 = Home Loan Upper Limit
        £47,5000</p>
