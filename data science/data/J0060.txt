Provides a second opinion and provides a search engines that can help you find new and used cars in your area.
Provides For Sale-By-Owner deals.<BR><p>  
<li><a href="http://www.autobytel.com/kbb/">****Auto-By-Tel</a><BR>
Primarily geared toward new car shoppers, Auto-By-Tel can save you the trauma of scrambling among dealerships, shopping for the lowest price.  Simply enter your desired make, model, color, and options into Auto-By-Tel's database, and the search engine will automatically find the dealer nearest you with the lowest possible price.  Within 48 hours, somebody from the dealership will call you up with an offer.
<li><a href="http://www.dealernet.com/">***DealerNet</a><BR>
Provides a second opinion and provides a search engines that can help you find new and used car dealers in your area.
<li><a href="http://www.classifieds2000.com">***Classifieds 2000</a><BR>
Search database for a used car, place a free ad to sell your car.
<li><a href="http://www.financenter.com/autoloan/newcalcs/leasvbuy.htm">***Financenter</a><BR>
Leasing a car?  This site offers an interactive calculator to help compare the costs and benefits of leasing vs. buying a new car.  Leasing might not save you dollars off the purchase price, but if could definitely save you the hassle of repairs.<P>
