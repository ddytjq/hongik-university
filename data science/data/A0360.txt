    hear from you.</small></font><p><small><font color="#FFFFFF">Letting us know when you are
    unhappy with the service you experience gives us the opportunity to put matters right for
    you and to improve our service in the future for everybody.</font></small></p>
    <p><small><font color="#FFFFFF">Our staff are the best placed to receive your complaint
    and to work with you to resolve it. You can share your concerns with them in person, by
    phone, by letter or email.</font></small></p>
    <p><small><font color="#FFFFFF">Alternatively you can contact our Customer Relation Team
    at:</font></small></p>
    <p><font color="#FFFFFF"><small>Customer Relations</small><br>
    <small>Barclays Bank PLC</small><br>
    <small>54 Lombard Street</small><br>
    <small>FREEPOST LON13542</small><br>
    <small>London </small><br>
    <small>EC3B 3PJ</small></font></p>
    <p></font><font face="Verdana" color="#FFFFFF"><small>Telephone:0800 282 390 (UK only free
    call) or 020 7699 4998</small><br>
    <small>E-mail: <a style="color: rgb(255,255,255)" href="mailto:customer.relations@barclays.co.uk">customer.relations@barclays.co.uk</a>
    </small><br>
    </font><font face="Verdana"><strong><font color="#FF0000"><small>(for confidentiality
    reasons, please do not send your account details if submitting your complaint via e-mail).</small></font></strong></p>
    <b><p><small><font color="#FFFFFF">Contacting us by e-mail</font></small></b></p>
    <p><small><font color="#FFFFFF">Our main websites contain links that enable you to advise
    us of your complaints by email. These websites are:</font></small></p>
    <p><font color="#FFFFFF"><a href="http://www.personal.barclays.com" style="color: rgb(255,255,255)"><small>www.personal.barclays.com</small></a><br>
    <a href="http://www.smallbusiness.barclays.com" style="color: rgb(255,255,255)"><small>www.smallbusiness.barclays.com</small></a><br>
    <a href="http://www.corporate.barclays.com" style="color: rgb(255,255,255)"><small>www.corporate.barclays.com</small></a></font></p>
    <p><small><font color="#FFFFFF">We usually reply to your email address but there may be
    occasions when we need to do so by post, for example when we need to refer to confidential
    information or enclose copies of important documents.</font></small></p>
    <p><b><small><font color="#FFFFFF">What you need to provide</font></small></p>
    </b><p><small><font color="#FFFFFF">To help us investigate and resolve your complaint as
    quickly as possible, please provide the following information:</font></small><ul>
      <li><small><font color="#FFFFFF">Your name and address</font></small></li>
      <li><small><font color="#FFFFFF">Your account number and sort code </font><font color="#FF0000"><strong>(for confidentiality reasons, please do not send these details if
        submitting via email.)</strong></font></small></li>
      <li><small><font color="#FFFFFF">A daytime telephone number where we can contact you</font></small></font></li>
      <li><font face="Verdana"><small><font color="#FFFFFF">A clear description of your concern or
        complaint</font></small></li>
      <li><small><font color="#FFFFFF">Details of what you would like us to do to put it right.</font></small></li>
    </ul>
    <p><small><font color="#FFFFFF">Where applicable please also provide:</font></small><ul>
      <li><small><font color="#FFFFFF">Copies of any relevant documents, such as letters or
        policies</font></small></li>
      <li><small><font color="#FFFFFF">The policy number or other appropriate reference.</font></small></li>
    </ul>
    <b><p><small><font color="#FFFFFF">How will we respond</font></small></b></p>
    <p><small><font color="#FFFFFF">We will do our best to resolve your complaint immediately
    and with the minimum of inconvenience to you.</font></small></p>
    <p><small><font color="#FFFFFF">Our first step is to understand what the problem is and to
    find out what we can do to put the matter right.</font></small></p>
    <p><small><font color="#FFFFFF">Sometimes we may not be able to sort it out straight away.
    Where we cannot resolve it by the following day, this is what you can expect from us:</font></small></p>
    <p><small><font color="#FFFFFF">A written acknowledgement of your complaint within five
    working days.</font></small></p>
    <p><small><font color="#FFFFFF">Details of who is handling your complaint and how to
    contact them. Sometimes this may not be the person who received your complaint but the
    individual best placed to deal with it.</font></small></p>
    <p><small><font color="#FFFFFF">If your complaint will take longer to investigate, we will
    keep you informed of progress.</font></small></p>
    <p><small><font color="#FFFFFF">We aim to resolve your complaint within four weeks. If we
    are unable to do so, we will write explaining what&#146;s happening and let you know when
    we expect to do so. </font></small></p>
    <p><small><font color="#FFFFFF">After eight weeks we will send you a final response or a
    further progress report on our investigations.</font></small></p>
    <b><p><small><font color="#FFFFFF">If you are still not happy&#133;</font></small></b></p>
    <p><small><font color="#FFFFFF">Our aim is to resolve your complaint as quickly as
    possible and to your complete satisfaction. If, for whatever reason, you are unhappy with
    the response you receive from us please get in touch directly with the person or
    department who handled your complaint. They will then agree with you what the next steps
    are.</font></small></font></p>
    <font face="Verdana"><p><small><font color="#FFFFFF">If you are still dissatisfied , you
    can request a review from the Financial Ombudsman Service, which was set up by the
    Financial Services Authority to review unresolved complaints from: </font></small></p>
    <p><small><font color="#FFFFFF">Personal customers, or Business customers with a turnover
    under £1m per annum.</font></small></p>
    <p><small><font color="#FFFFFF">We can let you know if you are eligible to refer your
    complaint to the Financial Ombudsman Service directly at:</font></small></p>
    <p><font color="#FFFFFF"><small>The Financial Ombudsman Service </small><br>
    <small>South Quay Plaza</small><br>
    <small>183 Marsh Wall</small><br>
    <small>London E14 9SR</small><br>
    <small>Telephone: 0845 080 1800</small><br>
    <small>Email: <a href="mailto:Enquiries@financial-ombudsman.org.uk" style="color: rgb(255,255,255)">Enquiries@financial-ombudsman.org.uk</a></small><br>
    <small>Website: <a href="http://www.financial-ombudsman.org.uk" style="color: rgb(255,255,255)">www.financial-ombudsman.org.uk</a></small></font></p>
    <b><p><small><font color="#FFFFFF">Finally</b>&#133;</font></small></p>
    <p><small><font color="#FFFFFF">We value your feedback.</font></small></p>
    <p><small><font color="#FFFFFF">Please help us to get it right every time,</font></small></p>
    <p><small><font color="#FFFFFF">For every customer.</font></small></font></p>
