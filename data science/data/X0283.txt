<CENTER><P><B><I>The objects of darts is to score</I></B> <B><I><FONT COLOR="#FF0000">301/501/1001</FONT></I></B>
<B><I>points in as few throws as possible. You play with groups of 3 darts
- the total score of all 3 darts is removedd from your remaining total on
the scoreboard. Any dart landing outside the board does not score. Darts
landing in the outer ring double the nominal score, darts landing in the
middle ring score triple the nominal value. The very centre of the board
is called the <FONT COLOR="#FF0000">Bullseye</FONT>, or</I></B> <B><I><FONT COLOR="#FF0000">Bull</FONT>,
and is worth</I></B> <B><I><FONT COLOR="#FF0000">50 points</FONT>. The
Bull is surrounded by the Outer which is worth</I></B> <B><I><FONT COLOR="#FF0000">25
points.</FONT></I></B></P></CENTER>

<CENTER><P><B><I>To finish the game you must score the</I></B> <B><I><FONT COLOR="#FF0000">exact
number of points</FONT>. If at any time your cumulative score exceeds the
target you are</I></B> <B><I><FONT COLOR="#FF0000">'bust' </FONT>and the
score of that dart</I></B> <B><I><FONT COLOR="#FF0000">AND </FONT>any other
dart in that group of three does not count. You</I></B> <B><I><FONT COLOR="#FF0000">MUST</FONT></I></B>
<B><I>also finish on a</I></B> <B><I><FONT COLOR="#FF0000">double</FONT></I></B>
<B><I><FONT COLOR="#0000FF">(the dart must land in the outer circle and
the double must be the exact score you require to finish)</FONT>. There
is one exception to this rule, you may also</I></B> <B><I><FONT COLOR="#FF0000">finish
with a Bullseye</FONT></I></B> <B><I>- but beware, it is more difficult
than may appear!</I></B></P></CENTER>
