                                        <B>HOCKEY TERMINOLOGY<BR>
                                  <font size="1"><BR>
                                  </font></B></FONT>
                                <font size="1" face="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica"><B>Are 
                                you sick of wondering what all those obscure bits 
                                of jargon are... the ones that commentators spit 
                                forth every three seconds! Well, here's a big 
                                list of commonly used hockey words and phrases. 
                                Please let us know if we left anything out!</B></font><FONT SIZE="-1" FACE="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica"><B><BR>
                                &nbsp;
<P ALIGN=LEFT></B></FONT><DIV ALIGN="CENTER">
                                        
                                  <TABLE ID="Table8" BORDER=0 BGCOLOR="#FFCC00" CELLSPACING=3 CELLPADDING=3 WIDTH="86%">
                                    <TR> 
                                      <TD VALIGN=TOP WIDTH="33%" BGCOLOR="#FFFFFF"> 
                                        <div align="right"><font size="1"><b><font face="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">Altercation:</font></b></font></div>
                                      </TD>
                                      <TD WIDTH="66%" BGCOLOR="#FFFFFF"> 
                                        <P><font size="1" face="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">Physical 
                                          incident between at least two players 
                                          resulting in at least one penalty.</font>
                                      </TD>
                                    </TR>
                                    <TR> 
                                      <TD VALIGN=TOP WIDTH="33%" BGCOLOR="#FFFFCC" HEIGHT=32> 
                                        <div align="right"><font size="1"><b><font face="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">Assist:</font></b></font></div>
                                      </TD>
                                      <TD WIDTH="66%" BGCOLOR="#FFFFCC"> 
                                        <P><font size="1" face="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">A 
                                          pass or the last two passes which immediately 
                                          proceed a goal</font>
                                      </TD>
                                    </TR>
                                    <TR> 
                                      <TD VALIGN=TOP WIDTH="33%" BGCOLOR="#FFFFFF"> 
                                        <div align="right"><font size="1"><b><font face="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">Attacking 
                                          Zone:</font></b> </font></div>
                                      </TD>
                                      <TD VALIGN=TOP WIDTH="66%" BGCOLOR="#FFFFFF"> 
                                        <P><font size="1" face="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">Area 
                                          between opponent's blue line and goal 
                                          line. </font>
                                      </TD>
                                    </TR>
                                    <TR> 
                                      <TD VALIGN=TOP WIDTH="33%" BGCOLOR="#FFFFCC"> 
                                        <div align="right"><font size="1"><b><font face="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">Backcheck:</font></b></font></div>
                                      </TD>
                                      <TD WIDTH="66%" BGCOLOR="#FFFFCC"> 
                                        <P><font size="1" face="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">This 
                                          is when a player attempts to check or 
                                          harass a person of the opposite team 
                                          as they are reentering their defensive 
                                          zone.</font>
                                      </TD>
                                    </TR>
                                    <TR> 
                                      <TD VALIGN=TOP WIDTH="33%" BGCOLOR="#FFFFFF"> 
                                        <div align="right"><font size="1"><b><font face="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">Backhand 
                                          Shot: </font></b> </font></div>
                                      </TD>
                                      <TD WIDTH="66%" BGCOLOR="#FFFFFF"> 
                                        <P><font size="1" face="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">A 
                                          shot or pass made by a player using 
                                          his weaker shooting hand</font>
                                      </TD>
                                    </TR>
                                    <TR> 
                                      <TD VALIGN=TOP WIDTH="33%" BGCOLOR="#FFFFCC"> 
                                        <div align="right"><font size="1"><b><font face="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">Bench 
                                          Penalty: </font></b></font></div>
                                      </TD>
                                      <TD WIDTH="66%" BGCOLOR="#FFFFCC"> 
                                        <P><font size="1" face="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">The 
                                          removal of a player from the game for 
                                          two minutes following a breach of the 
                                          rules. Any player of the team may serve 
                                          the penalty (except a goaltender).</font>
                                      </TD>
                                    </TR>
                                    <TR> 
                                      <TD VALIGN=TOP WIDTH="33%" BGCOLOR="#FFFFFF"> 
                                        <div align="right"><font size="1"><b><font face="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">Biscuit:</font></b> 
                                          </font></div>
                                      </TD>
                                      <TD VALIGN=TOP WIDTH="66%" BGCOLOR="#FFFFFF"> 
                                        <P><font size="1" face="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">A 
                                          slang term for the puck </font>
                                      </TD>
                                    </TR>
                                    <TR> 
                                      <TD VALIGN=TOP WIDTH="33%" BGCOLOR="#FFFFCC"> 
                                        <div align="right"><font size="1"><b><font face="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">Blind 
                                          Pass:</font></b></font></div>
                                      </TD>
                                      <TD VALIGN=TOP WIDTH="66%" BGCOLOR="#FFFFCC"> 
                                        <P><font size="1" face="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">A 
                                          pass made without first looking up to 
                                          see to whom the puck is going to be 
                                          passed.</font>
                                      </TD>
                                    </TR>
                                    <TR> 
                                      <TD VALIGN=TOP WIDTH="33%" BGCOLOR="#FFFFFF"> 
                                        <div align="right"><font size="1"><b><font face="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">Blocker:</font></b> 
                                          </font></div>
                                      </TD>
                                      <TD WIDTH="66%" BGCOLOR="#FFFFFF"> 
                                        <P><font size="1" face="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">Pad 
                                          goaltender wears on arm used to hold 
                                          stick.</font>
                                      </TD>
                                    </TR>
                                    <TR> 
                                      <TD VALIGN=TOP WIDTH="33%" BGCOLOR="#FFFFCC" HEIGHT=19> 
                                        <div align="right"><font size="1"><b><font face="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">Blueliner:</font></b></font></div>
                                      </TD>
                                      <TD WIDTH="66%" BGCOLOR="#FFFFCC"> 
                                        <P><font size="1" face="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">Another 
                                          name for a defenseman. It's derived 
                                          from defensemen lining up on the blue 
                                          line to start a game, playing near the 
                                          blue line in the offensive zone and 
                                          protecting the defensive blue line against 
                                          opposing forwards.</font>
                                      </TD>
                                    </TR>
                                    <TR> 
                                      <TD VALIGN=TOP WIDTH="33%" BGCOLOR="#FFFFFF"> 
                                        <div align="right"><font size="1"><b><font face="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">Blue 
                                          Lines:</font></b></font></div>
                                      </TD>
                                      <TD WIDTH="66%" BGCOLOR="#FFFFFF"> 
                                        <P><font size="1" face="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">The 
                                          pair of 1 foot-wide blue (hence the 
                                          name) lines which break the ice into 
                                          thirds - they fall 60 ft from each goal. 
                                          These lines mark the attacking, neutral 
                                          and defending zones.</font>
                                      </TD>
                                    </TR>
                                    <TR> 
                                      <TD VALIGN=TOP WIDTH="33%" BGCOLOR="#FFFFCC"> 
                                        <div align="right"><font size="1"><b><font face="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">Board 
                                          Checking: </font></b></font></div>
                                      </TD>
                                      <TD WIDTH="66%" BGCOLOR="#FFFFCC"> 
                                        <P><font size="1" face="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">The 
                                          body checking, crosschecking, elbowing, 
                                          charging, or tripping of an opponent 
                                          in such a way that he is thrown violently 
                                          into the boards. This is also known 
                                          as boarding. It can incur a penalty 
                                          if done illegally. Also knowns as <B>Boarding</B></font>
                                      </TD>
                                    </TR>
                                    <TR> 
                                      <TD VALIGN=TOP WIDTH="33%" BGCOLOR="#FFFFFF" HEIGHT=64> 
                                        <div align="right"><font size="1"><b><font face="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">Boards:</font></b></font></div>
                                      </TD>
                                      <TD WIDTH="66%" BGCOLOR="#FFFFFF"> 
                                        <P><font size="1" face="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">The 
                                          panels surrounding the ice that are 
                                          42 inches wide. They are topped by glass 
                                          panels that protect the spectators from 
                                          flying pucks and flying players.</font>
                                      </TD>
                                    </TR>
                                    <TR> 
                                      <TD VALIGN=TOP WIDTH="33%" BGCOLOR="#FFFFCC"> 
                                        <div align="right"><font size="1"><b><font face="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">Body 
                                          Check:</font></b></font></div>
                                      </TD>
                                      <TD WIDTH="66%" BGCOLOR="#FFFFCC"> 
                                        <P><font size="1" face="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">The 
                                          legal use of the body (most often hip 
                                          or shoulder) to knock an opponent off 
                                          the puck.</font>
                                      </TD>
                                    </TR>
                                    <TR> 
                                      <TD VALIGN=TOP WIDTH="33%" BGCOLOR="#FFFFFF"> 
                                        <div align="right"><font size="1"><b><font face="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">Box:</font></b></font></div>
                                      </TD>
                                      <TD WIDTH="66%" BGCOLOR="#FFFFFF"> 
                                        <P><font size="1" face="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">A 
                                          penalty-killing setup in the defensive 
                                          zone where the defensive team forms 
                                          a &quot;box&quot; in front of their 
                                          goalie and keeps the opponents on the 
                                          outskirts.</font>
                                      </TD>
                                    </TR>
                                    <TR> 
                                      <TD VALIGN=TOP WIDTH="33%" BGCOLOR="#FFFFCC"> 
                                        <div align="right"><font size="1"><b><font face="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">Breakaway: 
                                          </font></b></font></div>
                                      </TD>
                                      <TD WIDTH="66%" BGCOLOR="#FFFFCC"> 
                                        <P><font size="1" face="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">An 
                                          offensive rush in which the player carrying 
                                          the puck moves past all of the other 
                                          teams defenseman and has a clear shot 
                                          on the goaltender. </font>
                                      </TD>
                                    </TR>
                                    <TR> 
                                      <TD VALIGN=TOP WIDTH="33%" BGCOLOR="#FFFFFF" HEIGHT=32> 
                                        <div align="right"><font size="1"><b><font face="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">Breaking 
                                          Pass: </font></b></font></div>
                                      </TD>
                                      <TD WIDTH="66%" BGCOLOR="#FFFFFF"> 
                                        <P><font size="1" face="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">A 
                                          pass to a teammate who is accelerating 
                                          ready for a breakaway. Also called a 
                                          <B>Breakout</B></font>
                                      </TD>
                                    </TR>
                                    <TR> 
                                      <TD VALIGN=TOP WIDTH="33%" BGCOLOR="#FFFFCC"> 
                                        <div align="right"><font size="1"><b><font face="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">Center: 
                                          </font></b></font></div>
                                      </TD>
                                      <TD WIDTH="66%" BGCOLOR="#FFFFCC"> 
                                        <P><font size="1" face="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">The 
                                          middle of the three attacking players, 
                                          who are &quot;forwards&quot;.</font>
                                      </TD>
                                    </TR>
                                    <TR> 
                                      <TD VALIGN=TOP WIDTH="33%" BGCOLOR="#FFFFFF"> 
                                        <div align="right"><font size="1"><b><font face="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">Center 
                                          Face-Off Circle: </font></b></font></div>
                                      </TD>
                                      <TD WIDTH="66%" BGCOLOR="#FFFFFF"> 
                                        <P><font size="1" face="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">Another 
                                          name for the center circle. It is where 
                                          the opening face-off is conducted, and 
                                          every subsequent face-off after a goal.</font>
                                      </TD>
                                    </TR>
                                    <TR> 
                                      <TD VALIGN=TOP WIDTH="33%" BGCOLOR="#FFFFCC"> 
                                        <div align="right"><font size="1"><b><font face="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">Center 
                                          Red Line: </font></b> </font></div>
                                      </TD>
                                      <TD WIDTH="66%" BGCOLOR="#FFFFCC"> 
                                        <P><font size="1" face="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">The 
                                          line that divides the ice in half. The 
                                          circle on the red line is referred to 
                                          as center ice.</font>
                                      </TD>
                                    </TR>
                                    <TR> 
                                      <TD VALIGN=TOP WIDTH="33%" BGCOLOR="#FFFFFF"> 
                                        <div align="right"><font size="1"><b><font face="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">Changing 
                                          on the Fly:</font></b> </font></div>
                                      </TD>
                                      <TD VALIGN=TOP WIDTH="66%" BGCOLOR="#FFFFFF"> 
                                        <P><font size="1" face="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">Teams 
                                          switch players during play.</font>
                                      </TD>
                                    </TR>
                                    <TR> 
                                      <TD VALIGN=TOP WIDTH="33%" BGCOLOR="#FFFFCC"> 
                                        <div align="right"><font size="1"><b><font face="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">Charging: 
                                          </font></b></font></div>
                                      </TD>
                                      <TD WIDTH="66%" BGCOLOR="#FFFFCC"> 
                                        <P><font size="1" face="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">Charging 
                                          is deemed to have been committed if, 
                                          in taking two steps or strides, a player 
                                          runs, jumps into, or charges into an 
                                          opponent, or body-checks or pushes an 
                                          opponent from behind.</font>
                                      </TD>
                                    </TR>
                                    <TR> 
                                      <TD VALIGN=TOP WIDTH="33%" BGCOLOR="#FFFFFF"> 
                                        <div align="right"><font size="1"><b><font face="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">Check/Checki 
                                          ng: </font></b></font></div>
                                      </TD>
                                      <TD WIDTH="66%" BGCOLOR="#FFFFFF"> 
                                        <P><font size="1" face="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">A 
                                          player is checked when an opponent shoves 
                                          or pushes him with his body or stick. 
                                          The act is called checking, the hit 
                                          is called a check. Pretty basic stuff.</font>
                                      </TD>
                                    </TR>
                                    <TR> 
                                      <TD VALIGN=TOP WIDTH="33%" BGCOLOR="#FFFFCC"> 
                                        <div align="right"><font size="1"><b><font face="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">Clearing 
                                          the Puck:</font></b> </font></div>
                                      </TD>
                                      <TD VALIGN=TOP WIDTH="66%" BGCOLOR="#FFFFCC"> 
                                        <P><font size="1" face="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">Knocking 
                                          the puck from in front of net.</font>
                                      </TD>
                                    </TR>
                                    <TR> 
                                      <TD VALIGN=TOP WIDTH="33%" BGCOLOR="#FFFFFF"> 
                                        <div align="right"><font size="1"><b><font face="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">Corners:</font></b></font></div>
                                      </TD>
                                      <TD WIDTH="66%" BGCOLOR="#FFFFFF"> 
                                        <P><font size="1" face="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">The 
                                          four curved areas of the hockey rink 
                                          where a lot of the action takes place.</font>
                                      </TD>
                                    </TR>
                                    <TR> 
                                      <TD VALIGN=TOP WIDTH="33%" BGCOLOR="#FFFFCC"> 
                                        <div align="right"><font size="1"><b><font face="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">Covering: 
                                          </font></b></font></div>
                                      </TD>
                                      <TD WIDTH="66%" BGCOLOR="#FFFFCC"> 
                                        <P><font size="1" face="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">Sticking 
                                          close to an attacker thus preventing 
                                          him from breaking away.</font>
                                      </TD>
                                    </TR>
                                    <TR> 
                                      <TD VALIGN=TOP WIDTH="33%" BGCOLOR="#FFFFFF"> 
                                        <div align="right"><font size="1"><b><font face="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">Crease: 
                                          </font></b></font></div>
                                      </TD>
                                      <TD WIDTH="66%" BGCOLOR="#FFFFFF"> 
                                        <P><font size="1" face="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">This 
                                          is the blue semicircle directly in front 
                                          of the goaltender. it's 6 feet in radius. 
                                          If a player does not have possession 
                                          of the puck, he may not enter the crease. 
                                          As well, if a player scores a goal while 
                                          another player is in the crease, the 
                                          goal will be deemed illegal and not 
                                          credited to the scoring team.</font>
                                      </TD>
                                    </TR>
                                    <TR> 
                                      <TD VALIGN=TOP WIDTH="33%" BGCOLOR="#FFFFCC"> 
                                        <div align="right"><font size="1"><b><font face="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">Criss-Cross 
                                          Attack: </font></b> </font></div>
                                      </TD>
                                      <TD WIDTH="66%" BGCOLOR="#FFFFCC"> 
                                        <P><font size="1" face="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">The 
                                          swapping of sides by the two wingmen 
                                          during an attack.</font>
                                      </TD>
                                    </TR>
                                    <TR> 
                                      <TD VALIGN=TOP WIDTH="33%" BGCOLOR="#FFFFFF"> 
                                        <div align="right"><font size="1"><b><font face="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">Crossbar: 
                                          </font></b> </font></div>
                                      </TD>
                                      <TD WIDTH="66%" BGCOLOR="#FFFFFF"> 
                                        <P><font size="1" face="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">The 
                                          horizontal bar along the top of the 
                                          goal</font>
                                      </TD>
                                    </TR>
                                    <TR> 
                                      <TD VALIGN=TOP WIDTH="33%" BGCOLOR="#FFFFCC"> 
                                        <div align="right"><font size="1"><b><font face="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">Cross-<br>
                                          Checking:</font></b></font></div>
                                      </TD>
                                      <TD WIDTH="66%" BGCOLOR="#FFFFCC"> 
                                        <P><font size="1" face="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">Holding 
                                          a stick with both hands to check an 
                                          opponent by using the shaft of the stick 
                                          with no part of the stick on the ice. 
                                          It incurs a penalty.</font>
                                      </TD>
                                    </TR>
                                    <TR> 
                                      <TD VALIGN=TOP WIDTH="33%" BGCOLOR="#FFFFFF"> 
                                        <div align="right"><font size="1"><b><font face="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">Cross-Over: 
                                          </font></b></font></div>
                                      </TD>
                                      <TD WIDTH="66%" BGCOLOR="#FFFFFF"> 
                                        <P><font size="1" face="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">A 
                                          skating move in which one foot is crossed 
                                          over the other alternately. It is mostly 
                                          used in circling on the ice.</font>
                                      </TD>
                                    </TR>
                                    <TR> 
                                      <TD VALIGN=TOP WIDTH="33%" BGCOLOR="#FFFFCC"> 
                                        <div align="right"><font size="1"><b><font face="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">Cupping:</font></b> 
                                          </font></div>
                                      </TD>
                                      <TD WIDTH="66%" BGCOLOR="#FFFFCC"> 
                                        <P><font size="1" face="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">Illegally 
                                          closing your hand around the puck.</font>
                                      </TD>
                                    </TR>
                                    <TR> 
                                      <TD VALIGN=TOP WIDTH="33%" BGCOLOR="#FFFFFF" HEIGHT=45> 
                                        <div align="right"><font size="1"><b><font face="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">Dead 
                                          Puck: </font></b></font></div>
                                      </TD>
                                      <TD WIDTH="66%" BGCOLOR="#FFFFFF" height="45"> 
                                        <P><font size="1" face="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">A 
                                          puck that is shot over the boards and 
                                          out of play by a player or a puck which 
                                          is taken out of play when a player closes 
                                          his hand around it.</font>
                                      </TD>
                                    </TR>
                                    <TR> 
                                      <TD VALIGN=TOP WIDTH="33%" BGCOLOR="#FFFFCC"> 
                                        <div align="right"><font size="1"><b><font face="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">Defending 
                                          Zone:</font></b> </font></div>
                                      </TD>
                                      <TD VALIGN=TOP WIDTH="66%" BGCOLOR="#FFFFCC"> 
                                        <P><font size="1" face="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">Area 
                                          inside blue line when opponents attack.</font>
                                      </TD>
                                    </TR>
                                    <TR> 
                                      <TD VALIGN=TOP WIDTH="33%" BGCOLOR="#FFFFFF"> 
                                        <div align="right"><font size="1"><b><font face="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">Defensemen:</font></b></font></div>
                                      </TD>
                                      <TD WIDTH="66%" BGCOLOR="#FFFFFF"> 
                                        <P><font size="1" face="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">The 
                                          left and right defensemen try to stop 
                                          the incoming play from the other teams 
                                          before they have any chance to score. 
                                          They block shots, clear the puck from 
                                          in front of their own net and cover 
                                          the other team's forwards. Offensively, 
                                          they pass the puck up the ice to the 
                                          forwards, then follow the play into 
                                          the attacking zone and try to keep it 
                                          there.</font>
                                      </TD>
                                    </TR>
                                    <TR> 
                                      <TD VALIGN=TOP WIDTH="33%" BGCOLOR="#FFFFCC" HEIGHT=20> 
                                        <div align="right"><font size="1"><b><font face="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">Deke:</font></b></font></div>
                                      </TD>
                                      <TD WIDTH="66%" BGCOLOR="#FFFFCC" height="20"> 
                                        <P><font size="1" face="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">When 
                                          a player fakes his opponent out of position, 
                                          it is called a deke.</font>
                                      </TD>
                                    </TR>
                                    <TR> 
                                      <TD VALIGN=TOP WIDTH="33%" BGCOLOR="#FFFFFF"> 
                                        <div align="right"><font size="1"><b><font face="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">Delayed 
                                          Offside: </font></b></font></div>
                                      </TD>
                                      <TD WIDTH="66%" BGCOLOR="#FFFFFF"> 
                                        <P><font size="1" face="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">When 
                                          an attacking player crosses the attacking 
                                          blue line ahead of the puck, but the 
                                          defending team collects the puck, and 
                                          is in a position to bring it out of 
                                          their defending zone without any delay 
                                          or contact with an attacking player, 
                                          the offside call is delayed. It is similar 
                                          to the advantage rule in football. However, 
                                          if the puck goes beyond the top end 
                                          of the face-off circles, the offside 
                                          is called immediately.</font>
                                      </TD>
                                    </TR>
                                    <TR> 
                                      <TD VALIGN=TOP WIDTH="33%" BGCOLOR="#FFFFCC" HEIGHT=32> 
                                        <div align="right"><font size="1"><b><font face="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">Dig: 
                                          </font></b></font></div>
                                      </TD>

                                          but if no team can score in the five 
                                          minute period, the game ends in a tie.</font>
                                      </TD>
                                    </TR>

                                          the penalty takes varies according to 
                                          the severity of the offense.</font>
                                      </TD>
                                    </TR>

                                          a clear path to the goal. The penalty 
                                          shot is a free shot given to the player, 
                                          where only the goaltender remains to 
                                          defend the goal.</font>
                                      </TD>
                                    </TR>

                                        <P><font size="1" face="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">Goalposts.</font>
                                      </TD>
                                    </TR>

                                          progress. Each line, which consists 
                                          of groups of three forwards and two 
                                          defensemen, change about every minute. 
                                          Each team usually carries four lines 
                                          of forwards (center, left and right 
                                          wing) and three sets of defensemen.</font>

                                        <P><font size="1" face="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">Position

                                          the opponent's blue line (near the boards), 
                                          to keep the puck from escaping the opponents' 
                                          end zone.</font>

                                          goaltenders</font>
