              <img border="0" src="../images/begwindsurfing.gif" width="310" height="28"><BR><font face="Verdana,arial" size="2">Wind Surfing is another sport, which can be enjoyed by any age, race and ability.</font>
              <p><img border="0" src="../images/kit.gif" width="120" height="28"><BR><font face="Verdana,arial" size="2">The basic kit for windsurfing consists of a board and rig, buoyancy aid, windsurfer boots and a wetsuit (optional).</font></p>
              <p><img border="0" src="../images/start.gif" width="110" height="28"><BR><font face="Verdana,arial" size="2">You should never ever go windsurfing by yourself because if you get into difficulty there is no one can help you.<br>
              Once you have mastered getting on to the board without falling off it, then you are ready to go.<br>
              Really if you want to learn you should find your local club which can also provide all your equipment for you. Also there will be someone qualified there to help you learn the sport easily and safely.</font></p>
              <p><img border="0" src="../images/basic.gif" width="110" height="28"><BR><font face="Verdana,arial" size="2">The basics in windsurfing are as follows: -</font></p>
              <ol style="color: #000000; font-family: Verdana, arial; font-size: 10pt; font-weight: bold">
                <li>Being able to get on and off the board without falling off.</li>
                <li>Going in a straight line and going backwards safely.</li>
                <li>Turning while on the move.</li>
                <li>Launching the board safely.</li>
              </ol>
              <p><img border="0" src="../images/tricks.gif" width="200" height="28"><BR><font face="Verdana,arial" size="2">As you advance in windsurfing and you gain more confidence you will learn a few tricks, from simple jumps to cartwheels!<br>
              When you enter competition in windsurfing you are marked on how well you can do tricks..</font>
</p>
