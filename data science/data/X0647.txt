    <td width="58%"><strong><font face="Arial" color="#004000"><small>Race 1 - Class 5 Hcp
    2264m </small></font></strong><p><font face="Arial" color="#004000"><small>On settling
    Dane's Reward led from Paringa then Gianni followed by Zoff's Lad and Maranello with Rose
    of Siena three wide. Little changed until the 1000m where Paringa and Rose of Siena joined
    Dane's Reward in front from He's The Saint moving up but wide around Maranello. Around the
    home turn Paringa hit the front from Rose of Siena with Maranello next inside He's A Saint
    and Entropy looking for a run near the rails. By the 200m Paringa and Rose of Siena were
    in front from Maranello starting a run with Zoff's Lad starting to run out wide and
    Entropy still looking for a way clear. Maranello hit the front inside the final 100m as
    Entropy followed him past the tiring frontrunners with Zoff's Lad making ground next.
    Entropy tried to bridge the gap but Maranello was too strong winning by 1 1/4 lengths from
    Entropy with Zoff's Lad third. </small></font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" color="#004000"><small>Maranello is a 3yo bay colt by Barathea from
    Balm In Gilead and was ridden by Jason Holder. He is trained by Dean Lawson for JM
    Cappellin, G Saro, J Laino, J Hearn, M Beswick, J Del Mastro, G Cappellin, SL Lawson, PR
    Darby and J Toussas. </small><br>
    </font></td>
  </tr>

    <td width="58%"><strong><font face="Arial" color="#004000"><small>Race 2 - Flying 1000m </small></font></strong><p><font face="Arial" color="#004000"><small>Only a small field of four began in the race after two
    raceday scratchings. Soon after the start Mint of Gold shaded Catango in front of Sir
    Archer and Irish Jantee. Little changed until they turned for home where the field fanned
    to make a line of four but the lead pair soon started to draw clear. By the 200m Catango
    was putting in her challenge to Mint of Gold as they drew well clear of Irish Jantee then
    Sir Archer. Though expected to dominate Mint of Gold started to struggle to hold the lead.
    In the final 50 Catango edged ahead to hit the post 3/4 length in front with Irish Jantee
    third. </small></font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" color="#004000"><small>Catango is a 4yo brown mare by Catrail from
    Dancer's Choice and was ridden by Luke Nolen. She is trained by Grant Dalziel for GV &amp;
    MP Smith, CW House, PG Doran, D McGrath, C Oxlade, C Curran and MJ Oliphant. </small><br>

    <td width="58%"><strong><font face="Arial" color="#004000"><small>Race 3 - 2yo Hcp 1460m </small></font></strong><p><font face="Arial" color="#004000"><small>Prince D'Or went straight to the front from Little
    Miss Cindy then You Little Beaut followed by Lady Ace then Sunturwasi and Work and Play.
    Little changed until they turned for home where Prince D'Or led You Little Beaut then
    Little Miss Cindy followed by Sunturwasi as Work and Play make ground along the rail. With
    200m to go Prince D'Or was in front as Sunturwasi challenged on the outside of You Little
    Beaut as Work and Play found a gap along the rails. By the 100m Work and Play hit the
    front from Sunturwasi then Prince D'Or from You Little Beaut. In the run to the line Work
    and Play came away to win by 2 1/2 lengths from Sunturwasi with Prince D'Or holding third.
    </small></font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" color="#004000"><small>Work and Play is a grey 2yo gelding by

    <td width="58%"><strong><font face="Arial" color="#004000"><small>Race 4 - Grand National
    Hurdle 3474m </small></font></strong><p><font face="Arial" color="#004000"><small>After
    the field settled into stride Currinsky led from Just Dynamite as the pair raced well
    clear of Real Zeal then Noble Jon from Voodoo Sunset and Apollo's Ghost. In the straight
    the first time Currinsky and Just Dynamite led Paisano then Real Zeal followed by Voodoo
    Sunset, Lennin and Noble Jon. By the 1600m Noble Jon had moved to third around Paisano
    then Voodoo Sunset with Apollo's Ghost moving up around them. Passing the 1200m Noble Jon
    hit the front and started to pull away from Currinsky as Voodoo Sunset moved past Just
    Dynamite from Paisano. Noble Jon raced 10 lengths clear past the 800m from Voodoo Sunset
    and Currinsky as Apollo's Ghost started to sprint. Over the third last fence at the 700m
    the field had started to close on the leader Noble Jon but Voodoo Sunset dipped on landing
    in second from Apollo's Ghost then Real Zeal moving up out wider from Paisano and
    Currinsky as Lenin was pulled out of the event. The second last fence saw Noble Jon still
    clear of Apollo's Ghost then Voodoo Sunset and Real Zeal. Despite starting to drift out
    from the last fence Noble Jon was too strong and powered home to win by a length from
    Apollo's Ghost with Real Zeal a long neck away third with a gap to Voodoo Sunset in
    fourth. </small></font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" color="#004000"><small>Noble Jon&nbsp; (below) is a 7yo chestnut
    gelding by Star Way from Bon Cher and was ridden by Willy Harnett. He is trained by
    accomplished showjumping rider David Brideoake for himself, P Green, M Biasin, T Clarke
    and D Couzner. </small><br>
    </font></td>
