	<img src="img/paymentservices.gif" width="360" height="24"  alt="payment and international trade services" border="0"><P>
Businesses involved in importing and exporting operate against a very complicated backdrop of different trading conventions, foreign currencies, exchange rate fluctuations and varying paperwork requirements.<P>
Our experienced International Services team understands the complexities and pressures of this environment and provides hands-on support to minimise the time-consuming aspects of financial issues associated with international trade.<P>
Bank of Scotland provides a wide range of domestic and international payment services to meet the needs of corporate customers. Domestic payments are handled largely through BACS and CHAPS, whilst international payments are processed through an extensive correspondent network or the specialised euro clearing systems.<P>
Our range of international services also includes foreign currency accounts, travel services and more specialised services available to customers who export/import goods.<P>
Our core services include:<P>
