<p><IMG align="left" SRC="/Images/Event_Logos/RyderCup03.gif" WIDTH="130" HEIGHT="122" BORDER="0" ALT="35th Ryder Cup Matches logo">The 2004 U.S. Ryder Cup Team is chosen on the basis of points compiled by The PGA of America, which players accumulate from Jan. 3, 2002, through the 86th PGA Championship at Whistling Straits in Kohler, Wis., which concludes Aug. 15, 2004. The top-10 finishers on the points list qualify for the 12-man team, and U.S. Captain (selected by Dec. 2002) will select the two final players to complete the team.  

<p>The <a href="/tour/pgaofamerica/rydercup04/hospitality/index.html">35th Ryder Cup Matches</a> will be held Sept., 2004, at Oakland Hills Country Club in Bloomfield Hills, Mich.
