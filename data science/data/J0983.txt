	<H6>How to Get There</H6>
	<P><B>From the M5:</B>
	<BR>Exit the M5 at Crossroads (Camden Valley Way).
	<BR>Turn right at the lights onto Camden Valley Way.
	<BR>Follow this road for approximately 15 minutes.
	<BR>Turn right at Cobbity Road (there is a sign here to Oran Park).
	<BR>The Oran Park main gate is on the right just before the junction with The Northern Road.</P>
