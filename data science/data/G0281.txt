            color="#FFFFFF"><strong>&nbsp; <br>
            Amalthea is a satellite of the planet Jupiter.  It was discovered on 09/09/1892 by Edward Emerson Barnard.<br>
            <br>
            Compared with the satellites of other planets of the solar system, Amalthea is a small Moon with a diameter of 262(X 146 X 134) km and a mass of 7.17E+18 kg.<br>
            <br>
            Amalthea is an average distance of 181300 km from Jupiter and completes its rotation of Jupiter in 0.50 days.<br>
