      <p><span class="prodbodorange">TAX</span><br>
        <span class="b2"> Interest will be payable net of income tax at the rate 
        required by law. Tax may be reclaimed from the Inland Revenue where the 
        amount deducted exceeds your liability to tax (if any). For individuals 
        whose income falls within the lower or basic rate bands, the tax deducted 
        will match their liability to tax on the interest and they will have no 
        more tax to pay on it. If you are liable to a higher rate of income tax 
        you will have to pay additional tax on the interest to cover the difference 
        between the tax deducted and the higher rate of tax due. If you are eligible 
        to receive your interest without tax deducted, upon receipt of a completed 
        Inland Revenue Registration Form (R85), we will pay your interest gross.</span></p>
      <p><span class="prodbodorange">Statement</span><br>
        <span class="b2">Interest is payable on 31 January each year. During the 
        month of February the Society will provide each investor with a statement 
        detailing the amount of interest you have received and the interest rates 
        applicable during the period.</span></p>
      <p><span class="b2">AER stands for Annual Equivalent Rate and Illustrates 
        what the interest rate would be if interest would be paid and added each 
        year.</span></p>
