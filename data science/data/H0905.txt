      <p><font face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">Yeast could be confused 
        with small protozoans like E. nana or with Cryptosporidium or Cyclospora 
        oocysts in wet preparation. </font></p>
      <p><font face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">The coccidian should 
        always be confirmed with the modified-Kinyoun acid-fast stain. In the 
        acid-fast stain, the oocysts of Cryptosporidium and Cyclospora species 
        stain pink to red. Yeasts are not acid fast and stain green. </font></p>
