<p>
All lists apart from accu-info are for members only. However, we're
fairly relaxed about accu-general.
<br>

<em>
Moderated
</em>
 means that every message sent to the list is re-directed
to a moderator for approval before being allowed to appear on the list.
<br>

<em>
Open
</em>
 lists can be joined by anyone, but the list owner gets a
notification. Requests to join 
<em>
Closed
</em>
 lists are redirected to
the list owner for approval.  

<p>
<strong>
IMPORTANT
</strong>
 To subscribe or unsubscribe from any list,
<strong>
do not
</strong>
 send messages to any of the main mailing list
addresses as shown above.  Requests must be sent to the special request
address, e.g.: to join accu-hosts, send your request to
accu-hosts-request
(at)
accu.org, 
<strong>
not
</strong>

accu-hosts
(at)
accu.org.
</p>
