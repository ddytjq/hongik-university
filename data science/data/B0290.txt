          (c) if the services or facilities on your account are changed;<br>
          (d) to take account of any new legislation, or to comply with an appropriate 
          code of practice or the decision of any court, ombudsman, regulator 
          or similar body;<br>
          (e) to enable us to improve our efficiency;<br>
          (f) because of changes in technology or general commercial practice;<br>
          (g) in response to general market conditions;<br>
          (h) in response to the actions of our competitors.</font></p>
