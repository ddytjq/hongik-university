				<td class=body>The reason the Government introduced the Individual Savings Account (ISA) in 1999 is the same reason Scottish Friendly was established almost 150 years ago - to make saving and investing a possibility for everyone.<P>
You don't need to be an investment expert and you don't need to invest a large lump sum to take out an ISA. Scottish Friendly's straightforward ISA choice is designed to make your money grow over the medium to long term (i.e. over at least 5 years). And with our history and success in tax exempt investments, who better to manage your ISA.
<P>
