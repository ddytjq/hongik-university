<B>Blackfish (Tautog)</B> <BR ALIGN=Center>
<I>[</I><I>Tautoga onitis]</I>
<P ALIGN=Left>
&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;The Blackfish is a large Atlantic Wrasse, usually found
around rocks and around wrecks and pilings. Females and young fish are a
brownish grey with dark blotches. Adult males are darker with much less
blotching. These fish are found all around Long Island on both The North
and South Shores usually in water that is 55 degrees or colder. Blackfish
feed on crustations, crabs and mussels and its average weight is about 3
lbs. This species is stressed due to overfishing for commercial reasons.
When fishing add a small piece of sandworm to your bait. This has great results!
