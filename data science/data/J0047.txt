        <br>
        The 1st Annual Chicagoland Showdown for Mid-American cars will top the
        card<br>
        of racing this Friday night at the Grundy County Speedway, in Morris,<br>
        Illinois.<br>
        <br>
        The first half of this specail event presented by Truckers Oil Pro's and<br>
        Romar Transportation Systems Inc was held at the Illiana Motor Speedway
        on<br>
        Sunday afternoon, May 26th.<br>
        <br>
        This second half of the event comes to Grundy County Speedway, with a 50
        lap<br>
        main event for the Mid-Am division, with $1000 to win plus heat race and<br>
        qualifying money on the line.<br>
        <br>
        The best of the best will be on hand for this big dollar event, with<br>
        twenty-four cars&nbsp; slotted for the feature race, the biggest show
        for this<br>
        division of the season.<br>
        <br>
        A full card of late model and street stock racing is also scheduled.
        with<br>
        time trials at 6:45 and the first race slated for an 8 p.m. start.<br>
        <br>
        The Grundy County Speedway is located just north of i-80, at the
        Fairgrounds,<br>
        in Morris, Illinois.<br>

        Model drivers in the Midwest.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>Also

        at the Wynn’s Sportsman Nationals held at the Illinois State
        Fairgrounds in Springfield in September.</p>

        <h1 style="margin-left:.5in;text-indent:.5in">DUQUOIN MILE HOSTS FIRST
        CAR RACE UNDER LIGHTS</h1>
        <p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left:1.0in">&nbsp;<o:p>
        </o:p>
        </p>
        <p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left:1.0in">The DuQuoin Magic Mile
        will host the UMP Spring Nationals Sunday, May 19.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp;
        </span>This will be the first time the UMP cars have raced under the new
        lights on the DuQuoin Mile.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>The
        UMP late models will be competing for a top prize of $12,000 in their
        30-lap feature event.</p>
        <p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left:1.0in">&nbsp;<o:p>
        </o:p>
        </p>
        <p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left:1.0in">Ken Schrader, current
        driver of the M &amp; M’s Pontiac on NASCAR’s Winston Cup tour,
        returns to the one-mile dirt track driving his own Federated Auto Parts
        Late Model.</p>
        <p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left:1.0in">&nbsp;<o:p>
        </o:p>
        </p>
        <p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left:1.0in">Over 100 race cars are
        expected to dot the pit area, headlined by four-time late model division
        winner of Springfield’s Fall Nationals Billy Moyer, of Batesville,
        Arkansas.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>Also expected to
        compete are Shannon Babb of Moweaqua, IL, Washington, Missouri’s Ed
        Dixon, Mt. Vernon, Illinois’ Jimmy Burwell, Terry English from
        Kentucky, Kevin Claycomb from Indiana, and USAC standout Jack Hewitt, as
        well as a legion of local and national stars.</p>
        <p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left:1.0in">&nbsp;<o:p>
        </o:p>
        </p>
        <p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left:1.0in">Racing action will
        include UMP Late Models and Modifieds, the modified division will have a
        20 lap feature.</p>
        <p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left:1.0in">&nbsp;<o:p>
        </o:p>
        </p>
        <p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left:1.0in">The last time Late Models
        raced at the famous one mile dirt oval Billy Moyer took home the
        checkers.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp;&nbsp; </span>Fans will
        also be treated to many improvements including a new concrete wall.</p>
        <p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left:1.0in">&nbsp;<o:p>
        </o:p>
        </p>
        <p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left:1.0in">Gates will open at 12:00
        p.m., Late Model qualifying will be at 5:00 p.m., and preliminary races
        begin at 6:00 p.m.</p>
