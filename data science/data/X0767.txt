                                                <TD WIDTH="100%" BGCOLOR="#FFCC00"><P><B><FONT SIZE="-1" FACE="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">How much do these services cost?</FONT></B></TD>
                                            </TR>
                                            <TR>
                                                
                                      <TD WIDTH="100%" BGCOLOR="#FFFFCC" height="49"> 
                                        <P><font size="1" face="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">These 
                                          services are provided to all hockey 
                                          fans for free. When Alia's Hockey Card 
                                          Forum was first started in 1996, it 
                                          was free to anyone who wished to post 
                                          cards. The Phreak has always maintained 
                                          that there should be a free place to 
                                          post cards for sale or trade, so, this 
                                          services remains free even though we 
                                          have upgraded how it works and what 
                                          capabilities it has, and even its name!</font>
                                      </TD>
                                            </TR>
                                            <TR>
                                                <TD WIDTH="100%" BGCOLOR="#FFCC00"><P><B><FONT SIZE="-1" FACE="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">How do I register?</FONT></B></TD>
                                            </TR>
                                            <TR>
                                                
                                      <TD WIDTH="100%" BGCOLOR="#FFFFCC" height="72"> 
                                        <P><font size="1" face="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">To 
                                          register for our card and memorabilia 
                                          exchanges, you merely have to click 
                                          the <B>Register</B> button located on 
                                          the initial login screen. If you register 
                                          for the Card Exchange and later decide 
                                          to post with the Memorabilia Exchange 
                                          as well, you do not need to register 
                                          again. Both systems function with the 
                                          same set of users.</font><FONT SIZE="-1" FACE="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica"> 
                                          </FONT>
                                      </TD>
                                            </TR>
                                            <TR>
                                                <TD WIDTH="100%" BGCOLOR="#FFCC00"><P><B><FONT SIZE="-1" FACE="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">I don't feel comfortable giving out my address, but I still want to register. What do I do?</FONT></B></TD>
                                            </TR>
                                            <TR>
                                                
                                      <TD WIDTH="100%" BGCOLOR="#FFFFCC" height="108"> 
                                        <P><font size="1" face="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">You 
                                          do not need to provide us with your 
                                          address if it makes you uncomfortable, 
                                          but you do need to select a username 
                                          and give us your email address. If you 
                                          do not provide your email address, we 
                                          cannot notify you when people are interested 
                                          in the items you have posted. Our script, 
                                          however, requires that all fields are 
                                          filled out, so if you do not wish to 
                                          give us you address, please type <B>N/A</B> 
                                          in those fields. We respect your privacy, 
                                          and none of the information you give 
                                          us will be disclosed to anyone. We do 
                                          like to have this information available 
                                          because we are considering implementing 
                                          random monthly drawings for various 
                                          prizes. Also, we want to keep mischief 
                                          makers from registering with our Exchanges.</font><FONT SIZE="-1" FACE="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica"> 
                                          </FONT>
                                      </TD>
                                            </TR>
                                            <TR>
                                                <TD WIDTH="100%" BGCOLOR="#FFCC00"><P><B><FONT SIZE="-1" FACE="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">How do I know if someone is interested in my posted items?</FONT></B></TD>
                                            </TR>
                                            <TR>
                                                
                                      <TD WIDTH="100%" BGCOLOR="#FFFFCC" HEIGHT=106> 
                                        <P><font size="1" face="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">We 
                                          have a system whereby interested parties 
                                          can fill out a form making an offer 
                                          on any items they are interested in, 
                                          and their interest is directly emailed 
                                          to the party who owns the item. The 
                                          person will not know your email address, 
                                          but you will recieve an email with their 
                                          username, email address and any offer 
                                          or comments that they have about the 
                                          item. You are then free to email them 
                                          back at your convenience. Please be 
                                          sure and clear any sold or traded items 
                                          from our system so that users do not 
                                          continue to contact you about them.</font>
                                      </TD>
                                            </TR>
                                            <TR>
                                                <TD WIDTH="100%" BGCOLOR="#FFCC00"><P><B><FONT SIZE="-1" FACE="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">Do my items get automatically deleted from the database after a certain amount of time?</FONT></B></TD>
                                            </TR>
                                            <TR>
                                                
                                      <TD WIDTH="100%" BGCOLOR="#FFFFCC" height="85"> 
                                        <P><font size="1" face="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">At 
                                          this time, there is no limit of how 
                                          long your items will stay posted. They 
                                          will stay in our database until you 
                                          remove them. This is why we urge all 
                                          users to delete items that they no longer 
                                          have in their inventory. If you have 
                                          sold your items or traded them, please 
                                          remove them from your listing. We would 
                                          like our database to remain fairly uncluttered 
                                          so that users can access postings at 
                                          optimal speed and with little pain as 
                                          possible. If we find that there are 
                                          just too many cards posted, we might 
                                          implement a 100 day limit - unless the 
                                          card is modified.</font>
                                      </TD>
                                            </TR>
                                            <TR>
                                                <TD WIDTH="100%" BGCOLOR="#FFCC00"><P><B><FONT SIZE="-1" FACE="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">Does The Hockey Phreak make any guarantee that I won't get gyped?</FONT></B></TD>
                                            </TR>
                                            <TR>
                                                
                                      <TD WIDTH="100%" BGCOLOR="#FFFFCC" HEIGHT=113> 
                                        <P><font size="1" face="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">We 
                                          are really sorry, but we cannot know 
                                          for certain who is honest and who is 
                                          not. The Phreak, personally, has never 
                                          been stiffed by a collector from the 
                                          internet, but it does happen. Be very 
                                          careful about your dealings online, 
                                          and do not make any transactions that 
                                          make you feel uncomfortable. If we hear 
                                          of any harrassing emails, or any problematic 
                                          users, we will do our best to correct 
                                          the situation by keeping them from making 
                                          trouble, but The Hockey Phreak is not 
                                          responsible and will in no way be held 
                                          liable for any damages directly or indirectly 
                                          resulting from the use of these services. 
                                          So, be careful out there, and insure 
                                          your precious items.</font>
                                      </TD>
                                            </TR>
                                            <TR>
                                                <TD WIDTH="100%" BGCOLOR="#FFCC00"><P><B><FONT SIZE="-1" FACE="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">How do I post my items?</FONT></B></TD>
                                            </TR>
                                            <TR>
                                                
                                      <TD WIDTH="100%" BGCOLOR="#FFFFCC" height="65"> 
                                        <P><font size="1" face="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">First 
                                          you must register a username and password. 
                                          Then, you login to the system. Once 
                                          there, press the <B> Add an Item</B> 
                                          button. You then enter the pertinent 
                                          information for the item, and any comments 
                                          you have and click submit. Your item 
                                          will now appear in the database.</font>
                                      </TD>
                                            </TR>
                                            <TR>
                                                <TD WIDTH="100%" BGCOLOR="#FFCC00"><P><B><FONT SIZE="-1" FACE="Verdana,Tahoma,Arial,Helvetica">How do I modify items I have already posted?</FONT></B></TD>
                                            </TR>
