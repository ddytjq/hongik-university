                                    <h1><font color="#003399" size="5" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">FLOODING
                                    - A MESSAGE FROM THE BRITISH INSURANCE
                                    BROKERS' ASSOCIATION</font><o:p>
                                    </o:p>
                                    </h1>
                                    <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family:Arial">Following
                                    the weekend of extensive flooding, the
                                    British Insurance </span><span style="font-family:Arial">Brokers'
                                    Association (BIBA) has this morning issued
                                    the following statement and advice to those
                                    hit.</span></p>
                                    <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family:Arial">Even
                                    prior to this weekend, it is thought that
                                    flood damage to homes and </span><span style="font-family:Arial">businesses
                                    has cost insurers over £500 million during
                                    the last three </span><span style="font-family:Arial"><span style="mso-spacerun:
yes">&nbsp;</span>years. The flooding in the UK over the past weekend and that
                                    which is </span><span style="font-family:Arial">expected
                                    still to come,<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">
                                    </span>will add considerably to this figure
                                    and is bound </span><span style="font-family:Arial">to
                                    have a knock on effect on household
                                    insurance premiums in coming years.<o:p>
                                    </o:p>
                                    </span></p>
                                    <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family:Arial">BIBA
                                    is advising people caught in the recent
                                    flooding to take note of the following
                                    advice before acting and </span><span style="font-family:Arial">sanctioning
                                    repair work: <o:p>
                                    </o:p>
                                    </span></p>
                                    <p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-top:4.0pt;margin-right:0in;margin-bottom:0in;
margin-left:17.85pt;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-indent:-17.85pt;mso-list:l0 level1 lfo1;
tab-stops:list .25in"><span style="font-size:11.0pt;
mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Wingdings;color:red">§<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</span></span><span style="font-family:Arial">Temporary
                                    emergency repairs at a reasonable cost
                                    should be paid by the insurers, but make
                                    sure you keep all of your receipts.</span></p>
                                    <p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-top:4.0pt;margin-right:0in;margin-bottom:0in;
margin-left:17.85pt;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-indent:-17.85pt;mso-list:l0 level1 lfo1;
tab-stops:list .25in"><span style="font-size:11.0pt;
mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Wingdings;color:red">§<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    </span></span><span style="font-family:Arial">As
                                    long as you have an up-to-date household
                                    buildings and contents policy, you should be
                                    covered against damage by flood.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">
                                    </span>Many business policies also cover
                                    damage by flood.&nbsp;</span></p>
                                    <p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-top:4.0pt;margin-right:0in;margin-bottom:0in;
margin-left:17.85pt;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-indent:-17.85pt;mso-list:l0 level1 lfo1;
tab-stops:list .25in"><span style="font-size:11.0pt;
mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Wingdings;color:red">§<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    </span></span><span style="font-family:Arial">If
                                    your car has suffered as a result of the
                                    flooding, this should be covered by a
                                    comprehensive motor policy.&nbsp;</span></p>
                                    <p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-top:4.0pt;margin-right:0in;margin-bottom:0in;
margin-left:17.85pt;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-indent:-17.85pt;mso-list:l0 level1 lfo1;
tab-stops:list .25in"><span style="font-size:11.0pt;
mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Wingdings;color:red">§<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</span></span><span style="font-family:Arial">If
                                    your home has been made uninhabitable, the
                                    reasonable cost of<span style="mso-spacerun:
yes">&nbsp;</span>alternative accommodation will usually be met up to stated
                                    limits by your<span style="font-family: Arial">
                                    </span><span style="mso-spacerun:
yes">&nbsp;</span>insurer. </span><span style="font-size:11.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;
font-family:Arial;mso-bidi-font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;">
                                    </o:p>
                                    </span></p>
                                    <p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-top:4.0pt;margin-right:0in;margin-bottom:0in;
margin-left:17.85pt;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-indent:-17.85pt;mso-list:l0 level1 lfo1;
tab-stops:list .25in"><span style="font-size:11.0pt;
mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Wingdings;color:red">§<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
                                    </span></span><span style="font-family:Arial">You
                                    should always try to salvage your belongings
                                    but without putting <span style="mso-spacerun:
yes">&nbsp;</span>yourself at personal risk.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp;&nbsp;</span></span></p>
                                    <p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-top:4.0pt;margin-right:0in;margin-bottom:0in;
margin-left:17.85pt;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-indent:-17.85pt;mso-list:l0 level1 lfo1;
tab-stops:list .25in"><span style="font-size:11.0pt;
mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Wingdings;color:red">§<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</span></span><span style="font-family:Arial">Contact
                                    your insurance company, broker or agent as
                                    soon as possible </span><span style="font-family:Arial">for
                                    advice on how to progress your claim.&nbsp;</span><span style="font-family:Arial"><span style="mso-tab-count:1">&nbsp;&nbsp;</span></span></p>
                                    <p class="MsoNormal" style="mso-list: l0 level1 lfo1; tab-stops: list .25in"><span style="font-family:Arial">Mike
                                    Williams, BIBA Chief Executive said,
                                    &quot;Each year&nbsp; millions of people <span style="mso-spacerun:
yes">&nbsp;</span>are potentially at risk from flooding and each year many fall
                                    victim to <o:p>
                                    </o:p>
                                    swelling rivers or full blown flooding.
                                    There is nothing any of us can do <span style="mso-spacerun:
yes">&nbsp;</span>about the forces of nature but we can all act to reduce the
                                    potential<br>
                                    </span><span style="font-family:Arial">damage.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp;
                                    </span>It is worrying to think that some
                                    people living in flood-prone areas continue
                                    to live without adequate insurance
                                    protection against </span><span style="font-family:Arial">damage,
                                    not only to their buildings but to their
                                    contents too.<o:p>
                                    </o:p>
                                    </span></p>
                                    <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family:Arial">'Anyone
                                    living in a flood-prone area should contact
                                    their local broker who will have specialist
                                    knowledge about insurance products that may
                                    specialise in </span><span style="font-family:Arial">the
                                    exact form of protection they require.&quot;<o:p>
                                    </o:p>
                                    </span></p>
                                    <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family:Arial">There
                                    is a possibility of further flooding in the
                                    UK and BIBA is advising </span><span style="font-family:Arial">those
                                    in flood-prone areas to reduce the risk of
                                    flood damage by:&nbsp;</span></p>
                                    <p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-top:4.0pt;margin-right:0in;margin-bottom:0in;
margin-left:17.85pt;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-indent:-17.85pt;mso-list:l0 level1 lfo1;
tab-stops:list .25in"><span style="font-size:11.0pt;
mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Wingdings;color:red">§<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</span></span><span style="font-family:Arial">contacting
                                    your insurance company as soon as possible,
                                    as they can often<span style="mso-spacerun:
yes">&nbsp;</span>offer helpful advice in this situation to help you to reduce
                                    the <span style="mso-spacerun:
yes">&nbsp;</span>possibility of the damage getting any worse;&nbsp;</span></p>
                                    <p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-top:4.0pt;margin-right:0in;margin-bottom:0in;
margin-left:17.85pt;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-indent:-17.85pt;mso-list:l0 level1 lfo1;
tab-stops:list .25in"><span style="font-size:11.0pt;
mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Wingdings;color:red">§<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</span></span><span style="font-family:Arial">moving
                                    as much as possible upstairs as soon as you
                                    hear the flood alert;</span></p>
                                    <p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-top:4.0pt;margin-right:0in;margin-bottom:0in;
margin-left:17.85pt;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-indent:-17.85pt;mso-list:l0 level1 lfo1;
tab-stops:list .25in"><span style="font-size:11.0pt;
mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Wingdings;color:red">§<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</span></span><span style="font-family:Arial">remembering
                                    that doors and windows may be swollen tight
                                    and floors<span style="mso-spacerun:
yes">&nbsp;</span>may bulge, therefore making buildings unsafe following a
                                    flood;</span></p>
                                    <p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-top:4.0pt;margin-right:0in;margin-bottom:0in;
margin-left:17.85pt;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-indent:-17.85pt;mso-list:l0 level1 lfo1;
tab-stops:list .25in"><span style="font-size:11.0pt;
mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Wingdings;color:red">§<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</span></span><span style="font-family:Arial">electricity
                                    and gas should not be used and mains
                                    drinking water should not be consumed as it
                                    may have become contaminated;</span><span style="font-size:11.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;
font-family:Arial;mso-bidi-font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;"></o:p>
                                    </span></p>
                                    <p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-top:4.0pt;margin-right:0in;margin-bottom:0in;
margin-left:17.85pt;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-indent:-17.85pt;mso-list:l0 level1 lfo1;
tab-stops:list .25in"><span style="font-size:11.0pt;
mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Wingdings;color:red">§<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</span></span><span style="font-family:Arial">leaving
                                    windows, doors and built-in cupboards open
                                    and keeping rooms heated where
                                    possible;&nbsp;</span></p>
                                    <p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-top:4.0pt;margin-right:0in;margin-bottom:0in;
margin-left:17.85pt;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-indent:-17.85pt;mso-list:l0 level1 lfo1;
tab-stops:list .25in"><span style="font-size:11.0pt;
mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Wingdings;color:red">§<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</span></span><span style="font-family:Arial">pulling
                                    furniture away from walls and oiling all
                                    hinges and locks to prevent them
                                    corroding;&nbsp;</span></p>
                                    <p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-top:4.0pt;margin-right:0in;margin-bottom:0in;
margin-left:17.85pt;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-indent:-17.85pt;mso-list:l0 level1 lfo1;
tab-stops:list .25in"><span style="font-size:11.0pt;
mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Wingdings;color:red">§<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</span></span><span style="font-family:Arial">drying
                                    out a building can take months so don't be
                                    in a hurry to <span style="mso-spacerun:
yes">&nbsp;</span>redecorate;<span style="mso-spacerun: yes"> </span></span><span style="font-size:11.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;
font-family:Arial;mso-bidi-font-family:&quot;Times New Roman&quot;"></o:p>
                                    </span></p>
                                    <p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-top:4.0pt;margin-right:0in;margin-bottom:0in;
margin-left:17.85pt;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-indent:-17.85pt;mso-list:l0 level1 lfo1;
tab-stops:list .25in"><span style="font-size:11.0pt;
mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Wingdings;color:red">§<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</span></span><span style="font-family:Arial">treating
                                    walls with an anti-mould solution before
                                    decorating;<span style="mso-spacerun:
yes">&nbsp;</span><span style="mso-tab-count:1">&nbsp;</span></span></p>
                                    <p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-top:4.0pt;margin-right:0in;margin-bottom:0in;
margin-left:17.85pt;margin-bottom:.0001pt;text-indent:-17.85pt;mso-list:l0 level1 lfo1;
tab-stops:list .25in"><span style="font-size:11.0pt;
mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Wingdings;color:red">§<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</span></span><span style="font-family:Arial">make
                                    sure you keep a copy of important telephone
                                    numbers and policy <span style="mso-spacerun:
yes">&nbsp;</span>documents in an easily accessible place in case you require
