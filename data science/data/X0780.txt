trailbuilding are the 3-1/2 lb. Fiberglass handled Pulaski, 7" and 10"

</UL>

<P> These are the tools that have been awarded for years in the RockShox/IMBA
Trailwork Tool Grants. Both tools feature fiberglass handles and are specifically
designed for heavy-duty trailwork. </P>

<P> Order directly from Zac Tools at 1-800-454-7868 and speak with Larry or
Susan. Alternatively, you can email them at <A HREF="mailto:zactools@aol.com">zactools@aol.com</A>. When ordering, ask
for the IMBA member discount and have your IMBA member number handy. </P>

<P>Address: 190 Etowah Industrial Ct.,Canton, GA 30114<BR>
Tel: 800-241-6401<BR>

Web: <A HREF="http://www.benmeadows.com">www.benmeadows.com</A><BR>

<P>If you work outdoors Ben Meadows sells equipment you use. From a
trailbuilder's perspective, their selection of flagging tap, pin flags,
clinometers, and hand tools is hard to beat.  Two favorites of ours are
the:<BR> 

<UL><LI><B>Suunto Self Damping Clinometers</B> - #102200 Suunto PM5/360PC - Percent and Degrees<BR>
For fast, accurate height, slope, and vertical angle measurements. Graduated 0
to &#177;90&deg;, 0 to &nbsp;&#177;150%. Accuracy to 1&deg; or 1%. Cosines to

Rust-resistant aluminum. Skylon leather-like carrying case with belt loop, snap
closure.

<LI><B>Adze Hoe</B> - #170602 Wardwood
</ul>

<P> Though they don't offer an IMBA discount, Ben Meadows provides excellent
customer service and pricing on a number of great products from lovely Suunto
brand Clinometers to your everyday pin flag. </P>

<P>Tel: 207-947-2723<BR>
	Fax: 207-945-6050<BR>

<P>Trail Services provides quality and hard to find products for
trailbuilding, such as Griphoist winches, slings, blocks, and stone
working tools.

<H3>Digital Levels</H3>

<P>Digital levels are a useful tool for identifying trail features like
tread outslope. Professional trailbuilders use the Smart Tool Digital
Level, which measures about 5"x2"x2" making it easy to carry on the
trail. While a little tough to find, check a high-end hardware store to
see if they have on one hand or are able to do a special order. They
usually run about $120. </p>
