<H1>Links - Internet</H1>
<P><A href="http://www.activeworlds.com/" target="_top" valign="center">Activeworlds</A>
<BR><I>Activeworlds.com, Inc. provides software products and on-line services that permit users to enter, move about and interact with others in a computer-generated, three-dimensional virtual environment using the Internet.</I></P>
<P><A href="http://www.deja.com/usenet/" target="_top" valign="center">Deja.com's Usenet Discussion Service</A>
<BR></P>
<P><A href="http://www.flatland.com/" target="_top" valign="center">Flatland</A>
<BR><I>Flatland develops innovative rich media products for the Web, including the Rover rich media player, 3DML and the Spotnik builder.</I></P>
<P><A href="http://www.icq.com/" target="_top" valign="center">ICQ</A>
<BR><I>ICQ is a utility that allow to know when choosen ICQ users (by giving ICQ a list of ICQ user numbers to look for) are online. You can then chat, exchange files in "real time" over the Internet. I don't have ICQ at this time... The old ICQ list is no more here. Check the vbdirectx ICQ interest group instead...</I></P>
<P><A href="http://www.faqs.org/" target="_top" valign="center">Internet FAQ Consortium</A>

<BR><I>The Requests for Comments (RFCs) form a series of notes, started in 1969, about the Internet (originally the ARPANET). [...] The RFC Editor is the publisher of the RFCs and is responsible for the final editorial review of the documents.</I></P>
<P><A href="http://searchpdf.adobe.com/" target="_top" valign="center">Search Adobe PDF Online</A>
