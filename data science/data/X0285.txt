<FONT FACE="verdana,helvetica" SIZE="-1">
Welcome to our <B>LINKS</B> page and our <B>INTERNATIONAL DIRECTORY</B> of hockey associations, federations, clubs, schools, universities, commercial organizations and individual hockey sites from around the world.<P>
If you would like to see a link to your site included in our database, send us an
<A HREF="mailto:info@fieldhockeytournament.com"><B>email</B></A> with "Links" in the subject line and give us the following information: the full URL (no logo please), the name of your hockey club/team, your city/country, the estimated number of men and ladies players in your club.<BR>
In return for your listing, we ask that you add a link to FHT on your own web site, and join those who actively participate in trying to further promote tournament participation. &nbsp;Keep in mind that by helping us, you help promote hockey, and in turn help yourself as well.  &nbsp;It does not take much, just good will and a few minutes of your time.  &nbsp;And for that, we thank you.
<BR><BR>
<P ALIGN="center">
<IMG SRC="images/new.gif"><P>
To help you find, or get in touch with a club you may wish to either join if you are moving to another city/country, or visit while traveling on business or pleasure, we have now added an entry field for telephone/fax numbers. &nbsp;So, when available, do add that information and we will include it in your listing.<BR>
For those of you already in our database, please check your listing and if you wish to update the information shown, <A HREF="mailto:info@fieldhockeytournament.com"><B>email us</B></A>.
<P ALIGN="center"><IMG SRC="images/new.gif"><P>
<B>DON'T HAVE A WEB SITE??</B> --- Not to worry!<BR>
We now also welcome entry requests from associations, clubs and schools/universities who do not have a site but do have an email address, and from those who just have a phone/fax contact point.
