<p><font face="Verdana,Geneva,Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif" size="-1">
<p>Final Year-End Points are posted on this website, we are not having an annual banquet this year, so you should be receiving your ribbons in the mail. All previous winners are asked to contact Pat Burns at patticake@numail.org  for information regarding sending your trophy on to the current winner. We thank you for your cooperation in this.</p>
<p>There are plenty of new rules in place for 2002 - be sure you have a rule book and are familiar with the new rules. In particular, you should look at the more clearly-defined rules regarding horse abuse.</p>
<p>Please be sure to answer and send in the survey in the Jan. issue of the Area III Newsletter. Thank you.</p>

<p>Updates! - The Area III Newsletter is now online! Please go <a href="/newsletters.html"><b>HERE</b></a> to view the latest Area III Newsletter. The Proposed Calendar for 2002 for Area III is also now online! Please go <a href="/02calendar.html"><b>HERE</b></a> to view.</p>
<p>The deadline for the next Area III News is April 10 at noon. Please email all articles to Martie Healy - martie@inetw.net asap. Please snail mail any photos (to arrive by April 10). Snail mail: HC 4 Box 412, Old Town, FL 32680 Thank you. We are hoping to put the newsletter on the website starting with the fall edition. Check back!</p>

<p>AHSA has changed it's name and it's URL - the URL for USA Equestrian is: <a href="http://www.equestrian.org">www.equestrian.org</a></p>
<p>This website will be updated on a monthly basis beginning January, 2001. All classifieds will be purged after 2 month. </p>
