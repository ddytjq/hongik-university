            <p>Rather than a single Maxi ISA you can hold up to 3 Mini ISAs, one
              for each of the options listed below. Each Mini ISA may be held
              with a different ISA Manager. This allows you to choose up to 3
              different ISA providers if you so wish. You can save up to a total
              of &pound;7,000 in Mini ISAs in the first tax year, subject to the
              following maximum limits for the individual options: </p>

            <p>Each tax year you will need to decide whether to put your allowance in a Maxi with a single Manager, or choose 
              up to three different Mini ISAs. You cannot invest in both a Maxi and Mini ISA in the same tax year or two Mini 
              ISAs investing in the same option (for example you cannot invest in 2 Mini Cash ISAs). By opening a Cash ISA, 
              you will be limiting the amount of tax-free savings in stocks and shares that you can make, if you do not already have 
              a mini stocks and shares or insurance ISA.</p>

Most depositors, including individuals and small firms, are covered.  The Scheme covers deposits made with offices of the bank within the European Economic Area and deposits denominated in all currencies are treated alike.
