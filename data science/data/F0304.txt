	<font size='-1'><h4>A New Window on Installation</h4>  Installing your own code or customizing others' setup routines for your environment is not fun. But Wise for Windows Installer eliminates the laborious script editing and antiquated "wizards" in development tools. This rock-solid upgrade for Windows Installer setups adds .NET support, script customizations to MSIs, MSIDiff to review differences in scripts, and several enhancements to existing features. <p> WFWI's unique design approach guides you through a taskpad view. You can jump around the installation wizard from key package files to installation parameters to supporting documents and files. For tuning others' setup routines, you simply setupcapture a before and after into your own script, or just transform another Windows Installer-based package to your own, new WFWI package.
