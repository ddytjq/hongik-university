      <h4><a href="opinion.html">New opinion from Jeremy Clayton of Calgary</a>
      </h4>
      <p align="Left">       <br>
       <font face="Bell MT">Dear Visitor,</font></p>
       
      <p><font face="Bell MT">The overinvolvement of parents is becoming more
and more a factor in youth and high school hockey, not only for the boys'
sport but for the girls'. This issue keeps resurfacing after a parent was
killed last year in an altercation with another parent after a youth hockey
practice. We have added a number of new links on this subject as well as
others.</font></p>
       
      <p><font face="Bell MT">In particular, Joe Fitzgerald of the <a href="http://www.bostonherald.com/">
     Boston Herald</a>
      has written an excellent piece on the subject. It is available as a 
PDF   file <b><a href="http://www.masshockey.com/PDF/Learned.pdf">here</a>
       </b>. (For Adobe Acrobat Reader, which is required to view a PDF file, 
  click <b><a href="http://www.adobe.com/products/acrobat/readstep2.html">
   here</a>
       </b>. Article posting thanks to <a href="mailto:massachusettshockey@yahoo.com">
     Bob Rogers</a>
      of <a href="http://www.masshockey.com/">Massachusetts Hockey.</a>
     )</font></p>
       
      <p><font face="Bell MT">For information about parents and hockey, please
   go to our <font color="#000000"><b><a href="http://www.safehockey.com/coaches.html">
     Coaches &amp; Parents</a>
       </b> page. For those of you who are return visitors, the newest links 
  are placed at the beginning of each category. As always, you can search 
for  hockey safety issues on these pages via our&nbsp;<!-- Begin FusionBot.com site search engine code --></font><b><font color="#cc0000"><a href="http://ss618.logika.net/cgi-bin/ss_sitemap?sitenbr=60602250&amp;ct=0">
     search engine<input type="hidden" name="sitenbr" value="60602250"><input type="hidden" name="ct" value="0"><!-- End FusionBot.com site search engine code --></a>
       </font></b><font color="#000000">, thanks to <b><a href="http://www.fusionbot.com/">
     FusionBot</a>
       </b>.</font></font></p>
       
      <p><font face="Bell MT">Our purpose:&nbsp;</font></p>
       
      <p><font face="Bell MT">Hockey is a great sport, perhaps one of the 
  most difficult to learn. A good athlete can walk onto a basketball court 
 or a football or soccer field and do reasonably well in a short period of 
  time. Not so with hockey. It requires putting together some very complex 
 skills  and takes years to perfect.</font></p>
       
      <p><font face="Bell MT">The passing, the skating, the shooting are all
beautiful to watch and fun to play. However, the bashing, hitting, and  fighting
are detrimental to the quality of the sport and dangerous for our young athletes.
It is for this reason this website is maintained.</font></p>
       
      <p><font face="Bell MT">As always, comments and suggestions are welcome.</font></p>
