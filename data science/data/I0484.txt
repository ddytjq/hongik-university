        <center>
          <h2><font face="Times New Roman, Times, serif">Draw</font></h2>
        </center>
        <p><font size="+2">T</font>his is the comprehensive explanation of UEFA's 
          seeding system and draw procedure.</p>
        <center>
          <h3><font face="Times New Roman, Times, serif">Country ranking</font></h3>
        </center>
        <p>Every country receives two points for every victory of its team and 
          one point for draw. Cups' qualification matches are counted half and 
          bonus points are awarded for participation in Champions' League group 
          stage and quarter-finals, semi-finals and final of all cups. The country 
          coefficient for one year is the sum of all earned points in that year 
          divided by number of participating clubs. The sum of five consecutive 
          year coefficients gives value for <b>country rank</b>. This value will 
          be used for club ranking in next season and for determination of number 
          of participating clubs in season after next.<br>
          <br>
          <a href="http://www.geocities.com/Colosseum/Arena/4125/UC200001.html" target="_blank">Country 
          ranking 1996/97 to 2000/01</a><BR>
		  <a href="http://www.geocities.com/Colosseum/Arena/4125/UC200102.html" target="_blank">Country 
          ranking 1997/98 to 2001/02</a></p>
        <center>
          <h3><font face="Times New Roman, Times, serif">Club ranking</font></h3>
        </center>
        <p>Club coefficient is the sum of half country coefficent and points earned 
          in cup by that club (including bonus points, but without points from 
          qualifications). The sum of five consecutive year coefficents gives 
          value for <b>club rank</b>. This value will be used for seeding in next 
          season.<br>
          <br>
          <a href="http://www.xs4all.nl/%7Ekassiesa/bert/uefa/trank02.html" target="_blank">Club 
          ranking for 2002/2003</a><br>
        </p>
        <center>
          <h3><font face="Times New Roman, Times, serif">Draw procedure </font></h3>
        </center>
        Clubs participating some round are always divided in two groups - seeded 
        and unseeded. Seeded group contains the half of clubs better ranked by 
        club ranking. Before that they can be divided by geographical criteria 
        in some smaller groups, but not always. If the draw takes place before 
        the previous round has ended, the pairs are seeded as club with better 
        coefficient in that pair.<br>
        <br>
        <a href="http://www.xs4all.nl/%7Ekassiesa/bert/uefa/seedcl2002.html" target="_blank">Seeding 
        in Champions' League 2002/2003</a><br>
        <a href="http://www.xs4all.nl/%7Ekassiesa/bert/uefa/seeduc2002.html" target="_blank">Seeding 
        in UEFA Cup 2002/2003</a><br>
