      <p>Interest will not be paid during a period when the account balance falls 
        below &pound;2,500.</p>
      <p></p>
	  <STRONG><a name="Account"></a><span class="redtext">Chelsea Guaranteed Bond 
        (3rd Issue)</span></STRONG><br>
      Offers you an attractive variable rate of interest which is guaranteed not 
      to fall below the Bank of England Base Rate before 1st July 2003 (see terms 
      for details).
      <table border="0" cellpadding="10" cellspacing="0" align="right" width="157">
        <tr> 
          <td width="144"><img src="../images/guaranteed_bond.jpg" width="140" height="140" alt="*"></td>
        </tr>
      </table>
      <p>Choice of annual or monthly interest - annual interest is paid on 1st 
        October. Monthly interest is also variable and pays a rate which is approximately 
        equivalent to the annual gross&#134;/AER*, if added to the account each 
        month.</p>
      <p>Interest can be added to your account (where it immediately starts earning 
        more interest), transferred to another Chelsea account or to your bank 
        account.<br>
      </p>
      <p>And for those unexpected events, the Chelsea Guaranteed Bond (3rd Issue) 
        features an immediate withdrawal facility - see <a href="#Access">Access 
        to your money when you need it</a>.<br>
      </p>
      <p></p>
	  <STRONG><a name="Howmuch"></a><span class="redtext">How much can I invest? 
        </span></STRONG><br>
      You will need a minimum of &pound;2,500 to open the account.
      <p>You can then invest up to a maximum investment of &pound;500,000, provided 
        that your total investment in this and all other Chelsea accounts does 
        not exceed &pound;1 million.<br>
      </p>
      <p></p>
	  <STRONG><a name="Access"></a><span class="redtext">Access to your money when 
        you need it</span></STRONG><br>
      Before 1st July 2003, by giving us 60 days' written notice, you can make 
        penalty-free withdrawals.
      <p>Or, you can have immediate access to your money, before 1st July 2003, 
        subject to 60 days' loss of interest on the amount withdrawn.</p>
      <p>From 2nd July 2003, you have instant, penalty-free access to your money.</p>
      <p>Up to &pound;250 in cash or &pound;5,000 by cheque may be withdrawn immediately. 
        Larger sums may be withdrawn by cheque within a few days.</p>
      <p>Withdrawals during the guarantee period may reduce the AER*.<br>
      </p>

        The net figures quoted are illustrative assuming liability at the applicable 
        tax level of 20%.</p>
