But he brushed aside such digs from the likes of Sunil Gavaskar and went about the business of getting the best out of his under-strength side in a professional and admirable manner.<br><br>The Indian-born captain will be able to call-upon his main strike bowlers Gough and Caddick as well as top batsman Graham Thorpe, whose Test contributions ended after Mohali when he went home to sort out personal problems.<br><br>The one-day squad is another combination of exciting youth and dependable experience. Paul Collingwood, Ben Hollioake and Owais Shah are three names who the selectors will be hoping can follow in the footsteps of Hoggard, Flintoff and Foster and make the most of their chances in the sub-continent.<br><br>With the England management looking to youth in their hopes of making a better show of things at the World Cup next year, it will be interesting to see how the youngsters fare in the upcoming series.</font></p><br></td>
