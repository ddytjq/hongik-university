<font class="smallprint">* The rates quoted (correct at &nbsp;14 March 2002
        and subject to variation) apply as follows for non chequebook accounts:
        <b>4.1%</b> AER for credit balances over &pound;1       but below &pound;50,000,  &nbsp;
    		<b>4.0%</b>  AER for credit balances of &pound;50,000  but below &pound;250,000,  &nbsp;
    		<b>3.7%</b> AER for credit balances of  &pound;250,000 +.
    		
        
        
        
        
        AER stands for the Annual Equivalent Rate and illustrates the interest rate as if paid 
        and compounded annually. Interest is calculated daily, based on the amount in your 
        account at the end of each 
        day (not including any cheques that have yet to clear) and is added to your account 
        each month.
