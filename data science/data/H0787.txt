Both strands			can accept methyl groups. 			<LI>The replication product of a methylated site is hemimethylated.			The product of an unmethylated site is unmethylated. 		</UL>		<CENTER>			<P><A HREF="#Facts">Facts</A> | <A HREF="#Interp">Interpretations</A> | <A HREF="#Info">Further Info.</A> | <A HREF="#bottom">Other Pages</A> <BR>			</P>		</CENTER>		<H4><A NAME="Info"></A>Further information</H4>		<UL TYPE="square">			<LI>Enzymes that require the presence of methyl groups in one strand			in order to methylate the other have been identified.
