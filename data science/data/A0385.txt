<H1>Disability Discrimination Act</H1>

<p><B>The implementation of Part III of the Disability Discrimination Act has placed additional responsibilities on service providers. Birmingham Midshires strive to do the right thing for their customers and will take necessary steps to ensure that its products, policies and premises comply with the requirements of the legislation. </B></p>

<p align="center"><img src="graphics/dis.gif" width="299" height="100" alt='Disability symbols'></p>

<p>Hearing/Speech impaired customers can use Typetalk or contact us on our Textphone number 01902 302666.</p>

<p>Visually impaired customers can be provided with documentation in large print, Braille or audio cassette on request, just call 01902 302043.</p>

<P>Email Birmingham Midshires to request leaflets on any of our products and services detailed in this website <a href="mailto:info@birminghammidshires.co.uk">info@birminghammidshires.co.uk </A> </P>
