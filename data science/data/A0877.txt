<h1>Want more freedom to spend?</h1>
<h3>Want more freedom to spend? Wherever you are, whatever you're doing, the <a href="index.asp?page=moneyback">MoneyBack

<h3>Spread the cost of your holidays with your MoneyBack Card and be assured of

    And remember, you get MoneyBack on all UK and foreign purchases</li>
  <li>Get free travel accident insurance. You will also benefit from Purchase 
    Protection on most worldwide purchases over &pound;50 and an optional <a href="index.asp?page=card-protection-plus">Card&nbsp;Protection&nbsp;Plan</a>

  <li>Your card is accepted as payment at over 22 million outlets worldwide, and

    access local currency wherever you see the Visa or MasterCard sign</li>
</ul>

<h3>Spread the cost of motoring expenses with your MoneyBack card</h3>
<ul>

  <li>Get double MoneyBack when you purchase cover with <a href="http://www.greenflag.co.uk/" target="_blank">Green

    covers most motoring purchases over&nbsp;&pound;50</li>

  <li>You can transfer your balances from existing store cards and pay only 1.9%*

    <a href="http://www.indexshop.com/" target="_blank">Littlewoods Index</a> 
    and <a href="http://www.currys.co.uk/" target="_blank">Currys</a></li>
