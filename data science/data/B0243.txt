      <p align="center"><font color="#030F5B" face="Arial"><b><font size="4"><u>Homecover</u></font></b></font></p>

        and Contents Insurance specifically for Society borrowers</font></font></p>
      <div align="left"> 
        <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2" color="#030F5B"><b>HOME INSURANCE 
          AT A RATE THAT IS RIGHT</b></font></p>
        <p><b><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2" color="#030F5B">WHAT 
          IS HOMECOVER?</font><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2"><br>
          </font></b><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">Homecover 
          is a special Home Insurance Scheme developed by Clay Cross Building 
          Society. It is offered as an alternative to the insurance cover on your 
          home buildings which is required as a general condition of the mortgage 
          advance.</font></p>
        <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">Homecover also includes 
          contents, freezer contents, valuables, personal possessions, pedal cycles, 
          money and credit and cash cards.</font></p>
        <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">Homecover provides 
          home and contents cover in a single policy at very competitive rates.</font></p>
        <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">A brief summary 
          of cover is given within this leaflet - FULL POLICY WORDING IS AVAILABLE 
          ON REQUEST.</font></p>
        <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2" color="#030F5B"><b>HOW 
          DOES IT WORK?<br>
          </b></font><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">Your home 
          buildings must be insured for an amount sufficient to cover the full 
          cost of rebuilding. The valuer when he inspects your property will recommend 
          an appropriate figure, but you should check this to ensure that it is 
          adequate.</font></p>
        <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">A rebuilding cost 
          guarantee ensures that the full cost of the repair is paid even if this 
          exceeds the sum insured.</font></p>
        <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">If the total valuables 
          in your home do not exceed &pound;6,000 you are eligible for home cover.</font></p>
        <p><b><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2" color="#030F5B">WHAT 
          ARE THE MAJOR BENEFITS?</font><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2"><br>
          </font></b></p>
      </div>

            and contents cover in one policy</font></div>

          <div align="left"><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">Wide 
            cover for your valuables, personal possessions, pedal cycles, money 
            and credit and cash cards</font></div>
        </li>
        <li> 
          <div align="left"><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">Easy

                Homecover are settled on the basis of full repair or replacement 
                cost subject to the maximum amount payable - no deduction for 
                wear and tear except for clothing.</font></p>
