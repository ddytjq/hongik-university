RESULTS</b><br>																					Posted Thu, Jun 13, 2002.  												<p><b>The West Bromwich Building Society has announced another year of record results with asset growth in double figures for the 5th year in succession.<br></b></p>												<p>- Results for the year ended 31st March 2002 show: <br><br>- Record &#163;20m extra rate benefits for members – demonstrating that mutuality works.<br><br>- Record lending - up to &#163;738m for the year.<br><br>- Record inflow of in savings balances  up &#163;419m.<br><br>Record gross other income – up 10% to &#163;28m. <br><br>- Record Group pre-tax profit - up 19% to &#163;24.2m.<br><br>- Total Assets up to &#163;3.7bn. <br><br>- Group Management expenses ratio down to 1.13p%<br><br>- Made a major investment in a new mortgage and investment administration computer system.<br><br>- Implemented a state-of the art Contact Centre to give more choice to customers in how they do business with the Society.<br><br>- Won 10 external awards, including the prestigious Midlands Excellence Awards for the Best Large Company and Best Equal Opportunities Employer.<br><br>MEMBERS BENEFITS<br><br>Over &#163;20m extra rate benefits passed on to members this year alone through:  <br><br>- The Society’s privileged membership scheme to reward loyal customers: <br><br>- Lower mortgage rate (presently 0.75% below most high street banks) <br>saving qualifying loyal members &#163;375 a year on a typical &#163;50,000 mortgage.  <br><br>- Higher investment rates on Privileged Membership Accounts for members of 2 years standing or more (presently 0.5% more than bank base rate).  On a &#163;25,000 investment the extra interest earned would be &#163;125 per annum. <br><br>- Advanced Notification - on limited issue investment products launched from <br>time to time.  For example, the Society currently offers a Privileged Members <br>Investment Bond paying a guaranteed market leading rate of 5.1% gross to 31st December 2003.<br> <br>- &#163;500,000 annual investment through our community programme       benefiting hundreds of charities and good causes. <br><br>- Best Buy mortgage and savings products.  The Society’s mortgage products appeared in numerous Best Buy tables during the year.<br><br><br>And more benefits are on their way from the West Brom:<br><br>- The Society will inform members (with more than &#163;100 in their account) individually by post of each and every rate change together with details of all our rates. This will ensure members have written confirmation of revised rates, but also have the opportunity to compare different investment options to meet their changing needs. This action reflects the Society's commitment to greater customer choice and transparency.<br>      <br>- Existing borrowers with the West Brom will be offered a free annual review to ensure they receive the best rate and product to suit their needs. It will allow, for example, mortgage customers to move to a flexi-mortgage product at any time(subject to normal redemption terms) to benefit from flexible features, such as over and under payments and savings from daily calculation of interest payments.<br><br>Business Transformation<br><br>On top of delivering record results, the Society has made significant progress in implementing a major business transformation programme to streamline processes and improve productivity, to enhance customer service, reduce running costs and identify and meet customer needs.<br><br>The Society has already completed the implementation of a new Contact Centre, new mortgage and investment computer systems and credit scoring. Further projects include a review of lending and sales processes to ensure more staff are in customer facing roles. The Business Transformation programme represents an investment of &#163;20m.<br><br>COMMENT ON THE FUTURE<br><br>Andrew Messenger, Chief Executive, West Bromwich Building Society said: “Once again, it's been a great year for everyone at the West Brom, and I am confident that we can continue to make significant progress in the years ahead.<br><br>Mutuality is about sharing our success with our members. This is demonstrated in the record &#163;20m we have passed on this year to our members.<br><br>This year alone, we have created 44 new jobs.  As a major employer in the West Midlands, 15% of our employees are from minority ethnic groups which reflects the rich and diverse community we serve. <br><br>We were delighted that our equal opportunities polices and practices were recognised at the Midlands Excellence Awards when we were presented with the Equal Opportunities Employer Award. <br><br>There are many advantages of being a member of mutual building society, not least of all the chance for members to have their say and influence our thinking.  We have commenced a programme of Members' Forums at locations across our heartland . These give members the opportunity to have their say and to tell Executives and Directors what we can do to improve our service or to meet members needs better. I greatly value this feedback.<br><br>The West Brom is in expansion mode. Crucially, we have great people doing business with us, working for us and in the communities we serve. It all adds up to an unbeatable combination.&#34; <br><br><br><br><br><br>													<br>													</p>											</td>											<td width="15"></td>										</tr>									</table>											</td>										</tr>										<tr valign="top">											<td valign="top" bgcolor="white" width="10"></td>											<td valign="top" width="10" bgcolor="white"></td>											<td valign="top" width="20" bgcolor="white"></td>											<td valign="top" bgcolor="white"><br>																							</td>										</tr>									</table>									<br>								</td>								<td width="10"><img src="images/space.gif" width="15" height="25" border="0"></td>								<td valign="top" width="280">								<img height="100" width="100" src="images/society.gif"><br>									<table border="0" cellpadding="0" cellspacing="0" width="190">
