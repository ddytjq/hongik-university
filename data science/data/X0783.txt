              <p><img border="0" src="../images/begsurfing.gif" width="300" height="28"><BR><font face="Verdana,arial" size="2">Surfing is a sport that can be enjoyed by any age or race of people.</font>
</p>
              <p><img border="0" src="../images/kit.gif" width="120" height="28"><BR><font face="Verdana,arial" size="2">The basic kit you will need to start you off in the sport of surfing is a board and a wet suit, which is optional depending on how cold, the water is.</font>
</p>
              <p><img border="0" src="../images/start.gif" width="110" height="28"><BR><font face="Verdana,arial" size="2">Again we say it is very important that you do not surf alone so that is why we recommend joining your local club.</font>
</p>
              <p><img border="0" src="../images/basic.gif" width="110" height="28"><BR><font face="Verdana,arial" size="2">The basics in surfing are as follows: -&nbsp;</font>

</p>
              <ol style="font-size: 10pt; font-family: Verdana, arial; font-weight: bold">
                <li><font face="Verdana,arial" size="2">	Getting on the board.</font></li>
                <li><font face="Verdana,arial" size="2">	Staying on the wave without losing your balance.</font></li>
                <li><font face="Verdana,arial" size="2">	Being able to turn while on the move.</font></li>
                <li><font face="Verdana,arial" size="2">	Keeping your balance</font>.</li>
              </ol>
              <p><img border="0" src="../images/tricks.gif" width="200" height="28"><BR><font face="Verdana,arial" size="2">As you become more confident on the wave you will start to learn tricks of the sport.
There are not as many tricks as you would think but there are a few such as jumps or spins.</font></p>
