Since participating in the development of the Forties field, the Bank has deepened and expanded its knowledge of the UK North Sea to become a recognised industry leader in financing the oil and gas sector world-wide.
<P>
The Natural Resources team combines over 50 years' industry experience in petroleum engineering and provides technical expertise and analysis. Complimenting our Scottish base, we have representatives based in Sydney and in Houston, where locally based teams manage a portfolio of small and medium corporate customers.
<P>
Our Australian team provides the local knowledge base to allow us to offer finance throughout Australia and the South Pacific. Furthermore, our ownership interest in BankWest has allowed us to utilise their expertise in the Australian mining sector to expand our portfolio in the extraction industries.

<P>
Far from concentrating exclusively on upstream activities, we have also financed petrochemical plants, refineries, smelting plants and pipeline systems.
