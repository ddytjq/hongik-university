			<SPAN CLASS=header>E-Business Deposit Account</SPAN>

			<P>
			This deposit account is deliberately simple and straightforward, making it incredibly easy to earn high rates of interest on your surplus funds day-to-day.  It's an ideal way to earn more from your money.

			<P>
			<B>Guaranteed competitive rates of interest
</B>
			<BR>
			Highly attractive rates of interest are guaranteed with no restriction on when you can access your deposit.<br><br>

Furthermore, Bank of Scotland offers an interest rate guarantee, so you can be confident that you are always earning competitive interest on your balance.  <a class="topnav" href="interestrateguarantee.html">Click here</A> to find out more about our interest rate guarantee.


			<P>
			<B>Complete flexibility</B>
			<BR>
			You will have complete control to move funds into and out of your account whenever you wish ensuring you are always maximising the interest you earn.<br><br>

Inter-account transfers can be made quickly and easily via the Internet at any time, ensuring your funds are where you want them, when you want them.<br><br>

Inter-account transfers take effect the same day provided they are instructed before 6pm on a business day. Transfers can also be instructed to a pre-selected account outside Bank of Scotland, but may take up to 5 working days.
