          <td class="content">
          


            <p class="contentsubhead">Currency Accounts </p>
            <p> If you make and receive payments in a foreign 
              currency, a Clydesdale Bank Currency Account can help minimise exchange 
              risk and reduce borrowing costs where foreign currency interest 
              rates are lower than sterling. </p>
            <p> There are two main 
              accounts: </p>
            <ul>
              <li class="contenttext"><a href="#curr"  class="link">Currency 
                Current Account</a>; or a 
              <li class="contenttext"><a href="#fixed"  class="link">Fixed 
                Term Deposit Account</a>. 
            </ul>
            <p>Both accounts are available in a wide range of currencies. </p>
            <p> <a name="curr"><span class="contenttextbold">Currency 
              Current Accounts</span></a><br />
              The Currency Current Account has a cheque book facility available 
              in most currencies. The account can help you to manage foreign exchange 
              risk if you: </p>
            <ul>
              <li class="contenttext">Are involved 
                in international trading; or 
              <li class="contenttext">Have a regular 
                turnover in a foreign currency. 
            </ul>
            <p>The account can also be used to facilitate settlement of international 
            banking transactions and forward exchange contracts. </p>
            <p> This account is 
              ideal for people who trade across the various European countries 
              that form the euro zone. <!--Amalgamating the separate euro zone foreign currency accounts into one euro Currency Current-->The 
              account offers the potential to minimise borrowing costs even further 
              and maximise interest earning potential. </p>
            <p> You can also transfer 
              funds between your Currency Accounts or to your sterling account 
              without any commission charge. This can be done through our International 
              Banking Centre (for Direct Dealing customers) or by contacting your 
              local branch. </p>
            <p> <a name="fixed"><span class="contenttextbold">Fixed 
              Term Deposit Account</span></a><br />
              This account offers an alternative to investing funds in Sterling. 
              Funds are deposited in a specified currency for a fixed period at 
              a fixed rate of interest. This can be particularly helpful to meet 
              future cash flow requirements and will generally offer more attractive 
              rates of interest than the <a href="#curr" class="link">Currency Current Account</a>. </p>
              
            <p> All accounts are 
              maintained within our International Banking Centre. Arrangements 
              can be made for customer instructions and communications to be made 
              direct with our International Banking Centre, thereby streamlining 
              service delivery. </p>
            <p> <span class="contenttextbold">Terms and Conditions</span><br />
              Minimum opening balances: </p>
            <p> <span class="contenttextbold">Currency Current 
              Accounts</span> - US $1001 or other currency equivalent unless overdraft 
              facility approved. </p>
            <p><span class="contenttextbold"> Fixed Term Deposit </span>
              - GB10,000 or other currency equivalent for euro, US$, JPY, CA$ 
              and AU$. For other currencies please contact our <a href="296.php"  class="link">International 
              Banking Centre</a> on <nobr>0141 223 4444</nobr> or <a href="../about/201.php" class="link">email 
              us</a>. </p>
            <p><span class="contenttextbold"> Value Date</span><br />
              Represents the date from when interest will be calculated and is 
              determined by the date from which the Bank has been able to invest 
              or borrow funds on the Inter-Bank Market. (Note this will be affected 
              by local and overseas holidays). </p>
            <p><span class="contenttextbold"> Interest</span><br />
              This is calculated on an Actual/360 or Actual/365 day year basis 
              depending on market practice. Interest on Currency Current Accounts 
              is applied quarterly in arrears on the 30th of March, June, September 
              and December. </p>
            <p> Interest rates 
              can fluctuate daily in line with market conditions. Details of these 
              rates are available from the International Banking Centre (a charge 
              of &#163;15 is made if rates are required to be faxed.) </p>
            <p> Interest earned 
              on a Currency Current Account is payable on a tiered basis details 
              of which are available on request. </p>
            <p> Fixed Term Deposit 
              interest is payable on maturity. </p>
            <p> A service charge 
              is applied quarterly where a cheque book facility is required and 
              also for cheques lodged to your account. The current charge and 
              other transaction charges are available on request. </p>
            <p><span class="contenttextbold"> Overdrafts</span><br />
              Where borrowing for business is agreed, the lending margin added 
              to the current currency Base Rate will be advised by your Relationship 
              Manager. Unauthorised borrowing will be charged at a default rate 
              (above the relevant currency Base Rate), details of which are available 
              on request. </p>
            <p><span class="contenttextbold"> Statements</span><br />
              Currency Current Account statements will be issued on a quarterly 
              basis or at other frequencies on request. </p>
            <p><span class="contenttextbold"> Confirmations</span><br />
              A confirmation is produced for all Fixed Term Deposits. Reinvestment 
              instructions must be received by the International Banking Centre 
              two business days prior to maturity of the deposit. Arrangements 
              can be made to rollover/re-invest deposits on a standard basis. 
              </p>
            <p><span class="contenttextbold"> Tax Deduction 
              Scheme for Interest</span><br />
              If applicable UK Income Tax will be deducted from interest paid 
              at the current rates unless a valid IR Form R85 (Interest Without 
              Tax Deducted) or IR Form R105 (Declaration of Non Residence) has 
              been completed for the account in the case of personal customers. 
              </p>
            <p> <span class="contenttextbold">Tailored Services</span><br />
              Special arrangements other than those detailed above can be made 
              available to customers for a negotiated fee. </p>
            <p><span class="contenttextbold"> Opening an Account</span><br />
              To open a Currency Current Account just contact your local branch 
              or Relationship Manager. You will need to complete a standard Account 
              Opening Application Form or in the case of a Limited Company, a 
              Letter of Request signed by the authorised signatories. The Bank's 
              Form 1655 will also need to be signed where a cheque book is required 
              or Fixed Term Deposit Account is being opened. </p>
