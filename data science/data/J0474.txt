    <td width="100%"><!--mstheme--><font face="arial, helvetica, geneva"><h2 align="left"><!--mstheme--><font face="Arial, helvetica, geneva" color="#000000"><a href="../who.htm" target="main">Who We Are:</a> <!--webbot bot="Navigation" S-Type="banner"

      <p><img border="0" src="../images/sitegraphix/headeritems/new_8b.jpg" align="right" alt="outside the research center" width="129" height="89">Built expressly to preserve
    the heritage of motor racing, the International Motor Racing Research Center
      at Watkins Glen is a
    5,000 square-foot facility that is both a research library and an archive.&nbsp; The
    research facility is available to all interested persons for both serious scholarship and
    pure pleasure.<img SRC="../images/sitegraphix/headeritems/new_7b.jpg" BORDER="0" ALIGN="left" alt="inside the research center" width="129" height="108"> The archive is intended to serve as a safe repository for primary documents
    related to the history and heritage of worldwide motorsport.</p>
    <p> The International Motor Racing Research Center at Watkins Glen is neither an
    austere library or a hands-off museum. Instead, it is a comfortable place to experience the
    spirit and will of the people who have shaped the sport.</p>
