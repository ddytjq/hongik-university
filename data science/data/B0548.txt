			<br><p>
		The Flexible Investment Bond is a lump sum investment with no fixed term,which invests in the units of one or more of Norwich Union's investment funds. Investing in the Bond allows you to balance the return you can potentially receive with the level of security you require.
		<br><p>
		You should see your bond as a long term investment. This usually means at least 5 years. You can keep your bond for as long as you choose, there is no fixed date when it has to end.
		<br><p>
		There are four investment funds available, allowing you to choose the one which best suits your individual requirements in terms of security and potential rewards. The value of an investment in the Flexible Investment Bond can go down as well as up and is not guaranteed, apart from the Guaranteed Fund which guarantees to return the whole of your initial investment at the fifth policy anniversary. Withdrawals, switches and additional life cover charges, if any, from the fund will reduce the guaranteed amount in proportion to the number of units cancelled.
