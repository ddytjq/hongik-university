	<strong>How much can I borrow?</strong><BR><BR>
	The amount that can be borrowed is calculated on the basis of the expected rental income as indicated by the valuer or if you are remortgaging with an existing tenant in place, the actual rent you charge.<BR>
	<BR>
	For a standard Bristol &amp; West Buy to Let mortgage the rent should be equal to or greater than:<BR>
	<BR>
	<ul><li>For all variable products and fixed/capped with a term of less than 5 years:<br>125% of the annual interest due on the loan, assuming an interest rate of 7.75%.</li><br><br>
	<li>For fixed/capped products with a term of 5 years or more:<br>125% of the annual interest due on the loan, using the actual product rate.</li></ul>
	<BR><BR>
	<strong>Portfolio Buy to Let </strong><br>
We have special criteria if any of the following apply:<br><br>

<ul><li>If you are looking to have 6 or more buy to let properties with us.</li>
<li>The value of the property you are looking to buy to let is &pound;250,000 or over.</li>
<li>The value of your existing portfolio with us plus the new loan takes your total borrowing over &pound;500,000.</li></ul>
