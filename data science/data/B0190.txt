<P><B>(75%) 5.60% FIXED RATE MORTGAGE UNTIL 31st March 2007</B></p>

<P><B>APR 5.90%</B></p>

<P>Assumes a fixed rate of 5.60% until 31st March 2007 after which the mortgage will revert to the Society's Variable Residential Rate (currently 5.25%) for the remaining term of the mortgage and a buildings insurane premium of £7.94 per month as reflected in the total amount payable and APR, a capital and interest repayment mortgage of £50,000 over 25 years and a purchase price of £70,000.</p>
