	<H4>V8 SUPERCAR # 1</H4>

	<H5>Race History</H5>
	<DL>
	<DT>1995<DD>First raced at Bathurst by Tony Scott and John Cleland. 6th Outright and 1st Privateer.
	<DT>1996<DD>Privateer Cup round winner at Calder driven by Mal Rose. Also won Best Presented Car and Crew.
	<DT>1997<DD>Won the inaugural ARDC AMSCAR Series at Eastern Creek and Amaroo Park.
	<DT>1998<DD>Won the FAI 1000 Privateer Cup. 10th outright FAI 1000. 3rd in the Privateer Championship in the SATCC.
	<DT>2000<DD>Konica V8 Lites Series.
	<DT><B>2001</B><DD><B>Konica V8 Supercar Series </B><FONT SIZE="-1"><A HREF="racing.html">&nbsp;&nbsp;Click here to see the latest racing news!</A></FONT>
	</DL>

	<IMG SRC="44rears.JPG" WIDTH=400 HEIGHT=271 ALIGN="RIGHT" VSPACE="5" BORDER=0 ALT="44 rear view">
	<H5>Specifications</H5>

	<DL>
	<DT>MAKE:<DD>Holden VS Commodore
	<DT>ENGINE:<DD>5.0 litre Chev. Electronic fuel injection and engine management system.<BR>Estimated output 440 kW (570 bhp), 569 Nm (420 ft/lbs).
	<DT>DRIVELINE:<DD>Transmission: Hollinger 6 speed.<BR>Differential: Australian Ford 9" CWP. Ratios vary from 2.9:1 to 4.5:1.
	<DT>SUSPENSION:<DD>Front: McPherson struts with adjustable Proflex shocks.<BR>Rear: Four link trailing arms with Watts linkage locating live axle with coil over Proflex shocks.
	<IMG SRC="44fronts.JPG" WIDTH=250 HEIGHT=220 BORDER=0 ALIGN="RIGHT" VSPACE="5" ALT="44 front view">
	<DT>BRAKES:<DD>Front: 356mm (14") discs with Harrop calipers.<BR>Rear: 330mm (13") discs with Harrop calipers.
	<DT>WHEELS:<DD>Speedline cast magnesium 17" x 11" rims.
	<DT>TYRES:<DD>Bridgestone 280/640x17" control tyre.
	<DT>WEIGHT:<DD>1350 kgs (class weight limit).
	<DT>TOP SPEED:<DD>295 km/h (184 mph).
	</DL>
