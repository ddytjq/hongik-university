<p><b>1.2  In the interpretation and construction of these Conditions, unless there

 Words in the singular include the plural and vice versa;<br><b>1.2.2</b>  Words referring

persons, any event referred to in these Conditions shall be deemed to have happened

 References in these Conditions to statutory provisions are to those provisions as

<p><b>2.  Payments: </b>The Borrower covenants with the Society:</p>
<p><b>2.1</b>  To pay the Monthly Payments or such other amount as may become payable
under these Conditions until the Whole Debt shall have been fully paid and satisfied.
Such Monthly Payments shall become due on the first day of each Month (beginning with
the first day of the month immediately following the date of the release of the Advance
monies) or on such later date in the Month as the Society shall specify.</p>
<p><b>2.2</b>  All payments to the Society shall be made in pounds Sterling in immediately
available funds. If any sum falls due for payment on a day which is not a Business
Day it shall be made on or before the preceding Business Day. All monies payable by
the Borrower shall be paid in full without any set-off or counterclaim and (except
as may otherwise be required by law) free and clear without any deduction or withholding
whatsoever. If the Borrower is at any time required by law to make any deduction or
withholding from any payment to the Society, then the Borrower may be required by
the Society to pay to the Society such additional amount or amounts as will result
in the Society receiving the full amount it would have received had no such deduction
or withholding been required. Where the Society does require the Borrower to make
such additional payment(s) and subsequently the Society receives any credit or rebate
of part or the whole of such additional payment(s) from a third party, then the Society
will, following such credit or rebate, credit the Borrower's account with the Society
with an amount equal to such credit or rebate.</p>
<p><b>2.3</b>  That if on the realisation of its security by the Society the net proceeds
shall not be sufficient to pay off the Whole Debt, the Borrower will immediately pay
to the Society the amount of the shortfall together with Interest at the current rate
until payment.</p>
<p><b>2.4</b>  On the expiration of one Month's notice to that effect given by the
Society to the Borrower or immediately in the case of the Property being purchased
compulsorily or requisitioned, to pay to the Society the Whole Debt with Interest
at the current rate until payment.</p>
<p><b>2.5</b>  To pay all Costs reasonably incurred by the Society or any Receiver
appointed by the Society in connection with the enforcement or with a view to the
enforcement of its security or in exercising all or any of its powers or remedies
arising under the Offer of Advance or otherwise:-<br><b>2.5.1</b>  Any cost of the
Society's solicitors shall be assessed as between a Solicitor and his own Client on
a complete indemnity basis together with Interest at the current rate until payment
by the Borrower.<br><b>2.5.2</b>  Any administrative costs or fees attributable to
work, whether legal or otherwise, relating to the enforcement or proposed enforcement
of the Society's security carried out by the Society's officers shall be assessed
by reference to the actual cost to the Society, such costs becoming immediately due
and payable by the Borrower with Interest at the current rate until payment by the
Borrower.</p>
<p><b>2.6</b>  Time shall be of the essence as regards the payment of all monies due
from the Borrower to the Society.</p>
<p><b>3.  Non Standard Payments:</b></p>
<p>The Society may in its absolute discretion in writing permit the Borrower to repay
the Whole Debt by payments other than those payable under the Offer of Advance, or
may return any payment, or give further time for payment, of any money payable under
the Offer of Advance, or otherwise make an arrangement with the Borrower as to the
method and time of any payment under the Offer of Advance. PROVIDED THAT no such arrangement
shall affect the Society's powers, or render the Society liable in damages, or otherwise
for the exercise of any such power where no such liability would have arisen if such
arrangement had not been made.</p>
<p><b>4.  Appropriation and Variation of Monthly Payments:</b></p>
<p><b>4.1</b>  The Society shall apply payments received from the Borrower in the
following order of priority, as appropriate to the circumstances of each case:-<br><b>4.1.1</b>
 First, towards payment of Costs;<br><b>4.1.2</b>  Secondly, towards payment of any
premium of insurance relating to the Property;<br><b>4.1.3</b>  Thirdly, towards payment
of Interest; and<br><b>4.1.4</b>  Fourthly, towards repayment of capital.</p>
<p><b>4.2</b>  The Monthly Payment may be varied at any time and from time to time

<p><b>5.  Interest:</b></p>
<p><b>5.1</b>  The Society will charge Interest on the Advance and all other sums
which become owing under the Charge by either the Yearly Interest Method or the Daily
Interest Method as set out below. The method which applies in the case of an Advance
will be set out in the Offer of Advance.</p>
<p><b>5.2  Yearly Interest Method</b><br>The Society will charge Interest on a yearly
basis in the following manner:-<br><b>5.2.1</b>  Interest will be payable on the Advance
from the date on which the Advance cheque (or telegraphic transfer) is issued by the
Society until the following 31 December.<br><b>5.2.2</b>  In each succeeding Year,
Interest will be charged on the balance of the Whole Debt outstanding at the end of
the preceding Year.<br><b>5.2.3</b>  In addition, if any further sums become owing
during any Year, Interest will be charged from the date such sums become owing until
the end of that Year.<br><b>5.2.4 </b> In the Year of repayment of the Whole Debt,
Interest will be charged on the Whole Debt outstanding at the end of the preceding
Year (and on any further sums becoming owing during that Year) up to and including
the date on which cleared funds are received by the Society to repay the Whole Debt
in full.<br><b>5.2.5</b>  Interest will accrue from day to day but will be payable
partly in advance (if appropriate having a regard to the Payment Date) and partly
in arrears by monthly instalments during the Year (each month being treated for this
purpose as a twelfth of the Year).</p>
<p><b>5.3  Daily Interest Method</b><br>The Society will charge Interest on a daily
basis in the following manner:-<br><b>5.3.1</b>  Interest will be calculated daily
on the balance outstanding of the Whole Debt at the end of the previous day and accrued
until such time as a payment is received.<br><b>5.3.2</b>  Payments received will,
after being applied towards payment of costs and any premium of insurance relating
to the Property as mentioned in Condition 4, be applied towards the accrued Interest
and then towards repayment of capital.<br><b>5.3.3</b>  Where such payment does not
cover the amount of accrued Interest the outstanding Interest will continue to accrue.<br><b>5.3.4</b>
 The Society may on 31 December each Year add the amount of any Interest which has
accrued and is unpaid to the Whole Debt and charge Interest on it until it is paid.
The Society will notify the Borrower if it does so.<br><b>5.3.5</b>  Upon repayment
of the Whole Debt the amount required to redeem the Charge will be the capital balance
outstanding and any accrued Interest up to and including the date on which cleared
funds are received by the Society to repay the Whole Debt in full.</p>
<p><b>5.4</b>  The Society may vary its method of charging Interest from time to time
if it considers it reasonable to do so. The Society will give reasonable notice in
advance to any Borrower who may be affected by any such variation.</p>
<p><b>5.5</b>  The rate of Interest shall be the initial rate or such other rate as
the Society may in its absolute discretion decide, subject to the following:<br><b>5.5.1</b>
 A reduction in the current rate may be made without formality and so as to take effect
from such date as the Society may decide. For an increase in the current rate the
Society shall give notice in accordance with Condition 14.<br><b>5.5.2  </b>Without
