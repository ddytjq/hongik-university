			<H2>V8 Lites - Lakeside - August 6th, 2000.</H2>
			<IMG SRC="r2000rd401s.JPG" WIDTH=400 HEIGHT=269 BORDER=0 ALIGN="RIGHT" ALT="Mal on the grid at Lakeside">
			<P>The weekend got off to a good start when Mal put The Supercar Experience Commodore 3rd on the grid. At the press conference interviews, he explained where the funding to compete at this round came from...</P>
			<BLOCKQUOTE><I>I'd like to thank the people who came along to my Supercar Experience ride day earlier this week. It's thanks to them that I'm here".</I></BLOCKQUOTE>
			<P>The start of Race 1 was marred by a major incident at the Karussel and had to be red flagged, but not long after the restart Ryan McLeod tapped Mal sending him into a spin. After the field streamed by Mal was able to rejoin the race, and fought back to finish 9th.</P>
			<P>Following the first race incident, McLeod and Salmon (separate incident) were forced to start race 2 from the rear of the grid elevating Mal up to 7th for the start of race 2. Staying out of trouble, he gradually picked up places till he was running 5th. On the last corner coming onto the main straight, McLeod came unstuck when attempting to pass Mal, spinning off and clouting the wall.</P>
			<P>Race 3 was one of consolidation for Mal. The incredible battle raging at the head of the pack between Besnard, Wakefield and Canto grabbed most of the attention but Mal was there to take advantage of the casualties up the front, finishing the last race in 3rd. </P>
			<P>Mal had started the Round with a third in qualifying and finished Sunday with a third place. All things considered, it was a fine effort and a good result. </P>
