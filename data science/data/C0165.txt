<TR>

<TD bgColor="white" class="tablet" width="400"><FONT face="Verdana" size="1"><B>How do I work out the 'sum insured' and 'contents insurance'?</B><BR><BR></FONT></TD>
<TD align="right" bgColor="white" class="tablet" width="19"></TD></TR>
<TR>
<TD colSpan="3">
<P><FONT face="Verdana" size="1"><A href="glossary.html?ID=9ZSGBGXB4HW00BK">Sum insured</a> is the maximum amount you can claim on your policy. It is the cost of rebuilding the entire property and note, not the market value that is usually a greater figure and sometimes a lower figure. If you move house your surveyor should calculate this for you although it is advisable to check it yourself using the table provided by the Association of British Insurers, it's a free leaflet called <A href="glossary.html?ID=9ZSGBGXB4HW00BK">Buildings Insurance for Homeowners</a>.<br>Contents Insurance can be calculated using the relevant <A href="contents.xls">table</a> suplied by A.B.I.<br><br></FONT></P></TD></TR>
