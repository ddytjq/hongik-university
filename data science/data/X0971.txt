<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>STILLWATER, Okla.&nbsp;-- A win over rival Oklahoma in Wednesday night's edition of Sooner State Bedlam would've been nice. But make no mistake: The Cowboys got the one they needed this week.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<?xml:namespace prefix = o ns = "urn:schemas-microsoft-com:office:office" /><o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>That came Saturday.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>OSU senior guard Maurice Baker busted out of a slump to score 16 points in leading coach Eddie Sutton's squad to a 64-55 win against Colorado here at Gallagher-Iba Arena.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>But altogether joyful these Cowboys weren't. Relieved? Probably. Moved? Certainly. Such was inevitable&nbsp;-- such was appropriate&nbsp;-- on an afternoon during which the 10 victims of last year's plane crash were remembered with a halftime moment of silence. Players Daniel Lawson and Nate Fleming were among those on board Jan. 27 a season ago.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>An ugly contest&nbsp;-- the two teams combined to hit just six of 36 three-point attempts&nbsp;-- never looked so good.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>"The game today was very, very important," Sutton said after the fact. "In the first half, we were a little tight, and that was because they recognized the importance of the ballgame. They were very much aware of it."</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>Not that that was a bad thing.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>"We all understood that the players and the guys on that plane were there for us today," sophomore forward Ivan McFarlin said. "We know they were looking down at us today, and we were going to win it for them.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>"They're happy right now&nbsp;-- just like we're happy right now."</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>And not just because they won for their fallen friends. Pretty much just because they won at all.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>Oklahoma State opened this season with a 69-62 home win Nov. 16 over Cincinnati&nbsp;-- still the Bearcats' only loss&nbsp;-- and made it all the way into the New Year without dropping so much as a game.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>But Big 12 losses to Texas (Jan. 5), Kansas (Jan. 15), Texas Tech (Jan. 19) and now Oklahoma have left the Cowboys at 4-4 in league play&nbsp;-- and on the brink of falling out of the nation's top 10 for the second time in three weeks.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>They managed only 53 points down in Norman, and the 64 points they posted Saturday against Colorado constituted anything but textbook offense.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>"There are times we know what to do," McFarlin said after the nine-point victory over the Buffs, "but there are times when four people know the play and one doesn't and the play is ruined. Coach tells us to read the play because there are times we don't."</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>Swedish senior Fredrik Jonzen is struggling a bit&nbsp;-- has been all season long&nbsp;-- and there are only so many offensive putbacks and garbage buckets McFarlin can get in any given game.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>Guards Victor Williams (an undersized point) and Melvin Sanders (a 6-foot-5 wing) aren't exactly Jason Williams and Chris Duhon.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>After scoring big at times in non-conference action&nbsp;-- 95 points against TCU, 95 against New Orleans, 90 against Jackson State&nbsp;-- OSU is tallying around 65 a game in their eight Big 12 dates.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>The solution could be simple: Maurice Baker.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>"We can't win," Sutton explained, "without him being more aggressive offensively."</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>And healthy. He's been nursing a nagging groin injury.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>Baker was back at it in the Colorado game&nbsp;-- aggressive (at least in the second half) and healthy (enough). The 6-1 shooting guard rebounded from a dreadful one-point performance before intermission to finish with a respectable 16&nbsp;-- right at his average&nbsp;-- at the buzzer.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>"I just had a little talk to myself at halftime," Baker said. "My team was looking for me. I was feeling pretty good in the second half."</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>It didn't carry over to the showdown with OU. He was limited to just six points in Wednesday night's game in Norman.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>That's three losses in the last five outings for Sutton's bunch. But at least the Cowboys won when they had to&nbsp;-- when it was imperative to honor those that are no longer here in this Great Plains college town.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>"We knew coming out of halftime that Coach Sutton was going to challenge his guys to step up," Colorado coach Ricardo Patton said, "and they did. This is a very good OSU team."</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>Not great, though. A top-10 ranking seems too high.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>"We don't have the best players," Sutton admitted. "But I'll put our guys against anybody as far as good people are concerned. That's one reason they've been able to hang in there (this past year). They have such strong character."</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>Especially on an afternoon when it mattered most.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<H1 style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>DARDEN WALKS (ON) TO STARDOM</FONT></H1>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>Antonio Darden is 6-3. But the Coastal Carolina forward sure doesn't play like it.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>"He has a five man's mentality," Coastal coach Pete Strickland said. "He thinks he's 6-8. Even though he's smaller than others at his position, he has that I'm-going-to-show-you kind of pride."</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>Darden, known by the Chanticleers and the folks on the school's Conway, S.C., campus as Tony, certainly showed the Big South Conference this week.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>The former walk-on from Randallstown, Md., earned the honor by posting an 18-and-10 double-double in CCU's 88-78 win last week over UNC Asheville and then adding 20 and seven in another victory at Charleston Southern.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>"I was very surprised," Darden said. "It wasn't something I was really thinking about. So it wasn't expected at all."</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>Then again, though, neither was playing Division I basketball.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>He got some interest from Lehigh and the Citadel&nbsp;-- as well as a whole host of Division III school&nbsp;-- coming out of Randallstown High. But he visited Coastal, liked what he saw, got a partial academic scholarship and decided to try to earn a roster spot with the Chants.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>"Any story of a walk-on has to start with effort and belief," said Strickland, who was in his first year at Coastal when Darden showed up as a freshman.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>"He's one those guys that fits the walk-on stereotype. He almost bugs you until you take notice. But he backs it up. He's not a talker&nbsp;-- he's a doer."</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>Darden did enough in his first two and a half years at Coastal to garner a scholarship last year at semester break. Now he's going to school and playing DI hoops on a full ride&nbsp;-- and averaging 13.5 points and better than seven rebounds an outing in Big South play.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>He credits Strickland for the opportunity as well as his individual growth as a basketball talent.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>"Coach is one of the top teachers in the country," Darden said. "That's not just my opinion. I've heard that from everybody. And he's made me a believer."</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>Funny&nbsp;-- Darden tends to have that effect on others, too.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>"He has a great knack of getting a shot off over bigger buys," Strickland explained. "He's a very gifted leaper, he can shoot it, and he's beyond competitive&nbsp;-- he's combative. Tony's not a guy you can write off."</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<H1 style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>QUAKERS BIGGEST OF BIG 5</FONT></H1>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>Perennial Ivy contender Penn might not win the league's championship this season. Brown, Yale and even Harvard are capable of nabbing the conference's NCAA berth, and Princeton, as always, is Princeton.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>But the Quakers will have at least one title to put on their 2001-2002 resume: Coach Fran Dunphy's squad beat LaSalle Tuesday night in Philadelphia to earn the city's coveted Big Five title&nbsp;-- Penn's first in 28 years.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>"To be able to say that we were 4-0, Big 5 champions, it's very special," guard Andrew Toole told the Daily Pennsylvanian.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>So is this group of Quakers. At least they could be.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>Ugonna Onyekwe and Koko Archibong&nbsp;-- Penn's junior forward tandem&nbsp;-- are versatile threats who are all but unstoppable against Ivy competition. They combine to average almost 33 points a game.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>Andrew Toole, a junior guard who started his college career at Elon in North Carolina, is good for 13.9. Sophomore swingman Jeff Schiffner also averages in double figures with his 10.6 clip.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>"We're feeling very fortunate," Dunphy told the Pennsylvanian. "It feels great for these guys. I feel great for them because they work real hard.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>"I hope there's something special about this week and that we can now continue to play well and win some games."</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">The Quakers are set to take on the Ivy's New York duo&nbsp;-- Cornell and Columbia&nbsp;-- this weekend.<SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Arial"><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<H1 style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>AROUND AND ABOUT</FONT></H1>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>*** Mike Skrocki made history last week by becoming the first Richmond Spider to win Player of the Week honors in the Atlantic 10. UR is in its first season as an A-10 member.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>The sophomore forward from Hopewell, N.J., averaged 21.5 points and 4.5 rebounds in a pair of league wins over Temple and Duquesne. He hit seven threes en route to a career-high 25 points in the Spiders' 75-68 victory over the Owls.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>*** Princeton, meanwhile, held Division III opponent Western Maryland to seven first-half points en route to a 78-24 win over the Green Terror Monday night at Jadwin Gym. Western's point total was the lowest in facility history. "I think we're really starting to jell as a team," Tiger freshman Tom McLaughlin said. Evidently.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>*** Who needs Rodney White anyway? Bobby Lutz has his Charlotte team at 13-6 overall and 7-1 in Conference USA. The 49ers, behind strong play from guards Jobey Thomas and Demon Brown, have won six straight and eight of nine. All six of their losses have come to teams ranked in the top 100 of the RPI.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>*** Tennessee Tech is hot&nbsp;-- the Golden Eagles have won six of seven&nbsp;-- but not as hot as its gym. The campus' central cooling system is down due to some dormitory construction. So Tech officials sent out a memo to students and fans warning them to dress lightly at Thursday night's showdown with defending Ohio Valley Conference tourney champ Eastern Illinois.</FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT size=3><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;<o:p></o:p></FONT></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt; TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><FONT face="Times New Roman" size=3>*** Kudos to Gary Waters and his Rutgers Scarlet Knights. Wednesday night's upset over UConn in Piscataway&nbsp;-- the program's first as a member of the Big East&nbsp;-- rocked the RAC. These kids have come a long way since dropping their first two games of the season to East Carolina and VCU.</FONT></P></DIV>
