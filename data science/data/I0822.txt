The greatest 'libero' of his age, Beckenbauer is the only European to have won the FIFA World Cup as a player - with West Germany in 1974 - and as a coach - with the same country in 1990. </P>
<P><table cellpadding="0" cellspacing="0" border="0" width="104" align="right">  <tr>    <td width="1"><img src="/images/all/spacer.gif" border="0" width="1" height="1"></td>    <td width="103"><img src="/images/all/spacer.gif" border="0" width="103" height="1"></td>  </tr>  <tr>    <td width="104" colspan="2"><A HREF='javascript:fnOpenWindow("/multimedia/PopUpPhoto600x400.htmx?strUrl=/MultimediaFiles/Photo/magazine/Magazine/27751_600X400.jpg","Photo","no","no","no","no","no","no","no",599,470,"","")'><IMG width="104" height="102" src="/MultimediaFiles/Photo/magazine/Magazine/27751_104X102.jpg" border="0"></A></td>  </tr>  <tr>    <td width="1" rowspan="2" bgcolor="#000000"><img src="/images/all/black_spacer.gif" border="0" width="1" height="1"></td>    <td width="103"><table cellpadding="2" cellspacing="0" border="0" width="103">      <tr>        <td width="103" class="PictureCaption">Beckenbauer has been observing facilities at the 2002 FIFA World Cup finals<br>(©empics)</td>      </tr>    </table></td>  </tr>  <tr>    <td width="103"><img src="/images/all/black_spacer.gif" border="0" width="103" height="1"></td>  </tr></table><B>Organisational role</B><BR>Now club president at FC Bayern München - where he won three European Champion Clubs' Cups, four 1. Bundesliga titles, four German Cups, the UEFA Cup Winners' Cup and the FIFA World Club Championship as a player - Beckenbauer travelled to the Far East for the 2002 World Cup finals to inspect the facilities as part of his role in oreganising Germany's hosting of the 2006 finals - the first major tournament the country has hosted since reunification.<BR>&nbsp;<BR><B>uefa.com: How do compare the performance of this German side at the World Cup finals to previous German teams?</B></P>
<P><B>Franz Beckenbauer: </B>"Unlike previous tournaments our national team were outsiders this time. Firstly, Germany only qualified after the play-off games against Ukraine. Secondly important players like [Jens] Nowotny, [Mehmet] Scholl, [Sebastian] Deisler and [Christian] Wörns were injured. So, to reach the final was a real surprise and because of that everybody in Germany was euphoric. It was like the 1986 World Cup in Mexico where the national team also had personnel problems but we nevertheless reached the final with a good team spirit."</P>
<P><table cellpadding="0" cellspacing="0" border="0" width="104" align="right">  <tr>    <td width="1"><img src="/images/all/spacer.gif" border="0" width="1" height="1"></td>    <td width="103"><img src="/images/all/spacer.gif" border="0" width="103" height="1"></td>  </tr>  <tr>    <td width="104" colspan="2"><A HREF='javascript:fnOpenWindow("/multimedia/PopUpPhoto600x400.htmx?strUrl=/MultimediaFiles/Photo/magazine/Magazine/27738_600X400.jpg","Photo","no","no","no","no","no","no","no",599,470,"","")'><IMG width="104" height="102" src="/MultimediaFiles/Photo/magazine/Magazine/27738_104X102.jpg" border="0"></A></td>  </tr>  <tr>    <td width="1" rowspan="2" bgcolor="#000000"><img src="/images/all/black_spacer.gif" border="0" width="1" height="1"></td>    <td width="103"><table cellpadding="2" cellspacing="0" border="0" width="103">      <tr>        <td width="103" class="PictureCaption">Beckenbauer (left) lifts the 1974 FIFA World Cup as West Germany captain<br>(©empics)</td>      </tr>    </table></td>  </tr>  <tr>    <td width="103"><img src="/images/all/black_spacer.gif" border="0" width="103" height="1"></td>  </tr></table><B>uefa.com: Were you surprised that Germany reached the final?</B></P>
<P><B>Beckenbauer: </B>"I had expected other teams to be there. My tip for the final was France against Argentina. The fact that both teams were eliminated in the group stage was one of the weird things of this World Cup. And the Korean Republic being the first Asian team to reach the semi-finals was curious too. If anyone had told me before world cup that Germany would reach the final, I would have bet against it. But this is football."</P>
<P><table cellpadding="0" cellspacing="0" border="0" width="104" align="right">  <tr>    <td width="1"><img src="/images/all/spacer.gif" border="0" width="1" height="1"></td>    <td width="103"><img src="/images/all/spacer.gif" border="0" width="103" height="1"></td>  </tr>  <tr>    <td width="104" colspan="2"><A HREF='javascript:fnOpenWindow("/multimedia/PopUpPhoto600x400.htmx?strUrl=/MultimediaFiles/Photo/magazine/Magazine/18493_600X400.jpg","Photo","no","no","no","no","no","no","no",599,470,"","")'><IMG width="104" height="102" src="/MultimediaFiles/Photo/magazine/Magazine/18493_104X102.jpg" border="0"></A></td>  </tr>  <tr>    <td width="1" rowspan="2" bgcolor="#000000"><img src="/images/all/black_spacer.gif" border="0" width="1" height="1"></td>    <td width="103"><table cellpadding="2" cellspacing="0" border="0" width="103">      <tr>        <td width="103" class="PictureCaption">Beckenbauer is still very much active in German football<br>(©empics)</td>      </tr>    </table></td>  </tr>  <tr>    <td width="103"><img src="/images/all/black_spacer.gif" border="0" width="103" height="1"></td>  </tr></table><B>uefa.com: With young players like Christophe Metzelder, Michael Ballack, Sebastian Kehl, Thorsten Frings, Miroslav Klose and Sebastian Deisler, should Germany be optimistic about winning the 2006 World Cup on home turf?</B></P>
<P><B>Beckenbauer: </B>"Two years ago in Germany everybody and everything was criticised. Now everyone is happy. But I can only warn everybody about that - in no way must we allow the great efforts to bring on new talent to be reduced. On the other hand our national team is on the right path, but it would be fatal if we assumed that winning the World Cup title in 2006 is just a formality. The fact that we are the host of the next World Cup will give us a major boost but is does not mean that we will automatically be the next world champions.&nbsp;A cautious but confident approach was what won us&nbsp;the World Cup in 1974."</P>
<P><B>uefa.com: After all the disappointment of EURO 2000™ and then the 5-1 defeat by England, is there now a new mood of confidence in German football?<BR></B>&nbsp;<BR><B>Beckenbauer: </B>"The play-off games against the Ukraine were very important for our team because they could forget the 5-1 defeat against England in Munich. The 4-1 win against Ukraine in the second leg in Dortmund was very important because the spectators were enthusiastic and because of this, we had a much better mood around the national team. This has given us a lot of self-confidence and has been an important basis for a successful performance at the World Cup."</P>
<P><B>uefa.com: What do you think Germany can learn from the hosting of the 2002 World Cup?</B></P>
<P><B>Beckenbauer: </B>"First of all I was very impressed by the good hospitality. I had the feeling that every representative of the host nations was full of enthusiasm the World Cup and proud to be the host of so many guests of the whole world. We need such a good mood in Germany in 2006 too."</P>
<P><B>uefa.com: Concerning the World Cup 2006, will all of the facilities be ready in time for the finals?</B></P>
<P><B>Beckenbauer: </B>"It was the right decision to establish our organisation committee very early. At first we visited the World Cup in Korea/Japan with 14 collaborators to have a look behind the scenes.&nbsp;We have already nominated the stadiums in Germany for the World Cup 2006. I have a good feeling about it all."</P>
<P><B>uefa.com: Do you think that fatigue was a major factor in the failure of France, Argentina and Portugal in the competition?</B></P>
<P><B>Beckenbauer: </B>"We know this problem from the UEFA Champions League. 17 games are too much - we have to reduce that. Also the 18 qualification matches in South America are too much. This is an insult to all the participants. The World Cup is the biggest football event - all of the top stars should be fit as a fiddle. This was the reason why a real top star did not come to the fore at this World Cup."</P>
<P><B>uefa.com: How do you think that problem can be avoided in 2006? For example, will there be a longer break after the end of the season?</B></P>
<P><B>Beckenbauer:</B> "The opening game is scheduled for 9 June 2006. I hope that the national leagues and the finals of the international club competitions are not set back in their timetables. After the long season the players need time for physical and mental recuperation. Otherwise there will always be a risk of injury. The best example is France's Zinedine Zidane."</P><B>
<P>uefa.com: Which player impressed you most in the World Cup?</P>
<P></B><B>Beckenbauer:</B>&nbsp;"Without a doubt Oliver Kahn. He gave the best performance and was rightly awarded the Golden Ball. It was his misfortune that his only mistake resulted in Brazil going 1-0 up in the final. Anyway this does not change anything in terms of his outstanding performance."</P>
