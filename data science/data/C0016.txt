                    <h1>Mortgage Payment Protection Insurance</h1>
                    <h3><b>MPPI Policy Wording</b></h3>
                    <a href="mppi-policy-wording.asp"><font size="1" face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">(back)</font></a><br>
                    <h2>1. The special words we use</h2>
                    <p> The special words we use in these terms and conditions 
                      are shown below with their meanings alongside them. Except 
                      where the context otherwise requires, the masculine shall 
                      include the feminine, the singular shall include the plural 
                      and vice versa as appropriate. </p>
                    <table class=white_border cellspacing=0 cellpadding=3 width="100%" 
            border=0>
                      <tbody> 
                      <tr> 
                        <td class=white_border valign=top width="30%"> 
                          <p><b>Additional cover</b> </p>
                        </td>
                        <td class=white_border> 
                          <p>an amount up to 25% of the mortgage payment you have 
                            chosen under the policy.</p>
                        </td>
                      </tr>
                      <tr> 
                        <td class=white_border valign=top 
                  width="30%"> 
                          <p><b>Administrator</b> </p>
                        </td>
                        <td class=white_border> 
                          <p>Paymentshield Limited.</p>
                        </td>
                      </tr>
                      <tr> 
                        <td class=white_border valign=top width="30%"> 
                          <p><b>Benefit period</b> </p>
                        </td>
                        <td class=white_border> 
                          <p>a period of up to 12 months for each separate period 
                            of claim.</p>
                        </td>
                      </tr>
                      <tr> 
                        <td class=white_border valign=top width="30%"> 
                          <p><b>Benefit split</b> </p>
                        </td>
                        <td class=white_border> 
                          <p>either single cover or split cover as shown on your 
                            certificate of cover.</p>
                        </td>
                      </tr>
                      <tr> 
                        <td class=white_border valign=top width="30%"> 
                          <p><b>Business</b> <br>
                          </p>
                        </td>
                        <td class=white_border> 
                          <p>a company, profession, trade or industry owned and 
                            run by you (either alone, or with others) which is 
                            registered in the United Kingdom, Channel Islands 
                            or Isle of Man.</p>
                        </td>
                      </tr>
                      <tr> 
                        <td class=white_border valign=top width="30%"> 
                          <p><b>Certificate of cover</b> <br>
                          </p>
                        </td>
                        <td class=white_border> 
                          <p>the document you receive confirming the specific 
                            type and level of cover we give you under the policy.</p>
                        </td>
                      </tr>
                      <tr> 
                        <td class=white_border valign=top 
                  width="30%"> 
                          <p><b>Consultant</b> </p>
                        </td>
                        <td class=white_border> 
                          <p>a suitably qualified registered medical specialist 
                            (other than you or a relative) at a major hospital 
                            in the United Kingdom, Channel Islands or Isle of 
                            Man.</p>
                        </td>
                      </tr>
                      <tr> 
                        <td class=white_border valign=top 
                  width="30%"> 
                          <p><br>
                            <b>Controlling director</b> </p>
                        </td>
                        <td class=white_border> 
                          <p>you own more than 20% of the issued shares of the 
                            business you work in.</p>
                        </td>
                      </tr>
                      <tr> 
                        <td class=white_border valign=top width="30%"> 
                          <p><b><br>
                            Direct debit</b> </p>
                        </td>
                        <td class=white_border> 
                          <p>the instruction which gives us the authority to collect 
                            the premium from your bank or building society.</p>
                        </td>
                      </tr>
                      <tr> 
                        <td class=white_border valign=top 
                  width="30%"> 
                          <p><b>Disability</b> </p>
                        </td>
                        <td class=white_border> 
                          <p>an accident or sickness which a doctor (or consultant) 
                            confirms prevents you from doing your normal work 
                            or any other work you are reasonably able to do given 
                            your experience, education or training.</p>
                        </td>
                      </tr>
                      <tr> 
                        <td class=white_border valign=top width="30%"> 
                          <p><b>Disabled</b> </p>
                        </td>
                        <td class=white_border> 
                          <p>you are suffering from a disability.</p>
                        </td>
                      </tr>
                      <tr> 
                        <td class=white_border valign=top width="30%"> 
                          <p><b>Doctor</b> </p>
                        </td>
                        <td class=white_border> 
                          <p>a medical practitioner (other than you or a relative) 
                            who is registered with the General Medical Council 
                            and working in the United Kingdom, Channel Islands 
                            or Isle of Man.</p>
                        </td>
                      </tr>
                      <tr> 
                        <td class=white_border valign=top width="30%" 
                  height=40> 
                          <p><b>Eligible for cover</b> </p>
                        </td>
                        <td class=white_border height=40> 
                          <p>you meet the eligibility requirements of the policy.<br>
                          </p>
                        </td>
                      </tr>
                      <tr> 
                        <td class=white_border valign=top width="30%"> 
                          <p><b>Employed</b> <br>
                          </p>
                        </td>
                        <td class=white_border> 
                          <p>your employer is taking PAYE tax and national insurance 
                            contributions for you and:<br>
                            <br>
                            your work is permanent; or<br>
                            <br>
                            you work on a fixed term contract and you have at 
                            least two years continuous service. <br>
                            <br>
                            If you work on a fixed term contract and you have 
                            less than two years continuous service, you are not 
                            insured against your employer not renewing your contract 
                            and your entitlement to receive monthly benefit will 
                            end on the date that your contract should originally 
                            end.</p>
                        </td>
                      </tr>
                      <tr> 
                        <td class=white_border valign=top width="30%"> 
                          <p><b>End date</b> </p>
                        </td>
                        <td class=white_border> 
                          <p>the date cover under the policy ends.</p>
                        </td>
                      </tr>
                      <tr> 
                        <td class=white_border valign=top width="30%"> 
                          <p><b>Exclusion period</b> <br>
                          </p>
                        </td>
                        <td class=white_border> 
                          <p>a period of three calendar months immediately after 
                            the policy start date.</p>
                        </td>
                      </tr>
                      <tr> 
                        <td class=white_border valign=top width="30%"> 
                          <p><b>Existing borrower</b> </p>
                        </td>
                        <td class=white_border> 
                          <p>your mortgage has been in force for more than 30 
                            days on the policy start date.</p>
                        </td>
                      </tr>
                      <tr> 
                        <td class=white_border valign=top width="30%"> 
                          <p><b>Free cover</b> </p>
                        </td>
                        <td class=white_border> 
                          <p>the period immediately after the policy start date 
                            during which you will not have to pay any premium. 
                            The free cover you will receive is shown on your certificate 
                            of cover.</p>
                        </td>
                      </tr>
                      <tr> 
                        <td class=white_border valign=top width="30%"> 
                          <p><b>Important facts</b> </p>
                        </td>
                        <td class=white_border> 
                          <p>the circumstances that you must reveal on or before 
                            the policy start date.<br>
                            Lender the bank, building society or finance company 
                            that grants you a mortgage.</p>
                        </td>
                      </tr>
                      <tr> 
                        <td class=white_border valign=top width="30%"> 
                          <p><b>Monthly benefit</b> </p>
                        </td>
                        <td class=white_border> 
                          <p>the total amount of monthly cover you have under 
                            the policy. <br>
                            This is also the most we will pay you each month if 
                            you make a claim (this will depend on the benefit 
                            split). The monthly benefit you have chosen is shown 
                            on your certificate of cover.</p>
                        </td>
                      </tr>
                      <tr> 
                        <td class=white_border valign=top width="30%"> 
                          <p><b>Mortgage</b> </p>
                        </td>
                        <td class=white_border> 
                          <p>the first loan you took out with a lender and secured 
                            against the property you are living in as your private 
                            home.</p>
                        </td>
                      </tr>
                      <tr> 
                        <td class=white_border valign=top width="30%"> 
                          <p><b>Mortgage payment</b> </p>
                        </td>
                        <td class=white_border> 
                          <p>the normal monthly amount you must pay your lender 
                            under the terms of your mortgage agreement.</p>
                        </td>
                      </tr>
                      <tr> 
                        <td class=white_border valign=top width="30%"> 
                          <p><b>New borrower</b> </p>
                        </td>
                        <td class=white_border> 
                          <p>your mortgage has been in force for 30 days or less 
                            on the policy start date.</p>
                        </td>
                      </tr>
                      <tr> 
                        <td class=white_border valign=top width="30%"> 
                          <p><b>Normal income</b> </p>
                        </td>
                        <td class=white_border> 
                          <p>if you are employed - the average monthly income 
                            you have received before deductions (or if you are 
                            paid every week, the monthly equivalent) during the 
                            past 12 months; or<br>
                            <br>
                            if you are self-employed - the monthly average of 
                            your yearly income as declared on your self- assessment 
                            return for the previous tax year. This must be confirmed 
                            by the Inland Revenue.</p>
                        </td>
                      </tr>
                      <tr> 
                        <td class=white_border valign=top width="30%"> 
                          <p><b>Payment in lieu of notice</b> </p>
                        </td>
                        <td class=white_border> 
                          <p>any payment, or any compensation for losing your 
                            job (including any payment made under a compromise 
                            agreement), that relates to any period of notice that 
                            you are entitled to, or should have been served by 
                            your employer, under the terms of your contract of 
                            employment or letter of appointment.</p>
                        </td>
                      </tr>
                      <tr> 
                        <td class=white_border valign=top width="30%"> 
                          <p><b><br>
                            Period of claim</b> <br>
                          </p>
                        </td>
                        <td class=white_border> 
                          <p>any separate period of unemployment or disability 
                            for which you are receiving monthly benefit.</p>
                        </td>
                      </tr>
                      <tr> 
                        <td class=white_border valign=top 
                  width="30%"> 
                          <p><b><br>
                            Policy</b> </p>
                        </td>
                        <td class=white_border> 
                          <p>the insurance cover we provide under these conditions.</p>
                        </td>
                      </tr>
                      <tr> 
                        <td class=white_border valign=top width="30%"> 
                          <p><b>Policy start date</b> </p>
                        </td>
                        <td class=white_border> 
                          <p>the date cover begins as shown on your certificate 
                            of cover.</p>
                        </td>
                      </tr>
                      <tr> 
                        <td class=white_border valign=top width="30%"> 
                          <p><b>Pre-existing medical condition</b> </p>
                        </td>
                        <td class=white_border> 
                          <p>any condition or disease for which you received treatment, 
                            medication or advice (including examinations or consultations 
                            to monitor the condition) in the 12 months before 
                            the policy start date.</p>
                        </td>
                      </tr>
                      <tr> 
                        <td class=white_border valign=top 
                  width="30%"> 
                          <p><br>
                            <b>Premium</b> </p>
                        </td>
                        <td class=white_border> 
                          <p>the monthly amount you pay to us by direct debit 
                            for insurance under the policy. The monthly premium 
                            you pay is shown on your certificate of cover. </p>
                        </td>
                      </tr>
                      <tr> 
                        <td class=white_border valign=top width="30%"> 
                          <p><b>Proposal</b> </p>
                        </td>
                        <td class=white_border> 
                          <p>your application for insurance under the policy including 
                            the direct debit.</p>
                        </td>
                      </tr>
                      <tr> 
                        <td class=white_border valign=top 
                  width="30%"> 
                          <p><b>Proprietor</b> </p>
                        </td>
                        <td class=white_border> 
                          <p>you own (either alone or with others, except as a 
                            shareholder) the business that you work in.</p>
                        </td>
                      </tr>
                      <tr> 
                        <td class=white_border valign=top width="30%"> 
                          <p><b>Relative</b> <br>
                          </p>
                        </td>
                        <td class=white_border> 
                          <p>your husband, wife, partner, or any other immediate 
                            family member related to you by blood, marriage, or 
                            law.</p>
                        </td>
                      </tr>

                        <td class=white_border valign=top

                          </p>

                            contributions: and/or<br>

                            you are a proprietor;<br>
                            <br>
                            you are a controlling director; or<br>
                            <br>
                            you are a relative of either the proprietor or a controlling 
                            director of the business that you work in.</p>
                        </td>
                      </tr>
                      <tr> 
                        <td class=white_border valign=top width="30%"> 
                          <p><b>Single cover</b> </p>
                        </td>
                        <td class=white_border> 
                          <p>you alone, are insured for 100% of the monthly benefit. 
                          </p>
                        </td>
                      </tr>

                          <p><b>Split cover</b> <br>
                          </p>

                        <td class=white_border> 
                          <p>both people named on the certificate of cover are 
                            insured for a percentage of the monthly benefit.</p>
                        </td>
                      </tr>

                        <td class=white_border valign=top width="30%"> 
                          <p><b>Type of cover</b> </p>
                        </td>
                        <td class=white_border> 
                          <p>unemployment and disability cover;<br>
                            <br>
                            unemployment only cover; or<br>
                            <br>
                            disability only cover.<br>
                            <br>
                            The type of cover you have chosen is shown on your 
                            certificate of cover.</p>
                        </td>

                  width="30%"> 
                          <p><b>Unemployed</b> </p>

                          <p>you no longer have a job because of circumstances

                          <p><b>Waiting period</b> </p>

                            or disability as shown on your certificate.</p>
