<p class="MsoTitle" style="margin-top: 0; margin-bottom: 0">Mountainbikers from
all around the world are invited for the <span lang="EN-US">Cappadocia MTB
Festival Tour </span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-weight:normal">for Racers
and Non-Racers, from June 25 to July 2 in Turkey.</span></p>
<p class="MsoTitle" style="margin-top: 0; margin-bottom: 0"><span style="font-weight: normal" lang="EN-US">This
event runs along with the Capadocia 2nd Annual International Mountainbike Stage
Race and Festival.</span></p>
<p class="MsoTitle" style="margin-top: 0; margin-bottom: 0"><span style="font-weight: normal" lang="EN-US">World
Wide Cycling organizes in cooperation with the race organizer the travel to this
great MTB-event.</span></p>
<p class="MsoTitle" style="margin-top: 0; margin-bottom: 0">&nbsp;</p>
<p class="MsoTitle" style="margin-top: 0; margin-bottom: 0"><a href="information.htm"><font color="#FF0000">If you are interested and
would like to be updated about this travel, Or if you have
questions click here and fill out your name, e-mailadress or telephone number
and your question.</font></a></p>
<p class="MsoTitle" style="margin-top: 0; margin-bottom: 0">&nbsp;</p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: justify; margin-top: 0; margin-bottom: 0"><span lang="EN-US">Enjoy
one week of bicycle racing and festival activities in the area of Turkey that
Fox Racing’s <i>Krank 3</i> video called “one of the Meccas of mountain
biking.”<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp;&nbsp;</span></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: justify; margin-top: 0; margin-bottom: 0">&nbsp;</p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: justify; margin-top: 0; margin-bottom: 0"><span lang="EN-US">Beginning
Monday June 25 and lasting until Monday July 2<sup>nd</sup> . During that time
there will be guided cycling tours throughout the Cappadocia region as well as a
chance to pre-ride the same cross country, down hill and time trial course the
pro racers will use during this UCI minor category stage race.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp;
</span>Pro-riders from 12 countries and Turkey’s best are expected to race in
what is becoming Europe’s secret “return to the roots” racing with
technical, challenging courses set in the picture-postcard lunar landscapes of
Cappadocia.<span style="mso-spacerun:
yes">&nbsp;&nbsp;</span></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: justify; margin-top: 0; margin-bottom: 0">&nbsp;</p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: justify; margin-top: 0; margin-bottom: 0"><img border="0" src="cpadociamtb.jpg" width="599" height="283"></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: justify; margin-top: 0; margin-bottom: 0">&nbsp;</p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: justify; margin-top: 0; margin-bottom: 0"><span lang="EN-US">Stay
in 4 star hotels with all you can eat open buffet breakfasts and dinners in the
town of Urgup within a few kilometers of all the race courses, including the
road bike criterium course that is the highlight of Saturday night’s events.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp;
</span>Participants will be given VIP access to all events including the opening
VIP/press party as well as the closing party for the racers.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp;
</span>You may race in the events with or without a license (though without a
UCI license you will be restricted to only 2 or 3 events), or just come and ride
for the fun and sun of it!<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>You may
bring your own bike or rent one from our fleet of race ready 2001 Giant ATX
bikes.&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: justify; margin-top: 0; margin-bottom: 0">&nbsp;</p>
<div style="float: left; width: 375; height: 473">

</div>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: justify; margin-top: 0; margin-bottom: 0"><span lang="EN-US"><b>This
tour is open to all!!<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>There is no
limit on the amount of people that can join.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp;
</span>Note that the times and the order of the races will not be officially
determined until March 2001.</b></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: justify; margin-top: 0; margin-bottom: 0">&nbsp;</p>

</span>Arrive in Ankara.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>Group
