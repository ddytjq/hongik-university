 <tr><td><p class="PageTitle">Policy statement
      <p class="bodytext">Barclays has a long-standing commitment 
    to environmental responsibility stretching back to the establishment 
    of our Energy Unit in 1979 and the Environmental Risk Management 
    Unit in 1992.
      <p class="bodytext"><a href="http://www.society.barclays.co.uk/pdfs/environment-policy.pdf" class="bodylink">Our current environmental policy</a> and programme 
    commits us to managing the indirect and direct environmental 
    impacts of our business including encouraging improved performance 
    of our business customers and our suppliers. Over the last 
    year, we have: 
      <ul>
    <li><span class="bodytext">Progressed implementation of our Environmental Management 
      System (EMS) in the UK;</span></li>
    <li><span class="bodytext">Initiated EMS activity in continental 
      Europe in key businesses (by headcount). However, roll-out 
      to the Far East was not achieved as originally anticipated;</span></li>
    <li><span class="bodytext">Undertaken an internal audit of Group 
      environmental governance arrangements. Audit recommendations 
      to strengthen environmental management controls in Group 
      businesses will be implemented in 2002.</span></li>
      </ul>
<br></td>
