<center><h1>Web Sites</h1></center>

<center><a href="mailto:hector@c-parr.freeserve.co.uk"><h3>Hector C. Parr</h3></a></center>

<br><center><img  src="web0.gif" width="235" height="225" alt="internet"></center>

<p>There is a vast amount of good material on the Web covering almost every topic. All I can do here is to name a few of those sites which I have personally found of particular quality and value.</p>

<p><center>***</center></p>

<h4>PERIODICALS</h4>

<p>Many scientific magazines and journals make the whole, or selected parts, of their publications freely available on the internet. The following are recommended.

<p><a href="http://www.newscientist.com/">New Scientist</a>

<br><a href="http://www.nature.com/nature/">Nature</a>

<br><a href="http://www.kalmbach.com/astro/">Astronomy Magazine</a>

<br><a href="http://www.astronomynow.com/">Astronomy Now Magazine</a>

<br><a href="http://exosci.com/">Helios Science News</a>

<br><a href="http://www.sciencenews.org/">Science News Online</a>

<br><a href="http://www.sciam.com/">Scientific American</a>

<br><a href="http://www.sigmaxi.org/amsci/amsci-text.html">American Scientist</a>

<br><a href="http://unisci.com/">Daily Science News</a></p>

<p><center>***</center></p>

<p><center>***</center></p>

<h4>PHILOSOPHY</h4>

<a href="http://www.jyu.fi/~rakahu/kirjasto.html">Library of Philosophy</a>
<br>A remarkable collection of all the most significant writings on Philosophy from Plato to the present day. At present some of the site seems to be 'under construction'. 

<p><a href="http://www.marxists.org/reference/subject/philosophy/">Library of Philosophy and Physics</a>
<br>Another remarkable collection, quoting large sections from the writings of all the greatest philosophers, and many scientists, including Descartes, Newton, Hume, Kant, Bohr and Einstein. 

<p><a href="http://www.utm.edu/research/iep/">Encyclopaedia of Philosophy</a>

<br><a href="http://psyche.cs.monash.edu.au/psyche-webinfo.html">Consciousness: Useful Websites</a>

<br><a href="http://www.lucifer.com/virus/alt.memetics/">Meme Theory</a></p>
