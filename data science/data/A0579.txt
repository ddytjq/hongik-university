<P>
It can be difficult to maintain employee loyalty due to the fluidity of the labour market.  The tightening of taxation rules makes it increasingly challenging for employers to incentivise staff.  Worksite Financial Services offers employers a flexible way to show that they value their staff.
<P>
Worksite Financial Services provides employees with customised banking, in partnership with Bank of Scotland.  Employees receive experienced, customer-focused service from one of the leading British clearing banks, negotiated discounts and an easier way to manage their finances around work.

			<TD class=body COLSPAN=2><span class="topnav">OnSite banking</span>
<P>
OnSite Banking offers employers the ability to provide their staff with banking facilities within the workplace.  OnSite Banking provides an Automatic Telling Machine and telephone links to customer service specialists able to discuss and arrange loan facilities, mortgages, credit cards and to deal with any other account queries.
<P>
OnSite Banking not only provides employees with a flexible and convenient way to organise their finances, but also with specially negotiated discounts.
