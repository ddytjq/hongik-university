<p align="justify"><font face="Garamond" size="3">Hampshire Trust Factors Limited, a subsidiary of the Bank, is able to provide factoring
and credit management services to small and medium sized businesses.</font></p>

<p align="justify"><font face="Garamond" size="3">Factoring is a facility whereby Hampshire Trust Factors advance funds against the
invoices that a business raises in the normal course of trading. Not only does this
provide valuable working capital at a far more generous level than traditional bank
funding but it also links cash availability to the growth of the business.</font></p>

<p align="justify"><u><strong><i><font color="#00224C" size="3" face="Garamond">The Facility:</font></i></strong></u></p>
<blockquote>

<p align="justify"><font face="Garamond" size="3">Effectively a proportion of the invoice is advanced as soon as it is raised. So rather
than wait 30 or 60 days to receive payment Hampshire Trust Factors advance the client up
to 85% of the invoice value on day one. When payment is made the client receives the
balance of the invoice less charges.</font></p>

<p align="justify"><font face="Garamond" size="3">An added bonus is that Hampshire Trust Factors can advance against the existing debtor
book, so a business with a debtor ledger of £100,000 could receive up to £85,000 from
the facility at the outset.</font></p>

</blockquote>

<p align="justify"><strong><u><i><font color="#00224C" size="3" face="Garamond">The Service:</font></i></u></strong></p>
<blockquote>

<p align="justify"><font face="Garamond" size="3">As well as being able to provide a valuable source of working capital, Hampshire Trust
Factors also provides an invaluable credit control package for small businesses. This
service does not upset the clients&#146; relationship with their customers as credit
control is undertaken in partnership with the client. The benefits of credit control are
therefore available all year round with no breaks for holidays or sickness. Quality is
assured with one of the most sophisticated accounting systems in the UK ensuring that
reporting and sales ledger management is not only comprehensive but also comprehensible.</font></p>

</blockquote>
