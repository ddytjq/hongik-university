<H1>Everards Brewery Leicestershire Senior League</H1>
<H2>Founded: 1896</H2>
<H3>Founder Members</H3>
<P>Not known.</P>
<H2>Area Covered</H2>
<P>The league covers the County of Leicestershire and its smaller neighbour
Rutland.</P>
<H2>Structure</H2>
<P>2 divisions, 36 clubs<BR><A HREF="#leicsp">Premier Division</A> (18 clubs)<BR><A
HREF="#leics1">Division One</A> (18 clubs)</P>
<HR SIZE="4">
<H2><A NAME="leicsp">Premier Division</A></H2>
<H3>Promotion: 1 club to the <A HREF="midalliance.html">Interlink Express
Midland Alliance</A> (subject to meeting the League's entry criteria)</H3>
<H3>Relegation: 2 clubs to <A HREF="#leics1">Division One</A></H3>
<H3>Composition 1998-99</H3>
<P>Anstey Nomads, Aylestone Park, Barrow Town, Birstall, Coalville, Cottesmore
Amateurs, Downes Sports, Ellistown, Friar Lane Old Boys, Highfield Rangers,
Holwell Sports, Ibstock Welfare, Kirby Muxloe SC, Lutterworth Town, Oadby Town,
Quorn, St. Andrews SC, Thringstone MW.</P>
<H4>Joining</H4>
<P>None.</P>
<H4>Leaving</H4>
<P>None.</P>
<H4>Name Change</H4>
<P>United Collieries to Ellistown.</P>
