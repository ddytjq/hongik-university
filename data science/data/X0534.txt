    <td width="100%" align="center" colspan="2"><font SIZE="2"><p align="left"></font><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">On the day when the focus of racing was on Melbourne
    for the Newmarket Handicap won by the Rubiton horse Rubitano and on Sydney for the Rawson
    Stakes won by Universal Prince and the Coolmore Classic won by Australasia's champion
    racehorse Sunline the racing in South Australia was at Cheltenham. </font></td>

  <tr>
    <td width="50%" align="center"><img src="1milanova.jpg" alt="1milanova.jpg (14593 bytes)" border="2" WIDTH="300" HEIGHT="193"></td>
    <td width="50%"><font SIZE="2"><p align="center"></font><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000"><strong>Race 1 - 2yo Hcp 1266m</strong></font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Regicide led Dodger and Dark Horizon then
    Milanova and Making the Road. Around the turn Regicide led as Dodger challenged with
    Milanova making a run as Dark Horizon tried to make ground along the rails. By the 200m
    Dark Horizon was checked as Regicide held the front with Milanova challenging as Making
    the Road started to make ground. Milanova took the lead past the 50m from Regicide as
    Making the Road flew down the outside. On the post Milanova was still in front by a short
    neck from Making the Road with a head to Regicide third.</font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Milanova is a 2yo bay filly by Danehill
    from L'On Vite and was ridden by Joe Bowditch. She was having her first start and is
    trained by Tony McEvoy for Swettenham Stud Synd, The Lord Vestey, R Bonnycastle, A Gurney
    and Lindsay Park Racing Synd.</font></td>
  </tr>
  <tr>
    <td width="50%" align="center"><font SIZE="2"></font><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000"><strong>Race 2 - Open Hcp 1000m</strong></font><p align="left"><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">El Diablo led Hand Pass and Irish Jantee then
    Inkamulla and Peat Bog. Around the turn El Diablo was travelling easily from Hand Pass
    then a gap to Inkamulla followed by Irish Jantee and Peat Bog. El Diablo made it a track
    gallop in the straight winning by seven lengths from Hand Pass with two lengths to
    Inkamulla third and four lengths to Peat Bog and Irish Jantee.</font></p>
    <p align="left"><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">El Diablo is a 4yo bay gelding
    by El Moxie from Movie Symbol and was ridden by Darren Holland. He is trained by Jeremy
    Gask for RG Gask, LR Colbert, JM Levy and R Bussenschutt. El Diablo was having his 5 start
    and takes his record to 5 starts for three wins and a second.</font></td>

    <td width="50%"><font SIZE="2"><p align="center"></font><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000"><strong>Race 3 - Open Hcp 2271m</strong></font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">King Royale went to the front from Evans
    then Exceptional Chance followed by Satisha then Matheson Boy followed by Tristace and
    Little Neeco from Red Astaire. Little changed to the 1000m where Red Astaire increased the
    tempo to take the lead from King Royale and Evans then Matheson Boy moving up wide then
    Little Neeco. On straightening Red Astaire led Evans as King Royale tried hard on the
    rails and Little Neeco made ground on the outside. By the 200m Little Neeco challenged Red
    Astaire from Evans. Little Neeco hit the front inside the 100m to win by 3/4 length from
    Red Astaire with Evans just holding Satisha for third. </font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Little Neeco is a bay 6yo gelding by
    Niccolo Polo from Daisy Clover and was ridden by David Tootell. He is trained by Brian
    Mueller for LN Mueller.</font></td>
  </tr>
  <tr>
    <td width="50%" align="center"><font SIZE="2"></font><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000"><strong>Race 4 - 3yo Hcp 1316m</strong></font><p align="left"><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Fava led from Luskin's Proud then Gironde followed
    by Kegon then Euro Connection. By the turn Luskin's Proud edged ahead of Fava as Gironde
    started a run from Kegon. On straightening Fava hit the front but was under pressure from
    Gironde then Kegon with Elathea wider on the track. Inside the final 200m Gironde hit the
    front from Kegon then Fava followed by Euro Connection. In the run to the line Gironde
    came away to win by three lengths from Kegon with Mount Batten third.</font></p>
    <p align="left"><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Gironde is a 3yo chestnut
    gelding by Lake Coniston from Enshrine and was ridden by Wayne Kerford. He is trained by
    John Hawkes for Woodlands Stud Synd.</font></td>

    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Renano Brightia proved hard to load in the
    barrier. Flighting led from Kooda Tiara and merrimak then Fonnix and Renano Brightia.
    Little changed until straightening where Flighting raced clear of Kooda Tiara and Merrimak
    as Fonnix chased with Renano Brightia starting to make ground. Inside the final 100m
    Flighting was holding Fonnix but Renano Brightia started to storm down the outside. Renano
    Brightia grabbed the lead in the shadows of the post to win by a long neck from Flighting
    with Fonnix third. </font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Renano Brightia is a chestnut 3yo filly by
    Sky Classic from Moonbeam McQueen and was ridden by Jarrod Marks. She is trained by Len
    Smith for M Kobayashi.</font></td>

    <td width="50%" align="center"><font SIZE="2"></font><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000"><strong>Race 6 - 2MW Hcp 1266m</strong></font><p align="left"><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Pure Gold led with Bricklin's Brother and Squirtle
    but soon Squirtle settled in front from Pure Gold then Bricklin's Brother and Jetway
    followed by Mr Iceman. Around the turn Pure Gold joined Squirtle from Jetway wide and Mr
    Iceman with Bricklin's Brother along the rails. By the 200m Pure Gold and Squirtle were in
    front from Bricklin's Brother starting a run then Mr Iceman and Sciatica. By the 50m
    Bricklin's Brother hit the front to draw away and win by a half length from Pure Gold who
    fought back strongly with Sciatica third.</font></p>
    <p align="left"><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Bricklin's Brother is a 4yo

    <td width="50%" align="center"><font SIZE="2"><p align="center"></font><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000"><strong>Race 8 - Class 6 Hcp 1821m</strong></font></p>
    <p align="left"><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Thunder Creek led You're
    Joking then Mr Mandaree followed by Royal Bell. By the 1100m Jester's Edge pulled its way
    to the lead from Thunder Creek then Mr Mandaree and You're Joking then Royal Bell as St
    Fraar headed the rest. Around the turn Jester's Edge led Thunder Creek then You're Joking
    as Simply Magical made ground along the rails. By the 250m Simply Magical and You're
    Joking hit the front together from Jester's Edge with bad boy Buck running on from
    Jezarich. Simply Magical drew to the lead from You're Joking with Bad Boy Buck next. In
    the final stages Simply Magical did the better to win by a head from You're Joking with
    Bad Boy Buck third and a gap to Jezarich well clear of the rest.</font></p>
    <p align="left"><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Simply Magical is a 5yo
    chestnut gelding by Made of Gold from Mornington Belle and was ridden by Glen Dorol. He is
    trained by David Balfour for himself, JJ O'Connor, OL Peisley, KC Cheong, L Langanis and T
    Papadopoulos.</font></td>

    <td width="50%"><font SIZE="2"><p align="center"></font><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000"><strong>Sydney - Group 1 Ranvet Stakes 2000m</strong></font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Universal Prince settled third last just
    behind Tie the Knot as Dress Circle led. By the 1000m Universal Prince moved up to pocket
    Tie The Knot as Dress Circle led Freemason. On straightening the leaders drifted wide
    leaving space for Tie The Knot while Universal Prince found himself boxed in. By the 200m
    Tie The Knot jined Dress Circle as Universal Prince shouldered his way out from Freemason.
    By the 100m Tie The Knot led as Dress Circle fought back and Universal Prince started to
    storm down the outside. Universal Prince hit the front inside the 50m to win from Dress
    Circle with Freemason third.</font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Universal Prince has now taken his record
    to 24 starts for 6 wins and 8 placings.</font></td>

  <tr>
    <td width="50%" align="center"><font SIZE="2"></font><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000"><strong>Sydney - Group 1 Coolmore Classic 1500m</strong></font><p align="left"><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Sunline jumped to the lead early
    but was soon taken on by Poppett. Poppett settled in front as Sunline checked around her
    heels followed by Maitland Gold and Lady Marion then Ha Ha. By the 800m Poppett led
    Sunline with Primrose Sands moving up on the inside with Maitland Gold wide and Ha Ha
    between them. On straightening Sunline hit the front wide on the track from Poppett and
    Dottoressa with Nanny Maroon and Gentle Genius winding up even wider on the track. By the
    100m Sunline was a length clear of the opposition as Gentle Genius and Nanny Maroon tried
    valiantly but the champion despite carrying 60kg fought back strongly to hold Gentle
    Genius (carrying 52kg) by a long neck with a further half length to Nanny Maroon (53kg)
    third.</font></p>
    <p align="left"><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Sunline has now taken her
    record to 42 starts for 29 wins and 11 victories at Group 1 level. This was Sunline's
    second win in the Coolmore a feat never achieved before and has carried the maximum
    topweight on both occasions.</font></td>
