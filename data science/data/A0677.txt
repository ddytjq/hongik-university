            <p class="contentsubhead">Current Account Types</p>
            <p>Anyone can apply to open a Current Account. It's convenient, flexible and can be accessed anytime of the day or night. Its main benefits include:</p>
            <ul>
			  <li>an AutoBank (Multi Function) card which combines Switch, cheque guarantee up to £100, and automated cash machine facilities allowing you to draw up to £350 per day, or alternatively, an AutoBank (Single Function) card which only allows access to the cash machine facilities 
			  <li>a cheque book 
			  <li>overdraft facility available - with the agreement of the Bank 
			  <li>24 hour telephone access to your account
			  <li>the option of a Clydesdale Bank MasterCard 
			</ul>
            <p class="contenttextBold">Current Account Extra</p>
            <p>Anyone can apply to open a Current Account Extra. This account offers the following benefits:</p>
            <ul>
			  <li>the option of a Clydesdale Bank MasterCard 
			  <li>interest is paid on your account when it's in credit. 
			  <li>an automatic fee-free £100 overdraft 
			  <li>an AutoBank (Multi Function) card combines Switch, cheque guarantee up to £100, and automated cash machine facilities allowing you to draw up to £350 per day 
			</ul>
            <p class="contenttextBold">Current Account Silver </p>
            <p>Again, anyone can apply for a Current Account Silver. With a Current Account Silver, for a small monthly fee, you can benefit from:-</p>
            <ul>
			  <li>attractive tiered rates of interest which mean the more you save the more you make 
			  <li>a fee-free MasterCard (normal cost £10 per annum) 
			  <li>automatic £1,000 overdraft facility with no arrangement fee 
			  <li>a cheque book and AutoBank (Multi Function) card which combines Switch, cheque guarantee up to £100 and automated cash machine facilities which allows you to draw up to £350 per day 
			</ul>
            <p class="contenttextBold">Current Account Gold </p>
            <p>To apply for a current Account Gold, you need an annual income of at least £25,000,or joint annual income of at least £40,000, or net liquid assets of £30,000 or more. In addition to all the services of Current Account Silver, for a small monthly fee youcan also benefit from: - </p>
            <ul>
			  <li>highly attractive rate of interest 
			  <li>automatic £2,500 overdraft facility with no arrangement fee 
			  <li>an AutoBank (Multi Function) card which combines Switch and cheque guarantee up to £250 
			  <li>automated cash machine facilities allowing you to draw up to £500 per day 
			  <li>automatic fee-free Gold MasterCard 
			</ul>
            <p class="contenttextBold">Using Your Current Account to the Best Advantage</p>
            <p>AutoBank (Multi Function) Card </p>
            <p>The beauty of this card is that you never have to carry large amounts of money around with you, and that you can carry out many different transactions. You can use it: </p>
            <ul>
			  <li>to pay for goods and services wherever you see the Switch sign 
			  <li>to withdraw cash at 23,000 automated cash machines in the UK and at any branch counter 
			  <li>as a cheque guarantee card 
			  <li>to check the balance of your account 
			  <li>to order a statement or a cheque book 
			  <li>to pay into your account on the same day across the counter with no paying-in slip at any branch 
			  <li>to receive a mini-statement of your last ten transactions in any branch 
			  <li>to buy goods over the phone 
			</ul>
