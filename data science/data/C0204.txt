<tt><p><b><a name="combined" href="#top">What is combined business insurance</a></b><br><br>Running your own business has its responsibilities as well as its rewards and it makes sense to protect your interests from expensive claims that could be a serious financial burden. You should ensure that you are sufficiently covered for accidents involving members of the public or their property, yourself, your employees and your property.</p></tt>
<tt><p>We have tailored business insurance packages (<b><a name="combined">combined business insurance</a></b>) to suit most small and medium sized businesses which will allow you to obtain quotes for some or all of the following:<ul><li>buildings and contents cover</li><li>employers liability, public liability, professional indemnity, products liability, treatment risk and personal accident cover</li><li>goods in transit and contract works</li><li>tax and vat investigation, book debts, business interuption and legal expenses</li><li>equipment, machinery, tools and signs</li><li>equipment breakdown and loss of licence</li><li>business money cover, event cancellation cover and copyright cover</li><li>engineering business interuption and engineering material damage</li><li>fidelity guarantee (staff honesty), financial loss, libel and slander</li></ul></p></tt>
<tt><p><b><a name="building" a href="#top">What is buildings insurance</a></b><br><br>Buildings insurance usually covers the structure of your property and any fixtures or fittings, including out-buildings, garages, drives, walls, fences, etc.</p></tt>
<tt><p>Cover allows for the cost to repair or rebuild your property if it is damaged. Most policies cover for damage caused through: fire, lightning, earthquake, storm, flood, burst and leaking pipes, theft, vandalism, collision by vehicles, aircraft and animals, falling trees and in some cases subsidence and heave or landslip.</p></tt>
<tt><p>Cover can be on a <a href="#risks">specified perils</a> or an <a href="#risks">all risk</a> basis.<br><br></p></tt>
<tt><p><b><a name="contents" a href="#top">What is contents insurance</a></b><br><br>The contents insurance can be some or all of the following: machinery, plant, trade furniture, tenant's improvements to landlord's property, fixtures and fittings including office equipment and computers, work in progress, office furniture and stock.</p></tt>
<tt><p>Cover can be on a <a href="#risks">specified perils</a> or an <a href="#risks">all risk</a> basis.<br><br></p></tt>
<tt><p><b><a name="risks" href="#top">What are specified perils and all risks</a></b><br><br>Buildings and contents can be insured against loss or damage caused by Specified Perils or All Risks.</p></tt>
<tt><p><b>Specified perils</b> vary with each policy, but generally include cover from the following causes:</p></tt>
<tt><p><i>fire, burst pipes, malicious damage, riot, storm, flood, falling trees, lightning, aircraft, leakage of fuel from fixed oil or heating installations, impact by road vehicles, explosions and earthquakes</i></p></tt>
<tt><p><b>All risks</b> includes the specified perils above, plus Accidential Damage which covers the insured items against being accidentally dropped, knocked, lost or damaged.<br><br></p></tt>
<tt><p><b><a name="liability" href="#top">What is public and employers liability insurance</a></b><br><br>Every private individual and organisation can incur liabilities if others suffer loss through their careless or negligent acts, defects in their property or goods supplied to them.</p></tt>
<tt><p>Various liability policies have been devised to provide financial protection to meet the costs of defending claims and damages that may become payable.</p></tt>
<tt><p><b>Employers' liability insurance</b> is compulsory by law. It provides cover for sums which employers may be liable to pay to any employee in respect of Accidental Death, Bodily Injury or Disease arising out of and in the course of his or her employment.</p></tt>
<tt><p><b>Public liability</b> covers the liability of the insured at law to members of the public for injuries suffered or property damaged.<br><br></p></tt>
<tt><p><b><a name="indemnity" href="#top">What is professional indemnity insurance</a></b><br><br>Professional indemnity covers the professional person or firm against claims for loss due to wrong advice or failure to do what should have been done.<br><br></p></tt>
<tt><p><b><a name="products" href="#top">What is products liability insurance</a></b><br><br>Products liability insurance covers the liability of the seller or manufacturer of goods which have caused injury or damage to others or their property.<br><br></p></tt>
<tt><p><b><a name="businterupt" href="#top">What is business interruption insurance</a></b><br><br>Business interruption covers loss of gross profit, gross revenue or gross rentals due to reduction in turnover and increased cost of working following loss or damage caused by any of the insured perils.<br><br></p></tt>
<tt><p><b><a name="bookdebts" href="#top">What is books debts protection</a></b><br><br>Books debts protection covers cost of monies due following loss of or damage to books/records.<br><br></p></tt>
<tt><p><b><a name="legalexp" href="#top">What is legal expenses cover</a></b><br><br>Legal expenses can be purchased to cover the legal costs arising from a variety of disputes in connection with your business. Cover varies with each policy, but generally include cover for the following:</p></tt>
<tt><p><i>employment disputes and awards, legal defence, property protection, personal injury, inland revenue investigations and vat disputes</i></p></tt>
<tt><p><b><a name="fidelity" href="#top">What is fidelity guarantee</a></b><br><br>Fidelity guarantee covers you against fraud or dishonesty by your employees.<br><br></p></tt>
<tt><p><b><a name="personalaccident" href="#top">What is personal accident cover</a></b><br><br>
This cover provides benefits for you or your named employees if you or they are killed or injured in an accident. Cover is for:</p></tt>
<tt><p><ul><li>Death, loss of limb(s) or eye(s)</li>
<li>Permanent and total disablement</li>
<li>Total disablement (temporary)</li>
<li>Partial disablement (temporary)</li>
<li>Medical expenses</li></ul></p></tt>
<tt><p><b><a name="licence" href="#top">What is loss of licence cover</a></b><br><br>
Loss of licence covers depreciation of your interest in the business resulting from non-renewal or withdrawal of your licence from causes beyond your control.</p></tt>
<tt><p><b><a name="tools" href="#top">What is tools and equipment cover</a></b><br><br>
Tools and equipment cover, covers loss of or damage to tools and equipment owned by you or your employees or hired/borrowed by you. Tools and equipment are those that are usual to your trade and this does not cover office equipment, portable telephones and the like.</p></tt>
<tt><p><b><a name="tax" href="#top">What is tax and VAT investigation cover</a></b><br><br>
Tax and VAT investigation insurance covers the legal costs and expenses of any appeal by the insured against any terms and conditions imposed during the period of the insurance by the Inland Revenue or HM customs and Excise upon completion of an in-depth investigation into the Insured's most recently submitted business accounts in respect of the insured's:</p></tt>
<tt><li>PAYE tax arrangements</li>
<li>business tax arrangements</li>
<li>VAT arrangements</li>
<tt><p>Provided that the insured:</tt>
<tt><li>has fully complied with the legal requirements for the maintenance of books of accounts and tax returns.</li>
<li>has all account and tax returns within the required time limits</li>
<li>was VAT-registered when any alleged VAT offence occurred</li></tt>
<tt><p><b><a name="money" href="#top">What is business money cover</a></b><br><br>
Business money cover includes the loss of business money in the following circumstances:</p></tt>
<tt><li>during business hours, in transit or on contract sites while you or your employees are working there</li>
<li>after business hours; in a locked safe, not in a locked safe, at your or your employees' homes</li>
<li>loss of crossed cheques, credit company sales vouchers and other non-negotiable forms of payment</li></tt>
<tt><p><b><a name="services" href="#top">Corporate and financial services</a></b><br><br>Large businesses have different needs to small and medium sized businesses and require individual solutions to ensure that the correct cover is obtained.</p></tt>
<tt><p><b>Specialised corporate services</b> . commercial all risks . loss of profits . domestic and worldwide liability schemes . hazardous occupations . financial loss . professional indemnity . marine . aviation . goods in transit . contractors all risks . health & safety audits . disaster recovery programmes . claims assessing services . risk control & insurance audit . directors and officers liability . building defects . agricultural and livestock . executive kidnap and ransom . film producers all risks indemnity . engineering and computer breakdown . property portfolios . engineering . motor trade</p></tt>
