  <tr>
    <td width="200%" colspan="2"><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">This years
    Adelaide Horses In Training sale is open to all ages with a number of 2,3 and 4yos entered
    for the sale. Breeze Ups for the sale were held in late August to give buyer the
    opportunity to consider all the offerings with plenty of time before bidding commences.</font><p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000"><strong>Seventy six 2yos are catalogued for sale
    including:</strong></font></p>
    <p><img src="lot4racersedgelisset.jpg" alt="lot4racersedgelisset.jpg (17718 bytes)" align="right" border="2" hspace="10" WIDTH="300" HEIGHT="254"><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Lot 4 a
    chestnut gelding offered by Misty Downs Stud sired by Racer's Edge from Lisset whose only
    foal is On The Snip a multiple city class winner. Amongst the stakeswinners included in
    his family is the sprinter Swift Gun and multiple Group 1 winning stayer Coco Cobanna.
    Breeze up - Stocky type he worked solo and wasn't asked for too much during his workout.</font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Lot 15 is a bay colt by Danehill Dancer
    from No No Annette and will be offered by Yallambee Stud. The 3rd dam of this colt is
    Irish stakeswinner Welsh Garden the top ranked filly on the 1975 Irish 2yo Free Hcp and
    who produced 15 foals for 14 to race all of whom won including the stakeswinners,
    Molesnes, Celtic Heir, Subjective and stakesplaced Celtic Assembly dam of two
    stakeswinners including the group 1 winner Cherokee Rose. Be Yourself dam of No No Annette
    produced six named foals for four to race with all being winners. Breeze up - Well grown
    type and worked very kenly in his gallop. Beaten narrowly but neither horse was put under
    full pressure.</font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Lot 19 represents one of two Rubiton stock
    offer for sale. This bay colt offered by Toorak Thoroughbreds as agents is from multiple
    winner Pseudo Angel who in turn is out of Reelia Goddess the dam of stakeswinner Red Hope.
    Other stakeswinners in his damline include the Sydney stayer Jenkins and Adelaide multiple
    winner Aviate. Breeze up - Worked out in a pair at Morphettville. Looked forward in
    condition and with little effort drew clear of his galloping partner in the final stages.</font></p>
    <p><img src="corpscommander.jpg" alt="corpscommander.jpg (13406 bytes)" align="left" border="2" hspace="10" WIDTH="300" HEIGHT="203"><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Lot 23 is sired by Bite
    The Bullet who has been represented by 151 starters for 101 winners and 10 stakeswinners
    including Fireball, Perak Gold, Star Bullet and Pleasure Giver. A chestnut colt from
    Rapide Pied this offering from Eagle Ridge Thoroughbreds is a half brother to promising
    sprinter Corps Commander the stakesplaced winner of 8 races during his short career.
    Rapide Pied herself is a Group 3 winner in France and is a half sister to stakeswinners
    Grey Beret, Joli Vert and Alleged Jr. Breeze up - Flashy chestnut with four white socks
    and strode out nicely when asked to quicken during the final stages of his workout.</font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Lot 32 (pictured below) is offered on
    behalf of Yallambee Stud a bay colt by Barathea from Sajama. Barathea is the sire of
    recent stakeswinners Easy Rocking and Porto Roca amongst his successful progeny while
    Sajama (by Bletchingly) is the dam of four foals to race for three winners. This colt's
    grand dam is the stakeswinner Beaumont Babe a half sister to Montrose Lass and dam of top
    class stakeswinning stayer Skybeau, stakesplaced Natchez and Classy Babe and multiple
    winners Bay Tram and Mr Logic.</font></p>
    <p><img src="lot32baratheasajama.jpg" alt="lot32baratheasajama.jpg (16767 bytes)" align="right" border="2" hspace="10" WIDTH="271" HEIGHT="255"><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Lot 34
    Binalong Park presents for sale a relation of the legendary sprinter Manikato in this
    chestnut filly. She is by Western Symphony from Secret of Success. Secret of Success has
    produced two winners and out of Red God mare Tumerah. Tumerah is the dam of multiple
    winner Take the Music and is from Markato making her a half sister to Manikato. Markato
    also produced two further winners in addition to Manikato while two of her non winning
    daughters produced the Group 1 winning Abstraction and Glen's Secret the winner of 14
    races.</font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Lot 38 is a bay filly (pictured below) by
    Geiger Counter from Sister Sue and is offered for sale by Mill Park. This filly is the
    second foal of her dam with Collegiate (Aust) - Able Win (HK) the winner of three races in
    Hong Kong. Stakeswinner Bridal Falls is the grand dam of this filly and produced five
    foals to race with all winning. Bridal Falls is from Group 1 winner Runaway Bride making
    her a half sister to Vowed the stakeswinning dam of Charm Scene Land and Kingsgate. Breeze
    up - When asked briefly for an effort appeared to have the ability to sprint clear of her
    partner but was restrained soon after going to the line with ears pricked under a hold.</font></p>
    <p><img src="lot38geigercountersistersue.jpg" alt="lot38geigercountersistersue.jpg (15272 bytes)" align="left" border="2" hspace="10" WIDTH="300" HEIGHT="263"><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Lot 41 represents a renown US stakeswinning family.
    By Rory's Jester from Spinjive this colt is offered by Brueghel Lodge. A mulitple winner
    herself Spinjive has yet to be represented on the racetrack but her dam Out on the Town
    produced foals to race with all winning and two stakesplaced. The dam of Out on the Town
    is the champion mare Fanfreluche (by Northern Dancer) who was Canadian Horse of the Year
    and Champion 3yo Filly. At stud she produced 18 foals for 16 to race and 14 winners
    including the stakeswinners L'Enjoleur (15 wins), La Voyageuse (26 wins Champion Canadian
    3yo filly), Medaille D'Or (Champion Canadian 2yo colt), D'Accord, Grand Luxe and the
    stakesplaced L'Extravagante. Breeze up - Given an easy time in wet conditions only
    striding out in the final 50m.</font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Lot 48 is a bay gelding by Fuji Kiseki from
    Tatiana and is offered by Rubiton Bloodstock as agent. Tatiana has produced the 2yo winner
    Tyson's Bite and is from the stakeswinning Twig Moss mare Rory's Twig. Rory's Twig is from
    Rory's Rocket the dam of champion 2yo and champion sire Rory's Jester, stakeswinner Sushi
    Rocket and Rory's Girl the unraced dam of stakeswinner Very Droll who has produced the
    Golden Slipper winner Ha Ha and stakeswinner Dry Humour. This colt has been nominated for
    the 2002 Golden Slipper, Blue Diamond and is eligible for the San Miguel Heroic
    Championship. Breeze up - In very trying conditions with heavy wind and rain setting in
    this striking type galloped well under his own steam. With four white socks and a blaze he
    really knuckled down to the task striding out well to the line.</font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Lot 50 Offered on behalf of Rosemount Stud
    is a brown gelding by Snaadee from The Ultimate. A winner herself The Ultimate is the dam
    of 9 time winner Yallah Breeze and is out of the stakesplaced Marquise. Marquise produced
    three winners at stud and is from the stakeswinner Birthright (by Rego) and is also the
    3rd dam of the Group 1 winner in New Zealand Cinder Bella. Birthright is a foal of the
    famous broodmare Dark Jewel. At stud Dark Jewel produced 10 named foals for 9 winners
    including Baguette (by Rego and winner of the Golden Slipper Stakes plus Champion
    Australian 2yo), Heirloom (by Rego and equal top 2yo filly of her year plus winner of 9
    races including the Group 1 Thousand Guineas), Cabochon (15 wins including the Group 1
    Epsom Hcp) and Betelgeuse (stakeswinner with 13 career wins). Breeze up - In extremely wet
    conditions at Geelong this gelding strode out well towards the line.</font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Lot 52 is a brown gelding by Last Tycoon
    from Tudor Primrose offered by Forextra Lodge. This colt is the second foal of Tudor
    Primrose who in turn is from stakeswinner Jewel Flight. Jewel Flight produced 13 foals for
    11 to race and 6 winners including multiple Group 1 winner Diamond Shower (dam of
    stakeswinner Double Your Bet and stakesplaced Diamond Dane), stakesplaced Diamond Storm
    and Diamond Prelude the dam of stakeswinners Our Cashel and Diamond Cashel. Breeze up -
    Appears a tall type and while not pushed for an effort worked keenly to the line.</font></p>
    <p><img src="lot70royalacademybriko.jpg" alt="lot70royalacademybriko.jpg (18076 bytes)" align="right" border="2" hspace="10" WIDTH="300" HEIGHT="266"><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Lot 61
    Sired by Regal Classic this bay filly is from Anaconda and is offered for sale by Brika
    Stud. Anaconda is the dam of 8 living foals with 6 to race and all have won. Her foals
    including the stakeswinner Yarli Gold winner of 14 races plus winners Marcin On, Madame
    Conqueror, Devouring, Miss Intent and Peacherino. This filly's 2nd dam is the stakeswinner
    Blackadder dam of 13 named foals for 10 to race and 9 winners. Breeze up - Worked solo and
    stretched out nicely when asked for effort in final 50m. </font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Lot 70 is a chestnut colt by Royal Academy
    from Briko offered by Yallambee Stud as agent. This colt is the first foal of Sir Tristram
    mare Briko but his grandam is the unraced Bletchingly mare Nilkerie who has produced 4 to
    race with 3 winners including Educate. The dam of Nilkerie is the multiple stakeswinner
    Nunkalowe who produced 9 named foals 8 to race for 4 winners. Amongst those winners was
    the stakeswinner Biscalowe (by Biscay) who in turn produced the Golden Slipper winner and
    sire Marauding (by Sir Tristram), the stakeswinner and new season sire Springsteen (by
    Danehill) and Cajun Magic the dam of stakeswinner Voodoo Beat. Breeze up - In the rain
    this finer built type galloped well under his own steam in the straight holding off his
    partner by a length.</font></p>
    <p><img src="lot86gallodiferroimposalot.jpg" alt="lot86gallodiferroimposalot.jpg (19969 bytes)" align="left" border="2" hspace="10" WIDTH="274" HEIGHT="253"><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Lot 78 is by Suave Dancer from Estalista and this
    bay colt is offered by Eliza Park Stud. Estalista has produced 12 foals for 7 to race and
    6 winners including the stakeswinner Courtalista (dam of stakeswinner Command Post) and
    the winners Kruger Park, Balmoral Red, Lista Court, Renoirs and Straightest. Other
    stakeswinners featured in this colts family are Red Nose, Manawapoi, Distancia and Run
    Riot. Breeze up - Solid type kept under a hold for his workout.</font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Lot 86 Offered by Boldmount Pty Ltd is the
    chestnut colt by Gallo Di Ferro from Imposalot. Imposalot has produced four foals with her
    other three progeny all winners including Silent Comedian and Sideshow Charlie. The grand
    dam of this colt is Salote the dam of winners Jewelled Star and Diamonds plus Ziegfield
    Lass an unraced mare who produced All Grace the dam of champion racehorse Saintly. Salote
    is out of Dark Queen who also produced multiple Group 1 winner Taj Rossi, Memine (dam of
    stakeswinner Lady Nurmi) and the unraced Majoram dam of stakeswinners Dark Eclipse (Golden
    Slipper) and Bagalot. Breeze up - Sprinted well despite the rain and kept on a tight hold
    throughout.</font></p>
    <p><img src="kolase.jpg" alt="kolase.jpg (13390 bytes)" align="right" border="2" hspace="10" WIDTH="300" HEIGHT="215"><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Lot 94 is the final lot of the
    sale but is worth staying for. He is a chestnut gelding offered by R. P. &amp; M. E. Boyle
    sired by Kenny's Best Pal from Kololo. Kololo has produce four foals for three winners
    including Kolase a winner in both Adelaide and Melbourne. The third dam of this gelding is
    the UK winner Near the Line who produced four winner with three of those stakeswinners
    including Sapper, Ombra Del Sol and Borderline.</font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">The 15 3yos catalogued for sale include:</font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Lot 1 Lady Spice. This 3yo filly offered by
    Rubiton Bloodstock is by Danzig Connection from Dutchy's Gem. Three previous foals of
    Dutchy's Gem are all winners including the stakeswinner Spice Girl and stakesplaced Shaye
    Spice (pictured below). Breeze up - Under a tight hold this solidly built filly sprinted
    nicely beating her companion to the line.</font></p>
    <p><img src="shayespice.jpg" alt="shayespice.jpg (13368 bytes)" align="left" border="2" hspace="10" WIDTH="273" HEIGHT="235"><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Lot 7 Major Rambo. This 3yo
    gelding, offered on behalf of Chatswood Stud, is by Military Plume from Jewel In Flight.
    Biscay dam has produced six named foals for three winners including Addition and Jewel
    Catcher. The grand dam of this colt is the multiple G1 stakeswinner My Gold Hope (dam of
    stakeswinner Blue Storm, Only Hope and the unraced Fibre dam of stakeswinner Byzance
    Star). My Gold Hope is from Gold Diana who also produced the G1 winner Old Currency among
    her 8 winning progeny. Breeze up - Extended well but under a tight hold throughout.</font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Lot 83 Gogan's Image. This 3yo gelding is
    offered by Chatswood Stud and is&nbsp; by Naturalism from Etoile De Paris. Etoile De Paris
    has produced 13 named foals for 7 to race and 6 winners including stakeswinner Only Royale
    and stakesplaced Green Lightning plus Chanson de Paris (dam of stakeswinner Brandon
    Prince) and Bluebell Dancer dam of Adelaide stakeswinner Torezal. Breeze up - When asked
    for his effort in the straight quickened nicely.</font></p>
    <p><strong><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Completing the catalogue are two
    four year olds. </font></strong></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Lot 65 Ascot Prancer (Ascot Knight-Sweet
    Prancer) from the damline of Sex Appeal (who produced El Gran Senor (top of the 2yo and
    3yo European Classifications during his career) and Try My Best (who headed the 2yo
    English and Irish 2yo Free Hcp) plus the stakeswinner Solar. Breeze up - Lightly built
    filly who worked keenly though kept under a tight hold.</font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" size="2" color="#008000">Lot 67 Beyond Broke (Covetous-Gay Rossina)
    from the family of Canterbury Belle (3yo filly of the year and winner of 10 races
    including 2 at Group 1 level), stakeswinner Callao Star and Kiwi Bride (this geldings
    second dam and grand dam of stakeswinner Coup Wood). Breeze up - Tall strong type this
    gelding worked well in a solo gallop at Morphettville and strode out well at the finish.</font></td>
