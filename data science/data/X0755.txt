    <td width="100%" align="left" colspan="2"><font face="Arial" color="#004000"><br>
    <small>Adelaide racing said farewell to Morphettville today as the track is now due for
    renovations planned to be completed in February next year in time for the 2002 Magic
    Millions raceday. A training double was taken out by Richard Jolly while Dwayne Dunn rode
    and double to maintain his lead in the premiership and apprentice Travis Creek recorded a
    treble for the day. </small><br>
    </font></td>

    <td width="42%" align="center"><img src="1tawtrice.jpg" alt="1tawtrice.jpg (13328 bytes)" border="2" WIDTH="300" HEIGHT="208"></td>
    <td width="58%"><strong><font face="Arial" color="#004000"><small>Race 1 - Last Tycoon Hcp
    2024m </small></font></strong><p><font face="Arial" color="#004000"><small>Tawtrice
    settled in front from Dane's Reward then Brownlow and Yuvaraj with Showgazer next. Little
    changed until the turn where Tawtrice kicked clear from Brownlow and Yuvaraj with
    Showgazer moving to the outside to start his run. By the 200m Tawtrice was going well in
    front as Showgazer moved to second from Brownlow then a break to the others. In the run to
    the line Tawtrice moved further ahead to win by five lengths from Showgazer with Brownlow
    third. The winner is now destined for a career over hurdles. </small></font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" color="#004000"><small>Tawtrice is a 5yo chestnut gelding by
    Tawriffic from Acetrim and was ridden by Travis Creek. He is trained by Richard Jolly for
    RC Dennett, IJ Donchi, BP Frazzetto, JL Bertalli, PD Constance, PE Quinn, LW Ross and JP
    McNamara. </small></font></td>
  </tr>
  <tr>
    <td width="42%" align="center"><img src="2letswent.jpg" alt="2letswent.jpg (17772 bytes)" border="2" WIDTH="300" HEIGHT="254"></td>
    <td width="58%"><strong><font face="Arial" color="#004000"><small>Race 2 - Rubiton Hcp
    1200m </small></font></strong><p><font face="Arial" color="#004000"><small>On settling
    Let's Went led from Wyndam All the Ansqueak inside Unbridled Lad. Little changed until
    they turned for home where Let's Went straightened clear of Wyndam All and Unbridled Lad
    with Ansqueak along the rails and Catlight starting a run out wider. By the 200m Let's
    Went was under pressure as Catlight challenged well clear of Unbridled Lad. In the run to
    the line Let's Went fought on strongly to hold off the determined effort of Catlight to
    win by a half neck with Unbridled Lad many lengths back in third. </small></font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" color="#004000"><small>Let's Went is a brown 2yo gelding by Senor
    Pete from Joking Jessie and was ridden by Dwayne Dunn. He is trained by Russell Cameron
    for WM Brown, GJ Miller, GJ Gray, DP Gilham, J Nicholas, G Buckingham, NN Columb, KM
    Quinn, MS Williams and RD Heathershaw. </small></font></td>
  </tr>
  <tr>
    <td width="42%" align="center"><img src="3ambroc.jpg" alt="3ambroc.jpg (12636 bytes)" border="2" WIDTH="300" HEIGHT="219"></td>
    <td width="58%"><strong><font face="Arial" color="#004000"><small>Race 3 - Umatilla Hcp
    1050m </small></font></strong><p><font face="Arial" color="#004000"><small>Ambroc moved to
    the front soon after jumping from Cobb's Girl the Icy Gaze followed by Freedom Funds and
    Miss Victory. Around the turn Ambroc led Cobb's Girl as Icy Gaze became unbalanced in the
    going and dropped back with Freedom Funds making ground from Miss Victory. On
    straightening Ambroc raced clear as Cobb's Girl blundered a couple of times with Freedom
    Funds improving on her outside and Another Fare making ground along the rails. With 150m
    to go Ambroc was holding the opposition and went on to win by 3 1/2 lengths from Freedom
    Funds with Another Fare third. </small></font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" color="#004000"><small>Ambroc is a 3yo brown gelding by Amen Brother
    from Fager's Charm and was ridden by Cheree Buchiw. He is trained by Joe Lockyer for MA
    Lockyer, GP Boseley and AR Drewett. </small></font></td>
  </tr>
  <tr>
    <td width="42%" align="center"><img src="4gunnit.jpg" alt="4gunnit.jpg (13202 bytes)" border="2" WIDTH="300" HEIGHT="223"></td>
    <td width="58%"><strong><font face="Arial" color="#004000"><small>Race 4 - Blue Gum Farm
    Hcp 1800m </small></font></strong><p><font face="Arial" color="#004000"><small>Blue Gum
    Farm is located in Victoria at Euroa and stands a classy group of stallions. Champion sire
    Last Tycoon, promising sire Encosta De Lago, proven sire Umatilla and champion racehorse,
    proven sire and promising broodmare sire Rubiton makes up their stallion roster. </small></font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" color="#004000"><small>Centurian led early from Gunnit then Scalded
    Cat with Tezza Raami next. By the 1200m Centurian led Scalded Cat the Gunnit followed by
    Edmac with Gilgamesh at the head of the others. Around the turn Centurian led Gunnit as
    they kicked Clear of Scalded Cat then Gilgamesh. Passing the 200m Gunnit hit the front and
    started to race away from Centurian who was well clear of the field. On the line Gunnit
    was in front by nearly 10 lengths from Centurian with a further 12 lengths back to A
    Chorus Line in third. </small></font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" color="#004000"><small>Gunnit is a grey 3yo rig by Toy Pindarri from
    Zan Zooner and was ridden by Travis Creek. He is trained by Melody Keegan for R Connor, S
    Forte, F&amp;M Trimboli, C Verantsis and J Makepeace. </small></font></td>
  </tr>

    Memorial Flying 1200m </small></font></strong><p><font face="Arial" color="#004000"><small>Manetti
    settled in front from Easy Landing with Mustinair third with a break to Star Joe followed
    by Major Wilkes then Newsflash with Barnacles at the rear. Little changed until they
    turned for home where Manetti led Easy Landing as Star Joe made his run down the outside
    with Mustinair next. With 200m to run Manetti was under pressure from Star Joe as
    Mustinair started to run on between them. Inside the final 50m Star Joe was in front but
    Manetti was fighting back and Mustinair started to dive through the centre. Right on the
    post Mustinair stuck his nose in front to win by that margin from Star Joe with Manetti a
    half length back in third from Newsflash. </small></font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" color="#004000"><small>Mustinair is a bay 7yo gelding by Mustin Lake
    from Diavola and was ridden by Bradley Vale. He is trained by John Hall for RL Bradmore,
    JH Bryant&lt; RKC Cornish, GW Ormsby and WM Raven. </small></font></td>

    soon challenged by Olongopo. Gatton fought back to hold Olongopo in the run to the line
    winning by a head with Scenicy third. </small></font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" color="#004000"><small>Gatton is a 4yo bay gelding by Military Plume

    <p><font face="Arial" color="#004000"><small>Sir Archer and Checa shared the early lead
    from I Am The Man then Oriental Queen with Torque next. Sir Archer and Checa turned for
    home from Torque was switched across heels to the rails from Oriental Queen with Bluestone
    Villa next as Pataudi started to weave a passage through the field from the rear. By the
    200m Torque had hit the front from Sir Archer as Bluestone Villa started to charge down
    the outside. At the 50m Bluestone Villa drew level with Torque as the pair started to race
    tightly well clear of Sir Archer then Pataudi. They went to the line stride for stride
    with Bluestone Villa winning by a nose from Torque with Sir Archer third and Pataudi
    fourth. </small></font></p>
    <p><font face="Arial" color="#004000"><small>Bluestone Villa is a 5yo grey gelding by
