<p><font face="Arial,Helvetica"><font size=-1>Here my daily updates: the
pictures are in the pictures section of the Santa Barbara competition.</font></font>
<p><b>new years throw</b>
<br>January 01, 2001
<br>threw with Chris and Erik on Storke Field. winds
<br>were high but died out soon. picture of the day
<br>shows a Voyager (Schuetz), Isla Vista (Fields),
<br>Backdraft (Gubler), Milpas Reaper (Horvath),
<br>S-Boom (Jamieson), Millenium (Cotter). In brackets
<br>the name of the builder of the particular boom.
<br>oh, and I updated the 100m list. Had to kick out a
<br>few names...
<p><b>new years eve</b>
<br>January 01, 2001
<br>ok. threw and measured some. wind was up and
<br>down. no official score.
<br>at night we watched the fireworks. picture of the
<br>fireworks from Eriks house
<br>here is the picture for yesterday. picture of the day.
<p><b>official comp</b>
<br>December 30, 2000
<br>ok, just a shortie: Erik won the first day hands down
<br>with 148m. Andy Cross egalised the Canadian
<br>record of his brother 114m. more later, including
<br>pictures.
<p><b>first (casual) day</b>
<br>December 29, 2000
<br>Dan Neelands and Bob Foresi were already on the
<br>field when Erik and I turned up. we were just
<br>starting to throw when Kelly Neelands came. Later
<br>that day other people showed up, like Rick
<br>Denuccio, Chris Cotter, Steve Conaway. We
<br>showed a passerby (Scott) how to throw.
<br>Leaving the field on sunset, I was really happy to
<br>take a shower. Tomorrow starts the offical part of
<br>the sb gathering.
<br>here the picture of the day.
<br>and here some pictures of the sunset: one, two,
<br>three.
<p><b>building</b>
<br>December 23, 2000
<br>on the third day, the weather is still sunny and nice.
<br>no surprises. erik and I were building booms out in
<br>his yard. comparing building styles. here is the
<br>picture of the day. I am the one in the marshmellow
<br>suit. (yesterdays picture was censored by my
<br>sponsor)
<p><b>threw a bit</b>
<br>December 22, 2000
<br>hmm, out on storke field with erik. a nice breeze
<br>from the west (mainly). leisure throwing. erik made
<br>a few very good booms, but our throwing wasn't very
<br>consistent. however it was nice being out in the
<br>sun; we had a few far throws, but only good returns
<br>when winds were up a bit.
<p><b>Santa Barbara</b>
<br>December 21, 2000
<br>Being in the 2nd capital of long distance booming
<br>not much happened yet. We getting ready for the
<br>comp next week by sitting around and talking about
<br>booms. not much throwing since we're kind of lazy,
<br>and its also a bit foggy. ahem, take a look at the
<br>picture.
