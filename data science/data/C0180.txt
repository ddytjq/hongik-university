				<td class=body colspan=2>
<b>A safer way for your client to enjoy stockmarket growth</b> <br>
Scottish Friendly's Growth &#38; Security Bond could make your client's savings work much harder.  This special investment gives their savings a higher growth potential than a building society because it offers the potential of stockmarket returns but with less risk than direct stockmarket investment.  So it's a safer way to ensure savings can grow steadily and do more than keep pace with inflation.
<p>
Your client's money is invested in our highly successful With Profits Fund.  The profits it makes in times of strong stockmarket growth are pooled to create a 'cushion' of reserves.  These are distributed over time in the form of bonuses - even when stockmarkets are low.  And once a bonus is added to the Bond, your client has the reassurance of knowing it cannot be taken away, unless a Market Value Adjustment (MVA) is made.  Further details of the MVA can be found in the Key Features document online.
