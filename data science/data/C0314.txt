  <p align="center" class="body"><span class="body">The Tax Exempt Junior Bond 
    is a long term savings plan that is specially designed for an adult to save 
    on behalf of a child, without paying tax. Thanks to the special status the 
    Government gives friendly societies, our savings plan pays virtually no tax 
    on the growth it achieves and neither do you when it pays out. </span></p>
  <p align="center" class="body"><span class="body">The plan is a special tax-free 
    investment that grows as your child does. You must save for at least 10 years 
    to enjoy the full benefits of its tax exempt status, however, you can choose 
    to extend it to pay out on a special day such as an 18th, 21st or 25th birthday.</span></p>
  <p align="center" class="body"><span class="body">Premiums range from between
