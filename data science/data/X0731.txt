 ok, since I know that the forum gets about 5 hits per day, I will post a few things that are on my mind... <BR> <BR>I tested my current g11 sheet &#40;now bear with me since I dont know the correct terms&#41;: <BR> <BR>when you build booms out of wood, you have to be carefull as to cut the shape so it along the lines of the wood. <BR>now g11 has also a kind of priority direction. or rather it is week in a certain direction. <BR>hmmm, kinda hard to describe really: you see the directions of the glas fibers in the resin. usually there are 2 directions with an angle of 90 deg to each other. now these directions I consider the strong ones. WEAK is the direction 45 deg to it. <BR>I did tests with long thin pieces and found this results. was no surprise though, as Lorenz &#40;materials scientist, he has to know this stuff...&#41; pointed it out to me a few years ago. But I never believed it was relevant. now I do. see to it that the narrow area of your booms wings is not 45 deg to the strong directions of the g11. <BR> <BR>Its gonna give you one thing less to worry while building...
